Inyongera

1 Izina ry’Imana mu Vyanditswe vy’igiheburayo no mu Vyanditswe vy’ikigiriki vya gikirisu

2 Amazina y’icubahiro akoreshwa kuri Yehova be n’amazina amudondora

3 Yezu—Uwumeze nk’Imana; asangiye akamere n’Imana

4 Ukuhaba kwa Kristu (Parousia)

5 “Igiti co kubabarizwako”

6 Ijambo ry’igiheburayo “neʹphesh” (נפש) be n’iry’ikigiriki psy·kheʹ (ψυχή)

7 “Ukuzimu”—Imva rusangi y’abantu; Icobo

8 “Gehena”—Ikigereranyo c’ukurandurwa buhere

9 “Ubusambanyi”—Ubwoko bwose bw’uguhuza ibitsina kutemewe n’amategeko

10 Imvugo “Isezerano rya kera” n’“Isezerano risha”

11 Amahera be n’ingero zitandukanye

12 Ikirangamisi c’amezi yo muri Bibiliya

13 Ivyambarwa vy’umuherezi mukuru

14 Itaberenakulo nk’uko idondorwa muri Kuvayo 26

15 Urusengero mu gihe ca Salomo

16 Ikarata ijanye n’Itanguriro

17 Akarere k’imiryango ya Isirayeli

18 Ubwami bwunze ubumwe bwa Sauli, Dawidi, Salomo

19 Palesitina mu gihe c’ubusuku bwa Yezu

20 Ibintu nyamukuru vyabaye mu buzima bwa Yezu bwo kw’isi

21 Insiguro y’amajambo

Imitwe yo kuganirako ishingiye kuri Bibiliya
