Itanguriro  21 : 1 - 34

21 Nuko Yehova yitaho Sara nk’uko yari yaravuze, maze Yehova agirira Sara nk’uko yari yaravuze. 2 Sara aribungenga, araheza avyarira umuhungu Aburahamu mu busaza bwiwe kuri ca gihe cashinzwe Imana yari yaramubwiye. 3 Nuko rero Aburahamu yita Izahaki umuhungu wiwe yari yavyariwe, uwo Sara yari yamuvyariye. 4 Maze Aburahamu agenyera Izahaki umuhungu wiwe amaze imisi umunani, nk’uko Imana yari yaramutegetse. 5 Aburahamu yari afise imyaka ijana igihe yavyarirwa Izahaki umuhungu wiwe. 6 Maze Sara avuga ati: “Imana yanteguriye agatwengo: uwuzovyumva wese azontwenga.” 7 Kandi yongerako ati: “Ni nde yobwiye Aburahamu ati: ‘Sara azokwonsa abana ata kabuza?’ Yamara jewe nibarutse umuhungu mu busaza bwiwe.”

8 Nya mwana aguma akura, barahava baramucutsa; maze Aburahamu ategura inzimano ihambaye ku musi wo gucutsa Izahaki. 9 Kandi Sara yaguma abona umuhungu wa Hagari Umunyamisirikazi, uwo yari yaravyariye Aburahamu, ashinyagura. 10 Atangura rero kubwira Aburahamu ati: “Irukana uyu mushumbakazi n’umuhungu wiwe, kuko umuhungu w’uyu mushumbakazi atazoba samuragwa kumwe n’umuhungu wanje, kumwe na Izahaki!” 11 Mugabo ico kintu kibabaza cane Aburahamu ku bijanye n’umuhungu wiwe. 12 Maze Imana ibwira Aburahamu iti: “Ntureke ngo hagire ikintu na kimwe Sara aguma akubwira kikubabaza ku vyerekeye uwo muhungu no ku vyerekeye umushumbakazi wawe. Umviriza ijwi ryiwe, kuko ikizokwitwa uruvyaro* rwawe kizoboneka biciye kuri Izahaki. 13 Nayo uwo muhungu w’uwo mushumbakazi, na we nyene nzomugira ihanga, kuko ari uruvyaro rwawe.”

14 Aburahamu avyuka kare mu gitondo, afata umukate n’umukuza w’urushato w’amazi abiha Hagari mu kubishira ku rutugu rwiwe, amuha na nya mwana, araheza aramurungika. Nuko Hagari aragenda, ayerera mu gahinga ka Beyeri-sheba. 15 Amaherezo amazi arahera muri nya mukuza w’urushato, hanyuma ata nya mwana munsi ya kimwe mu bisaka vyaho. 16 Maze arabandanya, yicara ukwiwe, nk’ahoraswa n’umuheto, kuko yavuga ati: “Sindabibone umwana niyapfa.” Nuko yicara nka hariya, ashira ijwi hejuru ararira.

17 Maze Imana yumva ijwi rya nya muhungu, hanyuma umumarayika w’Imana ahamagara Hagari ari mu majuru, amubwira ati: “Ni amaki ga Hagari? Ntutinye, kuko Imana yumvirije ijwi ry’uriya muhungu, hariya ari. 18 Haguruka, ukure hasi uwo muhungu umufate n’ukuboko kwawe, kuko nzomugira ihanga rikomeye.” 19 Maze Imana ihumura amaso yiwe, araheza abona iriba ry’amazi; nuko aragenda atangura kwuzuza nya mukuza w’urushato amazi no guha nya muhungu utwo kunywa. 20 Nuko Imana ibandanya kubana na nya muhungu, na we abandanya gukura no kuba mu gahinga; acika umurashi. 21 Aba mu gahinga ka Parani, maze nyina amuronkera umugore wo mu gihugu ca Misiri.

22 Nuko ico gihe, Abimeleki ari kumwe na Fikoli umukuru w’ingabo ziwe, abwira Aburahamu ati: “Imana iri kumwe nawe mu kintu cose ukora. 23 Ubu rero, ndahira Imana ng’aha yuko utazoduhemukira twe n’uruvyaro rwanje n’abazorukomokako; yuko, nk’uko nakugiriye urukundo rudahemuka, ari ko uzotugirira twe n’igihugu ubayemwo uri kavamahanga.” 24 Nuko Aburahamu avuga ati: “Ndarahira.”

25 Aburahamu akariye Abimeleki ku bijanye n’iriba ry’amazi abasuku ba Abimeleki bari banyaze ku nguvu, 26 Abimeleki aca avuga ati: “Sinzi uwakoze ico kintu, eka nawe ubwawe ntiwabimbwiye, kandi nanje ubwanje sinari bwavyumve atari uno musi.” 27 Nuko Aburahamu afata intama n’inka abiha Abimeleki, maze bompi bagirana isezerano. 28 Aburahamu ashize imyagazi y’intama y’amashashi indwi yo mu mukuku ukwa yonyene, 29 Abimeleki yongera kubwira Aburahamu ati: “Aha iyi myagazi y’intama y’amashashi indwi washize ukwa yonyene isobanura iki?” 30 Maze avuga ati: “Wakire mu kuboko kwanje iyo myagazi y’intama y’amashashi indwi, kugira bimbere icabona yuko nimvye iri riba.” 31 Ni co catumye yita aho hantu Beyeri-sheba, kuko aho ari ho bompi barahiriye indahiro. 32 Nuko bapfundika isezerano i Beyeri-sheba; maze Abimeleki ahagurukana na Fikoli umukuru w’ingabo ziwe basubira mu gihugu c’Abafilisitiya. 33 Inyuma y’ivyo atera igiti c’umukwaju i Beyeri-sheba maze ahambariza izina rya Yehova, Imana yamaho ibihe bidahera. 34 Nuko Aburahamu abandanya kuba mu gihugu c’Abafilisitiya imisi myinshi ari kavamahanga.

^ Ita. 21:12 Ijambo ku rindi ni “urubuto.”
