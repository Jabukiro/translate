Itanguriro  33 : 1 - 20

33 Mu nyuma Yakobo araramura amaso araraba, abona Esawu ariko araza ari kumwe n’abagabo amajana ane. Ku bw’ivyo aca agabura abana abaha Leya na Rasheli na ba basukukazi babiri, 2 maze ashira nya basukukazi n’abana babo imbere, Leya n’abana biwe inyuma ya bano, hanyuma Rasheli na Yozefu inyuma yabo. 3 Na we ubwiwe aja imbere yabo, atangura kwunama gushika hasi, incuro indwi, gushika yegereye mwene wabo.

4 Nuko Esawu yiruka kumusanganira, maze atangura kumugumbira, amugwa kw’izosi aramusoma, baraturikisha bararira. 5 Maze araramura amaso abona nya bagore na nya bana aca avuga ati: “Aba bari kumwe nawe ni bande?” Na we ati: “Ni abana Imana yahaye umusavyi wawe ku butoni.” 6 Nya basukukazi baca begera imbere, bo n’abana babo, maze barunama; 7 na Leya yegera imbere, be n’abana biwe, maze barunama, mu nyuma Yozefu yegera imbere, na Rasheli, maze barunama.

8 Aca avuga ati: “Iyo kambi y’ingenzi yose twahuye wayirungikiye iki?” Na we ati: “Ni ukugira ngo ngire ubutoni mu maso y’umukama wanje.” 9 Maze Esawu avuga ati: “Mfise vyinshi cane, mwene wacu. Ivyawe nibibandanye kuba ivyawe.” 10 Ariko Yakobo avuga ati: “Oya, ndakwinginze. Nimba rero nagize ubutoni mu maso yawe, niwakire ingabirano yanje mu kuboko kwanje. Nayizanye kugira nshobore kubona mu maso hawe. Kandi nahabonye nk’aho mbonye mu maso h’Imana, kuko wanyakiranye agahimbare. 11 Ndakwinginze wakire ingabirano iserura umuhezagiro wanje wazaniwe, kuko Imana yantonesheje kandi nkaba mfise ikintu cose.” Nuko abandanya kumwinginga, ku buryo yakira.

12 Mu nyuma Esawu avuga ati: “Dufate inzira tugende, ngende imbere yawe.” 13 Mugabo amubwira ati: “Umukama wanje arazi ko abana batagira intege be n’uko njejwe intama n’inka vyonsa; nibabiyobora gihutihuti mu musi umwe, umukuku wose rero urapfa ata kabuza. 14 Ndakwinginze, umukama wanje aje imbere y’umusavyi wiwe, mugabo jewe mbandanye urugendo uko bimpimbara nisunze uko ibitungwa biri imbere yanje bigenda be n’uko abana bagenda, gushika nshitse iw’umukama wanje i Seyiri.” 15 Maze Esawu avuga ati: “Ndakwinginze ureke nkuronse bamwe mu bantu bari kumwe nanje.” Na we ati: “Kubera iki ivyo? Ningire ubutoni mu maso y’umukama wanje.” 16 Nuko kuri uwo musi Esawu afata inzira, asubira i Seyiri.

17 Yakobo na we afata inzira aja i Sukoti, maze atangura kwiyubakira inzu, ubusho bwiwe na bwo abukorera insago. Ni co catumye aho hantu ahita Sukoti.

18 Mu nyuma Yakobo ashika impore mu gisagara ca Shekemu, ikiri mu gihugu ca Kanani, mu gihe yari avuye i Padani-aramu; araheza ashinga ikambi imbere y’ico gisagara. 19 Maze yironsa mu kuboko kwa bene Hamori se wa Shekemu igice c’itongo yashinzemwo ihema ryiwe, ku bice ijana vy’amahera. 20 Hanyuma ahashinga igicaniro, maze acita ngo Imana ni Imana ya Isirayeli.
