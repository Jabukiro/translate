Itanguriro  34 : 1 - 31

34 Kandi Dina umukobwa wa Leya, uwo uno yari yaravyariye Yakobo, yari amenyereye gusohoka kuraba abakobwa bo muri ico gihugu. 2 Nuko Shekemu mwene Hamori Umuhiwi, umukuru wo muri ico gihugu, aramubona maze aramufata, aryamana na we, aramwonona. 3 Nuko umutima wiwe wumira kuri Dina umukobwa wa Yakobo, akunda uwo mwigeme kandi aguma avugisha nya mwigeme amuryosharyosha. 4 Amaherezo Shekemu abwira Hamori se wiwe ati: “Nsabira uno mukobwa ambere umugore.”

5 Nuko Yakobo yumva ko Shekemu yari yahumanije Dina umukobwa wiwe. Kandi abahungu biwe bari kumwe n’ubusho bwiwe mu gahinga; Yakobo aguma acereje gushika baje. 6 Mu nyuma Hamori se wa Shekemu arasohoka, aja aho Yakobo ari kuvugana na we. 7 Bene Yakobo bakimara kuvyumva, baraza bavuye mu gahinga; maze abo bagabo barababara, barashavura cane, kuko yari yakoreye ubusazi bw’agaterasoni Isirayeli mu kuryamana n’umukobwa wa Yakobo, kandi ata kintu nk’ico gikwiye kugirwa.

8 Nuko Hamori avugana na bo ati: “Shekemu umuhungu wanje, umutima wiwe ufatanye n’umukobwa wanyu. Ndabinginze mumumuhe amubere umugore, 9 kandi mwunge natwe ubucuti bw’ukwabirana. Muzoduhe abakobwa banyu, namwe muzokwitwarire abakobwa bacu. 10 Muzoshobora kubana natwe, kandi igihugu kizoba kiri imbere yanyu. Nimukibemwo mwongere mugikoreremwo urudandaza kandi mukigereremwo.” 11 Maze Shekemu abwira se w’uwo mukobwa na basaza biwe ati: “Ningire ubutoni mu maso yanyu, kandi ico mumbwira cose ndagitanga. 12 Nimuduze cane amahera y’inkwano be n’ingabirano nsabwa, nanje nditeguriye gutanga ivyo mwombwira vyose; mumpe gusa uwo mwigeme ambere umugore.”

13 Nuko bene Yakobo bishurana uruhendo Shekemu na Hamori se wiwe, bavuga gutyo kuko yari yahumanije Dina mushiki wabo. 14 Nuko bababwira bati: “Ntitwoshobora gukora ikintu nk’ico, ngo duhe mushiki wacu umugabo atagenyerewe, kuko ico ari iceyi kuri twebwe. 15 Nimwemera iki kintu ni ho gusa dushobora kubemerera: mube nkatwe, uw’igitsina-gabo wese muri mwebwe agenyerwe. 16 Tuzoheza tubahe abakobwa bacu, n’abakobwa banyu tubatware, kandi tuzobana namwe, tube igisata kimwe. 17 Mugabo nimutatwumviriza ngo mugenyerwe, tuzotora umukobwa wacu twigire.”

18 Nuko amajambo yabo aboneka ko ari meza mu maso ya Hamori no mu maso ya Shekemu, umuhungu wa Hamori, 19 maze uwo musore ntiyateba gushitsa ico gisabwa, kuko yahimbarwa koko n’umukobwa wa Yakobo, akaba kandi yarubahwa kuruta abandi bose bo mu nzu yose ya se.

20 Nuko Hamori na Shekemu umuhungu wiwe baja kw’irembo ry’igisagara cabo maze batangura kubwira abagabo bo mu gisagara cabo bati: 21 “Aba bagabo badushakira amahoro. Ku bw’ivyo nibabe mu gihugu kandi bagikoreremwo urudandaza, kuko igihugu cagutse rwose imbere yabo. Abakobwa babo turashobora kubatwara bakaba abagore bacu, abakobwa bacu bwite na bo tugashobora kubabaha. 22 Twemeye iki kintu ni ho gusa abo bagabo bazotwemerera kubana natwe kugira ducike igisata kimwe: uw’igitsina-gabo wese wo muri twebwe agenyerwe nk’uko bagenyerewe. 23 Ibintu vyabo n’ubutunzi bwabo n’ibitungwa vyabo vyose, ntibizoca none biba ivyacu? Gusa tubemerere, kugira babane natwe.” 24 Nuko abasohoka bose baciye mw’irembo ry’igisagara ciwe bumviriza Hamori na Shekemu umuhungu wiwe, maze ab’igitsina-gabo bose baragenyerwa, abasohoka bose baciye mw’irembo ry’igisagara ciwe.

25 Ariko ku musi ugira gatatu, bacumva ububabare, ba bahungu babiri ba Yakobo, Simeyoni na Lewi, basaza ba Dina, umwumwe wese muri bo afata inkota yiwe, bagenda mu gisagara ata wubicura, batangura kwica uw’igitsina-gabo wese. 26 Bicisha ubugi bw’inkota Hamori na Shekemu umuhungu wiwe. Maze batora Dina mu nzu ya Shekemu, barasohoka. 27 Abandi bahungu ba Yakobo batera abagabo bari bakomerekejwe ibikomere vyica baraheza baja mu vyo gusahura nya gisagara, kuko bari bahumanije mushiki wabo. 28 Imikuku yabo n’amasho yabo n’indogobwa zabo n’ivyari mu gisagara n’ivyari mu gahinga baratwara. 29 Ivyababeshaho vyose n’abana babo batoyi bose n’abagore babo babitwara ari inyagano, ku buryo basahura ivyari mu mazu vyose.

30 Nuko Yakobo abwira Simeyoni na Lewi ati: “Mwankwegeye gukumirwa mu kungira akanuka ku baba mu gihugu, ku Banyakanani n’Abaperizi; ko ndi imparurwa, ntibazobura kunkoranira bakantera, nkazimanganywa koko, jewe n’inzu yanje.” 31 Na bo bavuga bati: “Mbega mushiki wacu yari akwiye gufatwa nk’umumaraya?”
