Itanguriro  43 : 1 - 34

43 Kandi ikigoyi cari gikaze mu gihugu. 2 Bakimara kurangiza kurya intete bari barakuye mu Misiri, se ababwira ati: “Subireyo, mutugurire ibifungurwa bikeyi.” 3 Mugabo Yuda amubwira ati: “Nya mugabo yaratwihanikirije bimwe bikomeye ati: ‘Ntimuze musubire kubona mu maso hanje mwene wanyu atari kumwe namwe.’ 4 Niwarungika mwene wacu tukajana, turiteguriye kumanuka kukugurira ibifungurwa. 5 Mugabo nutamurungika, ntituzomanuka, kuko nya mugabo yatubwiye koko ati: ‘Ntimuze musubire kubona mu maso hanje mwene wanyu atari kumwe namwe.’” 6 Isirayeli avuga ati: “Kubera iki mwangiriye nabi mu kubwira nya mugabo ko mufise uwundi mwene wanyu?” 7 Na bo bavuga bati: “Nya mugabo yarabajije adacisha ku ruhande ibitwerekeye be n’ivyerekeye incuti zacu ati: ‘So aracariho? Hari uwundi mwene wanyu mufise?’ Natwe tubimubwira nk’uko biri. Twari kumenya gute koko yuko yovuze ati: ‘Nimumanukane mwene wanyu’?”

8 Amaherezo Yuda abwira Isirayeli se wiwe ati: “Ndungikana n’uwo muhungu, kugira duhaguruke tugende, tubone kuguma turi bazima ntidupfe, twe nawe n’abana bacu batoyi. 9 Ni je nzomwishinga. Nze ndamuhanirwe. Nintamukuzanira ngo ndamugushikirize, nzoba rero ngucumuyeko ibihe vyose. 10 Mugabo iyo tudahinahina, ubu twari kuba twagiye tukongera tukagaruka incuro zibiri.”

11 Nuko Isirayeli se ababwira ati: “Nimba rero ari ukwo biri, nimukore ibi: Nimutware ibintu vyiza kuruta ibindi vyo mu gihugu mu vyo mutekeramwo, mubimanukanire nya mugabo bwa ngabirano: umusomozo mukeyi n’ubuki bukeyi, ibirirarira bimota neza vy’ibimera* n’ibishishwa vy’uburembo, imise y’ikungu na shakedi*. 12 Vyongeye, mwitwaze amahera agwijwe kabiri; amahera yasubijwe mu minwa y’amasaho yanyu muzoyasubizeyo. Kumbure kwabaye ukwihenda. 13 Mutore mwene wanyu maze muhaguruke, musubire kwa nya mugabo. 14 Kandi Imana Mushoboravyose ibahe kugirirwa ikigongwe imbere ya nya mugabo, kugira abarekurire mwene wanyu wundi na Benyamini. Nanje nimba ari ngombwa ko mpekurwa, mpekurwe nta kundi!”

15 Nuko nya bagabo batora iyo ngabirano, bongera batora amahera agwijwe kabiri be na Benyamini. Maze barahaguruka, bamanuka mu Misiri, amaherezo bahagarara imbere ya Yozefu. 16 Yozefu abonye Benyamini ari kumwe na bo, aca abwira umuntu yari ajejwe inzu yiwe ati: “Jana abo bagabo i muhira uheze ukerere ibitungwa maze utegure, kuko abo bagabo baza gusangira nanje ku mutaga.” 17 Buno nyene nya mugabo akora nk’uko Yozefu yari avuze. Uwo mugabo ajana rero abo bagabo i muhira kwa Yozefu. 18 Mugabo nya bagabo bagira ubwoba kuko bari bajanywe i muhira kwa Yozefu, maze batangura kuvuga bati: “Amahera twasubiranye inyuma mu masaho yacu mu ntango ni yo atumye tuzanwa ng’aha ngo badusimbireko, badusǔmire baheze badufate nk’abashumba be n’indogobwa zacu!”

19 Ku bw’ivyo begera nya mugabo yari ajejwe inzu ya Yozefu maze bavuganira na we ku bwinjiriro bw’inzu, 20 bati: “Uradutunga mukama wanje! Twaramanutse koko mu ntango kugura ibifungurwa. 21 Mugabo dushitse ahantu h’uburaro tugatangura kwugurura amasaho yacu, twagiye tubona amahera y’umwumwe wese ari mu munwa w’isaho yiwe, amahera yacu yose uko yangana. Twagomba rero kuyasubiza n’amaboko yacu bwite. 22 Kandi twamanukanye ayandi mahera yo kugura ibifungurwa. Ntituzi vy’ukuri uwashize amahera yacu mu masaho yacu.” 23 Maze avuga ati: “Mwebwe nta ngorane. Ntimutinye. Imana yanyu ikaba n’Imana ya so yashize itunga mu masaho yanyu. Amahera yanyu yabanje kuza kuri jewe.” Inyuma y’ivyo asohora Simeyoni amuzana iyo bari.

24 Maze uwo mugabo azana nya bagabo mu nzu ya Yozefu hanyuma atanga amazi kugira bozwe ibirenge, yongera atanga indya z’ibitungwa ku bw’indogobwa zabo. 25 Nuko bategura ya ngabirano mu kurindira ko Yozefu aza ku mutaga, kuko bari bumvise yuko aho ari ho baza kurira umukate. 26 Yozefu yinjiye mu nzu, bamuzanira rero mu nzu nya ngabirano bari bazanye, maze bakubita inkoro hasi imbere yiwe. 27 Inyuma y’ivyo ababaza nimba bisohora maze avuga ati: “Mbega so, wa mutama mwavuga, arisohora? Aracariho?” 28 Na bo bati: “Data umusavyi wawe arisohora. Aracariho.” Maze barunama, bakubita inkoro hasi.

29 Araramuye amaso akabona Benyamini mwene wabo, umuhungu wa nyina, aca avuga ati: “Uyu ni mwene wanyu, umwe w’umuhererezi mwambwira?” Yongerako ati: “Imana ikugirire ubutoni, mwananje.” 30 Maze Yozefu, kubera yari arengewe n’ikigumbagumba afitiye mwene wabo, arihuta arondera aho aririra, aja mu cumba, aririrayo. 31 Inyuma y’ivyo yiyoga mu maso hanyuma arasohoka, arigumya avuga ati: “Nimuzane ibifungurwa.” 32 Bamuzanira ivyiwe ukwiwe, n’ivyabo ukwabo, n’ivy’Abanyamisiri basangira na we ukwabo; kuko Abanyamisiri batashobora gusangira imfungurwa n’Abaheburayo, kubera ko ico ari ikintu giteye ishishi ku Banyamisiri.

33 Kandi bicarikwa imbere yiwe, imfura yicara hisunzwe uburenganzira bwayo bw’imfura, n’umuhererezi yicara hisunzwe ubuto bwiwe; kandi nya bagabo baguma barabana bumiwe. 34 Nuko aguma atuma barungikirwa imigabane ivuye imbere yiwe, mugabo akongereza umugabane wa Benyamini incuro zitanu kuruta uko imigabane y’abandi bose ingana. Babandanya kwihereza inzimano no kunywa bari kumwe na we, barahaga.

^ Ita. 43:11 Ijambo ku rindi ni “labudano.”

^ Ita. 43:11 Shakedi ni ubwoko bw’utwamwa tugomba kumera nk’ivyema.
