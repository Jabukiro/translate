Guharūra  12 : 1 - 16

12 Nuko Miriyamu na Aroni batangura kuvuga nabi Musa kubera umugore w’Umukushikazi yari yabiriye, kuko yari yabiriye umugore w’Umukushikazi. 2 Baguma bavuga bati: “Mbega Yehova yavuze biciye kuri Musa gusa? Ntiyavuze none biciye no kuri twebwe?” Kandi Yehova yariko yumviriza. 3 Kandi uwo mugabo Musa yari uwutekereza kuruta kure n’iyo abantu bose bari ku buringanire bw’ubutaka.

4 Maze ako kanya Yehova abwira Musa na Aroni na Miriyamu ati: “Nimusohoke uko muri batatu, muje kw’ihema ry’ihuriro.” Nuko uko ari batatu barasohoka. 5 Inyuma y’ivyo Yehova amanuka mu nkingi y’igicu, ahagarara ku bwinjiriro bw’ihema maze ahamagara Aroni na Miriyamu. Bompi baca barasohoka. 6 Nuko abandanya kuvuga ati: “Ndabinginze mwumve amajambo yanje. Iyo muri mwebwe haba hari umuhanuzi wa Yehova, nari kumwimenyesha mw’iyerekwa. Novuganye na we mu ndoto. 7 Si ukwo biri ku musavyi wanje Musa! Azezwa inzu yanje yose. 8 Mvugana na we akanwa ku kandi, nkamwereka ibintu, ntakoresheje ubupfindo; kandi ishusho ya Yehova ni yo yitegereza. Kubera iki none mutatinye kuvuga nabi umusavyi wanje, kuvuga nabi Musa?”

9 Nuko ishavu rya Yehova rirabarurumbira, maze arigira. 10 Nya gicu kiva hejuru y’ihema, kandi ehe Miriyamu yari yatejwe imibembe yera derere*. Maze Aroni ahindukira kuraba Miriyamu, kandi ehe, yari yatejwe imibembe. 11 Buno nyene Aroni abwira Musa ati: “Ntunga mukama wanje! Ndakwinginze ntuduharureko igicumuro twakoranye ubupfu, egome, igicumuro twakoze! 12 Ndakwinginze ntureke ngo agume ameze nk’uwapfuye avuye mu gitereko ca nyina inyama ziwe ziriko zirabora!” 13 Nuko Musa atangura gutakambira Yehova ati: “Ewe Mana, ndakwinginze! Mukize, ndakwinginze!”

14 Maze Yehova abwira Musa ati: “Hamwe se wiwe yomucira mu maso, ntiyoteterezwa imisi indwi? Nakumirwe imisi indwi inyuma y’ikambi, mu nyuma abone kugaruka.” 15 Miriyamu akumirirwa rero inyuma y’ikambi imisi indwi, maze abantu ntibagenda gushika Miriyamu yakiriwe. 16 Mu nyuma abantu baragenda bavuye i Hazeroti, bakambika mu gahinga ka Parani.

^ Guh. 12:10 Canke, “yera nka shelegi.” “Shelegi” ni ibintu vy’ibivunjivunji vyera cane bigwa nk’imvura.
