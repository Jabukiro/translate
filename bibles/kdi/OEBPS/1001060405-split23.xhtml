Guharūra  23 : 1 - 30

23 Maze Balamu abwira Balaki ati: “Nyubakira aha hantu ibicaniro indwi wongere untegurire aha nyene amashūri indwi n’impfizi z’intama indwi.” 2 Balaki aca akora nk’uko Balamu avuze. Inyuma y’ivyo, Balaki na Balamu bashikana ishūri n’impfizi y’intama ku gicaniro kimwekimwe cose. 3 Balamu abandanya kubwira Balaki ati: “Hagarara iruhande y’ishikanwa ryawe riturirwa, hanyuma undeke ngende. Hari aho Yehova yobonana nanje, agahura nanje. Ico ari bunyereke cose rero, simbura kukikubwira.” Nuko aja ku murambi ugaragara.

4 Imana ibonanye na Balamu, ayibwira ati: “Nashinze vya bicaniro indwi ndabitondetse ku misitari, maze nshikana ishūri n’impfizi y’intama ku gicaniro kimwekimwe cose.” 5 Yehova rero ashira ijambo mu kanwa ka Balamu, avuga ati: “Subira kuri Balaki, maze uku abe ari ko uvuga.” 6 Nuko asubira kuri we, kandi ehe we n’abaganwa bose ba Mowabu bari bahagaze iruhande y’ishikanwa riturirwa ryiwe. 7 Maze avuga ijambo ryiwe ry’akagani ati:

“Balaki umwami wa Mowabu yagerageje kundongōra ankuye i Aramu,

Ankuye mu misozi yo mu buseruko, ambwira ati:

‘Nuze umvumire Yakobo.

Egome, nuze usibe Isirayeli.’

 8 Mbega noshobora nte kuvuma abo Imana itavumye?

Kandi noshobora nte gusiba abo Yehova atasivye?

 9 Kuko ndababona ndi kw’isonga ry’ibitandara,

Nkabitegereza ndi ku mirambi.

Uko bagize igisata, baguma bacumbitse hariya bari ukwa bonyene,

Kandi ntibiharura mu mahanga.

10 Ni nde yaharūye udutete tw’umukungugu twa Yakobo,

Kandi ni nde yaharūye ica kane ca Isirayeli?

Nimpfe urupfu rw’abagororotsi,

Iherezo ryanje na ryo mu nyuma ribe nk’iryabo.”

11 Nuko Balaki abwira Balamu ati: “Unkoreye ibiki? Nakuzanye ngo uvume abansi banje, none ehe wabahezagiye uherezako.” 12 Na we arishura ati: “Mbega ico Yehova ashize mu kanwa kanje cose si co nkwiye kwitwararika kuvuga?”

13 Maze Balaki amubwira ati: “Ndakwinginze uze tujane ahandi hantu ushobora kubabona. Uza kubona gusa igice cabo co ku mpera; ntuza kubabona bose. Uheze ubamvumire uri ng’aho.” 14 Nuko amujana mu gahinga ka Zofimu, kw’isonga rya Pisiga, maze atangura kwubaka ibicaniro indwi no gushikana ishūri n’impfizi y’intama ku gicaniro kimwekimwe cose. 15 Inyuma y’ivyo abwira Balaki ati: “Hagarara ng’aha iruhande y’ishikanwa ryawe riturirwa, nanje mbonanire na we hariya.” 16 Mu nyuma Yehova abonana na Balamu maze ashira ijambo mu kanwa kiwe, avuga ati: “Subira kuri Balaki, kandi uku abe ari ko umubwira.” 17 Nuko aza kuri we, kandi ehe yari ahagaze iruhande y’ishikanwa riturirwa ryiwe, ari kumwe n’abaganwa ba Mowabu bose. Maze Balaki amubwira ati: “Yehova yavuze iki?” 18 Na we avuga ijambo ryiwe ry’akagani ati:

“Haguruka Balaki, wumvirize.

Ntega ugutwi, ewe mwene Zipori.

19 Imana si umuntu ngo ibeshe,

Canke umwana w’abantu ngo yicuze.

Mbega ico ubwayo ivuze, ntigikora?

Mbega iryo ivumereye, ntirishitsa?

20 Ehe nazanywe kugira mpezagire,

Na yo yahezagiye, kandi sindi bubisubize inyuma.

21 Ntiyitegereje ububasha bw’ivy’amageza na bumwe bwo kugirira nabi Yakobo,

Kandi nta vyago yabonye kuri Isirayeli.

Yehova Imana yiwe ari kumwe na we,

Kandi muri we hasamirana impundu y’umwami.

22 Imana ibakuye mu Misiri.

Afise ubunyarutsi nk’ubw’ishūri y’inyeshamba.

23 Kuko ata mutongero mubi uhari wo kugirira nabi Yakobo,

Canke ukuragura na kumwe kwo kugirira nabi Isirayeli.

Ubu vyovugwa ku bijanye na Yakobo na Isirayeli ngo:

‘Ico Imana yakoze!’

24 Raba, igisata c’abantu kizohaguruka nk’intambwe,

Ciduze nk’intambwe.

Ntikizoryama kitararya ico cacakiye,

Kandi kizonywa amaraso y’abagandaguwe.”

25 Nuko Balaki abwira Balamu ati: “Nimba udashoboye kumuvuma, pfuma rero ureka no kumuhezagira.” 26 Balamu na we yishura Balaki ati: “Sinakubwiye none nti: ‘Ivyo Yehova aza kumbwira vyose ni vyo nza gukora’?”

27 Maze Balaki abwira Balamu ati: “Enda nuze, ndakwinginze, nongere ndakujane ahandi hantu! Hari aho vyoba ibibereye mu maso y’Imana y’ukuri, ku buryo utabura kumumvumira uri ng’aho.” 28 Nuko Balaki ajana Balamu kw’isonga rya Peyori riraba i Yeshimoni. 29 Maze Balamu abwira Balaki ati: “Nyubakira aha hantu ibicaniro indwi wongere untegurire aha nyene amashūri indwi n’impfizi z’intama indwi.” 30 Balaki rero akora nk’uko Balamu avuze, ashikana ishūri n’impfizi y’intama ku gicaniro kimwekimwe cose.
