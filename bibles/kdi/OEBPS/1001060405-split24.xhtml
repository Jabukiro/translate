Guharūra  24 : 1 - 25

24 Balamu abonye ko vyari vyiza mu maso ya Yehova guhezagira Isirayeli, ntiyagenda, nko ku zindi ncuro, kurondera ibimenyetso bibi na bimwe bitara ibizoba, mugabo yerekeza mu maso hiwe mu gahinga. 2 Balamu araramuye amaso akabona Isirayeli acumbitse akurikije imiryango yiwe, impwemu y’Imana ica iza kuri we. 3 Maze avuga ijambo ryiwe ry’akagani ati:

“Ijambo rya Balamu mwene Beyori,

Be n’ijambo ry’umuntu w’umugabo afise ijisho rikanuye,

 4 Ijambo ry’uwumva amajambo y’Imana,

Uwabonye iyerekwa rya Mushoboravyose

Igihe yagwa, amaso yiwe akanuye:

 5 Ese ukuntu amahema yawe asa neza, ewe Yakobo, amataberenakulo yawe, ewe Isirayeli!

 6 Ashika kure nk’imyonga,

Nk’imirima iri iruhande y’uruzi.

Nk’ibiterwa vy’imibazibazi Yehova yateye,

Nk’amasederi ari iruhande y’amazi.

 7 Amazi aguma aseseka avuye mu ndobo ziwe zibiri z’inshato,

Kandi imbuto yiwe iri iruhande y’amazi menshi.

Eka umwami wiwe azoba hejuru kuruta Agagi,

Kandi ubwami bwiwe buzoshirwa hejuru.

 8 Imana imukuye mu Misiri;

Afise ubunyarutsi bw’ishūri y’inyeshamba.

Azoyigiza amahanga, abamuhahaza

Amagufa yabo azoyahekenya, kandi azobamenaguza imyampi yiwe.

 9 Yarunamye, yararyamye nk’intambwe,

Kandi, nk’intambwe, ni nde yubahuka kumuvyura?

Abaguhezagira ni abahezagiwe,

Abakuvuma na bo ni ibivume.”

10 Ishavu rya Balaki rica rirurumbira Balamu, akubita amashi ku yandi, hanyuma Balaki abandanya kubwira Balamu ati: “Naguhamagaye ngo uvume abansi banje, none ehe wabahezagiye uherezako izi ncuro zitatu zose. 11 Ubu rero iruka uje iwawe. Nari nibwiye yuko ntobuze kugutera iteka, mugabo ehe Yehova atumye udaterwa iteka.”

12 Balamu na we abwira Balaki ati: “Mbega sinabwiye n’intumwa zawe wantumyeko nti: 13 ‘Naho Balaki yompa inzu yiwe yuzuye ifeza n’inzahabu, sinoshobora kurenga itegeko rya Yehova ngo ngire ico nkora, caba ciza canke kibi kivuye mu mutima wanje bwite; ico Yehova ari buvuge cose ni co nzovuga’? 14 Ubu rero, ehe ngiye ku bantu banje. Enda nuze, ndakugire inama y’ivyo aba bantu bazokorera abantu bawe mu nyuma, kw’iherezo ry’imisi.” 15 Nuko avuga ijambo ryiwe ry’akagani ati:

“Ijambo rya Balamu mwene Beyori,

Ijambo ry’umuntu w’umugabo afise ijisho rikanuye,

16 Ijambo ry’uwumva amajambo y’Imana,

N’uwuzi ubumenyi bwa Musumbavyose . . .

Yarabonye iyerekwa rya Mushoboravyose

Igihe yagwa, amaso yiwe akanuye:

17 Nzomubona, mugabo si ubu;

Nzomwitegereza, mugabo si hafi.

Inyenyeri izova muri Yakobo koko,

Kandi agakoni k’ubutware kazohaguruka koko kavuye muri Isirayeli.

Kandi azomenagura imisaya y’umutwe wa Mowabu

N’agahanga k’abahungu bose b’uruhagarara rw’intambara.

18 Kandi Edomu azokwigarurirwa,

Egome, Seyiri azokwigarurirwa n’abansi biwe,

Mu gihe Isirayeli azokwerekana umutima rugabo wiwe.

19 Muri Yakobo hazova uwuzogenda kuganza,

Kandi azorandura uwarokotse wese mu gisagara.”

20 Abonye Amaleki, ashishikara ijambo ryiwe ry’akagani ati:

“Amaleki yari uwa mbere mu mahanga,

Mugabo iherezo ryiwe mu nyuma rizoba uguhona.”

21 Abonye Abanyakeni, ashishikara ijambo ryiwe ry’akagani ati:

“Aho uba hararamba, kandi ikibanza ubamwo kiri ku rutare.

22 Mugabo hazoba uwo gutongora Kayine.

Hazoca igihe kingana iki imbere y’uko Ashuri agutwara uri imbohe?”

23 Nuko ashishikara ijambo ryiwe ry’akagani ati:

“Mbega amagorwa! Ni nde azorokoka Imana niyatuma biba?

24 Hazoba imeri zivuye ku nkengera ya Kitimu;

Kandi ata gukeka zizoteza amarushwa Ashuri,

Kandi zizoteza koko amarushwa Eberi.

Mugabo na we nyene uburuhiro azoyogera.”

25 Inyuma y’ivyo Balamu arahaguruka aragenda, asubira iwe. Balaki na we nyene arigira.
