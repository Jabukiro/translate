Guharūra  30 : 1 - 16

30 Maze Musa abwira abakuru b’imiryango ya bene Isirayeli ati: “Iri ni ryo jambo Yehova ategetse: 2 Mu gihe umugabo agize indagano kuri Yehova canke arahiye indahiro kugira yibohereko indagano y’ukwiyima, ntaze arenge kw’ijambo ryiwe. Nk’uko ivyasohotse mu kanwa kiwe vyose biri ni ko azokora.

3 “Mu gihe umukobwa agize indagano kuri Yehova canke yibohesheje koko indagano y’ukwiyima mu nzu ya se mu buto bwiwe, 4 hanyuma se wiwe akumva vy’ukuri indagano yiwe canke indagano yiwe y’ukwiyima yiboheyeko maze se wiwe agacereza ku bimwerekeye, indagano ziwe zose rero zizomugumako, kandi indagano y’ukwiyima yose yaboheye ku buzima bwiwe izogumaho. 5 Mugabo nimba se wiwe yamubujije ku musi yumviyeko indagano ziwe zose canke indagano ziwe z’ukwiyima yiboheyeko, ntizizogumaho, mugabo Yehova azomubabarira, kuko se wiwe yamubujije.

6 “Ariko aramutse yabiriwe, kandi indagano yiwe ikaba iri kuri we canke umuhango w’iminwa yiwe utaranga ukwiyumvira yiboheyeko, 7 maze umugabo wiwe akavyumva vy’ukuri hanyuma agacereza ku bimwerekeye ku musi avyumviyeko, indagano ziwe rero zizogumaho, canke indagano ziwe z’ukwiyima yiboheyeko zizogumaho. 8 Ariko nimba umugabo wiwe ku musi avyumviyeko amubujije, azoba rero akuyeho indagano yari kuri we canke umuhango w’iminwa yiwe utaranga ukwiyumvira yiboheyeko, kandi Yehova azomubabarira.

9 “Ku vy’indagano y’umupfakazi canke y’umugore yahukanye, ikintu cose yiboheyeko kizoguma kimuboshe.

10 “Ariko nimba mu nzu y’umugabo wiwe ari ho yaraganiye canke yiboheyeko indagano y’ukwiyima biciye ku ndahiro, 11 maze umugabo wiwe akaba yavyumvise hanyuma akaba yacereje ku bimwerekeye, ntazoba yamubujije; indagano ziwe zose zizogumaho, canke indagano y’ukwiyima iyo ari yo yose yiboheyeko izogumaho. 12 Ariko nimba umugabo wiwe yazikuyeho buhere ku musi yumviyeko ijambo iryo ari ryo ryose iminwa yiwe yavuze bwa ndagano ziwe canke bwa ndagano yiwe y’ukwiyima, ntizizogumaho. Umugabo wiwe azoba yazikuyeho, kandi Yehova azomubabarira. 13 Indagano iyo ari yo yose canke indahiro iyo ari yo yose y’indagano y’ukwiyima ku bwo kwibabaza, umugabo wiwe ni we akwiye kuyishinga canke kuyishingura. 14 Ariko nimba umugabo wiwe acereje rwose ku bimwerekeye ku musi ku musi, nya mugore azoba rero yashinze indagano ziwe zose canke indagano ziwe zose z’ukwiyima zizoba ziri kuri we. Azoba yazishinze, kuko yacereje ku bimwerekeye ku musi yazumviyeko. 15 Nimba na ho azikuyeho buhere amaze kuzumva, azoba rero yikoreye ikosa rya nya mugore.

16 “Izo ni zo ngingo nyobozi Yehova yategetse Musa hagati y’umugabo n’umugore wiwe, hagati y’umukobwa na se mu buto bwa nya mukobwa mu nzu ya se.”
