Gusubira mu vyagezwe  18 : 1 - 22

18 “Nta muturi canke intoranwa muri Isirayeli bikwiye kwegukira abaherezi, Abalewi, umuryango wose wa Lewi. Amashikanwa ashikanishwa umuriro ya Yehova, mbere intoranwa yiwe, ni yo bakwiye kurya. 2 Nta ntoranwa rero ikwiye kumwegukira hagati ya bene wabo. Yehova ni we ntoranwa yiwe, nk’uko yamubwiye.

3 “Ibi rero ni vyo bikwiye kuguma ari uburenganzira bw’abaherezi bivuye ku bantu, bivuye ku bashikana ikimazi c’imbagwa, yaba ishūri canke intama: Umuntu ategerezwa guha umuherezi ibega n’imibangabanga n’umushishito. 4 Ivya mbere ku ntete zawe, ku muvinyu wawe mushasha no ku mavuta yawe n’ubwoya bwa mbere ku bukemuwe ku mukuku wawe, ukwiye kubimuha. 5 Kuko ari we Yehova Imana yawe yatoye mu miryango yawe yose ngo ahagarare akore igikorwa mw’izina rya Yehova, we hamwe n’abahungu biwe, nantaryo.

6 “Kandi Umulewi niyasohoka hanze ya kimwe mu bisagara vya Isirayeli yose, aho yari amaze igihe aba, maze akaza azanywe n’ikintu ico ari co cose umutima wiwe uhahamiye ahantu Yehova azotora, 7 ategerezwa rero gukora igikorwa mw’izina rya Yehova Imana yawe cokimwe na bene wabo bose, Abalewi, bahagaze ng’aho imbere ya Yehova. 8 Umuturi ungana n’uw’abandi ni wo akwiye kurya, iruhande y’ivyo aronka bivuye ku vyo agurishije ku bintu vya ba sekuruza.

9 “Niwamara kwinjira mu gihugu Yehova Imana yawe agira aguhe, ntuze wige gukora wisunze ibintu biteye ishishi vy’ayo mahanga. 10 Muri wewe ntihaze haboneke umuntu acisha umuhungu wiwe canke umukobwa wiwe mu muriro, uwuragura, umunyamageza canke umuntu arondera ibimenyetso bitara ibizoba canke umupfumu, 11 canke uwuzirikisha abandi ubupfumu canke umuntu ahanuza uwuvugana n’ibiremwa vy’impwemu canke uwagize umwuga ukubura ibizoba canke umuntu asiguza abapfuye. 12 Kuko uwukora ivyo bintu wese ari ikintu giteye ishishi kuri Yehova, kandi ivyo bintu biteye ishishi akaba ari vyo bitumye Yehova Imana yawe abirukana bakava imbere yawe. 13 Ukwiye kwerekana ko utagira amahinyu kuri Yehova Imana yawe.

14 “Kuko ayo mahanga uriko uromora yahora yumviriza abagira ivy’amageza n’abaragura; ariko weho, Yehova Imana yawe nta kintu na kimwe yaguhaye kimeze nk’ico. 15 Umuhanuzi avuye hagati yawe, avuye muri bene wanyu, nka jewe, ni we Yehova Imana yawe azoguhagurukiriza, ari na we mukwiye kwumviriza, 16 bwa nyishu ku vyo wasavye Yehova Imana yawe vyose i Horebu kuri wa musi ishengero ryari rikoranye, uti: ‘Sinsubire kwumva ijwi rya Yehova Imana yanje, n’uyu muriro ukomeye sinongere kuwubona, kugira simpfe.’ 17 Kuri ivyo Yehova ambwira ati: ‘Bagize neza mu kuvuga batyo. 18 Umuhanuzi ameze nka wewe ni we nzobahagurukiriza hagati ya bene wabo; kandi nzoshira amajambo yanje mu kanwa kiwe, na we azobabwira ivyo nzomutegeka vyose. 19 Kandi umuntu atazokwumviriza amajambo yanje uno azovuga mw’izina ryanje, jewe ubwanje nzobimubaza.

20 “‘Ariko umuhanuzi yihaye kuvuga mw’izina ryanje ijambo ntamutegetse kuvuga canke akavuga mw’izina ry’izindi mana, uyo muhanuzi ategerezwa gupfa. 21 Niwibwira na ho mu mutima wawe uti: “Tuzomenya gute ijambo Yehova atavuze?” 22 Nya muhanuzi niyavuga mw’izina rya Yehova hanyuma nya jambo ntiribe canke ngo riranguke, iryo ni ryo jambo Yehova atazoba yavuze. Nya muhanuzi azoba yarivuganye ukwita imbere. Ntaze agutere ubwoba.’
