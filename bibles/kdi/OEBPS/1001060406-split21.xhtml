Gusubira mu vyagezwe  21 : 1 - 23

21 “Nihagira umuntu basanga yagandaguwe ku butaka Yehova Imana yawe agira aguhe ngo ubwigarurire, arambaraye mu gahinga, kandi uwamwishe akaba atamenyekanye, 2 abagabo b’inararibonye b’iwanyu n’abacamanza b’iwanyu baze bagende bapime gushika ku bisagara bikikuje uwo yagandaguwe: 3 icagirwa n’ikosa kizoba ari igisagara kirushirije kuba hafi y’uwo yagandaguwe. Abagabo b’inararibonye b’ico gisagara baze batore ishashi yo mu busho itarakoreshwa, itarakwega ingǒgo*; 4 abagabo b’inararibonye b’ico gisagara baze bamanukane iyo shashi mu mwonga utemba amazi aho badasanzwe barima canke ngo babibe imbuto, baheze bavunire izosi ry’iyo shashi ng’aho muri nya mwonga.

5 “Abaherezi, bene Lewi, baze begere, kuko ari bo Yehova Imana yawe yatoye ngo bamukorere bongere bahezagire mw’izina rya Yehova, kandi biciye ku kanwa kabo akaba ari ho amatati yose ajanye n’igikorwa cose c’ubukazi akwiye kudadanurwa. 6 Maze abagabo b’inararibonye bose b’ico gisagara barushirije kuba hafi y’uwo yagandaguwe baze bakarabire amaboko yabo hejuru ya ya shashi, imwe izosi ryayo rizoba ryavuniwe mu mwonga; 7 kandi baze bishure bati: ‘Amaboko yacu si yo yasheshe ayo maraso, eka n’amaso yacu ntiyabonye ingene aseswa. 8 Ntuyaharure ku bantu bawe Isirayeli, abo wacunguye, ewe Yehova, kandi ntushire amaraso y’intungane hagati y’igisata cawe Isirayeli.’ Kandi ayo maraso ntazoharurwa kuri bo. 9 Wewe rero, uzokura hagati yawe icaha c’ukwagirwa n’amaraso y’intungane, kuko uzokora ibigororotse mu maso ya Yehova.

10 “Niwaja ku rugamba kurwana n’abansi bawe, kandi Yehova Imana yawe akaba yabagabuye mu kuboko kwawe, nawe ukabatwara ari imbohe, 11 maze muri izo mbohe ukabonamwo umugore mwiza ku mubiri, hanyuma ukamugomwa, ukamutwara ngo abe umugore wawe, 12 uze umuzane rero mu nzu yawe. Aze yimoshe umutwe yongere atunganye inzara ziwe, 13 yambure umutamana yajemwo ari imbohe aheze abe mu nzu yawe, aririre se na nyina ukwezi* kwose. Inyuma y’ivyo uze uryamane na we, umutware akubere umugeni, abe umugore wawe. 14 Niwabona na ho ataguhimbaye, uze umurungike, uko umutima wiwe ugomba; mugabo ntuze umugurishe amahera. Ntuze umucuranguze umaze kumutetereza.

15 “Umugabo niyagira abagore babiri, umwe akundwa uwundi yankwa, maze bompi, uwukundwa be n’uwankwa, bakamuvyarira abahungu, umuhungu w’imfura akaba uw’uyo yankwa, 16 ku musi rero azoha abahungu biwe intoranwa y’ivyo afise, ntazokwemererwa gufata umuhungu w’umugore akundwa ngo amugire imfura ngo arenganye umuhungu w’umwe yankwa, kandi ari we mfura. 17 Kuko akwiye kwemera ko imfura ari umuhungu w’umwe yankwa mu kumuha imigabane ibiri ku kintu cose afise, kuko uyo ari we buzibūra bwiwe*. Uburenganzira bw’imfura ni we bwegukira.

18 “Umuntu niyaba afise umwana w’intagondwa kandi w’umugarariji, akaba atumviriza ijwi rya se canke ijwi rya nyina, kandi bakaba baramukosoye mugabo ntabumvirize, 19 se na nyina rero baze bamufate, bamusohore bamujane ku bagabo b’inararibonye bo mu gisagara c’iwabo no kw’irembo ry’aho aba, 20 baheze babwire abagabo b’inararibonye b’igisagara c’iwabo bati: ‘Uyu mwana wacu ni intagondwa akaba n’umugarariji; ntiyumviriza ijwi ryacu, kandi ni umukunzi w’inda akaba n’imborerwa.’ 21 Maze abagabo bose bo mu gisagara c’iwabo baze bamutere amabuye, apfe. Ukwo ni ko utegerezwa gukura ikibi hagati yawe, kandi Isirayeli yose izovyumva itinye koko.

22 “Nihagira umuntu abonekamwo igicumuro gikwiriye urubanza rwo gupfa, akicwa, kandi mukaba mwamumanitse ku giti, 23 umuvyimba wiwe ntuze urare ku giti; ahubwo utegerezwa kumuhamba kuri uyo musi, kuko uwumanitswe ari ikintu kivumwe ku Mana. Ntuze uhumanye ubutaka bwawe, ubwo Yehova Imana yawe agira aguhe bwa ntoranwa.

^ Gus. 21:3 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ Gus. 21:13 Kwari ukwezi guharurirwa ku mboneko.

^ Gus. 21:17 Ijambo ku rindi ni “Intango y’ububasha bwiwe bwo kurondoka.”
