Gusubira mu vyagezwe  34 : 1 - 12

34 Maze Musa araduga avuye mu biyaya vy’ubugaragwa vy’i Mowabu aja ku musozi Nebo, kw’isonga rya Pisiga, aharabana n’i Yeriko. Hanyuma Yehova amwereka nya gihugu cose: kuva i Gileyadi gushika i Dani, 2 na Nafutali yose be n’igihugu ca Efurayimu na Manase n’igihugu cose ca Yuda gushika ku kiyaga co mu burengero, 3 na Negebu n’intara ya Yorodani, ikiyaya c’umubande c’i Yeriko, ca gisagara c’ibigazi, gushika i Zowari.

4 Maze Yehova amubwira ati: “Iki ni co gihugu narahiye Aburahamu, Izahaki na Yakobo, nti: ‘Uruvyaro* rwawe ni rwo nzogiha.’ Ndakikweretse ngo ukibone n’amaso yawe bwite, kuko utazojabuka ngo ujeyo.”

5 Inyuma y’ivyo Musa umusavyi wa Yehova apfira ng’aho mu gihugu ca Mowabu, kw’itegeko rya Yehova. 6 Hanyuma amuhamba mu mubande mu gihugu ca Mowabu imbere ya Beti-peyori, kandi nta wigeze amenya imva yiwe gushika n’uyu musi. 7 Kandi Musa yari afise imyaka ijana na mirongo ibiri igihe yapfa. Amaso yiwe ntiyari bwabone ivyijiji, n’inkomezi ziwe ntizari bwamuveko. 8 Nuko bene Isirayeli batangura kuririra Musa mu biyaya vy’ubugaragwa vy’i Mowabu imisi mirongo itatu. Amaherezo, imisi y’amarira y’ukugandarira Musa irarangira.

9 Kandi Yosuwa mwene Nuni yari yuzuye impwemu y’ubukerebutsi, kuko Musa yari yamurambitseko ikiganza. Bene Isirayeli batangura kumwumviriza, baraheza bakora nk’uko Yehova yari yategetse Musa. 10 Mugabo ntiharigera hongera guhaguruka muri Isirayeli umuhanuzi ameze nka Musa, uwo Yehova yari azi amaso mu yandi, 11 ku bijanye na vya bimenyetso vyose na vya bitangaro Yehova yamutuma gukora mu gihugu ca Misiri abikorera Farawo n’abasavyi biwe bose n’igihugu ciwe cose, 12 no ku biraba ca kiganza gikomeye cose na bwa bubasha bukomeye kandi buteye akoba Musa yakoresha mu maso ya Isirayeli yose.

^ Gus. 34:4 Ijambo ku rindi ni “urubuto.”
