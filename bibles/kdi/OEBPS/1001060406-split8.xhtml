Gusubira mu vyagezwe  8 : 1 - 20

8 “Amabwirizwa yose ndagutegetse uno musi mukwiye kuba maso mukayagumya, kugira mubandanye kubaho, kandi koko mugwire, mwinjire, mwigarurire igihugu Yehova yarahiye ba sogokuruza banyu. 2 Kandi uze wibuke inzira yose Yehova Imana yawe yagucanyemwo iyi myaka mirongo ine yose mu gahinga, kugira agucishe bugufi, ngo akugeze amenye ikiri mu mutima wawe, ko wogumije amabwirizwa yiwe canke utoyagumije. 3 Nuko agucisha bugufi, arakureka urasonza araheza akugaburira manu*, iyo wewe canke ba so wawe mutari mwaramenye. Yayikugaburiye kugira akumenyeshe yuko umuntu atabeshwaho n’umukate gusa, ahubwo ko abeshwaho n’ijambo ryose ry’akanwa ka Yehova. 4 Umutamana wawe ntiwagushiriyeko, eka n’ikirenge cawe nticavyimvye iyi myaka mirongo ine yose. 5 Kandi urazi neza n’umutima wawe bwite yuko nka kurya nyene umuntu akosora umwana wiwe, ari ko Yehova Imana yawe yariko aragukosora.

6 “Kandi utegerezwa kugumya amabwirizwa ya Yehova Imana yawe mu kugendera mu nzira ziwe no mu kumutinya. 7 Kuko Yehova Imana yawe ariko arakujana mu gihugu ciza, igihugu c’imyonga y’amazi, c’imbizi be n’inyenga z’amazi bidudubiza mu kiyaya c’umubande no mu karere k’imisozi, 8 igihugu c’ingano na sayiri* n’imizabibu n’insukoni n’amakomamanga, igihugu c’imyelayo itanga amavuta menshi kikaba n’igihugu c’ubuki, 9 igihugu aho utazorya umukate mu mukeno, aho ata co uzohaza, igihugu amabuye yaco ari icuma, mu misozi yaco na ho ukazohacukura umujumpu.

10 “Niwamara kurya ugahaga, uze uhezagire rero Yehova Imana yawe ku bwa nya gihugu ciza yaguhaye. 11 Wiyubare ntiwibagire Yehova Imana yawe ngo bitume utagumya amabwirizwa yiwe n’ingingo z’ubutungane ziwe n’ivyagezwe vyiwe ndagutegetse uno musi; 12 ngo ntuhave urya ugahaga koko, ukubaka inzu nzizanziza, ukibera muri zo, 13 ubusho bwawe n’umukuku wawe bikongerekana, n’ifeza n’inzahabu bikongerekana kuri wewe, n’ivyawe vyose bikongerekana, 14 hanyuma umutima wawe ukiduza hejuru koko, ukibagira Yehova Imana yawe, uwagukuye mu gihugu ca Misiri, mu nzu y’abashumba; 15 uwagucishije muri ka gahinga kanini kandi gateye ubwoba, karimwo inzoka z’ubumara n’utumina, karimwo n’ubutaka bugadutse, butagira amazi; uwagukuriye amazi mu gitandara c’isare; 16 uwakugaburiye manu mu gahinga, iyo ba so batari baramenye, kugira ngo agucishe bugufi no kugira ngo akugeze, akugirire neza mu misi yawe yo hanyuma; 17 maze ukavuga koko mu mutima wawe ngo: ‘Ububasha bwanje n’ubushobozi bwose bw’ukuboko kwanje bwite ni vyo vyandonkeje ubu butunzi.’ 18 Uze wibuke Yehova Imana yawe, kuko ari we aguha ububasha bwo kurondera ubutunzi; kugira ashitse isezerano ryiwe yarahiye ba sogokuruza bawe, nk’uko biri uyu musi.

19 “Niwaramuka wibagiye Yehova Imana yawe, ukagendera inyuma y’izindi mana, ukazikorera ukongera ukazunamira, nshinze intahe yo kubagiriza uno musi yuko muzoyogera ata kabuza. 20 Nka kumwe kw’amahanga Yehova ariko ararandura ngo ave imbere yanyu, ni ko muzoyogera, kuko mutazoba mwumvirije ijwi rya Yehova Imana yanyu.

^ Gus. 8:3 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ Gus. 8:8 Sayiri ni igiterwa kimeze nk’ingano.
