Yosuwa  14 : 1 - 15

14 Iyi rero ni yo ntoranwa bene Isirayeli baronse mu gihugu ca Kanani, iyo Eleyazari umuherezi na Yosuwa mwene Nuni n’abakuru ba ba se b’imiryango ya bene Isirayeli batumye batorana. 2 Intoranwa yabo bayihawe biciye ku bupfindo, nk’uko nyene Yehova yari yarategetse biciye kuri Musa ku vyerekeye ya miryango icenda na ca gice c’umuryango. 3 Kuko Musa yari yaratanze intoranwa ya ya miryango yindi ibiri na ca gice kindi c’umuryango hakurya ya Yorodani; Abalewi ntiyabahaye intoranwa hagati muri bo. 4 Kuko bene Yozefu bari bacitse imiryango ibiri, Manase na Efurayimu; kandi Abalewi ntibari barahawe umuturi mu gihugu, kiretse ibisagara vyo kubamwo n’amatongo y’uburagiriro yavyo ku bw’ibitungwa vyabo n’ivyo batunze. 5 Uko Yehova yari yarategetse Musa, ni ko bene Isirayeli bakoze; nuko batangura kugabanganya igihugu.

6 Maze bene Yuda begera Yosuwa i Gilugali, hanyuma Kalebu mwene Yefune Umukenizi amubwira ati: “Ubwawe urazi neza ijambo Yehova yabwiye Musa wa muntu w’Imana y’ukuri ku binyerekeye no ku bikwerekeye, i Kadeshi-baruneya. 7 Nari mfise imyaka mirongo ine igihe Musa umusavyi wa Yehova yandungika mvuye i Kadeshi-baruneya ngo nje gutata igihugu, hanyuma nza ndamugarukaniye inkuru nk’uko nyene vyari mu mutima wanje. 8 Bene wacu baduganye nanje batumye umutima w’abantu ushonga; mugabo jewe, nakurikiye Yehova Imana yanje bimwe bishitse. 9 Musa yaciye rero arahira kuri uwo musi ati: ‘Igihugu ikirenge cawe cahonyoyemwo kizoba icawe n’abahungu bawe bwa ntoranwa gushika igihe kitagira urugero, kuko wakurikiye Yehova Imana yanje bimwe bishitse.’ 10 Ubu rero, ehe Yehova yaranzigamiye ubuzima, nk’uko nyene yasezeranye, iyi myaka mirongo ine n’itanu kuva Yehova ahaye Musa uwo muhango igihe Isirayeli yagendagenda mu gahinga; none ehe uyu musi mfise imyaka mirongo umunani n’itanu. 11 Yamara, uyu musi nkomeye nk’uko nari nkomeye ku musi Musa yandungika. Uko inkomezi zanje zari ico gihe, ni ko ziri n’ubu, haba mu kuja mu rugamba no mu kuvayo. 12 Ubu rero numpe aka karere k’imisozi Yehova yasezeranye kuri uwo musi, kuko ubwawe wumvise kuri uwo musi yuko ng’aho hari Abanakimu n’ibisagara bikomeye bikingijwe ibihome. Biboneka yuko Yehova azobana nanje, ndabomore ata gukeka, nk’uko nyene Yehova yasezeranye.”

13 Yosuwa aca aramuhezagira maze aha Kalebu, mwene Yefune, Heburoni ngo ibe intoranwa yiwe. 14 Ni co catumye Heburoni yegukira Kalebu mwene Yefune Umukenizi bwa ntoranwa gushika n’uyu musi, kuko yakurikiye Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli bimwe bishitse. 15 Imbere y’aho izina rya Heburoni ryari Kiriyati-aruba (uwo Aruba yari we muntu akomeye mu Banakimu). Nuko igihugu nticahungabanywa n’intambara.
