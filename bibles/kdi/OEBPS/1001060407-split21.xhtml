Yosuwa  21 : 1 - 45

21 Nuko abakuru ba ba se b’Abalewi begera Eleyazari umuherezi na Yosuwa mwene Nuni n’abakuru ba ba se b’imiryango ya bene Isirayeli, 2 bababwirira i Shilo mu gihugu ca Kanani bati: “Yehova yategetse biciye kuri Musa ko duhabwa ibisagara vyo kubamwo, kumwe n’amatongo y’uburagiriro yavyo ku bw’ibikoko vyacu vy’ibitungano.” 3 Bene Isirayeli, bakurikije itegeko rya Yehova, baha rero Abalewi ibi bisagara n’amatongo y’uburagiriro yavyo mu ntoranwa yabo.

4 Maze ubupfindo busohoka ku bw’imiryango y’Abakohati, hanyuma ibisagara cumi na bitatu biraheza vyegukira bene Aroni umuherezi, bo mu Balewi, biciye ku bupfindo, bikuwe mu muryango wa Yuda no mu muryango w’Abasimeyoni no mu muryango wa Benyamini.

5 Bene Kohati bari basigaye, baronka biciye ku bupfindo ibisagara cumi mu miryango y’umuryango wa Efurayimu no mu muryango wa Dani no mu gice c’umuryango wa Manase.

6 Bene Gerishoni baronka biciye ku bupfindo ibisagara cumi na bitatu mu miryango y’umuryango wa Isakari no mu muryango wa Asheri no mu muryango wa Nafutali no mu gice c’umuryango wa Manase muri Bashani.

7 Bene Merari, hakurikijwe imiryango yabo, baronka ibisagara cumi na bibiri mu muryango wa Rubeni no mu muryango wa Gadi no mu muryango wa Zebuloni.

8 Ukwo ni ko bene Isirayeli bahaye Abalewi bino bisagara n’amatongo y’uburagiriro yavyo biciye ku bupfindo, nk’uko nyene Yehova yari yarategetse biciye kuri Musa.

9 Mu muryango wa bene Yuda no mu muryango wa bene Simeyoni batanga rero ibi bisagara bigira bivugwe amazina, 10 maze vyegukira bene Aroni mu miryango y’Abakohati bo muri bene Lewi, kuko ubupfindo bwa mbere bwabaye ubwabo. 11 Gutyo babaha Kiriyati-aruba (uwo Aruba yari se wa Anaki), ari yo Heburoni, mu karere k’imisozi ka Yuda, be n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho ryari rihakikuje; 12 akarere kegukira ico gisagara be n’imihana irundanye igikukira na vyo babiha Kalebu mwene Yefune ngo avyigabire.

13 Bene Aroni umuherezi babaha igisagara c’ubuhungiro ku bw’uwishe umuntu, ari co Heburoni, n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaco, na Libuna n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, 14 na Yatiri n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, na Eshitemowa n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, 15 na Holoni n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, na Debiri n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, 16 na Ayini n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, na Yuta n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, na Beti-shemeshi n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho; ibisagara icenda bikuwe muri iyo miryango ibiri.

17 Mu muryango wa Benyamini hatangwa Gibeyoni n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, Geba n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, 18 Anatoti n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, na Alumoni n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho; ibisagara bine.

19 Ibisagara vyose vya bene Aroni, abaherezi, vyari ibisagara cumi na bitatu n’amatongo y’uburagiriro yavyo.

20 Imiryango ya bene Kohati, Abalewi bari basigaye muri bene Kohati, ironka biciye ku bupfindo bwabo, ibisagara mu muryango wa Efurayimu. 21 Babaha rero igisagara c’ubuhungiro ku bw’uwishe umuntu, ari co Shekemu, n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaco mu karere k’imisozi ka Efurayimu, na Gezeri n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, 22 na Kibuzayimu n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, na Beti-horoni n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho; ibisagara bine.

23 Mu muryango wa Dani hatangwa Eliteke n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, Gibetoni n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, 24 Ayaloni n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, Gati-rimoni n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho; ibisagara bine.

25 Mu gice c’umuryango wa Manase hatangwa Tayanaki n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, na Gati-rimoni n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho; ibisagara bibiri.

26 Ibisagara vyose kumwe n’amatongo y’uburagiriro yavyo imiryango ya bene Kohati bari basigaye baronse vyari cumi.

27 Bene Gerishoni, bo mu miryango y’Abalewi, baronka, mu gice c’umuryango wa Manase, igisagara c’ubuhungiro ku bw’uwishe umuntu, ari co Golani muri Bashani, n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaco, na Beyeshitera n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho; ibisagara bibiri.

28 Mu muryango wa Isakari hatangwa Kishiyoni n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, Daberati n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, 29 Yarimuti n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, Eni-ganimu n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho; ibisagara bine.

30 Mu muryango wa Asheri hatangwa Mishali n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, Abudoni n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, 31 Helikati n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, na Rehobu n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho; ibisagara bine.

32 Mu muryango wa Nafutali hatangwa igisagara c’ubuhungiro ku bw’uwishe umuntu, ari co Kedeshi muri Galilaya, n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaco, na Hamoti-dori n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, na Karutani n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho; ibisagara bitatu.

33 Ibisagara vyose vy’Abagerishoni hakurikijwe imiryango yabo vyari ibisagara cumi na bitatu n’amatongo y’uburagiriro yavyo.

34 Imiryango ya bene Merari, Abalewi bari basigaye, ironka mu muryango wa Zebuloni, Yokineyamu n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, Karuta n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, 35 Dimuna n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, Nahalali n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho; ibisagara bine.

36 Mu muryango wa Rubeni hatangwa Bezeri n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, na Yahazi n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, 37 Kedemoti n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, na Mefayati n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho; ibisagara bine.

38 Mu muryango wa Gadi hatangwa igisagara c’ubuhungiro ku bw’uwishe umuntu, ari co Ramoti i Gileyadi, n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, na Mahanayimu n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, 39 Heshiboni n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho, Yazeri n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaho; ibisagara vyose bikaba vyari bine.

40 Ibisagara vyose vyegukiye bene Merari hakurikijwe imiryango yabo, abari basigaye mu miryango y’Abalewi, vyari ibisagara cumi na bibiri baheshejwe ubupfindo.

41 Ibisagara vyose vy’Abalewi hagati mu karere bene Isirayeli bigabira vyari ibisagara mirongo ine n’umunani kumwe n’amatongo y’uburagiriro yavyo. 42 Igisagara kimwe cose muri ivyo kiba igisagara kiri kumwe n’itongo ry’uburagiriro ryaco rigikikije; ukwo ni ko vyari kuri ivyo bisagara vyose.

43 Yehova aha rero Isirayeli igihugu cose yari yararahiye ko azoha ba sekuruza babo; nuko baracigarurira, bakibamwo. 44 Vyongeye, Yehova abaha akaruhuko impande zose, yisunze ivyo yari yararahiye ko azoha ba sekuruza babo vyose; nta n’umwe mu bansi babo bose yabahagaze imbere. Abansi babo bose Yehova yabatanze mu kuboko kwabo. 45 Nta muhango n’umwe utashitse mu mihango myiza yose Yehova yari yarahaye inzu ya Isirayeli; yose yararangutse.
