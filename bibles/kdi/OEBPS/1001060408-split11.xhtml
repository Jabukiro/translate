Abacamanza  11 : 1 - 40

11 Buno Yefuta Umugileyadi yari yarabaye umugabo w’umunyenkomezi kandi w’intwari, akaba yari umuhungu w’umugore w’umumaraya, maze Gileyadi avyara Yefuta. 2 Nuko umugore wa Gileyadi aguma amuvyarira abahungu. Abahungu ba nya mugore bakuze, birukana Yefuta, bamubwira bati: “Ntuzogenda uronse intoranwa mu rugo rwa data, kuko uri umuhungu w’uwundi mugore.” 3 Yefuta arahunga rero kubera bene wabo, aba mu gihugu ca Tobu. Maze abagabo batagira ico bakora baguma bakoranira kuri Yefuta, bakaza barasohokana na we.

4 Haciye igihe, bene Amoni batangura kurwanya Isirayeli. 5 Nuko bene Amoni barwanije Isirayeli, abagabo b’inararibonye ba Gileyadi ubwo nyene baca baja kuzana Yefuta mu gihugu ca Tobu. 6 Maze babwira Yefuta bati: “Enda nuze utubere umukomanda, duheze turwanye bene Amoni.” 7 Mugabo Yefuta abwira nya bagabo b’inararibonye ba Gileyadi ati: “Si mwebwe mwanyanse mukanyirukana mu nzu ya dawe? Kubera iki none muje kuri jewe ubu muri marushwa?” 8 Nya bagabo b’inararibonye ba Gileyadi baca babwira Yefuta bati: “Ni co gituma ubu tugarutse kuri wewe; nujane natwe kurwanya bene Amoni, kandi utubere umukuru aganza ababa i Gileyadi bose.” 9 Yefuta abwira rero nya bagabo b’inararibonye ba Gileyadi ati: “Nimba mungarukanye kugira ndwanye bene Amoni, maze Yehova akabagabura koko mu kuboko kwanje, jewe nzoba umukuru wanyu!” 10 Abo bagabo b’inararibonye ba Gileyadi na bo babwira Yefuta bati: “Yehova nabe icabona* hagati yacu, nitutakugirira nk’uko ijambo ryawe riri.” 11 Yefuta rero ajana n’abagabo b’inararibonye ba Gileyadi maze abantu bamugira umukuru n’umukomanda wabo. Nuko Yefuta avugira amajambo yiwe yose imbere ya Yehova i Mizipa.

12 Maze Yefuta atuma intumwa ku mwami wa bene Amoni ati: “Mpuriye ku ki nawe ko uje kundwanya mu gihugu canje?” 13 Nuko umwami wa bene Amoni abwira izo ntumwa za Yefuta ati: “Ni kubera yuko Isirayeli yantwaye igihugu igihe baduga bavuye mu Misiri, guhera kuri Arunoni gushika kuri Yaboki no gushika kuri Yorodani. Ubu rero gisubize ku neza.” 14 Mugabo Yefuta yongera gutuma intumwa ku mwami wa bene Amoni, 15 amubwira ati:

“Ibi ni vyo Yefuta avuze: ‘Isirayeli ntiyatwaye igihugu ca Mowabu n’igihugu ca bene Amoni. 16 Kuko igihe baduga bavuye mu Misiri, Isirayeli yaciye mu gahinga gushika ku kiyaga Gitukura hanyuma ishika i Kadeshi. 17 Maze Isirayeli ituma intumwa ku mwami wa Edomu iti: “Ndakwinginze ureke nce mu gihugu cawe,” ariko umwami wa Edomu ntiyumviriza. Batuma no ku mwami wa Mowabu, ariko ntiyemera. Isirayeli ibandanya rero kuba i Kadeshi. 18 Igihe bashishikara kugenda baciye mu gahinga, barazungurutse igihugu ca Edomu n’igihugu ca Mowabu, ku buryo bagenda berekeza mu burasirazuba bw’igihugu ca Mowabu baraheza bakambika mu karere ka Arunoni; ntibarenze urubibe rwa Mowabu, kuko Arunoni yari rwo rubibe rwa Mowabu.

19 “‘Inyuma y’aho, Isirayeli ituma intumwa kuri Sihoni umwami w’Abamori, umwami wa Heshiboni, maze Isirayeli imubwira iti: “Ndakwinginze ureke duce mu gihugu cawe kugira tuje ahantu hacu.” 20 Mugabo Sihoni agira amakenga ku vy’uko Isirayeli yorenganye gusa iciye mu karere kiwe, maze Sihoni akoraniriza hamwe abantu biwe bose, akambika i Yahazi hanyuma arwanya Isirayeli. 21 Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli aca atanga Sihoni n’abantu biwe bose mu kuboko kwa Isirayeli, barabakubita maze Isirayeli yigarurira igihugu cose c’Abamori baba muri ico gihugu. 22 Gutyo bigarurira akarere kose k’Abamori, guhera kuri Arunoni gushika kuri Yaboki no guhera mu gahinga gushika kuri Yorodani.

23 “‘Ubu rero Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli ni we yomoye Abamori abakura imbere y’abantu biwe ari bo Isirayeli, none nawe ugomba kwomora abantu biwe. 24 Mbega uwo wese Kemoshi imana yawe itumye womora, si we womora? Kandi umuntu wese Yehova Imana yacu yomoye imbere yacu, ni we tuzokwomora. 25 None wewe uruta Balaki mwene Zipori, umwami wa Mowabu? Hari aho yigeze agiranira amatati na Isirayeli, canke hari aho yigeze abarwanya? 26 Ko Isirayeli yamaze imyaka amajana atatu iba i Heshiboni no mu bisagara bihakukira, n’i Aroweri no mu bisagara bihakukira, no mu bisagara vyose biri iruhande y’inkombe za Arunoni, kubera iki none utigeze ubishikura Isirayeli muri ico gihe? 27 Jewe sinagucumuyeko, mugabo wewe uriko urangirira nabi mu kundwanya. Yehova Umucamanza nace urubanza uno musi hagati ya bene Isirayeli na bene Amoni.’”

28 Mugabo umwami wa bene Amoni ntiyumviriza amajambo ya Yefuta, ayo yari yamutumyeko.

29 Nuko impwemu ya Yehova iza kuri Yefuta, maze aca muri Gileyadi no muri Manase, aca no muri Mizipe ha Gileyadi, hanyuma ava i Mizipe ha Gileyadi, ararengana aja kuri bene Amoni.

30 Maze Yefuta araganira indagano Yehova ati: “Niwatanga koko bene Amoni mu kuboko kwanje, 31 uwuzosohoka, agasohokana mu miryango y’inzu yanje aje kunsanganira ngarutse amahoro mvuye kuri bene Amoni, emwe, azoba uwa Yehova, kandi nzomushikana bwa shikanwa riturirwa.”

32 Nuko Yefuta arashishikara aja kuri bene Amoni kugira abarwanye, maze Yehova abatanga mu kuboko kwiwe. 33 Atangura rero kubasasika kuva i Aroweri gushika i Miniti—ibisagara mirongo ibiri—no gushika i Abeli-keramimu: inganda ziratikira bimwe bikomeye. Gutyo bene Amoni bamarwa igabo imbere ya bene Isirayeli.

34 Amaherezo Yefuta ashika i Mizipa i muhira iwe, maze ehe umukobwa wiwe arasohoka kumusanganira avuza akagoma yongera atamba! Kandi yari umwana umwe rudende. Uretse we, nta muhungu canke umukobwa yari afise. 35 Amukubise ijisho, aca atantamura impuzu ziwe, maze avuga ati: “Yoo, ewe mukobwa wanje! Unteye kwicurikira koko, kandi ni wewe ubaye uwo nariko nkumīra. Jewe nateranuriye akanwa Yehova, nkaba rero ntashobora kwisubirako.”

36 Mugabo amubwira ati: “Dawe, nimba wateranuriye akanwa Yehova, nungirire nk’uko vyakwiviriye mu kanwa, kuko Yehova yaguhaye kwihora abansi bawe, ari bo bene Amoni.” 37 Abandanya kubwira se wiwe ati: “Ningirirwe iki kintu: Undeke mu kiringo c’amezi abiri, ngende; nzomanuka ku misozi, ndirire ubusugi bwanje, twe n’abagenzi banje b’abakobwa.”

38 Na we aca avuga ati: “Genda!” Nuko aramurungika kumara amezi abiri; aragenda, ari kumwe n’abagenzi biwe b’abakobwa, aririra ubusugi bwiwe ku misozi. 39 Amezi abiri aheze, agaruka kwa se, hanyuma se ashitsa indagano yiwe yari yaragize ku bijanye na we. Uwo mukobwa ntiyigeze aryamana n’umugabo. Nuko ibi biba ingingo nyobozi muri Isirayeli: 40 Ku mwaka ku mwaka, abakobwa ba Isirayeli baragenda gukeza umukobwa wa Yefuta Umugileyadi, imisi ine mu mwaka.

^ Abac. 11:10 Ijambo ku rindi ni “nabe uwumviriza.”
