Abacamanza  4 : 1 - 24

4 Maze bene Isirayeli bongera gukora ivyari bibi mu maso ya Yehova Ehudi amaze gupfa. 2 Nuko Yehova abagurisha mu kuboko kwa Yabini umwami wa Kanani yaganza i Hazori; kandi umukuru w’ingabo ziwe yari Sisera, uno akaba yaba i Harosheti h’amahanga. 3 Bene Isirayeli batangura rero kuririra Yehova, kuko Yabini yari afise imikogote y’intambara amajana icenda ifise ibikeruzo vy’icuma, kandi ubwiwe akaba yari amaze imyaka mirongo ibiri ahahaza bene Isirayeli bimwe bibi.

4 Kandi Debora, umuhanuzikazi, umugore wa Lapidoti, ni we yacira imanza Isirayeli ico gihe nyene. 5 Yaba munsi y’ikigazi ca Debora hagati ya Rama na Beteli mu karere k’imisozi ka Efurayimu; kandi bene Isirayeli baraduga iwe ngo abacire imanza. 6 Atumako rero Baraki mwene Abinowamu i Kedeshi-nafutali, amubwira ati: “Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli ategetse ati: ‘Genda ushware umusozi Tabori, kandi ujane n’abagabo ibihumbi cumi bo muri bene Nafutali no muri bene Zebuloni. 7 Nanje ata gukeka, Sisera umukuru w’ingabo za Yabini n’imikogote yiwe y’intambara be n’isinzi ryiwe nzomukwegakwegera kuri wewe, mu mwonga Kishoni, kandi nzomutanga koko mu kuboko kwawe.’”

8 Nuko Baraki amubwira ati: “Niwajana nanje, nanje sinzobura kugenda; mugabo nutajana nanje, sinzogenda.” 9 Na we avuga ati: “Sinzobura kujana nawe. Ariko ubwiza ntibuzoba ubwawe ku nzira ujanye, kuko mu minwe y’umugore ari ho Yehova azogurisha Sisera.” Debora aca arahaguruka, ajana na Baraki i Kedeshi. 10 Baraki atangura gukoraniriza Zebuloni na Nafutali i Kedeshi; nuko abagabo ibihumbi cumi baduga bakurikiye intambuko ziwe; Debora na we adugana na we.

11 Kandi Heberi Umukeni yari yaratandukanye n’Abakeni, bene Hobabu, uwo Musa yari yarabereye umukwe, akaba yari yashinze ihema ryiwe iruhande y’igiti kinini i Zayananimu, ahari i Kedeshi.

12 Maze Sisera amenyeshwa yuko Baraki mwene Abinowamu yari yaduze ku musozi Tabori. 13 Sisera aca akoranya imikogote y’intambara yiwe yose, ya mikogote y’intambara amajana icenda ifise ibikeruzo vy’icuma, n’abantu bose bari kumwe na we, bava i Harosheti h’amahanga baja ku mwonga Kishoni. 14 Nuko Debora abwira Baraki ati: “Haguruka, kuko uyu ari wo musi Yehova aza gutanga ata gukeka Sisera mu kuboko kwawe. None Yehova si we akugiye imbere?” Nuko Baraki atangura kumanuka avuye ku musozi Tabori, inyuma yiwe hari abagabo ibihumbi cumi. 15 Yehova atangura guta mu muvurungano Sisera n’imikogote y’intambara yiwe yose n’ikambi yose, abicisha ubugi bw’inkota imbere ya Baraki. Amaherezo Sisera yururuka umukogote, ahunga n’amaguru. 16 Nuko Baraki yoma inyuma nya mikogote y’intambara na nya kambi gushika i Harosheti h’amahanga, ku buryo ikambi ya Sisera yose igwishwa n’ubugi bw’inkota. Ntihasigara n’umwe.

17 Nayo Sisera, ahungira n’amaguru kw’ihema rya Yayeli umugore wa Heberi Umukeni, kuko hari amahoro hagati ya Yabini umwami wa Hazori n’urugo rwa Heberi Umukeni. 18 Maze Yayeli asohoka gusanganira Sisera, amubwira ati: “Kebereza ng’aha, mukama wanje, kebereza ng’aha iwanje. Ntutinye.” Akebereza iwe mw’ihema. Mu nyuma amupfuka uburengeti. 19 Haciye umwanya Sisera amubwira ati: “Ndakwinginze umpe utuzi two kunywa, kuko nyotewe.” Nuko yugurura umukuza w’urushato w’amata, amuha utwo anywa, araheza aramupfuka. 20 Sisera yongera kumubwira ati: “Hagarara ku bwinjiriro bw’ihema, maze nihagira uwuza akakubaza ati: ‘Hari umugabo ari ng’aha?’, uce uvuga uti: ‘Oya!’”

21 Yayeli umugore wa Heberi rero atora urumambo rwa nya hema, hanyuma afata inyundo mu kuboko kwiwe. Maze aja iyo ari yomboka, amushinga nya rumambo mu misaya, araruhonda rwinjira mw’isi, mu gihe yari asinziriye ubutikoma, yarushe. Nuko arapfa.

22 Maze ehe, Baraki yari akurikiye Sisera. Nuko Yayeli asohoka kumusanganira, amubwira ati: “Ingo ndakwereke umugabo uriko urarondera.” Yinjira rero iwe, maze asanga Sisera yari arambaraye yapfuye, rwa rumambo ruri mu misaya yiwe.

23 Gutyo kuri uwo musi Imana imara igabo Yabini umwami wa Kanani imbere ya bene Isirayeli. 24 Maze ukuboko kwa bene Isirayeli kugenda kurushiriza gukomera ku bwo kurwanya Yabini umwami wa Kanani, gushika baranduye Yabini umwami wa Kanani.
