Rusi  2 : 1 - 23

2 Kandi Nawomi yari afise incuti y’umugabo wiwe, umugabo akomeye w’umutunzi wo mu muryango wa Elimeleki, akaba yitwa Bowazi.

2 Mu nyuma Rusi Umunyamowabukazi abwira Nawomi ati: “Ndakwinginze undeke ngende mu mirima gutorora* mu mahundo y’intete, nkurikira uwo wese nogira ubutoni mu maso yiwe.” Nuko amubwira ati: “Genda, mukobwa wanje.” 3 Aca aragenda maze arinjira, atangura gutorora mu murima inyuma y’abimbuzi. Gutyo aza asanga ari mu gice c’itongo cegukira Bowazi, uno akaba yari uwo mu muryango wa Elimeleki. 4 Maze ehe Bowazi arashika avuye i Betelehemu, aza arabwira abimbuzi ati: “Yehova abane namwe.” Na bo bakamubwira bati: “Yehova aguhezagire.”

5 Mu nyuma Bowazi abwira umusore yari ajejwe abimbuzi ati: “Uyu mugore akiri muto ni uwa nde?” 6 Nuko nya musore arishura, ati: “Uwo mugore akiri muto ni Umunyamowabukazi yagarukanye na Nawomi avuye mu karere ka Mowabu. 7 Kandi yavuze ati: ‘Ndakwinginze undeke ntorore, ntoragure mu mahundo y’intete aciwe, ngendere inyuma y’abimbuzi.’ Nuko arinjira, aguma ahagaze kuva ico gihe mu gitondo gushika n’ubu, akaba ari ho acicara mu nzu akanya gatoyi.”

8 Mu nyuma Bowazi abwira Rusi ati: “Urumva ga mukobwa wanje? Ntuje gutorora mu wundi murima, kandi ntujabuke ngo uve hano uje ahandi; gutyo ugume hafi y’abasukukazi banje bakiri bato. 9 Amaso yawe abe ku murima bimbura, kandi ujane na bo. None sinategetse abasore ngo ntibagukoreko? Niwanyoterwa, uze kugenda ku vyombo maze unywe ku yo nya basore bazoba bavomye.”

10 Rusi aca agwa yubamye, arunama gushika hasi, maze amubwira ati: “Bigenze gute ngo ngire ubutoni mu maso yawe nkabonwa, kandi ndi umunyamahangakazi?” 11 Maze Bowazi aramwishura ati: “Banyiganiye neza ivyo wakoreye nyokobukwe vyose inyuma y’urupfu rw’umugabo wawe, n’ingene wasize so na nyoko n’igihugu c’incuti zawe ukaja ku bantu utari uzi. 12 Yehova aguhembere ivyo ukora, kandi Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli aguhe impembo itunganye, uwo waje kuronderera ubuhungiro munsi y’amababa yiwe.” 13 Na we aca avuga ati: “Ningire ubutoni mu maso yawe, mukama wanje, kuko umpojeje kandi ukavugisha umusukukazi wawe mu buryo buhumuriza, naho ntari nk’umwe mu basukukazi bawe.”

14 Igihe co gufungura kigeze, Bowazi amubwira ati: “Egera ng’aha, urye ku mukate wongere udumbe ikimanyu cawe mu nkarihe.” Nuko yicara iruhande y’abimbuzi, aza aramuhereza intete zikaranze na we akarya, arahaga agira n’ibisigara. 15 Maze arahaguruka kugira atorore. Nuko Bowazi abwiriza abasore biwe ati: “Mumureke atorore no mu mahundo y’intete yaciwe, kandi ntimumusindire. 16 Vyongeye, mwitwararike kumusokorera amahundo makeya mu mitekero y’amahundo, muyasige inyuma kugira ayatorore, kandi ntimumuhambarire.”

17 Abandanya gutorora mu murima gushika ku mugoroba, maze akubita izo yari yatoroye, ziba nka efa* imwe ya sayiri. 18 Nuko arazitora hanyuma yinjira mu gisagara, maze inabukwe abona izo yari yatoroye. Inyuma y’ivyo, asokorora ibifungurwa yari yasigaje amaze guhaga, arabimuha.

19 Nuko inabukwe amubwira ati: “Watoroye hehe uno musi, kandi wakoreye hehe? Uwakubonye aragahezagirwa.” Yiganira rero inabukwe nyen’umurima yari yakozemwo uwo ari we; hanyuma abandanya kuvuga ati: “Nyen’umurima nakozemwo uno musi ni Bowazi.” 20 Nawomi aca abwira umukazana wiwe ati: “Ahezagirwe na Yehova, we ataretse kugirira ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo abazima n’abapfuye.” Nawomi abandanya kumubwira ati: “Uwo mugabo ni incuti yacu. Ni umwe mu bacunguzi bacu.” 21 Maze Rusi Umunyamowabukazi avuga ati: “Yambwiye kandi ati: ‘Uraguma hafi y’abantu banje bakiri bato gushika barangije umwimbu wose mfise.’” 22 Nawomi araheza abwira Rusi umukazana wiwe ati: “Ni vyiza, mukobwa wanje, ko ujana n’abasukukazi biwe bakiri bato, kugira ngo ntibakugore mu wundi murima.”

23 Aguma hafi y’abasukukazi ba Bowazi bakiri bato kugira atorore, gushika iyimbura rya sayiri n’iyimbura ry’ingano rirangiye. Aguma abana na inabukwe.

^ Rusi 2:2 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ Rusi 2:17 Efa yari ingero yangana n’amalitiro 22.
