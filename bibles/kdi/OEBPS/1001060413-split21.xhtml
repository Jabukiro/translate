2 Abami  21 : 1 - 26

21 Manase yari afise imyaka cumi n’ibiri igihe yatangura kuganza, maze amara imyaka mirongo itanu n’itanu aganza i Yeruzalemu. Nyina yitwa Hefuziba. 2 Atangura gukora ivyari bibi mu maso ya Yehova, yisunze ibintu biteye ishishi vy’amahanga Yehova yari yarirukanye imbere ya bene Isirayeli. 3 Asubira rero kwubaka ibibanza bikirurutse Hezekiya se wiwe yari yararanduye, kandi ashingira ibicaniro Bayali yongera akora igikingi ceranda, nk’uko Ahabu umwami wa Isirayeli yari yaragize; atangura kwunamira ingabo zose zo mu majuru no kuzikorera. 4 Yubaka ibicaniro mu nzu ya Yehova, iyo Yehova yari yaravuze ku biyerekeye ati: “I Yeruzalemu ni ho nzoshira izina ryanje.” 5 Abandanya kwubakira ibicaniro ingabo zose zo mu majuru mu bigo bibiri vy’inzu ya Yehova. 6 Acisha umuhungu wiwe mu muriro, akora ivy’amageza yongera arondera ibimenyetso bitara ibizoba kandi ashiraho abavugana n’ibiremwa vy’impwemu be n’abagize umwuga ukubura ibizoba. Akora ku rugero runini ivyari bibi mu maso ya Yehova, kugira ngo amubabaze.

7 Vyongeye, igishusho c’ikibazano c’igikingi ceranda yari yarakoze, agishira mu nzu Yehova yari yarabwiye Dawidi na Salomo umuhungu wiwe ku biyerekeye ati: “Muri iyi nzu be n’i Yeruzalemu, aho natoye mu miryango yose ya Isirayeli, nzohashira izina ryanje gushika igihe kitagira urugero. 8 Kandi sinzokwongera gutuma ikirenge ca Isirayeli kiyerera kivuye ku butaka nahaye ba sekuruza babo, bakire gusa kwitwararika gukora bisunze ivyo nabategetse vyose, egome, ku vyerekeye itegeko ryose umusavyi wanje Musa yabategetse.” 9 Nuko ntibumviriza, mugabo Manase aguma abosha gukora ivyari bibi kurusha amahanga Yehova yari yarazimanganije imbere ya bene Isirayeli.

10 Nuko Yehova aguma avuga biciye ku basavyi biwe abahanuzi, ati: 11 “Ko Manase umwami wa Yuda yakoze ivyo bintu biteye ishishi, yakoze ivy’ububisha kurusha ivyo Abamori bamwitangiye imbere bakoze vyose, kandi yatanguye gucumurisha na Yuda ibigirwamana vyiwe vy’umwanda. 12 Ni co gituma ibi ari vyo Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli avuze: ‘Ehe ngira nzane ivyago kuri Yeruzalemu na Yuda, uwuzovyumva wese amatwi yiwe yompi akaba azovugiramwo iminzerere. 13 Kandi nzoramvura ata gukeka kuri Yeruzalemu ikamba yo gupimisha yakoreshejwe kuri Samariya be n’ikiringanisho cakoreshejwe ku nzu ya Ahabu; kandi nzohanagura koko Yeruzalemu nk’uko umuntu ahanagura ibakure itagira ibigōmbo, akayihanagura hanyuma akayubika. 14 Kandi nzoheba koko amasigarira y’intoranwa yanje, ndabatange mu kuboko kw’abansi babo, bacike koko isahu n’umunyago ku bansi babo bose, 15 kubera ko bakoze ivyari bibi mu maso yanje, bakaguma bambabaza kuva umusi ba sekuruza bava mu Misiri gushika n’uyu musi.’”

16 Manase kandi asesa amaraso y’intungane menshi cane, gushika aho ayuzuza Yeruzalemu kuva ku mpera imwe gushika ku yindi, hambavu y’igicumuro ciwe yacumuje Yuda mu gukora ivyari bibi mu maso ya Yehova. 17 Nayo ibisigaye mu vya Manase n’ivyo yakoze vyose, n’igicumuro ciwe yacumuye, none ntivyanditswe mu gitabu c’ivyo mu misi y’abami ba Yuda? 18 Amaherezo Manase aryama hamwe na ba sekuruza biwe hanyuma ahambwa mu murima w’inzu yiwe, mu murima wa Uza; nuko Amoni umuhungu wiwe atangura kuganza mu kibanza ciwe.

19 Amoni yari afise imyaka mirongo ibiri n’ibiri igihe yatangura kuganza, maze amara imyaka ibiri aganza i Yeruzalemu. Nyina yitwa Meshulemeti umukobwa wa Haruzi w’i Yotiba. 20 Abandanya gukora ivyari bibi mu maso ya Yehova, nk’uko Manase se wiwe yari yarakoze. 21 Aguma agendera mu nzira yose se wiwe yari yaragendeyemwo, kandi abandanya gukorera ibigirwamana vy’umwanda se yari yarakoreye, abandanya no kuvyunamira. 22 Gutyo aheba Yehova Imana ya ba sekuruza biwe, ntiyagendera mu nzira ya Yehova. 23 Amaherezo abasuku ba Amoni bamugīra umugambi mubi maze bicira umwami mu nzu yiwe. 24 Mugabo abanyagihugu bica abantu bose bari bagīriye Umwami Amoni umugambi mubi. Maze abanyagihugu bimika Yosiya umuhungu wiwe ngo abe umwami mu kibanza ciwe. 25 Nayo ibisigaye mu vya Amoni, ivyo yakoze, none ntivyanditswe mu gitabu c’ivyo mu misi y’abami ba Yuda? 26 Nuko bamuhamba mu mva yiwe mu murima wa Uza; maze Yosiya umuhungu wiwe atangura kuganza mu kibanza ciwe.
