2 Abami  6 : 1 - 33

6 Nuko abahungu b’abahanuzi batangura kubwira Elisha bati: “Ehe rero! Ahantu tuba imbere yawe haratwagiye birenze. 2 Turakwinginze tugende gushika kuri Yorodani, umwe wese muri twebwe akureyo ikigiga hanyuma twikorere ng’aho ahantu ho kuba.” Nuko avuga ati: “Nimugende.” 3 Mugabo umwe muri bo avuga ati: “Turakwinginze wemere kujana n’abasuku bawe.” Aca avuga ati: “Ndagenda.” 4 Nuko ajana na bo; amaherezo bashika kuri Yorodani maze batangura guca ibiti. 5 Maze umwe muri bo ariko yicira ikigiga ciwe, umutwe w’ishoka ugwa mu mazi. Atangura kuboroga avuga ati: “Yoo, databuja, kuko yari intirano!” 6 Nuko nya muntu w’Imana y’ukuri avuga ati: “Yaguye he?” Amwereka rero nya hantu. Aca atema ingegene y’igiti araheza ayitererayo maze atuma nya mutwe w’ishoka ureremba. 7 Nuko avuga ati: “Yidugirize.” Aca atuma ukuboko, arayitora.

8 Umwami wa Siriya yinjira mu ntambara yo kurwanya Isirayeli. Nuko agisha inama abasuku biwe ati: “Muzokambikana nanje aha n’aha.” 9 Maze nya muntu w’Imana y’ukuri atuma ku mwami wa Isirayeli ati: “Wirinde guca aha hantu, kuko ari ho Abasiriya bamanutse.” 10 Umwami wa Isirayeli atuma rero abantu nya hantu uwo muntu w’Imana y’ukuri yari yamubwiye. Nuko Elisha aramuburira, aguma kure yaho; ivyo ntivyaba rimwe canke kabiri.

11 Nuko umutima w’umwami wa Siriya urashangashirwa kubera ico kintu, ku buryo ahamagara abasuku biwe akababwira ati: “Mbega ntimwombwira uwo mu bacu ari ku ruhande rw’umwami wa Isirayeli?” 12 Maze umwe mu basuku biwe avuga ati: “Nta n’umwe, mukama wanje umwami; mugabo Elisha umuhanuzi ari muri Isirayeli ni we abwira umwami wa Isirayeli ibintu uvugira mu cumba cawe c’uburyamo.” 13 Nuko avuga ati: “Nimugende, murabe aho ari, kugira ngo ntume kumufata.” Mu nyuma amenyeshwa ngo: “Ehe ari i Dotani.” 14 Aca arungikayo amafarasi n’imikogote y’intambara n’inteko za gisirikare nyinshi; bashika mw’ijoro maze bakikuza igisagara.

15 Umusuku wa nya muntu w’Imana y’ukuri avyutse kare ngo ahaguruke asohoke, aza abona inteko za gisirikare zikikuje ico gisagara n’amafarasi n’imikogote y’intambara. Nya musuku wiwe aca amubwira ati: “Yoo, databuja! Tugire iki?” 16 Mugabo avuga ati: “Ntutinye, kuko abari kumwe natwe ari benshi kuruta abari kumwe na bo.” 17 Maze Elisha atangura gusenga ati: “Ewe Yehova, ndakwinginze wugurure amaso yiwe, kugira ngo abone.” Yehova aca yugurura amaso ya nya musuku, ku buryo abona; maze ehe, nya karere k’imisozi kari kuzuye amafarasi n’imikogote y’intambara y’umuriro hirya no hino ya Elisha.

18 Batanguye kumanuka aho ari, Elisha abandanya gusenga Yehova ati: “Ndakwinginze uteze uruhumyi iri hanga.” Abateza rero uruhumyi, nk’uko Elisha yari avuze. 19 Elisha ababwira rero ati: “Iyi si yo nzira, kandi iki si co gisagara. Nkurikire ndabajane ku mugabo muriko murarondera.” Ariko abajana i Samariya.

20 Bakimara gushika i Samariya, Elisha avuga rero ati: “Ewe Yehova, niwugurure amaso y’aba bantu kugira ngo babone.” Yehova aca yugurura amaso yabo, barabona; basanga bari hagati na hagati muri Samariya. 21 Umwami wa Isirayeli akimara kubabona, abwira rero Elisha ati: “Ndabasasike, ndabasasike ga dawe?” 22 Mugabo avuga ati: “Ntubasasike. Mbega abo wagize imbohe ukoresheje inkota yawe n’umuheto wawe urabasasika? Shira umukate n’amazi imbere yabo kugira barye bongere banywe baheze baje ku mukama wabo.” 23 Nuko abatereka inzimano ikomeye; batangura kurya no kunywa, hanyuma arabarungika, baja ku mukama wabo. Maze twa tugwi tw’abambuzi tw’Abasiriya ntitwongera kugaruka na rimwe mu gihugu ca Isirayeli.

24 Inyuma y’aho, Beni-hadadi umwami wa Siriya atangura gukoraniriza hamwe ikambi yiwe yose maze araduga, yugariza Samariya. 25 Mu nyuma haduka ikigoyi gikomeye i Samariya; maze ehe, barahugariza gushika aho umutwe w’indogobwa uba uwuciye ibice mirongo umunani vy’ifeza, ica kane c’ingero ya kabu* y’amavyi y’inuma na co kiba igiciye ibice bitanu vy’ifeza. 26 Nuko umwami wa Isirayeli ariko ararengana ku ruhome, umugore umwe aramuririra, ati: “Nkiza, ewe mukama wanje umwami!” 27 Na we avuga ati: “Nimba Yehova atagukijije, nogukirisha ibivuye hehe? Ibivuye ku mbuga y’ikubitiro canke mu ruganiro rw’umuvinyu canke mu ruganiro rw’amavuta?” 28 Umwami abandanya kumubwira ati: “Ni amaki?” Na we ati: “Uwu mugore ubona yambwiye ati: ‘Zana umuhungu wawe tumurye uno musi, umuhungu wanje na we tuzomurya ejo.’ 29 Nuko duteka umuhungu wanje, turamurya. Maze ku musi ukurikira ndamubwira nti: ‘Zana umuhungu wawe tumurye.’ Mugabo anyegeza umuhungu wiwe.”

30 Umwami akimara kwumva amajambo ya nya mugore, aca atantamura impuzu ziwe; hanyuma ariko ararengana ku ruhome, abantu barabibona, maze ehe, igunira* ryari munsi y’impuzu ziwe, ku mubiri wiwe. 31 Hanyuma avuga ati: “Imana ingirire gutyo, mbere n’ibirenze ivyo, umutwe wa Elisha mwene Shafati niwaguma kuri we uno musi!”

32 Elisha yari yicaye mu nzu yiwe, kandi abagabo b’inararibonye bari bicaranye na we, igihe umwami yatuma umuntu avuye imbere yiwe. Iyo ntumwa itarashika iwe, Elisha abwira nya bagabo b’inararibonye ati: “Mwabonye ingene uwu muhungu w’umwicanyi atumye intumwa kunkurako umutwe? Mube maso: iyo ntumwa niyaba ikimara gushika, mwugare umuryango, muyisunike n’umuryango. Mbega inyuma yiwe ntihumvikana urwamo rw’ibirenge vy’umukama wiwe?” 33 Akiriko aravugana na bo, ehe nya ntumwa imanuka aho ari, maze umwami avuga ati: “Ehe ibi ni ivyago bivuye kuri Yehova. Ndacarindirira iki Yehova?”

^ 2 Abm. 6:25 Kabu imwe ni nk’ilitiro 1,22.

^ 2 Abm. 6:30 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
