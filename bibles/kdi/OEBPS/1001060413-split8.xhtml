2 Abami  8 : 1 - 29

8 Elisha yari yarabwiye wa mugore yari yaragarukaniye umuhungu wiwe ubuzima ati: “Haguruka ugende, wewe n’urugo rwawe, maze ubeho uri kavamahanga ahantu hose ushobora kuba uri kavamahanga; kuko Yehova ahamagaye ikigoyi, vyongeye kikaba kizomara imyaka indwi mu gihugu.” 2 Nuko nya mugore arahaguruka, akora nk’uko nya muntu w’Imana y’ukuri yavuze; aragenda, we hamwe n’urugo rwiwe, abaho ari kavamahanga mu gihugu c’Abafilisitiya imyaka indwi.

3 Haheze imyaka indwi, nya mugore arahindukira avuye mu gihugu c’Abafilisitiya, aja kuririra umwami ku bw’inzu yiwe no ku bw’itongo ryiwe. 4 Kandi umwami yariko aravugana na Gehazi umusuku wa wa muntu w’Imana y’ukuri, ati: “Ndakwinginze unyiganire ibintu vyose bihambaye Elisha yakoze.” 5 Nuko ariko ariganira umwami ingene yari yaragarukaniye ubuzima uwapfuye, erega, ehe wa mugore Elisha yari yaragarukaniye ubuzima umuhungu wiwe, yariko aririra umwami ku bw’inzu yiwe no ku bw’itongo ryiwe. Gehazi aca avuga ati: “Mukama wanje umwami, ng’uyu wa mugore, kandi ng’uyu wa muhungu wiwe Elisha yagarukanira ubuzima.” 6 Umwami aca abaza nya mugore, uno na we atangura kumwiganira iyo nkuru. Maze umwami amuha umukozi wo ku kirimba, avuga ati: “Musubize ivyiwe vyose n’ivyavuye muri iryo tongo vyose kuva umusi yava mu gihugu gushika ubu.”

7 Nuko Elisha aza i Damasiko; kandi Beni-hadadi umwami wa Siriya yari arwaye. Bamumenyesha rero bati: “Wa muntu w’Imana y’ukuri yashitse ino.” 8 Umwami aca abwira Hazayeli ati: “Tora ingabirano mu kuboko kwawe, ugende guhura n’uwo muntu w’Imana y’ukuri, usiguze Yehova biciye kuri we uti: ‘Mbega nzokira iyi ndwara?’” 9 Nuko Hazayeli agenda guhura na we, kandi atora ingabirano mu kuboko kwiwe, emwe, ubwoko bwose bw’ibintu vyiza vy’i Damasiko, umutwaro w’ingamiya mirongo ine; arashika, ahagarara imbere yiwe, hanyuma avuga ati: “Umuhungu wawe Beni-hadadi umwami wa Siriya antumye kuri wewe ati: ‘Mbega nzokira iyi ndwara?’” 10 Maze Elisha amubwira ati: “Genda umubwire uti: ‘Uzokira ata kabuza,’ mugabo Yehova yanyeretse ko azopfa ata kabuza.” 11 Nuko aguma amushinze ijisho, aguma arimushinze gushika aho Hazayeli yumva ubuyega. Maze uwo muntu w’Imana y’ukuri atangura kurira. 12 Hazayeli aca avuga ati: “Kubera iki umukama wanje ariko ararira?” Na we ati: “Kubera nzi neza ikibi uzokorera bene Isirayeli. Ibibanza vyabo bikingijwe ibihome uzobiha umuriro, abagabo b’abahizi babo ubicishe inkota, abana babo ubasavye, abagore babo bibungenze na bo ubafomoze.” 13 Hazayeli aca avuga ati: “Mbega umusuku wawe, imbwa y’imbwa, ni iki, ngo akore ico kintu gihambaye?” Ariko Elisha avuga ati: “Yehova yakunyeretse uri umwami wa Siriya.”

14 Inyuma y’ivyo ava aho Elisha ari aragenda, aja ku mukama wiwe, maze uno amubwira ati: “Elisha yakubwiye iki?” Na we ati: “Yambwiye ngo: ‘Uzokira ata kabuza.’” 15 Ku musi ukurikira, Hazayeli atora igisaswa agikoza mu mazi araheza agisanza mu maso hiwe, arapfa. Nuko Hazayeli atangura kuganza mu kibanza ciwe.

16 Mu mwaka ugira gatanu wa Yehoramu mwene Ahabu umwami wa Isirayeli, igihe Yehoshafati yari umwami wa Yuda, Yehoramu mwene Yehoshafati umwami wa Yuda aba umwami. 17 Yari afise imyaka mirongo itatu n’ibiri igihe yaba umwami, maze amara imyaka umunani aganza i Yeruzalemu. 18 Abandanya kugendera mu nzira y’abami ba Isirayeli, nk’uko ab’inzu ya Ahabu bari baragize; kuko umukobwa wa Ahabu ari we yari yabaye umugore wiwe; aguma akora ivyari bibi mu maso ya Yehova. 19 Mugabo Yehova ntiyashaka guhonya Yuda kubera Dawidi umusavyi wiwe, nk’uko yari yaramusezeraniye kumuha itara we n’abahungu biwe nantaryo.

20 Mu misi yiwe, Edomu igarariza ukuboko kwa Yuda, bimika umwami wo kubaganza. 21 Nuko Yehoramu arajabuka aja i Zayiri, ari kumwe n’imikogote yose. Araheza ahaguruka mw’ijoro, asasika Abanyedomu bari bamukikuje n’abakuru b’imikogote; maze abantu bahungira mu mahema yabo. 22 Mugabo Edomu ibandanya kugarariza ukuboko kwa Yuda gushika n’uyu musi. Ico gihe ni ho Libuna yatangura kugarariza.

23 Ibisigaye mu vya Yehoramu n’ivyo yakoze vyose, none ntivyanditswe mu gitabu c’ivyo mu misi y’abami ba Yuda? 24 Amaherezo Yehoramu aryama hamwe na ba sekuruza hanyuma ahambwa hamwe na ba sekuruza mu Gisagara ca Dawidi. Nuko Ahaziya umuhungu wiwe atangura kuganza mu kibanza ciwe.

25 Mu mwaka ugira cumi na kabiri wa Yehoramu mwene Ahabu umwami wa Isirayeli, Ahaziya mwene Yehoramu umwami wa Yuda aba umwami. 26 Ahaziya yari afise imyaka mirongo ibiri n’ibiri igihe yatangura kuganza, amara umwaka umwe aganza i Yeruzalemu. Nyina yitwa Ataliya umwuzukuru wa Omuri umwami wa Isirayeli. 27 Abandanya kugendera mu nzira y’inzu ya Ahabu kandi aguma akora ivyari bibi mu maso ya Yehova, nk’inzu ya Ahabu, kuko yari incuti y’inzu ya Ahabu biciye ku kwabirana. 28 Nuko ajana na Yehoramu mwene Ahabu mu ntambara yo kurwanya Hazayeli umwami wa Siriya i Ramoti-gileyadi, mugabo Abasiriya bagarika Yehoramu. 29 Yehoramu umwami aragaruka rero kugira ngo yivurize i Yezireyeli ibikomere Abasiriya bari bamuteje i Rama* igihe yarwanya Hazayeli umwami wa Siriya. Nayo Ahaziya mwene Yehoramu umwami wa Yuda, amanuka kuraba Yehoramu mwene Ahabu i Yezireyeli, kuko yari arwaye.

^ 2 Abm. 8:29 Ni impfunyapfunyo ya Ramoti-gileyadi.
