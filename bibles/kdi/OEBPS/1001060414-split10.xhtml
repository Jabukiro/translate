1 Ngoma  10 : 1 - 14

10 Nuko Abafilisitiya barwana na Isirayeli; maze abagabo ba Isirayeli barahunga bava imbere y’Abafilisitiya, baguma bagwa bagandaguwe ku musozi Gilibowa. 2 Abafilisitiya baguma basatira Sauli n’abahungu biwe; Abafilisitiya bica Yonatani na Abinadabu na Maliki-shuwa, abahungu ba Sauli. 3 Urugamba rwo kurwanya Sauli ruba urukomeye; abarasisha umuheto amaherezo baramubona, maze akomeretswa n’abo barashi. 4 Nuko Sauli abwira uwumutwaza ibirwanisho ati: “Sokora inkota yawe uyinsogote, kugira ngo abo bagabo batagenyerewe ntibaze, ngo bangirire koko ivy’agashinyaguro.” Mugabo uwumutwaza ibirwanisho aranka, kuko yatinye cane. Nuko Sauli afata inkota, ayīsukako. 5 Uwumutwaza ibirwanisho abonye ko Sauli apfuye, na we nyene aca yīsuka ku nkota yiwe, arapfa. 6 Gutyo Sauli n’abahungu biwe batatu barapfa, n’abo mu nzu yiwe bose bapfira hamwe. 7 Abagabo ba Isirayeli bose bari mu kiyaya co hasi babonye ko bahunze be n’uko Sauli n’abahungu biwe bapfuye, batangura rero guta ibisagara vyabo, barahunga; hanyuma Abafilisitiya barashika maze babibamwo.

8 Ku musi ukurikira, Abafilisitiya baje kwambura abagandaguwe, bubuka kuri Sauli n’abahungu biwe baguye ku musozi Gilibowa. 9 Nuko baramwambura baraheza bamukurako umutwe n’ibirwanisho vyiwe maze batuma intumwa mu gihugu c’Abafilisitiya hirya no hino kumenyesha ayo makuru ibigirwamana vyabo be n’abantu. 10 Amaherezo bashira ibirwanisho vyiwe mu nzu y’imana yabo, agahanga kiwe na ko bakamanika ku nzu ya Dagoni.

11 Ab’i Yabeshi muri Gileyadi bose bumva ivyo Abafilisitiya bari bagiriye Sauli vyose. 12 Nuko abagabo b’intwari bose barahaguruka, batwara ikiziga ca Sauli n’ibiziga vy’abahungu biwe, babijana i Yabeshi, maze bahamba amagufa yabo munsi y’igiti kinini i Yabeshi; hanyuma bamara imisi indwi bisonzesheje.

13 Ukwo ni ko Sauli yapfuye bivuye ku buhemu bwiwe yari yarahemukiye Yehova, kubera ijambo rya Yehova atari yaragumije no kubera ko yituye uwuvugana n’ibiremwa vy’impwemu kugira ngo abaze. 14 Ntiyasiguza Yehova. Ku bw’ivyo aramwica maze ingoma ayiha Dawidi mwene Yese.
