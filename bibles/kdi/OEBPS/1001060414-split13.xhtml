1 Ngoma  13 : 1 - 14

13 Nuko Dawidi aja inama n’abakuru b’ibihumbi n’abakuru b’amajana be n’indongozi zose; 2 hanyuma Dawidi abwira ishengero rya Isirayeli ryose ati: “Nimba bibabereye vyiza kandi bikaba vyemerwa kuri Yehova Imana yacu, dutume intumwa kuri bene wacu basigaye mu turere twose twa Isirayeli no ku baherezi be n’Abalewi bari mu bisagara vyabo bifise amatongo y’uburagiriro, kugira ngo bakoranire aho turi. 3 Maze tuzane hafi yacu isandugu y’Imana yacu.” Kuko batari barayitayeho mu misi ya Sauli. 4 Nuko ishengero ryose rivuga ko bigirwa gutyo, kuko ico kintu caboneka ko kibereye mu maso y’abantu bose. 5 Dawidi akoranya rero Isirayeli yose, kuva ku ruzi rwa Misiri gushika ku bwinjiriro bwa Hamati, kugira ngo bazane isandugu y’Imana y’ukuri bayikuye i Kiriyati-yeyarimu.

6 Maze Dawidi na Isirayeli yose baduga i Bayala, i Kiriyati-yeyarimu, ahegukira Yuda, kugira ngo bakureyo isandugu y’Imana y’ukuri, Yehova, uwicara ku bakerubi*, iyitirirwa izina ryiwe. 7 Ariko buriza isandugu y’Imana y’ukuri ku mukogote mushasha bayikuye mu nzu ya Abinadabu, Uza na Ahiyo bakaba ari bo bari bayoboye uwo mukogote. 8 Dawidi na Isirayeli yose bariko bahimbaza ico kintu imbere y’Imana y’ukuri n’inkomezi zose, n’indirimbo, n’inanga, n’ibicurarangisho vy’imirya, n’utugoma, n’ivyuma bisāma be n’inzumbete. 9 Amaherezo bashika ku mbuga y’ikubitiro ya Kidoni, maze Uza atuma ukuboko kugira ngo afate nya Sandugu, kuko amashūri yari ahatswe kuyitembagaza. 10 Ishavu rya Yehova rica rirurumbira Uza ku buryo amugarika, kuko yari atumye ukuboko kuri nya Sandugu, apfira ng’aho nyene imbere y’Imana. 11 Maze Dawidi ashavuzwa n’uko Yehova yari arandamuye* Uza; aho hantu rero bahita Perezi-uza gushika n’uyu musi.

12 Nuko uwo musi Dawidi atinya Imana y’ukuri, avuga ati: “Nzozana gute iwanje isandugu y’Imana y’ukuri?” 13 Dawidi ntiyimurira nya Sandugu iwe, mu Gisagara ca Dawidi, mugabo ayikebereza mu nzu ya Obedi-edomu Umugiti. 14 Nya sandugu y’Imana y’ukuri ibandanya kubana n’urugo rwa Obedi-edomu, mu nzu yiwe, amezi atatu; kandi Yehova yaguma ahezagira urugo rwa Obedi-edomu n’ivyiwe vyose.

^ 1 Ngo. 13:6 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ 1 Ngo. 13:11 Ijambo ku rindi ni “aturikiye kuri.”
