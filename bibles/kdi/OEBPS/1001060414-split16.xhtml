1 Ngoma  16 : 1 - 43

16 Gutyo binjiza isandugu y’Imana y’ukuri, bayishira mw’ihema Dawidi yari yarayishingiye; maze batangura gushikiriza imbere y’Imana y’ukuri amashikanwa aturirwa n’ibimazi vy’ugusangira. 2 Dawidi arangije gushikana ishikanwa riturirwa n’ibimazi vy’ugusangira, atangura guhezagira abantu mw’izina rya Yehova. 3 Vyongeye, agabanganiriza ibifungurwa Abisirayeli bose, umugabo n’umugore, umwe wese amuha akamanyu k’umukate k’umuzingi n’agatsima ko mu ngazi z’itende n’agatsima k’inzabibu zumye. 4 Maze ashira imbere y’isandugu ya Yehova bamwe mu Balewi ngo babe abasuku, kugira ngo bibutse abantu no kugira ngo bakengurukire bongere bashemeze Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli: 5 Asafu umukuru, uwugira kabiri kuri we akaba yari Zekariya, hanyuma Yeyeli na Shemiramoti na Yehiyele na Matitiya na Eliyabu na Benaya na Obedi-edomu na Yeyeli, bafise ibicurarangisho vyo mu bwoko bw’ibifise imirya, n’inanga, Asafu akaba yari afise ivyuma bisāma vyo kuvuza n’ijwi ryumvikana, 6 na Benaya na Yahaziyeli abaherezi bafise inzumbete ubudasiba imbere y’isandugu y’isezerano ry’Imana y’ukuri.

7 Kuri uwo musi ni ho rero Dawidi yatanga intererano ari bwo bwa mbere kugira akengurukire Yehova biciye kuri Asafu no kuri bene wabo:

 8 “Nimukengurukire Yehova; nimwambaze izina ryiwe,

Mumenyeshe ibikorwa vyiwe mu bisata vy’abantu!

 9 Nimumuririmbire, mumuhibongoreze,

Mwitwararike ibikorwa vy’agatangaza vyiwe vyose.

10 Nimwiratire izina ryiwe ryeranda,

Umutima w’abarondera Yehova nunezerwe.

11 Nimurondere Yehova be n’inkomezi ziwe,

Murondere mu maso hiwe nantaryo.

12 Nimwibuke ibikorwa vyiwe vy’agatangaza yaranguye,

Ibitangaro vyiwe n’ingingo z’ubutungane* z’akanwa kiwe,

13 Yemwe ruvyaro rwa Isirayeli umusavyi wiwe,

Mwebwe bene Yakobo, abo yatoye.

14 Ni we Yehova Imana yacu; mw’isi yose hari ingingo ziwe z’ubutungane.

15 Nimwibuke isezerano ryiwe, egome, gushika igihe kitagira urugero,

Mwibuke ijambo yategetse, gushika ku mayaruka igihumbi,

16 Isezerano yagiranye na Aburahamu,

N’indahiro yarahiye Izahaki.

17 Iyo yagumijeho bwa ngingo nyobozi kuri Yakobo,

Bwa sezerano ryamaho igihe kitagira urugero kuri Isirayeli,

18 Akavuga ati: ‘Nzoguha igihugu ca Kanani,

Kibe wo mugabane w’intoranwa yanyu.’

19 Aho hari igihe mwari imparurwa,

Emwe, bake cane, mukaba na ba kavamahanga muri co.

20 Kandi baguma bagendagenda bava mw’ihanga baja mu rindi,

Bava mu bwami bumwe bagana ikindi gisata.

21 Ntiyemeye ko hagira n’umwe abagunga,

Mugabo kubera bo, yarakangiriye abami,

22 Ati: ‘Ntimukore ku bo narobanuje amavuta,

Kandi ntihagire ikintu kibi na kimwe mugirira abahanuzi banje.’

23 Ririmbire Yehova, yemwe abo kw’isi mwese!

Mutangaze umusi ku wundi ubukiriro atanga!

24 Nimwigane mu mahanga ubuninahazwa bwiwe,

Mwigane mu bisata vy’abantu vyose ibikorwa vyiwe vy’agatangaza.

25 Kuko Yehova ahambaye kandi ni uwo gushemezwa rwose,

Kandi akaba akwiye gutinywa kurusha izindi mana zose.

26 Kuko imana zose z’ibisata vy’abantu ari imana ata co zimaze.

Mugabo Yehova we, yakoze amajuru.

27 Iteka n’ubwiza bwakaka biri imbere yiwe,

Inkomezi n’akanyamuneza biri ahantu hiwe.

28 Nimuherereze kuri Yehova, yemwe miryango y’ibisata vy’abantu,

Nimuherereze kuri Yehova ubuninahazwa n’inkomezi.

29 Nimuhe Yehova ubuninahazwa bw’izina ryiwe,

Nimutware ingabirano mwinjire imbere yiwe.

Nimwunamire Yehova mu gushaza kweranda.

30 Nimugire ububabare bukaze kubera we, yemwe abo kw’isi mwese!

N’ubutaka bwimbuka burashimangiwe:

Ntibuzokwigera bunyiganyizwa.

31 Amajuru nahimbarwe, isi na yo inezerwe,

Nibivuge mu mahanga biti: ‘Yehova ubwiwe acitse umwami!’

32 Ikiyaga nigituragare be n’ibicuzuye,

Agahinga nikigine be n’ibiri muri ko vyose.

33 Ico gihe nyene, ibiti vyo mu kibira nibiturikishe binezerwe kubera Yehova,

Kuko aje gucira imanza isi.

34 Nimukengurukire Yehova, kuko ari mwiza,

Kuko ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwiwe ari ubwo gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

35 Muvuge muti: ‘Dukize, ewe Mana y’ubukiriro bwacu,

Utwegeranye maze udukize udukuye mu mahanga,

Ngo dukengurukire izina ryawe ryeranda, ngo tuvugane ukwigina tugushemeza.

36 Hahezagirwe Yehova ya Mana ya Isirayeli kuva igihe kitagira urugero gushika igihe kitagira urugero.’”

Nuko abantu bose bavuga bati: “Amen!” bongera bashemeza Yehova.

37 Maze asiga Asafu na bene wabo ng’aho imbere y’isandugu y’isezerano rya Yehova ngo bame bakorera imbere y’iyo Sandugu, nk’uko bibwirizwa kugirwa ku musi ku musi; 38 asiga na Obedi-edomu na bene wabo, mirongo itandatu n’umunani, na Obedi-edomu mwene Yedutuni na Hosa ngo babe abarinzi b’irembo; 39 asiga na Zadoki umuherezi na bene wabo abaherezi imbere y’itaberenakulo ya Yehova ku kibanza gikirurutse* cari i Gibeyoni, 40 kugira ngo bame bashikanira Yehova amashikanwa aturirwa ku gicaniro c’ishikanwa riturirwa, mu gitondo no ku mugoroba, no kugira ngo bitwararike ibintu vyose vyanditswe mw’itegeko rya Yehova, iryo yategetse Isirayeli; 41 kandi hamwe na bo ashiraho Hemani na Yedutuni n’abasigaye bo mu bagabo batowe, bavuzwe amazina, kugira ngo bakengurukire Yehova, kuko “ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwiwe ari ubwo gushika igihe kitagira urugero”; 42 Hemani na Yedutuni, abashira hamwe na bo kugira ngo bavuze inzumbete n’ivyuma bisāma n’ibicurarangisho vy’ururirimbo rw’Imana y’ukuri; bene Yedutuni na bo bashirwa kw’irembo. 43 Maze abantu bose baragenda, umwe wese ku nzu yiwe. Nuko Dawidi aja guhezagira inzu yiwe.

^ 1 Ngo. 16:12 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ 1 Ngo. 16:39 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
