1 Ngoma  18 : 1 - 17

18 Inyuma y’ivyo, Dawidi atangura gukubita Abafilisitiya no kubamara igabo, maze afata i Gati n’ibisagara bihakukira ahakuye mu kuboko kw’Abafilisitiya. 2 Hanyuma atsinda Mowabu, maze Abanyamowabu baba abasuku ba Dawidi bamuzanira ikibuguro.

3 Dawidi atsindira kandi Hadadezeri umwami wa Zoba i Hamati, mu gihe uno yariko aja gushinga ububasha bwiwe ku ruzi Efurate. 4 Vyongeye, Dawidi amutwara imikogote igihumbi n’abagendera ku mafarasi ibihumbi indwi n’abagabo ibihumbi mirongo ibiri bari ku maguru. Maze Dawidi aca ibitsi amafarasi yose y’imikogote, mugabo asigaza muri yo amafarasi ijana y’imikogote. 5 Siriya y’i Damasiko ije gutabara Hadadezeri umwami wa Zoba, Dawidi aca asasika mu Basiriya abagabo ibihumbi mirongo ibiri na bibiri. 6 Inyuma y’ivyo, Dawidi ashira ibirindiro muri Siriya y’i Damasiko, maze Abasiriya baba abasuku ba Dawidi bamuzanira ikibuguro. Yehova yaguma aha ubukiriro Dawidi aho yaja hose. 7 Vyongeye, Dawidi atora inkinzo z’umuzingi z’inzahabu zari ku basuku ba Hadadezeri hanyuma azijana i Yeruzalemu. 8 Dawidi atwara umujumpu mwinshi cane awukuye i Tibuhati n’i Kuni, ibisagara vya Hadadezeri. Salomo awukoramwo ca kiyaga c’umujumpu na za nkingi be n’ibikoresho vy’umujumpu.

9 Toyu* umwami wa Hamati yumvise ko Dawidi yari yasasitse inteko za gisirikare zose za Hadadezeri umwami wa Zoba, 10 aca atuma Hadoramu umuhungu wiwe ku Mwami Dawidi kumubaza uko amerewe no kumukeza kubera yari yarwanije Hadadezeri akamutsinda (kuko Hadadezeri yari amaze kumenyera kurwanya Toyu), kandi yari kumwe n’ubwoko bwose bw’ibintu vy’inzahabu n’ifeza n’umujumpu. 11 Ivyo na vyo nyene Umwami Dawidi avyereza Yehova, kumwe n’ifeza be n’inzahabu yari yarakuye mu mahanga yose: muri Edomu n’i Mowabu no kuri bene Amoni no ku Bafilisitiya no kuri Amaleki.

12 Nayo Abishayi mwene Zeruwiya, asasika Abanyedomu mu Mubande w’Umunyu, asasika ibihumbi cumi n’umunani. 13 Ashira rero ibirindiro muri Edomu, maze Abanyedomu bose baba abasuku ba Dawidi. Yehova yaguma akiza Dawidi aho yaja hose. 14 Nuko Dawidi abandanya kuganza Isirayeli yose, kandi yaguma ashitsa ingingo y’ubutungane be n’ubugororotsi ku bw’abantu biwe bose. 15 Yowabu mwene Zeruwiya ni we yagaba ingabo, Yehoshafati mwene Ahiludi na we akaba umukarani. 16 Zadoki mwene Ahitubu na Ahimeleki mwene Abiyatari bari abaherezi, Shavusha na we akaba umunyamabanga. 17 Benaya mwene Yehoyada ni we yagaba Abakereti n’Abapeleti; kandi bene Dawidi bari bafise ikibanza ca mbere iruhande y’umwami.

^ 1 Ngo. 18:9 Muri 2Sm 8:9, 10 ni “Toyi.”
