1 Ngoma  20 : 1 - 8

20 Nuko umwaka utanguye, igihe abami basanzwe basohoka ku rugamba, Yowabu ayobora inteko zo kurwana zo mu ngabo maze ageseza igihugu ca bene Amoni yongera aja gusugereza Raba, mu gihe Dawidi yaba i Yeruzalemu; maze Yowabu asasika Raba, ayisenyurira hasi. 2 Mugabo Dawidi akura urugori rwa Malikamu* ku mutwe wayo, maze asanga uburemere bwarwo bupima italanto y’inzahabu, kandi rwarimwo amabuye y’agaciro; nuko ruba ku mutwe wa Dawidi. Isahu ry’ico gisagara, iryo yasohoye, ryari ryinshi cane. 3 Abantu bari muri co arabasohora, maze abakoresha mu gikorwa co kugegenesha amabuye imisumeno n’ico gukoresha ibikoresho bikarishe vy’icuma be n’ico gukoresha amashoka; ukwo ni ko Dawidi yagirira ibisagara vyose vya bene Amoni. Amaherezo Dawidi n’abantu bose basubira i Yeruzalemu.

4 Inyuma y’ivyo haduka intambara i Gezeri yo kurwana n’Abafilisitiya. Ico gihe ni ho Sibekayi Umuhusha yagarika Sipayi* wo mu bavutse ku Barefayimu, ku buryo Abafilisitiya bamarwa igabo.

5 Hongera kwaduka intambara yo kurwanya Abafilisitiya; nuko Elihanani mwene Yayiri agarika Lahumi mwene wabo na Goliyati Umugiti, uruti rw’icumu ryiwe rukaba rwari rumeze nk’igiti kizingirwako inyuzi c’abajishi b’impuzu.

6 Hongera kandi kwaduka intambara i Gati, mu gihe hari umugabo w’igihagararo c’akataraboneka yari afise intoke n’amano bitandatubitandatu, vyose hamwe bikaba vyari mirongo ibiri na bine; na we nyene yari yavutse ku Barefayimu. 7 Nuko aguma acokora Isirayeli. Amaherezo Yonatani umuhungu wa Shimeya mwene wabo na Dawidi aramugarika.

8 Abo ni bo bari bavutse ku Barefayimu i Gati; nuko bagwa biciye ku kuboko kwa Dawidi no ku kuboko kw’abasuku biwe.

^ 1 Ngo. 20:2 Raba 2Sm 12:30, akajambo k’epfo.

^ 1 Ngo. 20:4 Muri 2Sm 21:18 ni “Safu.”
