2 Ngoma  20 : 1 - 37

20 Inyuma y’ivyo, bene Mowabu na bene Amoni, bari kumwe na bamwe mu Bamonimu, baza gutera Yehoshafati mu ntambara. 2 Nuko abantu baza kubimenyesha Yehoshafati, bati: “Hari isinzi rinini rije kugutera rivuye mu karere k’ikiyaga, muri Edomu; kandi ehe bari i Hazazoni-tamari, ari ho Eni-gedi.” 3 Yehoshafati aca aratinya maze yiyemeza kurondera Yehova. Atangaza rero ko Yuda yose yisonzesha. 4 Amaherezo abo muri Yuda bakoranirizwa hamwe kugira ngo basiguze Yehova. Egome, abo mu bisagara vyose vya Yuda baza kubaza Yehova.

5 Maze Yehoshafati ahagarara mw’ishengero rya Yuda n’irya Yeruzalemu mu nzu ya Yehova, imbere y’ikigo gishasha, 6 hanyuma avuga ati:

“Ewe Yehova Mana ya ba sogokuruza bacu, mbega nturi Imana mu majuru, kandi ntuganza ubwami bwose bw’amahanga, kandi mu kuboko kwawe ntihari ububasha n’ubushobozi, akaba ata n’umwe yoguhagarara imbere? 7 Mbega si wewe, ewe Mana yacu, wirukanye ababa muri iki gihugu bakava imbere y’abantu bawe Isirayeli maze ukagiha uruvyaro rwa Aburahamu umukunzi wawe, gushika igihe kitagira urugero? 8 Barakibayemwo, maze batangura kukikwubakiramwo aheranda ku bw’izina ryawe, mu kuvuga bati: 9 ‘Icago nicatuzako, ari inkota canke urubanza rubi canke ikiza canke ikigoyi, tuze duhagarare imbere y’iyi nzu n’imbere yawe (kuko izina ryawe riri muri iyi nzu), kugira ngo tugutabaze mu marushwa yacu, uheze wumve maze udukize.’ 10 None ehe bene Amoni, na Mowabu n’akarere k’imisozi ka Seyiri, abo utemereye Isirayeli kubashwaramwo igihe bari bavuye mu gihugu ca Misiri, mugabo bagaca kure yabo ntibabazimanganye, 11 egome, ehe baduhemvye kwinjira ngo batwirukane mu ntoranwa yawe watumye twigabira. 12 Ewe Mana yacu, mbega ntuzobashitsako urubanza? Kuko tudafise ububasha imbere y’iri sinzi rinini rije kudutera; kandi twebwe ntituzi ico turi bukore, mugabo ni wewe duhanze amaso.”

13 Muri ico gihe abo muri Yuda bose bari bahagaze imbere ya Yehova, mbere n’abana babo batoyi n’abagore babo n’abahungu babo.

14 Nayo Yahaziyeli mwene Zekariya, Zekariya wa Benaya, Benaya wa Yeyeli, Yeyeli wa Mataniya, Umulewi wo muri bene Asafu, impwemu ya Yehova imuzako hagati mw’ishengero. 15 Nuko avuga ati: “Nimwumvirize, ewe Yuda yose namwe ababa i Yeruzalemu nawe Mwami Yehoshafati! Ibi ni vyo Yehova ababwiye: ‘Ntimutinye canke ngo mutekerwe n’ubwoba kubera iryo sinzi rinini; kuko urugamba atari urwanyu, mugabo ni urw’Imana. 16 Ejo muze mumanuke kubategera. Ehe bariko badugana akanyuro ka Zizi; muzobasanga koko ku mpera y’umwonga imbere y’agahinga ka Yeruweli. 17 Ntimuzorinda kurwana ico gihe. Muze mufate ikibanza canyu, muhagarare mudakoma maze mubone ubukiriro bwa Yehova muzokirishwa. Ewe Yuda na Yeruzalemu, ntimutinye canke ngo mutekerwe n’ubwoba. Ejo muze musohoke kubategera, Yehova azoba ari kumwe namwe.’”

18 Yehoshafati aca arunama, mu maso hiwe hakora hasi, na Yuda yose n’ababa i Yeruzalemu bikubita hasi imbere ya Yehova kugira bunamire Yehova. 19 Maze Abalewi bo mu bahungu b’Abakohati n’abo mu bahungu b’Aba-Kora barahaguruka kugira bashemeze Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli n’ijwi rirenga bimwe bidasanzwe.

20 Nuko bahaguruka kare mu gitondo maze barasohoka baja mu gahinga ka Tekowa. Bariko barasohoka, Yehoshafati arahaguruka maze avuga ati: “Nimunyumve, ewe Yuda namwe ababa i Yeruzalemu! Nimwizere Yehova Imana yanyu kugira ngo mumare ikiringo kirekire. Nimwizere abahanuzi biwe mubone kuroranirwa.”

21 Vyongeye, aja inama n’abantu maze ashiraho abaririmvyi bo kuririmbira Yehova n’abamushemeza bashazishije impuzu nyeranda, bakabigira igihe basohotse bitangiye imbere abagabo bafise ibirwanisho, bavuga bati: “Shemeze Yehova, kuko ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwiwe ari ubwo gushika igihe kitagira urugero.”

22 Aho batanguriye kwiha akamo k’umunezero no kumushemeza, Yehova ashiraho abagabo bo kubundira bene Amoni, Mowabu n’akarere k’imisozi ka Seyiri bariko baza muri Yuda, maze bano batangura gukubitana. 23 Nuko bene Amoni na Mowabu bahagurukira ababa mu karere k’imisozi ka Seyiri kugira ngo babatikize, babazimanganye; bakimara kurangiza ababa i Seyiri, baca bafashanya umwe wese kwica mugenziwe.

24 Nayo Yuda, iza ku munara w’inderetsi wo mu gahinga. Bagize ngo barahindukira kuraba nya sinzi, baza bababona bari ng’aho, imivyimba yabo irambaraye hasi ata n’umwe yacitse kw’icumu. 25 Nuko Yehoshafati n’abantu biwe baza gusahura isahu ryabo; baronka muri bo ibintu n’ivyambarwa n’ibintu bihimbaye, vyinshi; batangura kubambura ivyo bitwarira gushika aho badashobora gutwara ikindi kintu na kimwe. Bamara imisi itatu basahura nya sahu, kuko ryari ryinshi. 26 Maze ku musi ugira kane bakoranira mu kiyaya co hasi ca Beraka, kuko ari ho bahezagiriye Yehova. Ni co catumye aho hantu bahita Ikiyaya co Hasi ca Beraka*, gushika uno musi.

27 Maze abagabo bose ba Yuda na Yeruzalemu barahindukira barongōwe na Yehoshafati, kugira basubire i Yeruzalemu banezerewe, kuko Yehova yari yabanezereje ku bijanye n’abansi babo. 28 Baza rero i Yeruzalemu ku nzu ya Yehova, bafise ibicurarangisho vy’imirya n’inanga n’inzumbete. 29 Nuko ubwoba buvuye ku Mana buba ku bwami bwose bw’ibihugu, aho bumviye ko Yehova yari yarwanije abansi ba Isirayeli. 30 Gutyo akarere k’ubwami ka Yehoshafati ntikongera kudurumbanywa, kandi Imana yiwe ibandanya kumuha akaruhuko impande zose.

31 Nuko Yehoshafati abandanya kuganza Yuda. Yari afise imyaka mirongo itatu n’itanu igihe yatangura kuganza, amara imyaka mirongo ibiri n’itanu aganza i Yeruzalemu. Nyina yitwa Azuba umukobwa wa Shilihi. 32 Aguma agendera mu nzira ya se wiwe Asa, ntiyakevya ngo ayivemwo, akora ivyari bigororotse mu maso ya Yehova. 33 Gusa, ibibanza bikirurutse ntivyazimangana; kandi abantu ntibari bwategurire umutima wabo Imana ya ba sekuruza babo.

34 Nayo ibisigaye mu vya Yehoshafati, ivya mbere n’ivya nyuma, ehe vyanditswe mu majambo ya Yehu mwene Hanani, ayasobetswe mu Gitabu c’Abami ba Isirayeli. 35 Inyuma y’ivyo, Yehoshafati umwami wa Yuda yifatanya na Ahaziya umwami wa Isirayeli, uwakora ivy’ububisha. 36 Yifatanya rero na we mu vyo gukora imeri zo kuja i Tarushishi, bakora kandi imeri i Eziyoni-geberi. 37 Ariko Eliyezeri mwene Dodavahu w’i Maresha avuga mu buryo bw’ubuhanuzi abwira Yehoshafati, ati: “Ko wifatanije na Ahaziya, Yehova ntazobura gusenyura ibikorwa vyawe.” Nuko nya meri zirasaba, ntibasubira kugira inguvu zo kuja i Tarushishi.

^ 2 Ngo. 20:26 Bisobanura ngo “Umuhezagiro.”
