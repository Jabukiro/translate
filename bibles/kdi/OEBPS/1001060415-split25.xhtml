2 Ngoma  25 : 1 - 28

25 Amaziya yabaye umwami afise imyaka mirongo ibiri n’itanu, maze amara imyaka mirongo ibiri n’icenda aganza i Yeruzalemu. Nyina yitwa Yehowadani w’i Yeruzalemu. 2 Abandanya gukora ivyari bigororotse mu maso ya Yehova, mugabo ntiyabigirana umutima ukwiye. 3 Ubwami bukimara gukomera kuri we, aca yica abasuku biwe bari barishe umwami se wiwe. 4 Mugabo abana babo ntiyabica, ariko akora nk’uko ivyanditswe mw’itegeko biri, mu gitabu ca Musa, ivyo Yehova yategetse ati: “Ba se ntibaze bicwe bahowe abana babo, eka n’abana ntibaze bicwe bahowe ba se; mugabo baze bicwe umwe wese ahowe igicumuro ciwe bwite.”

5 Nuko Amaziya akoraniriza hamwe Yuda maze abahagarika yisunze inzu ya ba sekuruza, akurikije abakuru b’ibihumbi n’abakuru b’amajana, ku bwa Yuda yose na Benyamini yose; hanyuma arabandika kuva ku myaka mirongo ibiri kuduga, amaherezo asanga ari abagabo b’abahizi ibihumbi amajana atatu baja mu ngabo, bakoresha icumu n’inkinzo yagutse. 6 Vyongeye, ahēra muri Isirayeli abagabo b’intwari kandi b’abanyenkomezi ibihumbi ijana, abahēra italanto ijana z’ifeza. 7 Hanyuma umuntu umwe w’Imana y’ukuri aza kuri we, avuga ati: “Ewe mwami, ingabo za Isirayeli ntizijane nawe, kuko Yehova atari kumwe na Isirayeli, ni ukuvuga abahungu bose ba Efurayimu. 8 Mugabo ingo wewe ubwawe, ukore, ugire umutima rugabo ku bw’intambara. Imana y’ukuri yohava igutsitaza imbere y’umwansi; kuko Imana ifise ububasha bwo gufasha no gutsitaza.” 9 Amaziya aca abwira uwo muntu w’Imana y’ukuri ati: “Mugabo none bigende gute ku bijanye n’italanto ijana nahaye abasirikare ba Isirayeli?” Nya muntu w’Imana y’ukuri na we ati: “Yehova arafise uburyo bwo kuguha ibiruta ivyo.” 10 Nuko Amaziya arabatandukanya, ni ukuvuga abasirikare bari baje kuri we bavuye muri Efurayimu, kugira bigire iwabo. Ariko ishavu ryabo rirurumbira cane Yuda, ku buryo basubira iwabo barunguriye n’ishavu.

11 Nuko Amaziya agira umutima rugabo, ayobora abantu biwe, aja mu Mubande w’Umunyu; maze asasika bene Seyiri, ibihumbi cumi muri bo. 12 Bene Yuda bafata ibihumbi cumi ari bazima. Babajana rero hejuru ku rutare maze batangura kubakoragura bahanutse hejuru kuri nya rutare; bose baramenagurika. 13 Nayo ba basirikare Amaziya yari yasubije inyuma ngo ntibajane na we mu ntambara, batangura kugaba ibitero ku bisagara vya Yuda, kuva i Samariya gushika i Beti-horoni, basasika muri bo ibihumbi bitatu bongera batwara isahu rinini.

14 Mugabo Amaziya agarutse avuye gusasika Abanyedomu, aza rero azanye imana za bene Seyiri araheza azīshiriraho ngo zimubere imana; atangura kuzunamira, atangura no kuzifumbishiriza umwotsi w’ibimazi. 15 Maze ishavu rya Yehova rirurumbira Amaziya, ku buryo atuma umuhanuzi kuri we akamubwira ati: “Kubera iki warondeye imana z’abo bantu zitarokoye abantu bazo mu kuboko kwawe?” 16 Avuganye na we, umwami aca amubwira ati: “Mbega twakugize umujanama w’umwami? Sigaho ku neza yawe. Barinde kukwica?” Nuko nya mujanama asigaho, mugabo avuga ati: “Ndazi ntakeka yuko Imana yiyemeje kuguhonya, kuko wakoze ivyo kandi ntiwumvirize impanuro yanje.”

17 Maze Amaziya umwami wa Yuda agisha inama hanyuma atuma kuri Yehowasi mwene Yehowahazi, Yehowahazi wa Yehu, umwami wa Isirayeli, ati: “Ingo duhangane.” 18 Yehowasi umwami wa Isirayeli aca atuma kuri Amaziya umwami wa Yuda ati: “Icatsi c’amahwa cari muri Libani catumye kw’isederi ryari muri Libani giti: ‘Nuhe umukobwa wawe umuhungu wanje amubere umugore.’ Ariko igikoko co mw’ishamba cari muri Libani kirarengana, gikandagira ico catsi c’amahwa. 19 Wibwiye ngo: Ehe warasasitse Edomu. Kandi umutima wawe wagushize hejuru kugira ngo uninahazwe. Nugume rero wibera mu nzu yawe. Kubera iki worinda kwinjira mu matati kandi ibintu bimeze nabi, ukabwirizwa gutemba, ugatembana na Yuda?”

20 Mugabo Amaziya ntiyumviriza; kuko vyari bivuye ku Mana y’ukuri kugira ngo ibatange mu kuboko kwiwe, kubera ko bari barondeye imana za Edomu. 21 Nuko Yehowasi umwami wa Isirayeli araduga, maze barahangana, we na Amaziya umwami wa Yuda, bahanganira i Beti-shemeshi hegukira Yuda. 22 Maze Yuda iratsindwa imbere ya Isirayeli, ku buryo bahunga, umwe wese akaja kw’ihema ryiwe. 23 Amaziya umwami wa Yuda, mwene Yehowasi, Yehowasi wa Yehowahazi, ni we Yehowasi umwami wa Isirayeli yafatiye i Beti-shemeshi, amuzana i Yeruzalemu maze aca igihengeri mu ruhome rwa Yeruzalemu, kuva kw’Irembo rya Efurayimu gushika kw’Irembo ryo mu Mfuruka, igihengeri c’amatambwe amajana ane. 24 Atora inzahabu zose n’ifeza zose n’ibintu vyose vyari mu nzu y’Imana y’ukuri, kwa Obedi-edomu no mu matunga y’inzu y’umwami, n’abagizwe imbohe, araheza asubira i Samariya.

25 Amaziya mwene Yehowasi umwami wa Yuda abandanya kubaho imyaka cumi n’itanu inyuma y’urupfu rwa Yehowasi mwene Yehowahazi umwami wa Isirayeli. 26 Nayo ibisigaye mu vya Amaziya, ivya mbere n’ivya nyuma, none ntivyanditswe mu Gitabu c’Abami ba Yuda na Isirayeli? 27 Kuva igihe Amaziya ahindukiriye akareka gukurikira Yehova, batangura kumugīra umugambi mubi i Yeruzalemu. Amaherezo ahungira i Lakishi; mugabo batuma abamukurikira i Lakishi bamwicirayo. 28 Nuko bamutwara ku mafarasi hanyuma bamuhamba hamwe na ba sekuruza biwe mu gisagara ca Yuda.
