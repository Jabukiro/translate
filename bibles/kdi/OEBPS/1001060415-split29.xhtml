2 Ngoma  29 : 1 - 36

29 Hezekiya yabaye umwami afise imyaka mirongo ibiri n’itanu, maze amara imyaka mirongo ibiri n’icenda aganza i Yeruzalemu. Nyina yitwa Abiya umukobwa wa Zekariya. 2 Aguma akora ivyari bigororotse mu maso ya Yehova, yisunze ivyo Dawidi sekuruza yari yarakoze vyose. 3 Ni we yuguruye mu mwaka wa mbere w’ingoma yiwe, mu kwezi kwa mbere, imiryango y’inzu ya Yehova hanyuma atangura kuyisanasana. 4 Maze azana abaherezi n’Abalewi, abakoraniriza mu kibanza co hanze amaja mu buseruko. 5 Hanyuma ababwira ati: “Nimunyumvirize, yemwe Balewi. Nimwiyeze rero mwongere mweze inzu ya Yehova Imana ya ba sogokuruza banyu, maze musohore ikintu gihumanye mugikure ahantu heranda. 6 Kuko ba data bahemutse, bagakora ivyari bibi mu maso ya Yehova Imana yacu, ku buryo bamuhevye, bagakura amaso yabo kw’itaberenakulo ya Yehova, bakamuha ibitugu. 7 Mbere barugaye imiryango y’ibaraza kandi baguma bazimije amatara, ntibaturira imibavu kandi Imana ya Isirayeli ntibayishikanira ikimazi giturirwa ahantu heranda. 8 Maze uburakari bwa Yehova buza kuri Yuda na Yeruzalemu, ku buryo abagira akajugumiza akabagira n’akajoreza be n’abo kuvugiriza agahwa, nk’uko muvyibonera n’amaso yanyu. 9 None ehe ba sogokuruza bacu baragwishijwe n’inkota, kandi abahungu bacu n’abakobwa bacu n’abagore bacu baragizwe imbohe kubera ico. 10 Ubu, mfise icipfuzo mu mutima wanje co kugirana isezerano na Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli, kugira ngo ishavu ryiwe rirurumba rituveko. 11 None rero bana banje, ntimwihe akaruhuko, kuko ari mwebwe Yehova yatoye ngo muhagarare imbere yiwe kugira ngo mumukorere no kugira ngo mubandanye kuba abasuku biwe n’abamufumbishiriza umwotsi w’ibimazi.”

12 Abalewi baca barahaguruka, Mahati mwene Amasayi na Yoweli mwene Azariya bo mu bahungu b’Abakohati; muri bene Merari hahaguruka Kishi mwene Abudi na Azariya mwene Yehaleleli; mu Bagerishoni hahaguruka Yowa mwene Zima na Edeni mwene Yowa; 13 muri bene Elizafani hahaguruka Shimuri na Yehuweli; muri bene Asafu hahaguruka Zekariya na Mataniya; 14 muri bene Hemani hahaguruka Yehiyele na Shimeyi; muri bene Yedutuni na ho hahaguruka Shemaya na Uziyeli. 15 Maze bakoraniriza hamwe bene wabo, bariyeza hanyuma baraza nk’uko ibwirizwa ry’umwami ryari, bisunze amajambo ya Yehova, kugira ngo bahumanure inzu ya Yehova. 16 Nuko abaherezi binjira mu nzu ya Yehova kugira ngo bakore igikorwa c’uguhumanura, maze basohora imicafu yose basanze mu rusengero rwa Yehova bayishira mu kigo c’inzu ya Yehova. Abalewi na bo barayakira kugira bayijane mu mwonga Kidironi hanze. 17 Gutyo batangura iryo yeza ku musi wa mbere w’ukwezi kwa mbere, maze ku musi ugira umunani w’ukwo kwezi bashika kw’ibaraza rya Yehova; ku buryo beza inzu ya Yehova mu misi umunani, maze ku musi ugira cumi na gatandatu w’ukwezi kwa mbere bararangiza.

18 Inyuma y’ivyo binjira indani aho Hezekiya umwami ari hanyuma bavuga bati: “Twahumanuye inzu ya Yehova yose, n’igicaniro c’ishikanwa riturirwa be n’ibikoresho vyaco vyose, n’imeza y’imikate igerekeranye be n’ibikoresho vyayo vyose. 19 Ibikoresho vyose Umwami Ahazi yari yarakuye mu gikorwa mu gihe c’ingoma yiwe mu buhemu bwiwe twabiteguye twongera turavyeza; ehe biri imbere y’igicaniro ca Yehova.”

20 Nuko Hezekiya umwami avyuka kare maze akoraniriza hamwe abaganwa b’igisagara hanyuma aduga ku nzu ya Yehova. 21 Bazana amashūri indwi, n’impfizi z’intama indwi, n’imyagazi y’intama y’amasuguru indwi, n’amasuguru y’impene indwi, bwa shikanwa ry’igicumuro ku bw’ubwami no ku bw’aheranda no ku bwa Yuda. Nuko abwira bene Aroni abaherezi ngo babishikane ku gicaniro ca Yehova. 22 Bakerera rero nya nka, maze abaherezi bakira amaraso bayamijagira ku gicaniro; hanyuma bakerera nya mpfizi z’intama maze bamijagira amaraso ku gicaniro, bakerera na nya myagazi y’intama y’amasuguru maze bamijagira amaraso ku gicaniro. 23 Maze nya masuguru y’impene y’ishikanwa ry’igicumuro bayegeza hafi imbere y’umwami n’ishengero hanyuma bayarambikako ibiganza. 24 Nuko abaherezi barayakerera, bashikana ku gicaniro ishikanwa ry’igicumuro bakoresheje amaraso yayo, kugira batangire impongano* Isirayeli yose; kuko umwami yari yavuze ko ishikanwa riturirwa be n’ishikanwa ry’igicumuro bitangirwa Isirayeli yose.

25 Muri ico gihe, ashira Abalewi mu nzu ya Yehova, bafise ivyuma bisāma n’ibicurarangisho vy’imirya n’inanga, nk’uko vyategetswe na Dawidi na Gadi umubonyi w’umwami be na Natani umuhanuzi, kuko iryo bwirizwa ryari ryatanzwe n’ukuboko kwa Yehova biciye ku bahanuzi biwe. 26 Abalewi baguma rero bahagaze bafise ibicurarangisho vya Dawidi, be n’abaherezi, bafise inzumbete.

27 Maze Hezekiya avuga ko hashikanwa ku gicaniro ikimazi giturirwa; ishikanwa riturirwa ngo ritangure, ururirimbo rwa Yehova ruca ruratangura n’inzumbete nyene, ziyobowe n’ibicurarangisho vya Dawidi umwami wa Isirayeli. 28 Ishengero ryose ryari ryunamye mu gihe ururirimbo rwariko rusamirana, inzumbete na zo zasirana—ivyo vyose birabandanya gushika ishikanwa riturirwa rirangiye. 29 Bakimara kurangiza kurishikana, umwami n’abari kumwe na we bose barunama, bakubita inkoro hasi. 30 Nuko Hezekiya umwami n’abaganwa babwira Abalewi ngo bashemeze Yehova mu majambo ya Dawidi n’aya Asafu umubonyi. Batangura rero kumushemeza banezerewe, baguma bunamye, bakubise inkoro hasi.

31 Amaherezo Hezekiya yishura ati: “Ubu rero murujuje ukuboko kwanyu ububasha ku bwa Yehova. Nimwegere, muzane ku nzu ya Yehova ibimazi be n’ibimazi vy’ugukenguruka.” Nuko ishengero ritangura kuzana ibimazi be n’ibimazi vy’ugukenguruka, kandi umuntu wese w’umutima ukunze azana amashikanwa aturirwa. 32 Igitigiri c’amashikanwa aturirwa ishengero ryazanye kiba inka mirongo irindwi, impfizi z’intama ijana n’imyagazi y’intama y’amasuguru amajana abiri—ivyo vyose vyari ishikanwa riturirwa rihabwa Yehova; 33 amashikanwa meranda na yo aba inka amajana atandatu n’ibitungwa ibihumbi bitatu vyo mu mukuku. 34 Ariko abaherezi baba bake cane, ntibashobora gukura urushato ku mashikanwa aturirwa yose. Nuko bene wabo, ari bo Balewi, barabafasha gushika igikorwa kirangiye no gushika abaherezi biyejeje, kuko Abalewi bari barajwe ishinga cane no kwiyeza kuruta abaherezi. 35 Vyongeye, amashikanwa aturirwa yari menshi be n’ibikenye binuze vy’ibimazi vy’ugusangira be n’amashikanwa y’ikinyobwa aherekeza amashikanwa aturirwa. Ukwo ni ko igikorwa c’inzu ya Yehova categuwe. 36 Nuko Hezekiya n’abantu bose banezerezwa n’uko Imana y’ukuri yari yagiriye abantu imyiteguro, kuko ico kintu cari cabaye giturumbuka.

^ 2 Ngo. 29:24 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
