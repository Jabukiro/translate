2 Ngoma  30 : 1 - 27

30 Nuko Hezekiya atuma kuri Isirayeli yose na Yuda, mbere yandikira amakete Efurayimu na Manase, ngo baze ku nzu ya Yehova i Yeruzalemu kugira bahimbarize pasika* Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli. 2 Ariko umwami n’abaganwa biwe be n’ishengero ryose i Yeruzalemu biyemeza guhimbaza pasika mu kwezi kugira kabiri; 3 kuko batari bashoboye kuyihimbaza ico gihe, kubera ko ku ruhande rumwe ata baherezi bahagije bari biyejeje, ku rundi ruhande na ho abantu batari bakoraniye i Yeruzalemu. 4 Ico kintu kiba ikibereye mu maso y’umwami no mu maso y’ishengero ryose. 5 Bafata rero ingingo y’uko haterwa akamo muri Isirayeli yose, kuva i Beyeri-sheba gushika i Dani, kugira baze guhimbariza pasika Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli i Yeruzalemu; kuko batari babigize ari benshi, nk’uko vyanditswe.

6 Nuko abirutsi bafise amakete avuye mu kuboko kw’umwami n’ukw’abaganwa biwe bakwiragira muri Isirayeli yose na Yuda, egome, bisunze ibwirizwa ry’umwami, kugira ngo bavuge bati: “Yemwe bene Isirayeli, nimugaruke kuri Yehova Imana ya Aburahamu na Izahaki na Isirayeli, kugira ngo agaruke ku basigaye muri mwebwe bakize ikiganza c’abami ba Ashuri. 7 Ntimube nka ba sogokuruza banyu na bene wanyu bahemutse kuri Yehova Imana ya ba sekuruza babo, ku buryo abagira akajoreza, nk’uko muvyibonera. 8 None rero ntimudadaze izosi nk’uko ba sogokuruza banyu bagize. Nimworohere Yehova, muze aheranda hiwe yejeje gushika igihe kitagira urugero, mukorere Yehova Imana yanyu, kugira ngo ishavu ryiwe rirurumba ribaveko. 9 Kuko nimwagaruka kuri Yehova, bene wanyu n’abana banyu bazoba abo kwumvirwa imbabazi imbere y’ababagize imbohe, maze bemererwe kugaruka muri iki gihugu; kuko Yehova Imana yanyu ari umunyempuhwe n’umunyembabazi, kandi ntazobakurako amaso nimwamugarukako.”

10 Nuko nya birutsi barabandanya, bava mu gisagara baja mu kindi mu gihugu cose ca Efurayimu na Manase, egome, gushika muri Zebuloni; mugabo baguma babashinyagurira bongera babatwengera mw’ijigo. 11 Abantu bo muri Asheri na Manase no muri Zebuloni ni bo gusa bicishije bugufi baza i Yeruzalemu. 12 Ukuboko kw’Imana y’ukuri kuba no muri Yuda kugira kubahe umutima umwe ngo bashitse ibwirizwa ry’umwami n’abaganwa ku bijanye n’ibintu vya Yehova.

13 Nuko bakoranira hamwe i Yeruzalemu, ari abantu benshi, kugira bahimbaze umusi mukuru w’udutsima tutarimwo umwambiro mu kwezi kugira kabiri, ari ishengero ryinshi cane. 14 Maze barahaguruka, bakuraho ibicaniro vyari i Yeruzalemu, bakuraho n’ibicaniro vyose vy’imibavu baraheza babiterera mu mwonga Kidironi. 15 Inyuma y’ivyo bakerera imbagwa ya pasika ku musi ugira cumi na kane w’ukwezi kugira kabiri; abaherezi n’Abalewi bari bacishijwe bugufi ku buryo biyeza maze bakazana mu nzu ya Yehova amashikanwa aturirwa. 16 Nuko baguma bahagaze mu kibanza cabo nk’uko ingingo yabo yari, bisunze itegeko rya Musa umuntu w’Imana y’ukuri, abaherezi bakamijagira amaraso bahawe n’Abalewi. 17 Kuko mw’ishengero hari benshi batari biyejeje; kandi Abalewi bari bajejwe gukerera imbagwa za pasika ku bw’abari bahumanye bose, kugira babereze Yehova. 18 Kuko benshi mu bantu, benshi bo muri Efurayimu na Manase na Isakari na Zebuloni, batari bihumanuye, kuko batariye pasika nk’uko vyanditswe; mugabo Hezekiya arabasengera, ati: “Yehova ubwiwe, we mwiza, agirire imbabazi 19 umuntu wese yateguriye umutima wiwe kurondera Imana y’ukuri, Yehova, Imana ya ba sekuruza biwe, naho atoba yityoroye ku bw’ivyeranda.” 20 Nuko Yehova yumviriza Hezekiya, akiza abantu.

21 Bene Isirayeli bari i Yeruzalemu bahimbaza rero umusi mukuru w’udutsima tutarimwo umwambiro mu kiringo c’imisi indwi banezerewe cane; Abalewi n’abaherezi baguma bashemeza Yehova ku musi ku musi bakoresheje ibicurarangisho bivuga cane, egome, bashemeza Yehova. 22 Vyongeye, Hezekiya aremesha Abalewi bose bariko bakorana ukwiyubara kwiza mu gikorwa ca Yehova. Nuko bamara imisi indwi barya ivy’uwo musi mukuru washinzwe, bashikana ibimazi vy’ugusangira bongera bâturira ibicumuro vyabo Yehova Imana ya ba sekuruza babo.

23 Maze ishengero ryose rifata ingingo yo kuwuhimbaza n’iyindi misi ndwi, gutyo bawuhimbaza imisi indwi banezerewe. 24 Kuko Hezekiya umwami wa Yuda ubwiwe yaterereye ku bw’ishengero amashūri igihumbi n’intama ibihumbi indwi, abaganwa na bo bagaterera ku bw’ishengero amashūri igihumbi n’intama ibihumbi cumi; maze abaherezi baguma biyeza ari benshi. 25 Nuko ishengero ryose rya Yuda, n’abaherezi, n’Abalewi, n’ishengero ryose ryari ryavuye muri Isirayeli, na ba kavamahanga bari bavuye mu gihugu ca Isirayeli, n’ababa muri Yuda, babandanya kunezerwa. 26 Haba umunezero mwinshi i Yeruzalemu, kuko kuva mu misi ya Salomo mwene Dawidi umwami wa Isirayeli hatari harigeze kuba ibimeze nk’ivyo i Yeruzalemu. 27 Amaherezo abaherezi, Abalewi, barahaguruka hanyuma bahezagira abantu; nuko Imana yumva ijwi ryabo, ku buryo isengesho ryabo rishika ahantu heranda iba, mu majuru.

^ 2 Ngo. 30:1 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
