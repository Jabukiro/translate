2 Ngoma  33 : 1 - 25

33 Manase yari afise imyaka cumi n’ibiri igihe yatangura kuganza, maze amara imyaka mirongo itanu n’itanu aganza i Yeruzalemu.

2 Atangura gukora ivyari bibi mu maso ya Yehova, yisunze ibintu biteye ishishi vy’amahanga Yehova yari yarirukanye imbere ya bene Isirayeli. 3 Asubira rero kwubaka ibibanza bikirurutse Hezekiya se wiwe yari yarasituriye hasi, kandi ashingira ibicaniro za Bayali yongera akora ibikingi vyeranda; atangura kwunamira ingabo zose zo mu majuru no kuzikorera. 4 Yubaka ibicaniro mu nzu ya Yehova, iyo Yehova yari yaravuze ku biyerekeye ati: “I Yeruzalemu ni ho izina ryanje rizoba gushika igihe kitagira urugero.” 5 Abandanya kwubakira ibicaniro ingabo zose zo mu majuru mu bigo bibiri vy’inzu ya Yehova. 6 Acisha abahungu biwe mu muriro mu mubande wa mwene Hinomu, akora ivy’amageza, agira ivyo kuragura, akora ivy’ubupfumu, ashiraho abavugana n’ibiremwa vy’impwemu be n’abagize umwuga ukubura ibizoba. Akora ku rugero runini ivyari bibi mu maso ya Yehova, kugira ngo amubabaze.

7 Vyongeye, igishusho c’ikibazano yari yarakoze agishira mu nzu y’Imana y’ukuri, iyo Imana yari yarabwiye Dawidi na Salomo umuhungu wiwe ku biyerekeye iti: “Muri iyi nzu be n’i Yeruzalemu, aho natoye mu miryango yose ya Isirayeli, nzohashira izina ryanje gushika igihe kitagira urugero. 8 Kandi sinzokwongera gukura ikirenge ca Isirayeli ku butaka nahaye ba sekuruza babo, bakire gusa kwitwararika gukora ivyo nabategetse vyose ku vyerekeye itegeko ryose n’ingingo nyobozi n’ingingo z’ubutungane vyatanzwe biciye ku kuboko kwa Musa.” 9 Nuko Manase aguma yosha Yuda n’ababa i Yeruzalemu ngo bakore nabi kurusha amahanga Yehova yari yarazimanganije imbere ya bene Isirayeli.

10 Yehova aguma abwira Manase n’abantu biwe, mugabo ntibumviriza. 11 Amaherezo Yehova abateza abakuru b’ingabo z’umwami wa Ashuri; nuko bafatira Manase mu myobo, bamubohesha amapingu abiri y’umujumpu hanyuma bamujana i Babiloni. 12 Ivyo bikimara kumushira mu marushwa, atakambira* Yehova Imana yiwe kandi aguma yicisha bugufi cane imbere y’Imana ya ba sekuruza biwe. 13 Aguma ayisenga, ku buryo Imana yemera ko ayinginga maze yumva ugusaba ubutoni kwiwe, imusubiza i Yeruzalemu ku ngoma yiwe; nuko Manase amenya ko Yehova ari we Mana y’ukuri.

14 Inyuma y’ivyo, yubakira Igisagara ca Dawidi uruhome rw’inyuma, mu burengero bwa Gihoni, mu mwonga no gushika kw’Irembo ry’Amafi, araruzungurutsa gushika i Ofeli; nuko arugira rurerure cane. Vyongeye, ashira abakuru b’inteko za gisirikare mu bisagara vyose bikingijwe ibihome vyo muri Yuda. 15 Atangura gukura imana z’inyamahanga na ca gishusho c’ikigirwamana mu nzu ya Yehova, n’ibicaniro vyose yari yarubatse ku musozi w’inzu ya Yehova n’i Yeruzalemu, araheza ategeka ko babita hanze y’igisagara. 16 N’ikindi kandi, ategura igicaniro ca Yehova maze atangura kugishikanako ibimazi vy’ugusangira n’ibimazi vy’ugukenguruka, hanyuma abwira Yuda gukorera Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli. 17 Ariko abantu bari bagishikana ibimazi ku bibanza bikirurutse; mugabo babishikanira Yehova Imana yabo.

18 Nayo ibisigaye mu vya Manase n’isengesho yatuye Imana yiwe be n’amajambo y’ababonyi baguma bamubwira mw’izina rya Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli, ehe biri mu vy’abami ba Isirayeli. 19 Ivy’isengesho ryiwe, n’ukuntu ukwinginga kwiwe yakwishuwe, n’igicumuro ciwe cose, n’ubuhemu bwiwe be n’ahantu hatandukanye yubatse ibibanza bikirurutse akahashinga ibikingi vyeranda n’ibishusho bibajwe imbere y’uko yicisha bugufi, ehe vyanditswe mu majambo y’ababonyi biwe. 20 Amaherezo Manase aryama hamwe na ba sekuruza biwe, maze bamuhamba iwe; nuko Amoni umuhungu wiwe atangura kuganza mu kibanza ciwe.

21 Amoni yari afise imyaka mirongo ibiri n’ibiri igihe yatangura kuganza, maze amara imyaka ibiri aganza i Yeruzalemu. 22 Atangura gukora ivyari bibi mu maso ya Yehova, nk’uko Manase se wiwe yari yarakoze; Amoni ashikanira ibimazi ibishusho bibajwe vyose Manase se wiwe yari yarakoze, kandi abandanya kubikorera. 23 Ntiyicisha bugufi imbere ya Yehova nk’uko Manase se wiwe yicishije bugufi, kuko Amoni uwo, yatumye ukwagirwa n’icaha kwongerekana. 24 Amaherezo abasuku biwe bamugīra umugambi mubi maze bamwicira mu nzu yiwe. 25 Mugabo abanyagihugu bica abantu bose bari bagīriye Umwami Amoni umugambi mubi, maze abanyagihugu baca bimika Yosiya umuhungu wiwe ngo abe umwami mu kibanza ciwe.

^ 2 Ngo. 33:12 Ijambo ku rindi ni “yorosha mu maso ha.”
