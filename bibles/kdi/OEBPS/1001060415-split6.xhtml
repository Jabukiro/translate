2 Ngoma  6 : 1 - 42

6 Ico gihe ni ho Salomo yavuga ati: “Yehova ubwiwe yavuze ko yobaye mu muzimagiza w’umuzitanya; 2 nanje nakwubakiye inzu y’uburaro buhanitse n’ahantu hashimangiye kugira ngo uhabe gushika igihe kitagira urugero.”

3 Maze umwami arahindukira, atangura guhezagira ishengero rya Isirayeli ryose, mu gihe iryo shengero rya Isirayeli ryose ryari rihagaze. 4 Hanyuma avuga ati: “Hahezagirwe Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli, we yavuganye na Dawidi dawe akoresheje akanwa kiwe, kandi agashitsa n’amaboko yiwe, mu kuvuga ati: 5 ‘Kuva umusi nakura abantu banje mu gihugu ca Misiri, nta gisagara natoye mu miryango yose ya Isirayeli ku bwo kwubaka inzu kugira ngo izina ryanje ribeyo, kandi nta muntu natoye ngo abe indongozi y’abantu banje Isirayeli. 6 Mugabo nzotora Yeruzalemu kugira ngo izina ryanje ribeyo, kandi nzotora Dawidi kugira ngo aganze abantu banje Isirayeli.’ 7 Nuko Dawidi dawe agira icipfuzo mu mutima wiwe co kwubakira inzu izina rya Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli. 8 Mugabo Yehova abwira Dawidi dawe ati: ‘Ko wagize icipfuzo mu mutima wawe co kwubakira inzu izina ryanje, wagize neza, kubera ko ivyo vyari ku mutima wawe. 9 Gusa wewe si we uzokwubaka iyo nzu, ahubwo umuhungu wawe azova muri wewe ni we azokwubakira inzu izina ryanje.’ 10 Nuko Yehova atangura gushitsa ijambo ryiwe yari yaravuze, kugira ngo mpaguruke mu kibanza ca Dawidi dawe maze nicare ku ntebe y’ubwami ya Isirayeli, nk’uko Yehova yari yaravuze, no kugira ngo nubakire inzu izina rya Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli, 11 no kugira ngo ndahashire Isandugu irimwo isezerano rya Yehova, iryo yagiranye na bene Isirayeli.”

12 Nuko Salomo ahagarara imbere y’igicaniro ca Yehova, imbere y’ishengero rya Isirayeli ryose, hanyuma agaranzura ibiganza. 13 (Kuko Salomo yari yarakoze umukiruruko w’umujumpu, akawushira hagati na hagati mu rugo. Uburebure bwawo bwari amatambwe atanu, ubwaguke bwawo amatambwe atanu, amahagarara yawo na yo akaba amatambwe atatu; yari awuhagazeko.) Apfukama rero imbere y’ishengero rya Isirayeli ryose, hanyuma agaranzura ibiganza avyerekeje mu majuru. 14 Maze avuga ati: “Ewe Yehova Mana ya Isirayeli, nta Mana iriho imeze nkawe mu majuru canke kw’isi, wewe ugumya isezerano n’ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo ku bijanye n’abasavyi bawe bagendera imbere yawe n’umutima wabo wose; 15 wewe wagumije ku bijanye n’umusavyi wawe Dawidi dawe ivyo wamusezeraniye, ku buryo utanga umuhango n’akanwa kawe, ugashitsa n’ukuboko kwawe nk’uko biri uyu musi. 16 None rero, ewe Yehova Mana ya Isirayeli, nugumye ku bijanye n’umusavyi wawe Dawidi dawe ivyo wamusezeraniye uti: ‘Imbere yanje ntihazobura umugabo wo mu bawe wo kwicara ku ntebe y’ubwami ya Isirayeli, abahungu bawe nibakira gusa kwitwararika inzira yabo mu kugendera mw’itegeko ryanje, nk’uko wagendeye imbere yanje.’ 17 None rero, ewe Yehova Mana ya Isirayeli, umuhango wahaye umusavyi wawe Dawidi uboneke ko ari uwo kwizigirwa.

18 “Ariko none, Imana izobana vy’ukuri n’abantu kw’isi? Ehe ijuru, egome, ijuru ry’amajuru ubwayo ntirishobora kugukwira; nkanswe rero iyi nzu nubatse! 19 Mugabo nushire umutima kw’isengesho ry’umusavyi wawe n’ugusaba ubutoni kwiwe, ewe Yehova Mana yanje, mu kwumviriza akamo k’ukwinginga n’isengesho umusavyi wawe ariko atura imbere yawe, 20 kugira ngo ugumize amaso yawe kuri iyi nzu umurango n’ijoro, aha hantu wavuze ko woshize izina ryawe, mu kwumviriza isengesho umusavyi wawe atura yerekeje aha hantu. 21 Uze wumvirize ukwinginga kw’umusavyi wawe n’ukw’abantu bawe Isirayeli nibasenga berekeje aha hantu, kugira ngo wewe ubwawe wumve uri ahantu uba, uri mu majuru; uze wumve maze ubabarire.

22 “Umuntu niyacumura kuri mugenziwe, maze uno agashira umuvumo kuri we kugira ngo atume aba uwushobora gushikirwa na nya muvumo, hanyuma akaza ari muri nya muvumo imbere y’igicaniro cawe muri iyi nzu, 23 wewe ubwawe uze wumve rero uri mu majuru, maze ugire ico ukoze, ucire urubanza abasavyi bawe kugira ngo usubirize mu nkoko umubisha, mu gushira ibikorwa vyiwe ku mutwe wiwe, no mu kuvuga ko umugororotsi agororotse mu kumuha nk’uko ubugororotsi bwiwe bwite buri.

24 “Abantu bawe Isirayeli nibatsindwa imbere y’umwansi kubera yuko bazoba bagumye bagucumurako, hanyuma bakagaruka koko, bagahaya izina ryawe, bagasenga kandi bagasaba ubutoni imbere yawe muri iyi nzu, 25 wewe ubwawe uze wumve rero uri mu majuru, maze ubabarire igicumuro c’abantu bawe Isirayeli, ubagarukane ku butaka wabahaye, bo na ba sekuruza babo.

26 “Amajuru niyugarwa ku buryo hataboneka imvura kubera yuko bazoba bagumye bagucumurako, hanyuma bagasenga koko berekeje aha hantu, bagahaya izina ryawe kandi bagahindukira bakava mu gicumuro cabo, kuko uzoba wagumye ubateza amarushwa, 27 wewe ubwawe uze wumve rero uri mu majuru, maze ubabarire igicumuro c’abasavyi bawe, egome, c’abantu bawe Isirayeli, kuko ubigisha ivyerekeye inzira nziza bakwiye kugenderamwo; kandi uze utange imvura ku gihugu cawe wahaye abantu bawe ngo kibabere intoranwa.

28 “Nihatera ikigoyi mu gihugu, nihatera ikiza, nihatera ukubabuka kw’ibirimwa n’ururyi, inzige n’inyenzi; abansi babo nibabugariza mu gihugu c’amarembo yabo—ubwoko ubwo ari bwo bwose bw’icago n’ubwoko ubwo ari bwo bwose bw’indwara— 29 isengesho iryo ari ryo ryose, ugusaba ubutoni ukwo ari kwo kwose kuzoba kuvuye ku muntu uwo ari we wese canke ku bantu bawe Isirayeli bose, kuko umwumwe wese azoba azi icago ciwe n’ububabare bwiwe; niyagaranzura koko ibiganza vyiwe avyerekeje kuri iyi nzu, 30 wewe ubwawe uze wumve rero uri mu majuru, ahantu uba, maze ubabarire kandi uhe umwumwe wese nk’uko inzira ziwe zose ziri, kuko uzi umutima wiwe (kubera ko ari wewe wenyene uzi umutima w’abana b’abantu); 31 kugira ngo bagutinye mu kugendera mu nzira zawe imisi yose bazoba ari bazima ku buringanire bw’ubutaka wahaye ba sogokuruza bacu.

32 “Kandi n’umunyamahanga, atari uwo mu bantu bawe Isirayeli, azoza koko avuye mu gihugu ca kure kubera izina ryawe rihambaye n’ikiganza cawe gikomeye be n’ukuboko kwawe kuramvuye, akaza koko gusenga yerekeje kuri iyi nzu, 33 wewe ubwawe uze rero wumvirize uri mu majuru, uri ahantu hashimangiye uba, maze ukore wisunze ivyo uwo munyamahanga azoba aguhamagariye vyose; kugira ngo ibisata vyo kw’isi vyose bimenye izina ryawe kandi bigutinye, nk’uko igisata cawe Isirayeli kigutinya, kandi bimenye yuko izina ryawe ryambarijwe kuri iyi nzu nubatse.

34 “Abantu bawe nibasohoka bakaja mu ntambara kurwanya abansi babo mu nzira uzobarungikamwo, maze bakagusenga vy’ukuri berekeje kuri iki gisagara watoye no kuri iyi nzu nubakiye izina ryawe, 35 uze rero wumve uri mu majuru isengesho ryabo n’ugusaba ubutoni kwabo, maze ushitse urubanza ku bwabo.

36 “Nibagucumurako (kuko ata muntu adacumura), maze bikaba ngombwa ko ubarurumbira ishavu, ukabagabura ku mwansi, hanyuma bene kubagira imbohe bakabatwara koko ari imbohe mu gihugu ca kure canke ca hafi; 37 maze bakagarura koko ubwenge mu gihugu bazoba batwawemwo ari imbohe, hanyuma bakagaruka koko kuri wewe, bakagusaba ubutoni mu gihugu bazoba barimwo ari imbohe, mu kuvuga bati: ‘Twaracumuye, twarahuvye kandi twakoze ivy’ububisha’; 38 bakagaruka koko kuri wewe n’umutima wabo wose n’ubuzima bwabo bwose mu gihugu c’ababatwaye ari imbohe, ico bazoba barimwo ari imbohe, maze bagasenga koko berekeje mu gihugu cabo wahaye ba sekuruza babo n’igisagara watoye n’iyi nzu nubakiye izina ryawe; 39 uze rero wumve uri mu majuru, uri ahantu hashimangiye uba, isengesho ryabo n’ugusaba ubutoni kwabo, maze ushitse urubanza ku bwabo wongere ubabarire abantu bawe bazoba bagucumuyeko.

40 “Ubu rero, ewe Mana yanje, ndakwinginze amaso yawe abe ayuguruye, n’amatwi yawe abe ayumviriza isengesho rijanye n’aha hantu. 41 None rero nuhaguruke, ewe Yehova Mana, kugira ngo winjire mu karuhuko kawe, wewe n’Isandugu y’inkomezi zawe. Abaherezi bawe, ewe Yehova Mana, nibambare ubukiriro, kandi intahemuka zawe zinezererwe ineza. 42 Ewe Yehova Mana, ntiwamirire kure mu maso h’uwo warobanuje amavuta*. Ese wokwibuka ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo wagiriye Dawidi umusavyi wawe!”

^ 2 Ngo. 6:42 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
