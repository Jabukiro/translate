Ezira  5 : 1 - 17

5 Nuko Hagayi umuhanuzi na Zekariya umwuzukuru wa Ido umuhanuzi bavuga ubuhanuzi mw’izina ry’Imana ya Isirayeli yari hejuru yabo, babwira Abayuda bari muri Yuda n’i Yeruzalemu. 2 Ico gihe ni ho Zerubabeli mwene Sheyalutiyeli na Yeshuwa mwene Yehozadaki bahaguruka bagatangura gusubira kwubaka inzu y’Imana yari i Yeruzalemu; bari kumwe n’abahanuzi b’Imana babafasha. 3 Ico gihe Tatenayi buramatari wo hakurya ya rwa Ruzi na Shetari-bozenayi na bagenzi babo baza kuri bo, bababwira bati: “Ni nde yabahaye itegeko ryo kwubaka iyo nzu no kurangiza ico kintu cubakishijwe ibigiga?” 4 Maze bababwira bati: “Amazina y’abagabo bariko bubaka iyi nyubakwa ni ayahe?” 5 Kandi ijisho ry’Imana yabo ryari ku bagabo b’inararibonye b’Abayuda, ntibabahagarika gushika bimenyeshejwe Dariyo maze urwandiko rwemewe rujanye n’ico kintu rukagaruka.

6 Ng’iyi ikopi y’ikete Tatenayi buramatari wo hakurya ya rwa Ruzi na Shetari-bozenayi na bagenziwe, ba buramatari batobato bari hakurya ya rwa Ruzi, barungikiye Dariyo umwami; 7 bamurungikira ubutumwa, muri bwo hakaba hari handitswe ngo:

“Kuri Dariyo umwami:

“Gira amahoro! 8 Umwami namenye yuko twagiye mu ntara y’ubutware ya Yuda, ku nzu ya ya Mana ihambaye; iriko yubakishwa amabuye atembagazwa* gushika mu kibanza cayo, kandi n’ibiti biriko birashirwa mu mpome; ico gikorwa bariko baragikora babishashaye kandi kiriko kiratera imbere mu maboko yabo. 9 Twarabajije rero abo bagabo b’inararibonye. Ibi ni vyo twababwiye: ‘Ni nde yabahaye itegeko ryo kwubaka iyo nzu no kurangiza ico kintu cubakishijwe ibigiga?’ 10 Vyongeye, twarababajije amazina yabo ngo tuyakumenyeshe, kugira ngo twandike amazina y’abagabo babarongōye.

11 “Ibi rero ni vyo batwishuye: ‘Turi abasavyi b’Imana y’amajuru n’isi, kandi turiko dusubira kwubaka inzu yari yarubatswe kera, ubu hakaba haciye imyaka myinshi, iyo umwami ahambaye wa Isirayeli yubatse akayirangiza. 12 Ariko kubera yuko ba data bateye ishavu Imana y’amajuru, yarabatanze mu kuboko kwa Nebukadinezari umwami wa Babiloni Umukaludaya, maze uno abomora iyi nzu yongera atwara abantu mu bunyagano i Babiloni. 13 Yamara rero, mu mwaka wa mbere wa Kuro umwami wa Babiloni, Kuro umwami yaratanze itegeko ryo gusubira kwubaka iyi nzu y’Imana. 14 Vyongeye, ivyombo vy’inzahabu n’ifeza vyo mu nzu y’Imana Nebukadinezari yari yarakuye mu rusengero rwari i Yeruzalemu akabijana mu rusengero rw’i Babiloni, ivyo vyombo Kuro umwami yarabisohoye mu rusengero rw’i Babiloni, maze bihabwa Sheshibazari, rino rikaba ari izina ry’uwo yagize buramatari. 15 Hanyuma amubwira ati: “Tora ibi vyombo. Genda ubishire mu rusengero ruri i Yeruzalemu, kandi inzu y’Imana isubire kwubakwa mu kibanza cayo.” 16 Igihe uwo Sheshibazari yaza, yarashinze imishinge y’inzu y’Imana yari i Yeruzalemu; kuva ico gihe gushika ubu, iriko isubira kwubakwa, mugabo ntirarangira.’

17 “Nuko rero, nimba umwami abona ko ari vyiza, nihagirwe itohoza mu nzu y’amatunga y’umwami iri ng’aho i Babiloni, barabe ko vyoba ari ivy’ukuri yuko hari itegeko ryatanzwe na Kuro umwami ryo gusubira kwubaka iyo nzu y’Imana i Yeruzalemu; maze ingingo y’umwami ijanye n’ico kintu ayiturungikire.”

^ Ezr. 5:8 Ni ukuvuga, amabuye aremereye cane ku buryo ata woshobora kuyatwara.
