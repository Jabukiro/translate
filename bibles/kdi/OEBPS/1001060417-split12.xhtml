Nehemiya  12 : 1 - 47

12 Aba ni bo baherezi n’Abalewi baduganye na Zerubabeli mwene Sheyalutiyeli be na Yeshuwa: Seraya, Yeremiya, Ezira, 2 Amariya, Maluku, Hatushi, 3 Shekaniya, Rehumu, Meremoti, 4 Ido, Ginetoyi, Abiya, 5 Miyamini, Mayadiya, Biliga, 6 Shemaya, Yoyaribu, Yedaya, 7 Salu, Amoki, Hilikiya, Yedaya. Abo ni bo bari abakuru b’abaherezi na bene wabo, mu misi ya Yeshuwa.

8 Abalewi bari Yeshuwa, Binuwi, Kadumiyeli, Sherebiya, Yuda, Mataniya, uno akaba yari ahagarikiye ivy’ugukenguruka, we na bene wabo. 9 Bakibukiya na Uni bene wabo bari babangabanganye na bo ku bijanye n’ibikorwa vy’ukurinda. 10 Yeshuwa yavyaye Yoyakimu, Yoyakimu avyara Eliyashibu, Eliyashibu na we avyara Yoyada. 11 Yoyada yavyaye Yonatani, Yonatani na we avyara Yaduwa.

12 Mu misi ya Yoyakimu hariho abaherezi, abakuru b’amazu ya ba sekuru, ari bo b’aba: ku bwa Seraya yari Meraya; ku bwa Yeremiya yari Hananiya; 13 ku bwa Ezira yari Meshulamu; ku bwa Amariya yari Yehohanani; 14 ku bwa Maluki yari Yonatani; ku bwa Shebaniya yari Yozefu; 15 ku bwa Harimu yari Aduna; ku bwa Merayoti yari Helikayi; 16 ku bwa Ido yari Zekariya; ku bwa Ginetoni yari Meshulamu; 17 ku bwa Abiya yari Zikiri; ku bwa Miniyamini yari——*; ku bwa Mowadiya yari Pilitayi; 18 ku bwa Biliga yari Shamuwa; ku bwa Shemaya yari Yehonatani; 19 ku bwa Yoyaribu yari Matenayi; ku bwa Yedaya yari Uzi; 20 ku bwa Salayi yari Kalayi; ku bwa Amoki yari Eberi; 21 ku bwa Hilikiya yari Hashabiya; ku bwa Yedaya yari Netaneli.

22 Mu misi ya Eliyashibu na Yoyada na Yohanani na Yaduwa, Abalewi banditswe bwa bakuru b’amazu ya ba sekuru, n’abaherezi nyene, gushika ku ngoma ya Dariyo Umuperesi.

23 Bene Lewi, bwa bakuru b’amazu ya ba sekuru, banditswe mu gitabu c’ivyo muri ivyo bihe, egome, gushika mu misi ya Yohanani mwene Eliyashibu. 24 Abakuru b’Abalewi bari Hashabiya, Sherebiya na Yeshuwa mwene Kadumiyeli na bene wabo babangabanganye na bo mu gushemeza Imana no kuyikengurukira bisunze ibwirizwa rya Dawidi umuntu w’Imana y’ukuri, umugwi w’abarinzi ugahwana n’uwundi. 25 Mataniya na Bakibukiya na Obadiya na Meshulamu na Talimoni na Akubu bacungera ari abarinzi b’amarembo, ukaba wari umugwi w’abarinzi wakorera hafi y’ububiko bwo ku marembo. 26 Abo bariho mu misi ya Yoyakimu mwene Yeshuwa, Yeshuwa mwene Yozadaki no mu misi ya Nehemiya buramatari na Ezira umuherezi, umwimuzi.

27 Mu gihe co gushikiriza uruhome rwa Yeruzalemu, barondera Abalewi ngo babazane i Yeruzalemu babakuye aho baba hose, kugira ngo hagirwe ishikiriza habe n’umunezero, egome, biherekejwe n’ugukenguruka n’ururirimbo, be n’ukuvuza ivyuma bisāma n’ibicurarangisho vy’imirya n’inanga. 28 Nuko abahungu b’abaririmvyi bakoranira hamwe bavuye mu Ntara, bavuye mu micungararo ya Yeruzalemu no mu mihana irundanye y’Abanyanetofa, 29 n’i Beti-gilugali no mu turere twa Geba na Azimaveti, kuko abaririmvyi bari bariyubakiye mu micungararo ya Yeruzalemu imihana irundanye. 30 Nuko abaherezi n’Abalewi barihumanura hanyuma bahumanura abantu n’amarembo n’uruhome.

31 Maze nuriza abaganwa ba Yuda ku ruhome. Vyongeye, nshiraho imigwi ibiri mininiminini y’abaririmvyi y’ugukenguruka hamwe n’imigwi yo kubakurikira, umwe ukaba wagendera iburyo ku ruhome ugana kw’Irembo ry’Ibirundo vy’Umunyota. 32 Nuko Hoshaya n’igice c’abaganwa ba Yuda batangura kugendera inyuma yabo, 33 na Azariya na Ezira na Meshulamu, 34 na Yuda na Benyamini na Shemaya na Yeremiya; 35 be n’abo mu bahungu b’abaherezi bafise inzumbete, ari bo Zekariya mwene Yonatani, Yonatani mwene Shemaya, Shemaya mwene Mataniya, Mataniya mwene Mikaya, Mikaya mwene Zakuri, Zakuri mwene Asafu, 36 na bene wabo ari bo Shemaya na Azareli na Milalayi na Gilalayi na Mayayi na Netaneli na Yuda na Hanani, bafise ibicurarangisho vy’ururirimbo vya Dawidi umuntu w’Imana y’ukuri; Ezira umwimuzi yari imbere yabo. 37 Kw’Irembo ryo kw’Isôko, batumbereye imbere yabo, baduga ku Nzira y’Ingazi y’Igisagara ca Dawidi, baciye ku mudugo w’uruhome hejuru y’Inzu ya Dawidi bashika kw’Irembo ry’Amazi, amaja mu buseruko.

38 Uwundi mugwi w’abaririmvyi wo gukengurukira Imana wariko ugendera imbere, nanje ndi inyuma yawo, be n’ica kabiri c’abantu, ku ruhome, hejuru ku Munara w’Amafuru y’Ukwotsa, baragenda gushika ku Ruhome Rwagutse, 39 no hejuru y’Irembo rya Efurayimu no gushika kw’Irembo ry’Igisagara ca Kera, no gushika kw’Irembo ry’Amafi no ku Munara wa Hananeli no ku Munara wa Meya, gushika kw’Irembo ry’Intama; bahagarara kw’Irembo ry’Abarinzi.

40 Amaherezo iyo migwi ibiri y’abaririmvyi yo gukengurukira Imana ihagarara ku nzu y’Imana y’ukuri, na jewe nyene ndi kumwe n’ica kabiri c’ivyegera vy’abatware, 41 n’abaherezi Eliyakimu, Mayaseya, Miniyamini, Mikaya, Eliyowenayi, Zekariya, Hananiya bafise inzumbete, 42 na Mayaseya na Shemaya, na Eleyazari na Uzi na Yehohanani na Malikiya na Elamu na Ezeri. Kandi abaririmvyi bari kumwe na Izirahiya umucungezi baguma baririmba n’ijwi ryumvikana.

43 Nuko kuri uwo musi bashikana ibimazi vyinshi kandi baranezerwa, kuko Imana y’ukuri ubwayo yatuma banezerwa akanyamuneza kenshi. N’abagore be n’abana baranezerwa, ku buryo umunezero wa Yeruzalemu wumvikana kure.

44 Vyongeye, kuri uwo musi hagenwa abagabo bajejwe ivyumba bigenewe ububiko, intererano, imishuzo, n’ivy’icumi, kugira ngo bavyegeranirizemwo imigabane ivuye mu turere tw’ibisagara, iyo itegeko ryasaba guha abaherezi n’Abalewi; kuko Yuda yari yanezerejwe n’uko abaherezi n’Abalewi bari ku gikorwa. 45 Nuko batangura kwitwararika ico basabwa n’Imana yabo be n’ico basabwa ku bijanye n’ityorora, be n’abaririmvyi n’abarinzi b’amarembo, hakurikijwe ibwirizwa rya Dawidi n’irya Salomo umuhungu wiwe. 46 Kuko mu misi ya Dawidi na Asafu, mu gihe ca kera cane, hariho abakuru b’abaririmvyi n’ururirimbo rwo gushemeza Imana no kuyikengurukira. 47 Kandi Isirayeli yose, mu misi ya Zerubabeli no mu misi ya Nehemiya, yaratanga imigabane y’abaririmvyi n’iy’abarinzi b’amarembo hisunzwe ivyo bakenera ku musi ku musi maze ikerezwa Abalewi; Abalewi na bo barayereza bene Aroni.

^ Neh. 12:17 Biboneka yuko mu gisomwa c’igiheburayo ata zina rihari.
