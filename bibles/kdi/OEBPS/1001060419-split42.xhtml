Yobu  42 : 1 - 17

42 Maze Yobu arishura Yehova, ati:

 2 “Ndamenye ko ushobora gukora vyose,

Kandi nta ciyumviro utoshobora gushikako.

 3 ‘Uyo ni nde apfukiranya impanuro ata bumenyi?’

Erega naravuze, ariko ntategera

Ibintu vy’agatangaza bindengeye ntazi.

 4 ‘Enda niwumvirize, nanje mvuge.

Ndakubaza, nawe umbwire.’

 5 Numvise ivyawe ku matwi,

Ariko ubu ijisho ryanje rirakubonye koko.

 6 Ni co gituma nisubiyeko,

Nkigaya koko ndi mu mukungugu no mu minyota.”

7 Yehova amaze kubwira Yobu ayo majambo, Yehova abwira Elifazi Umutemani ati:

“Ishavu ryanje rirakururumbiye wewe na bagenzawe babiri, kuko mutamvuzeko ivy’ukuri nk’uko umusavyi wanje Yobu yagize. 8 Ubu rero nimwitorere amashūri indwi n’impfizi z’intama indwi muheze muje ku musavyi wanje Yobu, mushikane ku bwanyu ikimazi giturirwa; kandi Yobu umusavyi wanje ubwiwe arabasabira. Ni we gusa nza kwemera kugira sindabakorere ubusazi bw’agaterasoni, kuko mutamvuzeko ivy’ukuri, nk’uko umusavyi wanje Yobu yagize.”

9 Elifazi Umutemani na Biludadi Umushuhi na Zofari Umunayamati baraheza baragenda, bakora nk’uko nyene Yehova yari yababwiye; nuko Yehova aremera ugutakamba* kwa Yobu.

10 Yehova abohora Yobu mu mvuto igihe yasabira bagenziwe, maze Yehova yongera guha Yobu ivyari ivyiwe, abigwiza kabiri. 11 Hanyuma bene wabo bose na bashikiwe bose n’abari bamuzi bose baguma baza, batangura gusangira na we umukate mu nzu yiwe no gusangira na we umubabaro no kumuhoza ku vyago vyose Yehova yari yaretse bikamuzako; umwe wese amuha ihera, kandi umwe wese amuha impeta y’inzahabu.

12 Nayo Yehova ahezagira iherezo rya Yobu mu nyuma kuruta intango yiwe, ku buryo agira intama ibihumbi cumi na bine, n’ingamiya ibihumbi bitandatu, n’inka ibihumbi bibiri zifatanijwe ari zibirizibiri, be n’indogobwa igihumbi z’ingore. 13 Agira n’abahungu indwi n’abakobwa batatu. 14 Umukobwa wa mbere amwita Yemima, uwa kabiri amwita Keziya, uwa gatatu na we amwita Kereni-hapuki. 15 Kandi nta bagore beza nk’abakobwa ba Yobu baboneka mu gihugu cose. Maze se abaha intoranwa muri basaza babo.

16 Inyuma y’ivyo Yobu abandanya kubaho imyaka ijana na mirongo ine, arabona abahungu biwe n’abuzukuru biwe: amayaruka ane. 17 Yobu arateba arapfa, yisaziye kandi ahaze imisi.

^ Yobu 42:9 Ijambo ku rindi ni “mu maso ha.”
