Zaburi  102 : 1 - 28

Isengesho ry’umurushwa mu gihe yaregeye, agasuka amaganya yiwe imbere ya Yehova ubwiwe.

102 Ewe Yehova, niwumve isengesho ryanje;

Kandi ugutabaza kwanje nikugushikire.

 2 Ntumpishe mu maso hawe ku musi ngeze habi.

Ntega ugutwi;

Ku musi mpamagaye, nunyaruke, unyishure.

 3 Kuko imisi yanje irangiye nk’umwotsi,

N’amagufa yanje akaba arunguriye nk’iziko.

 4 Umutima wanje ukubiswe nk’ivyatsi kandi urumye,

Kuko nibagiye gufungura udufungurwa twanje.

 5 Kubera ijwi ry’ukuniha kwanje,

Amagufa yanje amatanye n’inyama zanje.

 6 Nsa n’ikinyoni ntamayuruzi co mu gahinga.

Ncitse nk’agahuna gatoyi k’ahantu hagizwe umusaka.

 7 Ndanamvye;

Mbaye nk’akanyoni kari ukwa konyene ku gisenge.

 8 Abansi banje bansize iceyi burinda bwira.

Abankengereza ni jewe barahiye.

 9 Kuko nariye iminyota nk’umukate;

Kandi ivyo nywa nabivanze n’amarira,

10 Kubera imisibo yawe n’uburakari bwawe;

Kuko wandugije hejuru, kugira ngo unte kure.

11 Imisi yanje ni nk’igitutu kiyonze,

Kandi jewe numye nk’ivyatsi.

12 Nayo wewe, ewe Yehova, uzobaho gushika igihe kitagira urugero,

Kandi icibutso cawe kizokwamaho uko iyaruka rikurikira irindi.

13 Wewe ubwawe uzohaguruka, uzogirira imbabazi Siyoni,

Kuko ari co kiringo co kuyigirira ivyiza,

Kuko igihe cashinzwe gishitse.

14 Kuko abasavyi bawe bahimbawe n’amabuye yaho,

Kandi ubutoni bwabo babwerekeza ku mukungugu waho.

15 Amahanga azotinya izina rya Yehova,

Abami bose bo mw’isi na bo batinye ubuninahazwa bwawe.

16 Kuko Yehova atazobura kwubaka Siyoni;

Emwe, azoseruka mu buninahazwa bwiwe.

17 Ntazobura guhindukira ngo yumve isengesho ry’abakobwe ibintu vyose,

Kandi ntazosuzugura isengesho ryabo.

18 Ibi vyandikiwe iyaruka rizoza;

Kandi igisata c’abantu kizoremwa kizoshemeza Ya*.

19 Kuko yaravye hasi ari iyo hejuru hiwe heranda,

Yehova ubwiwe yaravye isi ari mu majuru,

20 Kugira ngo yumve ukuniha kw’imbohe,

Kugira ngo abohore abagenewe urupfu;

21 Kugira ngo izina rya Yehova rimenyeshwe i Siyoni,

Ishemezo ryiwe na ryo rimenyeshwe i Yeruzalemu,

22 Ibisata vy’abantu nivyakoranirizwa hamwe vyose,

Be n’ubwami, kugira bikorere Yehova.

23 Ku nzira, yaruhije inkomezi zanje,

Yagabanije imisi yanje.

24 Naciye mvuga nti: “Ewe Mana yanje,

Ntunkureho ncagatije imisi yanje;

Imyaka yawe yamaho mu mayaruka yose.

25 Kera washinze imishinge y’isi,

Kandi amajuru ni igikorwa c’amaboko yawe.

26 Vyo bizoyogera, mugabo wewe uzogumaho;

Bizoshira vyose nka kurya kw’impuzu.

Nka kurya kw’icambarwa, uzobisubiriza, bimare ikiringo cavyo.

27 Ariko wewe wama uri wa wundi, kandi imyaka yawe ntizohera.

28 Abana b’abasavyi bawe bazobandanya kwiberaho;

Kandi imbere yawe, uruvyaro rwabo ruzoshimangirwa.”

^ Zab. 102:18 “Ya” ni impfunyapfunyo y’izina “Yehova.”
