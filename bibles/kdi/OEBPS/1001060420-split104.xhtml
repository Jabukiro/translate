Zaburi  104 : 1 - 35

104 Hezagira Yehova, mutima wanje!

Ewe Yehova Mana yanje, wabonetse ko uhambaye cane.

Wiyambika iteka n’ubwiza bwakaka,

 2 Witwikira umuco nk’impuzu,

Uramvura amajuru nk’igitambara c’ihema,

 3 Ukaba Umwe yubaka mu mazi ivyumba vyiwe vyo hejuru akoresheje ibigiga,

Akagira ibicu umukogote wiwe,

Akagendera ku mababa y’umuyaga,

 4 Akagira abamarayika biwe impwemu,

Abasuku biwe akabagira umuriro urigata.

 5 Yashinze isi ku bibanza vyayo bishimangiye;

Ntizogenda inyiganyijwe gushika igihe kitagira urugero, emwe gushika ibihe bidahera.

 6 Wayipfutse inyenga y’amazi nk’impuzu.

Amazi yareremba hejuru y’imisozi.

 7 Warahambaye atangura guhunga;

Yumvise ijwi ry’umuturagaro wawe, yiruka yataye umutwe,

 8 Imisozi itangura kuduga,

Ibiyaya vy’imibande bitangura kumanuka,

Yiruka aja mu kibanza wayashingiye.

 9 Washinze urubibe adakwiye kurenga,

Ngo ntiyongere gupfuka isi.

10 Arungika imbizi mu myonga;

Ziguma zitemba hagati y’imisozi.

11 Ntizihengeshanya kunywesha ibikoko vyose vyo mu gahinga;

Imparage zama zîzimya inyota.

12 Hejuru yazo ni ho ibiremwa biguruka vyo mu kirere vyikinga;

Biguma bijwiragirira mu mababi azitanye.

13 Anywesha imisozi ari mu vyumba vyiwe vyo hejuru.

Isi ihazwa n’ivyamwa vy’ibikorwa vyawe.

14 Amereza ibikoko ivyatsi bitoto,

N’ibimera ngo abantu bavyikoreko,

Ngo atume ibifungurwa bisokoroka biva mw’ivu,

15 N’umuvinyu unezereza umutima w’umuntu buntu,

Ngo akayanganishe mu maso amavuta,

N’umukate utunga umutima w’umuntu apfa.

16 Ibiti vya Yehova birahaga,

Amasederi y’i Libani yateragiye,

17 Aho inyoni ubwazo zarika.

Nayo agasoza, ibiti vy’imiberoshi ni vyo nzu yako.

18 Imisozi miremire ni iy’impene zo mu misozi;

Ibibuye vy’urutare ni ubuhungiro bw’inkezi zo mu bitandara.

19 Yakoze ukwezi ku bw’ibihe vyashinzwe;

Izuba ubwaryo rirazi neza iyo rirengera.

20 Uzana umwiza, ngo habe mw’ijoro;

Ibikoko vyo mu kibira vyose bigendagenda muri ryo.

21 Intambwe z’imikangara z’imigāra ziroha zirondera ico zicakira,

No kugira zirondere ku Mana ico zirya.

22 Izuba ritangura kwaka, bikigira,

Bikiryamira mu bwikingo bwavyo.

23 Umuntu arasohoka akaja ku murimo wiwe,

Akaja ku gikorwa ciwe gushika ku mugoroba.

24 Ewe kuntu ibikorwa vyawe ari vyinshi, Yehova we!

Vyose wabikoranye ubukerebutsi.

Isi yuzuye ivyo wakoze.

25 Nayo iki kiyaga amahanga kandi kigereza kure gutyo,

Muri co hariyo ibigendagenda bitagira igitigiri,

Ibinyabuzima, bitobito na bininibinini.

26 Aho ni ho imeri zigendagenda;

Nayo Lewiyatani, wayibumvye ngo yiginagure muri co.

27 Vyose, biguma bikurindiriye

Ngo ubihe indya zavyo mu gihe cazo.

28 Ico ubihaye biratora.

Upfumbaturura ikiganza cawe, bigahaga ivyiza.

29 Uhishije mu maso hawe, bihagarika umutima.

Utwaye impwemu yavyo, biracikana,

Bigasubira mu mukungugu wavyo.

30 Urungitse impwemu yawe, biraremwa;

Ku buringanire bw’ubutaka uhagira hashasha.

31 Ubuninahazwa bwa Yehova buzokwamaho gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

Yehova azonezererwa ibikorwa vyiwe.

32 Araba isi, ikajugumira;

Akora ku misozi, igafumba umwotsi.

33 Nzoririmbira Yehova ubuzima bwanje bwose;

Nzohibongoreza Imana yanje igihe cose nzoba nkiriho.

34 Ukuzirikana ibiyerekeye kwanje nikube akaryoshe.

Nayo jewe, nzonezerwa muri Yehova.

35 Abacumuzi bazoherengetezwa kw’isi;

Nayo ababisha, ntibazokwongera kubaho.

Hezagira Yehova, mutima wanje! Shemeze Ya!
