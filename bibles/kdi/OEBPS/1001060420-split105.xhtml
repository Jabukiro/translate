Zaburi  105 : 1 - 45

105 Nimukengurukire Yehova, mwambaze izina ryiwe,

Mumenyeshe mu bisata vy’abantu ivyo akora.

 2 Nimumuririmbire, mumuhibongoreze,

Mwitwararike ibikorwa vy’agatangaza vyiwe vyose.

 3 Nimwiratire izina ryiwe ryeranda.

Umutima w’abarondera Yehova nunezerwe.

 4 Nimurondere Yehova be n’inkomezi ziwe.

Nimurondere mu maso hiwe nantaryo.

 5 Nimwibuke ibikorwa vyiwe vy’agatangaza yaranguye,

Ibitangaro vyiwe n’ingingo z’ubutungane z’akanwa kiwe,

 6 Yemwe ruvyaro rwa Aburahamu umusavyi wiwe,

Mwebwe bene Yakobo, abo yatoye!

 7 Ni we Yehova Imana yacu.

Ingingo ziwe z’ubutungane ziri mw’isi yose.

 8 Yaributse isezerano ryiwe, egome, gushika igihe kitagira urugero,

Ijambo yategetse, gushika ku mayaruka igihumbi,

 9 Isezerano yagiranye na Aburahamu,

N’indahiro yarahiye Izahaki,

10 Iyo yagumijeho bwa ngingo nyobozi kuri Yakobo,

Bwa sezerano ryamaho igihe kitagira urugero kuri Isirayeli,

11 Akavuga ati: “Nzoguha igihugu ca Kanani

Kibe wo mugabane w’intoranwa yanyu.”

12 Aho hari igihe bari imparurwa,

Emwe, bake cane, bakaba na ba kavamahanga muri co.

13 Kandi baguma bagendagenda bava mw’ihanga baja mu rindi,

Bava mu bwami bumwe bagana ikindi gisata.

14 Ntiyemeye ko hagira umuntu n’umwe abagunga,

Mugabo kubera bo, yarakangiriye abami,

15 Ati: “Ntimukore ku bo narobanuje amavuta,

Kandi ntihagire ikintu kibi na kimwe mugirira abahanuzi banje.”

16 Nuko ahamagarira ikigoyi ku gihugu;

Avuna inkoni yose yatungirwako imikate imeze nk’ikigōmbo.

17 Arungika imbere yabo umuntu

Yagurishijwe ngo abe umushumba, ari we Yozefu.

18 Bababarisha amapingu ibirenge vyiwe,

Ubuzima bwiwe bwinjira mu vyuma;

19 Gushika aho ijambo ryiwe riziye,

Ijambo rya Yehova ubwaryo riramutyorora.

20 Umwami atuma umuntu kugira amubohore,

Umutware w’ibisata vy’abantu arabikora, kugira amurekure.

21 Amugira shebuja w’urugo rwiwe

N’umutware aganza ivyiwe vyose,

22 Kugira agabe abaganwa biwe uko umutima wiwe ugomba

No kugira ngo yigishe ubukerebutsi abahumure biwe.

23 Hanyuma Isirayeli araza mu Misiri,

Yakobo ubwiwe abaho ari kavamahanga mu gihugu ca Hamu.

24 Aguma atuma abantu biwe barondoka cane,

Amaherezo abaha kugira inkomezi kuruta abarwanizi babo.

25 Areka umutima wabo urahinduka ngo wanke abantu biwe,

Ngo bagire ivy’urwenge ku basavyi biwe.

26 Atuma Musa umusavyi wiwe,

Atuma Aroni, uwo yari yaratoye.

27 Bashira hagati yabo ibintu vy’ibimenyetso vyiwe,

N’ibitangaro mu gihugu ca Hamu.

28 Arungika umwiza, atuma hijima;

Kandi ntibagararije amajambo yiwe.

29 Ahindura amazi yabo amaraso,

Yica amafi yabo.

30 Igihugu cabo kinyagaramwo ibikere,

Mu vyumba vy’abami babo.

31 Avuga ngo ibibugu bize,

Imibu mu turere twabo twose.

32 Imvura zabo z’isegenya azigira urubura,

Umuriro ururumba ku gihugu cabo.

33 Akubita imizabibu yabo n’ibiti vyabo vy’imisukoni,

Avunagura n’ibiti vyo mu karere kabo.

34 Avuga ngo inzige zize,

N’inzige zikiri nto, emwe, zitagira igitigiri.

35 Zirya ibimera vyose mu gihugu cabo;

Zirya n’umwimbu w’ubutaka bwabo.

36 Hanyuma yica zina mfura mu gihugu cabo,

Ubuzibūra bwabo* bwose.

37 Atangura kubasohorana ifeza n’inzahabu;

Kandi mu miryāngo yiwe nta n’umwe yagenda aratsitara.

38 Misiri yaranezerewe basohotse,

Kuko ukubagirira ubwoba kwari kwabatekeye.

39 Asanza igicu ngo kibe igikingirizo,

N’umuriro wo gutanga umuco mw’ijoro.

40 Barasaba, azana inkware,

Aguma abahaza umukate uva mw’ijuru.

41 Yugurura igitandara, amazi atangura gutemba;

Ano agenda aca mu turere tugadutse, atemba nk’uruzi.

42 Kuko yibutse ijambo ryiwe ryeranda yavuganye na Aburahamu umusavyi wiwe.

43 Nuko asohora abantu biwe n’umwigino,

Abo yatoye abasohorana akamo k’umunezero.

44 Amaherezo abaha ibihugu vy’amahanga,

Baguma bigarurira ibiva mu gikorwa c’ubutame c’imiryango y’amahanga,

45 Kugira ngo bagumye ingingo nyobozi ziwe,

Bubahirize amategeko yiwe.

Shemeze Ya!

^ Zab. 105:36 Ijambo ku rindi ni “Intango y’ububasha bwabo bwo kurondoka.”
