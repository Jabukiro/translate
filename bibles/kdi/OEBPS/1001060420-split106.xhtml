Zaburi  106 : 1 - 48

106 Shemeze Ya!

Nimukengurukire Yehova, kuko ari mwiza;

Kuko ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwiwe ari ubwo gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

 2 Ni nde ashoboye kuvuga ibikorwa vy’ubushobozi vya Yehova,

Canke agashobora kwumvikanisha ishemezo ryiwe ryose?

 3 Hahiriwe abubahiriza ubutungane,

Bagakora ivy’ubugororotsi igihe cose.

 4 Nunyibuke, ewe Yehova, unyibukishe bwa butoni ugirira abantu bawe.

Nunyitwararikishe ubukiriro bwawe,

 5 Kugira ngo mbone ineza igirirwa abo watoye,

Kugira ngo nezerwe umunezero w’ihanga ryawe,

Kugira ngo niratane n’intoranwa yawe.

 6 Twaracumuye cokimwe na ba sogokuruza bacu;

Twaragize nabi; twakoze ivy’ububisha.

 7 Nayo ba sogokuruza bacu mu Misiri,

Nta gutahura kwimbitse na gukeyi berekanye ku bijanye n’ibikorwa vy’agatangaza vyawe.

Ntibibutse umurengera w’ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwawe buhambaye,

Ahubwo baragararije ku kiyaga, kuri ca kiyaga Gitukura.

 8 Hanyuma arabakiza ku bw’izina ryiwe,

Kugira atume ubushobozi bwiwe bumenyekana.

 9 Nuko ahambarira ikiyaga Gitukura, kirakama;

Abacisha mu nyenga y’amazi nko mu gahinga;

10 Nuko abakiza ukuboko kw’umwankanyi,

Abahabuza mu kuboko kw’umwansi.

11 Amazi atangura gupfuka abarwanizi babo;

Ntihasigara n’umwe.

12 Maze bizera ijambo ryiwe;

Batangura kuririmba ishemezo ryiwe.

13 Bibagira ningoga ibikorwa vyiwe;

Ntibarindira impanuro yiwe.

14 Mugabo berekana icipfuzo cabo c’ubwikunzi mu gahinga,

Bagerageza Imana mu bugaragwa.

15 Na yo ibaha ivyo basavye,

Mugabo ibarungikamwo indwara y’ubugesera.

16 Batangura kugirira agashari Musa mw’ikambi,

Na Aroni wa mweranda wa Yehova.

17 Isi ica iruguruka imira miyonzwa Datani,

Ipfukiranya ikoraniro rya Abiramu.

18 Nuko umuriro urarurumba mw’ikoraniro ryabo;

Urubeya rurigata ababisha.

19 Vyongeye, bakora inyana i Horebu,

Bunamira igishusho c’icuma gishongeshejwe,

20 Ku buryo bakāba ubuninahazwa bwanje

Ishusho y’ishūri, iyirya ibimera.

21 Bibagira Imana yo Mukiza wabo,

Iyakoze ibintu bikomeye mu Misiri,

22 Ibikorwa vy’agatangaza mu gihugu ca Hamu,

Ibintu biteye ubwoba kuri ca kiyaga Gitukura.

23 Yagira ivuge ivy’ukubazimanganya,

Iyo hataba Musa uwo yatoye,

Uwahagaze mu mukokwe imbere yayo,

Kugira asubize inyuma ubushangashirwe bwayo ngo ntibubahonye.

24 Bakengera ca gihugu gihimbaye;

Ntibizeye ijambo ryayo.

25 Baguma bidogera mu mahema yabo;

Ntibumviriza ijwi rya Yehova.

26 Nuko aduza ukuboko kwiwe ararahira ku biberekeye,

Yuko yobatsinze mu gahinga,

27 Yuko yotumye uruvyaro rwabo rugwa mu mahanga,

Be n’uko yobasanzaje mu bihugu.

28 Batangura kwifatanya na Bayali y’i Peyori

No kurya ibimazi vy’abapfuye.

29 Kubera ko bariko bamutera ishavu biciye ku bikorwa vyabo,

Ikiza caciye caduka muri bo.

30 Igihe Finehasi yahaguruka akabijamwo,

Ni ho nya kiza cahagarikwa.

31 Abiharurwako nk’ubugororotsi

Uko iyaruka rikurikira irindi gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

32 Vyongeye, batera ishavu kuri ya mazi ya Meriba,

Ku buryo bigendera nabi Musa bivuye kuri bo.

33 Kuko bababaje impwemu yiwe,

Atangura guhuruduka n’iminwa yiwe.

34 Ntibazimanganije ibisata vy’abantu,

Nk’uko Yehova yari yababwiye.

35 Bivanga n’amahanga,

Batangura kwiga ibikorwa vyabo.

36 Baguma bakorera ibigirwamana vyabo,

Bino biraheza bibabera umutego.

37 Bashikana bwa bimazi abahungu babo

N’abakobwa babo, babashikanira amadayimoni*.

38 Baguma bavisha amaraso y’intungane,

Amaraso y’abahungu babo n’abakobwa babo,

Abo bashikaniye ibigirwamana vy’i Kanani;

Igihugu kiraheza gihumanywa n’ugusesa amaraso.

39 Bahumanywa n’ibikorwa vyabo,

Baguma bashurashura biciye ku vyo bakora.

40 Maze ishavu rya Yehova ritangura kururumbira abantu biwe,

Araheza yanka urunuka intoranwa yiwe.

41 Incuro n’izindi abatanga mu kuboko kw’amahanga,

Kugira ngo ababanka babaganze,

42 No kugira ngo abansi babo babahahaze,

Kugira ngo bamarwe igabo munsi y’ukuboko kwabo.

43 Kenshi yarabarokora,

Mugabo bo bakagarariza mu ngendo yabo y’ukugambarara,

Bagacishwa bugufi kubera ikosa ryabo.

44 Yarabona amarushwa yabo

Igihe yumva akamo kabo k’ukwinginga.

45 Yaribuka ku biberekeye isezerano ryiwe,

Akumva yicujije, nk’uko umurengera w’ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwiwe buhambaye uri.

46 Yaratuma batera ikigongwe

Ababagira imbohe bose.

47 Dukize, ewe Yehova Mana yacu,

Utwegeranye udukuye mu mahanga

Ngo dukengurukire izina ryawe ryeranda,

Ngo tuvugane ukwigina tugushemeza.

48 Hahezagirwe Yehova ya Mana ya Isirayeli

Kuva igihe kitagira urugero no gushika igihe kitagira urugero;

Kandi abantu bose nibavuge Amen.

Shemeze Ya!

^ Zab. 106:37 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
