Zaburi  107 : 1 - 43

IGITABU CA GATANU

(Zaburi 107 – 150)

107 Yoo! nimukengurukire Yehova, kuko ari mwiza;

Kuko ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwiwe ari ubwo gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

 2 Abahabujwe ba Yehova nibavuge gutyo,

Abo yahabuje mu kuboko kw’umurwanizi,

 3 Akabegeraniriza hamwe abakuye mu bihugu,

Abakuye i burasirazuba n’i burengerazuba,

Abakuye mu buraruko no mu bumanuko.

 4 Barayerereye mu gahinga, mu bugaragwa;

Ntibaronse inzira n’imwe ijana ku gisagara co kubamwo.

 5 Barashonje, baranyoterwa;

Batangura kudendebukirwa mu mutima.

 6 Baguma baririra Yehova mu marushwa yabo;

Abarokora mu maganya barimwo,

 7 Abacisha mu nzira nziza,

Kugira ngo bashikire igisagara co kubamwo.

 8 Yoo! abantu nibakengurukire Yehova kubera ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwiwe

No kubera ibikorwa vyiwe vy’agatangaza akorera abana b’abantu!

 9 Kuko yahagije imitima igadutse;

Imitima ishonje yayujuje ivyiza.

10 Hari abari babayeho mu mwiza no mu gitutu kizitanye,

Imbohe ziri mu marushwa no mu vyuma.

11 Kuko bari bagararije amajambo y’Imana;

Kandi impanuro ya Musumbavyose bari bayigaye.

12 Nuko umutima wabo awumara igabo akoresheje ivyago;

Baratsitaye, kandi nta wufasha n’umwe yariho.

13 Batangura gutaka Yehova ngo abatabare mu marushwa yabo;

Abakiza amaganya barimwo nk’uko yama.

14 Arabasohora abakura mu mwiza no mu gitutu kizitanye,

Acagagura mbere n’imizana yabo.

15 Yoo! abantu nibakengurukire Yehova kubera ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwiwe

No kubera ibikorwa vyiwe vy’agatangaza akorera abana b’abantu!

16 Kuko yavunaguye imiryango y’umujumpu,

Atemagurira hasi n’ibihindizo vy’icuma.

17 Abari ibipfu, kubera inzira y’ukugabitanya kwabo,

No kubera amakosa yabo, uburuhiro biteye amarushwa.

18 Umutima wabo wanka urunuka ubwoko bwose bw’ibifungurwa,

Bashika ku marembo y’urupfu.

19 Batangura gutaka Yehova ngo abatabare mu marushwa yabo;

Abakiza amaganya barimwo nk’uko yama.

20 Arungika ijambo ryiwe, arabakiza,

Arabakiza ivyobo vyabo.

21 Yoo! abantu nibakengurukire Yehova kubera ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwiwe

No kubera ibikorwa vyiwe vy’agatangaza akorera abana b’abantu!

22 Nibashikane ibimazi vy’ugukenguruka,

Bamenyeshe ibikorwa vyiwe biha akamo k’umunezero.

23 Abamanuka ku kiyaga bari mu meri,

Bagakora urudandaza ku mazi agereza kure,

24 Ni bo babonye ibikorwa vya Yehova

N’ibikorwa vy’agatangaza vyiwe ibwina;

25 Ingene avuga ijambo agatuma umuyaga w’igihuhusi uhaguruka,

Ku buryo uvyura imipfunda yaco.

26 Baraduga mu majuru,

Bakamanuka ibwina.

Kubera ivyago, umutima wabo urashonga.

27 Baragira ibizunguzungu bakagenda badandabagirana nk’imborerwa,

Mbere n’ubukerebutsi bwabo bwose bukavurungana.

28 Batangura kuririra Yehova mu marushwa yabo,

Akabakura mu maganya barimwo.

29 Atururuza ikirura c’umuyaga kigatekana,

Imipfunda y’ikiyaga igatekereza.

30 Kandi baranezerwa kuko ivyo bigumye hamwe,

Na we akabayobora ku kivuko c’agahimbare kabo.

31 Yoo! abantu nibakengurukire Yehova kubera ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwiwe

No kubera ibikorwa vyiwe vy’agatangaza akorera abana b’abantu!

32 Nibamuhayagize mw’ishengero ry’abantu;

Aho abahumure bicaye, nibamushemeze!

33 Ahindura inzuzi agahinga,

Amasôko atemba y’amazi akayahindura ubutaka bunyotewe,

34 Isi yimbuka ayihindura igihugu c’umunyu,

Kubera ububi bw’abahaba.

35 Ahindura agahinga ikidengeri c’amazi cuzuyeko amarenga,

Isi yo mu ntara igadutse akayihindura amasôko atemba y’amazi.

36 Kandi atuma abashonje bibera ng’aho,

Ku buryo bashimangira igisagara co kubamwo.

37 Barabiba amatongo bagatera n’imirima y’imizabibu,

Kugira ngo bitange ivyimburwa bigwira.

38 Arabahezagira bakagwira cane;

Kandi ntareka ngo inka zabo zigabanuke.

39 Barongera bakagabanuka, bakituna

Kubera ukuzigirizwa, ivyago n’intuntu.

40 Asuka agakengerwe ku banyacubahiro,

Ku buryo atuma bayerera ahantu h’agahinga, hatagira inzira.

41 Mugabo akingira umworo amarushwa

Akamuhindura imiryāngo nk’umukuku.

42 Abagororotsi barabibona bakanezerwa;

Nayo ukutagororoka kwose, kubwirizwa kwugara akanwa.

43 Ni nde ari inkerebutsi? Azokwitegereza ivyo bintu

Yongere yiteho ibikorwa vy’ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo vya Yehova.
