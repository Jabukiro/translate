Zaburi  111 : 1 - 10

111 Shemeze Ya!

א [ʼAlefu]

Nzohaya Yehova n’umutima wanje wose

ב [Beti]

Mu mugwi w’abagenzi somambike bagororotse no mw’ikoraniro.

ג [Gimelu]

 2 Ibikorwa vya Yehova birahambaye,

ד [Daleti]

Bironderwa n’abo bose bahimbarwa na vyo.

ה [Heʼ]

 3 Igikorwa ciwe ni iteka n’ubwiza bwakaka,

ו [Vau]

Ubugororotsi bwiwe na bwo bwamaho ibihe bidahera.

ז [Zayini]

 4 Yashiriyeho icibutso ibikorwa vyiwe vy’agatangaza.

ח [Heti]

Yehova ni umunyempuhwe n’umunyembabazi.

ט [Teti]

 5 Yahaye ibifungurwa abamutinya.

י [Yodi]

Azokwibuka isezerano ryiwe ibihe vyose.

כ [Kafu]

 6 Yahishuriye abantu biwe ububasha bw’ibikorwa vyiwe,

ל [Lamedi]

Mu kubaha intoranwa y’amahanga.

מ [Memu]

 7 Ibikorwa vy’amaboko yiwe ni ukuri be n’ubutungane;

נ [Nuni]

Amateka atanga yose ni ayo kwizigirwa,

ס [Sameki]

 8 Ashigikiwe ibihe bidahera, gushika igihe kitagira urugero,

ע [ʽAyini]

Yatunganijwe mu kuri no mu kugororoka.

פ [Peʼ]

 9 Yarungikiye abantu biwe ugucungurwa.

צ [Tsade]

Yategetse isezerano ryiwe kugumaho gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

ק [Kofu]

Izina ryiwe ni ryeranda kandi rirateye ubwoba.

ר [Reshi]

10 Ugutinya Yehova ni yo ntango y’ubukerebutsi.

ש [Sini]

Abashitsa amateka yiwe bose bafise ugutahura nyakwo.

ת [Tau]

Ishemezo ryiwe ryamaho ibihe bidahera.
