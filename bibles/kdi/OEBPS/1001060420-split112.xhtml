Zaburi  112 : 1 - 10

112 Shemeze Ya!

א [ʼAlefu]

Hahiriwe umuntu atinya Yehova,

ב [Beti]

Yahimbawe cane n’amabwirizwa yiwe.

ג [Gimelu]

 2 Uruvyaro rwiwe ruzogira inkomezi mw’isi.

ד [Daleti]

Nayo iyaruka ry’abagororotse, rizohezagirwa.

ה [Heʼ]

 3 Ibintu vy’agaciro n’ubutunzi biri mu nzu yiwe;

ו [Vau]

Kandi ubugororotsi bwiwe bwamaho ibihe bidahera.

ז [Zayini]

 4 Yarabije mu mwiza nk’umuco ku bagororotse.

ח [Heti]

Ni umunyempuhwe n’umunyembabazi n’umugororotsi.

ט [Teti]

 5 Umuntu w’umunyempuhwe kandi aguranana ni mwiza.

י [Yodi]

Ivyiwe abishigikiza ubutungane.

כ [Kafu]

 6 Kuko atazokwigera anyiganyizwa.

ל [Lamedi]

Umugororotsi azoguma yibukwa gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

מ [Memu]

 7 Mbere n’inkuru mbi ntazoyitinya.

נ [Nuni]

Umutima wiwe urahamye, wahawe kwizigira Yehova.

ס [Sameki]

 8 Umutima wiwe ntushobora kunyiganyizwa; ntazotinya,

ע [ʽAyini]

Gushika yitegereje abarwanizi biwe.

פ [Peʼ]

 9 Yaratanze cane; yarahaye aboro.

צ [Tsade]

Ubugororotsi bwiwe bwamaho ibihe bidahera.

ק [Kofu]

Ihembe ryiwe rizoshirwa hejuru mu buninahazwa.

ר [Reshi]

10 Umubisha azobibona kamurye koko.

ש [Sini]

Azohekenya amenyo, ashonge ata kabuza.

ת [Tau]

Icipfuzo c’ababisha kizoyogera.
