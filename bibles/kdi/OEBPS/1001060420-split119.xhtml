Zaburi  119 : 1 - 176

א [ʼAlefu]

119 Hahiriwe abatagira amahinyu mu nzira yabo,

Abagendera mw’itegeko rya Yehova.

 2 Hahiriwe abubahiriza ivyibutsa vyiwe;

Baguma bamurondeza umutima wose.

 3 Vy’ukuri, nta bitagororotse baza barakora.

Bagendeye mu nzira ziwe.

 4 Wewe ubwawe watanze amateka yawe bwa bwirizwa

Ngo bayagumye bavyitondeye.

 5 Yoo! ese iyo inzira zanje ziba zishimangiwe

Ngo ngumye ingingo nyobozi zawe!

 6 Ni ho ntomaramara

Igihe nihweza amabwirizwa yawe yose.

 7 Nzoguhaya mu kugororoka kw’umutima,

Igihe niga ingingo zawe z’ubutungane zigororotse.

 8 Ingingo nyobozi zawe mbandanya kuzigumya.

Yoo! ntumpebe buhere!

ב [Beti]

 9 Mbega umusore azotyorora inzira yiwe gute?

Mu kuguma yirinze nk’uko ijambo ryawe rivuga.

 10 Nakurondeje umutima wanje wose.

Ntutume nzimira ngo mve ku mabwirizwa yawe.

 11 Nabitse muri ntibate ijambo ryawe mu mutima wanje,

Kugira ngo singucumureko.

 12 Urahezagiwe, ewe Yehova!

Nunyigishe ingingo nyobozi zawe.

 13 Namenyesheje n’iminwa yanje

Ingingo z’ubutungane zose z’akanwa kawe.

 14 Niginye mu nzira y’ivyibutsa vyawe,

Nk’uko bigina ibindi bintu vyose vy’agaciro.

 15 Nzokwitwararika amateka yawe,

Kandi nzokwihweza iminyuro yawe.

 16 Nzohimbarwa rwose n’ivyagezwe vyawe.

Sinzokwibagira ijambo ryawe.

ג [Gimelu]

 17 Girira umusavyi wawe uko bibereye, kugira mbeho

No kugira ngumye ijambo ryawe.

 18 Pfukura amaso yanje, kugira nihweze

Ibintu vy’agatangaza vyo mw’itegeko ryawe.

 19 Nta kindi ndi atari kavamahanga mu gihugu.

Ntumpishe amabwirizwa yawe.

 20 Umutima wanje ujanjaguwe n’ugucambirwa

Ingingo zawe z’ubutungane igihe cose.

 21 Wahambariye abita imbere b’ibivume,

Bazimira bakava ku mabwirizwa yawe.

 22 Tembagaza iceyi n’agakengerwe bimveko,

Kuko nubahirije ivyibutsa vyawe.

 23 Mbere n’abaganwa bicaye; bavuganye bandwanya.

Nayo umusavyi wawe, yitwararika ingingo nyobozi zawe.

 24 Kandi, ivyibutsa vyawe ndabikunda cane,

Ni nk’abagabo bangira inama.

ד [Daleti]

 25 Ubuzima bwanje bumase ku mukungugu.

Nunzigame nk’uko ijambo ryawe riri.

 26 Namenyesheje inzira zanje, kugira unyishure.

Nunyigishe ingingo nyobozi zawe.

 27 Nuntahuze inzira y’amateka yawe,

Kugira nitwararike ibikorwa vyawe vy’agatangaza.

 28 Umutima wanje wabuze agatiro kubera intuntu.

Numpagurutse nk’uko ijambo ryawe riri.

 29 Nunkureko inzira y’ikinyoma,

Untoneshe ukoresheje itegeko ryawe.

 30 Nahisemwo inzira y’ubwizigirwa.

Ingingo zawe z’ubutungane nabonye ko zibereye.

 31 Numiye ku vyibutsa vyawe.

Ewe Yehova, ntumaramaze.

 32 Nzokwiruka mu nzira y’amabwirizwa yawe,

Kuko utuma umutima wanje wagukirwa.

ה [Heʼ]

 33 Nunyigishe, ewe Yehova, inzira y’ingingo nyobozi zawe,

Kugira ndayubahirize gushika kw’iherezo.

 34 Nuntahuze, kugira nubahirize itegeko ryawe

No kugira ndarigumye n’umutima wanje wose.

 35 Nutume ngendera mu munyuro w’amabwirizwa yawe,

Kuko nahimbawe na wo.

 36 Hengekera umutima wanje ku vyibutsa vyawe,

Ntuwuhengekere ku nyungu.

 37 Nutume amaso yanje arengana ntabone ibitagira akamaro;

Nunzigamire ubuzima mu nzira yawe.

 38 Nushitse ku musavyi wawe ijambo ryawe

Riganisha ku kugutinya.

 39 Nutume iceyi canje gihita, ari co nagiriye ubwoba,

Kuko ingingo zawe z’ubutungane ari nziza.

 40 Raba! Naracambiwe amateka yawe.

Mu bugororotsi bwawe, nunzigamire ubuzima.

ו [Vau]

 41 Ibikorwa vyawe vy’ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo nibinzeko, ewe Yehova,

Ubukiriro bwawe bunzeko nk’uko ijambo ryawe riri,

 42 Kugira nishurishe ijambo uwunsiga iceyi,

Kuko nizigiye ijambo ryawe.

 43 Ntukūre rwose mu kanwa kanje ijambo ry’ukuri,

Kuko narindiriye ingingo yawe y’ubutungane.

 44 Nzogumya itegeko ryawe nantaryo,

Gushika igihe kitagira urugero, emwe, ibihe bidahera.

 45 Nzogendagenda ahantu hagutse,

Kuko narondeye amateka yawe.

 46 Nzoyaga kandi ivyibutsa vyawe imbere y’abami,

Kandi sinzomaramara.

 47 Nzohimbarwa rwose n’amabwirizwa yawe

Nakunze.

 48 Nzoduza ibiganza vyanje ndavyerekeza ku mabwirizwa yawe nakunze,

Kandi nzokwitwararika ingingo nyobozi zawe.

ז [Zayini]

 49 Niwibuke rya jambo wabwiye umusavyi wawe,

Iryo wandindirije.

 50 Urwo ni rwo ruhoza rwanje mu marushwa yanje,

Kuko ijambo ryawe ryanzigamiye ubuzima.

 51 Abita imbere bantwengeye mw’ijigo bimwe birengeje.

Sinakebeje ngo nte itegeko ryawe.

 52 Naributse ingingo zawe z’ubutungane zo kuva igihe kitagira urugero, ewe Yehova,

Kandi ndironkera uruhoza.

 53 Umururumbo w’ubushangashirwe waramfashe kubera ababisha

Baheba itegeko ryawe.

 54 Ingingo nyobozi zawe zacitse uduhibongozo kuri jewe

Mu nzu mbamwo ndi kavamahanga.

 55 Mw’ijoro naributse izina ryawe, ewe Yehova,

Kugira ngumye itegeko ryawe.

 56 Emwe, ivyo vyacitse rwanje,

Kuko nubahirije amateka yawe.

ח [Heti]

 57 Yehova ni we muturi wanje;

Nasezeranye kugumya amajambo yawe.

 58 Nagutakambiye* n’umutima wanje wose.

Nungirire ubutoni nk’uko ijambo ryawe riri.

 59 Nararimbuye inzira zanje,

Kugira nsubize ibirenge vyanje ku vyibutsa vyawe.

 60 Naranyarutse, sinateba

Kugumya amabwirizwa yawe.

 61 Imigozi y’ababisha yarankikuje.

Sinibagiye itegeko ryawe.

 62 Mu gicugu ndavyuka ngo ngukengurukire

Ku bw’ingingo zawe z’ubutungane zigororotse.

 63 Ndi umugenzi w’abo bose bagutinya,

N’uw’abo bagumya amateka yawe.

 64 Ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwawe, ewe Yehova, bwuzuye isi.

Nunyigishe ingingo nyobozi zawe.

ט [Teti]

 65 Wagiriye neza koko umusavyi wawe,

Ewe Yehova, nk’uko ijambo ryawe riri.

 66 Nunyigishe ukumera neza, ukwiyumvira neza n’ubumenyi,

Kuko nizeye amabwirizwa yawe.

 67 Imbere y’uko nshikirwa n’amarushwa, naracumura bivuye ku kwihenda,

Ariko ubu naragumije ijambo ryawe.

 68 Uri mwiza, kandi ukora ivyiza.

Nunyigishe ingingo nyobozi zawe.

 69 Abita imbere bansize ibinyoma.

Nayo jewe, nzokwubahiriza amateka yawe n’umutima wanje wose.

 70 Umutima wabo waratimvye nk’ikinure.

Nayo jewe, itegeko ryawe ndarikunda cane.

 71 Ni vyiza kuba naratejwe amarushwa,

Kugira nige ingingo nyobozi zawe.

 72 Itegeko ryo mu kanwa kawe rimbereye ryiza,

Kuruta ibice ibihumbi vy’inzahabu n’ifeza.

י [Yodi]

 73 Amaboko yawe ni yo yankoze, aranshimangira.

Nuntahuze, kugira nige amabwirizwa yawe.

 74 Abagutinya barambona bakanezerwa,

Kuko narindiriye ijambo ryawe.

 75 Ndazi neza, ewe Yehova, yuko ingingo zawe z’ubutungane ari ubugororotsi

Be n’uko wanteje amarushwa mu bwizigirwa.

 76 Ndakwinginze ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwawe bube ubwo kumpoza,

Nk’uko ijambo ryawe wabwiye umusavyi wawe riri.

 77 Imbabazi zawe nizinzeko, kugira mbandanye kubaho;

Kuko itegeko ryawe ndarikunda cane.

 78 Abita imbere nibamaramare, kuko bampubishije ata gituma.

Nayo jewe, nitwararika amateka yawe.

 79 Abagutinya nibagaruke kuri jewe,

Be n’abazi ivyibutsa vyawe.

 80 Umutima wanje nuboneke ko utagira amahinyu mu ngingo nyobozi zawe,

Kugira ngo simaramare.

כ [Kafu]

 81 Umutima wanje ugwabijwe no kwipfuza ubukiriro bwawe;

Narindiriye ijambo ryawe.

 82 Amaso yanje agwabijwe no kwipfuza ijambo ryawe,

Nanje nkavuga nti: “Uzompoza ryari?”

 83 Kuko mbaye nk’umukuza w’urushato mu mwotsi.

Sinibagiye ingingo nyobozi zawe.

 84 Imisi y’umusavyi wawe ni ingahe?

Uzoshitsa imanza ryari ku bampama?

 85 Abita imbere bimvye ivyobo ngo bamfate,

Barya batabaho bisunga itegeko ryawe.

 86 Amabwirizwa yawe yose ni ubwizigirwa gusa.

Bampamye ata gituma. Yoo! nuntabare!

 87 Mu kanya gatoyi bari kuba baranzimanganije kw’isi;

Ariko jewe sinahevye amateka yawe.

 88 Nunzigamire ubuzima nk’uko ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwawe buri,

Kugira ngumye icibutsa co mu kanwa kawe.

ל [Lamedi]

 89 Gushika igihe kitagira urugero, ewe Yehova,

Ijambo ryawe rishinzwe mu majuru.

 90 Ubwizigirwa bwawe ni ubw’iyaruka ku rindi.

Washimangiye isi, kugira igume ihagaze.

 91 Vyararagamye gushika uno musi vyisunze ingingo zawe z’ubutungane,

Kuko vyose ari abasavyi bawe.

 92 Iyo mba ntakunda cane itegeko ryawe,

Mba narazimanganiye mu marushwa yanje.

 93 Gushika igihe kitagira urugero sinzokwibagira amateka yawe,

Kuko wanzigamiye ubuzima biciye kuri yo.

 94 Ndi uwawe. Yoo! nunkize,

Kuko narondeye amateka yawe.

 95 Ababisha bandindiriye, ngo bandandure.

Nguma nerekana ko nzirikana ivyibutsa vyawe

 96 Ukutagira akanenge kwose nabonye gufise iherezo.

Ibwirizwa ryawe riragutse cane.

מ [Memu]

 97 Ewe kuntu nkunda itegeko ryawe!

Ni ryo nitwararika bukarinda bwira.

 98 Ibwirizwa ryawe ringira inkerebutsi kuruta abansi banje,

Kuko ari rwanje gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

 99 Nsigaye ndusha abigisha banje bose ugutahura kwimbitse,

Kuko ivyibutsa vyawe ari ikintu nitwararika.

100 Nkorana ugutegera kuruta abagabo b’inararibonye,

Kuko nubahirije amateka yawe.

101 Natangiriye ibirenge vyanje ndabibuza umunyuro mubi wose,

Kugira ngumye ijambo ryawe.

102 Sinakebeje ngo mve ku ngingo zawe z’ubutungane,

Kuko ari wewe wanyigishije.

103 Ewe kuntu amajambo yawe yasoseye amagage yanje,

Agasosera akanwa kanje kuruta ubuki!

104 Ndabikesheje amateka yawe, nkorana ugutegera.

Ni co gituma nanse umunyuro w’ikinyoma wose.

נ [Nuni]

105 Ijambo ryawe ni itara ry’ikirenge canje,

Rikaba n’umuco w’ibarabara ryanje.

106 Nararahiye indahiro, kandi nzoyishitsa,

Yo kugumya ingingo zawe z’ubutungane zigororotse.

107 Natejwe amarushwa bimwe bikomeye.

Ewe Yehova, nunzigamire ubuzima nk’uko ijambo ryawe riri.

108 Ndakwinginze uhimbarwe n’amashikanwa atangwa ku bushake y’akanwa kanje, ewe Yehova,

Kandi unyigishe ingingo zawe z’ubutungane.

109 Ubuzima bwanje bwama mu kaga*;

Mugabo sinibagiye itegeko ryawe.

110 Ababisha banteze umutego,

Mugabo sinangaye ngo nte amateka yawe.

111 Ivyibutsa vyawe nabifashe nk’ikintu nigabira gushika igihe kitagira urugero,

Kuko ari vyo mwigino w’umutima wanje.

112 Nahengekeye umutima wanje ku gushira mu ngiro ingingo nyobozi zawe

Gushika igihe kitagira urugero, gushika kw’iherezo.

ס [Sameki]

113 Nanse ab’umutima wigabuye,

Ariko nakunze itegeko ryawe.

114 Uri ubwihisho bwanje n’inkinzo yanje.

Narindiriye ijambo ryawe.

115 Mve iruhande, mwa nkozi z’ikibi mwe,

Kugira nubahirize amabwirizwa y’Imana yanje.

116 Nunshigikire nk’uko ijambo ryawe riri, kugira mbandanye kubaho,

Kandi ntumaramaze kubera icizigiro canje.

117 Nunshigikire, kugira nkizwe,

Kandi nzokwama nihweza ingingo nyobozi zawe.

118 Washibuye abo bose bazimira bakava ku ngingo nyobozi zawe;

Kuko amayeri yabo ari ibinyoma gusa.

119 Wazimanganije ababisha bose bo kw’isi nk’inkamba.

Ni co gituma nakunze ivyibutsa vyawe.

120 Inyama zanje zahinze agashitsi kubera ukukugirira ubwoba;

Kandi kubera ingingo zawe z’ubutungane, naratinye.

ע [ʽAyini]

121 Nashikije imanza be n’ubugororotsi.

Yoo! ntumpebere mu maboko y’abangunga!

122 Niwishinge umusavyi wawe ku bw’iciza.

Abita imbere ntibangunge.

123 Amaso yanje agwabijwe no kwipfuza ubukiriro bwawe

Be no kwipfuza ijambo ryawe rigororotse.

124 Nugirire umusavyi wawe ibihuye n’ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwawe,

Kandi unyigishe ingingo nyobozi zawe.

125 Ndi umusavyi wawe. Nuntahuze,

Kugira menye ivyibutsa vyawe.

126 Ni igihe c’uko Yehova agira ico akoze.

Barenze itegeko ryawe.

127 Ni co gituma nakunze amabwirizwa yawe

Kuruta inzahabu, emwe, inzahabu ityoroye.

128 Ni co gituma nabonye ko amateka yose yerekeye ibintu vyose agororotse;

Nanse umunyuro w’ikinyoma wose.

פ [Peʼ]

129 Ivyibutsa vyawe ni agatangaza.

Ni co gituma umutima wanje wavyubahirije.

130 Ihishurwa ry’amajambo yawe ritanga umuco,

Rigatuma abantu batazi utuntu n’utundi batahura.

131 Nataburuye akanwa kanje, kugira mpezagirike,

Kuko nacambiwe amabwirizwa yawe.

132 Hindukirira kuri jewe, ungirire ubutoni,

Wisunze ingingo yawe y’ubutungane kuri barya bakunda izina ryawe.

133 Nushimangire intambuko zanje mw’ijambo ryawe,

Kandi ntihagire ikibi na kimwe ciganza kuri jewe.

134 Nuncungure unkize umuntu w’ingunge wese,

Nanje nzogumya amateka yawe.

135 Nutume mu maso hawe hakayanganira ku musavyi wawe,

Kandi unyigishe ingingo nyobozi zawe.

136 Imigezi y’amazi yatemvye mu maso yanje

Kubera ko batagumije itegeko ryawe.

צ [Tsade]

137 Uri umugororotsi, ewe Yehova,

Kandi ingingo zawe z’ubutungane ziragororotse.

138 Watanze ivyibutsa vyawe bwa tegeko, mu bugororotsi

No mu bwizigirwa buhebuje.

139 Ishaka ryanje ryamaze,

Kubera ko abarwanizi banje bibagiye amajambo yawe.

140 Ijambo ryawe riratyoroye cane,

Kandi umusavyi wawe ararikunda.

141 Nta co ndi, kandi ndagayitse.

Sinibagiye amateka yawe.

142 Ubugororotsi bwawe ni ubugororotsi bwo gushika igihe kitagira urugero,

Kandi itegeko ryawe ni ukuri.

143 Amarushwa n’ingorane vyaranshikiriye.

Amabwirizwa yawe narayakunda cane.

144 Ubugororotsi bw’ivyibutsa vyawe ni ubwo gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

Nuntahuze, kugira mbandanye kubaho.

ק [Kofu]

145 Nahamagaye n’umutima wanje wose. Nunyishure, ewe Yehova!

Nzokwubahiriza ingingo nyobozi zawe.

146 Narakwambaje. Yoo! nunkize!

Kandi nzogumya ivyibutsa vyawe.

147 Navyutse kare mu bucagucagu, kugira ntabaze.

Narindiriye amajambo yawe.

148 Amaso yanje yitangiye imbere amaterama y’ijoro,

Kugira nitwararike ijambo ryawe.

149 Yoo! niwumve ijwi ryanje nk’uko ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwawe buri!

Ewe Yehova, nunzigamire ubuzima nk’uko ingingo yawe y’ubutungane iri.

150 Abakurikirana ivy’ubushirasoni begereye;

Bagiye kure y’itegeko ryawe.

151 Uri hafi, ewe Yehova,

Kandi amabwirizwa yawe yose ni ukuri.

152 Namenye kera bimwe mu vyibutsa vyawe,

Kuko wabishinze ngo bigumeho gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

ר [Reshi]

153 Yoo! nubone amarushwa yanje, untabare;

Kuko ntibagiye itegeko ryawe.

154 Yoo! niwifatire mu minwe urubanza rwanje, ungarukane;

Nunzigamire ubuzima nk’uko ijambo ryawe riri.

155 Ubukiriro buri kure y’ababisha,

Kuko batarondeye ingingo nyobozi zawe.

156 Imbabazi zawe ni nyinshi, ewe Yehova!

Yoo! nunzigamire ubuzima, nk’uko ingingo zawe z’ubutungane ziri!

157 Abampama be n’abarwanizi banje ni benshi.

Sinakebeje ngo mve ku vyibutsa vyawe.

158 Narabonye abantu b’ibihemu,

Kandi ndashishwa koko, kuko batagumije ijambo ryawe.

159 Yoo! raba narakunze amateka yawe!

Ewe Yehova, nunzigamire ubuzima nk’uko ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwawe buri.

160 Ijambo ryawe uko riri ni ukuri,

Kandi ingingo yawe yose y’ubutungane igororotse ni iyo gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

ש [Sini] canke [Shini]

161 Abaganwa barampamye ata gituma,

Mugabo umutima wanje wagiriye ubwoba amajambo yawe.

162 Ndigina kubera ijambo ryawe

Kurya umuntu yigina iyo aronse isahu ryinshi.

163 Naranse ikinyoma, kandi nguma ndacanka urunuka.

Nakunze itegeko ryawe.

164 Nagushemeje incuro indwi ku musi

Kubera ingingo zawe z’ubutungane zigororotse.

165 Abakunda itegeko ryawe bagira amahoro menshi,

Kandi kuri bo nta gitsitaza kihaba.

166 Narizigiye ubukiriro bwawe, ewe Yehova,

Kandi nashize mu ngiro amabwirizwa yawe.

167 Umutima wanje wagumije ivyibutsa vyawe,

Kandi ndabikunda bihebuje.

168 Nagumije amateka yawe n’ivyibutsa vyawe,

Kuko inzira zanje zose ziri imbere yawe.

ת [Tau]

169 Akamo kanje k’ukwinginga nikegere imbere yawe, ewe Yehova!

Yoo! nuntahuze, nk’uko ijambo ryawe riri.

170 Ugusaba ubutoni kwanje nigushike imbere yawe.

Yoo! nundokore, nk’uko ijambo ryawe riri.

171 Iminwa yanje nidudubize ishemezo,

Kuko unyigisha ingingo nyobozi zawe.

172 Ururimi rwanje niruririmbe ijambo ryawe,

Kuko amabwirizwa yawe yose ari ubugororotsi.

173 Ukuboko kwawe nikumfashe,

Kuko nahisemwo amateka yawe.

174 Nacambiwe ubukiriro bwawe, ewe Yehova,

Kandi itegeko ryawe ndarikunda cane.

175 Ese nobandanya kubaho no kugushemeza,

Kandi ese ingingo zawe z’ubutungane zomfasha!

176 Nangaye nk’intama yazimiye. Yoo! rondera umusavyi wawe,

Kuko ntibagiye amabwirizwa yawe.

^ Zab. 119:58 Ijambo ku rindi ni “noroheje mu maso hawe.”

^ Zab. 119:109 Ijambo ku rindi ni “mu kiganza canje.”
