Zaburi  12 : 1 - 8

Ku muyobozi, kuri ogitave yo hasi. Agahibongozo ka Dawidi.

12 Nkiza, Yehova we, kuko intahemuka irangiye;

Kuko abantu b’abizigirwa bakamanganye mu bana b’abantu.

 2 Baguma babwirana ivy’ububeshi;

Baguma bavugisha akanwa gasosa, n’imitima ibiri.

 3 Yehova azorandura iminwa isosa yose,

Ururimi ruvuga ibihambaye,

 4 Abavuze ngo: “Ku rurimi rwacu tuzonesha.

Iminwa turafise. Ni nde azoba umutware wo kutuganza?”

 5 “Kubera ukunyagwa kw’abarushwa, kubera ukuniha kw’aboro,

Ubu ho ngira mpaguruke,” ni ko Yehova avuga.

“Ngira ndamushire ahatagira inkomanzi, aho ata n’umwe amuhemerako.”

 6 Amajambo ya Yehova ni amajambo atavanze,

Nk’ifeza ityororewe mw’itanure ry’ivu riyaza ivyuma, ikaba itosoye incuro indwi.

 7 Wewe ubwawe, ewe Yehova, uzobarinda;

Uzozigama umwe wese umurinde iri yaruka gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

 8 Ababisha bagendagenda impande zose,

Kubera yuko ivy’agahomerabunwa bishirwa hejuru mu bana b’abantu.
