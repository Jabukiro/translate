Zaburi  129 : 1 - 8

Ururirimbo rw’Ukuduga.

129 “Barerekanye bihagije ko banyanka kuva mu buto bwanje,”

Isirayeli ubu navuge ati:

 2 “Barerekanye bihagije ko banyanka kuva mu buto bwanje;

Yamara jewe ntibanesheje.

 3 Abarimyi barimye ku mugongo wanje;

Barehuye imirongo yabo y’ubusizi.”

 4 Yehova ni umugororotsi.

Yacagaguye imigozi y’ababisha.

 5 Bazomaramara basubire inyuma,

Abo bose banka Siyoni.

 6 Bazocika nk’ivyatsi bitoto vyo ku bisenge,

Vyuma imbere y’uko birandurwa,

 7 Ivyo umushwabuzi atuzuza ukuboko kwiwe,

Canke ngo hagire uwegeranya imiganda avyuzuza igikiriza ciwe.

 8 Eka n’abahita ntibavuze ngo:

“Umuhezagiro wa Yehova ube kuri mwebwe.

Tubahezagiye mw’izina rya Yehova.”
