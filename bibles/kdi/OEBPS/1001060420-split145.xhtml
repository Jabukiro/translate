Zaburi  145 : 1 - 21

Ishemezo. Ni izaburi ya Dawidi.

א [ʼAlefu]

145 Nzogushira hejuru, ewe Mana yanje, wewe Mwami,

Kandi nzohezagira izina ryawe gushika igihe kitagira urugero, mbere ibihe bidahera.

ב [Beti]

 2 Nzoguhezagira burinde bwira,

Kandi nzoshemeza izina ryawe gushika igihe kitagira urugero, mbere ibihe bidahera.

ג [Gimelu]

 3 Yehova arahambaye kandi ni uwo gushemezwa rwose,

Kandi ubukuru bwiwe ntibuserangurika.

ד [Daleti]

 4 Uko iyaruka rikurikira irindi, rizokeza ibikorwa vyawe,

Kandi bazotangaza ibikorwa vyawe vy’inkomezi.

ה [Heʼ]

 5 Ubwiza bwakaka buninahaye bw’iteka ryawe

Be n’ibintu vy’ibikorwa vyawe vy’agatangaza, nzovyitwararika.

ו [Vau]

 6 Bazoyaga inkomezi z’ibintu vyawe biteye ubwoba;

Nayo ubukuru bwawe, nzobumenyesha.

ז [Zayini]

 7 Bazodudubiza amajambo bavuga ubwinshi bw’ukumera neza kwawe,

Kandi kubera ubugororotsi bwawe, bazokwiha akamo k’umunezero.

ח [Heti]

 8 Yehova ni umunyempuhwe n’umunyembabazi,

Ateba gushavura, kandi agwije ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo.

ט [Teti]

 9 Yehova ni mwiza kuri bose,

Kandi imbabazi ziwe ziri ku bikorwa vyiwe vyose.

י [Yodi]

10 Ibikorwa vyawe vyose bizoguhaya, ewe Yehova,

Kandi intahemuka zawe bazoguhezagira.

כ [Kafu]

11 Bazoyaga ubuninahazwa bw’ingoma yawe,

Bongere bavuge ubushobozi bwawe,

ל [Lamedi]

12 Kugira abana b’abantu bamenyeshwe ibikorwa vyiwe vy’inkomezi,

Be n’ubuninahazwa bw’ubwiza bwakaka bw’ingoma yiwe.

מ [Memu]

13 Ingoma yawe ni ingoma y’ibihe vyose bitagira urugero,

Kandi inganji yawe irabandanya mu mayaruka akurikirana yose.

ס [Sameki]

14 Yehova ashigikira abatemba bose,

Kandi ahagurutsa abunamye bose.

ע [ʽAyini]

15 Vyose bihanga amaso wewe bifise ukwizigira,

Nawe ukabiha indya zavyo mu kiringo cazo.

פ [Peʼ]

16 Upfumbaturura ikiganza cawe

Ugahaza icipfuzo c’ikiri n’ubuzima cose.

צ [Tsade]

17 Yehova ni umugororotsi mu nzira ziwe zose

Akaba n’intahemuka mu bikorwa vyiwe vyose.

ק [Kofu]

18 Yehova ari hafi y’abamwambaza bose,

Hafi y’abamwambaza mu kuri bose.

ר [Reshi]

19 Icipfuzo c’abamutinya azogishitsa,

Kandi azokwumva ugutabaza kwabo, abakize.

ש [Sini]

20 Yehova arinda abamukunda bose,

Mugabo ababisha bose azobazimanganya.

ת [Tau]

21 Akanwa kanje kazovuga ishemezo rya Yehova;

Ikiri n’umubiri cose nigihezagire izina ryiwe ryeranda gushika igihe kitagira urugero, mbere ibihe bidahera.
