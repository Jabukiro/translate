Zaburi  146 : 1 - 10

146 Shemeze Ya!

Shemeza Yehova, mutima wanje!

 2 Nzoshemeza Yehova mu buzima bwanje.

Nzohibongoreza Imana yanje igihe cose nzoba nkiriho.

 3 Ntimushire ivyizigiro vyanyu mu banyacubahiro,

Canke mu mwana w’umuntu yakuwe mw’ivu, ata bukiriro ashobora gutanga.

 4 Impwemu yiwe irasohoka, agasubira mu butaka bwiwe;

Uwo musi ivyiyumviro vyiwe bigahera.

 5 Hahiriwe uwufise Imana ya Yakobo bwa mutabazi wiwe,

Icizigiro ciwe kikaba kiri muri Yehova Imana yiwe,

 6 Wa Muhinguzi w’ijuru n’isi,

W’ikiyaga, be n’uw’ibiri muri vyo vyose,

Umwe agumya ukuri gushika igihe kitagira urugero,

 7 Umwe ashitsa urubanza ku bw’abagunzwe,

Umwe aha umukate abashonje.

Yehova arabohora ababoshwe.

 8 Yehova arahumura amaso y’impumyi;

Yehova arunamura abahetamye;

Yehova arakunda abagororotsi.

 9 Yehova ararinda ba kavamahanga;

Impfuvyi* be n’umupfakazi arabatabara,

Mugabo inzira y’ababisha arayihetarika.

10 Yehova azoba umwami gushika igihe kitagira urugero,

Imana yawe, ewe Siyoni, izoba umwami uko iyaruka rikurikira irindi.

Shemeze Ya!

^ Zab. 146:9 Ijambo ku rindi ni “agahungu katagira se.”
