Zaburi  149 : 1 - 9

149 Shemeze Ya!

Ririmbire Yehova ururirimbo rushasha,

Ririmbe ishemezo ryiwe mw’ishengero ry’intahemuka.

 2 Isirayeli ninezererwe Umuhinguzi wayo Ahambaye,

Abana ba Siyoni, emwe, nibahimbarirwe Umwami wabo.

 3 Nibashemeze izina ryiwe batamba.

Nibamuhibongoreze bavuza akagoma n’inanga.

 4 Kuko Yehova ahimbarwa n’abantu biwe.

Aha ubwiza abatekereza mu kubambika ubukiriro.

 5 Intahemuka nibigine mu buninahazwa;

Nibihe akamo k’umunezero ku bitanda vyabo.

 6 Indirimbo zihayagiza Imana nizibe mu muhogo wabo,

Inkota y’ubugi bubiri ibe mu kuboko kwabo,

 7 Ngo hashitswe ukwihōra ku mahanga,

Uguhambarirwa ku miryango y’amahanga,

 8 Ngo abami babo baboheshwe iminyororo,

Abaninahaye babo na bo amapingu y’icuma,

 9 Ngo bashitsweko ingingo y’ubutungane yanditswe.

Mwene ubwo bwiza bwakaka ni ubw’intahemuka ziwe zose.

Shemeze Ya!
