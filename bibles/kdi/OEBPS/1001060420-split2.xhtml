Zaburi  2 : 1 - 12

2 Kubera iki amahanga yagiye mu ruhagarara,

N’imiryango y’amahanga ikaguma ivugira mu ntamatama ikintu ata co kimaze?

 2 Abami b’isi barahagaze

Kandi abategetsi bakurubakuru bakoraniye hamwe nk’umuntu umwe

Ngo barwanye Yehova n’uwo yarobanuje amavuta*,

 3 Bakavuga ngo: “Tuvutagure imizana yabo

Dushiburire kure yacu imigozi yabo!”

 4 Wa Wundi yicaye mu majuru azokwitwengera;

Yehova ubwiwe azobatwengera mw’ijigo.

 5 Ico gihe azobabwirira mw’ishavu ryiwe,

Kandi mu bushangashirwe bukaze bwiwe azobahagarika umutima,

 6 Ati: “Jewe, jewe nyene, nimitse umwami wanje

Kuri Siyoni, umusozi wanje mweranda.”

 7 Reka mvuge ingingo ya Yehova;

Yambwiye ati: “Uri umwana wanje;

Jewe uno musi ncitse so.

 8 Nsaba, nguhe amahanga abe intoranwa yawe

N’impera z’isi zibe itongo wigabira.

 9 Uzobamenaguza agakoni k’ubutware k’icuma;

Nka kurya kw’icombo c’umubumvyi, uzobasavya.”

10 None rero, emwe mwa bami, nimukoreshe ugutahura kwimbitse;

Nimwemere mukosorwe, emwe mwa bacamanza b’isi.

11 Nimukorere Yehova hariko n’ugutinya,

Munezerwe hariko n’ukujugumira.

12 Nimusome wa mwana, Imana ntirurumbe ishavu

Kandi ntimuyogere ngo muve mu nzira,

Kuko ishavu ryiwe rirurumba bitagoranye.

Hahiriwe abo bose bahungira muri we.

^ Zab. 2:2 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
