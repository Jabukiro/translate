Zaburi  20 : 1 - 9

Ku muyobozi. Agahibongozo ka Dawidi.

20 Yehova akwishure ku musi w’amarushwa.

Izina ry’Imana ya Yakobo rigukingire.

 2 Narungike ugutabarwa kwawe ari ha hantu heranda,

Agushigikire ari i Siyoni ubwaho.

 3 Niyibuke amashikanwa yawe yose,

Yemere ishikanwa ryawe riturirwa, abone ko rinuze. Sela.

 4 Naguhe nk’uko umutima wawe ugomba,

Ashitse inama yawe yose.

 5 Tuzokwiha akamo k’umunezero kubera ubukiriro bwawe,

Kandi mw’izina ry’Imana yacu tuzoduza amabendera yacu.

Yehova ashitse ivyo usaba vyose.

 6 Ubu ndazi koko yuko Yehova atabura gukiza uwo yarobanuje amavuta.

Amwishura ari mu majuru yiwe meranda

Biciye ku bikorwa vy’inkomezi vyo gukiza vy’ukuryo kwiwe.

 7 Bamwe bavuga ivyerekeye imikogote abandi ivyerekeye amafarasi,

Ariko tweho ivyerekeye izina rya Yehova Imana yacu ni vyo tuzovuga.

 8 Abo nyene baravunitse, baragwa;

Ariko tweho turahagurutse, kugira dusubirane.

 9 Ewe Yehova, kiza umwami!

Azotwishura ku musi duhamagaye.
