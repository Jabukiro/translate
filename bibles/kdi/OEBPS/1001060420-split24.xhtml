Zaburi  24 : 1 - 10

Ni izaburi ya Dawidi. Agahibongozo.

24 Isi n’ibiyuzuye ni ivya Yehova,

Ubutaka bwimbuka n’ababubako.

 2 Kuko ku biyaga ari ho we ubwiwe yayishinze ikaguma,

Kandi ku nzuzi ni ho ayigumiza ishimangiye.

 3 Ni nde yoduga ku musozi wa Yehova,

Kandi ni nde yohagarara ahantu heranda hiwe?

 4 Ni uwo wese w’intungane mu biganza vyiwe kandi adahumanye mu mutima wiwe,

Atatwaye ubuzima bwanje* mu bitagira mvura,

Canke ngo arahire ahendana.

 5 Azotwara umuhezagiro uvuye kuri Yehova

N’ubugororotsi buvuye ku Mana y’ubukiriro bwiwe.

 6 Iri ni ryo yaruka ry’abayirondera,

Ry’abahiga mu maso hawe, ewe Mana ya Yakobo! Sela.

 7 “Rarike imitwe, yemwe marembo,

Mwikirurutse, yemwe minjiriro aramba,

Kugira Umwami aninahaye yinjire!”

 8 “Ni nde none uwo Mwami aninahaye?”

“Yehova uwukomeye kandi w’umunyabubasha,

Yehova w’umunyabubasha mu rugamba.”

 9 “Duze imitwe, yemwe marembo;

Egome, nimuyikirurutse, yemwe minjiriro aramba,

Kugira Umwami aninahaye yinjire!”

10 “Na we ni nde none, uwo Mwami aninahaye?”

“Yehova nyen’ingabo*, uyo ni we Mwami aninahaye.” Sela.

^ Zab. 24:4 “Bwanje” bishaka kuvuga “bw’Imana.”

^ Zab. 24:10 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
