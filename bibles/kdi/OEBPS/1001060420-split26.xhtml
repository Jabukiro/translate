Zaburi  26 : 1 - 12

Ni izaburi ya Dawidi.

26 Ncira urubanza, Yehova we, kuko jewe nagendeye mu gutungana kwanje,

Kandi Yehova ni we nizigiye, kugira ngo sinyoganyoge.

 2 Nsuzuma, Yehova we, ungeze;

Tyorora amafyigo yanje n’umutima wanje.

 3 Kuko ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwawe buri imbere y’amaso yanje,

Kandi nagendeye mu kuri kwawe.

 4 Sinicaranye n’abantu b’ababeshi;

Kandi sininjirana n’abanyegeza ico bari.

 5 Nanse ishengero ry’inkozi z’ikibi,

Kandi sinicarana n’ababisha.

 6 Nzokaraba ibiganza vyanje mu gutungana,

Kandi nzotambuka nkikuza igicaniro* cawe, ewe Yehova,

 7 Kugira numvikanishe cane ugukenguruka,

No kugira menyeshe ibikorwa vyawe vy’agatangaza vyose.

 8 Yehova, nakunze inzu ubamwo

Be n’ahantu ubuninahazwa bwawe buba.

 9 Ntunjaniranye n’abacumuzi,

Canke ubuzima bwanje ngo ubujaniranye n’abagirwa n’amaraso,

10 Abo mu maboko yabo hari ubushirasoni*,

Ukuryo kwabo na kwo kukaba kwuzuye ibiturire.

11 Nayo jewe, ugutungana kwanje ni kwo nzogenderamwo.

Nuncungure kandi ungirire ubutoni.

12 Ikirenge canje ntikizobura guhagarara ahashashe;

Nzohezagira Yehova mu makinja akoranye.

^ Zab. 26:6 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ Zab. 26:10 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
