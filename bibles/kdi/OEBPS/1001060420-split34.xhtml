Zaburi  34 : 1 - 22

Ni izaburi ya Dawidi, igihe yigira umusazi imbere ya Abimeleki, bigatuma amwirukana, akagenda.

א [ʼAlefu]

34 Nzohezagira Yehova ibihe vyose;

Ishemezo ryiwe rizokwama mu kanwa kanje.

ב [Beti]

 2 Umutima wanje uzokwirata muri Yehova;

Abatekereza bazokwumva banezerwe.

ג [Gimelu]

 3 Yoo! nimuninahaze Yehova hamwe nanje,

Dushirane hejuru izina ryiwe!

ד [Daleti]

 4 Narasiguje Yehova, na we aranyishura,

Andokora mu binca ivutu vyose.

ה [Heʼ]

 5 Baramwitegereje, barakayangana,

Mu maso habo ntihoshobora kumaramara.

ז [Zayini]

 6 Uyu murushwa yarahamagaye, Yehova ubwiwe arumva.

Yaramukijije amarushwa yiwe yose.

ח [Heti]

 7 Umumarayika wa Yehova akambitse irya n’ino y’abatinya Imana,

Kandi arabakiza.

ט [Teti]

 8 Nimuhonje, mubone ko Yehova aryoshe;

Hahiriwe umuntu w’umugabo ahungira muri we.

י [Yodi]

 9 Nimutinye Yehova, yemwe beranda biwe,

Kuko abamutinya ata co babura.

כ [Kafu]

10 Intambwe z’imikangara z’imigāra ubwazo zaraburaniwe zirasonza;

Yamara abarondera Yehova bobo, nta kintu ciza bazobura.

ל [Lamedi]

11 Nimuze, bana, munyumvirize;

Ugutinya Yehova ni kwo nzobigisha.

מ [Memu]

12 Ni nde muntu ahimbarwa n’ubuzima,

Akunda imisi ihagije ngo abone iciza?

נ [Nuni]

13 Zigama ururimi rwawe ururinde ikibi,

N’iminwa yawe uyirinde kuvuga ivy’ubuhendanyi.

ס [Sameki]

14 Nureke ikibi, ukore iciza;

Rondera amahoro, uyakurikirane.

ע [ʽAyini]

15 Amaso ya Yehova ari ku bagororotsi,

Kandi amatwi yiwe ari ku gutabaza kwabo.

פ [Peʼ]

16 Amaso ya Yehova arwanya abakora ikibi,

Kugira abarandure ntibabe bakivugwa kw’isi.

צ [Tsade]

17 Bararize, Yehova na we ubwiwe arumva,

Arabarokora abakiza amarushwa yabo yose.

ק [Kofu]

18 Yehova ari hafi y’ab’umutima umenetse;

Kandi abajanjaguritse mu mutima arabakiza.

ר [Reshi]

19 Ivyago vy’umugororotsi ni vyinshi,

Yamara Yehova aramurokora akabimukiza vyose.

ש [Shini]

20 Arinda amagufa yiwe yose;

Nta na rimwe muri yo ryavunywe.

ת [Tau]

21 Ivyago bizokwica umubisha;

Kandi abanka umugororotsi bazoharurwako icaha.

22 Yehova acungura ubuzima bw’abasavyi biwe;

Kandi nta n’umwe mu bahungira muri we azoharurwako icaha.
