Zaburi  37 : 1 - 40

Ni izaburi ya Dawidi.

א [ʼAlefu]

37 Nturungure kubera inkozi z’ikibi.

Ntiwumvire agashari abakora ibitagororotse.

 2 Kuko bazokabirana ningoga nk’ivyatsi,

Bayame nk’ivyatsi bitoto bikiri bishasha.

ב [Beti]

 3 Niwizigire Yehova, ukore iciza;

Niwibere mw’isi, ukorane ubwizigirwa.

 4 Vyongeye, nusoserwe na Yehova,

Na we azoguha ivyo umutima wawe usaba.

ג [Gimelu]

 5 Nuragize Yehova inzira yawe,

Kandi umwizigire, na we ubwiwe azogira ico akoze.

 6 Kandi ntazobura kuzana ubugororotsi bwawe nk’umuco,

N’ubutungane bwawe nk’umutaga.

ד [Daleti]

 7 Guma ucereje imbere ya Yehova,

Umurindire ubishashaye.

Nturungure kubera uwutuma inzira yiwe iroranirwa,

Kubera umuntu ashitsa ivyiyumviro afise.

ה [Heʼ]

 8 Reka ishavu, uhebe ubushangashirwe;

Nturungure kugira gusa ngo ukore ikibi.

 9 Kuko inkozi z’ikibi zizorandurwa,

Ariko abizigira Yehova ni bo bazokwigabira isi.

ו [Vau]

10 Kandi hasigaye akanya gatoyi, umubisha ntabe akiriho;

Kandi uzokwitegereza koko aho yahora, umubure.

11 Mugabo abatekereza bazokwigabira isi,

Kandi bazokwinovora amahoro menshi.

ז [Zayini]

12 Umubisha agīra umugambi mubi umugororotsi,

Akamuhekenyera amenyo.

13 Yehova ubwiwe azomutwenga,

Kuko abona neza yuko umusi wiwe uzoza.

ח [Heti]

14 Ababisha basokoye inkota, bafora n’umuheto wabo,

Ngo bagwishe umuntu w’umurushwa kandi w’umworo,

Ngo bakerere abagororotse mu nzira yabo.

15 Inkota yabo izobinjira mu mutima,

Imiheto yabo na yo ivunagurwe.

ט [Teti]

16 Hopfuma dukeyi tw’umugororotsi

Kuruta umurengera w’ababisha benshi.

17 Kuko amaboko y’ababisha azovunagurwa,

Mugabo Yehova azoshigikira abagororotsi.

י [Yodi]

18 Yehova arazi imisi y’abantu batagira amahinyu,

Kandi intoranwa yabo izogumaho no gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

19 Ntibazomaramara mu gihe c’ivyago,

Kandi mu misi y’ikigoyi bazohaga.

כ [Kafu]

20 Kuko ababisha bazoyogera,

Abansi ba Yehova na bo bakazomera nk’akaryoshe k’amaragiriro;

Bategerezwa guherengetera. Emwe, bazoherengetera bacike umwotsi.

ל [Lamedi]

21 Umubisha aragurana ntiyishure,

Mugabo umugororotsi agira impuhwe kandi agatanga ingabirano.

22 Kuko abo ahezagira bazokwigabira isi,

Mugabo abo ahamagarirako akabi bazorandurwa.

מ [Memu]

23 Intambuko z’umuntu w’umugabo zateguwe na Yehova,

Kandi Imana irahimbarwa n’inzira yiwe.

24 Naho yotemba, ntazoraririzwa hasi,

Kuko Yehova ashigikiye ukuboko kwiwe.

נ [Nuni]

25 Narabaye umusore, mbere ndashaje,

Yamara sinabonye umugororotsi yahebwe buhere,

Canke uruvyaro rwiwe rusēga umukate.

26 Agira impuhwe kandi agatanga ingurane bukarinda bwira,

Bigatuma uruvyaro rwiwe rwitega umuhezagiro.

ס [Sameki]

27 Nureke ikibi, ukore iciza,

Uheze wibereho gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

28 Kuko Yehova akunda ubutungane,

Kandi ntazoheba intahemuka ziwe.

ע [ʼAyini]

Vy’ukuri zizorindwa gushika igihe kitagira urugero;

Nayo uruvyaro rw’ababisha, ruzorandurwa koko.

29 Abagororotsi bazokwigabira isi,

Kandi bazoyibako ibihe bidahera.

פ [Peʼ]

30 Akanwa k’umugororotsi ni ko kavuga ubukerebutsi n’ijwi ritoyi,

Kandi ururimi rwiwe ni rwo rucisha aho ukuri kuri.

31 Itegeko ry’Imana yiwe riri mu mutima wiwe;

Intambuko ziwe ntizizonyoganyoga.

צ [Tsade]

32 Umubisha aguma areretse umugororotsi,

Akarondera kumwica.

33 Nayo Yehova, ntazomuhebera mu kuboko kw’uyo,

Ntazomwita umubisha igihe acirwa urubanza.

ק [Kofu]

34 Niwizigire Yehova, ugumye inzira yiwe,

Na we azogushira hejuru wigarurire isi.

Ababisha nibarandurwa, uzobibona.

ר [Reshi]

35 Narabonye umubisha ari intwazamukazo,

Agatagarara nk’igiti gitotahaye mw’ivu ryiwe kavukire.

36 Yamara arahita, ntiyaba akiriho;

Nagumye ndamurondera, arabura.

ש [Shini]

37 Itegereze uwutagirako umugayo, ugume uraba uwugororotse,

Kuko kazoza k’uyo muntu kazorangwa n’amahoro.

38 Ariko abagabitanya bazozimanganirizwa icarimwe ata kabuza;

Kazoza k’ababisha kazorandurwa koko.

ת [Tau]

39 Ubukiriro bw’abagororotsi buva kuri Yehova;

Ni we ababera iboma mu gihe c’amarushwa.

40 Kandi Yehova azobatabara, abarokore.

Azobarokora abakure mu vyara vy’ababisha, abakize,

Kuko bahungiye muri we.
