Zaburi  40 : 1 - 17

Ku muyobozi. Ni izaburi ya Dawidi, ni agahibongozo.

40 Narizigiye Yehova rwose,

Na we araheza antega ugutwi, yumva ugutabaza kwanje.

 2 Kandi yarandugije ankura mu cobo kiroha,

Mu rwondo rw’ibishanga vyo mu ndiba.

Maze yadugije ibirenge vyanje ku rutare;

Yashimangiye intambuko zanje.

 3 Vyongeye, yashize mu kanwa kanje ururirimbo rushasha,

Ururirimbo rwo gushemeza Imana yacu.

Benshi bazobibona batinye,

Kandi bazokwizigira Yehova.

 4 Hahiriwe umuntu w’umugabo yagize Yehova amizero yiwe

Kandi atahindukirije mu maso hiwe ku bantu ata co bibanga,

Canke ku bantu bihereza ibinyoma.

 5 Wewe ubwawe wakoze ibintu vyinshi,

Ewe Yehova Mana yanje, mbere n’ibikorwa vy’agatangaza vyawe n’ivyiyumviro udufiseko;

Nta n’umwe ariho yogereranywa nawe.

Hamwe norondera kubivuga no kubiyaga,

Ni vyinshi kuruta ivyo nodonda.

 6 Ibimazi n’amashikanwa ntivyaguhimbaye;

Aya matwi yanje warayazibūye.

Amashikanwa aturirwa be n’amashikanwa y’igicumuro nta yo wasavye.

 7 Ku bw’ivyo, navuze nti: “Ehe naje,

Mu muzingo w’igitabu bikaba vyanditswe kuri jewe.

 8 Gukora ukugomba kwawe, ewe Mana yanje, ni vyo vyampimbaye,

Kandi itegeko ryawe riri imbere muri jewe.

 9 Naravuze inkuru nziza y’ubugororotsi mw’ishengero rinini.

Ehe iminwa yanje sindayitangira.

Ewe Yehova, wewe ubwawe ivyo urabizi neza.

10 Ubugororotsi bwawe sinabupfukiranije mu mutima wanje.

Ubwizigirwa bwawe n’ubukiriro bwawe narabitangaje.

Sinanyegeje ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwawe n’ukuri kwawe mw’ishengero rinini.”

11 Ewe Yehova, ubwawe ntutangīre ikigongwe cawe ngo ntikinshikire.

Ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwawe be n’ukuri kwawe nibinzigame nantaryo.

12 Kuko ivyago vyankikuje ku buryo bidaharurika.

Amakosa yanje yarampambije ari menshi kuruta ayo nashobora kubona;

Yabaye menshi kuruta umushatsi wo ku mutwe wanje,

Kandi umutima wanje waramvuyemwo.

13 Ewe Yehova, nuhimbarwe no kundokora.

Ewe Yehova, nyaragasa untabare.

14 Nibamaramare barye ivyatsi bose hamwe,

Abo barondera ubuzima bwanje ngo babuzimanganye.

Nibahindukire bateterwe, abo bahimbarwa n’ivyago vyanje.

15 Nibarabe bumiwe kubera ibimaramare vyabo,

Abo bambwira ngo: “Ahaa! Ahaa!”

16 Abo nibigine, banezerwe muri wewe,

Abo bose bakurondera.

Abo nibame bavuga bati: “Yehova nashimagizwe,”

Abo bakunda ubukiriro bwawe.

17 Mugabo jewe ndi umurushwa n’umworo.

Yehova ubwiwe aranyitaho.

Uri umufasha wanje ukaba n’uwunkiza.

Ewe Mana yanje, ntutebe birenze.
