Zaburi  50 : 1 - 23

Agahibongozo ka Asafu.

50 Nyenubumana, Imana, Yehova, ubwiwe aravuze,

Atangura guhamagara isi,

Kuva iyo izuba rirasira gushika iyo rirengera.

 2 Imana ubwayo yasayanganye iri i Siyoni, bwa bwiza butagira akanenge.

 3 Imana yacu izoza, kandi ntiyoshobora kuguma icereje.

Imbere yayo umuriro urarigata,

Kandi irya n’ino yayo hose habaye ikirura gikaze bihebuje.

 4 Ihamagara amajuru hejuru be n’isi

Kugira ngo ishitse urubanza ku bantu bayo:

 5 “Koranirize kuri jewe intahemuka zanje,

Barya bapfundika isezerano ryanje biciye ku bimazi.”

 6 Amajuru ayaga ubugororotsi bwayo,

Kuko Imana ubwayo ari Umucamanza. Sela.

 7 “Umvirize, bantu banje, ngira mvuge,

Ewe Isirayeli, ngira nshinge intahe ikwagiriza.

Ndi Imana, Imana yawe.

 8 Singukangira ku vyerekeye ibimazi vyawe

Canke amashikanwa yawe aturirwa yama imbere yanje.

 9 Nta shūri nzotora mu nzu yawe,

Canke amasuguru y’impene mu mpongore zawe.

10 Kuko igikoko cose co mu kibira ari rwanje,

Ibikoko vyo ku misozi igihumbi.

11 Ndazi neza ikiremwa c’amababa cose co mu misozi,

Kandi amasinzi y’ibikoko vyo mu gahinga ari kumwe nanje.

12 Hamwe noba nshonje, sinobikubwira;

Kuko ubutaka bwimbuka n’ibibwuzuye ari rwanje.

13 Mbega ndye inyama z’amashūri y’amanyabubasha?

Mbega nywe amaraso y’amasuguru y’impene?

14 Shikanira Imana ugukenguruka bwa bimazi vyawe,

Kandi ushitse indagano zawe kuri Musumbavyose;

15 Numpamagare ku musi w’amarushwa.

Nzogutabara, nawe uzoninahaza.”

16 Mugabo umubisha, Imana ntizobura kumubwira iti:

“Ni uburenganzira ki ufise bwo kudondagura ingingo nyobozi zanje,

Ngo utware isezerano ryanje mu kanwa kawe?

17 Erega wewe, waranse indero,

Kandi uguma uterera amajambo yanje inyuma yawe.

18 Igihe cose ubonye igisuma, warahimbarwa na co;

Kandi wasangira n’abarenga ibigo.

19 Akanwa kawe warakarekuye ngo kivugire ibibi,

Kandi ururimi rwawe uguma urufatanije n’ubuhendanyi.

20 Uricara ukavuga nabi mwene wanyu,

Ukandagaza akanenge ka mwene nyoko.

21 Ivyo bintu warabikoze, nanje nguma nicerereje.

Wiyumviriye yuko ntobuze kumera nkawe.

Ngira ndagukangire, kandi nzoshira ibintu mu buryo mu maso yawe.

22 Enda nimubitahure, mwebwe abibagira Imana,

Sindabatabagure ata wuri bubarokore.

23 Uwushikana ugukenguruka bwa kimazi ciwe ni we aninahaza;

Nayo uwugumya inzira yashinzwe,

Nzomuha kubona ubukiriro Imana itanga.”
