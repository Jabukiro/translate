Zaburi  55 : 1 - 23

Ku muyobozi, ku bicurarangisho vy’imirya. Masikili. Ni izaburi ya Dawidi.

55 Ewe Mana, tega ugutwi isengesho ryanje;

Ntiwinyegeze ngo ntiwumve ugusaba ubutoni kwanje.

 2 Nyumviriza, unyishure.

Nembembeshwa n’amaganya yanje,

Nkicwa n’ukudurumbana,

 3 Kubera ijwi ry’umwansi, kubera umukazo uterwa n’umubisha.

Kuko baguma bantibagurirako ibintu bibabaza,

Bakambikira akazikira bashavuye.

 4 Umutima wanje uri mu mubabaro ukaze muri jewe,

Kandi uguca ivutu kw’urupfu kunguyeko.

 5 Ubwoba, egome, ukujugumira kunyinjiramwo,

Uguhinda agashitsi na kwo kukampfuka.

 6 Nguma mvuga nti: “Ese iyo mba mfise amababa nk’inuma!

Nogurukiye kure nkiberayo.

 7 Ehe! Nokwomokeye kure;

Nokwibereye mu gahinga.—Sela—

 8 Nokwihuse nja ahantu mpungira

Nkikinga umuyaga w’ikivumbuzi, nkikinga igihuhusi.”

 9 Nubavurunganye, Yehova we, utume batavuga rumwe,

Kuko mbonye ubukazi n’ibihari mu gisagara.

10 Umurango n’ijoro barakizunguruka ku mpome zaco;

Kandi ibibabaza be n’ivyago bibarizwa muri co.

11 Amarushwa abarizwa muri co;

Kandi agahahazo n’ubuhendanyi ntivyavuye mu kibanza caho ca bose.

12 Kuko atari umwansi yansize iceyi;

Ahandiho nari kuvyihanganira.

Uwunyanka bikaze si we yanyirīyeko;

Ahandiho nari kumwihisha.

13 Ariko ni wewe, umuntu buntu yari nk’uwo tungana,

Uwo dusanganywe kandi tuzinanyi,

14 Kuko twahora twinovorera hamwe ubugenzi somambike busosa;

Twahora tujana n’ikinja ry’abantu mu nzu y’Imana.

15 Ugucika umusaka nikube kuri bo!

Nibamanuke mu kuzimu babona;

Kuko igihe bari babayeho ari ba kavamahanga, muri bo hari ibintu bibi.

16 Nayo jewe, nzosemerera ntakambira Imana;

Kandi Yehova ubwiwe azonkiza.

17 Umugoroba n’igitondo n’umutaga nicwa n’amaganya kandi nkanihagira,

Na we akumva ijwi ryanje.

18 Ntazobura gucungura ubuzima bwanje akabushira mu mahoro, akabukiza indwano yo kundwanya,

Kuko abandwanya babaye indiri.

19 Imana izokwumva, ibishure,

Umwe avyagiye nka kera—Sela—

Bamwe batagira amahinduka

Kandi batatinye Imana.

20 Yatumye amaboko ngo agirire nabi ababanye na we amahoro;

Yahumanije isezerano ryiwe.

21 Amajambo y’akanwa kiwe aremvye kuruta amasoro,

Mugabo umutima wiwe uguma urondera indwano.

Amajambo yiwe yoroshe kuruta amavuta,

Mugabo ni inkota zisokoye.

22 Terera umuzigo wawe kuri Yehova ubwiwe,

Na we ubwiwe azogushigikira.

Ntazokwigera yemera ko umugororotsi anyiganyiga.

23 Ariko wewe, ewe Mana, uzobamanukana mu cobo cimbitse kuruta ibindi.

Nayo abagīrwa n’amaraso kandi b’abahendanyi, ntibazobaho n’ica kabiri c’imisi yabo.

Ariko jewe, nzokwizigira wewe.
