Zaburi  58 : 1 - 11

Ku muyobozi. “Ntuhonye.” Ni izaburi ya Dawidi. Mikitamu.

58 Mbega mu gacerere kanyu mwovuga koko ivy’ubugororotsi?

Mbega mwoca urubanza mu kugororoka, ga yemwe bana b’abantu?

 2 Ahubwo riho, ese ingene mukorana umutima wanyu ibitagororotse vyeruye mw’isi,

Mugategurira inzira ubukazi bw’amaboko yanyu!

 3 Ababisha bagoramye kuva bakiri mu gitereko;

Bayerereye kuva bakiri mu nda;

Bavuga ibinyoma.

 4 Ubwayi bwabo ni nk’ubwayi bw’inzoka,

Bakaba ibipfamatwi nk’imvuvyi yiziba ugutwi,

 5 Itumviriza ijwi ry’abagosi,

Naho umuntu w’inkerebutsi ayibohesha imitongero.

 6 Ewe Mana, nubahongore amenyo mu kanwa kabo.

Numenagure imibangabanga y’intambwe z’imikangara z’imigāra, ewe Yehova!

 7 Nibayonge nk’abaguye mu mazi yigira;

Nafore umuheto ku bw’imyampi yiwe, mu gihe na bo binaga.

 8 Nk’igifyera kiriko kirashonga, ni ko agenda;

Nk’inda y’umugore ikorotse, ntibazogenda babonye izuba.

 9 Imbere y’uko inkono zanyu zumva umubambangwe ucanywe,

Umubambangwe mubisi be n’uwaka, azoyivumbura nk’umuyaga w’ikirura.

10 Umugororotsi azonezerwa kuko abonye uguhōra.

Intambuko ziwe azozuhagira mu maraso y’umubisha.

11 Kandi abantu bazovuga bati: “Ni ukuri, haraba ivyamwa ku mugororotsi.

Ni ukuri, hariho Imana ica imanza mw’isi.”
