Zaburi  66 : 1 - 20

Ku muyobozi. Ururirimbo, agahibongozo.

66 Nimuvugirize Imana akaruru k’intsinzi, yemwe abo kw’isi mwese!

 2 Nimuhibongoreze ubuninahazwa bw’izina ryiwe.

Nimutume ishemezo ryiwe riba irininahaye.

 3 Bwire Imana muti: “Ese ingene ibikorwa vyawe biteye ubwoba!

Kubera ubwinshi bw’inkomezi zawe, abansi bawe bazoza kuri wewe bahinda agashitsi.

 4 Abo kw’isi bose bazokwunamira,

Kandi bazoguhibongoreza, bazohibongoreza izina ryawe.” Sela.

 5 Nimuze murabe ibikorwa vy’Imana.

Ivyo igirira abana b’abantu birateye ubwoba.

 6 Ikiyaga yagihinduye isi yumutse;

Bajabutse n’amaguru baciye mu ruzi.

Ng’aho, dutangura kunezerwa mu Mana.

 7 Iganzisha ubushobozi bwayo gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

Amaso yayo aguma areretse amahanga.

Nayo intagondwa, ntizishire hejuru. Sela.

 8 Hezagire Imana yacu, yemwe bisata vy’abantu,

Mwumvikanishe ijwi ry’ukuyishemeza.

 9 Idushira mu buzima,

Kandi ntiyaretse ikirenge cacu ngo kinyiganyige.

10 Kuko wadusuzumye, ewe Mana;

Wadutyoroye nk’uwutyorora ifeza.

11 Watwinjije mu rusenga rw’uruhigi;

Wadushize umukazo mu mafyinga.

12 Watumye umuntu buntu atugendera ku mutwe;

Twaciye mu muriro no mu mazi,

Uradusohora utuzana ku gutabarwa.

13 Nzoza mu nzu yawe mfise amashikanwa aturirwa uko yakabaye;

Nzoshitsa indagano zanje kuri wewe,

14 Izo iminwa yanje yasamye ikavuga,

N’umunwa wanje ukazivuga igihe nari ngeze habi.

15 Nzogushikanira amashikanwa aturirwa uko yakabaye y’ibitungwa bikiri bito binuze,

Kumwe n’umwotsi w’ibimazi w’impfizi z’intama.

Nzohereza ishūri hamwe n’amasuguru y’impene. Sela.

16 Nimuze mwumvirize, yemwe abatinya Imana mwese, nigane

Ivyo yankoreye.

17 Narayihamagaye n’akanwa kanje,

Ururimi rwanje na rwo rurayihayagiza.

18 Nimba mu mutima wanje naritegereje ikibi na kimwe,

Yehova ntazonyumva.

19 Vy’ukuri, Imana yarumvise;

Yateze ugutwi ijwi ry’isengesho ryanje.

20 Hahezagirwe Imana itigijeyo isengesho ryanje,

Canke ngo yigize ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwayo kure yanje.
