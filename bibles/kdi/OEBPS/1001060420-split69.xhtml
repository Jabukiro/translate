Zaburi  69 : 1 - 36

Ku muyobozi, ku Malisi. Ni izaburi ya Dawidi.

69 Nkiza Mana, kuko amazi yaje agashika no ku buzima bwanje.

 2 Ndanyitse mu rwondo rugera kure, ahatagira aho guhagarara.

Ninjiye mu mazi maremare,

Kandi umugezi utemba urankukumuye.

 3 Narushe no gusemerera;

Umuhogo wanje wasaraye.

Amaso yanje yagwabiye ndindiriye Imana yanje.

 4 Abanyankira ubusa babaye benshi mbere kuruta n’umushatsi wo ku mutwe wanje.

Abarondera kunumya, bakaba ari abansi banje ata mvo, babaye benshi.

Ico ntari natwaye ku bwambuzi, naciye ndagisubiza.

 5 Ewe Mana, wewe ubwawe waramenye ubupfu bwanje,

Kandi ukwagirwa n’icaha kwanje ntiwakunyegejwe.

 6 Ese abakwizigira batomaramara kubera jewe,

Ewe Mukama Segaba, Yehova nyen’ingabo!

Ese abakurondera batoteterwa kubera jewe,

Ewe Mana ya Isirayeli!

 7 Kuko nikoreye iceyi kubera wewe,

Agateterwe kampfutse mu maso.

 8 Nsigaye ninubwa na bene wacu,

Nkaba n’umunyamahanga kuri bene mama.

 9 Kuko umwete w’inzu yawe wandiye,

Kandi ibitutsi vy’abagutuka vyaguye kuri jewe.

10 Natanguye kurira hariko n’ukwisonzesha kwanje,

Mugabo vyamviriyemwo iceyi.

11 Igihe nagira igunira icambarwa canje,

Nacitse iciro ry’umugani kuri bo.

12 Abicaye mw’irembo batanguye kurazwa ishinga nanje,

Kandi ni je nari indirimbo z’abanywi b’inzoga iboreza.

13 Nayo jewe, isengesho ryanje naritura wewe, ewe Yehova,

Ku gihe cemerwa, ewe Mana!

Mu bwinshi bw’ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwawe nunyishurane ukuri kw’ubukiriro bwawe.

14 Ndokora unkure mu rwondo, kugira ngo sinyike.

Ese norokorwa ngakizwa abanyanka be n’amazi maremare.

15 Ese umugezi utemba w’amazi utonkukumura,

Canke ngo ibwina hamire,

Canke ngo iriba rindumanirizeko akanwa karyo.

16 Nunyishure, Yehova we, kuko ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwawe ari agahore.

Nk’uko imbabazi zawe ari indiri, nuhindukirire aho ndi,

17 Kandi ntuhishe mu maso hawe umusavyi wawe.

Kubera ko ngeze habi, nunyishure ningoga.

18 Egera ubuzima bwanje, ubuhabuze;

Nuncungure kubera abansi banje.

19 Wewe ubwawe waramenye iceyi canje n’ibimaramare vyanje n’agateterwe kanje.

Abanyanka bose bari imbere yawe.

20 Iceyi cavunye umutima wanje, kandi igikomere ntigikira.

Naguma nizigiye ko hagira uwunyumvira ikibabarwe, mugabo ntihaboneka n’umwe;

Naguma nizigiye ko haba abahoza, mugabo sinaronka n’umwe.

21 Mugabo bampaye igiterwa c’ubumara ngo kimbere ibifungurwa,

Bagerageza kunywesha inkarihe ngo banzimye inyota.

22 Imeza yabo nibe umutego imbere yabo,

Icotumye bamererwa neza na co kibe umushibuka.

23 Amaso yabo nacure umwiza kugira ngo ntibabone;

Nutume amafyinga yabo anyoganyoga nantaryo.

24 Nubasukeko imisibo yawe,

Ishavu ryawe rirurumba ribahamvye.

25 Ikambi yabo izitijwe uruhome nicike umusaka;

Mu mahema yabo ntihaboneke uwuhaba.

26 Kuko umuntu wewe ubwawe wakubise ari we bakurikiranye,

Imibabaro y’abo watobekeranije ikaba ari yo baguma badonda.

27 Ongera ikosa kw’ikosa ryabo,

Kandi ntibinjire mu bugororotsi bwawe.

28 Nibahanagurwe mu gitabu c’abazima,

Kandi ntibandikwemwo hamwe n’abagororotsi.

29 Mugabo jewe ndi umurushwa kandi ndasasatwa.

Ewe Mana, ubukiriro bwawe nibunkingire.

30 Nzoshemeza izina ry’Imana n’ururirimbo,

Kandi nzoyininaharisha ugukenguruka.

31 Ivyo kandi bizohimbara Yehova kuruta ishūri,

Kuruta ikimasa c’amahembe, gisatuye ikinono.

32 Abatekereza ntibazobura kubibona; bazonezerwa.

Mwebwe murondera Imana, umutima wanyu nubandanye kubaho.

33 Kuko Yehova yumviriza aboro,

Kandi ntazogenda asuzuguye imbohe ziwe.

34 Ijuru n’isi nibimushemeze,

Ibiyaga n’ibigendera muri vyo vyose.

35 Kuko Imana ubwayo izokiza Siyoni,

Yubake ibisagara vya Yuda;

Kandi ntibazobura kwiberayo, bahigarurire.

36 Uruvyaro rw’abasavyi bayo ruzohatorana,

Kandi abakunda izina ryayo ni bo bazohaba.
