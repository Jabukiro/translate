Zaburi  71 : 1 - 24

71 Muri wewe, ewe Yehova, ni ho mpungiye.

Yoo! ese ntokwigera maramara!

 2 Mu bugororotsi bwawe nundokore, unkize.

Ntega ugutwi, unkize.

 3 Numbere iboma ry’igitandara ryo kwinjiramwo nantaryo.

Tegeka nkizwe,

Kuko uri urutare rwanje n’igihome canje gikomeye.

 4 Ewe Mana yanje, nunkize ukuboko kw’umubisha,

Unkize ikiganza c’uwukora ivy’akarenganyo n’agahahazo.

 5 Kuko uri icizigiro canje, ewe Mukama Segaba Yehova, umwizero wanje kuva mu buto bwanje.

 6 Ni wewe nishimikije kuva nkiri mu nda;

Ni wewe wantandukanije n’ubura bwa mama.

Muri wewe ni ho ishemezo ryanje ryama.

 7 Nacitse nk’igitangaro ku bantu benshi;

Mugabo ni wewe buhungiro bwanje bukomeye.

 8 Akanwa kanje kuzuye ishemezo ryawe,

Kaguma kuzuye ubwiza bwawe bukarinda bwira.

 9 Ntunte mu gihe c’ubusaza;

Igihe nyene inkomezi zanje ziriko zirayama, ntumpebe.

10 Kuko abansi banje bavuze ku binyerekeye,

N’abaguma bareretse ubuzima bwanje bagiye hamwe baja inama,

11 Ngo: “Imana ubwayo yamuhevye.

Mukurikirane mumufate, kuko ata murokozi ariho.”

12 Ewe Mana, ntungume kure.

Ewe Mana yanje, nyaruka umfashe.

13 Nibamaramare, bahone, abo barwanya ubuzima bwanje.

Nibipfuke iceyi n’agateterwe, abo bandonderera ivyago.

14 Nayo jewe, nzoguma ndindiriye,

Kandi nzokwongereza ishemezo ryawe ryose.

15 Akanwa kanje kazodonda ubugororotsi bwawe,

Kadonde ubukiriro bwawe burinde bwira,

Kuko ntamenye ibitigiri vyavyo.

16 Nzozanana ubushobozi buhambaye, ewe Mukama Segaba Yehova;

Nzovuga ubugororotsi bwawe, ubwawe gusa.

17 Ewe Mana, wanyigishije kuva mu buto bwanje,

Kandi gushika n’ubu nguma ntangaza ibikorwa vyawe vy’agatangaza.

18 Mbere no gushika mu gihe c’ubusaza n’imvi ku mutwe, ewe Mana, ntumpebe,

Gushika mbwiye iyaruka ivy’ukuboko kwawe,

Mbwiye abazoza bose ivy’ubushobozi bwawe.

19 Ubugororotsi bwawe, ewe Mana, bugereza iyo hejuru;

Nayo ku biraba ibikomeye wakoze,

Ni nde ga Mana ameze nkawe?

20 Kubera ko watumye mbona amarushwa menshi n’ivyago vyinshi,

Nungarurire agatege;

Ongera unduze, unkure mu nyenga z’amazi z’isi.

21 Niwongereze ubukuru bwanje,

Unkikuze, umpoze.

22 Nanje, nzoguhaya ku gicurarangisho c’imirya,

Ku biraba ukuri kwawe, ewe Mana yanje!

Nzoguhibongoreza ku nanga, ewe Mweranda wa Isirayeli!

23 Iminwa yanje izokwiha akamo k’umunezero igihe nzoba numva noguhibongoreza,

Emwe, jewe uwo wacunguye.

24 Vyongeye, ururimi rwanje, ruzovuga ubugororotsi bwawe n’ijwi ritoyi burinde bwira,

Kuko bamaramaye, kuko bariye ivyatsi, abandonderera ivyago.
