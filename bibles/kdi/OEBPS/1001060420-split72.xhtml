Zaburi  72 : 1 - 20

Ku biraba Salomo.

72 Ewe Mana, nuhe umwami ingingo zawe z’ubutungane,

Uhe ubugororotsi bwawe umwana w’umwami.

 2 Naburanire abantu bawe yisunze ubugororotsi,

N’abarushwa bawe yisunze ingingo y’ubutungane.

 3 Imisozi nishīre abantu amahoro,

N’imirambi nyene, biciye ku bugororotsi.

 4 Nacire urubanza abarushwa bo mu bantu,

Nakize abana b’umworo,

Ajanjagure umugunzi.

 5 Bazogutinya igihe cose hazoba hakiriho izuba,

N’imbere y’ukwezi, uko iyaruka rikurikira irindi.

 6 Azomanuka nk’imvura ku vyatsi bitemwe,

Nk’imicuncubuko y’imvura itotesha isi.

 7 Mu misi yiwe umugororotsi azonaga,

Kandi hazobaho amahoro menshi gushika ukwezi kutakiriho.

 8 Azogira abatwarwa kuva ku kiyaga gushika ku kindi,

No kuva kuri rwa Ruzi gushika ku mpera z’isi.

 9 Imbere yiwe ababa mu turere tugadutse bazokwunama,

Kandi abansi biwe bazorigata umukungugu.

10 Abami ba Tarushishi n’ab’amazinga—

Bazoriha ikibuguro.

Abami ba Sheba n’aba Seba—

Bazoshikana ituro.

11 Abami bose bazomukubitira inkoro hasi;

Nayo amahanga yose azomukorera.

12 Kuko azorokora umworo atabaza,

N’umurushwa be n’uwo wese atagira gifasha.

13 Azokwumvira akagongwe umuntu mutoyi be n’umworo,

Kandi ubuzima bw’aboro azobukiza.

14 Ubuzima bwabo azobucungura abukize agahahazo n’ubukazi,

Kandi amaraso yabo azoba ay’agaciro mu maso yiwe.

15 Niyibereho, kandi ahabwe ku nzahabu z’i Sheba.

Nasengerwe nantaryo;

Nahezagirwe burinde bwira.

16 Hazoba umukimba w’intete kw’isi;

Kw’isonga ry’imisozi hazoba umusesekara.

Ivyamwa vyiwe bizoba nk’i Libani,

Kandi abo mu gisagara bazoshurika nk’ibimera vyo kw’isi.

17 Izina ryiwe niribeho gushika igihe kitagira urugero;

Imbere y’izuba izina ryiwe nirisagambe,

Kandi biciye kuri we biheshe umuhezagiro;

Amahanga yose namwite umuhirwe.

18 Hahezagirwe Yehova Imana, ya Mana ya Isirayeli,

We wenyene akora ibikorwa vy’agatangaza.

19 Izina ryiwe rininahaye nirihezagirwe gushika igihe kitagira urugero,

Ubuninahazwa bwiwe bwuzure isi yose.

Amen kandi Amen.

20 Amasengesho ya Dawidi, mwene Yese, ararangiye.
