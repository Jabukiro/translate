Zaburi  78 : 1 - 72

Masikili. Ni izaburi ya Asafu.

78 Nimwumvirize itegeko ryanje, emwe bantu banje!

Nimutege ugutwi amajambo y’akanwa kanje.

 2 Ngira nteranure akanwa kanje mvugire mu mvugo y’akagani;

Ngira ntume udupfindo twa kera tududubiza,

 3 Utwo twumvise kandi tuzi,

Utwo kandi ba data batwiganiye;

 4 Utwo tutanyegeza abana babo,

Tukatwiganira n’iyaruka rizoza,

Tukavuga amashemezo ya Yehova n’inkomezi ziwe

Be n’ibintu vyiwe vy’agatangaza yakoze.

 5 Ahagurutsa icibutsa muri Yakobo,

Ashinga itegeko muri Isirayeli,

Ibintu yategetse ba sogokuruza bacu,

Ngo bimenyeshwe abana babo;

 6 Kugira ngo iyaruka ryoje, ari bo bana bari kuvuka, ribimenye,

Bahaguruke, bavyiganire abana babo,

 7 Kandi bashire umwizero wabo mu Mana ubwayo,

Ntibibagire ibikorwa Imana yakoze, ahubwo bubahirize amabwirizwa yayo.

 8 Kandi ntibari bakwiye gucika nka ba sekuruza babo,

Iyaruka ry’akagagazo kandi ry’irigarariji,

Iyaruka ry’abatari barateguye umutima wabo,

Kandi umutima wabo ntube umwizigirwa ku Mana.

 9 Bene Efurayimu, naho bari abarasisha umuheto bafise ibirwanisho,

Barasubije umugongo inyuma ku musi w’indwano.

10 Ntibagumije isezerano ry’Imana,

Banse kugendera mw’itegeko ryayo.

11 Batanguye kandi kwibagira ivyo ikora

Be n’ibikorwa vyayo vy’agatangaza yaberetse.

12 Mu maso ya ba sekuruza babo, yari yakoze ibitangaje

Mu gihugu ca Misiri, ka karere ka Zowani.

13 Yasatuye ikiyaga ngo itume barengana,

Yongera ituma amazi ahagarara nk’urugomero.

14 Ibandanya kubayobora ikoresheje igicu ku murango,

Ijoro ryose na ho ikoresheje umuco w’umuriro.

15 Isatura ibitandara mu gahinga,

Kugira ngo itume banywa amazi menshi nk’inyenga z’amazi.

16 Ikūra imigezi mu rutare,

Ituma amazi amanuka nk’inzuzi.

17 Mugabo baguma barushiriza kuyicumurako

Mu kugarariza Musumbavyose mu karere kagadutse;

18 Bageza Imana mu mutima wabo

Mu kwisabira ico kurya.

19 Nuko batangura kuvuga nabi Imana.

Ngo “Mbega Imana irashobora gutunganya imeza mu gahinga?”

20 Ehe! Yakubise igitandara

Ngo amazi atembe, n’inzuzi z’isumo zisegenyuke.

“Mbega irashobora no gutanga umukate,

Canke irashobora gutegurira abantu bayo ivyo kurya?”

21 Ni co catumye Yehova yumva, agatangura kuraka;

Umuriro wakirizwa Yakobo,

N’ishavu ridugira Isirayeli.

22 Kuko batizeye Imana,

Ntibizigira ubukiriro itanga.

23 Maze itegeka ibirere vyuzuye ibicu hejuru,

Yugurura imiryango y’ijuru.

24 Iguma igwisha kuri bo manu* ngo barye,

Ibaha intete zo mw’ijuru.

25 Abantu barya umukate w’abanyabubasha;

Ibarungikira ibifungurwa, barahaga.

26 Itangura gutuma umuyaga wo mu buseruko waduka mu majuru,

No gutuma umuyaga wo mu bumanuko uhuhuta biciye ku nkomezi zayo.

27 Iraheza ituma ivyo kurya bigwa kuri bo nk’umukungugu,

Egome, ibiremwa biguruka vy’amababa bigwa kuri bo nk’udutete tw’umusenyi tw’ibiyaga.

28 Iguma ibigwisha hagati mw’ikambi ryayo,

Irya n’ino y’amataberenakulo yayo.

29 Baraheza bararya barahaga cane,

Ivyo bipfuza irabibazanira.

30 Batarava ku cipfuzo cabo,

Ibifungurwa vyabo bikiri mu kanwa kabo,

31 Uburake bw’Imana burabadugira.

Nuko yica bamwe mu bantu babo b’indyamunyu;

Itsinda hasi abasore ba Isirayeli.

32 Naho habaye ivyo vyose, bongera gucumura

Ntibizera ibikorwa vyayo vy’agatangaza.

33 Nuko irangiza imisi yabo nk’aho ari impemu nsa zisohotse,

Imarisha imyaka yabo uruhagarara.

34 Igihe cose yabica, ni ko na bo babaza ivyayo,

Bakagaruka, bakarondera Imana.

35 Batangura kwibuka yuko Imana yari yo Gitandara cabo,

Be n’uko Imana Musumbavyose yari yo Muhōzi wabo.

36 Bagerageza kuyihenda n’akanwa kabo;

Bagerageza kuyibesha n’ururimi rwabo.

37 Umutima wabo ntiwari uhamye kuri yo;

Kandi ntibabaye abizigirwa mw’isezerano ryayo.

38 Mugabo yari inyembabazi; yarapfuka ikosa, ntihonye.

Kenshi yarasubiza inyuma ishavu ryayo,

Kandi ntiyavyura ubushangashirwe bwayo bwose.

39 Yaguma yibuka ko bari umubiri,

Yuko impwemu isohoka ntigaruke.

40 Ese ukuntu bayigarariza kenshi mu gahinga,

Bakayibabariza mu bugaragwa!

41 Kandi incuro n’izindi barageza Imana,

Bagatuntuza Umweranda wa Isirayeli.

42 Ntibibuka ukuboko kwayo,

Wa musi yabacungura ikabakiza umurwanizi,

43 Ingene yashize ibimenyetso vyayo mu Misiri

N’ibitangaro vyayo mu karere ka Zowani;

44 N’ingene yatanguye guhindura amaraso imigende yabo ya Nili,

Ku buryo badashobora kunywa ku migezi yabo.

45 Ibarungikako ibibugu, ngo bibarigate,

N’ibikere, ngo bibahonye.

46 Itangura guha inyenzi umwimbu wabo,

Ubucumukure bwabo ibuha inzige.

47 Yicisha umuzabibu wabo urubura,

Ibiti vyabo vy’imisukomora ivyicisha iminana y’urubura.

48 Ibitwazi vyabo ibihereza urubura,

Ibitungwa vyabo ibihereza inyonko irurumba.

49 Ibarungikako ishavu ryayo rirurumba,

Uburake n’imisibo n’amarushwa,

Imigwi y’intumwa y’abamarayika izana ivyago.

50 Itegurira ishavu ryayo umunyuro.

Ntiyabarinze urupfu;

Ubuzima bwabo yabuhereje ikiza.

51 Amaherezo iririka imfura zose mu Misiri,

Ubuzibūra bwabo*, mu mahema ya Hamu.

52 Mu nyuma ituma abantu bayo bafata inzira nk’umukuku,

Ibarongōra nk’ubusho mu gahinga.

53 Iguma ibayobora mu mutekano, ntibumva ubwoba namba;

Ikiyaga gipfuka abansi babo.

54 Ibazana mu cibare cayo ceranda,

Aka karere k’imisozi ukuryo kwayo kwironkeje.

55 Kandi kubera bo, igenda irirukana amahanga,

Ibagabagurira intoranwa ikoresheje ikamba yo gupimisha,

Ku buryo ituma imiryango ya Isirayeli yibera mu mihana yayo bwite.

56 Mugabo batangura kugeza no kugarariza Imana Musumbavyose,

Ntibagumya ivyibutsa vyayo.

57 Vyongeye, baguma basubira inyuma kandi bahemuka nka ba sekuruza babo;

Bahindukira nk’umuheto ufororotse.

58 Baguma bayibabarisha ibibanza vyabo bikirurutse*,

Baguma bayitera ishari n’ibishusho vyabo bibajwe.

59 Imana irumva, iraraka,

Bituma ikengera Isirayeli bimwe bibi.

60 Amaherezo iheba itaberenakulo y’i Shilo,

Ihema yabayemwo hagati mu bantu bakuwe mw’ivu.

61 Itanga inkomezi zayo ngo zigirwe imbohe,

Ubwiza bwayo ibutanga mu kuboko kw’umurwanizi.

62 Iguma itanga abantu bayo ku nkota,

Irarakira intoranwa yayo.

63 Abasore bayo umuriro urarigata,

Abigeme bayo b’isugi ntibashemagizwa.

64 Nayo abaherezi bayo, bagwishwa n’inkota,

Abapfakazi babo na bo ntibarira.

65 Maze Yehova yikangura nk’uwuvuye mw’itiro,

Nk’umunyenkomezi umuvinyu uvuyeko.

66 Aririka abarwanizi biwe ari inyuma yabo;

Abaha kugira iceyi c’ikiringo kitagira urugero.

67 Ata ihema rya Yozefu;

Ntiyatora umuryango wa Efurayimu.

68 Mugabo atora umuryango wa Yuda,

Umusozi Siyoni, uwo yakunze.

69 Atangura kwubaka aheranda hiwe nk’amasonga y’imisozi,

Nk’isi yashinze ngo igumeho gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

70 Nuko atora Dawidi umusavyi wiwe,

Amukura mu ngo z’imikuku.

71 Amukura inyuma y’imvyeyi zonsa,

Amuzana ngo abe umwungere kuri Yakobo igisata ciwe,

No kuri Isirayeli intoranwa yiwe.

72 Atangura kubaragira nk’uko ugutungana kw’umutima wiwe kuri,

Atangura kubayobora akoresheje ubuhanga bw’amaboko yiwe.

^ Zab. 78:24 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ Zab. 78:51 Ijambo ku rindi ni “Intango y’ububasha bwabo bwo kurondoka.”

^ Zab. 78:58 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
