Zaburi  79 : 1 - 13

Agahibongozo ka Asafu.

79 Ewe Mana, amahanga yinjiye mu ntoranwa yawe;

Yahumanije urusengero rwawe rweranda;

Yagize Yeruzalemu ikirundo c’ibihomvagurike.

 2 Yatanze umuvyimba w’abasavyi bawe ngo ube indya z’ibiguruka vyo mu kirere,

Inyama z’intahemuka zawe aziha ibikoko vyo kw’isi.

 3 Yasheshe amaraso yabo nk’amazi

Irya n’ino ya Yeruzalemu, kandi nta wo kubahamba ariho.

 4 Twacitse iceyi ku babanyi bacu,

Agatwengo n’akamenyo ku badukikuje.

 5 Uzogeza ryari ga Yehova kururumba ishavu? Ibihe bidahera?

Ishaka ryawe rizogeza ryari kwaka nk’umuriro?

 6 Suka ubushangashirwe bwawe ku mahanga atakumenye,

No ku bwami butambaje izina ryawe.

 7 Kuko bamirāje Yakobo,

Bagatuma ahantu aba hacika umusaka.

 8 Ntiwibuke amakosa ya ba sogokuruza bacu ngo uyaduhore.

Nyaruka! Imbabazi zawe nizidusanganire,

Kuko dusigaye twifumbereje bimwe bibi.

 9 Dutabare, ewe Mana y’ubukiriro bwacu,

Ku bw’ubuninahazwa bw’izina ryawe;

Turokore, upfuke ibicumuro vyacu kubera izina ryawe.

10 Kuki amahanga yovuga ngo: “Imana yabo iri hehe?”

Uguhōra amaraso y’abasavyi bawe yasheshwe

Nikumenyekane mu mahanga mu maso yacu.

11 Ukuniha kw’imbohe nigushike n’imbere yawe!

Nk’uko ubukuru bw’ukuboko kwawe buri, nuzigame abagenewe urupfu.

12 Niwishure ababanyi bacu incuro indwi mu gikiriza cabo

Iceyi bagusize, ewe Yehova!

13 Nayo twebwe abantu bawe n’umukuku wo mu buragiriro bwawe,

Tuzogukengurukira gushika igihe kitagira urugero;

Kuva kw’iyaruka uja ku rindi, tuzomenyesha ishemezo ryawe.
