Zaburi  88 : 1 - 18

Ururirimbo, agahibongozo ka bene Kora. Ku muyobozi, kuri Mahalati ku bwo kwitabira. Masikili ya Hemani Umwezerahi.

88 Ewe Yehova, Mana y’ubukiriro bwanje,

Ku murango narasemereye,

No mw’ijoro, imbere yawe.

 2 Isengesho ryanje rizoza imbere yawe.

Tega ugutwi akamo kanje k’ukwinginga.

 3 Kuko umutima wanje urambiwe n’ivyago,

Ubuzima bwanje bukaba bushitse ku munwa w’ukuzimu.

 4 Naharūwe mu bamanuka mu cobo;

Nacitse nk’umuntu w’umugabo atagira inkomezi,

 5 Yagizwe uwidegemvya mu bapfuye,

Nk’abishwe baryamye mu mva,

Abo utongeye kwibuka

Kandi batandukanijwe n’ukuboko kwawe gutabazi.

 6 Wanshize mu cobo c’ibwina kure rwose,

Mu bibanza vy’umwiza, mu nyenga nini.

 7 Ubushangashirwe bwawe bwitaye kuri jewe,

Kandi wanteje amarushwa ukoresheje imipfunda isuriranya yawe yose. Sela.

 8 Washize abo tuzinanyi kure yanje;

Wangize ikintu giteye ishishi cane kuri bo.

Ndaboshwe, sinshobora gusohoka.

 9 Ijisho ryanje ryarotse kubera amarushwa yanje.

Narakwambaje, ewe Yehova, burinda bwira;

Ni wewe nagaranzuriye ibiganza vyanje.

10 Mbega uzokorera igitangaza abapfuye?

Canke, mbega barya ata co bashoboye mu rupfu bazohaguruka?

Mbega bazoguhaya? Sela.

11 Mbega ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwawe buzomenyekanishwa mu mva,

Ubwizigirwa bwawe bumenyekanishwe mu kibanza c’agatikizo?

12 Mbega igitangaza wakoze kizomenyekana mu mwiza,

Canke ubugororotsi bwawe mu gihugu c’urwiba?

13 Ariko wewe, ewe Yehova, ni wewe natabaje,

Kandi mu gitondo isengesho ryanje riguma imbere yawe.

14 Kubera iki ga Yehova ushibura ubuzima bwanje?

Kubera iki uguma umpisha amaso yawe?

15 Ndi umurushwa, kandi ndi mu gucikana, kuva mu bwana;

Nihanganiye cane ibintu vy’agacavutu biva iwawe.

16 Imishwarara yawe y’ishavu rirurumba yaciye hejuru yanje;

Ibica ivutu biva iwawe vyanumije.

17 Vyankikuje nk’amazi burinda bwira;

Vyanzinguje vyose icarimwe.

18 Washize kure yanje umukunzi n’umugenzi;

Abo tuzinanyi ni ahantu h’umwiza.
