Zaburi  89 : 1 - 52

Masikili. Ni izaburi ya Etani Umwezerahi.

89 Ivyo Yehova yerekaniramwo ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo, nzobiririmba mbere gushika igihe kitagira urugero.

Uko iyaruka rikurikira irindi, nzomenyesha ubwizigirwa bwawe n’akanwa kanje.

 2 Kuko navuze nti: “Ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo buzoguma bwubatswe mbere gushika igihe kitagira urugero;

Nayo amajuru, uguma ushimangiye ubwizigirwa bwawe muri yo.”

 3 “Nagize isezerano ku bijanye n’uwo natoye;

Narahiye Dawidi umusavyi wanje nti:

 4 ‘Nzoshimangira uruvyaro rwawe mbere gushika igihe kitagira urugero,

Kandi nzokwubaka intebe yawe y’ubwami iyaruka ku rindi.’” Sela.

 5 Amajuru azohaya igikorwa cawe gitangaje, ewe Yehova,

Egome, ubwizigirwa bwawe mw’ishengero ry’aberanda.

 6 Kuko, ni nde mu majuru yogereranywa na Yehova?

Ni nde yosa na Yehova mu bana b’Imana?

 7 Imana ni iyo kwumvirwa akoba mu mugwi w’abagenzi somambike w’aberanda;

Irahambaye kandi iteye ubwoba ku bayikikuje bose.

 8 Ewe Yehova Mana nyen’ingabo,

Ni nde yuzuye ingoga nkawe ga Ya*?

Ubwizigirwa bwawe bukwiye irya n’ino yawe.

 9 Uganza ukwisevya kw’ikiyaga;

Iyo kidugije imipfunda yaco, ni wewe uyitururuza.

10 Ni wewe wajanjaguye Rahabu, mbere nk’uwishwe.

Biciye ku kuboko kw’inkomezi zawe, washwiragije abansi bawe.

11 Ijuru ni rwawe, n’isi nyene ni rwawe;

Ubutaka bwimbuka n’ibibwuzuye, ni wewe wabishinze.

12 Uburaruko n’ubumanuko, ni wewe wabiremye;

Tabori na Herumoni, mw’izina ryawe biriha akamo k’umunezero.

13 Ukuboko kw’ubushobozi ni rwawe;

Ukuboko kwawe kurakomeye,

Ukuryo kwawe gushizwe hejuru.

14 Ubugororotsi be n’ubutungane ni vyo kibanza gishimangiye c’intebe yawe y’ubwami;

Ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo be n’ukuri biraza imbere yawe.

15 Hahiriwe abantu bazi akaruru k’umunezero ico ari co.

Ewe Yehova, mu muco wo mu maso hawe ni ho baguma bagendera.

16 Mw’izina ryawe baranezerwa bukarinda bwira,

Kandi mu bugororotsi bwawe barashirwa hejuru.

17 Kuko ari wewe bwiza bw’inkomezi zabo;

Kandi kubera ubutoni bwawe, ihembe ryacu rirashirwa hejuru.

18 Kuko inkinzo yacu ari iya Yehova,

N’umwami wacu akaba ari uwa wa Mweranda wa Isirayeli.

19 Ico gihe wavuganye n’intahemuka zawe mw’iyerekwa,

Uti:

“Natabaye umunyenkomezi;

Nashize hejuru umuntu yatowe mu bantu.

20 Nabonye Dawidi umusavyi wanje;

Namurobanuje amavuta yanje meranda,

21 Uwo ukuboko kwanje kuzoshikama kumwe na we,

Uwo kandi ukuboko kwanje kuzokomeza.

22 Nta mwansi azomuhohotera,

Eka nta mwana w’ukutagororoka azomuteza amarushwa.

23 Najanjaguye abarwanizi biwe ndabakura imbere yiwe;

Nagumye nkubitagura abamwanka urunuka.

24 Ubwizigirwa bwanje n’ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwanje biri kumwe na we,

Kandi mw’izina ryanje ihembe ryiwe rirashirwa hejuru.

25 Nashize ukuboko kwiwe ku kiyaga,

Ukuryo kwiwe na kwo ku nzuzi.

26 We ubwiwe arasemerera antabaza, ati: ‘Uri Data,

Imana yanje n’Igitandara c’ubukiriro bwanje.’

27 Vyongeye, jewe nzomugira imfura,

Uwusumba abami bo mw’isi.

28 Nzozigama ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwanje kuri we gushika igihe kitagira urugero,

Kandi isezerano ryanje rizoba iryizigirwa kuri we.

29 Kandi ata gukeka nzoshinga uruvyaro rwiwe ibihe bidahera,

Intebe yiwe y’ubwami na yo ndayishinge nk’imisi y’ijuru.

30 Abana biwe nibaheba itegeko ryanje,

Ntibagendere mu ngingo zanje z’ubutungane,

31 Nibahumanya ivyagezwe vyanje,

Ntibagumye amabwirizwa yanje,

32 Nanje nzotegerezwa kwitaho ikigabitanyo cabo nkoresheje inkoni,

Niteho ikosa ryabo mu kubakubita.

33 Mugabo ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwanje sinzobumukurako,

Canke ngo mpemuke ku bwizigirwa bwanje.

34 Sinzohumanya isezerano ryanje,

Kandi ijambo riva mu minwa yanje sinzorihindura.

35 Nararahiye rimwe mu bweranda bwanje,

Sinzobesha Dawidi.

36 Uruvyaro rwiwe ruzokwamaho mbere gushika igihe kitagira urugero,

Intebe yiwe y’ubwami na yo nk’izuba imbere yanje.

37 Nka kurya kw’ukwezi, izoshimangirwa igihe kitagira urugero,

Mbere nk’icabona cizigirwa mu birere.” Sela.

38 Mugabo wewe, warashibuye kandi uguma ukengera;

Wararakiye uwo warobanuje amavuta.

39 Wiyamye isezerano ry’umusavyi wawe;

Wahumanije umugāra wiwe gushika hasi.

40 Wasenyuye ingo z’ibitungwa ziwe zose zubakishijwe amabuye;

Ibihome vyiwe wabigize ibisenyagurike.

41 Abahita ku nzira bose baramusahuye;

Yacitse iceyi ku babanyi biwe.

42 Washize hejuru ukuryo kw’abarwanizi biwe;

Watumye abansi biwe bose banezerwa.

43 Mbere, inkota yiwe urasubira ukayifata nk’umwansi,

Kandi watumye atagenda aratsinda mu rugamba.

44 Watumye ataba agica ibibatsi,

Intebe yiwe y’ubwami na yo uyiraririza hasi.

45 Imisi y’ubuto bwiwe wayigize migufi;

Wamutwikiriye ibimaramare. Sela.

46 Uzogeza ryari ga Yehova kuguma wihishije? Igihe cose?

Mbega ubushangashirwe bwawe buzoguma bururumba nk’umuriro?

47 Ibuka ikiringo ubuzima bwanje bumara uko kireha.

Mbega waremeye ubusa gusa abana b’abantu bose?

48 Ni nde muntu w’umugabo muzima atazobona urupfu?

Mbega yokiza ubuzima bwiwe ukuboko kw’ukuzimu? Sela.

49 Ibikorwa vy’ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo vyawe vya kera biri hehe ga Yehova,

Ivyo warahiye Dawidi mu bwizigirwa bwawe?

50 Ewe Yehova, ibuka iceyi kiri ku basavyi bawe,

Ingene ntwara mu gikiriza iceyi c’ibisata vy’abantu vyinshi vyose,

51 Ingene abansi bawe basize iceyi, ewe Yehova,

Ingene basize iceyi amakanda y’uwo warobanuje amavuta.

52 Yehova nahezagirwe gushika igihe kitagira urugero. Amen kandi Amen.

^ Zab. 89:8 “Ya” ni impfunyapfunyo y’izina “Yehova.”
