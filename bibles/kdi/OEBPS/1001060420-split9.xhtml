Zaburi  9 : 1 - 20

Ku muyobozi, kuri Muti-labeni. Agahibongozo ka Dawidi.

א [ʼAlefu]

9 Nzoguhaya, Yehova we, n’umutima wanje wose;

Nzomenyesha ibikorwa vy’agatangaza vyawe vyose.

 2 Nzonezerwa nongere nigine muri wewe,

Nzohibongoreza izina ryawe, ewe Musumbavyose!

ב [Beti]

 3 Abansi banje nibasubira inyuma,

Bazotsitara, bayogere bave imbere yawe.

 4 Kuko washikije urubanza rwanje n’imburano zanje;

Wicaye ku ntebe y’ubwami, uca imanza mu bugororotsi.

ג [Gimelu]

 5 Wahambariye amahanga, waranduye umubisha.

Izina ryabo warihanaguye gushika igihe kitagira urugero, mbere ibihe bidahera.

 6 Wa mwansi we, ukugirwa umusaka kwawe kwarangutse rwose,

Mbere n’ibisagara waranduye.

No kuvugwa ntibazokwongera kuvugwa.

ה [Heʼ]

 7 Nayo Yehova, azovyagira gushika igihe kitagira urugero,

Ashimangire intebe yiwe y’ubwami ku bw’urubanza.

 8 Kandi ubwiwe azocira urubanza ubutaka bwimbuka, mu bugororotsi;

Azosomera urubanza imiryango y’amahanga mu kugororoka.

ו [Vau]

 9 Yehova azocika ahantu hakirurutse hatagira inkomanzi kuri uwo wese ajanjaguwe,

Ahantu hakirurutse hatagira inkomanzi mu bihe vy’amarushwa.

10 Abazi izina ryawe bazokwizigira,

Kuko utazogenda uhevye abakurondera, Yehova we!

ז [Zayini]

11 Nimuhibongoreze Yehova, umwe aba i Siyoni;

Nimuyage mu bisata vy’abantu ibikorwa vyiwe.

12 Kuko, niyarondera amaraso yasheshwe abo ntazobura kubibuka;

Ntashobora kwibagira amaborogo y’abarushwa.

ח [Heti]

13 Ngirira ubutoni, Yehova we; raba amarushwa yanje ntezwa n’abanyanka,

Wewe unduza ukankura mu marembo y’urupfu,

14 Kugira ngo menyeshe ibikorwa vyawe vyose bibereye gushemezwa

Mu marembo y’umukobwa wa Siyoni,

Kugira nezererwe ubukiriro bwawe.

ט [Teti]

15 Amahanga yarokeye mu cobo yimvye;

Urusenga yanyegeje, ni rwo ikirenge cayo cafatiwemwo.

16 Yehova amenyekanira ku rubanza yashikije.

Umubisha afashwe n’igikorwa c’amaboko yiwe.

Higayoni*. Sela.

י [Yodi]

17 Ababisha bazosubira mu kuzimu,

Mbere n’amahanga yose yibagira Imana.

18 Kuko umworo atazokwama yibagirwa,

Eka n’icizigiro c’abatekereza ntikizokwigera gihona.

כ [Kafu]

19 Haguruka, Yehova we! Umuntu buntu ntabe ari we yiganza mu nkomezi.

Amahanga nacirwe urubanza mu maso yawe.

20 Nubashiremwo ubwoba, Yehova we,

Kugira amahanga amenye ko ata kindi ari atari abantu buntu. Sela.

^ Zab. 9:16 “Higayoni” ni ijambo ry’igiheburayo rijanye n’ivy’umuziki ritazwi insobanuro.
