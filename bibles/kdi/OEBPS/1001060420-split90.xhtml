Zaburi  90 : 1 - 17

IGITABU CA KANE

(Zaburi 90 – 106)

Isengesho rya Musa, wa muntu w’Imana y’ukuri.

90 Ewe Yehova, wewe ubwawe watubereye ahantu nyakuri ho kuba

Iyaruka ku rindi.

 2 Imbere y’uko imisozi ivuka,

Imbere y’uko utangura kuvyara isi be n’ubutaka bwimbuka, nko mu bubabare bw’ibise,

Mbere kuva igihe kitagira urugero gushika igihe kitagira urugero, uri Imana.

 3 Utuma umuntu buntu asubira mu mukungugu,

Ukavuga uti: “Nimusubireyo, mwebwe bana b’abantu.”

 4 Kuko imyaka igihumbi mu maso yawe ari nk’ejo hahise,

Nk’iterama mw’ijoro.

 5 Warabakukumuye; bacika itiro risa;

Mu gitondo bameze nk’ivyatsi bitoto bihinduka.

 6 Mu gitondo birashurika, bigategerezwa guhinduka;

Ku mugoroba birakabirana, bikuma ata gisivya.

 7 Kuko mw’ishavu ryawe turangiye,

Kandi duhagaritswe umutima n’ubushangashirwe bwawe.

 8 Washize amakosa yacu imbere yawe,

Ibinyegejwe vyacu ubishira imbere yo mu maso hawe hakaka.

 9 Kuko imisi yacu yose iyonze kubera uburake bwawe;

Turangije imyaka yacu nk’ubwongoshwe.

10 Imisi y’imyaka yacu ni imyaka mirongo irindwi;

Kandi naho yoba imyaka mirongo umunani bivuye ku nkomezi zidasanzwe,

Nubundi ibiyiranga ni ivyago n’ibibi gusa;

Kuko ihita ningoga, natwe tukaba tugurutse.

11 Ni nde azi inkomezi z’ishavu ryawe

N’uburake bwawe hisunzwe ukugutinya?

12 Twereke ingene duharura imisi yacu mu buryo butuma

Turonka umutima w’ubukerebutsi.

13 Nugaruke, Yehova we! Bizogeza ryari?

Niwicuze ku bijanye n’abasavyi bawe.

14 Mu gitondo nuduhaze ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwawe,

Kugira ngo twihe akamo k’umunezero, tunezerwe mu misi yacu yose.

15 Nutume tunezerwa ibihwanye n’imisi waduteje amarushwa,

Imyaka twabonye ivyago.

16 Igikorwa cawe nikiboneke imbere y’abasavyi bawe,

Ubwiza bwakaka bwawe na bwo ku bana babo.

17 Ubwiza bwa Yehova Imana yacu nibube kuri twebwe,

Kandi igikorwa c’amaboko yacu ugishimangire kuri twebwe.

Egome, igikorwa c’amaboko yacu, nugishimangire.
