Zaburi  94 : 1 - 23

94 Ewe Mana y’ibikorwa vy’uguhōra, wewe Yehova,

Ewe Mana y’ibikorwa vy’uguhōra, nusayangane!

 2 Haguruka, ewe Mucamanza w’isi!

Subiriza mu nkoko abibona.

 3 Ababisha bazogeza ryari ga Yehova,

Ababisha bazogeza ryari kwigina?

 4 Baguma badudubiza amajambo, baguma bavuga bashirukanya;

Abakora ibibabaza bose baguma biyemera.

 5 Abantu bawe, ewe Yehova, baguma babajanjagura,

Intoranwa yawe bakaguma bayiteza amarushwa.

 6 Umupfakazi na kavamahanga barica,

Impfuvyi* bagacisha ku buhomba.

 7 Baguma bavuga ngo: “Ya* ntabibona;

Imana ya Yakobo ntitahura.”

 8 Nimutahure, mwa mburabwenge bo mu bantu;

Nayo mwebwe ibijuju, muzogira ugutahura kwimbitse ryari?

 9 Mbega Umwe abadika ugutwi, ntashobora kwumva?

Canke Umwe abumba ijisho, ntashobora kwitegereza?

10 Umwe akosora amahanga, ntashobora gukangira,

Mbere Umwe yigisha abantu ubumenyi?

11 Yehova arazi ivyiyumviro vy’abantu, ko ari nk’impemu zisohotse.

12 Hahiriwe umuntu w’umugabo ukosora, ewe Ya,

Uwo wigisha ivyo mw’itegeko ryawe,

13 Kugira ngo umuhe impore mu misi y’ivyago,

Gushika umubisha yimbiwe icobo.

14 Kuko Yehova atazoheba abantu biwe,

Eka ntazota intoranwa yiwe.

15 Kuko ingingo y’ubutungane izosubira ku bugororotsi,

Kandi ab’umutima ugororotse bose bazoyisunga.

16 Ni nde azohagurukira inkozi z’ikibi ngo abandwanirize?

Ni nde azohagarara ngo andwanirize abakora ibibabaza?

17 Iyo Yehova atamfasha,

Mu kanya isase ubuzima bwanje bwari guca buja kuba mu gacerere.

18 Igihe navuga nti: “Ikirenge canje ntikizobura kugenda kidandabagirana,”

Ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwawe, ewe Yehova, bwagumye bunshigikiye.

19 Igihe ivyiyumviro vyanje bidurumbanya vyaba vyinshi muri jewe,

Ivyo wiruza vyaciye bitangura gukuyakuya umutima wanje.

20 Mbega intebe y’ubwami iteza amarushwa izokwunga urunani nawe

Mu gihe itegura ivyago ikoresheje ingingo zishinzwe?

21 Bagaba ibitero bikaze ku buzima bw’umugororotsi,

Amaraso y’intungane bakayita amabisha.

22 Mugabo Yehova azocika ahantu hakirurutse hanje hatagira inkomanzi,

Imana yanje icike igitandara c’ubuhungiro bwanje.

23 Kandi izobasubizako ibibabaza vyabo,

Ibanumye ikoresheje ivyago ubwabo bategura.

Yehova Imana yacu azobanumya.

^ Zab. 94:6 Ijambo ku rindi ni “uduhungu tutagira se.”

^ Zab. 94:7 “Ya” ni impfunyapfunyo y’izina “Yehova.”
