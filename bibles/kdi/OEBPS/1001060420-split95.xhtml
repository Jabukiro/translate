Zaburi  95 : 1 - 11

95 Yoo! nimuze twihe akamo k’umunezero tukerekeza kuri Yehova!

Twihe akaruru k’intsinzi tukerekeza ku Gitandara cacu c’ubukiriro.

 2 Tuze imbere yiwe turi n’ugukenguruka;

Twihe akaruru k’intsinzi tukerekeza kuri we, hariko n’uduhibongozo.

 3 Kuko Yehova ari Imana ihambaye

Akaba n’Umwami ahambaye hejuru y’izindi mana zose.

 4 Ibwina kure h’isi hari mu kuboko kwiwe,

N’amasonga y’imisozi ni rwiwe;

 5 Ikiyaga ubwiwe yakoze ni rwiwe,

Kandi amaboko yiwe ni yo yabumvye isi yumutse.

 6 Yoo! nimuze dusenge, twuname;

Dupfukame imbere ya Yehova Umuhinguzi wacu.

 7 Kuko ari Imana yacu, natwe tukaba abantu bo mu buragiriro bwayo n’intama zo mu kuboko kwayo.

Uno musi nimwumviriza ijwi ryiwe,

 8 Ntimukomantaze umutima wanyu nk’i Meriba,

Nko kuri wa musi wa Masa mu gahinga,

 9 Hamwe ba sogokuruza banyu bangerageza;

Baransuzumye, barabona n’igikorwa canje.

10 Mu kiringo c’imyaka mirongo ine, nagumye numva nshishwe n’iryo yaruka,

Ndaheza mvuga nti:

“Ni abantu b’umutima wigira ibiro biteze,

Kandi bo ubwabo ntibamenye inzira zanje”;

11 Ni bo narahiye mw’ishavu ryanje nti:

“Ntibazokwinjira ahantu hanje h’ukuruhukira.”
