Zaburi  97 : 1 - 12

97 Yehova ubwiwe acitse umwami! Isi nigire akanyamuneza.

Ya mazinga menshi nanezerwe.

 2 Ibicu n’umuzimagiza w’umuzitanya bikwiye irya n’ino yiwe hose;

Ubugororotsi be n’ubutungane ni vyo kibanza gishimangiye c’intebe yiwe y’ubwami.

 3 Umuriro ugendera imbere yiwe,

Kandi uyigiza abarwanizi biwe bose irya n’ino.

 4 Imiravyo yiwe yabonesheje ubutaka bwimbuka;

Isi yarabibonye, igira ububabare bukaze.

 5 Imisozi ubwayo yatanguye gushonga nk’ishashara kubera Yehova,

Kubera Umukama w’isi yose.

 6 Amajuru yamenyesheje ubugororotsi bwiwe,

Kandi ibisata vy’abantu vyose vyabonye ubuninahazwa bwiwe.

 7 Abo bose bakorera igishusho c’ikibazano ico ari co cose nibamaramare,

Barya biratira imana ata co zimaze.

Nimumwunamire, yemwe mwa mana mwese!

 8 Siyoni yaravyumvise itangura kunezerwa,

Ibisagara bikukira Yuda na vyo bitangura kugira akanyamuneza

Kubera ingingo zawe z’ubutungane, ewe Yehova!

 9 Kuko wewe, ewe Yehova, ari wewe Musumbavyose hejuru y’isi yose;

Waduze hejuru cane y’izindi mana zose.

10 Yemwe abakunda Yehova, nimwanke ikibi.

Arinda ubuzima bw’intahemuka ziwe;

Abarokora mu kuboko kw’ababisha.

11 Umuco ubwawo warabije ku bw’umugororotsi,

Umunezero na wo ku bw’abantu b’umutima ugororotse.

12 Nimunezerwe muri Yehova, yemwe bagororotsi,

Mukengurukire icibutso ciwe ceranda.
