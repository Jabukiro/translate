Imigani  31 : 1 - 31

31 Amajambo ya Lemuweli umwami, ubutumwa buremereye nyina yamuhaye mu kumukosora:

2 Mvuge iki ga mwananje? Mvuge iki ga mwana w’inda yanje? Mvuge iki ga mwana w’indagano zanje?

3 Ntuhe inkomezi zawe abagore, canke ngo inzira zawe uzihe ibishikana abami ku guhanagurwa.

4 Si ivy’abami, Lemuweli we, si ivy’abami kunywa umuvinyu, canke ngo bibe ivy’abategetsi bakurubakuru kuvuga ngo: “Inzoga iboreza iri hehe?” 5 kugira ngo umuntu ntahave anywa akibagira ivyashinzwe, akagoreka imburano z’uwo ari we wese mu bana b’amarushwa. 6 Nimuhe inzoga iboreza uwuri mu guhwera, n’umuvinyu ab’umutima udundumiwe. 7 Umuntu nanywe yibagire ubworo bwiwe, kandi ntasubire kwibuka ivyago vyiwe.

8 Teranura akanwa uvugire ikiragi, uburanire abari mu kuvaho bose. 9 Teranura akanwa, uce imanza mu bugororotsi, uburanire umurushwa n’umworo.

א [ʼAlefu]

10 Umugore abishoboye, ni nde yomuronka? Agaciro kiwe karuta kure n’iyo ak’amabuye y’agaciro yitwa korali.

ב [Beti]

11 Umutima wa inawe waramwizigiye, kandi inyungu ntibura.

ג [Gimelu]

12 Uwo mugore yamuhemvye ivyiza, si ibibi, imisi yose y’ubuzima bwiwe.

ד [Daleti]

13 Yarondeye ubwoya n’inyuzi z’ilino, kandi akora ikintu cose gihimbara amaboko yiwe.

ה [Heʼ]

14 Ameze nk’imeri z’umudandaza. Azana ibifungurwa vyiwe abikuye kure.

ו [Vau]

15 Kandi avyuka hakiri mu kajoro, agaha ibifungurwa urugo rwiwe, agaha n’inkumi ziwe umugabane zigenewe.

ז [Zayini]

16 Yariyumviriye itongo, araryironsa; bivuye ku vyamwa vy’amaboko yiwe, yateye umurima w’imizabibu.

ח [Heti]

17 Yakenyeje amafyinga yiwe inkomezi, kandi aha ingoga amaboko yiwe.

ט [Teti]

18 Yiyumvisemwo ko urucuruza rwiwe ari rwiza; itara ryiwe ntirizima mw’ijoro.

י [Yodi]

19 Yatumye amaboko yiwe ku gakoni kazingîrwako urunyuzi, amaboko yiwe na yo afata agati kazungurutsa urunyuzi.

כ [Kafu]

20 Yagaranzuriye ikiganza umurushwa, kandi yaramvuriye amaboko umworo.

ל [Lamedi]

21 Ntatinyira urugo rwiwe kubera shelegi, kuko urugo rwiwe rwose rwambaye impuzu zirimwo zibirizibiri.

מ [Memu]

22 Yihinguriye ibisaswa. Ivyambarwa vyiwe ni ivy’ilino n’ubwoya buzize ibara ry’umuyugubwe ugomba gutukura.

נ [Nuni]

23 Inawe ni umuntu azwi mu marembo, iyo yicaranye n’abagabo b’inararibonye b’igihugu.

ס [Sameki]

24 Yarahinguye mbere n’impuzu zo munsi, arazigurisha, aha n’imikaba abacuruza.

ע [ʽAyini]

25 Inkomezi n’ubwiza bwakaka ni vyo vyambarwa vyiwe, kandi aratwenga umusi uzoza.

פ [Peʼ]

26 Yateranuye akanwa kiwe mu bukerebutsi, kandi itegeko ry’ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo riri ku rurimi rwiwe.

צ [Tsade]

27 Aracungera imirimo y’urugo rwiwe, kandi ntarya umukate w’ubunebwe.

ק [Kofu]

28 Abahungu biwe barahagurutse bamwita umuhirwe; inawe arahaguruka, akamushemeza.

ר [Reshi]

29 Hari abakobwa benshi berekanye ko babishoboye, mugabo wewe, emwe, waraduze ubaheta bose.

ש [Shini]

30 Igikundiro coshobora guhendana, n’ubwiza bwoshobora kuba ubusa; mugabo umugore atinya Yehova ni we yihesha ugushemezwa.

ת [Tau]

31 Nimumuhe ku vyamwa vy’amaboko yiwe, kandi ibikorwa vyiwe bimushemeze mu marembo.
