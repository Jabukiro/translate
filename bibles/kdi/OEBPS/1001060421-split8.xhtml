Imigani  8 : 1 - 36

8 Mbega ubukerebutsi ntibuguma busemerera, n’ugutahura kukaguma gusohora ijwi ryakwo? 2 Kw’isonga ry’ahantu hakirurutse, iruhande y’inzira, kw’ihwane ry’amabarabara, ni ho buhagaze. 3 Iruhande y’amarembo, ku munwa w’igisagara, mu bwinjiriro bw’imiryango, buguma busemerera n’ijwi rirenga, buti:

4 “Ni mwebwe mpamagara, mwa bantu mwe, kandi ijwi ryanje rija ku bana b’abantu. 5 Yemwe bantu batazi utuntu n’utundi, nimutahure ukwiyubara; namwe bijuju, nimutahure umutima. 6 Nimwumvirize, kuko mvuga ibintu bihambaye kuruta ibindi vyose, ngateranura iminwa yanje ngo mvuge ukugororoka. 7 Kuko amagage yanje avuga ukuri n’ijwi ritoyi; kandi ububisha ni ikintu giteye ishishi ku minwa yanje. 8 Amajambo yose y’akanwa kanje ni ay’ubugororotsi. Muri yo nta kintu na kimwe kihari kigoramanze canke gihetaraye. 9 Yose ararashe ku wutegera, kandi aragororotse ku baronka ubumenyi. 10 Nimutore indero yanje ntimutore ifeza, mutore ubumenyi aho gutora inzahabu z’indobanurwa. 11 Kuko ubukerebutsi ari bwiza kuruta amabuye y’agaciro yitwa korali, kandi ibindi bihimbaro vyose ntivyonganishwa na bwo.

12 “Jewe bukerebutsi, nabanye n’ukwiyubara, kandi ndaronka ubumenyi bw’ubushobozi bwo kwiyumvira. 13 Ugutinya Yehova gusobanura kwanka ibibi. Ukwishira hejuru n’ubwishime n’inzira mbi be n’akanwa kagoramye, naravyanse. 14 Ndafise impanuro n’ubukerebutsi ngirakamaro. Jewe ndafise ugutegera; ndafise ubushobozi. 15 Biciye kuri jewe, abami ubwabo baguma baganza, n’abategetsi bakurubakuru ubwabo bakaguma bashinga ingingo z’ubugororotsi. 16 Biciye kuri jewe, abaganwa ubwabo baguma batwara nk’abaganwa, n’abanyacubahiro bose bagaca imanza mu bugororotsi. 17 Abankunda nanje ndabakunda, kandi abandondera ni bo bandonka. 18 Ubutunzi n’ubuninahazwa biri kumwe nanje, ibintu vy’agaciro vy’iragi be n’ubugororotsi. 19 Ivyamwa vyanje ni vyiza kuruta inzahabu, mbere kuruta inzahabu ityoroye, umwimbu wanje na wo ukaba mwiza kuruta ifeza y’indobanurwa. 20 Mu munyuro w’ubugororotsi ni ho ngendera, hagati mu mabarabara y’ubutungane, 21 kugira abankunda ndabahe kwigabira amatungo; kandi nguma nujuje ibigega vyabo.

22 “Yehova ubwiwe ni we yampinguye nka yo ntango y’inzira yiwe, mba ica mbere na mbere mu bikorwa vyiwe vya kera. 23 Nashizweho kuva igihe kitagira urugero*, kuva mu ntango, kuva mu bihe vy’imbere y’uko isi ibaho. 24 Igihe ata nyenga z’amazi zariho ni ho navyarwa nko mu bubabare bw’ibise, igihe ata mbizi zibogaboga amazi zariho. 25 Imbere y’uko imisozi ubwayo ishirwaho, imbere y’uko imirambi ibaho, navyawe nko mu bubabare bw’ibise, 26 igihe atari bwakore isi n’uturere n’igice ca mbere c’ibirundo vy’umukungugu vy’ubutaka bwimbuka. 27 Igihe yategura amajuru ni ho nari; igihe yashinga ko haba umuzingi ku buringanire bw’inyenga y’amazi, 28 igihe yashikamisha ibigwi vy’ibicu hejuru, igihe yakomeza amasôko y’inyenga y’amazi, 29 igihe yashingira ikiyaga ingingo yiwe, kugira amazi ntarenge itegeko ryiwe, igihe yashinga ko haba imishinge y’isi, 30 nabaye iruhande yiwe ndi umukozi w’umuhanga, mba uwo yakunda bidasanzwe ku musi ku musi, mpimbarwa igihe cose imbere yiwe, 31 mpimbariwe ubutaka bwimbuka bw’isi yiwe, kandi ibintu nakunda cane vyari ivyerekeye abana b’abantu.

32 “None rero, bana, nimunyumvirize; egome, hahiriwe abagumya inzira zanje. 33 Nimwumvirize indero, mube inkerebutsi, kandi ntimwanjanjwe na gatoyi. 34 Hahiriwe umuntu anyumviriza akaguma arikanuye ku miryângo yanje ku musi ku musi, akaguma areretse iruhande y’ibikingi vy’aminjiriro yanje. 35 Kuko uwundonka atazobura kuronka ubuzima, kandi araronka ubutoni bwa Yehova. 36 Mugabo uwumbuze aba yigiriye nabi; abanyanka urunuka bose ni abakunda urupfu koko.”

^ Imig. 8:23 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
