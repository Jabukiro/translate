Umusiguzi  9 : 1 - 18

9 Kuko nashize ibi vyose ku mutima, mbere kugira ngo ndondere ibi vyose: yuko abagororotsi n’inkerebutsi be n’ibikorwa vyabo biri mu kuboko kw’Imana y’ukuri. Abantu ntibazi urukundo canke urwanko vyariho vyose imbere y’uko babaho. 2 Bose ni bamwe mu vyo bose bafise. Hari iherezo rimwe ku mugororotsi no ku mubisha, ku mwiza no ku wudahumanye no ku wuhumanye, ku wushikana ibimazi no ku wudashikana ibimazi. Umwiza ni kimwe n’umucumuzi; uwurahira ni kimwe n’uwatinye indahiro. 3 Ibi ni vyo birimwo ivyago mu vyakozwe munsi y’izuba vyose: kubera ko hari iherezo rimwe kuri bose, umutima w’abana b’abantu wuzuye ibibi; kandi hari ubusazi mu mutima wabo mu buzima bwabo bwose, inyuma yaho na ho⁠—​ni uguhamvya abapfuye!

4 Kuko ku bijanye n’uwo wese yifatanya n’abazima bose hariho umwizero, kubera ko imbwa ibona iruta intambwe ipfuye. 5 Kuko abazima bazi yuko bazopfa; mugabo abapfuye, nta co bazi namba, eka nta n’impembo bagifise, kuko ukwibukwa kwabo kwibagiwe. 6 Vyongeye, urukundo rwabo n’urwanko rwabo n’ishari ryabo vyaramaze gushira, kandi nta mugabane bagifise gushika igihe kitagira urugero mu kintu na kimwe kibwirizwa gukorwa munsi y’izuba.

7 Genda, wirire ibifungurwa vyawe n’umunezero, winywere umuvinyu wawe n’umutima mwiza, kuko Imana y’ukuri imaze guhimbarwa n’ibikorwa vyawe. 8 Igihe cose, impuzu zawe nizame zera, n’amavuta ntabure ku mutwe wawe. 9 Nubone ubuzima uri kumwe n’umugore ukunda imisi yose y’ubuzima bwawe bw’ubusa Imana yaguhaye munsi y’izuba, imisi yawe yose y’ubusa, kuko uwo ari wo mugabane wawe mu buzima no mu gikorwa cawe c’ubutame ukora ugatama munsi y’izuba. 10 Ikintu cose ukuboko kwawe kuronse co gukora, nugikorane ububasha bwawe, kuko ata gikorwa, canke umugambi, canke ubumenyi, canke ubukerebutsi mu kuzimu, aho uriko uraja.

11 Narahindukiye, mbona munsi y’izuba yuko abanyarutsi atari bo bene gutsinda isiganwa, n’abanyenkomezi ko atari bo bene gutsinda urugamba, eka n’inkerebutsi ko atari zo zene kugira ibifungurwa, eka n’abazi ubwenge ko atari bo bene kugira ubutunzi, mbere n’abafise ubumenyi ko atari bo bene kugira ubutoni; kuko ibihe be n’ibishika giturumbuka bibashikako bose. 12 Kuko umuntu mbere atazi igihe ciwe. Nka kurya kw’amafi afatirwa mu rusenga rubi, kandi nk’inyoni zifatirwa mu mutego, ni ko n’abana b’abantu bafatwa n’umutego mu gihe c’ivyago, igihe ubaguyeko bukwi na bukwi.

13 Ibi na vyo nyene narabibonye ku bijanye n’ubukerebutsi munsi y’izuba, kandi bwambereye ubuhambaye: 14 Habaye agasagara gatoyi, kandi abagabo muri ko bari bake; maze haza kuri nya gasagara umwami ahambaye, aragakikuza, yubaka ibihome bikomeye ngo agatere. 15 Maze muri ko habonekayo umuntu akenye mugabo w’inkerebutsi, uwo atabara nya gasagara ku bukerebutsi bwiwe. Mugabo nta n’umwe yibutse uwo muntu w’umukene. 16 Ndaheza mvuga nti: “Ubukerebutsi buraruta inguvu; yamara ubukerebutsi bw’umukene burasuzugurwa, n’amajambo yiwe ntiyumvirizwa.”

17 Amajambo y’abantu b’inkerebutsi avuganywe ubutekereji ni ayo kwumvirwa kuruta ugusemerera kw’uwuganza mu bijuju.

18 Ubukerebutsi buraruta ibikoresho vy’indwano, kandi umucumuzi umwe gusa arashobora kwica ivyiza vyinshi.
