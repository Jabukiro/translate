Yesaya  16 : 1 - 14

16 Nimurungike impfizi y’intama ku mutware w’igihugu, ive i Sela igana agahinga, ije ku musozi w’umukobwa wa Siyoni.

2 Kandi uku ni ko bitegerezwa kuba: kurya kw’ikiremwa c’amababa kiriko kirahunga, cirukanywe mu cari caco, ni ko abakobwa ba Mowabu bazomera, ku vyambu vya Arunoni.

3 “Nimuzane impanuro, mushitse ingingo yafashwe.

“Igitutu canyu nimukigire nk’ijoro hagati y’umutaga. Nimuhishe abashwiragiye; ntimutange umuntu n’umwe ariko arahunga. 4 Abashwiragiye banje nibibereho ari ba kavamahanga muri wewe, ewe Mowabu! Nubabere ubwihisho kubera umunyazi. Kuko umunyagahahazo ashitse kw’iherezo ryiwe; ubunyazi burarangiye; abakandagira abandi baraherengetejwe kw’isi.

5 “Kandi, bigiranywe ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo, intebe y’ubwami ntizobura gushimangirwa; kandi hari uwuzoyicarako mu kuri mw’ihema rya Dawidi, aca imanza kandi akarondera ubutungane, abanguka mu vy’ubugororotsi.”

6 Twarumvise ubwishime bwa Mowabu, yuko yishima cane; ubwibone bwiwe n’ubwishime bwiwe n’uburake bwiwe—amajambo yiwe agaragara si ko azomera. 7 Mowabu izoborogera Mowabu; mbere yose izoboroga. Abakubititse bazonihagira koko kubera udutsima tw’inzabibu zumye tw’i Kiri-hareseti, 8 kuko imikobeko y’i Heshiboni ikabiranye. Umuzabibu wa Sibuma—abagaba amahanga baciye amashami yawo atukuriye. Bari bashitse i Yazeri; bari bayerereye mu gahinga. Imivyaro yawo yari yaretswe ngo yitagararire; bari barenganye gushika ku kiyaga.

9 Ni co gituma nzorira amarira ya Yazeri, ndizwa n’umuzabibu wa Sibuma. Nzogutotesha amosozi yanje, ewe Heshiboni na Eleyale, kuko akaruru kaguye mbere kw’ici ryawe no kw’iyimbura ryawe. 10 Umunezero n’akanyamuneza vyakuwe mu murima w’ivyamwa; kandi mu mirima y’imizabibu nta kamo k’umunezero kariyo, nta karuru kahavuzwa. Uwuganisha ibirenge aragana ntihagire umuvinyu uboneka mu nganiro. Akaruru nagahagaritse.

11 Ni co gituma ubura bwanje busebagirika nk’inanga kubera Mowabu, hagati muri jewe na ho hagasebagirika kubera Kiri-hareseti.

12 Maze biboneka ko Mowabu yarambiwe ku kibanza gikirurutse; kandi yaje aheranda hiwe gusenga, ntiyagira ico ashikako namba.

13 Ng’iryo ijambo Yehova yavuze ku vyerekeye Mowabu kera. 14 Ubu na ho Yehova aravuze, ati: “Mu myaka itatu, nk’uko imyaka y’umucangero iri, ubuninahazwa bwa Mowabu butegerezwa rero kugirwa agaterasoni mu ntureka nyinshi y’ubwoko bwose, kandi abasigaye bazoba inkehwa ziri ku rushi, badakomeye.”
