Yesaya  20 : 1 - 6

20 Mu mwaka Tarutani yaza i Ashidodi, igihe Sarugoni umwami wa Ashuri yamutuma, agatangura kurwanya Ashidodi akayifata, 2 ico gihe Yehova avuga biciye kuri Yesaya mwene Amozi, ati: “Genda, kandi utegerezwa gutekurura igunira rikuve mu mafyinga; kandi inkweto* zawe ukwiye kuzikura ku birenge vyawe.” Nuko agira kuno, agendagenda ari gusa n’ibirenge bigaragara.

3 Yehova abandanya kuvuga ati: “Nk’uko umusavyi wanje Yesaya yagendagenze imyaka itatu ari gusa n’ibirenge bigaragara, bwa kimenyetso n’imburizi kuri Misiri na Etiyopiya, 4 ni ko umwami wa Ashuri azoshorera umugwi w’imbohe za Misiri n’abagizwe inyagano ba Etiyopiya, uduhungu n’abatama, abashorere bari gusa n’ibirenge bigaragara, n’ibibuno biri amenya, ubwambure bwa Misiri. 5 Kandi bazotekerwa n’ubwoba bamaramare kubera Etiyopiya yo cizigiro bari bahanze amaso, no kubera Misiri ubwiza bwabo. 6 Kandi uwuba muri ico gihugu co ku nkengera y’amazi ntazobura kuvuga kuri uwo musi, ati: ‘Ng’ukwo ukuntu ca cizigiro twari duhanze amaso kimeze, ico twahungirako ngo kidufashe, kugira turokorwe kubera umwami wa Ashuri! None twebwe tuzokira dute?’”

^ Yes. 20:2 Canke, “amasandali.”
