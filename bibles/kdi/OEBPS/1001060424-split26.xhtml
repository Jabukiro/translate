Yesaya  26 : 1 - 21

26 Kuri uwo musi uru ruririmbo ruzoririmbwa mu gihugu ca Yuda: “Dufise igisagara gikomeye. Ashiraho ubukiriro ngo bube impome n’igikuta. 2 Nimwugurure amarembo yemwe, ihanga rigororotse rigumya ingendo y’ubwizigirwa ryinjire. 3 Agatima gashigikiwe neza uzokazigama mu mahoro arama, kuko umuntu ari wewe yizigira. 4 Nimwizigire Yehova ibihe vyose, kuko Ya* Yehova ari we Gitandara c’ibihe bitagira urugero.

5 “Kuko yasenyuriye hasi ababa ahakirurutse, igisagara kidugijwe hejuru. Agishira hasi, agishira hasi gushika kw’isi; agikoza ku mukungugu. 6 Ikirenge kizogikandagira, ikirenge c’umurushwa, intambuko z’abantu batobato.”

7 Umunyuro w’umugororotsi ni ukugororoka. Kubera ko wewe ugororotse, uzosena inzira y’umugororotsi. 8 Egome, ku bijanye n’umunyuro w’imanza zawe ga Yehova, twarakwizigiye. Umutima wacu wipfuje izina ryawe n’icibutso cawe. 9 Narakwipfuje n’umutima wanje mw’ijoro; egome, nguma nkurondera n’umushaha wanje muri jewe; kuko, nihaba imanza zivuye iwawe ku bw’isi, ubugororotsi ni bwo ababa ku butaka bwimbuka bazokwiga. 10 Naho umubisha yogirirwa ubutoni, ntazogenda yize ubugororotsi. Mu gihugu c’ukugororoka azokora ivy’akarenganyo, kandi ntazobona ubukuru bwa Yehova.

11 Ewe Yehova, ukuboko kwawe kwabaye ugukirurutse, mugabo ntibakwitegereza. Bazovyitegereza bamaramare nibabona umwete ufitiye abantu bawe. Egome, umuriro ugenewe abarwanizi bawe uzobarigata. 12 Ewe Yehova, uzoca urubanza rwo kuturonsa amahoro, kuko n’ibikorwa vyacu vyose wabishikije ku bwacu. 13 Ewe Yehova Mana yacu, abandi batware kiretse wewe bigize abatugaba. Tubihawe na wewe ni ho gusa tuzovuga izina ryawe. 14 Barapfuye; ntibazobaho. Nta co bashoboye mu rupfu, ntibazovyuka. Waritayeho rero ivyabo kugira ngo ubazimanganye, ubatikize ntibabe bakivugwa na gato.

15 Wongereje ihanga; ewe Yehova, wongereje ihanga; wininahaje. Washikanye kure imipaka yose y’igihugu. 16 Ewe Yehova, mu marushwa baritayeho ivyawe; barasutse urwongoshwe rw’isengesho igihe wabatoza indero. 17 Kurya umugore yibungenze yegereza ukwibaruka, akagira ububabare bw’ibise, akaboroga mu misonga yiwe y’ukwibaruka, ni ko twabaye kubera wewe, Yehova we! 18 Twaribungenze, twaragize ububabare bw’ibise; ni nk’aho twibarutse umuyaga. Nta bukiriro nyakuri dushitsa ku bijanye n’igihugu, kandi nta bantu bo kuba ku butaka bwimbuka bavuka.

19 “Abapfuye bawe bazobaho. Ikiziga canje—bazovyuka. Ikangure mwihe akamo k’umunezero, yemwe ababa mu mukungugu! Kuko ikime canyu ari nk’ikime c’uduti tw’imyora, kandi isi izorekura barya ata co bashoboye mu rupfu bavuke.

20 “Genda, wa gisata canje, winjire mu vyumba vyawe, wugare imiryango yawe inyuma yawe. Niwinyegeze akanya gatoyi gusa gushika imisibo irenganye. 21 Kuko, ehe Yehova araje avuye ahantu hiwe ngo abaze uwuba mu gihugu ikosa ryiwe, aryishure; kandi igihugu kizobwirizwa gushira ku kabarore amaraso yahasheshwe, kandi ntikizosubira gupfuka abishwe baco.”

^ Yes. 26:4 “Ya” ni impfunyapfunyo y’izina “Yehova.”
