Yesaya  35 : 1 - 10

35 Agahinga be n’akarere kagadutse bizokwigina, kandi ikiyaya c’ubugaragwa kizonezerwa, gishurike nk’ishurwe ry’izafarani. 2 Kizoshurika ata kabuza, kandi kizonezerwa vy’ukuri n’akanyamuneza be n’akamo k’urweze. Emwe, kizohabwa ubuninahazwa bwa Libani, ubwiza bwakaka bwa Karumeli n’ubwa Sharoni. Hazoba ababona ubuninahazwa bwa Yehova, ubwiza bwakaka bw’Imana yacu.

3 Nimukomeze amaboko ya goyigoyi, mushikamishe amavi anyoganyoga. 4 Nimubwire abari n’amaganya mu mutima muti: “Nimukomere. Ntimutinye. Raba! Imana yanyu izozanana ukwihora, Imana izanane ugusubiriza mu nkoko. Yo ubwayo izoza, ibakize.”

5 Ico gihe amaso y’impumyi azohumuka, kandi amatwi y’ibipfamatwi azozibuka. 6 Ico gihe uwucumbagira azonanagira nk’ifumbēri*, kandi ururimi rw’ikiragi ruzokwiha akamo k’urweze. Kuko mu gahinga amazi azoba yadudubije, n’inzuzi z’isumo mu kiyaya c’ubugaragwa. 7 Ubutaka bwagatuwe n’ubushuhe buzoba bwacitse nk’ikidengeri cuzuyeko amarenga, ubutaka bunyotewe na bwo nk’imbizi z’amazi. Ahaba imbwebwe, ahantu ziruhukira, hazoba ivyatsi bitoto hamwe n’amarenga be n’urufunzo.

8 Kandi ng’aho hazoba inzira y’ikirari, emwe, inzira; izokwitwa Inzira y’Ubweranda. Uwuhumanye ntazoyicamwo. Izoba iy’uwugendera muri nya nzira, kandi nta bipfu bizoyiyereramwo. 9 Nta ntambwe izohaba, kandi nta bikoko vy’ameru bizoyidugamwo. Nta na kimwe kizohaboneka; abacunguwe ni bo bazohagendera. 10 Kandi abacunguwe na Yehova bazogaruka baze koko i Siyoni n’akamo k’umunezero; kandi umunezero wo gushika igihe kitagira urugero uzoba ku mutwe wabo. Bazoshikira umwigino n’umunezero, kandi intuntu n’ukuniha bizohunga.

^ Yes. 35:6 Canke, “impongo.”
