Yesaya  39 : 1 - 8

39 Ico gihe, Merodaki-baladani mwene Baladani, umwami wa Babiloni, arungikira amakete n’ingabirano Hezekiya, aho yumviye ko yari yarwaye mugabo akaba yari yasubiye gukomera. 2 Nuko Hezekiya atangura kunezerwa kubera bo no kubereka inzu y’itunga yiwe, ifeza n’inzahabu n’amavuta y’umusomozo n’amavuta meza n’ububiko bw’ibirwanisho bwiwe bwose n’ibintu vyose vyari mu matunga yiwe. Nta na kimwe Hezekiya ataberetse mu nzu yiwe no mu nganji yiwe yose.

3 Inyuma y’ivyo, Yesaya umuhanuzi yinjira aho Umwami Hezekiya ari maze amubwira ati: “Abo bantu bavuze iki, kandi baje iwawe bavuye he?” Hezekiya avuga rero ati: “Baje iwanje bavuye mu gihugu ca kure, i Babiloni.” 4 Yesaya abandanya kuvuga ati: “Babonye iki mu nzu yawe?” Hezekiya aca avuga ati: “Babonye ikintu cose kiri mu nzu yanje. Nta na kimwe ntaberetse mu matunga yanje.” 5 Nuko Yesaya abwira Hezekiya ati: “Niwumve ijambo rya Yehova nyen’ingabo: 6 ‘Ehe imisi iraje, kandi ibiri mu nzu yawe vyose be n’ivyo ba sogokuruza bawe barundanije gushika n’uyu musi bizotwarwa koko i Babiloni.’ ‘Nta na kimwe kizosigara,’ ni ko Yehova avuze. 7 ‘Kandi bamwe mu bahungu bawe bazova muri wewe, abo uzovyara, bazotwarwa babe koko abakozi bo ku kirimba, mu kirimba c’umwami wa Babiloni.’”

8 Hezekiya aca abwira Yesaya ati: “Ijambo rya Yehova uvuze ni ryiza.” Abandanya kuvuga ati: “Kubera ko amahoro n’ukuri bizogumaho mu misi yanje.”
