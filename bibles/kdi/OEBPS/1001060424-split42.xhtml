Yesaya  42 : 1 - 25

42 Ehe umusavyi wanje, uwo ngumije! Uwo natoye, uwo umutima wanje washimye! Nashize impwemu yanje muri we. Azozana ubutungane ku mahanga. 2 Ntazosemerera canke ngo aduze ijwi, kandi ntazokwumvikanisha ijwi ryiwe mw’ibarabara. 3 Ntazovuna irenga rijanjaguritse; nayo urutambi ruriko rurayama, ntazoruzimya. Azozana ubutungane mu kuri. 4 Ntazoyama canke ngo ajanjagurwe gushika ashizeho ubutungane mw’isi; kandi amazinga azoguma arindiriye itegeko ryiwe.

5 Ibi ni vyo Imana y’ukuri, Yehova, ivuze, Umuremyi w’amajuru akaba n’Umwe ahambaye ayaramvura, Umwe asasa isi n’umwimbu wayo, Umwe aha impemu abantu bo kuri yo, agaha n’impwemu abagendagenda kuri yo: 6 “Jewe Yehova, naguhamagaye mu bugororotsi, ndagufata ukuboko. Nzokuzigama, ndagutange nk’isezerano ry’abantu, nk’umuco w’amahanga, 7 kugira ngo uhumure amaso ahumye, usohore mu gasho imbohe, usohore mu nzu y’ibohero abicaye mu mwiza.

8 “Ndi Yehova. Iryo ni ryo zina ryanje; kandi nta wundi n’umwe nzoha ubuninahazwa bwanje, eka sinzoha ishemezo ryanje ibishusho bibajwe.

9 “Ivya mbere—ng’ivyo vyaje, mugabo ndiko menyesha ibishasha. Imbere y’uko biseruka, ndabibumvisha.”

10 Ririmbire Yehova ururirimbo rushasha, ishemezo ryiwe kuva ku mpera y’isi, mwebwe abamanuka ku kiyaga no ku bicuzuye, mwebwe mazinga namwe abayabamwo! 11 Agahinga be n’ibisagara vyako nibiduze ijwi, imihana irundanye Kedari abamwo. Ababa ku rutare nibihe akamo k’umunezero. Abantu nibakome akaruru bari kw’isonga ry’imisozi. 12 Nimuhe Yehova ubuninahazwa, kandi mu mazinga bamenyeshe ishemezo ryiwe.

13 Yehova azosohoka nk’umunyenkomezi. Azokangura umwete wiwe nk’umurwanyi. Azovuza akaruru, egome, azorekura akaruru k’intambara; ku bansi biwe, azokwerekana ko ari we munyenkomezi.

14 “Natekereje igihe kirekire. Nagumye ncereje. Nagumye nigumije. Kurya kw’umugore ariko yibaruka, ngiye kunihagira, guhezagirika, guhema vyanka, vyose icarimwe. 15 Nzotikiza imisozi n’imirambi, ibimera vyo kuri yo na vyo ndavyumishe. Inzuzi nzozihindura amazinga, ibidengeri vyuzuyeko amarenga na vyo ndavyumishe. 16 Abantu b’impumyi nzotuma bagendera mu nzira batamenye; nzotuma bahonyora mw’ibarabara batamenye. Ahantu h’umwiza nzohahindura umuco imbere yabo, ikibanza kitaringaniye ndagihindure intara ishashe. Ivyo ni vyo nzobakorera, kandi sinzobaheba.”

17 Bazobwirizwa guhindukira, bazomaramara rwose, barya bashira ivyizigiro vyabo mu gishusho c’ikibazano, barya babwira igishusho c’icuma gishongeshejwe ngo: “Ni mwe mana zacu.”

18 Nimwumve, yemwe bipfamatwi; nimwitegereze mubone, yemwe mpumyi. 19 Ni nde ari impumyi atari umusavyi wanje, kandi ni nde ari igipfamatwi nk’intumwa yanje ntuma? Ni nde ari impumyi nk’uwahawe impêra, canke ari impumyi nk’umusavyi wa Yehova? 20 Kwari ukubona ibintu vyinshi, mugabo ntiwagumye witegereza. Kwari ukwugurura amatwi, mugabo ntiwagumye wumviriza. 21 Yehova ubwiwe, ku bw’ubugororotsi bwiwe, yahimbawe n’uko yokuza itegeko akarigira agakomeye. 22 Mugabo ni igisata casahuwe congera kiramburwa, bose bagafatwa n’umutego mu binogo, kandi bagumye banyegejwe mu mazu y’ibohero. Babaye abo gusahurwa ata murokozi, abo kwamburwa ata wuri buvuge ngo: “Garukana!”

23 Ni nde muri mwebwe azoha ugutwi ibi? Ni nde azotega ugutwi akumviriza ku bw’ibihe vyo hanyuma? 24 Ni nde yatanze Yakobo ngo yamburwe, agatanga Isirayeli ku basahuzi? Mbega si Yehova, Umwe twacumurako, uwo banse kugendera mu nzira ziwe kandi ntibumvirize itegeko ryiwe? 25 Ku bw’ico yagumye amusukako ubushangashirwe, ishavu ryiwe, n’inkomezi z’intambara. Kandi vyagumye bimuyigiza irya n’ino, mugabo ntiyagize ico yitaho; kandi vyagumye bimururumbira, mugabo nta na kimwe yashira ku mutima.
