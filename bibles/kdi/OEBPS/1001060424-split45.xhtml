Yesaya  45 : 1 - 25

45 Ibi ni vyo Yehova abwiye uwo yarobanuje amavuta, Kuro, uwo mfashe ukuryo, ngo ntume amahanga ayoboka imbere yiwe, kugira ngo nkenyurure amafyinga y’abami; ngo nugurure imbere yiwe ya miryango y’inzugi zibirizibiri, ku buryo n’amarembo atazokwugarwa: 2 “Jewe nzogendera imbere yawe, kandi ibidunduri vy’isi nzobiringaniza. Imiryango y’umujumpu nzomenagura, ibihindizo vy’icuma ncagagure. 3 Kandi nzoguha amatunga ari mu mwiza n’amatunga anyegejwe mu bwihisho, kugira ngo umenye yuko jewe ndi Yehova, Umwe aguhamagara mw’izina ryawe, Imana ya Isirayeli. 4 Kubera umusavyi wanje Yakobo no kubera Isirayeli uwo natoye, naraguhamagaye mw’izina ryawe; naguhaye izina ry’iteka, naho utamenye. 5 Ndi Yehova, kandi nta wundi n’umwe ariho. Nta Mana iriho kiretse jewe. Nzogukenyeza cane, naho utamenye, 6 kugira ngo bimenyekane guhera iyo izuba rirasira no gushika iyo rirengera yuko ata wundi ariho atari jewe. Ndi Yehova, kandi nta wundi n’umwe ariho. 7 Uguhingura umuco n’ukurema umwiza, ugushiraho amahoro n’ukurema ivyago, jewe Yehova, ivyo ndabikora vyose.

8 “Emwe majuru, nimutonyangishe ubugororotsi buvuye hejuru; ibirere vyuzuye ibicu nibitembe ubugororotsi. Isi niyuguruke, yame cane ubukiriro, kandi nitume ubugororotsi bumera, icarimwe. Jewe Yehova, narabiremye.”

9 Aragowe uwaniganye n’Umubumvyi wiwe, nk’urujo runigana n’izindi njo zo ku butaka! Mbega ibumba ryobwira umubumvyi waryo ngo: “Ico ukora ni igiki?” Ico wakoze covuga ngo: “Nta maboko agira”? 10 Aragowe uwubwira se ngo: “Uvyara iki?”, akabwira umugore ngo: “Ikiguteye ububabare bwo kwibaruka ni igiki?”

11 Ibi ni vyo Yehova avuze, wa Mweranda wa Isirayeli n’Umubumvyi wiwe: “Nimumbaze mbere n’ibigira bize ku vyerekeye abana banje; kandi ku vyerekeye igikorwa c’amaboko yanje, nimuntegeke. 12 Jewe narakoze isi, ndarema n’umuntu kuri yo. Jewe—amaboko yanje ni yo yaramvuye amajuru, kandi nategetse ingabo zayo zose.”

13 “Jewe nahagurukije umuntu mu bugororotsi, kandi inzira ziwe zose nzozigorora. Ni we azokwubaka igisagara canje, kandi abanje bari mu bunyagano azobarekura, atari ku kiguzi canke ku giturire,” ni ko Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze.

14 Ibi ni vyo Yehova avuze: “Abakozi badahembwa ba Misiri be n’abadandaza ba Etiyopiya be n’Abasabeya, abagabo barebare, ubwabo bazoza bagushikire, bacike abawe. Bazogendera inyuma yawe; bazoza mu mapingu, bakwunamire. Bazogusenga bati: ‘Koko, Imana iri mu bumwe nawe, kandi nta yindi n’imwe iriho; nta yindi Mana iriho.’”

15 Vy’ukuri uri Imana iguma yihishije, Imana ya Isirayeli, Umukiza. 16 Bazomaramara koko mbere bateterwe, bose uko bangana. Abahinguzi b’amashusho y’ibigirwamana bazogendera hamwe bateterewe. 17 Nayo Isirayeli, azokizwa koko mu bumwe na Yehova, akirishwe ubukiriro ku bw’ibihe bitagira urugero. Mwebwe ntimuzomaramara, eka ntimuzoteterwa ibihe bitagira urugero, ibihe bidahera.

18 Kuko ibi ari vyo Yehova avuze, Umuremyi w’amajuru, We Mana y’ukuri, Uwabumvye isi akayihingura, We yayishimangiye, ntayiremere ubusa gusa, mugabo akayirema ngo ibeko abantu: “Ndi Yehova, kandi nta wundi n’umwe ariho. 19 Sinavugiye mu bwihisho, ahantu h’umwiza h’isi; eka sinabwiye uruvyaro rwa Yakobo nti: ‘Nimundonderere ubusa.’ Ndi Yehova, mvuga ibigororotse, ngahishura ibitumbereye.

20 “Nimutororokane muze. Nimwegere muri kumwe, yemwe abacitse kw’icumu mu mahanga! Abikorera igiti c’igishusho cabo c’ikibazano nta bumenyi bashitseko, eka n’abasenga imana idashobora gukiza ni ukwo. 21 Nimushikirize raporo yanyu n’ivyiyumviro vyanyu. Egome, nibaje inama mu bumwe. Ni nde yavyumvikanishije kuva kera? Ni nde yabimenyesheje kuva ico gihe? Mbega si jewe Yehova, umwe ata yindi Mana iriho atari we, Imana igororotse n’Umukiza, akaba ata wundi ariho kiretse jewe?

22 “Hindukire muze kuri jewe mukizwe, mwese abo ku mpera z’isi; kuko ndi Imana, kandi nta yindi n’imwe iriho. 23 Narirahiye jewe ubwanje—ijambo ryarasohotse mu kanwa kanje mu bugororotsi, ku buryo ritazosubira inyuma—yuko ivi ryose rizompfukamira, yuko ururimi rwose ruzorahira, 24 ruti: ‘Ni ukuri, muri Yehova hari ubugororotsi bwuzuye be n’inkomezi. Abamurungurira bose bazoza bamutumbereye, bamaramare. 25 Muri Yehova uruvyaro rwa Isirayeli rwose ruzoboneka ko ruri mu kuri, kandi rwirate.’”
