Yesaya  47 : 1 - 15

47 Manuka wicare mu mukungugu, ewe mukobwa w’isugi wa Babiloni! Icara hasi ahatagira intebe y’ubwami, ewe mukobwa w’Abakaludaya! Kuko batazokwongera kukwita uworohewe n’uwadibamye. 2 Tora urusyo rw’amaboko usye ifu. Ipfukure icitwikiro cawe. Ambura impuzu irekera*. Pfukura ukuguru. Jabuka inzuzi. 3 Ubwirizwa gupfukura ubwambure bwawe. Kandi iceyi cawe kibwirizwa kubonwa. Ngira nihore, kandi nta muntu n’umwe tuzohura ngo ngire ubuntu.

4 “Hariho uwuducungura. Yehova nyen’ingabo ni ryo zina ryiwe, Umweranda wa Isirayeli.”

5 Icara utekereje, winjire mu mwiza, ewe mukobwa w’Abakaludaya; kuko batazokwongera kukwita Umugabekazi w’Ibihugu vy’abami. 6 Nararakariye abantu banje. Narahumanije intoranwa yanje, ndabatanga mu kuboko kwawe. Nta mbabazi wabagiriye. Ku mutama ingǒgo yawe wayigize iyiremereye cane. 7 Waguma uvuga ngo: “Nzoba Umugabekazi gushika igihe kitagira urugero, ibihe bidahera.” Ivyo ntiwabishize ku mutima; ntiwibutse iherezo ry’ico kintu.

8 Ubu rero niwumve ibi, wa mugore wihaye ibihimbaro, wewe wiyicariye mu mutekano, wewe uvuga mu mutima wawe ngo: “Ndiho, kandi nta wundi. Sinzokwicara nk’umupfakazi, kandi sinzomenya ukubura abana ico ari co.” 9 Mugabo ibi bintu bibiri bizokuzako bukwi na bukwi, ku musi umwe: ukubura abana n’ugupfakara. Emwe, bizokuzako ku rugero rwuzuye, kubera umurengera w’ubupfumu bwawe, kubera ubushobozi bwose bw’imitongero yawe—bimwe birengeje. 10 Wagumye wizigiye ububi bwawe. Wavuze ngo: “Nta n’umwe ambona.” Ubukerebutsi bwawe n’ubumenyi bwawe—ivyo ni vyo vyagukweze; kandi uguma uvuga mu mutima wawe ngo: “Ndiho, kandi nta wundi.” 11 Ivyago bitegerezwa kukuzako; ntuzomenya kubigota namba. Amarushwa azokugwako; ntuzoshobora kuyatesha. Kandi kuri wewe hazoza bukwi na bukwi agatikizo utari umenyereye.

12 Hagarara udakakaza rero, n’imitongero yawe hamwe n’umurengera w’ubupfumu bwawe wacumukuriyemwo kuva mu buto bwawe; kugira ngo kumbure ushobore kwunguka, kugira ngo kumbure utere abantu akoba. 13 Wārāmbiwe n’abajanama indiri bawe. Nibahaguruke rero bagukize, bamwe basenga amajuru, bamwe bitegereza inyenyeri, bamwe batanga ubumenyi igihe c’imboneko z’ukwezi ku vyerekeye ibizokuzako. 14 Raba! Bacitse nk’ibishakara. Umuriro uzobatongora. Ntibazorokora ubuzima bwabo mu bubasha bw’urubeya. Nta kubengerana kw’amakara kuzohaba ngo abantu bisusurutse, nta gaco k’umuriro kazohaba ko kwicara imbere. 15 Ukwo ni ko bazomera koko kuri wewe, abo mwacumukuranye ari abagosi bawe kuva mu buto bwawe. Bazokwangara koko, umwe wese aja mu karere kiwe. Ntihazoba n’umwe wo kugukiza.

^ Yes. 47:2 Ijambo ku rindi ni “impuzu yo hepfo irekera.”
