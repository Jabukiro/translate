Yesaya  51 : 1 - 23

51 “Nimunyumvirize, mwebwe abakurikirana ubugororotsi, mwebwe abarondera Yehova. Nimwitegereze igitandara mwasatuweko, mwitegereze n’umwobo w’ikinogo mwimbwemwo. 2 Nimwitegereze Aburahamu so, na Sara uwahavuye abavyara mu bubabare bw’ukwibaruka. Kuko Aburahamu yari umwe igihe namuhamagara, ndaheza ndamuhezagira, ndamugwiza. 3 Kuko Yehova atazobura guhoza Siyoni. Ata gukeka azohoza ibibanza vyaho vyose vyatikijwe, agahinga kaho akagire nka Edeni, ikiyaya c’ubugaragwa caho na co nk’umurima wa Yehova. Umwigino n’umunezero ni ho bizosangwa, ugukenguruka n’ijwi ry’agahibongozo.

4 “Nunyumvirize, wa gisata canje; nawe muryango wanje w’amahanga, nuntege ugutwi. Kuko itegeko rizosohoka rivuye kuri jewe, kandi ingingo yanje y’ubutungane* nzoyishinga ibe nk’umuco ku bisata vy’abantu. 5 Ubugororotsi bwanje buri hafi. Ubukiriro bwanje ntibuzobura gusohoka, kandi amaboko yanje azocira imanza ibisata vy’abantu. Amazinga azonyizigira, arindire ukuboko kwanje.

6 “Nimuraramurire amaso mu majuru, mwongere murabe kw’isi hasi. Kuko amajuru azotegerezwa kuvungagurwa atumurwe kurya kw’umwotsi, kandi isi izoshira kurya kw’impuzu, kandi abayibako bazopfa kurya kw’umubu*. Nayo ubukiriro bwanje, buzoba mbere ubwo gushika igihe kitagira urugero, kandi ubugororotsi bwanje ntibuzomenagurwa.

7 “Nimunyumvirize, mwebwe abazi ubugororotsi, itegeko ryanje rikaba riri mu mutima wanyu. Ntimutinye iceyi c’abantu buntu, kandi ntimucike ivutu kubera gusa amajambo yabo y’ibitutsi. 8 Kuko agakoko kazobarya kabafyete nk’impuzu, kandi agakoko karya impuzu kazobarya kabafyete nk’ubwoya. Nayo ubugororotsi bwanje, buzoba mbere ubwo gushika igihe kitagira urugero, ubukiriro bwanje na bwo gushika ku mayaruka adaharurwa.”

9 Ikangure, ikangure, ambara inkomezi, ewe kuboko kwa Yehova! Ikangure nko mu misi ya kera, nko mu mayaruka yo mu bihe vyahise. Mbega si wewe wavunaguye Rahabu, ugacumita ca gikoko amahero co mu kiyaga? 10 Mbega si wewe wakamije ikiyaga, amazi y’inyenga amahanga? Si wewe wagize ibwina mu kiyaga inzira ngo abacunguwe bajabuke? 11 Maze abacunguwe ba Yehova bazogaruka, bashike i Siyoni n’akamo k’umunezero, kandi umunezero wo gushika igihe kitagira urugero uzoba ku mutwe wabo. Bazoshikira umwigino n’umunezero. Intuntu n’umuniho ntibizobura guhunga.

12 “Jewe—jewe ni je ndabahoza.

“Uri nde ngo utinye umuntu buntu azopfa, n’umwana w’abantu azogirwa nk’ivyatsi bitoto? 13 Uri nde ngo wibagire Yehova Umuhinguzi wawe, Umwe aramvura amajuru agashinga umushinge w’isi, ku buryo wama mu bwoba bukarinda bwira kubera ubushangashirwe bw’uwugusatira, nk’aho yoba agabirije kuguhonya? Ubushangashirwe bw’uwugusatira buri hehe?

14 “Uwunamye mu minyororo ntazobura kubohorwa ningoga, kugira ngo ntaje mu cobo mu rupfu, no kugira ngo umukate wiwe ntubure.

15 “Mugabo jewe Yehova, ndi Imana yawe, Umwe avyura ikiyaga kugira ngo imipfunda yaco isebagirike. Yehova nyen’ingabo ni ryo zina ryiwe. 16 Nzoshira amajambo yanje mu kanwa kawe, kandi nzogupfukisha koko igitutu c’ukuboko kwanje, kugira ngo ntere amajuru, nshinge umushinge w’isi, mbwire Siyoni nti: ‘Uri igisata canje.’

17 “Vyuka, vyuka, haguruka, ewe Yeruzalemu, wewe wanyoye mu kuboko kwa Yehova igikombe ciwe c’ubushangashirwe! Ikalisi, igikombe gitera ibizunguzungu, waranyoye urirangūza. 18 Mu bana bose yavyaye ntihagize n’umwe amurongōra, kandi mu bana bose yareze ntihagize n’umwe amufata ukuboko. 19 Ivyo bintu bibiri vyaragushikira. Ni nde azosangira nawe umubabaro? Ukunyagwa n’ugusenyuka, inzara n’inkota! Ni nde azoguhoza? 20 Abana bawe barávye. Barambaraye ku mutwe w’amabarabara yose nk’intama y’inyeshamba mu rusenga, nk’abuzuye ubushangashirwe bwa Yehova, uguhambara kw’Imana yawe.”

21 Ndakwinginze rero wumvirize ibi, wa mugore watejwe amarushwa kandi uborewe utaborejwe n’umuvinyu. 22 Ibi ni vyo Umukama wawe, Yehova, mbere Imana yawe, umwe aniganira abantu biwe, avuze: “Raba! Nzokura mu kuboko kwawe ca gikombe gitera ibizunguzungu. Ya kalisi, ca gikombe canje c’ubushangashirwe—ntuzosubira kwongera kukinywako. 23 Kandi nzogishira mu kuboko kw’abagutera ishavu, abakubwiye ngo: ‘Unama twijabukire,’ ku buryo wagira umugongo wawe nk’isi, nk’ibarabara ku bajabuka.”

^ Yes. 51:4 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ Yes. 51:6 Bwari ubwoko bw’agakoko gatoyi kameze nk’umubu kari kamenyerewe mu Misiri.
