Yesaya  53 : 1 - 12

53 Ni nde yizeye ico twebwe twumvise? Nayo ukuboko kwa Yehova, ni nde kwahishuriwe? 2 Azoduga nk’agashami imbere y’umuntu, nk’umuzi mu gihugu kigadutse. Nta shusho y’agakomeye agira, canke ubwiza bwakaka; kandi nitwamubona, nta sura* agira ku buryo twomwipfuza.

3 Yarasuzugurwa kandi akinubwa n’abantu; yari umuntu agenewe imibabaro n’ukumenya indwara. Wamenga twari duhishijwe mu maso h’umuntu. Yarasuzugurwa, tukamubona nk’ubusa. 4 Vy’ukuri, indwara zacu ubwiwe yaraziremetse; nayo imibabaro yacu, yarayikoreye. Mugabo twebwe twamubona nk’uwatejwe ivyago, uwakubiswe n’Imana kandi yatejwe amarushwa. 5 Mugabo yaracumitwa azira ukugabitanya kwacu; yarajanjagurwa azira amakosa yacu. Uguhanwa kwo kutuzanira amahoro kwari kuri we, kandi kubera ibikomere vyiwe twarashoboye gukira. 6 Twese twarayerereye nk’intama; twarahindukiye umwe wese agana inzira yiwe bwite; Yehova ubwiwe yatumye ikosa ryacu twese rihura n’uyo. 7 Yarasugerezwa, akemera agatezwa amarushwa; yamara ntiyakurako umunwa. Yazanwa nk’intama igiye gukererwa; kandi kurya kw’intama y’ingore yacitse ikiragi imbere y’abayikemura, na we nyene ntiyakurako umunwa.

8 Kubera ukuzigirizwa be n’urubanza, yarakuweho; kandi ni nde azokwitaho ido n’ido ry’amamuko yiwe*? Kuko yatandukanijwe n’igihugu c’abazima. Yakubiswe azira ukugabitanya kw’abantu banje. 9 Azomuha ikibanza ciwe co guhambwamwo kumwe n’ababisha, kumwe n’abatunzi mu rupfu rwiwe, naho ata bukazi yari yarakoze, kandi akaba ata buhendanyi bwari mu kanwa kiwe.

10 Mugabo Yehova ubwiwe yarahimbawe no kumujanjagura; yamuteye kurwara. Niwamugira nk’ishikanwa ry’ukwagirwa n’icaha, azobona uruvyaro rwiwe, azorehura imisi yiwe, kandi mu kuboko kwiwe ibihimbara Yehova bizokunda. 11 Kubera ivyago vyiwe, azobona, azohaga. Biciye ku bumenyi bwiwe, umugororotsi, umusavyi wanje, azozanira abantu benshi impagararo igororotse; kandi amakosa yabo we ubwiwe azoyikorera. 12 Ku bw’iyo mvo, nzomuha umugabane muri benshi, kandi azogabanganya isahu kumwe n’abanyabushobozi, kubera ko yasutse ubuzima bwiwe gushika ku rupfu, agaharurwa mu bagabitanya; kandi ubwiwe yiremetse igicumuro c’abantu benshi, aja hagati ku bw’abagabitanya.

^ Yes. 53:2 Canke, “ishusho.”

^ Yes. 53:8 Ijambo ku rindi ni “iyaruka ryiwe.”
