Yesaya  58 : 1 - 14

58 “Semerera uherezeko agahogo; ntiwitinye. Duza ijwi ryawe nk’inzamba, ubwire abantu banje umugararizo wabo, n’inzu ya Yakobo ibicumuro vyabo. 2 Yamara ni je bagumye barondera uko bukeye uko bwije, bakerekana ko ukumenya inzira zanje ari kwo kubahimbara, nk’ihanga ryashishikara ubugororotsi, ritari ryahevye ubutungane bw’Imana yabo, kuko baguma bansaba imanza zigororotse, bakegera Imana bahimbarirwa, ngo:

3 “‘Kubera iki twisonzesheje ntubibone, tukibabaza ntuvyiteho?’

“Vy’ukuri, mwahimbarwa n’umusi w’ukwisonzesha kwanyu, igihe mwahindagiza ku bikorwa abakozi banyu bose bacumukura. 4 Vy’ukuri, mwisonzeshereza imitongano n’intambara, be no gukubitisha igipfunsi c’ububisha. Mbega ntimwaguma mwisonzesha nko ku musi wo kwumvikanisha ijwi ryanyu iyo hejuru? 5 Mbega ukwo ni ko ukwisonzesha nshima gukwiye kumera, nk’umusi w’uko umuntu yakuwe mw’ivu yibabaza, akunamika umutwe nk’urukangaga, agasanza igunira n’iminyota ngo bibe uburiri bwiwe? Mbega ico ni co mwita ukwisonzesha be n’umusi vyemerwa na Yehova?

6 “Mbega uku si kwo kwisonzesha nshima: gutekurura amapingu y’ububisha, kubohora imizana y’umutambikwa w’ingǒgo, kurekura abajanjaguwe bakidegemvya, mukavuna umutambikwa wose w’ingǒgo? 7 Mbega si ukugaburira umukate wawe uwushonje, ukazana mu nzu yawe abantu b’abarushwa, batagira uburaro? Si kugira ngo ubonye uwuri gusa umwambike, ntiwinyegeze mwene wanyu?

8 “Ico gihe umuco wawe woseruka nk’umutwenzi; ugusubirana kwawe kwonaga ningoga. Kandi ubugororotsi bwawe bwogendera imbere yawe; ubuninahazwa bwa Yehova ni bwo bwokubera abacunzi b’inyuma. 9 Ico gihe wohamagara, Yehova ubwiwe akakwitaba; wotabaza, na we akavuga ati: ‘Ndi hano!’

“Niwakura hagati muri wewe umutambikwa w’ingǒgo, ukareka gutunga agatoki no kuvuga ibintu bibabaza; 10 niwaha uwushonje ico wipfuza, ugahaza uwuhahazwa, umuco wawe rero ntuzobura kurabiriza mu mwiza, n’umuzimagiza wawe ukamera nk’umutaga. 11 Yehova ntazobura kwama akuyobora kandi akaguhaza no mu gihugu kibabutse, kandi azogarukanira ingoga amagufa yawe; uzomera nk’umurima uvomerwa neza, umere kandi nk’isôko ry’amazi, amazi atabesha. 12 Bivuye kuri wewe, ntibazobura kwubaka ibibanza vyatikijwe kuva kera; uzoduza mbere imishinge y’amayaruka akurikirana. Kandi uzokwitwa vy’ukuri umusanuzi w’igihengeri, uwusubizaho amabarabara umuntu ashobora kuba iruhande.

13 “Kubera isabato, niwasubiza inyuma ikirenge cawe ku bijanye no gukora ibiguhimbara ubwawe ku musi wanje mweranda, kandi isabato ukayita vy’ukuri akaryoshe, umusi mweranda wa Yehova, umusi uninahazwa, kandi ukawuninahaza aho gukora ivy’inzira zawe bwite, aho kurondera ibiguhimbara no kuvuga amajambo, 14 ni ho uzososerwa na Yehova, nanje nzotuma ugendera ku bibanza bikirurutse vy’isi; kandi nzotuma urya ku ntoranwa ya Yakobo sogokuruza wawe, kuko akanwa ka Yehova ari ko kabivuze.”
