Yesaya  6 : 1 - 13

6 Ariko jewe, mu mwaka Umwami Uziya yapfa, mbona Yehova yicaye ku ntebe y’ubwami ikirurutse kandi ishizwe hejuru, kandi impuzu yiwe* yari yuzuye urusengero. 2 Abaserafi* bari bahagaze hejuru yiwe. Umwe wese yari afise amababa atandatu. Abiri yaguma ayapfukishije mu maso hiwe, yandi abiri akaguma ayapfukishije ibirenge vyiwe, yandi abiri na yo akayagurukisha. 3 Uyu yarahamagara uriya ati: “Mweranda, mweranda, mweranda ni Yehova nyen’ingabo. Ubuninahazwa bwiwe bunengesereye isi yose.” 4 Amasonga yo kuzungurukirako y’aminjiriro atangura kudegedwa humvikanye ijwi ry’uwariko ahamagara, inzu na yo ubwayo yuzura umwotsi.

5 Nanje mvuga nti: “Ndigorewe! Kuko mbaye nk’uwunumijwe, kubera ko ndi umuntu w’iminwa ihumanye, nkaba mba mu bantu b’iminwa ihumanye; kuko amaso yanje abonye Umwami, Yehova nyen’ingabo, ubwiwe!”

6 Kuri ivyo, umwe muri ba baserafi araguruka aza aho ndi, kandi mu kuboko kwiwe hari ikara rirurumba yari yatoje udupensi ku gicaniro*. 7 Araheza ankora ku munwa, avuga ati: “Raba! Iki kirakoze ku minwa yawe, kandi ikosa ryawe ryagiye, n’igicumuro cawe kirahongewe.”

8 Ntangura kwumva ijwi rya Yehova rivuga riti: “Ntume nde, kandi ni nde atugira?” Ndaheza mvuga nti: “Ndi hano! Ntuma.” 9 Abandanya kuvuga ati: “Genda, ubwire aba bantu uti: ‘Nimwumve yemwe incuro n’izindi, ariko ntimutegere; kandi mubone incuro n’izindi, ariko ntihagire ico mumenya.’ 10 Umutima w’aba bantu nuwudadaze, amatwi yabo uyakomantaze, umatanye amaso yabo, kugira ntibaboneshe amaso yabo, n’amatwi yabo ntibayumvishe, no kugira ngo umutima wabo ntutegere, no kugira ntibahindukire koko ngo bakizwe.”

11 Kuri ivyo mvuga nti: “Kugeza ryari ga Yehova?” Maze avuga ati: “Gushika ibisagara bihomvaguritse koko, bigasigara ata wubibamwo, n’amazu agasigara ata muntu yakuwe mw’ivu ayabamwo, n’ubutaka bugatikizwa bugacika umusaka; 12 gushika Yehova yigije kure koko abantu bakuwe mw’ivu, n’ugutabwa kugacika koko ugukomeye hagati mu gihugu. 13 Kandi muri co hazogumaho ic’icumi, kandi kizotegerezwa kwongera gucika ikintu co gutongorwa, nka kurya kw’igiti kinini n’uruti rusagaraye bigira igishitsi iyo vyiciwe; uruvyaro* rweranda ni rwo ruzoba igishitsi caco.”

^ Yes. 6:1 Ijambo ku rindi ni “ibice vyo hepfo vy’impuzu yiwe.”

^ Yes. 6:2 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ Yes. 6:6 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ Yes. 6:13 Ijambo ku rindi ni “urubuto.”
