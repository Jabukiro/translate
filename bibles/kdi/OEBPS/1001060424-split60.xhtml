Yesaya  60 : 1 - 22

60 “Haguruka, wa mugore we, rungika umuco, kuko umuco wawe ushitse, kandi ubuninahazwa bwa Yehova bukaba bukurasiyeko umuco. 2 Kuko, ehe umwiza uzopfuka isi, umuzimagiza w’umuzitanya na wo upfuke imiryango y’amahanga; mugabo wewe Yehova azokurasirako umuco, kandi ubuninahazwa bwiwe buzokubonekako. 3 Amahanga azogenda koko agana umuco wawe, abami na bo bagende bagana ubukayangane bw’ukwaka kwawe.

4 “Raramura amaso weraguze, urabe! Bose bakoranirijwe hamwe; baje kuri wewe. Abahungu bawe baguma baza bavuye kure, be n’abakobwa bawe, abazokwitwararikwa bari ku kiyunguyungu. 5 Ico gihe uzobona, ukayangane koko, kandi umutima wawe uzodegedwa vy’ukuri, waguke, kuko ubutunzi bw’ikiyaga buzoza bukugana; amatunga y’amahanga azoza kuri wewe. 6 Ikigwi kigigimba c’ingamiya kizogupfuka, ingamiya zikiri nto z’ingabo z’i Midiyani n’iz’i Efa. Iz’i Sheba zose, zizoza. Zizoza zikoreye inzahabu n’imibavu ya olibani. Kandi zizotangaza amashemezo ya Yehova. 7 Imikuku yose y’i Kedari, izokoranirizwa iwawe. Impfizi z’intama z’i Nebayoti, zizogukorera. Zizoduga ku gicaniro canje kandi zemerwe, kandi nzoshariza inzu yanje y’ubwiza.

8 “Abo ni bande baza baguruka nk’igicu, nk’inuma zija ku twenge tw’inzu yazo? 9 Kuko amazinga azoguma anyizigiye, n’imeri z’i Tarushishi nyene nko mu ntango, kugira ngo abana bawe bazanwe bakuwe kure, ifeza zabo n’inzahabu zabo biri kumwe na bo, baze bagana izina rya Yehova Imana yawe, bagana n’Umweranda wa Isirayeli, kuko azoba yagusharije. 10 Abanyamahanga bazokwubaka vy’ukuri impome zawe, abami babo na bo bagukorere; kuko mu burakari bwanje nzoba nagukubise, mugabo mu butoni bwanje sinzobura kukugirira imbabazi.

11 “Kandi amarembo yawe azoguma yuguruye nantaryo; ntazokwugarwa haba ku murango canke mw’ijoro, kugira ngo uzanirwe amatunga y’amahanga, kandi abami babo ni bo bazoba bari imbere. 12 Kuko ihanga ryose n’ubwami bwose bitazogukorera bizoyogera; kandi amahanga ntazobura gutikira.

13 “Ubuninahazwa bwa Libani buzoza kuri wewe, igiti c’umuberoshi, igiti c’umutidari be n’akajwari icarimwe, kugira ngo ikibanza c’aheranda hanje gisharizwe; kandi nzoninahaza ikibanza c’ibirenge vyanje.

14 “Abana b’abaguteza amarushwa bazotegerezwa kuza kuri wewe bakwunamira; abakugaya bose bazotegerezwa gukubita inkoro hasi imbere y’ubworo bw’ibirenge vyawe, kandi bazobwirizwa kukwita igisagara ca Yehova, Siyoni y’Umweranda wa Isirayeli.

15 “Aho kuba uwahebwe buhere be n’uwanswe, aho ata n’umwe arengana, nzokugira koko ikintu co kwishimira gushika igihe kitagira urugero, nkugire umwigino uko iyaruka rikurikira irindi. 16 Kandi uzokwonka vy’ukuri amaberebere y’amahanga, wonke ibere ry’abami; kandi ntuzobura kumenya yuko jewe Yehova ndi Umukiza wawe, kandi ko Umunyabubasha wa Yakobo ari we Mucunguzi wawe. 17 Mu kibanza c’umujumpu nzozana inzahabu, mu kibanza c’icuma nzane ifeza, mu kibanza c’igiti nzane umujumpu, mu kibanza c’amabuye na ho, icuma; kandi nzogena amahoro ngo akubere abacungezi, n’ubugororotsi ngo bukubere abagabuzi b’ibikorwa.

18 “Nta wuzosubira kwumva ubukazi mu gihugu cawe, ukunyaga canke ugusenyuka hagati y’imbibe zawe. Kandi impome zawe ntuzobura kuzita Ubukiriro, amarembo yawe na yo Ishemezo. 19 Izuba ntirizosubira kukubera umuco ku murango, n’ukwezi ntikuzosubira kuguha umuco ngo ukubere umwakaka. Mugabo Yehova azokubera koko umuco wamaho igihe kitagira urugero, Imana yawe ikubere ubwiza. 20 Izuba ryawe ntirizosubira kurenga, eka n’ukwezi kwawe ntikuzoyama; kuko Yehova ubwiwe azokubera umuco wamaho igihe kitagira urugero, kandi imisi y’ukugandara kwawe izoba yarangiye. 21 Nayo abantu bawe, bose bazoba abagororotsi; bazokwigabira igihugu gushika igihe kitagira urugero, babe umunago nitereye, igikorwa c’amaboko yanje, kugira ngo nsharizwe. 22 Umutoyi azocika igihumbi, uwo hasi na we acike ihanga rikomeye. Jewe Yehova, nzovyihutisha mu gihe cavyo.”
