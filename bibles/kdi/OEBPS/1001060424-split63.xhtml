Yesaya  63 : 1 - 19

63 Ni nde uyo aje ava i Edomu, uw’impuzu z’amabara abengagira aje ava i Bozira, uyo ateye iteka mu vyambarwa vyiwe, agendera mu bwinshi bw’inkomezi ziwe?

“Ni jewe, Umwe avuga mu bugororotsi, Umwe agwije inkomezi ku bwo gukiza.”

2 Kubera iki ivyambarwa vyawe bitukura, impuzu zawe na zo zigasa n’iz’uwuganisha ibirenge mu ruganiro rw’umuvinyu?

3 “Ubwato bw’umuvinyu nabuganishijemwo ibirenge jenyene, ata n’umwe turi kumwe wo mu bisata vy’abantu. Nagumye ndabaganisha ibirenge mw’ishavu ryanje, nguma ndabakandagira mu bushangashirwe bwanje. Amaraso yabo ataruka yaguma atarukira ku mpuzu zanje, kandi ivyambarwa vyanje vyose naravyanduje. 4 Kuko umusi w’ukwihora uri mu mutima wanje, kandi umwaka w’abacunguwe banje waje. 5 Nagumye nitegereza, mugabo ntihagira uwumfasha; ndajorerwa, mugabo ntihagira uwunshigikira. Nuko ukuboko kwanje kundonsa ubukiriro, kandi ubushangashirwe bwanje ni bwo bwanshigikiye. 6 Nagumye nkandagira ibisata vy’abantu mw’ishavu ryanje, ndababoreza ubushangashirwe bwanje, nsesa amaraso yabo yataruka.”

7 Nzovuga ibikorwa vy’ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo vya Yehova, amashemezo ya Yehova, nk’uko ivyo Yehova yatugiriye vyose biri, egome, ukumera neza kwinshi yagiriye inzu ya Isirayeli, ukwo yabagiriye nk’uko imbabazi ziwe ziri, nk’uko kandi ibikorwa vyiwe vyinshi vy’ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo biri. 8 Yavuze kandi ati: “Ni ukuri, ni abantu banje, abana batazohemuka.” Ni co gituma ari bo yabereye Umukiza. 9 Mu marushwa yabo yose, vyaramuteye amarushwa. Kandi intumwa yiwe bwite yarabakijije. Mu rukundo rwiwe no mu mpuhwe ziwe, we ubwiwe yarabacunguye, araheza abashira hejuru yongera arabaterura imisi yose ya kera.

10 Mugabo bo baragararije, bababaza impwemu nyeranda yiwe. Yaciye ahinduka umwansi wabo, we ubwiwe arabarwanya. 11 Maze batangura kwibuka imisi ya kera, na Musa umusavyi wiwe, ngo: “Ari hehe Umwe yabadugana abakuye mu kiyaga hamwe n’abungere b’umukuku wiwe? Ari hehe Umwe yamushiramwo impwemu nyeranda yiwe? 12 Ari hehe Umwe yatuma ukuboko kwiwe kwiza kugendera iburyo bwa Musa; Umwe yasatura amazi imbere yabo kugira ngo yiheshe izina ryamaho igihe kitagira urugero; 13 Umwe yabacisha mu mazi asuriranya, ku buryo badatsitara, nka kurya kw’ifarasi mu gahinga? 14 Nka kurya igikoko kimanuka mu kiyaya c’umubande, impwemu ya Yehova yaciye ituma baruhuka.”

Ukwo ni ko wayoboye abantu bawe kugira ngo wiheshe izina ryiza.

15 Niwitegereze uri mw’ijuru, urabe uri ahantu hakirurutse uba h’ubweranda n’ubwiza. Mbega umwete wawe n’ubushobozi bwawe bwuzuye, ukudobangana kw’amara yawe be n’imbabazi zawe, biri hehe? Vyanse kuza iyo ndi. 16 Kuko uri Data; naho Aburahamu yoba atatumenye, na Isirayeli ntatwemere, wewe, ewe Yehova, uri Data. Umucunguzi wacu wo kuva kera, ni ryo zina ryawe. 17 Kubera iki ga Yehova, uguma utwangariza kure y’inzira zawe? Kubera iki ukomantaza umutima wacu ngo ntugutinye? Nugaruke ku bw’abasavyi bawe, ku bw’imiryango y’intoranwa yawe. 18 Igisata cawe ceranda camaze akanya gatoyi gusa kihagaba. Abarwanizi bacu barahonyanze aheranda hawe. 19 Haheze igihe kirekire ducitse nk’abo utaganje, nk’abatitiriwe izina ryawe.
