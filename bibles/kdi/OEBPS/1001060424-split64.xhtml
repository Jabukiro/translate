Yesaya  64 : 1 - 12

64 Yoo! ese iyo uba waratantamuye amajuru, ukamanuka, imisozi ikanyiganyiga kubera wewe, 2 nk’igihe umuriro udometse ikigunda, kandi umuriro ukabiza amazi, kugira ngo wewe umenyeshe izina ryawe abarwanizi bawe, kugira ngo amahanga ahagarike umutima kubera wewe! 3 Hamwe wakora ibintu biteye ubwoba tutaziga, waramanutse. Imisozi yaranyiganyize kubera wewe. 4 Kandi kuva kera nta n’umwe yumvise, eka nta wateze ugutwi, eka nta jisho ryabonye iyindi Mana itari wewe, igira ico ikoreye uwuguma ayiteze. 5 Wahuye n’uwigina agakora ivy’ubugororotsi, abaguma bakwibuka mu nzira zawe.

Raba! Wewe ubwawe wararakaye, mu gihe twaguma ducumura, tubimaramwo igihe kirekire; none ubwo turakwiriye gukizwa? 6 Ducika nk’umuntu ahumanye, twese uko tungana, ibikorwa vyacu vy’ubugororotsi vyose na vyo bikaba bimeze nk’impuzu yo mu bihe vy’ubutinyanka; tuzoyama nk’amababi, twese, kandi amakosa yacu ni yo azodukukumura nk’umuyaga. 7 Nta n’umwe yambaza izina ryawe, nta n’umwe avyuka ngo agufate; kuko waduhishije mu maso hawe, ugatuma dushongeshwa n’ububasha bw’ikosa ryacu.

8 None rero, ewe Yehova, uri Data. Twebwe turi ibumba, nawe uri Umubumvyi wacu; kandi twese turi igikorwa c’ukuboko kwawe. 9 Ewe Yehova, nturakare bimwe birenze, kandi ntiwibuke ikosa ryacu ibihe bidahera. Ehe raba, turakwinginze: twese turi abantu bawe. 10 Ibisagara vyawe vyeranda vyacitse agahinga. Siyoni ubwayo yacitse agahinga gasa, Yeruzalemu icika igiharabuga. 11 Inzu yacu y’ubweranda n’ubwiza, iyo ba sogokuruza bacu bagushemerezamwo, ubwayo yacitse ikintu co guturira mu muriro; kandi kimwe cose mu bintu vyacu bihimbaye cacitse agatikizo. 12 None imbere y’ivyo bintu, mbega uzobandanya kwitangīra ga Yehova? Mbega uzoguma winumiye, ureke dutezwe amarushwa bimwe birenze?
