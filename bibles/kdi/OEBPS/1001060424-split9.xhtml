Yesaya  9 : 1 - 21

9 Ariko rero, umwijima ntuzoba nk’igihe igihugu cari mu maganya, nk’igihe ca kera aho umuntu yakengera igihugu ca Zebuloni n’igihugu ca Nafutali, be n’igihe mu nyuma umuntu yatuma haterwa iteka—ya nzira y’iruhande y’ikiyaga, mu karere ka Yorodani, Galilaya y’amahanga. 2 Abantu bahora bagendera mu mwiza babonye umuco mwinshi. Nayo ababa mu gihugu c’igitutu kizitanye, umuco wabakayanganiyeko. 3 Wagwije ihanga; warigwirije umunezero. Banezerewe imbere yawe n’umunezero nk’uwo mu gihe c’iyimbura, nk’abagira akanyamuneza igihe bagabura isahu.

4 Kuko ingǒgo* y’umutwaro wabo n’inkoni yo ku bitugu vyabo, ikibando c’uwubahindagiza ku bikorwa, wabijanjaguye nko ku musi wa Midiyani. 5 Kuko ibotine yose y’uwuhonyanga adodagura be n’umutamana ukumbagajwe mu maraso bibaye ivy’uguturirwa nk’indya z’umuriro. 6 Kuko hari umwana atuvukiye, hari umuhungu duhawe; ubutware bwa kiganwa buzoba ku rutugu rwiwe. Kandi izina azokwitwa ni Umujanama w’agatangaza, Imana ikomeye, Data ahoraho, Umuganwa w’amahoro. 7 Ukugwira kw’ubutware bwa kiganwa be n’amahoro ntibizogira iherezo, ku ntebe y’ubwami ya Dawidi no ku bwami bwiwe, kugira ngo bushimangirwe no kugira ngo bushigikizwe ubutungane n’ubugororotsi, kuva ubu no gushika igihe kitagira urugero*. Umwete nyene wa Yehova nyen’ingabo uzobikora.

8 Hari ijambo Yehova yatumye kuri Yakobo, rigwa kuri Isirayeli. 9 Kandi abantu bazorimenya, mbere bose, Efurayimu be n’uwuba i Samariya, kubera ubwibone bwabo no kubera ukutisonera kw’umutima wabo, mu kuvuga bati: 10 “Amatafari ni yo yaguye, mugabo tuzokwubakisha amabuye abajwe. Ibiti vy’imisukomora ni vyo vyiciwe, mugabo tuzobisubiriza amasederi.” 11 Kandi Yehova azoduza hejuru abarwanizi ba Rezini ngo bamurwanye, kandi abansi b’uyo azobashoreza umuhunda, 12 Siriya ituruke mu buseruko, Abafilisitiya na bo baturuke inyuma, barotse Isirayeli bataburuye akanwa. Kubera ivyo vyose, ishavu ryiwe ntiryasubiye inyuma, mugabo ukuboko kwiwe kuracabanguye.

13 Abantu ntibagarutse ku wabakubita, kandi ntibarondeye Yehova nyen’ingabo. 14 Kandi Yehova azokuraho muri Isirayeli umutwe n’umurizo, umuvyaro n’urukangaga, mu musi umwe. 15 Umusaza akaba n’uwubahwa cane ni we mutwe, nayo umuhanuzi atanga inyigisho y’ikinyoma ni we murizo. 16 Kandi abayobora aba bantu ni bo babatera kwangara; nayo abayoborwa bo muri bo, ni bo bavurugwa ubwenge. 17 Ni co gituma Yehova atazonezerwa n’imisore yabo, kandi ntazogirira imbabazi impfuvyi* zabo n’abapfakazi babo; kuko bose ari abahuni n’inkozi z’ikibi, kandi akanwa kose kakaba kavuga ivy’ubwenge buke. Kubera ivyo vyose, ishavu ryiwe ntiryasubiye inyuma, mugabo ukuboko kwiwe kuracabanguye.

18 Kuko ububisha busigaye bururumba nk’umuriro; buzorigata ibisaka vy’amahwa n’ivyatsi bibi. Buzokwaka umuriro mu bisaka vy’inzitane vy’ikibira, kandi bizoduzwa nk’agashururu k’umwotsi. 19 Mu burake bwa Yehova nyen’ingabo igihugu kiradometswe, kandi abantu bazocika nk’indya z’umuriro. Nta n’uwuzogirira impuhwe mwene wabo. 20 Umuntu azokeba iburyo, ariko ntazobura gusonza; umuntu azorya ibubamfu, ariko ntibazogenda bahaze. Umwe wese muri bo azorya inyama z’ukuboko kwiwe, 21 Manase arye Efurayimu, Efurayimu arye Manase. Bazorwaniriza hamwe Yuda. Kubera ivyo vyose, ishavu ryiwe ntiryasubiye inyuma, mugabo ukuboko kwiwe kuracabanguye.

^ Yes. 9:4 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ Yes. 9:7 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ Yes. 9:17 Ijambo ku rindi ni “uduhungu tutagira se.”
