Yeremiya  14 : 1 - 22

14 Iri ni ryo jambo rya Yehova ryaje kuri Yeremiya ku vyerekeye uruzuba: 2 Yuda iragandaye, kandi amarembo yayo arayamye. Ayo marembo yajonjogoye gushika hasi, kandi ukuboroga kwa Yeruzalemu kuraduze. 3 Abakomakomeye baho batumye ba nyarucari babo kurondera amazi. Bashitse ku mifurege. Nta mazi baronse. Bagarukanye ivyombo bigaragara. Bamaramaye, batashe zirayoye, kandi bitwikiriye umutwe. 4 Kubera ubutaka busataguritse bivanye n’uko ata mvura y’isegenya yaguye mu gihugu, abarimyi bamaramaye; bitwikiriye umutwe. 5 Kuko n’impongokazi yo mw’ishamba yavyaye, mugabo igata umwana, kubera ata vyatsi biremvye vyariho. 6 Imparage zahagaze ku mirambi igaragara; zamotēreye umuyaga nk’imbwebwe; amaso yazo yayamye kubera ata bimera biriho. 7 Naho amakosa yacu atwagiriza, ewe Yehova, nugire ico ukoze ku bw’izina ryawe; kuko ubuhemu bwacu bwabaye bwinshi; ni wewe twacumuyeko.

8 Ewe cizigiro ca Isirayeli, Umukiza wiwe mu gihe c’amarushwa, kubera iki uba nka kavamahanga mu gihugu, nk’ingenzi yakebeje kugira ngo irare ahantu? 9 Kubera iki uba nk’umuntu ajorewe, nk’umuntu w’umunyenkomezi adashoboye gukiza? Yamara wewe uri hagati muri twebwe, ewe Yehova, kandi twitiriwe izina ryawe. Ntuduhebe.

10 Ibi ni vyo Yehova avuze kuri aba bantu: “Gutyo bakunze kuyerera; ntibacungereye ibirenge vyabo. Ni co catumye Yehova adahimbarwa na bo. Ubu rero azokwibuka ikosa ryabo, yiteho ibicumuro vyabo.”

11 Nuko Yehova ambwira ati: “Ntusengere aba bantu ubaronderera iciza. 12 Igihe bisonzesha, sinumviriza akamo kabo k’ukwinginga; kandi igihe bashikana ishikanwa riturirwa uko ryakabaye n’ishikanwa ry’intete, ntibimpimbara; kuko ngira ndabamarishe inkota n’ikigoyi n’ikiza.”

13 Nca mvuga nti: “Yoo, ewe Mukama Segaba Yehova! Ehe abahanuzi bababwira ngo: ‘Nta nkota muzobona, kandi nta kigoyi kizobazako, mugabo amahoro nyakuri ni yo nzobaha aha hantu.’”

14 Yehova yongera kumbwira ati: “Ubuhanuzi bw’ibinyoma ni bwo abo bahanuzi bavuga mw’izina ryanje. Sinabatumye, eka kandi nta co nabategetse canke ngo ndababwire. Bababwira mu buryo bw’ubuhanuzi ukwerekwa kw’ikinyoma, ukuragura, ikintu ata co kimaze, n’amayeri yo mu mutima wabo. 15 Ibi rero ni vyo Yehova avuze: Ku bijanye n’abo bahanuzi bavuga ubuhanuzi mw’izina ryanje kandi jewe ntabatumye, bakavuga yuko ata nkota canke ikigoyi bizoza muri iki gihugu, ‘abo bahanuzi bazomarwa n’inkota n’ikigoyi. 16 Kandi abantu babwira ubuhanuzi bazoshiburirwa mu mabarabara ya Yeruzalemu kubera ikigoyi n’inkota, ntihagire uwubahamba, bo ubwabo n’abagore babo n’abahungu babo n’abakobwa babo. Nzobasukako ivyago vyabo.’

17 “Uze ubabwire iri jambo uti: ‘Amaso yanje natembe amosozi ijoro n’umurango, ntahagarike, kuko umukobwa w’isugi w’abantu banje avunitse agasenyuka bimwe bikomeye, akagira uruguma rusesereza cane. 18 Iyo nsohotse nkaja mu gahinga, ndahasanga abagandaguwe n’inkota! Ninjira mu gisagara na ho, nkahasanga indwara ziturutse ku kigoyi! Kuko umuhanuzi n’umuherezi banyuragiye mu gihugu batazi.’”

19 Mbega warataye Yuda buhere, canke umutima wawe warazinutswe Siyoni? Kubera iki none wadukubise, ku buryo tudashobora gukira? Twari twiteze amahoro, mugabo nta ciza caje; twari twiteze igihe c’ugukira, none ehe, haje ivutu! 20 Ewe Yehova, turemeye icese koko ububisha bwacu, ikosa rya ba sogokuruza bacu, kuko twagucumuyeko. 21 Ntutugaye ku bw’izina ryawe; ntusuzugure intebe y’ubwami yawe ininahaye. Niwibuke; nturenge kw’isezerano wagiranye natwe. 22 Mbega mu bigirwamana bitagira mvura vy’amahanga hari icoshobora kugwisha imvura, canke amajuru ubwayo yoshobora gutanga imvura nyinshi? None si wewe, ga Yehova Mana yacu? Kandi turakwizigiye, kuko ari wewe wakoze ivyo bintu vyose.
