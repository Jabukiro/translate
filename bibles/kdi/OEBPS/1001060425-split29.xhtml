Yeremiya  29 : 1 - 32

29 Aya ni yo majambo y’ikete Yeremiya umuhanuzi yarungitse ari i Yeruzalemu, arirungikiye amasigarira y’abagabo b’inararibonye b’abajanywe ari inyagano, n’abaherezi, n’abahanuzi, n’abantu bose, abo Nebukadinezari yari yaratwaye mu bunyagano i Babiloni abakuye i Yeruzalemu, 2 inyuma y’aho Yekoniya umwami, n’umugore w’iteka, n’abakozi bo ku kirimba, abaganwa ba Yuda n’aba Yeruzalemu, n’abahinguzi b’utugenegene, n’abubatsi b’ibibambazi baviriye i Yeruzalemu. 3 Ryarungikanywe Elasa mwene Shafani na Gemariya mwene Hilikiya, abo Zedekiya umwami wa Yuda yatumye i Babiloni kuri Nebukadinezari umwami wa Babiloni, rikaba ryavuga riti:

4 “Ibi ni vyo Yehova nyen’ingabo, Imana ya Isirayeli, abwiye abajanywe ari inyagano bose, abo natumye baja mu bunyagano i Babiloni bavuye i Yeruzalemu, 5 ati: ‘Nimwubake amazu muyabemwo, kandi mutere imirima murye ivyo yamye. 6 Nimwabire abagore muvyare abahungu n’abakobwa; nimusabire abahungu banyu abagore mwongere mushingire abakobwa banyu, kugira bavyare abahungu n’abakobwa; mugwirire hiyo, ntimube inkehwa. 7 Vyongeye, murondere amahoro y’igisagara mujamwo muri inyagano, mugisabire kuri Yehova, kuko nicaba mu mahoro namwe muzogira amahoro. 8 Kuko ibi ari vyo Yehova nyen’ingabo, Imana ya Isirayeli, avuze: “Abahanuzi banyu bari muri mwebwe n’abaraguzi banyu ntibabahende, kandi ntimwumvirize indoto zabo barota. 9 Kuko ‘bababwira ubuhanuzi mu kinyoma mw’izina ryanje. Sinabatumye,’ ni ko Yehova avuze.”’”

10 “Kuko ibi ari vyo Yehova avuze: ‘Imyaka mirongo irindwi niyarangira muri i Babiloni, nzobitaho, kandi nzoshitsa kuri mwebwe ijambo ryanje ryiza mu kubagarukana aha hantu.’

11 “‘Kuko jewe nzi neza ivyiyumviro ndabiyumvirako,’ ni ko Yehova avuze, ‘ivyiyumviro vy’amahoro, atari ivy’ivyago, kugira ngo ndabahe kazoza n’icizigiro. 12 Muzompamagara koko, muze, maze munsenge, nanje nzobumviriza.’

13 “‘Muzondondera koko mundonke, kuko muzondondera n’umutima wanyu wose. 14 Nanje nzoreka mundonke,’ ni ko Yehova avuze. ‘Nzokwegeranya umugwi w’imbohe wanyu, ndabakoranirize hamwe ndabakuye mu mahanga yose n’ahantu hose nabashwiragirije,’ ni ko Yehova avuze. ‘Kandi nzobagarukana ahantu nabakuye nkabajana mu bunyagano.’

15 “Mugabo mwavuze muti: ‘Yehova yaduhagurukirije abahanuzi i Babiloni.’

16 “Kuko ibi ari vyo Yehova abwiye umwami yicaye ku ntebe y’ubwami ya Dawidi n’abantu bose baba muri iki gisagara, bene wanyu batajanye namwe mu bunyagano, 17 ati: ‘Ibi ni vyo Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze: “Ehe ngira ndabarungikire inkota n’ikigoyi n’ikiza, kandi nzobagira nk’insukoni zituritse zidashobora kuribwa kubera ububi bwazo.”’

18 “‘Nzobakurikiza inkota n’ikigoyi n’ikiza, kandi nzobatanga babe akajugumiza ku bwami bwose bwo mw’isi, babe umuvumo n’akajoreza n’abo kuvugiriza agahwa n’iceyi mu mahanga yose nzobashwiragirizamwo ata kabuza, 19 kubera ko batumvirije amajambo yanje,’ ni ko Yehova avuze, ‘ayo nabarungikiye nkoresheje abasavyi banje abahanuzi, nkazinduka kare nkabatuma.’

“‘Mugabo ntimwumvirije,’ ni ko Yehova avuze.

20 “Nayo mwebwe, nimwumve ijambo rya Yehova, yemwe mwese abajanywe mu bunyagano, abo narungitse i Babiloni bavuye i Yeruzalemu. 21 Ibi ni vyo Yehova nyen’ingabo, Imana ya Isirayeli, avuze kuri Ahabu mwene Kolaya kandi abwiye Zedekiya mwene Mayaseya, abababwira ubuhanuzi bw’ikinyoma mw’izina ryanje: ‘Ehe ngira ndabatange mu kuboko kwa Nebukadirezari umwami wa Babiloni, na we azobicira mu maso yanyu. 22 Kandi umugwi wose w’abajanywe mu bunyagano ba Yuda bari i Babiloni uzokura kuri bo umuvumo, bavuge bati: “Yehova arakakugira nka Zedekiya na Ahabu, abo umwami wa Babiloni yataze ku muriro!” 23 kuko bakoze ivy’ubwenge buke muri Isirayeli, bakaba baguma barenga ibigo n’abagore ba bagenzi babo kandi bakaguma bavuga babesha mw’izina ryanje ijambo ntabategetse.

“‘“Ni jewe ndabizi kandi ndi icabona,” ni ko Yehova avuze.’”

24 “Shemaya w’i Nehelamu na we uze umubwire uti: 25 ‘Ibi ni vyo Yehova nyen’ingabo, Imana ya Isirayeli, avuze: “Ko warungikiye amakete mw’izina ryawe abantu bose bari i Yeruzalemu, na Zefaniya mwene Mayaseya, umuherezi, n’abaherezi bose, uti: 26 ‘Yehova ubwiwe yakugize umuherezi mu kibanza ca Yehoyada umuherezi, kugira ngo ube umucungezi mukuru w’inzu ya Yehova ku muntu uwo ari we wese yasaze kandi yifata nk’umuhanuzi, kandi umushire mu mpatane z’ibiti no mu mpatane zifata umutwe n’amaboko; 27 kubera iki none utahambariye Yeremiya w’i Anatoti, uwuriko yifata nk’umuhanuzi kuri mwebwe? 28 Kuko ico ari co catumye adutumako i Babiloni ngo: “Burabandanya! Nimwubake amazu muyabemwo; nimutere imirima murye ivyo yamye, . . .”’”’”

29 Nuko Zefaniya umuherezi atangura gusomera iryo kete mu matwi ya Yeremiya umuhanuzi.

30 Maze ijambo rya Yehova riza kuri Yeremiya riti: 31 “Tuma ku bajanywe mu bunyagano bose uti: ‘Ibi ni vyo Yehova avuze kuri Shemaya w’i Nehelamu: “Ko Shemaya yababwiye ubuhanuzi, kandi jewe ntamutumye, akagerageza kubizigiza ikinyoma, 32 ibi rero ni vyo Yehova avuze: ‘Ehe ngira niteho ivya Shemaya w’i Nehelamu n’uruvyaro rwiwe.’

“‘“‘Ntazoronka umuntu azoba hagati muri aba bantu; kandi ntazokwitegereza iciza ngira nkorere abantu banje,’ ni ko Yehova avuze, ‘kuko yavuze ivy’ukugarariza Yehova bimwe vyeruye.’”’”
