Yeremiya  37 : 1 - 21

37 Nuko Umwami Zedekiya mwene Yosiya atangura kuganza mu kibanza ca Koniya mwene Yehoyakimu, uwo Nebukadirezari umwami wa Babiloni yari yagize umwami mu gihugu ca Yuda. 2 Mugabo we hamwe n’abasuku biwe n’abanyagihugu ntibumviriza amajambo ya Yehova, ayo yari yaravuze biciye kuri Yeremiya umuhanuzi.

3 Nuko Umwami Zedekiya atuma Yehukali mwene Shelemiya na Zefaniya mwene Mayaseya umuherezi kuri Yeremiya umuhanuzi, ati: “Ndakwinginze udusabire kuri Yehova Imana yacu.” 4 Yeremiya yarinjira akongera agasohoka hagati mu bantu, kuko batari baramushize mu nzu y’ibohero. 5 Kandi inteko za gisirikare za Farawo zari zivuye mu Misiri; nuko Abakaludaya bari basugereje Yeruzalemu bumva inkuru yabo. Baca bavaho baheba gutera Yeruzalemu. 6 Maze ijambo rya Yehova riza kuri Yeremiya umuhanuzi riti: 7 “Ibi ni vyo Yehova Imana ya Isirayeli avuze: ‘Ibi abe ari vyo mubwira umwami wa Yuda abatumye kuri jewe kunsiguza, muti: “Ehe, inteko za gisirikare za Farawo zije kuri mwebwe kugira zibafashe zizobwirizwa gusubira mu gihugu cazo, ari co Misiri. 8 Kandi Abakaludaya bazogaruka ata kabuza, barwanye iki gisagara, bagifate maze bagiturize umuriro.” 9 Ibi ni vyo Yehova avuze: “Ntimwihende muvuga ngo: ‘Abakaludaya bazogenda ata kabuza batureke,’ kuko batazogenda. 10 Kuko naho mwoba mwatikije inteko za gisirikare zose z’Abakaludaya ziriko zibarwanya hagasigara muri bo abagabo batobekeranijwe, bohaguruka umwe wese mw’ihema ryiwe, bagaturiza koko iki igisagara umuriro.”’”

11 Nuko inteko za gisirikare z’Abakaludaya zimaze kuhava zikagenda, zikareka gutera Yeruzalemu kubera inteko za gisirikare za Farawo, 12 Yeremiya arasohoka ava i Yeruzalemu kugira aje mu gihugu ca Benyamini, ngo aronkeyo umugabane wiwe mu bantu. 13 Igihe yashika mw’Irembo rya Benyamini, umukuru w’abarinzi yitwa Iriya mwene Shelemiya, Shelemiya mwene Hananiya, ni ho yari ari. Aca afata Yeremiya umuhanuzi, amubwira ati: “Ugira wishikane ku Bakaludaya!” 14 Mugabo Yeremiya avuga ati: “Si vyo! Singira nishikane ku Bakaludaya.” Mugabo ntiyamwumviriza. Nuko Iriya agumya Yeremiya hanyuma amushira abaganwa. 15 Abaganwa barākarira rero Yeremiya, baramukubita maze bamushira mu nzu y’amapingu, mu nzu ya Yehonatani umunyamabanga, kuko bari bayigize inzu y’ibohero. 16 Yeremiya yinjiye mu nzu y’itangi no mu vyumba vyo mu kuzimu, agumayo imisi myinshi.

17 Nuko Umwami Zedekiya atuma kumuzana, maze atangura kumubaza ibibazo mu nzu yiwe, mu bwihisho. Amubwira ati: “Hoba hariho ijambo rivuye kuri Yehova?” Yeremiya na we ati: “Ririho koko!” Yongerako ati: “Uzotangwa mu kuboko kw’umwami wa Babiloni!”

18 Maze Yeremiya abwira Umwami Zedekiya ati: “Ni igiki nacumuye kuri wewe no ku basuku bawe no kuri aba bantu, catumye munshira mu nzu y’ibohero? 19 Bari hehe none ba bahanuzi banyu bababwira ubuhanuzi ngo: ‘Umwami wa Babiloni ntazoza kubatera no gutera iki gihugu’? 20 Ubu rero ndakwinginze wumvirize, ewe mukama wanje umwami! Ndakwinginze ugusaba ubutoni kwanje gushike imbere yawe, ntunsubize mu nzu ya Yehonatani umunyamabanga, kugira simpfireyo.” 21 Umwami Zedekiya atanga rero itegeko, maze bashira Yeremiya aho acungirwa mu Kigo c’Abarinzi; nuko baza baramuha ku musi ku musi agakate k’umuzingi kavuye kw’ibarabara ry’abakora igikorwa co kwotsa, gushika imikate yose iheze mu gisagara. Yeremiya abandanya kuba mu Kigo c’Abarinzi.
