Yeremiya  38 : 1 - 28

38 Nuko Shefatiya mwene Matani, na Gedaliya mwene Pashuri, na Yukali mwene Shelemiya, na Pashuri mwene Malikiya, bumva amajambo Yeremiya yariko abwira abantu bose ati: 2 “Ibi ni vyo Yehova avuze: ‘Uwuzoguma muri iki gisagara ni we azokwicwa n’inkota, n’ikigoyi be n’ikiza. Mugabo uwuzosohoka akaja ku Bakaludaya ni we azobandanya kubaho; azonyaga amagara yiwe, abeho.’ 3 Ibi ni vyo Yehova avuze: ‘Iki gisagara kizotangwa ata kabuza mu kuboko kw’inteko za gisirikare z’umwami wa Babiloni, na we azogifata.’”

4 Nuko abaganwa babwira umwami bati: “Turakwinginze uyu mugabo yicwe, kuko ukwo ari ko atuma amaboko y’abarwanyi basigaye muri iki gisagara n’amaboko y’abantu bose aba goyigoyi, mu kubabwira ayo majambo. Kuko uwu mugabo ataronderera amahoro aba bantu, mugabo abaronderera ivyago.” 5 Umwami Zedekiya avuga rero ati: “Ehe ari mu maboko yanyu! Kuko ata kintu na kimwe umwami ashobora kubabuza.”

6 Bafata rero Yeremiya bamuterera mw’itangi rya Malikiya umuhungu w’umwami, iryari mu Kigo c’Abarinzi. Bamanura Yeremiya bakoresheje imigozi. Ico gihe muri iryo tangi nta mazi yariyo, atari urwondo; nuko Yeremiya atangura kunyika muri nya rwondo.

7 Ebedi-meleki Umunyetiyopiya, umugabo yari inkone* kandi yaba mu nzu y’umwami, yumva ko bashize Yeremiya muri nya tangi; kandi umwami yari yicaye mw’Irembo rya Benyamini. 8 Ebedi-meleki arasohoka rero avuye mu nzu y’umwami maze abwira umwami ati: 9 “Ewe mukama wanje umwami, abo bagabo bagize nabi mu vyo bagiriye Yeremiya umuhanuzi vyose, uwo batereye mw’itangi kugira ngo apfire aho ari yishwe n’ikigoyi. Kuko ata mukate ugisigaye mu gisagara.”

10 Nuko umwami ategeka Ebedi-meleki Umunyetiyopiya ati: “Tora aha hantu abagabo mirongo itatu uza guha amategeko, hanyuma uduze Yeremiya umuhanuzi umukure muri nya tangi atarapfa.” 11 Ebedi-meleki atora rero nya bagabo ari buhe amategeko hanyuma yinjira mu nzu y’umwami, aja munsi y’ikigega, atorayo imishambara n’ibipande vy’impuzu vy’imishire, abimanurira Yeremiya muri nya tangi akoresheje imigozi. 12 Maze Ebedi-meleki Umunyetiyopiya abwira Yeremiya ati: “Ndakwinginze ushire iyo mishambara n’ivyo bipande vy’impuzu mu maha yawe munsi y’imigozi.” Nuko Yeremiya agira kuno. 13 Amaherezo bakwegesha Yeremiya nya migozi, baramuduza bamukuye muri nya tangi. Nuko Yeremiya abandanya kuba mu Kigo c’Abarinzi.

14 Umwami Zedekiya rero atuma abantu gutora Yeremiya umuhanuzi ngo bamuzane aho ari ku rwinjiriro rugira gatatu ruri mu nzu ya Yehova, maze umwami abwira Yeremiya ati: “Hari ico ngira nkubaze. Ntihagire ico unyegeza.” 15 Mugabo Yeremiya abwira Zedekiya ati: “Ninakiguhishurira, ubwo ntuhava unyica? Kandi naho nokugira inama, nturi bunyumvirize.” 16 Nuko Umwami Zedekiya arahira Yeremiya mu bwihisho ati: “Ndahiye ukubaho kwa Yehova, we yaduhaye ubu buzima, sindakwica, kandi sindagutanga mu kuboko kw’abo bagabo bariko barondera ubuzima bwawe.”

17 Yeremiya abwira rero Zedekiya ati: “Ibi ni vyo Yehova Imana nyen’ingabo, Imana ya Isirayeli, avuze: ‘Niwasohoka koko ukaja ku baganwa b’umwami wa Babiloni, ntuzobura rero kubandanya kubaho kandi iki gisagara ntikizoturizwa umuriro, kandi wewe n’urugo rwawe muzobandanya kubaho ata kabuza. 18 Mugabo nutasohoka ngo uje ku baganwa b’umwami wa Babiloni, emwe, iki gisagara kizotangwa rero mu kuboko kw’Abakaludaya, na bo bazogituriza umuriro, kandi wewe ntuzokira ukuboko kwabo.’”

19 Maze Umwami Zedekiya abwira Yeremiya ati: “Ndatinya Abayuda bishikanye ku Bakaludaya, kuko bohava bantanga mu kuboko kwabo na bo bakangirira nabi.” 20 Mugabo Yeremiya avuga ati: “Ntibazogutanga. Ndakwinginze ugamburuke ijwi rya Yehova mu vyo nkubwira, maze bizokugendera neza, ubandanye kubaho. 21 Mugabo niwanka gusohoka, iki ni co kintu Yehova anyeretse: 22 Ehe abagore bose basigaye mu nzu y’umwami wa Yuda bajanywe ku baganwa b’umwami wa Babiloni, kandi bariko bavuga bati:

‘Abagabo mufitaniye amahoro baguhendahenze urahendeka.

Banyikije ikirenge cawe mu rwondo; basubiye inyuma bajana iyindi nzira.’

23 Abagore bawe bose n’abana bawe bose barabasohoye babajana ku Bakaludaya, kandi wewe ntuzokira ukuboko kwabo, mugabo uzofatwa n’ukuboko kw’umwami wa Babiloni, kandi kubera wewe, iki gisagara kizoturizwa umuriro.”

24 Nuko Zedekiya abwira Yeremiya ati: “Ntihagire umuntu n’umwe agira ico amenya kuri ivyo bintu, kugira ntupfe. 25 Abaganwa nibumva yuko navuganye nawe hanyuma bakaza koko aho uri, bakakubwira bati: ‘Turakwinginze utubwire: Wabwiye iki umwami? Ntihagire ico utunyegeza, natwe ntitukwica. Kandi umwami yakubwiye iki?’, 26 ubabwire rero uti: ‘Nariko nisabira ubutoni imbere y’umwami, kugira ngo ntansubize mu nzu ya Yehonatani ngo mpfireyo.’”

27 Mu nyuma abaganwa bose baza aho Yeremiya ari, batangura kumubaza. Na we ababwira yisunze ayo majambo yose umwami yari yamutegetse. Nuko baracereza imbere yiwe, kuko ata wari yumvise ico kintu. 28 Maze Yeremiya abandanya kuba mu Kigo c’Abarinzi gushika umusi Yeruzalemu yafatwa. Vyabaye igihe Yeruzalemu yafatwa.

^ Yer. 38:7 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
