Yeremiya  8 : 1 - 22

8 “Ico gihe,” ni ko Yehova avuze, “abantu bazozikurura kandi amagufa y’abami ba Yuda, n’amagufa y’abaganwa baho, n’amagufa y’abaherezi, n’amagufa y’abahanuzi, n’amagufa y’ababa i Yeruzalemu, bayakure mu mva zabo. 2 Kandi bazoyasanza koko munsi y’izuba n’ukwezi n’ingabo zose zo mu majuru, ivyo bakunze, bakabikorera, bakabikurikira, bakabirondera bakongera bakavyunamira. Ntazokwegeranywa canke ngo ahambwe. Azoba nk’intabire ku butaka.”

3 “Amasigarira yose azoba asigaye muri uyu muryango mubi ahantu hose abasigaye bazoba bari, iyo nzoba nabashwiragirije, azohitamwo urupfu aho guhitamwo ubuzima,” ni ko Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze.

4 “Uze ubabwire uti: ‘Ibi ni vyo Yehova avuze: “Mbega bazogwa ntibasubire guhaguruka? Hamwe umwe yogaruka, mbega uwundi na we ntazogaruka? 5 Kubera iki aba bantu, ari bo Yeruzalemu, bahemuka n’ubuhemu budahera? Barafashe uruyeri baragumya; baranse kugaruka. 6 Narateze ugutwi, nguma numviriza. Ntibagumye bavuga nk’uko bibereye. Nta muntu n’umwe yigaye ububi bwiwe, ati: ‘Mbega ivyo nakoze ni ibiki?’ Umwe wese asubira mu nzira ya benshi, nk’ifarasi yirasa mu rugamba. 7 Mbere n’agasoza mu kirere—karazi neza ibihe vyako vyashinzwe; intunguru n’intamba n’ikirogorye—birubahiriza neza kimwekimwe cose igihe caco co gushika. Mugabo abantu banje bo ntibazi urubanza rwa Yehova.”’

8 “‘Mushobora mute kuvuga ngo: “Turi inkerebutsi, itegeko rya Yehova riri kumwe natwe”? Vy’ukuri rero, agasáruzo k’ikinyoma k’abanyamabanga kakoreshejwe mu kinyoma. 9 Inkerebutsi zamaramaye. Zagize ubwoba kandi zizofatwa. Ehe zataye ijambo rya Yehova, none ubukerebutsi zifise ni ubuhe? 10 Ku bw’ivyo, abagore babo nzobaha abandi bagabo, amatongo yabo ndayahe abayigarurira; kuko, kuva ku mutoyi muri bo gushika no ku wuhambaye kuruta abandi muri bo, umwe wese yironderera inyungu y’akarenganyo; kuva ku muhanuzi gushika no ku muherezi, umwe wese akorana ububeshi. 11 Bagerageza gukiza bimwe vy’indenzirenzi ugusenyuka kw’umukobwa w’abantu banje, bakavuga bati: “Hari amahoro! Hari amahoro!”, kandi ata mahoro ariho. 12 Boba baramaramajwe n’uko bakoze ibiteye ishishi? Kimwe coco, ntibamaramaye; vyongeye, ntibigeze bamenya no guteterwa ico ari co.

“‘Ku bw’ivyo, bazogwa mu bagwa. Bazotsitara ivyabo nivyarabwa,’ ni ko Yehova avuze.

13 “‘Ninimbura, nzobaherengeteza,’ ni ko Yehova avuze. ‘Nta nzabibu zizoba zikiri ku muzabibu, kandi nta nsukoni zizoba zikiri ku giti c’umusukoni, kandi urubabi ruzokabirana koko. Ibintu ndabaha bizobaca iruhande.’”

14 “Kubera iki twiyicarira dutekanye? Nimukoranire hamwe, twinjire mu bisagara bikingijwe ibihome, maze tunumireyo. Kuko Yehova Imana yacu atunumije, kandi atunywesha amazi arimwo ubumara, kubera ko twacumuye kuri Yehova. 15 Twari twiteze amahoro, mugabo nta ciza caje; twari twiteze igihe c’ugukira, mugabo ehe, haje ivutu! 16 Ugupfuhagira kw’amafarasi yiwe kwumvikaniye i Dani. Igihugu cose gitanguye gutigitishwa n’ijwi ry’ukwanaga kw’amafarasi y’ingabo. Baraza bakarigata igihugu n’ivyuzuye muri co, igisagara n’abakibamwo.”

17 “Kuko ehe ngira ndabarungikemwo inzoka, inzoka z’ubumara, zidashobora kugotwa, na zo zizobakomora ata kabuza,” ni ko Yehova avuze.

18 Intuntu idashobora kuvūrwa iranshwaye. Umutima wanje urarwaye. 19 Ehe hari ijwi ry’ugutabaza ry’umukobwa w’abantu banje ryumvikanira mu gihugu ca kure riti: “Mbega Yehova ntari i Siyoni? Canke umwami waho si yo ari?”

“Kubera iki none bambabarishije ibishusho vyabo bibajwe, imana zabo z’inyamahanga zitagira mvura?”

20 “Iyimbura riraheze, ici rirarangiye; mugabo tweho ntitwakijijwe!”

21 Nsabijwe n’ugusenyuka kw’umukobwa w’abantu banje. Ndababaye. Mfashwe n’akajoreza. 22 Mbega i Gileyadi ntihari umusomozo*? Canke ntihariyo umuvūzi? Kubera iki none umukobwa w’abantu banje atasubiranye?

^ Yer. 8:22 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
