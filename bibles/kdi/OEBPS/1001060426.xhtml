Gucura intimba  1 : 1 - 22

Gucura intimba

א [Alefu]

1 Ewe kuntu yicaye ari inyakamwe, umwe yari igisagara kigwiriyemwo abantu!

Ewe kuntu yabaye nk’umupfakazi, umwe yahoramwo abanyagihugu benshi ugereranije n’ayandi mahanga!

Ewe kuntu uwari umuganwakazi mu ntara z’ubutware asigaye ari uwo gukora ibikorwa vy’agahato!

ב [Beti]

 2 Ararira cane mw’ijoro, kandi amosozi yiwe ari ku matama yiwe.

Ntagira uwumuhoza mu bakunzi biwe bose.

Abagenzi biwe bose baramuhemukiye. Basigaye ari abansi kuri we.

ג [Gimelu]

 3 Yuda yagiye mu bunyagano kubera amarushwa no kubera ubushumba bukomeye.

Yabwirijwe kuba mu mahanga. Nta hantu ho kuruhukira yaronse.

Abahora bamuhama bose bamuhambirije mu marushwa.

ד [Daleti]

 4 Inzira za Siyoni ziragandaye, kuko ata n’umwe aza ku musi mukuru.

Amarembo yiwe yose bayagize umusaka; abaherezi biwe baraniha.

Abigeme biwe b’isugi bishwe n’intuntu, na we ubwiwe afise umururazi.

ה [Heʼ]

 5 Abarwanizi biwe basigaye bari hejuru yiwe. Abasanzwe ari abansi biwe nta co banezwe.

Kuko Yehova ubwiwe yamuteje intuntu kubera ubwinshi bw’ibigabitanyo vyiwe,

Abana biwe bagiye ari imbohe imbere y’umurwanizi.

ו [Vau]

 6 Ubwiza bw’umukobwa wa Siyoni bwose buramuvuyeko.

Abaganwa biwe babaye nk’amafumbēri yabuze ubwatsi bwo kurisha;

Baguma bagenda batagira inguvu imbere y’uwubakurikirana.

ז [Zayini]

 7 Yeruzalemu, mu misi y’amarushwa yiwe n’iy’abantu biwe batagira aho baba, yibutse

Ibintu bihimbaye vyiwe vyose vyariho kuva mu misi ya kera.

Igihe abantu biwe bagwa mu kuboko kw’umurwanizi, na we akabura uwumutabara,

Abarwanizi baramubonye. Baratwenze ugusenyuka kwiwe.

ח [Heti]

 8 Yeruzalemu yakoze igicumuro ceruye. Ni co gituma yabaye ikintu co kuzinukwa.

Abahora bamutera iteka bose basigaye bamukengera, kuko babonye ubwambure bwiwe.

We ubwiwe araniha, agahindukiza umugongo.

ט [Teti]

 9 Ubuhumane bwiwe buri mu mpuzu* ziwe. Ntiyibutse kazoza kiwe,

Kandi amanuka mu buryo bw’akajoreza. Ntagira uwumuhoza.

Ewe Yehova, raba amarushwa yanje, kuko umwansi yihambaje.

י [Yodi]

10 Umurwanizi yaramvuriye ukuboko ibintu bihimbaye vyiwe vyose.

Kuko yabonye amahanga yinjiye aheranda hiwe,

Ayo wategetse ko adakwiye kwinjira mw’ishengero ryawe.

כ [Kafu]

11 Abantu biwe bose baraniha; barondera umukate.

Batanze ibintu vyabo bihimbaye kugira baronke utwo kurya, ngo bahembure umutima.

Ewe Yehova, raba, witegereze, kuko nsigaye ndi nk’umugore ata co amaze.

ל [Lamedi]

12 Mbe nta co bibabwiye mwebwe mwese abariko barahita ku nzira? Nimwitegereze, murabe.

Mbega hari umubabaro uriho umeze nk’umubabaro wanje natejwe,

Uwo Yehova yaduteresheje intuntu ku musi w’ishavu ryiwe rirurumba?

מ [Memu]

13 Yarungitse ari hejuru umuriro mu magufa yanje, kandi anesha rimwerimwe ryose.

Yateze ibirenge vyanje urusenga. Yansubije inyuma.

Yampinduye umugore yagizwe umusaka. Umusi wose nirirwa ndwaye.

נ [Nuni]

14 Yagumye arikanuye kugira ngo abone ibigabitanyo vyanje. Mu kuboko kwiwe birasobekerana.

Vyaduze kw’izosi ryanje. Inkomezi zanje zatsitaye.

Yehova yantanze mu kuboko kw’abo ntashobora guhagurukira ngo ndabarwanye.

ס [Sameki]

15 Abanyenkomezi banje bose Yehova yabashibuye abakuye hagati muri jewe.

Yampamagariye ikoraniro, kugira risavye imisore yanje.

Yehova yaganishije ibirenge mu ruganiro rw’umuvinyu rw’umukobwa w’isugi wa Yuda.

ע [ʽAyini]

16 Jewe umugore, ivyo bintu ni vyo biriko bindiza. Ijisho ryanje, ijisho ryanje ririko ritemba amazi.

Kuko umuhoza yabaye kure yanje, uwohembura umutima wanje.

Abana banje bagizwe umusaka, kuko umwansi yihambaje.

פ [Peʼ]

17 Siyoni yashanje amaboko. Ntagira uwumuhoza.

Ku bijanye na Yakobo, Yehova yategetse abamukikije bose ngo babe abarwanizi biwe.

Yeruzalemu isigaye ari ikintu co kuzinukwa hagati muri bo.

צ [Tsade]

18 Yehova aragororotse, kuko nagararije akanwa kiwe.

Nimwumvirize rero, yemwe bisata vy’abantu mwese, murabe umubabaro wanje.

Abigeme banje b’isugi n’imisore yanje bajanywe kugirwa imbohe.

ק [Kofu]

19 Nahamagaye abahora bankunda rwinshi. Barampenze.

Abaherezi banje n’abatama banje bacikaniye mu gisagara,

Mu gihe bariko barondera utwo kurya kugira ngo bahembure umutima.

ר [Reshi]

20 Ewe Yehova, raba, kuko ngeze habi. Amara yanje ariko aradobangana.

Umutima wanje wacurangukiye muri jewe, kuko nagararije koko.

Hanze inkota yarahekuye. Mu nzu na ho nyene ni urupfu.

ש [Shini]

21 Abantu bumvise ingene jewe umugore niha. Nta wumpoza ariho.

Abansi banje bose barumvise ivyago vyanje. Bariginye, kuko ari wewe wabigize.

Emwe, uzozana umusi watangaje, kugira bamere nkanje.

ת [Tau]

22 Ububi bwabo bwose nibuze imbere yawe, ubamerere nabi,

Nk’uko wamereye nabi kubera ibigabitanyo vyanje vyose.

Kuko iminiho yanje ari myinshi, n’umutima wanje ukaba urwaye.

^ Int. 1:9 Ijambo ku rindi ni “ibice vyo hepfo vy’impuzu.”
