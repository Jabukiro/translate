Ezekiyeli  17 : 1 - 24

17 Ijambo rya Yehova ryongera kunzako, riti: 2 “Mwana w’umuntu, pfinda agapfindo, utunganye imvugo y’akagani ku nzu ya Isirayeli. 3 Uze uvuge uti: ‘Ibi ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze: “Ya nkona nini, imwe ifise amababa manini, ikagira n’amubi maremare, ikaba yuzuyeko amoya, imwe yari ifise amabara y’indobeke, yaraje i Libani itora ubushorishori bw’isederi. 4 Yāha isonga ry’imivyaro yaryo, iza irizanye mu gihugu ca Kanani; irishira mu gisagara c’abacuruza. 5 Vyongeye, itora ku mbuto y’ico gihugu, iyishira mu murima wo kubibamwo imbuto. Yayishizeho nk’igiti c’umusafusafu iruhande y’amazi menshi, nk’igiti c’umusafusafu. 6 Nuko itangura kunaga, buhorobuhoro icika umuzabibu utotahaye, mugufi, umeze nk’uwugondeye amababi imbere muri wo; nayo imizi yawo, yahavuye iba munsi yawo. Amaherezo ucika umuzabibu, uzana imivyaro, ushamika n’amashami.

7 “‘“Hanyuma haba iyindi nkona nini, ifise amababa manini, ikagira n’amubi manini, maze uwo muzabibu nyene ushora imizi yawo n’umunoho n’akantu uyerekeza kuri yo. Urungika amababi yawo kuri yo kugira ngo iwunyoshe, kure y’amatuta wari watewemwo. 8 Wari wamaze kubadikwa mu murima mwiza, iruhande y’amazi menshi, kugira ngo ushamike amashami wongere wame ivyamwa, ngo ucike umuzabibu w’agakomeye.”’

9 “Vuga uti: ‘Ibi ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze: “Mbega uwo muzabibu uzororanirwa? Mbega ntihazogira uwuwumomora imizi, agatuma ivyamwa vyawo bigira ibikaragashi? Mbega iminago yawo yose ikimara kwahwa ntizokwuma? Uwo muzabibu uzokwuma. Ntihazorinda gukoreshwa ukuboko gukomeye canke igisata c’abantu isinzi ngo urandurwe guhera mu mizi. 10 Kandi raba: naho wabaditswe, mbega uzororanirwa? Mbega ntuzokwuma rwose, nk’igihe uciweko n’umuyaga wo mu buseruko? Uzokwumira mu matuta y’umunago wawo.”’”

11 Ijambo rya Yehova ryongera kunzako, riti: 12 “Ndakwinginze ubwire ya nzu y’ingarariji uti: ‘Ariko vy’ukuri ntimuzi ico ivyo bisobanura?’ Vuga uti: ‘Raba! Umwami wa Babiloni yaraje i Yeruzalemu, atora umwami waho n’abaganwa baho, abazana iwe i Babiloni. 13 Vyongeye, atora umwe mu bo mu nda y’ingoma, agirana isezerano na we, amurahiza indahiro; atwara n’abantu bakurubakuru bo mu gihugu, 14 kugira ngo ubwami buce bugufi, ntibushobore kwiduza, bushobore guhagarara biciye ku kugumya isezerano ryiwe. 15 Mugabo amaherezo aramugarariza mu gutuma intumwa ziwe mu Misiri, kugira ngo imuhe amafarasi be n’igisata c’abantu isinzi. Mbega azororanirwa? Mbega azocika kw’icumu, uwukora ivyo kandi akaba yarenze isezerano? Mbega vy’ukuri azocika kw’icumu?’

16 “‘“Ndahiye ukubaho kwanje,” ni ko Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze, “ahantu nyene h’umwami yimitse uwasuzuguye indahiro yiwe kandi akarenga isezerano ryiwe, ni ho azopfira, iruhande yiwe, hagati muri Babiloni. 17 Kandi Farawo ntazotuma aroranirwa mu ntambara biciye ku nteko za gisirikare nyinshi no kw’ishengero ry’abantu isinzi, mu kuduza igikuta c’ugusugereza no mu kwubaka uruhome rw’ugusugereza, kugira ngo arandure benshi. 18 Yasuzuguye indahiro mu kurenga isezerano, kandi raba: yari yatanze ukuboko, none yakoze ivyo bintu vyose. Ntazogenda acitse kw’icumu.”’

19 “‘Ibi rero ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze: “Ndahiye ukubaho kwanje, ni ukuri, indahiro yanje yasuzuguye be n’isezerano ryanje yarenze, emwe, nzobizana ku mutwe wiwe. 20 Nzosanza urusenga rwanje hejuru yiwe, kandi ntazobura gufatwa mu rusenga rwanje rw’uruhigi; kandi nzomuzana i Babiloni, mburanireyo na we ku bijanye n’ubuhemu yangiriye. 21 Nayo impunzi ziwe zose zo mu nteko ziwe zose, zizogwishwa n’inkota, abasigaye na bo bashwiragizwe mu miyaga yose. Kandi muzobwirizwa kumenya yuko jewe Yehova nabivuze.”’

22 “‘Ibi ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze: “Nanje nzotora ku bushorishori buri hejuru bw’isederi ndabushire ahantu; kw’isonga ry’udushami twaryo nzokwahako akoroshe, ndakabadike ku musozi muremure kandi ukirurutse. 23 Nzokabadika ku musozi w’ahakirurutse wa Isirayeli, kandi ntikazobura gushamika amashami, kame ivyamwa, gacike isederi ry’agakomeye. Kandi munsi yaryo ni ho vy’ukuri inyoni zose z’amababa y’ubwoko bwose zizokwibera; zizokwibera mu gitutu c’amababi yaryo. 24 Kandi ibiti vyose vyo mu gahinga bizobwirizwa kumenya yuko jewe Yehova nashize hasi igiti kirekire, ngashira hejuru igiti kigufi, nkumisha igiti kikiri kibisi, ngatuma igiti cumye gishurika. Jewe Yehova narabivuze kandi ndabikora.”’”
