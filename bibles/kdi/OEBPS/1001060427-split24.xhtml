Ezekiyeli  24 : 1 - 27

24 Ijambo rya Yehova ryongera kunzako mu mwaka ugira icenda, mu kwezi kugira cumi, ku musi ugira cumi w’ukwo kwezi, riti: 2 “Mwana w’umuntu, niwiyandikire izina ry’uyu musi, uyu musi nyene. Umwami wa Babiloni yitaye kuri Yeruzalemu uyu musi nyene. 3 Tunganya imvugo y’akagani ku vyerekeye ya nzu y’ingarariji, uvuge ku biberekeye uti:

“‘Ibi ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze: “Shirako ya nkono y’umunwa munini; yishireko, uyisukemwo n’amazi. 4 Yegeranirizemwo ibipande, igipande ciza cose, itako n’urutugu; yuzuze mbere n’amagufa mezameza. 5 Nihatorwe intama nziza kuruta izindi, kandi munsi ya nya nkono urundeyo ingegene mu muzingi. Togotesha ibipande vyayo, uteke n’amagufa yayo hagati muri yo.”’”

6 “Ibi rero ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze: ‘Kiragowe ca gisagara c’ibikorwa vy’ugusesa amaraso, ya nkono y’umunwa munini, ingese zayo zikaba ziri muri yo, zikaba zitayivuyemwo! Kurayo ibipande biri muri yo, kimwe ku kindi; ntihagire ubupfindo ipfindirwako. 7 Kuko amaraso yaco ari neza na neza hagati muri co. Cayashize hejuru ku rutare hakayangana kandi hagaragara. Nticayasutse hasi, ngo apfukwe n’umukungugu. 8 Kugira ngo mvyure ubushangashirwe ku bwo guhōra, nashize amaraso yaco hejuru ku rutare hakayangana kandi hagaragara, ngo ntapfukwe.’

9 “Ibi rero ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze: ‘Kiragowe ca gisagara c’ibikorwa vy’ugusesa amaraso! Nanje rero ikirundo c’inkwi ngira ndakigire kinini. 10 Gwiza ingegene. Cana umuriro. Togotesha inyama zishe zinoge. Suka umufa wose, amagufa na yo ashuhe cane. 11 Yitereke ku makara yayo igaragara kugira ngo ishuhe; umujumpu wayo utegerezwa kurungura, kandi ubuhumane bwayo butegerezwa kuyagira hagati muri yo. Ingese zayo nizitokombere. 12 Erega ni ukuruhira ubusa! Yarateye uburuhe, mugabo ingese zayo nyinshi ntiziyivamwo. Yiterere mu muriro n’ingese zayo!’

13 “‘Mu buhamane bwawe habayemwo ubushirasoni. Ni co gituma nabwirijwe kuguhumanura, mugabo ntiwahumanutse ubuhumane bwawe. Ntuzokwongera kuba uwuhumanutse gushika ntururuje ubushangashirwe bwanje ku bikwerekeye. 14 Jewe Yehova ndavuze. Ivyo bizoba ata kabuza, kandi nzogira ico nkoze. Sinzobifata minenegwe, eka sinzokwumva akagongwe canke ngo nicuze. Bazogucira koko urubanza nk’uko inzira zawe ziri, nk’uko kandi ivyo ukora biri,’ ni ko Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze.”

15 Ijambo rya Yehova ryongera kunzako, riti: 16 “Mwana w’umuntu, ehe ngira nkwake ca kintu gihimbaye mu maso yawe biciye ku cago; ntiwikubite ku gikiriza canke ngo urire canke ngo amosozi yawe adengere. 17 Nunihe udakurako umunwa. Ntugandarire abapfuye. Niwizingireko icambarwa cawe co mu mutwe, wambare inkweto* zawe ku birenge. Ntupfuke ubwanwa bwo haruguru, kandi nturye umukate w’abantu.”

18 Maze mu gitondo mbwira abantu, amaherezo ku mugoroba umugore wanje arapfa. Nuko mu gitondo nkora nk’uko nari nategetswe. 19 Hanyuma abantu baguma bambaza bati: “Mbega ivyo bintu uriko urakora ntutubwira ingene bitwerekeye?” 20 Nanje ndababwira nti: “Ijambo rya Yehova ryanjeko, riti: 21 ‘Bwira inzu ya Isirayeli uti: “Ibi ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze: ‘Ehe ngira mpumanye aheranda hanje, ubwishime bw’inkomezi zanyu, ca kintu gihimbara amaso yanyu kikaba n’ico umutima wanyu ugirira impuhwe; kandi abahungu banyu n’abakobwa banyu mwasize inyuma, bazogwishwa n’inkota. 22 Namwe muzobwirizwa kugira nk’uko nagize. Ntimuzopfuka ubwanwa bwo haruguru, kandi ntimuzorya umukate w’abantu. 23 Ibitambara vyanyu vyo mu mutwe bizoba ku mitwe yanyu, inkweto zanyu na zo zibe ku birenge vyanyu. Ntimuzokwikubitagura canke ngo murire; muzobwirizwa kuborera mu makosa yanyu, kandi ntimuzobura kuniha, umwe wese anihira uwundi. 24 Kandi Ezekiyeli yababereye imburizi. Muzokora ibihwanye n’ivyo yakoze vyose. Nivyashika, muzobwirizwa rero kumenya yuko jewe ndi Umukama Segaba Yehova.’”’”

25 “Nayo wewe ga mwana w’umuntu, mbega ku musi nzobaka iboma ryabo, ca kintu ciza bigina, ca kintu gihimbara amaso yabo kikaba n’ico umutima wabo wipfuza, abahungu babo n’abakobwa babo, mbega 26 kuri uwo musi si ho uwucitse kw’icumu azoza kuri wewe kugira ngo atume amatwi yumva? 27 Kuri uwo musi akanwa kawe kazoteranurirwa uwo yacitse kw’icumu, uvuge, ntusubire kuba ikiragi; uzobabera koko imburizi, kandi bazobwirizwa kumenya yuko jewe ndi Yehova.”

^ Ezk. 24:17 Canke, “amasandali.”
