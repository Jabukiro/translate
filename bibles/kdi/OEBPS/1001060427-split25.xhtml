Ezekiyeli  25 : 1 - 17

25 Ijambo rya Yehova ryongera kunzako, riti: 2 “Mwana w’umuntu, erekeza mu maso hawe kuri bene Amoni, uvuge ubuhanuzi bubagiriza. 3 Kandi uvuge ku bijanye na bene Amoni uti: ‘Nimwumve ijambo ry’Umukama Segaba Yehova. Ibi ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze: “Ko wavuze ngo Ahaa! ku bijanye n’aheranda hanje kuko hahumanijwe, no ku bijanye n’ubutaka bwa Isirayeli kuko bwasizwe ari umusaka, no ku bijanye n’inzu ya Yuda kuko bagiye mu bunyagano, 4 ngira rero ndagutange ku Banyabuseruko ube ikintu co kwigabira, kandi bazoshinga muri wewe amakambi yabo azitijwe impome, bashire koko muri wewe amataberenakulo yabo. Ni bo bazorya ivyamwa vyawe, kandi ni bo bazonywa amata yawe. 5 Kandi i Raba nzohagira itongo ry’uburagiriro ry’ingamiya, bene Amoni na bo ndabagire aho umukuku uruhukira; kandi muzobwirizwa kumenya yuko jewe ndi Yehova.”’”

6 “Kuko ibi ari vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze: ‘Ko wakubise amashi ku yandi, ukadida ibirenge, ukaguma unezerwa n’akagaye kose mu mutima wawe, uhema ubutaka bwa Isirayeli, 7 ehe rero ndaje; nakuramvuriyeko ukuboko kwanje, kandi nzogutanga ku mahanga ube ikintu co gusahura; nzokurandura mu bindi bisata vy’abantu, ndaguhonye mu bindi bihugu. Nzokuzimanganya, kandi uzobwirizwa kumenya yuko jewe ndi Yehova.’

8 “Ibi ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze: ‘Ko Mowabu na Seyiri bavuze ngo: “Raba! Inzu ya Yuda ni nk’ayandi mahanga yose,” 9 ngira rero nugurure umumanuko wa Mowabu ku bisagara, ku bisagara vyaho gushika ku mupaka waho, ikirezi c’ico gihugu, Beti-yeshimoti, Bayali-meyoni, gushika n’i Kiriyatayimu, 10 ndahugururire Abanyabuseruko, hampande ya bene Amoni, kandi nzohagira ikintu co kwigabira, kugira ngo ntihaze hibukwe, ni ukuvuga bene Amoni, mu mahanga. 11 Nzoshitsa imanza i Mowabu; kandi bazobwirizwa kumenya yuko jewe ndi Yehova.’

12 “Ibi ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze: ‘Ko Edomu yahagurutse ikihora inzu ya Yuda, bakaguma bimonogoza mu gukora ikibi kandi bakaba barabihoye, 13 ibi rero ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze: “Nanje nzoramvurira ukuboko kwanje kuri Edomu, ndaharandure icitwa umuntu n’igitungwa, ndahagire ikibanza catikijwe kuva i Temani gushika n’i Dedani. Bazogwishwa n’inkota. 14 ‘Nzokwihora Edomu biciye ku kuboko kw’igisata canje Isirayeli; bazokorera muri Edomu ibihuye n’ishavu ryanje be n’ubushangashirwe bwanje; kandi bazobwirizwa kumenya ukwihora kwanje ico ari co,’ ni ko Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze.”’

15 “Ibi ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze: ‘Ko Abafilisitiya bakoze ivyo kwihora, bakaguma bihorana ukwihora kw’akagaye ko mu mutima, kugira ngo batere agatikizo, bivuye ku nyankane y’igihe kitagira urugero, 16 ibi rero ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze: “Ehe ngira ndamvurire ukuboko kwanje ku Bafilisitiya, kandi nzorandura Abakereti, mponye igice gisigaye c’inkengera y’ikiyaga. 17 Nzoshitsa muri bo ibikorwa bikomeye vy’ukwihora, n’ibikangiro vyuzuye ubushangashirwe; kandi bazobwirizwa kumenya yuko jewe ndi Yehova ninabihōra.”’”
