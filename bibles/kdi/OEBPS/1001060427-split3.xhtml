Ezekiyeli  3 : 1 - 27

3 Araheza ambwira ati: “Mwana w’umuntu, ico uronka, ukirye. Rya uwo muzingo, hanyuma ugende, ubwire inzu ya Isirayeli.”

2 Nuko nasamika akanwa kanje, na we andisha uwo muzingo. 3 Hanyuma ambwira ati: “Mwana w’umuntu, gaburira inda yawe, kugira ngo wuzuze amara yawe uwo muzingo nguhaye.” Nanje ntangura kuwurya, maze mu kanwa kanje usosa nk’ubuki.

4 Abandanya kumbwira ati: “Mwana w’umuntu, genda, winjire hagati mu nzu ya Isirayeli, ubabwire amajambo yanje. 5 Kuko utarungitswe ku gisata c’abantu b’imvugo itumvikana canke b’ururimi ruremereye: urungitswe ku nzu ya Isirayeli, 6 si ku bisata vyinshi vy’abantu b’imvugo itumvikana canke b’ururimi ruremereye, abo udashobora kwumva amajambo yabo ngo utegere. Iyaba ari bo nari ngutumyeko, abo bokwumvirije ivyo uvuga. 7 Nayo ab’inzu ya Isirayeli, ntibazoshaka kukwumviriza, kuko badashaka kunyumviriza; kuko ab’inzu ya Isirayeli bose ari ab’umutwe ukomantaye n’umutima ukomantaye. 8 Raba! Mu maso hawe ndahakomantaje neza na neza nko mu maso habo, n’uruhanga rwawe ndarukomantaje neza na neza nk’uruhanga rwabo. 9 Uruhanga rwawe ndarugize nk’idiyama, ndarukomantaje kurusha ibuye ry’isare. Ntukabatinye, kandi mu maso habo ntihakaguce ivutu, kuko ari inzu y’ingarariji.”

10 Hanyuma ambwira ati: “Mwana w’umuntu, amajambo yanje yose ngira nkubwire, yashire mu mutima wawe, wumvishe amatwi yawe. 11 Kandi genda, winjire hagati mu bajanywe mu bunyagano, hagati mu bana b’igisata cawe, ubabwire uti: ‘Ibi ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze,’ bumva canke batumva.”

12 Maze impwemu iranterura, nca ntangura kwumva inyuma yanje urwamo rw’ivumbuka rikomeye, numva ngo: “Hahezagirwe ubuninahazwa bwa Yehova aho ari.” 13 Kandi hari urwamo rw’amababa ya vya binyabuzima yakoranako hafi na hafi, n’urwamo rwa ya makurudumu iruhande cane yavyo, be n’urwamo rw’ivumbuka rikomeye. 14 Ya mpwemu iranterura, irantwara, ku buryo ngenda mbabaye, nshangashiwe mu mutima, kandi ukuboko kwa Yehova kwari kuri jewe kwari gukomeye. 15 Nuko ninjira hagati mu bajanywe mu bunyagano i Teli-abibu, ababa hafi y’uruzi Kebari, ntangura kuba aho bari babaye; mba ng’aho imisi indwi, njumarariwe hagati muri bo.

16 Maze iyo misi indwi iheze, ijambo rya Yehova rinzako, riti:

17 “Mwana w’umuntu, nakugize inderetsi y’inzu ya Isirayeli; uzokwumva ijambo riva mu kanwa kanje, nawe ubaburire ibivuye kuri jewe. 18 Ninabwira umubisha nti: ‘Ntuzobura gupfa,’ nawe ukareka kumuburira vy’ukuri no kuvuga kugira ngo umuburire ave mu nzira yiwe y’ububisha, abone kuzigamirwa ubuzima, uwo mubisha azopfira mw’ikosa ryiwe, mugabo amaraso yiwe nzoyishuza mu kuboko kwawe. 19 Mugabo wewe, niwaburira umubisha, na we ntahindukire vy’ukuri ngo ave mu bubisha bwiwe no mu nzira yiwe y’ububisha, we azopfa azize ikosa ryiwe; mugabo wewe, uzoba urokoye ubuzima bwawe. 20 Kandi umugororotsi niyahindukira akava mu bugororotsi bwiwe, agakora koko ibigoramye, nanje nkabwirizwa gushira igitsitaza imbere yiwe, we azopfa kubera yuko utamuburiye. Azopfa azize igicumuro ciwe, kandi ibikorwa vyiwe vy’ubugororotsi yakoze ntibizokwibukwa, mugabo amaraso yiwe nzoyishuza mu kuboko kwawe. 21 Nayo wewe, nimba uburiye umuntu w’umugororotsi ngo ntacumure, kandi vy’ukuri akirinda gucumura, ntazobura kubandanya kubaho kuko yari yaburiwe, kandi wewe uzoba urokoye ubuzima bwawe.”

22 Maze ukuboko kwa Yehova kuza kuri jewe ng’aho, araheza ambwira ati: “Haguruka, uje mu kiyaya c’umubande, ni ho ndi buvuganire nawe.” 23 Nuko ndahaguruka, nja mu kiyaya c’umubande, maze ngize ntya, mbona ubuninahazwa bwa Yehova buhagaze ng’aho, nka bwa buninahazwa nari nabonye hafi ya rwa ruzi Kebari, nca ngwa nubamye. 24 Maze impwemu iranyinjiramwo, impagarika ku birenge vyanje, hanyuma atangura kuvugana nanje, ati:

“Ingo, wugaranirwe mu nzu yawe. 25 Kandi wewe ga mwana w’umuntu, ehe bazogushirako imigozi koko, bayikuboheshe, ku buryo udashobora gusohoka ngo uje hagati muri bo. 26 Ururimi rwawe nzorumatanya n’amagage y’akanwa kawe, kandi ntuzobura gucika ikiragi, kandi ntuzobabera uwutanga igikangiro, kuko ari inzu y’ingarariji. 27 Ariko ninavugana nawe, nzoteranura akanwa kawe, ubabwire uti: ‘Ibi ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze.’ Uwumva niyumve, uwanka na we yanke, kuko abo ari inzu y’ingarariji.
