Ezekiyeli  7 : 1 - 27

7 Maze ijambo rya Yehova ryongera kunzako, riti: 2 “Nayo wewe ga mwana w’umuntu, ibi ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova abwiye ubutaka bwa Isirayeli: ‘Iherezo, rya herezo, ryaje ku mpera zine z’igihugu. 3 Ubu rero iherezo riragushitseko, ngira nkurungikeko ishavu ryanje, kandi nzogucira urubanza nk’uko inzira zawe ziri, nkuzaneko ibintu vyawe vyose biteye ishishi. 4 Ijisho ryanje ntirizokwumvira akagongwe, eka sinzokugirira impuhwe, kuko nzokuzanako inzira zawe, kandi ibintu vyawe biteye ishishi bizoba hagati muri wewe; kandi muzobwirizwa kumenya yuko jewe ndi Yehova.’

5 “Ibi ni vyo Umukama Segaba Yehova avuze: ‘Icago, icago kidasanzwe, ehe kiriko kiraza. 6 Iherezo ribwirizwa kuza. Iherezo ribwirizwa kuza; emwe, rizokwikangura rikuzeko. Ehe riraje! 7 Urugori rw’amashurwe ruzokuzako, ewe wa muntu uba mu gihugu, igihe kiraje, umusi uri hafi. Hari umuvurungano, si akaruru k’umunezero k’imisozi.

8 “‘Ubu vuba ngiye kugusukako ubushangashirwe bwanje, ndangize ishavu ryanje kuri wewe, kandi nzogucira urubanza nk’uko inzira zawe ziri, nkuzaneko ibintu vyawe vyose biteye ishishi. 9 Ijisho ryanje ntirizokwumva akagongwe, eka sinzogira impuhwe. Nzobikuzanako nk’uko inzira zawe ziri, kandi ibintu vyawe biteye ishishi bizoba neza na neza hagati muri wewe; kandi muzobwirizwa kumenya yuko jewe ndi Yehova, uwuriko abakubita.

10 “‘Ehe wa musi! Ehe uriko uraza! Urugori rw’amashurwe rwasohotse. Inkoni yashuritse. Ukwita imbere kwanaze. 11 Ubukazi bwahagurutse bucika inkoni y’ububisha. Ntibiva kuri bo canke ngo bive ku butunzi bwabo; ntibiva kuri bo ubwabo, eka nta bukuru na bumwe buri muri bo. 12 Igihe kiraje, wa musi uzoshika ata kabuza. Umuguzi ntanezerwe; uwugurisha na we ntaje mu kigandaro, kuko hari ishavu rirurumba rirurumbira isinzi ryaho ryose. 13 Kuko uwugurisha atazogaruka ku vyagurishijwe, ubuzima bwabo bukiri hagati mu bazima; kuko iyerekwa ari iry’isinzi ryaho ryose. Nta n’umwe azogaruka, kandi umwe wese muri bo ntazogaba ubuzima bwiwe biciye kw’ikosa ryiwe.

14 “‘Urumbete rwavugijwe, bose barateguzwa, mugabo nta n’umwe aja ku rugamba, kuko ishavu ryanje rirurumbira isinzi ryaho ryose. 15 Inkota iri hanze, ikiza n’ikigoyi na vyo bikaba imbere. Uwo wese ari mu gahinga, azokwicwa n’inkota; abo bose bari mu gisagara, ikigoyi n’ikiza bizobarotsa. 16 Abacitse kw’icumu babo ntibazobura guhunga, ku misozi bacike nk’inuma zo mu mibande zinihagira zose, umwe wese mw’ikosa ryiwe. 17 Amaboko yose aguma aregera*; nayo amavi yose, aguma atonyanga amazi. 18 Bakenyeye amagunira*, batekerwa n’uguhinda agashitsi; mu maso ha bose hari ibimaramare, ku mitwe yabo yose hakaba ubuhanza.

19 “‘Bazoshiburira ifeza zabo mu mabarabara, inzahabu zabo na zo zicike ikintu co kuzinukwa. Zaba ifeza zabo canke inzahabu zabo, ntibizoshobora kubarokora ku musi w’uburake bwa Yehova. Ntibizohaza imitima yabo, kandi amara yabo ntibizoyuzuza, kuko vyabaye igitsitaza candurutsa ikosa ryabo. 20 Ubwiza bw’udusharizo tw’umuntu—umuntu yatugize imvo y’ubwishime; kandi ibishusho vyabo biteye ishishi, ibintu vyabo bisesemye, babikoze muri vyo. Ni co gituma kuri bo nzobigira ikintu co kuzinukwa. 21 Kandi nzobitanga mu kuboko kw’ab’ahandi ngo babinyage, no ku babisha bo kw’isi ngo babisahure, kandi ntibazobura kubihumanya.

22 “‘Nzobwirizwa guhindukiza mu maso hanje ndahabakureko; bazohumanya vy’ukuri ahantu hanje hihishije, kandi abambuzi bazohinjira koko, bahahumanye.

23 “‘Kora umunyororo, kuko igihugu gisigaye cuzuye imanza zandujwe n’amaraso, igisagara na co kikaba gisigaye cuzuye ubukazi. 24 Nzozana abarusha abandi bose kuba babi mu mahanga, na bo ntibazobura kwigarurira amazu yabo; nzozimanganya ubwishime bw’abakomeye, kandi ibibanza vyabo vyeranda bizohumanywa ata kabuza. 25 Hazoza umubabaro mwinshi, kandi ntibazobura kurondera amahoro, mugabo ntazoboneka. 26 Amarushwa azoza akurikira ayandi, n’inkuru ize ikurikira iyindi; abantu bazoronderera vy’ukuri iyerekwa ku muhanuzi, kandi itegeko ubwaryo rizozimangana ku muherezi, n’impanuro ku bahumure. 27 Umwami ubwiwe azoja mu kigandaro; mbere n’umukuru azokwambara umusaka, kandi amaboko y’abanyagihugu azohungabana. Nzobagirira ibihuye n’inzira yabo, ndabacire urubanza ruhwanye n’imanza bacirwa; kandi bazobwirizwa kumenya yuko jewe ndi Yehova.’”

^ Ezk. 7:17 Ijambo ku rindi ni “aguma akoroka.”

^ Ezk. 7:18 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
