Daniyeli  10 : 1 - 21

10 Mu mwaka ugira gatatu wa Kuro umwami w’Ubuperesi, hari ikintu cahishuriwe Daniyeli, ari we yitwa Beluteshazari; ico kintu cari ic’ukuri, kandi haba igikorwa gikomeye ca gisirikare. Ico kintu aragitahura, aratahura ico abonye.

2 Muri iyo misi, jewe Daniyeli nari ngandaye mu kiringo c’amayinga atatu yuzuye. 3 Sinarya umukate uryoshe, kandi nta nyama canke umukate vyangera mu kanwa, kandi sinigeze nisiga amavuta gushika ayo mayinga atatu yuzuye arangiye. 4 Maze ku musi ugira mirongo ibiri na kane w’ukwezi kwa mbere, igihe nari ku nkombe ya rwa ruzi runini, ari rwo Hidekeli, 5 ndaramura amaso ndaraba, maze mbona umugabo umwe yambaye impuzu y’ilino, akenyeye mu mafyinga inzahabu y’i Ufazi. 6 Umubiri wiwe wari umeze nk’ibuye rya kirisolito, mu maso hiwe na ho nk’ishusho y’umuravyo, amaso yiwe na yo nk’ibimuri vy’umuriro; amaboko yiwe be n’ibirenge vyiwe vyaboneka nk’umujumpu usenye, ijwi ry’amajambo yiwe na ryo rikamera nk’ijwi ry’isinzi. 7 Kandi jewe Daniyeli nabonye ukwo kubonekerwa ndi jenyene; mugabo abantu twari kumwe bobo ntibabonye ukwo kubonekerwa. Ariko barafashwe n’ukujugumira gukomeye, bituma biruka, barinyegeza.

8 Nuko nsigara ndi jenyene, ndaheza mbona ukwo kubonekerwa gukomeye. Ntihagira inkomezi na nkeya zinsigaramwo, iteka ryanje rirahinduka, rirakamangana, sinasigarana inkomezi na nkeya. 9 Nuko ntangura kwumva ijwi ry’amajambo yiwe; maze nkiriko ndumva ijwi ry’amajambo yiwe, nca ndasinzira ubutikoma nubamye, mu maso hanje haraba hasi. 10 Maze ehe haza ukuboko kurankorako, kurahava kuramvyura kugira ngo mpfukame, nishimikize ibiganza vy’amaboko yanje. 11 Araheza ambwira ati:

“Ewe Daniyeli, muntu uhimbaye cane, nutegere amajambo ngira nkubwire, uhagarare aho wari uhagaze, kuko ubu ndungitswe kuri wewe.”

Ambwiye iryo jambo, ndahaguruka nsusumira.

12 Hanyuma ambwira ati: “Ntutinye, Daniyeli, kuko uhereye ku musi wa mbere washira umutima ku bwenge no ku kwicisha bugufi imbere y’Imana yawe, amajambo yawe yarumviswe, kandi nje kubera amajambo yawe. 13 Mugabo umuganwa w’akarere kaganzwa n’Ubuperesi yari ahagaze andwanya, imisi mirongo ibiri n’umwe, maze ehe Mikaheli, umwe mu baganwa bakurubakuru, aza kuntabara; nanje nsigara ng’aho iruhande y’abami b’Ubuperesi. 14 Kandi nzanywe no kugutahuza ibizoshikira igisata cawe mu kiringo ca nyuma c’imisi, kuko ari iyerekwa rikiri iry’imisi izoza.”

15 Igihe rero yambwira amajambo nk’ayo, mu maso hanje nari naharabishije hasi, kandi nari nacitse nk’ikiragi. 16 Maze, ehe uwufise ishusho nk’iy’abana b’abantu ankora ku minwa, nca ntangura guteranura akanwa ndavuga, mbwira uwari ahagaze imbere yanje nti: “Ewe mukama wanje, ukubonekerwa kwatumye izo mu nda zimpiramwo, sinasigarana inkomezi na nkeya. 17 Mbega none vyashobotse gute ngo umusavyi w’uyu mukama wanje avugane n’uyu mukama wanje? Nayo jewe, gushika ubu nta nkomezi na nkeya ziri muri jewe, eka nta n’impemu na nkeya zinsigayemwo.”

18 Maze umwe yasa n’umuntu yakuwe mw’ivu yongera kunkorako, arankomeza. 19 Araheza avuga ati: “Ntutinye, ewe muntu ahimbaye cane! Gira amahoro. Nukomere, emwe, ukomere.” Akivugana nanje, ndinanata, ndaheza mvuga nti: “Umukama wanje navuge, kuko unkomeje.” 20 Nuko, avuga kandi ati:

“Ubwo vy’ukuri urazi igituma naje kuri wewe? Ubu ngira nsubire kurwana na wa muganwa w’Ubuperesi. Ninasohoka, ehe umuganwa w’Ubugiriki na we araza. 21 Ariko ndakubwira ivyanditswe mu candikano c’ukuri, kandi nta n’umwe ashikamanye nanje muri ivyo atari Mikaheli, wa muganwa wanyu.
