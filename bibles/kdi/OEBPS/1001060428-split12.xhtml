Daniyeli  12 : 1 - 13

12 “Muri ico gihe Mikaheli azohaguruka, wa muganwa ahambaye ahagaze arwanira abana b’igisata cawe. Kandi hazoba koko igihe c’amarushwa atari bwigere abaho kuva habaho ihanga gushika ico gihe. Kandi muri ico gihe abantu bawe bazorokoka, uwo wese azosangwa yanditswe muri ca gitabu. 2 Kandi benshi mu basinziriye mu butaka bazokwikangura, bamwe bikangurire ubuzima bw’igihe kitagira urugero, abandi na bo bikangurire umugayo n’ukuzinukwa kw’igihe kitagira urugero.

3 “Kandi abafise ugutahura kwimbitse bazokayangana nk’ukwakaka kw’ikirere, abazana benshi ku bugororotsi na bo bakayangane nk’inyenyeri gushika igihe kitagira urugero, emwe, ibihe bidahera.

4 “Nayo wewe ga Daniyeli, ayo majambo nuyagire akabanga, ico gitabu ukibike nk’ikidomweko ikidodo, gushika mu gihe c’iherezo. Benshi bazonyuragira irya n’ino, kandi ubumenyi nyakuri buzogwira.”

5 Nuko jewe Daniyeli nditegereza, maze ehe abandi babiri bari bahagaze, umwe ku nkengera y’umugezi hakuno, uwundi na we ku nkengera y’umugezi hakurya. 6 Maze umwe muri bo abwira wa muntu yambaye impuzu y’ilino, wa wundi yari hejuru y’amazi y’umugezi, ati: “Hazoca igihe kingana gute gushika kw’iherezo ry’ivyo bintu vy’agatangaza?” 7 Nuko ntangura kwumva wa muntu yambaye impuzu y’ilino, wa wundi yari hejuru y’amazi y’umugezi, igihe yari adugije ukuryo kwiwe n’ukubamfu kwiwe yerekeza mu majuru akarahira Umwe abaho igihe kitagira urugero, ngo: “Ni ivy’igihe cashinzwe, ibihe vyashinzwe, n’igice. Kandi ukujanjagura inkomezi z’aberanda nikwamara kurangira, ivyo vyose bizoshika kw’iherezo ryavyo.”

8 Nayo jewe, ndumva, ariko sinategera, bituma mvuga nti: “Ewe mukama wanje, mbega ikiringo ca nyuma c’ivyo bintu kizoba ikihe?”

9 Yongera kuvuga ati: “Genda Daniyeli, kuko aya majambo yagizwe akabanga kandi akabikwa nk’ayadomweko ikidodo gushika mu gihe c’iherezo. 10 Benshi bazokwihumanura, biyeze, batyororwe. Ababisha bazokorana ububisha koko, kandi nta mubisha n’umwe azotegera; ariko abafise ugutahura kwimbitse bazotegera.

11 “Kandi kuva igihe ca kimazi c’ubudasiba kizoba cakuweho hagashirwaho ca kintu gisesemye giteza ubugesera, hazoca imisi igihumbi n’amajana abiri na mirongo icenda.

12 “Arahiriwe uwuguma arindiriye kandi agashika ku misi igihumbi n’amajana atatu na mirongo itatu n’itanu!

13 “Nayo wewe, niwigire ugana kw’iherezo; uzoruhuka, mugabo uzohaguruka ngo utorane umugabane wawe mu mpera y’imisi.”
