Hoseya  12 : 1 - 14

12 “Efurayimu arya umuyaga kandi yoma inyuma umuyaga wo mu buseruko bukarinda bwira. Agwiza ukubesha n’ukunyaga. Bagirana isezerano na Ashuri, bakajana amavuta mu Misiri.

2 “Yehova arafitaniye imburano na Yuda, egome, kugira ngo agire ico abaza Yakobo nk’uko inzira ziwe ziri; azomwishura nk’uko ibikorwa vyiwe biri. 3 Yafashe mwene wabo igitsintsiri bari mu nda, kandi yanyinyanye n’Imana akoresheje inguvu ziwe zose. 4 Yagumye anyinyana n’umumarayika, amaherezo aranesha. Yararize, kugira ngo yisabire ubutoni.”

Imana yamusanze i Beteli, maze ivuganira na we ng’aho. 5 Yehova Imana nyen’ingabo, Yehova ni ryo zina yibukirwako.

6 “Nayo wewe, ubwirizwa kugaruka ku Mana yawe, ukagumya ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo be n’ubutungane; niwame witeze Imana yawe. 7 Nayo umucuruza, mu kuboko kwiwe hari umunzane w’ubuhendanyi; yakunze ukugunga. 8 Efurayimu aguma avuga ati: ‘Ni ukuri narabaye umutunzi; narironkeye ibintu vy’agaciro. Ku bijanye n’ubucumukure bwanje bwose, ntibazombonako ikosa ryokwitwa igicumuro.’

9 “Mugabo jewe ndi Yehova Imana yawe kuva igihe wava mu gihugu ca Misiri. Nzosubira gutuma uba mu mahema nko mu gihe c’imisi mikuru yashinzwe. 10 Navuganye n’abahanuzi, nagwije ukwerekwa, kandi biciye ku kuboko kw’abahanuzi nagumye ntanga ibigereranyo.

11 “I Gileyadi habonetse ivy’amageza be n’ivy’ububeshi. I Gilugali bashikanye amashūri. Vyongeye, ibicaniro vyabo bimeze nk’ibirundo vy’amabuye mu mirongo y’ubusizi yo mu mirima. 12 Nuko Yakobo ahungira mu karere ka Siriya, Isirayeli aguma akora ngo aronke umugore, aragira intama kugira ngo aronke umugore. 13 Yehova yaraduganye Isirayeli amukura mu Misiri biciye ku muhanuzi, kandi yarinzwe biciye ku muhanuzi. 14 Efurayimu yarababaje Imana bimwe bibi, kandi ibikorwa vyiwe vy’ugusesa amaraso abirekera kuri we; iceyi ciwe, Shebuja wiwe Ahambaye azokimwishura.”
