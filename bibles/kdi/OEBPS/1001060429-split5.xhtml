Hoseya  5 : 1 - 15

5 “Nimwumve ibi, yemwe baherezi, mutege yompi, ewe nzu ya Isirayeli, mutege ugutwi namwe nzu y’umwami, kuko urubanza ari mwebwe rwerekeye; kubera ko mwabereye umutego Mizipa, mukaba nk’urusenga rushanje hejuru ya Tabori. 2 Abata umurongo bivobetse mu gikorwa c’ukwica, kandi narabahimirije bose. 3 Jewe ubwanje naramenye Efurayimu, kandi Isirayeli ntiyampishijwe. Kuko ubu, ewe Efurayimu, wafashe abagore nk’aho ari abamaraya; Isirayeli yarihumanije. 4 Ibikorwa vyabo ntibibakundira kugaruka ku Mana yabo, kuko hagati muri bo hari impwemu y’ubusambanyi; kandi Yehova ubwiwe, ntibamwemeye icese. 5 Ubwishime bwa Isirayeli bwaramwagirije amaso mu yandi; Isirayeli na Efurayimu batsitara mw’ikosa ryabo. Yuda na we nyene yatsitaranye na bo. 6 Bagiye kurondera Yehova bajanye umukuku wabo n’ubusho bwabo, mugabo ntibamubonye. Yari yigiriye arabaheba. 7 Bahemukiye Yehova ubwiwe, kuko bavyaye abana b’ahandi. Ubu rero mu kwezi kumwe bazorotsanywa n’imigabane yabo.

8 “Nimuvugirize inzamba i Gibeya, n’urumbete i Rama! Nimuvugirize akaruru k’intambara i Beti-aveni—inyuma yawe, ewe Benyamini! 9 Ewe Efurayimu, uzoba akajoreza ku musi w’uguhambarirwa. Namenyesheje mu miryango ya Isirayeli amajambo yo kwizigirwa. 10 Abaganwa ba Yuda babaye nk’abantu bigiza inyuma urubibe. Nzobasukako uburake bwanje nk’amazi. 11 Efurayimu arahahazwa, akajanjagurwa mu butungane, kuko yiyemeje gukurikira umurwanizi wiwe. 12 Kandi nabereye Efurayimu nk’agakoko k’akanyundo, mbera inzu ya Yuda nk’ukubora.

13 “Efurayimu yarabonye indwara yiwe, Yuda na we abona igisebe ciwe. Nuko Efurayimu aja i Ashuri, atuma intumwa ku mwami ahambaye. Mugabo uno, mwebwe ntiyashoboye kubakiza, kandi ntiyashoboye kubavura igisebe akoresheje umuti. 14 Kuko nzomerera Efurayimu nk’intambwe y’umukangara, n’inzu ya Yuda nk’intambwe y’umukangara y’umugāra. Jewe nzobatabagura, ngende nsengēse, kandi nta wuzobarokora. 15 Nzogenda, nisubirire ahantu hanje gushika bishuye icaha cabo; kandi bazorondera koko mu maso hanje. Nibaba bageze habi, bazondondera.”
