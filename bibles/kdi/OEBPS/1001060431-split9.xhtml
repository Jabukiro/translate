Amosi  9 : 1 - 15

9 Nabonye Yehova ahagaze hejuru y’igicaniro, araheza avuga ati: “Kubita umutwe w’inkingi, aminjiriro atigite. Zigegene ku mitwe, zose. Abasigaye bo muri bo nzobicisha inkota. Nta wo muri bo agerageza guhunga azoshobora guhunga ngo akire, kandi nta wo muri bo agerageza gucika kw’icumu azoshobora kuyanyaga. 2 Nibimba bakaja mu kuzimu*, ukuboko kwanje kuzobakurayo; kandi nibaduga mu majuru, nzobamanurayo. 3 Nibinyegeza kw’isonga rya Karumeli, nzobaronderayo nitonze nshirwe ndabafashe. Nibihisha amaso yanje hasi mu kiyaga, nzotegekerayo inzoka, ibakomore. 4 Nibajanwa kugirwa imbohe bashorewe n’abansi babo, nzotegekerayo inkota, ibice; kandi nzobashinga ijisho ku bw’inabi, atari ku bw’ineza. 5 Umukama Segaba, Yehova nyen’ingabo*, ni We akora ku gihugu, kigashonga; kandi abakibamwo bose bazobwirizwa kugandara; kandi ntikizobura kuduga cose nka Nili, kikadwibira nka Nili yo mu Misiri.

6 “‘Umwe yubaka ingazi ziwe mu majuru, akubaka n’inyubakwa yiwe hejuru y’isi yashizeho, umwe ahamagara amazi y’ikiyaga, ngo ayasuke ku buringanire bw’isi—Yehova ni ryo zina ryiwe.’

7 “‘Mbega kuri jewe ntimumeze nk’abana b’Abakushi, ga yemwe bene Isirayeli?’ ni ko Yehova avuze. ‘Mbega sinaduganye Isirayeli nkayikura mu gihugu ca Misiri, n’Abafilisitiya nkabakura i Kirete, na Siriya nkayikura i Kiri?’

8 “‘Raba! Amaso y’Umukama Segaba Yehova ari ku bwami bucumura, kandi azobuzimanganya koko ku buringanire bw’ubutaka. Yamara rero, sinzozimanganya buhere inzu ya Yakobo,’ ni ko Yehova avuze. 9 ‘Kuko ehe ndatanze itegeko; nzonyiganza inzu ya Isirayeli mu mahanga yose, nk’uko umuntu anyiganza ikiyunguruzo, ku buryo hatagira n’akabuye kagwa hasi. 10 Bazokwicishwa inkota, abacumuzi bose bo mu bantu banje, abavuga ngo: “Ivyago ntibizotwegera canke ngo bishike aho turi.”’

11 “‘Kuri uwo musi nzovyura urusago rwa Dawidi rwatemvye, nzibire n’ibihengeri vyabo. Ibihomvagurike vyarwo nzobivyura, ndarwubake nko mu misi ya kera, 12 kugira ngo bigarurire ibisigaye vyo kuri Edomu, be n’amahanga yose izina ryanje ryambarijwemwo,’ ni ko Yehova avuze, ari na we akora ivyo.

13 “‘Ehe imisi iraje,’ ni ko Yehova avuze: ‘umurimyi azohamvya umwimbuzi, n’uwuganisha ibirenge inzabibu ahamvye uwikorera imbuto; imisozi izotonyanga umuvinyu usosa, n’imirambi yose isange iriko irashonga. 14 Kandi nzogarukana abagizwe imbohe bo mu bantu banje Isirayeli, na bo bazokwubaka vy’ukuri ibisagara vyagizwe umusaka babibemwo, batere imirima y’imizabibu binywere umuvinyu wayo, barime n’imirima birire ivyamwa vyayo.’

15 “‘Nzobateragira koko ku butaka bwabo, kandi ntibazokwongera kurandurwa ku butaka bwabo nabihereye,’ ni ko Yehova Imana yawe avuze.”

^ Am. 9:2 Mu giheburayo ni “Sheoli” (she’ohlʹ). Raba Inyongera ya 7.

^ Am. 9:5 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
