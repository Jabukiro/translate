Yona  2 : 1 - 10

2 Maze Yona asengera Yehova Imana yiwe imbere muri nya rufi, 2 ati:

“Natakambiye Yehova ndi mu marushwa yanje, na we aranyishura.

Natabaje ndi mu nda y’ukuzimu*.

Warumvise ijwi ryanje.

 3 Igihe wanshiburira ibwina, mu nda y’igezi ry’ikiyaga*,

Uruzi rwaciye runkikuza.

Imikuba isuriranya yawe n’imipfunda yawe yose yaciye hejuru yanje.

 4 Jeho navuze nti: ‘Narirukanywe mva mu maso yawe!

Nzokwongera nte kwitegereza urusengero rwawe rweranda?’

 5 Amazi yarankikuje ageramira ubuzima bwanje; inyenga y’amazi yagumye inzitiye.

Ivyatsi bibi vyari binyizingiriye ku mutwe.

 6 Naramanutse mu matanguriro y’imisozi.

Nayo isi, ibihindizo vyayo vyaranyugaranye igihe kitagira urugero*.

Mugabo waradugije ubuzima bwanje ubukura mu cobo, ewe Yehova Mana yanje!

 7 Igihe umutima wanje wadendebukira muri jewe, Yehova ni we nibutse.

Maze isengesho ryanje ryaragushikiriye, mu rusengero rwawe rweranda.

 8 Nayo abubaha ibigirwamana vy’ububeshi, baheba ubuntu bwuzuye urukundo bwabo.

 9 Ariko jeho, nzogushikanira ikimazi njanisha n’ijwi ry’ugukenguruka.

Ico naraganye, nzogishitsa. Ubukiriro ni ubwa Yehova.”

10 Amaherezo Yehova ategeka nya rufi, rudahwa Yona kw’isi yumutse.

^ Yona 2:2 Mu giheburayo ni “Sheoli” (sheʹohlʹ). Raba Inyongera ya 7.

^ Yona 2:3 Ijambo ku rindi ni “mu mutima w’igezi ry’ikiyaga.”

^ Yona 2:6 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
