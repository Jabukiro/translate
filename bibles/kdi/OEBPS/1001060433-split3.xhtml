Yona  3 : 1 - 10

3 Maze ijambo rya Yehova riza kuri Yona ubugira kabiri, riti: 2 “Haguruka uje i Ninewe ca gisagara gikomeye, ugitangarize ijambo ngira nkubwire.”

3 Yona aca arahaguruka aja i Ninewe, nk’uko ijambo rya Yehova rivuze. Ico gihe Ninewe cari igisagara gikomeye ku Mana, kukigenda bikaba vyasaba urugendo rw’imisi itatu. 4 Amaherezo Yona yinjira muri nya gisagara, agenda urugendo rw’umusi umwe, aguma atangaza ati: “Hasigaye imisi mirongo ine gusa Ninewe ikarandurwa.”

5 Maze abantu b’i Ninewe batangura kwizera Imana; nuko batangaza ukwisonzesha bongera bambara amagunira*, kuva ku wuhambaye kuruta abandi muri bo gushika ku mutoyi muri bo. 6 Iryo jambo ngo rishikire umwami wa Ninewe, aca ahaguruka ku ntebe yiwe y’ubwami, yambura impuzu yiwe y’ibango maze yambara igunira, araheza yicara mu minyota. 7 Vyongeye, ategeka ko batangaza i Ninewe, nk’uko umwami n’abahambaye biwe bashinze, ngo:

“Ntihagire umuntu canke igitungwa canke ubusho canke umukuku bigira ico bihonja. Ntihagire uwurya imfungurwa. Mbere ntibinywe n’amazi. 8 Kandi bambare amagunira, umuntu n’igitungwa; nibatakambire Imana bakobeje kandi bahindukire, umwe wese ahebe inzira yiwe mbi n’ubukazi bwari buri mu maboko yabo. 9 Hari uwubizi? Kumbure Imana y’ukuri yohindukira ikicuza vy’ukuri, ikareka ishavu ryayo rirurumba, ntituyogere.”

10 Nuko Imana y’ukuri ibona ibikorwa vyabo, yuko bari bahindukiye bagaheba inzira yabo mbi; gutyo Imana y’ukuri iricuza ku bijanye n’ivyago yari yavuze ko yobateje, ntiyabibateza.

^ Yona 3:5 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
