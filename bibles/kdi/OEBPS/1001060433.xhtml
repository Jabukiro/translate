Yona  1 : 1 - 17

Yona

1 Ijambo rya Yehova riza kuri Yona mwene Amitayi, riti: 2 “Haguruka, uje i Ninewe ca gisagara gikomeye, ugitangarize yuko ububi bwabo bwaduze bugashika imbere yanje.”

3 Maze Yona arahaguruka, ava imbere ya Yehova ahungira i Tarushishi; amaherezo amanuka i Yopa, abona imeri ija i Tarushishi. Nuko ariha itike, amanuka muri yo kugira ajane n’abari muri yo i Tarushishi ave imbere ya Yehova.

4 Yehova arekurira rero umuyaga ukomeye ku kiyaga, haba igihuhusi gikomeye ku kiyaga; nuko nya meri ihakwa gusāba. 5 Abagendesha nya meri baratinya maze batangura gutabaza, umwe wese atabaza imana yiwe. Baguma bashiburira mu kiyaga ibintu vyari mu meri, kugira bayiremurure ivyo bintu. Mugabo Yona yari yamanutse epfo mu gice co hasi ca nya meri ifise idari, araryama, arasinzira ubutikoma. 6 Vyakabaye kera, umukapitene w’imeri aramwegera, amubwira ati: “Vyakugendeye gute ga wa muntu usinziriye? Haguruka wambaze imana yawe! Hari aho Imana y’ukuri yotwitaho ntituyogere.”

7 Maze batangura kubwirana bati: “Nimuze dupfinde ubupfindo, kugira tumenye uwutumye dushikirwa n’ibi vyago.” Baguma bapfinda rero ubupfindo, amaherezo ubupfindo bugwa kuri Yona. 8 Nuko bamubwira bati: “Turakwinginze utubwire: ni nde atumye dushikirwa n’ibi vyago? Ukora igikorwa nyabaki, kandi uva he? Igihugu cawe ni ikihe, kandi uva mu kihe gisata c’abantu?”

9 Na we ababwira ati: “Ndi Umuheburayo, nkaba ntinya Yehova Imana y’amajuru, uwashizeho ikiyaga n’isi yumutse.”

10 Nya bantu baratinya cane, bamubwira bati: “Ivyo wakoze ni ibiki?” Kuko nya bantu bari bamenye yuko yariko arahunga Yehova, kuko yari yabibabwiye. 11 Amaherezo, bamubwira bati: “Tukugire dute ngo ikiyaga gihwame kitureke?” Kuko ikiyaga caguma kirushiriza gusebura. 12 Ababwira rero ati: “Nimunterure munterere mu kiyaga, ikiyaga gica gihwama kibareke; kuko nzi yuko ico gihuhusi gikomeye kibateye kubera jewe.” 13 Mugabo nya bantu bagerageza kwicira inzira ngo bagarukane nya meri kw’isi yumutse, ariko ntibabishobora, kuko ikiyaga caguma kirushiriza gusebura.

14 Nuko bambaza Yehova bati: “Ewe Yehova, turakwinginze twoye kuyogera tuzira ubuzima bw’uyu muntu! Kandi ntudushireko amaraso y’intungane, kuko wewe, ewe Yehova, wakoze nk’uko biguhimbara!” 15 Maze baterura Yona, bamuta mu kiyaga; ikiyaga kibona guturura, uburake bwaco burahwama. 16 Nya bagabo baca batangura gutinya Yehova cane, gutyo bashikanira ikimazi Yehova bongera bagira indagano.

17 Maze Yehova arungika urufi runini ngo rumire Yona, bituma Yona amara imbere muri nya rufi imisi itatu n’amajoro atatu.
