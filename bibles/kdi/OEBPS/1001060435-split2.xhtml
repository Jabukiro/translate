Nahumu  2 : 1 - 13

2 Uwushwiragiza arakudugiye*. Rinda ikibanza gikingijwe ibihome. Rereka inzira. Komeza amafyinga. Ongereza inguvu cane.

2 Kuko Yehova azokoranya koko ubwiza bwa Yakobo nk’ubwiza bwa Isirayeli, kubera ko bageshejwe n’abageseza; kandi bahoneje imivyaro yabo.

3 Inkinzo y’abagabo biwe b’abanyenkomezi isizwe ibara ritukura; abagabo biwe b’inguvu bambaye ibisa n’ibara ry’umutukutuku rihinze. Ku musi w’ukwitegura kwiwe, umukogote w’intambara uraca ibibatsi nk’umuriro w’ibikoresho vy’icuma, kandi amacumu y’igiti c’umuberoshi yajugumiye. 4 Imikogote y’intambara iguma igenda nk’iyasaze mu mabarabara. Iguma yirasa irya n’ino mu bibanza vya bose. Isa n’ibimuri. Iguma yiruka nk’imiravyo.

5 Umwami azokwibuka abakomakomeye biwe. Bazogenda batsitagurika. Bazokwihutira kuja ku ruhome rwiwe*, kandi ibizibizo bizokomezwa. 6 Amarembo y’inzuzi azokwugururwa ata kabuza, ikirimba na co kizoshonga koko. 7 Ivyo vyarashinzwe; yakuweko impuzu, azojanwa ata kabuza, kandi abashumbakazi biwe bazonihagira n’ijwi nk’iry’inuma, baguma bikubitagura ku mutima. 8 Ninewe, kuva ibaho, yamye imeze nk’ikidengeri c’amazi; mugabo bariko barahunga. Barasemerera bati: “Hagarare! Hagarare!” Ariko nta n’umwe ahindukira.

9 Nimusahure ifeza; nimusahure inzahabu; kuko ibintu birundanijwe bitagira uko bingana. Hari isinzi ry’ibintu bihimbaye vy’ubwoko bwose.

10 Igisagara kiragaragara, cogejwe, kandi gisigaye ari igiharabuga! Umutima urashonze, amavi ariko aradegedwa, kandi amafyinga yose ari n’ububabare bukaze; nayo mu maso habo hose, hajonjogoye. 11 Buri hehe bwa butaho bw’intambwe, na rya senga ry’intambwe z’imikangara z’imigāra, hamwe intambwe yahora igendagenda ikongera ikinjira, hamwe hahora icana c’intambwe, ata n’umwe abijugumiza? 12 Intambwe yahora itabagurira ivyana vyayo ibikwiye, ikanigira ingore zayo. Yaguma yujuje ivyobo vyayo ivyo yacakiye, ikuzuza ubwikingo bwayo ibikoko yatabaguye.

13 “Ehe ngira nkurwanye,” ni ko Yehova nyen’ingabo* avuze, “kandi nzoturirira mu mwotsi umukogote w’intambara wiwe. Inkota izorigata intambwe zawe z’imikangara z’imigāra. Nzorandura kw’isi ivyo ucakira, kandi ijwi ry’intumwa zawe ntirizosubira kwumvikana.”

^ Nah. 2:1 Uwudugiwe ni Ninewe.

^ Nah. 2:5 Rwa Ninewe.

^ Nah. 2:13 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
