Nahumu  1 : 1 - 15

Nahumu

1 Ijambo ryamenyeshejwe kuri Ninewe: Igitabu c’ivyo Nahumu Umwelikoshi yeretswe:

2 Yehova ni Imana isaba kwiheberwa yonyene gusa kandi yihora; Yehova arihora kandi arashangashirwa. Yehova arihora abarwanizi biwe, kandi agirira inzika abansi biwe.

3 Yehova ateba gushavura kandi afise ububasha bwinshi; Yehova nta ho azoreka guhana.

Inzira yiwe iri mu muyaga utema ugatongora no mu kirura, ibicu ni vyo ntumo itumurwa n’ibirenge vyiwe.

4 Ahambarira ikiyaga, akagikamya; yumisha inzuzi zose.

Bashani na Karumeli birakabiranye, n’ishurwe rya Libani rirakabiranye.

5 Imisozi iratigise kubera we, n’imirambi irashonze.

Isi izosebagirika kubera mu maso hiwe; n’ubutaka bwimbuka buzosebagirika, be n’ababa kuri bwo bose.

6 Ni nde ashobora guhagarara imbere y’uburake bwiwe? Kandi ni nde ashobora guhangara ishavu ryiwe rirurumba?

Emwe, ubushangashirwe bwiwe buzosukwa nk’umuriro, kandi ibitandara bizositurirwa hasi kubera we.

7 Yehova ni mwiza, ni igihome gikomeye ku musi w’amarushwa.

Arazi abaronderera ubuhungiro muri we.

8 Azotikiza ahantu hiwe* biciye ku mwuzure w’amazi usendanisha, kandi umwiza uzokurikirana abansi biwe.

9 Ni ibiki muzokwiyumvira kugirira Yehova? Aratikiza ntihagire igisigara.

Amarushwa ntazoza ubugira kabiri.

10 Naho bariko basobanywa nk’amahwa kandi bakaba baborewe nk’ababorejwe n’inzoga yabo y’ingano, ntibazobura kuyigizwa nk’ibishakara vyumye neza.

11 Muri wewe hazova uwiyumvira kugirira nabi Yehova, agapfundika ibitagira ikimazi.

12 Ibi ni vyo Yehova avuze: “Naho boba ata co bahajije kandi benshi bakaba bameze gutyo, ivyo ntibizobuza ko barandurwa; hari uwuzoca muri bo. Nzoguteza amarushwa, ku buryo ntazosubira kuguteza amarushwa. 13 Ubu rero nzovuna ingǒgo yiwe yo gutwarako ibintu ikuveko, ncagagure n’imizana iri kuri wewe. 14 Kandi Yehova yategetse ku bikwerekeye ati: ‘Nta citirirwa izina ryawe kizosubira kubibwa. Nzorandura mu nzu y’imana zawe igishusho c’ikibazano n’igishushanyo co mu cuma gishongeshejwe. Nzogutegurira imva, kuko ata co wamaze.’

15 “Ehe ku misozi ibirenge vy’uwuzanye inkuru nziza, uwamamaza amahoro. Ewe Yuda, himbaza imisi mikuru yawe. Shitsa indagano zawe; kuko ata muntu w’imburakimazi azosubira guca muri wewe. Vy’ukuri azorandurwa wese uko yakabaye.”

^ Nah. 1:8 Ha Ninewe.
