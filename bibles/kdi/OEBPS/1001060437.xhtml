Zefaniya  1 : 1 - 18

Zefaniya

1 Ng’iri ijambo rya Yehova ryaje kuri Zefaniya mwene Kushi, Kushi mwene Gedaliya, Gedaliya mwene Amariya, Amariya mwene Hezekiya, mu misi ya Yosiya mwene Amoni umwami wa Yuda:

2 “Nzoherengeteza ata kabuza ikintu cose ku buringanire bw’ubutaka,” ni ko Yehova avuze.

3 “Nzoherengeteza umuntu yakuwe mw’ivu be n’igikoko. Nzoherengeteza ikiremwa kiguruka co mu kirere n’amafi yo mu kiyaga, n’ibitsitaza hamwe n’ababisha; kandi nzorandura abantu ku buringanire bw’ubutaka,” ni ko Yehova avuze. 4 “Nzoramvura ukuboko kwanje nkubite Yuda be n’ababa i Yeruzalemu bose, kandi aha hantu nzoharandura abasigaye ba Bayali, izina ry’abaherezi b’imana z’inyamahanga be n’abaherezi, 5 be n’abunamira ingabo zo mu majuru bibereye ku bisenge, n’abunama bakarahira indahiro Yehova bakongera bakarahira indahiro bazirahira Malikamu, 6 be n’abasubira inyuma bakareka gukurikira Yehova, bakaba kandi batarondeye Yehova canke ngo bamusiguze.”

7 Ceceka imbere y’Umukama Segaba Yehova; kuko umusi wa Yehova uri hafi, kuko Yehova yateguye ikimazi; yejeje abatumire biwe.

8 “Uku ni ko bizoba ku musi w’ikimazi ca Yehova: nzokwitaho ivy’abaganwa, n’ivy’abana b’umwami, n’ivy’abo bose bambara ivyambarwa binyamahanga. 9 Kandi kuri uwo musi nzokwitaho ivy’umuntu wese yurira ku mukiruruko, abuzuza inzu ya ba shebuja ubukazi n’ubuhendanyi. 10 Kandi kuri uwo musi,” ni ko Yehova avuze, “hazokwumvikana induru ivuye kw’Irembo ry’Amafi, n’umuborogo uvuye mu gice ca kabiri c’igisagara, n’urwamo rw’ugusenyuka gukomeye ruvuye mu mirambi. 11 Nimuboroge, yemwe ababa i Makiteshi, kuko abantu bose b’abacuruza banumijwe; abapima ifeza bose baranduwe.

12 “Kandi uku ni ko bizoba ico gihe: nzoseranguza i Yeruzalemu amatara, niteho ivy’abantu biyegeraniriza ku rutere, bakavuga mu mutima wabo ngo: ‘Yehova nta ciza azokora, kandi nta kibi azokora.’ 13 Kandi ubutunzi bwabo buzoba ubwo gusahurwa n’inzu zabo zibe izo kugirwa igiharabuga. Bazokwubaka amazu, ariko ntibazoyabamwo; bazotera imirima y’imizabibu, ariko ntibazonywa umuvinyu wayo.

14 “Umusi uhambaye wa Yehova uri hafi. Uri hafi, kandi urihuta cane. Umuhindo w’umusi wa Yehova urarura. Umunyenkomezi araboroga. 15 Uwo musi ni umusi w’uburake, umusi w’amarushwa n’umubabaro mwinshi, umusi w’ikirura n’imisaka, umusi w’umwiza n’umuzimagiza, umusi w’ibicu n’umuzimagiza w’umuzitanya, 16 umusi w’inzamba n’ikimenyetso c’ukugabisha, ukaba uzozira ibisagara bikingijwe ibihome be n’iminara miremire yo ku mfuruka. 17 Kandi nzoteza abantu amarushwa, bagende koko nk’impumyi; kuko Yehova ari we bacumuyeko. Amaraso yabo azoseswa vy’ukuri nk’umukungugu, amara yabo na yo nk’amayezi. 18 Yaba ifeza yabo canke inzahabu yabo, ntibizoshobora kubarokora ku musi w’uburake bwa Yehova; ariko isi yose izorigatwa n’umuriro w’umwete wiwe, kuko azotikiza ababa kw’isi bose, emwe, bimwe biteye ubwoba.”
