Hagayi  2 : 1 - 23

2 Mu kwezi kugira indwi, ku musi ugira mirongo ibiri na rimwe w’ukwo kwezi, ijambo rya Yehova riza biciye kuri Hagayi umuhanuzi, riti: 2 “Ndakwinginze ubwire Zerubabeli mwene Sheyalutiyeli, buramatari wa Yuda, na Yosuwa mwene Yehozadaki umuherezi mukuru, n’amasigarira y’ico gisata, uti: 3 ‘Ni nde asigaye muri mwebwe yabonye iyi nzu mu buninahazwa bwayo bwa mbere? None ubu muyibona gute? Mbega si nk’ubusa mu maso yanyu ugereranije na kera?’

4 “‘Mugabo noneho, komera, ewe Zerubabeli,’ ni ko Yehova avuze, ‘ukomere nawe, ewe Yosuwa mwene Yehozadaki umuherezi mukuru.’

“‘Mukomere namwe banyagihugu mwese,’ ni ko Yehova avuze, ‘hanyuma mukore.’

“‘Kuko ndi kumwe namwe,’ ni ko Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze. 5 ‘Mwibuke ikintu nasezeranye namwe igihe mwava mu Misiri, n’igihe impwemu yanje yari ihagaze hagati muri mwebwe. Ntimutinye.’”

6 “Kuko ibi ari vyo Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze: ‘Rindi rimwe, mu kanya gato, ngira ntigitishe amajuru n’isi n’ikiyaga n’ubutaka bwumutse.’

7 “‘Nzotigitisha amahanga yose, maze ibintu bihimbaye vyo mu mahanga yose bizoza ata kabuza; nzokwuzuza iyi nzu ubuninahazwa,’ ni ko Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze.

8 “‘Ifeza ni izanje, n’inzahabu ni izanje,’ ni ko Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze.

9 “‘Ubuninahazwa bw’iyi nzu yo mu nyuma buzoruta ubw’iya mbere,’ ni ko Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze.

“‘Kandi nzotanga amahoro aha hantu,’ ni ko Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze.”

10 Ku musi ugira mirongo ibiri na kane w’ukwezi kugira icenda, mu mwaka ugira kabiri wa Dariyo, ijambo rya Yehova riza kuri Hagayi umuhanuzi, riti: 11 “Ibi ni vyo Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze: ‘Ndakwinginze ubaze abaherezi ku bijanye n’itegeko, uti: 12 “Mu gihe umuntu yoba atwaye inyama nyeranda mu mpuzu* yiwe maze agakoza impuzu yiwe ku mukate canke mw’isupu canke mu muvinyu canke mu mavuta canke ku bwoko ubwo ari bwo bwose bw’ibifungurwa, mbega ico kintu coca kiba ceranda?”’”

Nuko abaherezi bishura bati: “Oya!”

13 Hagayi yongera kuvuga ati: “Mu gihe umuntu yahumanijwe n’uwapfuye yoba akoze kuri kimwe muri ivyo, mbega coca kiba igihumanye?”

Abaherezi na bo bishura bati: “Coca kiba igihumanye.”

14 Nuko Hagayi yishura ati: “‘Ukwo ni ko aba bantu bameze, kandi ukwo ni ko iri hanga rimeze imbere yanje,’ ni ko Yehova avuze, ‘kandi ukwo ni ko igikorwa cose c’amaboko yabo kimeze, n’ikintu cose bashikanira ng’aho. Kirahumanye.’

15 “‘Ariko noneho, ndabinginze mushire umutima kuri ibi kuva uyu musi, mwiyumvire ukuntu ibintu vyari bimeze imbere y’uko hagira ibuye rigerekwa ku rindi mu rusengero rwa Yehova, 16 kuva aho ibi bintu vyaba: umuntu yaraja ku kirundo c’intete c’ingero mirongo ibiri, agasanga ni ingero cumi; umuntu yaraja ku gikono baganiramwo kugira ngo avome ingero mirongo itanu mu bwato bw’umuvinyu, mugabo agasangamwo ingero mirongo ibiri; 17 narabateje ukubabuka kw’ibirimwa n’ururyi n’urubura, egome, ndabiteza igikorwa cose c’amaboko yanyu, ariko nta n’umwe muri mwebwe yangarutseko,’ ni ko Yehova avuze.

18 “‘Ndabinginze mushire umutima kuri ibi kuva uyu musi, kuva ku musi ugira mirongo ibiri na kane w’ukwezi kugira icenda, kuva umusi umushinge w’urusengero rwa Yehova washingwa; mushire umutima kuri ibi: 19 Mbega hari imbuto zigisigaye mu kinogo c’intete? Kandi gushika n’ubu, umuzabibu n’igiti c’umusukoni n’igiti c’ikomamanga n’igiti c’umwelayo, vyoba birama? Kuva uwu musi nzobahunda umuhezagiro.’”

20 Nuko ijambo rya Yehova riza kuri Hagayi ubugira kabiri, ku musi ugira mirongo ibiri na kane w’ukwo kwezi, riti: 21 “Bwira Zerubabeli buramatari wa Yuda uti: ‘Ngira ntigitishe amajuru n’isi. 22 Kandi nzokwubika koko intebe y’ubwami nongere nzimanganye inkomezi z’ubwami bw’amahanga; nzokwubika umukogote n’abawugenderamwo, kandi amafarasi n’abayagenderako bizogwa, umwe wese agwishwe n’inkota ya mwene wabo.’”

23 “‘Kuri uwo musi,’ ni ko Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze, ‘nzogutoranya, ewe Zerubabeli mwene Sheyalutiyeli, umusavyi wanje,’ ni ko Yehova avuze, ‘ndakugire koko impeta y’ikidodo, kuko ari wewe natoranije,’ ni ko Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze.”

^ Hag. 2:12 Ijambo ku rindi ni “mu gice co hepfo c’impuzu.”
