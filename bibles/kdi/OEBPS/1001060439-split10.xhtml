Zekariya  10 : 1 - 12

10 “Nimusabe Yehova imvura mu gihe c’imvura y’impeshi, egome, muyisabe Yehova we ashiraho ibicu vy’imvura y’igihuhusi, akaba ari na we abaha imvura y’isegenya, umwe wese akamuha ibimera mu murima. 2 Kuko terafimu* zavuze ivy’amageza; abaraguzi beretswe ibinyoma, kandi indoto ata co zimaze ni zo baguma bavuga, kandi bagerageza guhoreza abantu ubusa. Ni co gituma bazozimira koko nk’umukuku; bazokwibabaza, kuko ata mwungere ariho.

3 “Ishavu ryanje rirurumbiye abungere, kandi nzogira ico mbaza indongozi zimeze nk’amasuguru y’impene; kuko Yehova nyen’ingabo yitayeho ubusho bwiwe, inzu ya Yuda, kandi akaba yabagize nk’ifarasi yiwe y’iteka mu rugamba. 4 Muri we hava umukuru, muri we hava umutegetsi ashigikira, muri we hava umuheto w’urugamba; muri we hasohoka umugapita wese, bose hamwe. 5 Emwe, bazomera nk’abagabo b’abanyenkomezi bahonyanga mu rwondo rwo mu mabarabara mu rugamba. Bazoja mu rugamba ata kabuza, kuko Yehova ari kumwe na bo; kandi abagendera ku mafarasi ntibazobura kumaramara. 6 Nzotuma inzu ya Yuda itsinda, kandi inzu ya Yozefu nzoyikiza. Nzobaha aho kuba, kuko nzobagirira imbabazi; bazomera nk’abo ntari narashibuye; kuko ndi Yehova Imana yabo, kandi nzobishura. 7 Kandi abo muri Efurayimu bazoba nk’umugabo w’umunyenkomezi, umutima wabo unezerwe koko nk’uwunezerejwe n’umuvinyu. Abana babo bazobibona banezerwe koko; umutima wabo uzonezererwa Yehova.

8 “‘Nzobahamagaza agahwa ndabakoranirize hamwe; kuko nzobacungura ata kabuza, kandi bazogwira, nk’abari baragwiriye. 9 Nzobasanzaza nk’imbuto mu bisata vy’abantu, kandi bazonyibuka bari mu turere twa kure; emwe, bazogarura agatege hamwe n’abana babo, kandi bazogaruka. 10 Nzobagarukana ndabakuye mu gihugu ca Misiri; kandi nzobakoraniriza hamwe ndabakuye muri Ashuri; nzobajana mu gihugu ca Gileyadi no muri Libani, kandi ntibazoronka aho bakwirwa. 11 Azoca mu kiyaga kigire amarushwa; azokubita vy’ukuri imipfunda mu kiyaga, kandi ibwina ha Nili hose hazokama. Ubwishime bwa Ashuri buzoshirwa hasi, kandi agakoni k’ubutware ka Misiri kazovaho. 12 Jewe Yehova nzotuma batsinda, kandi bazogendera mw’izina ryanje,’ ni ko Yehova avuze.”

^ Zek. 10:2 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
