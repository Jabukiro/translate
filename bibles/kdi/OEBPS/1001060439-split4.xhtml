Zekariya  4 : 1 - 14

4 Maze nya mumarayika yariko avugana nanje aragaruka hanyuma arankangura, nk’umuntu bakanguye mw’itiro. 2 Nuko ambwira ati: “Ubona iki?”

Nanje nti: “Mbonye igitereko c’amatara, cose gikozwe mu nzahabu, hejuru kuri co hakaba hari ibakure. Kandi amatara indwi yaco ari kuri co, egome, indwi; ayo matara ari hejuru kuri co afise imiringoti indwi. 3 Iruhande yaco hari ibiti vy’imyelayo bibiri, kimwe iburyo bwa nya bakure, ikindi na co ibubamfu bwayo.”

4 Maze mbaza uwo mumarayika yariko avugana nanje nti: “Ibi bintu bisobanura iki ga mukama wanje?” 5 Uwo mumarayika yariko avugana nanje ambwira rero ati: “Ntuzi koko ico ivyo bisobanura?”

Nanje nti: “Oya, mukama wanje.”

6 Nuko anyishura ati: “Iri ni ryo jambo Yehova abwiye Zerubabeli ati: ‘“Si ku bw’inteko za gisirikare, canke ku bw’ububasha, mugabo ni ku bw’impwemu yanje,” ni ko Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze. 7 Uri iki ga wa musozi munini? Imbere ya Zerubabeli uzoba intara ishashe. Kandi azosohora koko ibuye ry’umusozo. Bazorivugiriza akaruru bati: “Ewe kuntu ari iry’igikundiro! Ewe kuntu ari iry’igikundiro!”’”

8 Ijambo rya Yehova ryongera kunzako riti: 9 “Amaboko ya Zerubabeli ni yo yashinze umushinge w’iyi nzu, kandi amaboko yiwe bwite ni yo azoyisozera. Uzomenya ata kabuza yuko Yehova nyen’ingabo ari we yantumye kuri mwebwe. 10 Kuko ni nde yasuzuguye umusi w’ibintu bitoyi? Bazonezerwa koko, babone karisho* mu kuboko kwa Zerubabeli. Ayo ndwi ni amaso ya Yehova. Anyuragira mw’isi yose.”

11 Ndamubaza rero nti: “Birya biti vy’imyelayo bibiri biri iburyo bwa kirya gitereko c’amatara n’ibubamfu bwaco, bisobanura iki?” 12 Nongera kumubaza nti: “Turya tugundu tubiri tw’iminago y’ibiti vy’imyelayo tuvamwo amazimazi asa n’inzahabu aciye muri irya miringoti ibiri y’inzahabu, dusobanura iki?”

13 Nuko ambwira ati: “Ntuzi koko ico ivyo bisobanura?”

Nanje nti: “Oya, mukama wanje.”

14 Avuga rero ati: “Turya ni bamwe babiri barobanujwe amavuta*, bahagaze iruhande y’Umukama w’isi yose.”

^ Zek. 4:10 Ako gakoresho ni akuma karemereye gacundeba ku mugozi, kagakoreshwa bwa nivo ipima amahagarara.

^ Zek. 4:14 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
