Zekariya  6 : 1 - 15

6 Nuko nongera kuraramura amaso, mbona imikogote ine isohotse ivuye hagati y’imisozi ibiri, iyo misozi ikaba yari imisozi y’umujumpu. 2 Umukogote wa mbere wariko amafarasi atukura, uwa kabiri na wo amafarasi yirabura. 3 Umukogote wa gatatu wariko amafarasi yera, uwa kane na wo amafarasi y’utudorodonzi, y’amabara avangavanze.

4 Maze mbaza nya mumarayika yariko avugana nanje nti: “Ivyo ni ibiki ga mukama wanje?”

5 Nya mumarayika anyishura rero ati: “Izo ni za mpwemu zine zo mu majuru zisohoka zimaze guhagarara imbere y’Umukama w’isi yose. 6 Ku bijanye n’umukogote uriko amafarasi yirabura, ano agiye mu gihugu co mu buraruko; ayera abwirizwa kuja hakurya y’ikiyaga; ay’utudorodonzi na yo abwirizwa kuja mu gihugu co mu bumanuko. 7 Ay’amabara avangavanze abwirizwa kugenda, akaguma arondera iyo aja, kugira ngo agendagende mw’isi.” Maze avuga ati: “Nimugende, mugendagende mw’isi.” Nya mafarasi atangura rero kugendagenda mw’isi.

8 Nuko atangura gusemerera yerekeza iyo ndi, ambwira ati: “Raba, ariya agiye mu gihugu co mu buraruko ni yo yatumye impwemu ya Yehova iruhuka mu gihugu co mu buraruko.”

9 Ijambo rya Yehova ryongera kunzako riti: 10 “Haze hagire ivyakīrwa bivuye ku bajanywe ari inyagano, egome, bivuye kuri Heludayi no kuri Tobiya no kuri Yedaya; uze winjire kuri uwo musi, winjire mu nzu ya Yosiya mwene Zefaniya uri kumwe n’abo bavuye i Babiloni. 11 Uze wakire ifeza n’inzahabu, ukore urugori ruhambaye hanyuma urushire ku mutwe wa Yosuwa mwene Yehozadaki umuherezi mukuru. 12 Uheze umubwire uti:

“‘Ibi ni vyo Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze: “Ng’uwu umugabo yitwa Munago. Azonagira mu kibanza ciwe bwite, kandi azokwubaka koko urusengero rwa Yehova. 13 Ni we azokwubaka urusengero rwa Yehova, kandi azogira iteka; azokwicara koko ku ntebe yiwe y’ubwami aganze, kandi azobera umuherezi ku ntebe y’ubwami yiwe; inama y’amahoro izoba hagati y’ayo mabanga yompi. 14 Urwo rugori ruhambaye ruzoba urwa Helemu n’urwa Tobiya n’urwa Yedaya n’urwa Heni mwene Zefaniya, rubabere icibutso mu rusengero rwa Yehova. 15 Kandi abari kure bazoza bubake koko mu rusengero rwa Yehova.” Maze muzomenya yuko Yehova nyen’ingabo ari we yabantumyeko. Ivyo bizoba, nimwumviriza koko ijwi rya Yehova Imana yanyu.’”
