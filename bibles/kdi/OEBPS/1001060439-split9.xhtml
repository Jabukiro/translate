Zekariya  9 : 1 - 17

9 Ijambo ryamenyeshejwe:

“Ijambo rya Yehova riziye igihugu ca Hadaraki, kandi i Damasiko ni ho rizovyagira; kuko Yehova ashinze ijisho umuntu yakuwe mw’ivu, n’imiryango yose ya Isirayeli. 2 Riziye kandi Hamati ihana urubibe na ho; riziye na Tiro be na Sidoni, kuko ari inkerebutsi cane. 3 Tiro yiyubakiye igikuta, yirundira ifeza nk’umukungugu be n’inzahabu nk’urwondo rwo mu mabarabara. 4 Raba! Yehova ubwiwe azomwomora, emwe, ingabo ziwe azozihonereza mu kiyaga; na we azorigatwa n’umuriro. 5 Ashikeloni azobibona atinye; nayo Gaza, azokwumva ububabare bukaze; na Ekuroni, kuko ico yari ahanze amaso kitazobura kumaramara. Kandi umwami ntazobura kuzimangana i Gaza, Ashikeloni na we nta bantu bazoba muri we. 6 Emwe, umwana w’ikivyarwa azovyagira i Ashidodi, kandi nzorandura ubwishime bw’Umufilisitiya. 7 Nzomukura mu kanwa ibintu vyiwe vyandujwe n’amaraso, ndamukure mu menyo ibintu vyiwe bisesemye, kandi na we nyene azosigarizwa Imana yacu; azoba vy’ukuri nk’umutware* muri Yuda, Ekuroni na we abe nk’Umuyebusi. 8 Nzokambika hanze y’inzu yanje kugira ndayirinde, ku buryo ata wuzohaca arengana canke agaruka; nta mugapita azosubira guca iwabo, kuko ubu ndavyiboneye n’amaso yanje.

9 “Nezerwa cane, wa mukobwa wa Siyoni! Niwihe akaruru k’intsinzi, wa mukobwa wa Yeruzalemu! Raba! Umwami wawe araje kuri wewe. Aragororotse kandi ni nyen’intsinzi; aricisha bugufi, kandi agendera ku ndogobwa, egome, ku gitungwa gikuze kivuka ku ndogobwa y’ingore. 10 Nzorandura koko umukogote w’intambara muri Efurayimu, n’ifarasi muri Yeruzalemu. Kandi umuheto w’urugamba uzorandurwa ata kabuza. Azobwira amahanga ivy’amahoro; kandi ubutware bwiwe buzohēra ku kiyaga bushike ku kindi, buhēre kuri rwa Ruzi bushike ku mpera z’isi.

11 “Nawe ga wa mugore, biciye ku maraso y’isezerano ryawe, nzorungika imbohe zawe ndazikuye mu cobo kitarimwo amazi.

12 “Nimugaruke ku gihome gikomeye, yemwe mbohe zifise icizigiro!

“Vyongeye, uyu musi nkubwiye nti: ‘Nzokwishura umugabane urimwo ibiri, ewe wa mugore! 13 Kuko nzoshinga ikirenge kuri Yuda nk’aho ndagishinze ku muheto wanje. Nzoshira Efurayimu mu muheto, nkangure abana bawe, ewe Siyoni, barwanye abana bawe, ewe Bugiriki, kandi nzokugira nk’inkota y’umugabo w’umunyenkomezi.’ 14 Yehova azoboneka hejuru yabo, kandi umwampi wiwe uzofyiruka nk’umuravyo. Umukama Segaba Yehova ubwiwe azovuza inzamba, ajane n’ibirura vy’umuyaga vyo mu bumanuko. 15 Yehova nyen’ingabo ubwiwe azobarwanira, kandi bazorotsa bongere baneshe amabuye atezwa imigozi. Emwe, bazonywa, bavuze uruyogoyogo, nk’aho boba banyoye umuvinyu; bazokwuzura nk’ibakure, nk’imfuruka z’igicaniro*.

16 “Kandi kuri uwo musi, Yehova Imana yabo azobakiza koko nk’umukuku w’abantu biwe; kuko bazoba nk’amabuye yo ku mugāra aca ibibatsi ku butaka bwiwe. 17 Nkako, ewe kuntu ukumera neza kwiwe ari kwinshi, n’ubwiza bwiwe ukuntu ari bwinshi! Intete ni zo zizotuma imisore isagamba, umuvinyu mushasha utume abigeme b’isugi basagamba.”

^ Zek. 9:7 Ijambo ku rindi ni “umushehe.” Ni ijambo ry’igiheburayo rivuye kw’itsitso risobanura “igihumbi,” rikaba risobanura “indongozi y’igihumbi.”

^ Zek. 9:15 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
