Zekariya  1 : 1 - 21

Zekariya

1 Mu kwezi kugira umunani, mu mwaka ugira kabiri wa Dariyo, ijambo rya Yehova riza kuri Zekariya mwene Berekiya, Berekiya mwene Ido, umuhanuzi, riti: 2 “Yehova yararakiye cane ba so.

3 “Uze ubabwire uti: ‘Ibi ni vyo Yehova nyen’ingabo* avuze: “‘Nimungarukeko,’ ni ko Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze, ‘nanje nzobagarukako,’ ni ko Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze.”’

4 “‘Ntimube nka ba so, abo abahanuzi ba kera bahamagaye, ngo: “Ibi ni vyo Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze: ‘Ndabinginze mugaruke muve mu nzira zanyu mbi no mu bikorwa vyanyu bibi.’”’

“‘Mugabo ntibumvirije, kandi ntibanteze ugutwi,’ ni ko Yehova avuze.

5 “‘None ba so bari hehe? Abahanuzi bo, mbega babandanije kubaho gushika igihe kitagira urugero? 6 Ariko amajambo yanje n’ingingo nyobozi zanje nategetse abasavyi banje, abahanuzi, mbega ntivyahambije ba so?’ Baragarutse rero, bavuga bati: ‘Nk’uko Yehova nyen’ingabo yari yariyumviriye kutugirira, nk’uko inzira zacu n’ibikorwa vyacu vyari, ni ko yatugiriye.’”

7 Ku musi ugira mirongo ibiri na kane w’ukwezi kugira cumi na rimwe, ari kwo kwezi kwa Shebati, mu mwaka ugira kabiri wa Dariyo, ijambo rya Yehova riza kuri Zekariya mwene Berekiya, Berekiya mwene Ido, umuhanuzi, riti: 8 “Mw’ijoro nagize ntya, mbona umugabo ari kw’ifarasi itukura, akaba yari ahagaze mu biti vy’imihadasi vyari ahantu hacokeye; inyuma yiwe hari amafarasi atukura, ayasa n’umuriro, be n’ayera.”

9 Nuko mvuga nti: “Abo ni bande ga mukama?”

Umumarayika yariko avugana nanje aca ambwira ati: “Ndakwereka abo ari bo.”

10 Maze nya mugabo yari ahagaze mu biti vy’imihadasi yishura ati: “Abo ni abo Yehova yatumye kugendagenda mw’isi.” 11 Nuko bishura nya mumarayika wa Yehova yari ahagaze mu biti vy’imihadasi bati: “Twagendagenze mw’isi dusanga isi yose iratekanye kandi ata kiyihungabanya.”

12 Nya mumarayika wa Yehova yishura rero ati: “Ewe Yehova nyen’ingabo, uzogeza ryari kutagirira imbabazi Yeruzalemu n’ibisagara vya Yuda, ivyo warakiye iyi myaka mirongo irindwi?”

13 Nuko Yehova yishura nya mumarayika yariko avugana nanje, amwishura amajambo meza, amajambo ahumuriza; 14 nya mumarayika yariko avugana nanje yongera kumbwira ati: “Semerera uti: ‘Ibi ni vyo Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze: “Nagiriye Yeruzalemu na Siyoni ishari ryinshi. 15 Mfitiye uburakari bwinshi amahanga yitekaniye; kuko jewe narakaye buhoro, yamara bo bagatuma ivyago vyunyuka.”’

16 “Ibi rero ni vyo Yehova avuze: ‘“Nzogaruka kuri Yeruzalemu mfise imbabazi. Inzu yanje izokwubakwa muri we,” ni ko Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze, “kandi ikamba yo gupimisha izoramvurwa kuri Yeruzalemu.”’

17 “Subira usemerere uti: ‘Ibi ni vyo Yehova nyen’ingabo avuze: “Ibisagara vyanje bizokwongera gusesekara ivyiza; kandi Yehova azokwongera koko kwicuza ku bijanye na Siyoni, yongere vy’ukuri gutoranya Yeruzalemu.”’”

18 Nuko ndaramuye amaso, mbona amahembe ane. 19 Mbwira rero umumarayika yariko avugana nanje nti: “Ivyo ni ibiki?” Na we ambwira ati: “Ayo ni yo mahembe yashwiragije Yuda, Isirayeli na Yeruzalemu.”

20 Vyongeye, Yehova anyereka abahinguzi b’utugenegene bane. 21 Nca mvuga nti: “Abo baje gukora iki?”

Yongera kuvuga ati: “Ayo ni yo mahembe yashwiragije Yuda ku buryo ata n’umwe yongeye kuvyura umutwe; barya rero bazoza kuyajugumiza, gutsinda hasi amahembe y’amahanga adugiriza ihembe igihugu ca Yuda kugira agishwiragize.”

^ Zek. 1:3 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
