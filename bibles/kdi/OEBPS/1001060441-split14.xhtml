Matayo  14 : 1 - 36

14 Ico gihe nyene, Herodi umutware w’intara, yumva inkuru yerekeye Yezu, 2 aca abwira abasavyi biwe ati: “Uwo ni Yohani Umubatizi. Yarazuwe mu bapfuye, kandi ni co gituma akora ibikorwa vy’ububasha.” 3 Kuko Herodi yari yafashe Yohani aramuboha, amuta mw’ibohero kubera Herodiya muka Filipo mwene wabo. 4 Kuko Yohani yari yahoze amubwira ati: “Ntivyemewe ko umutwara.” 5 Ariko naho yashaka kumwica, yaratinya isinzi, kubera ko bamufata nk’umuhanuzi. 6 Mugabo igihe hariko harahimbazwa umusi w’ivuka rya Herodi, umukobwa wa Herodiya yaratamvye arashimisha cane Herodi 7 ku buryo asezerana ashizeko n’indahiro ko amuha ico asaba cose. 8 Maze nya mukobwa, yohejwe na nyina, avuga ati: “Numpe ng’aha kw’isiniya umutwe wa Yohani Umubatizi.” 9 Naho umwami vyamuteye intuntu nyinshi, ku bwo kwubahiriza indahiro yari yarahiye no ku bw’abari bicaranye na we, ategeka ko bawumuha; 10 maze arungika abantu ngo bace umutwe Yohani mw’ibohero. 11 Nuko umutwe wiwe uzanwa kw’isiniya, uhabwa nya mwigeme, na we awuzanira nyina. 12 Amaherezo abigishwa biwe baraza bakuraho ikiziga ciwe baramuhamba, hanyuma baraza babibwira Yezu. 13 Yezu avyumvise afata ubwato avayo aja mu gahinga ngo abe ukwa wenyene; mugabo amasinzi aravyumva, aca amukurikira n’amaguru kuva mu bisagara.

14 Buno yururutse ubwato, abona isinzi rinini; bamutera ikigongwe, na we akiza abarwayi babo. 15 Mugabo bugorovye abigishwa biwe baza kuri we, bavuga bati: “Aha hantu ni mu gahinga kandi amasaha aramaze kwiterera; nurungike ayo masinzi, bagende mu bigwati bigurire ivyo kurya.” 16 Ariko Yezu ababwira ati: “Ntibirirwa baragenda: Be ari mwebwe mubaha ico barya.” 17 Bamubwira bati: “Aha nta kindi dufise atari imikate itanu n’ifi zibiri.” 18 Avuga ati: “Binzanire ng’aha.” 19 Maze ategeka nya masinzi kugandagaza ku vyatsi, hanyuma afata ya mikate itanu na za fi zibiri, arangamiza mw’ijuru, arabihezagira, hanyuma amaze kumanyagura ya mikate, ayihereza abigishwa, abigishwa na bo bayigaburira nya masinzi. 20 Bose bararya barahaga, begeranya utumanyu twasigaye, haboneka ibisimbo cumi na bibiri vyuzuye. 21 Yamara abariye bari abagabo nk’ibihumbi bitanu, hatarimwo abagore n’abana batoyi. 22 Maze bidatevye, agobera abigishwa biwe kwurira ubwato ngo bamwitangire imbere baje ku yindi nkengera, igihe arungika nya masinzi.

23 Mu nyuma, amaze kurungika ayo masinzi, aduga ku musozi ari wenyene gusenga. Naho bwari bwije, yari wenyene ng’aho. 24 Ico gihe bwa bwato bwari bumaze kugenda imetero amajana uvuye i musozi, bukagenda bukubagurwa n’imipfunda, kubera ko umuyaga waza ubaturutse imbere. 25 Mugabo mu gihe c’iterama rigira kane ry’ijoro, aza aho bari agendera ku kiyaga. 26 Abigishwa ngo bamubone agendera ku kiyaga, bahagarika umutima, bavuga bati: “Ni igihume!” N’ubwoba n’akantu bayiha umunwa. 27 Mugabo Yezu aca arabavugisha ati: “Nimureme, ni jewe; ntimutinye.” 28 Petero amwishura ati: “Mukama, nimba ari wewe, ntegeka nze aho uri ngendera ku mazi.” 29 Na we ati: “Ingo!” Petero aca yururuka ubwato, agendera ku mazi, agana Yezu. 30 Mugabo aravye ikirura c’umuyaga agira ubwoba, hanyuma atanguye kunyika, akoma induru ati: “Mukama, ntabara!” 31 Buno nyene Yezu aramvura ukuboko aramufata, amubwira ati: “Wa muntu w’ukwizera guke we, kubera iki waretse ugatwarwa n’ugukekeranya?” 32 Bamaze kwurira mu bwato, ca kirura c’umuyaga kiraturura. 33 Maze abari mu bwato bikubita imbere yiwe, bavuga bati: “Uri Umwana w’Imana vy’ukuri.” 34 Hanyuma barajabuka baja i musozi i Genesareti.

35 Abagabo baho bamumenye, barungika ubutumwa muri iyo ntara yose ihakikuje, maze abantu bamuzanira abari barwaye bose. 36 Baramwinginga ngo bapfe gusa gukora ku buyonga bw’impuzu yiwe yo hejuru; kandi ababukorako bose barakira neza.
