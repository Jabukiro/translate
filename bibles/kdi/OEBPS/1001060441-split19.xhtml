Matayo  19 : 1 - 30

19 Buno Yezu arangije ayo majambo, ava i Galilaya, aza ku mipaka ya Yudaya hakurya ya Yorodani. 2 Vyongeye, amasinzi menshi aramukurikira, na we ayakirizayo.

3 Hanyuma Abafarizayo baza kuri we barondera kumugerageza, bavuga bati: “Vyoba vyemewe ko umugabo yirukana umugore wiwe ku mvo iyo ari yo yose?” 4 Yishura ati: “Mbega ntimwasomye yuko uwabaremye kuva mu ntango yabagize umugabo n’umugore, 5 akavuga ati: ‘Ni co gituma umugabo azosiga se na nyina akifatanya* n’umugore wiwe, kandi abo babiri bazoba umubiri umwe’? 6 Ku buryo bataba bakiri babiri, ahubwo baba ari umubiri umwe. Ku bw’ivyo rero, ico Imana yaboheye ku ngǒgo imwe, umuntu ntakagitanye.” 7 Bamubwira bati: “Kubera iki none Musa yashizeho itegeko ry’uko umuntu atanga icete co gusezerera umugore agaheza akamwirukana?” 8 Ababwira ati: “Musa yabemereye kwirukana abagore banyu kubera ugukomantara kw’imitima yanyu, mugabo ukwo si ko vyari kuva mu ntango. 9 Ndababwiye yuko uwo wese yirukana umugore wiwe atamuhoye ubusambanyi akarongora uwundi, aba arenze ibigo.”

10 Abigishwa bamubwira bati: “Nimba ivy’umugabo n’umugore wiwe ari ukwo bimeze, umuntu yopfuma areka kwubaka.” 11 Arababwira ati: “Si bose bemera iryo jambo, kiretse gusa ababifitiye ingabirano. 12 Kuko hari inkone* zavutse gutyo kuva mu nda ya ba nyina, hakaba n’inkone zagizwe inkone n’abantu, hakaba n’inkone zigize inkone ku bw’ubwami bwo mw’ijuru. Uwushoboye kuvyemera navyemere.”

13 Maze abana batoyi bazanwa kuri we, kugira abarambikeko ibiganza yongere asenge; mugabo abigishwa barabakankamira. 14 Ariko Yezu avuga ati: “Nimureke abana batoyi, kandi yaco mubabuza kuza kuri jewe, kuko ubwami bwo mw’ijuru ari ubw’abameze nk’abo.” 15 Hanyuma abashirako ibiganza, araheza avayo.

16 Maze haza umuntu kuri we, avuga ati: “Mwigisha, ni iciza ikihe ntegerezwa gukora kugira ndonke ubuzima budahera?” 17 Amubwira ati: “Kubera iki umbaza iciza ico ari co? Umwiza ahari ni umwe. Mugabo, nimba ushaka kwinjira mu buzima, niwubahirize amabwirizwa ubudahengeshanya.” 18 Nya muntu amubwira ati: “Ayahe?” Yezu ati: “Emwe, Ntukice, Ntukarenge ibigo, Ntukibe, Ntugashinge intahe y’ikinyoma, 19 Tera iteka so na nyoko, kandi Utegerezwa gukunda mugenzawe nk’uko wikunda.” 20 Nya musore amubwira ati: “Ivyo vyose narabigumije; ikindi mpajije ni igiki?” 21 Yezu amubwira ati: “Nimba ushaka gutungana, genda ugurishe ivyo utunze, uhe aboro, ni ho uzogira itunga mw’ijuru, maze uze unkurikire.” 22 Uwo musore yumvise iryo jambo, agenda intuntu ari yose, kuko yari atunze ibintu vyinshi. 23 Mugabo Yezu abwira abigishwa biwe ati: “Ni ukuri, ndababwiye yuko kizoba ikigoye ku mutunzi kwinjira mu bwami bwo mw’ijuru. 24 Vyongeye ndabibabwiye: Biroroshe ku ngamiya guca mu zuru ry’urushinge kuruta kwinjira mu bwami bw’Imana ku mutunzi.”

25 Abigishwa bavyumvise barajongoroka cane, bavuga bati: “None ni nde vy’ukuri ashobora gukizwa?” 26 Yezu abaraba mu maso, ababwira ati: “Ku bantu ivyo ntibishoboka, mugabo ku Mana vyose birashoboka.”

27 Maze Petero amwishura ati: “Ehe twebwe twarasize vyose turagukurikira; none vy’ukuri tuzohakura iki?” 28 Yezu ababwira ati: “Ni ukuri ndabibabwiye: Muri kwa kurema busha, Umwana w’umuntu niyicara ku ntebe y’ubwami yiwe y’ubuninahazwa, mwebwe mwankurikiye namwe ubwanyu muzokwicara ku ntebe z’ubwami cumi na zibiri, mucire imanza imiryango cumi n’ibiri ya Isirayeli. 29 Kandi umuntu wese yasize amazu, canke bene wabo canke bashikiwe, canke se canke nyina, canke abana, canke amatongo ku bw’izina ryanje, azobironka incuro nyinshi yongere atorane ubuzima budahera.

30 “Mugabo benshi ba mbere bazoba aba nyuma, aba nyuma na bo babe aba mbere.

^ Mat. 19:5 Ijambo ry’igiheburayo risobanura “kwumira ku, kumata rwose ubutarekura, nk’aho hoba hakoreshejwe uburembo.”

^ Mat. 19:12 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
