Matayo  21 : 1 - 46

21 Nuko begereje i Yeruzalemu, bashika i Betifage ku musozi w’Imyelayo, maze Yezu atuma abigishwa babiri, 2 ababwira ati: “Nimuje muri kiriya kigwati mubona, muca muhasanga indogobwa iziritse iri kumwe n’umwana wayo; mubiziture, mubinzanire. 3 Nihagira uwubavugisha, muvuge muti: ‘Umukama arabikeneye.’ Aca abirungika buno nyene.”

4 Ivyo vyabaye koko kugira haranguke ivyavuzwe biciye ku muhanuzi ngo: 5 “Nimubwire umukobwa wa Siyoni muti: ‘Raba! Umwami wawe araje kuri wewe, aritonda, kandi agendera ku ndogobwa, egome, ku mwana w’indogobwa, umwana w’igitwazi.’”

6 Nuko nya bigishwa bafata inzira baragenda, bagira nk’uko Yezu yabategetse. 7 Bazana ya ndogobwa n’umwana wayo, babishirako impuzu zabo zo hejuru, na we azicarako*. 8 Benshi muri iryo sinzi basasa mu nzira impuzu zabo zo hejuru, abandi na bo baca amashami ku biti bayasasa mu nzira. 9 Nayo amasinzi, ayagendera imbere yiwe n’ayamukurikira, yaguma asemerera ati: “Turatakamvye, kiza Umwana wa Dawidi! Arahezagiwe uwuje mw’izina rya Yehova! Mukize, turatakamvye, wewe uri iyo hejuru mu majuru!”

10 Buno yinjiye i Yeruzalemu, igisagara cose kija mu ruyogoyogo, kivuga ngo: “Uyu ni nde?” 11 Amasinzi aguma avuga ngo: “Uyu ni wa muhanuzi Yezu, w’i Nazareti h’i Galilaya!”

12 Hanyuma Yezu yinjira mu rusengero, ahindira hanze abari mu vyo kugurishiriza no kugurira mu rusengero bose, ahenagura imeza z’abakaba amahera be n’intebe z’abariko bagurisha inuma. 13 Maze ababwira ati: “Vyanditswe ngo: ‘Inzu yanje izokwitwa inzu yo gusengeramwo,’ mugabo mwebwe muriko muyigira isenga ry’abambuzi.” 14 Vyongeye, impumyi n’abacumbagira baza kuri we mu rusengero, na we arabakiza.

15 Abakuru b’abaherezi n’abanditsi ngo babone ibintu vy’agatangaza akoze, n’abana b’abahungu bariko basemerera mu rusengero bongera bavuga ngo: “Turatakamvye, kiza Umwana wa Dawidi!”, barashavura, 16 bamubwira bati: “Urumva ivyo aba bariko baravuga?” Yezu ababwira ati: “Egome. Mbega ntimwigeze musoma ngo: ‘Bivuye mu kanwa k’inzoya n’abonka wazanye ishemezo’?” 17 Abasiga aho, ava mu gisagara aja i Betaniya, ararayo.

18 Igihe yariko aragaruka mu gisagara mu gatondo ka kare, arasonza. 19 Hanyuma abona igiti c’umusukoni hampande y’inzira, aja iyo kiri, mugabo ntiyagira ico agisangako atari amababi yonyene, akibwira ati: “Ntihabe hakigira icamwa kiva kuri wewe, ibihe vyose.” Nya giti c’umusukoni gica kiruma ako kanya nyene. 20 Mugabo abigishwa babibonye, baratangara, bavuga bati: “Bishoboka bite ngo ico giti c’umusukoni kibe caciye cuma ako kanya nyene?” 21 Yezu abishura ati: “Ni ukuri ndabibabwiye: Mufise ukwizera gusa kandi ntimukekeranye, ntimuzokora ivyo nakoze kuri ico giti c’umusukoni vyonyene, mugabo kandi mubwiye uyu musozi muti: ‘Shinguka ujugunywe mu kiyaga,’ bizoba. 22 Kandi ibintu vyose muzosaba mu gusenga, mufise ukwizera, muzobironka.”

23 Amaze kwinjira mu rusengero, abakuru b’abaherezi be n’abagabo b’inararibonye bo muri ico gisata baza kuri we igihe yariko arigisha, bavuga bati: “Ni ububasha ki butuma ukora ivyo bintu? Kandi ni nde yabuguhaye?” 24 Yezu abishura ati: “Nanje ndababaza ikintu kimwe. Nimwakimbwira, nanje ndababwira ububasha butuma nkora ivyo bintu ubwo ari bwo: 25 Ibatisimu ya Yohani, yava ku rihe sôko? Yava mw’ijuru canke ku bantu?” Mugabo batangura kwiyumvira hagati yabo, bati: “Nitwavuga ngo ‘Mw’ijuru,’ aca atubwira ati: ‘Kubera iki none mutamwemeye?’ 26 Nitwavuga na ho ngo ‘Ku bantu,’ turatinya isinzi, kuko bose Yohani bamufata nk’umuhanuzi.” 27 Nuko bishura Yezu bati: “Nta vyo tuzi.” Na we ababwira ati: “Nanje sindababwira ububasha butuma nkora ivyo bintu ubwo ari bwo.

28 “Muvyiyumvirako iki? Umuntu umwe yari afise abana babiri. Aja ku wa mbere, amubwira ati: ‘Mwananje, genda ukore uno musi mu murima w’imizabibu.’ 29 Uno yishura ati: ‘Ndagenda, mutungwa,’ mugabo ntiyagenda. 30 Yegera uwugira kabiri, amubwira kwa kundi. Uno yishura ati: ‘Singenda.’ Mu nyuma aricuza, aragenda. 31 Ni nde muri abo babiri yakoze ukugomba kwa se?” Bishura bati: “Ni uwo wa nyuma.” Yezu ababwira ati: “Ni ukuri, ndababwiye yuko abatozakori n’abamaraya bariko barabatanga kwinjira mu bwami bw’Imana. 32 Kuko Yohani yaje kuri mwebwe mu nzira y’ubugororotsi, mugabo ntimwamwemera. Ariko abatozakori n’abamaraya baramwemeye, mugabo mwebwe, naho ivyo mwabibonye, ntimwicujije mu nyuma ngo mumwemere.

33 “Umve ikindi kigereranyo: Habaye umuntu, nyen’urugo, yateye umurima w’imizabibu, awukikuza uruzitiro hanyuma awimbamwo uruganiro rw’umuvinyu yongera yubaka umunara, hanyuma awukotesha ku barimyi, araheza afata urugendo aja mu mahanga. 34 Iyera rigeze, arungika abashumba biwe kuri nya barimyi ngo bamutorere ivyamwa. 35 Ariko nya barimyi bafata ba bashumba biwe, umwe barahuragura, uwundi barica, uwundi batera amabuye. 36 Arongera arungika abandi bashumba, benshi kuruta aba mbere, mugabo babagira kwa kundi. 37 Ubwa nyuma abarungikira umwana wiwe, avuga ati: ‘Bazokwubaha umwana wanje.’ 38 Nya barimyi ngo babone nya mwana, bavugana hagati yabo bati: ‘Uyu ni we samuragwa; ingo tumwice, dutware intoranwa yiwe!’ 39 Nuko baramufata bamuterera hanze ya wa murima w’imizabibu, baramwica. 40 None nyen’umurima niyaza, azogira ate abo barimyi?” 41 Bamubwira bati: “Kubera ko ari babi, azobatikiza bimwe bibi, nya murima na wo awuhe abandi barimyi bazomuhereza ivyamwa igihe kigeze.”

42 Yezu ababwira ati: “Mbega ntimwigeze musoma mu Vyanditswe ngo: ‘Ibuye abubatsi bataye ni ryo ribaye ibuye nyamukuru rigumya imfuruka. Ivyo vyavuye kuri Yehova, kandi ni agatangaza mu maso yacu’? 43 Ni co gituma ndababwiye nti: Ubwami bw’Imana muzobwakwa buhabwe ihanga ryama ivyamwa vyabwo. 44 Vyongeye, umuntu agwa kuri iryo buye azovunagurika. Nayo uwo rizogwako wese, rizomusya.”

45 Buno abakuru b’abaherezi n’Abafarizayo bamaze kwumva ibigereranyo vyiwe, bamenya ko ari bo yariko aravuga. 46 Barondera kumufata, mugabo batinya amasinzi kubera ko ano yamufata nk’umuhanuzi.

^ Mat. 21:7 Bishoboka ko izo yicayeko ari nya mpuzu.
