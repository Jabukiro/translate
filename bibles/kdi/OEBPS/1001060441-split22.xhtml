Matayo  22 : 1 - 46

22 Yezu mu kubishura yongera kubabwirira mu bigereranyo, ati: 2 “Ubwami bwo mw’ijuru bumeze nk’umuntu, umwami, yatunganirije umuhungu wiwe inzimano y’ubugeni. 3 Maze atuma abashumba biwe guhamagara abatumiwe kuri nya nzimano y’ubugeni, mugabo baranka kuza. 4 Yongera atuma abandi bashumba, avuga ati: ‘Mubwire abatumiwe muti: “Ehe nateguye imfungurwa zanje zo ku mutaga, amashūri yanje n’ibitungwa vyadoheshejwe vyabazwe, kandi ibintu vyose birateguwe. Nimuze ku nzimano y’ubugeni.”’ 5 Mugabo ntibabinegwa, barigira, umwe mw’itongo ryiwe, uwundi mu rucuruza rwiwe; 6 abandi na bo, bacakira abashumba biwe, babagirira nabi bimwe biteye isoni, barabica.

7 “Mugabo nya mwami araraka, arungika ingabo ziwe, ziririka abo bicanyi zongera ziturira igisagara cabo. 8 Maze abwira abashumba biwe ati: ‘Inzimano y’ubugeni yateguwe koko, mugabo abatumiwe ntibari babibereye. 9 Nimugende rero mu mayira ava mu gisagara, uwo muronka wese mumutumire ku nzimano y’ubugeni.’ 10 Abo bashumba baca barasohoka baja mu mayira, bakorakoranya abo baronse bose, ababi n’abeza; inzu y’ibirori vy’ubugeni yuzura abari ku meza.

11 “Umwami yinjiye kugendura abashitsi, ahabona umuntu atambaye impuzu y’ubugeni. 12 Nuko amubwira ati: ‘Mugenzi, winjiye ng’aha gute utambaye impuzu y’ubugeni?’ Abura ico avuga. 13 Maze nya mwami abwira abasuku biwe ati: ‘Nimumubohe amaboko n’amaguru, mumuterere mu mwiza hanze. Aho ni ho hazoba ukurira kwiwe n’uguhekenya amenyo.’

14 “Kuko abatumiwe ari benshi, mugabo abatowe bakaba bake.”

15 Maze Abafarizayo baragenda, baja inama kugira bamutegere mu majambo avuga. 16 Nuko barungika kuri we abigishwa babo, bari kumwe n’abanywanyi b’umugambwe wa Herodi, bavuga bati: “Mwigisha, turazi ko uri imvugakuri be n’uko wigisha inzira y’Imana mu kuri, kandi ko ata n’umwe agukanga, kuko utaraba uko abantu baboneka inyuma. 17 Tubwire rero: Uvyibazako iki? Vyoba vyemewe kuriha Sezari ikori ry’umutwe canke ntivyemewe?” 18 Mugabo Yezu amenya ububisha bwabo, avuga ati: “Kubera iki mungeza, mwa biyorobetsi mwe? 19 Nyereka igiceri c’ikori ry’umutwe.” Bamuzanira idenariyo. 20 Ababwira ati: “Iyi shusho n’ibi vyandiko ni ivya nde?” 21 Bavuga bati: “Ni ivya Sezari.” Maze ababwira ati: “Nimwishure rero Sezari ivya Sezari, mugabo ivy’Imana muvyishure Imana.” 22 Nuko bavyumvise baratangara, maze baramureka, baragenda.

23 Kuri uwo musi Abasadukayo, bamwe bavuga ko ata zuka ririho, baza kuri we, bamubaza bati: 24 “Mwigisha, Musa yavuze ati: ‘Nihagira umuntu apfa ata bana asize, mwene wabo ategerezwa kwabira umugore wiwe, agahagurukiriza uruvyaro mwene wabo.’ 25 Iwacu rero hari abavukana indwi; uwa mbere ararongora hanyuma arahwera, maze kubera ko atagira uruvyaro, asigira umugore wiwe mwene wabo. 26 Bigenda gutyo nyene no ku wugira kabiri be no ku wugira gatatu, gushika bamwe indwi bose barangiye. 27 Hanyuma ya bose nya mugore arapfa. 28 None mw’izuka, azoba umugore wa nde muri abo indwi? Kuko bose baramwabiriye!”

29 Yezu abishura ati: “Mwarahuvye, kubera ko mutazi Ivyanditswe canke ngo mumenye ububasha bw’Imana; 30 kuko mw’izuka abagabo batarongora canke ngo abagore bashingirwe, mugabo baba bameze nk’abamarayika bo mw’ijuru. 31 Nayo ku vyerekeye izuka ry’abapfuye, mbega ntimwasomye ivyo mwabwiwe n’Imana ngo: 32 ‘Ndi Imana ya Aburahamu n’Imana ya Izahaki n’Imana ya Yakobo’? Ni Imana, atari iy’abapfuye, ahubwo y’abazima.” 33 Isinzi ngo rivyumve, rijorezwa n’inyigisho yiwe.

34 Abafarizayo bamaze kwumva ko yanumije Abasadukayo, baja hamwe bagira umugwi umwe. 35 Umwe muri bo, akaba yari umuhanga mu vy’Itegeko, amubaza amugeza, ati: 36 “Mwigisha, ibwirizwa risumba ayandi mw’Itegeko ni irihe?” 37 Amubwira ati: “‘Utegerezwa gukunda Yehova Imana yawe n’umutima wawe wose n’ubuzima bwawe bwose n’umuzirikanyi wawe wose.’ 38 Iryo ni ryo bwirizwa risumba ayandi rikaba na ryo rya mbere. 39 Irigira kabiri rimeze nka ryo, ni iri: ‘Utegerezwa gukunda mugenzawe nk’uko wikunda.’ 40 Ayo mabwirizwa abiri ni yo Itegeko ryose be n’Abahanuzi vyenenako.”

41 Igihe Abafarizayo bari bakoraniye hamwe, Yezu ababaza ati: 42 “Mwibaza iki kuri Kristu? Ni mwene nde?” Bamubwira bati: “Ni mwene Dawidi.” 43 Ababwira ati: “Bishoboka bite none ngo Dawidi, ahumekewe, amwite ‘Umukama,’ akavuga ngo: 44 ‘Yehova yabwiye Umukama wanje ati: “Icara iburyo bwanje gushika nshize abansi bawe munsi y’ibirenge vyawe”’? 45 Nimba rero Dawidi amwita ‘Umukama,’ none aba umwana wiwe gute?” 46 Ntihaboneka n’umwe ashobora kugira ijambo amwishuye, eka ntihagira n’umwe yubahuka kwongera kumubaza ikibazo kuva uyo musi.
