Matayo  25 : 1 - 46

25 “Maze ubwami bwo mw’ijuru buzomera nk’abigeme cumi b’isugi batoye amatara yabo, barasohoka baja gusanganira umukwe. 2 Batanu muri bo bari ibijuju, bandi batanu bari ab’ubwenge. 3 Kuko abari ibijuju batwaye amatara yabo mugabo ntibayatwarana n’amavuta, 4 ab’ubwenge bobo batwara amavuta mu macupa yabo, bayatwarana n’amatara yabo. 5 Umukwe agitebaganye, bose batangura guhunyiza hanyuma baja kuryama. 6 Mw’ijoro hagati humvikana akamo ngo: ‘Ng’uyu umukwe! Nimusohoke kumusanganira.’ 7 Maze abo bigeme b’isugi bose barahaguruka batunganya amatara yabo. 8 Bamwe b’ibijuju babwira ab’ubwenge bati: ‘Duheko ku mavuta yanyu, kubera ko amatara yacu agira azime.’ 9 Bamwe b’ubwenge bishura bati: ‘Hari aho hatoboneka ayahagije yodukwira twe namwe. Ahubwo nimuje ku bayagurisha mwigurire.’ 10 Bakigenda kugura, wa mukwe arashika, maze ba bigeme b’isugi bari biteguye binjirana na we ku nzimano y’ubugeni; umuryango uca urugarwa. 11 Mu nyuma ba bigeme bandi na bo baraza, bavuga bati: ‘Mutungwa, mutungwa, twugururire!’ 12 Yishura ati: ‘Ndababwiye ukuri: Sindabazi.’

13 “Mugume muri maso rero, kuko mutazi umusi canke isaha.

14 “Kuko bimeze nk’igihe umuntu yagira afate urugendo ngo aje mu mahanga, agahamagaza abashumba biwe akabazeza ivyo atunze. 15 Umwe amuha italanto zitanu, uwundi zibiri, uwundi na we imwe, umwe wese nk’uko ubushobozi bwiwe bwite buri, araheza aja mu mahanga. 16 Buno nyene umwe yaronka za talanto zitanu aca aragenda arazicuruza, yunguka zindi zitanu. 17 Umwe yaronka zibiri na we nyene yunguka zindi zibiri. 18 Mugabo umwe yaronka imwe gusa aragenda, yimba mw’ivu, anyegeza amafeza ya shebuja.

19 “Haciye igihe kirekire shebuja wa nya bashumba araza, arabishuza. 20 Nuko uwari yaronse italanto zitanu araza, azana izindi talanto zitanu, avuga ati: ‘Databuja, wanjeje italanto zitanu; raba nungutse izindi zitanu.’ 21 Shebuja amubwira ati: ‘Wakoze neza, mushumba mwiza kandi w’umwizigirwa! Wabaye umwizigirwa kuri bikeya. Nzogushinga vyinshi. Injira mu kanyamuneza ka shobuja.’ 22 Ubukurikira, uwari yaronse za talanto zibiri araza, avuga ati: ‘Databuja, wanjeje italanto zibiri; raba nungutse izindi zibiri.’ 23 Shebuja amubwira ati: ‘Wakoze neza, mushumba mwiza kandi w’umwizigirwa! Wabaye umwizigirwa kuri bikeya. Nzogushinga vyinshi. Injira mu kanyamuneza ka shobuja.’

24 “Amaherezo, uwari yaronse ya talanto imwe araza, avuga ati: ‘Databuja, nari nzi yuko uri umuntu w’umuhambazi, ushwabura aho utabivye, ugakumakuma aho utagosoreye. 25 Ivyo vyatumye ngira ubwoba, nca ndagenda nyegeza italanto yawe mw’ivu. Ehe ng’ibi ivyawe.’ 26 Shebuja amwishura ati: ‘Wa mushumba mubi kandi w’ikinebwe we! Wari uzi yuko nshwabura aho ntabivye ngakumakuma aho ntagosoreye? 27 Wari ukwiye rero kuba warabikije amafeza yanje ku banyebanki, nshitse nkaronka ivyanje kumwe n’inyungu.

28 “‘Mwake rero iyo talanto, muyihe uwufise za talanto cumi. 29 Kuko umuntu wese afise azokwongerwa agire umurengera; mugabo uwudafise wewe, n’ico yari afise azocakwa. 30 Nimuterere uwo mushumba w’imburakimazi mu mwiza hanze. Aho ni ho hazoba ukurira kwiwe n’uguhekenya amenyo.’

31 “Umwana w’umuntu niyashika mu buninahazwa bwiwe, n’abamarayika bose bari kumwe na we, ni ho azokwicara ku ntebe yiwe y’ubwami y’ubuninahazwa. 32 Maze amahanga yose azokoranirizwa imbere yiwe; na we azotandukanya abantu n’abandi, nka kurya umwungere atandukanya intama n’impene. 33 Azoshira intama iburyo bwiwe, mugabo impene azishire ibubamfu bwiwe.

34 “Maze umwami azobwira abari iburyo bwiwe ati: ‘Nimuze, mwebwe abahezagiwe na Dawe, mutorane ubwami mwateguriwe kuva kw’ishingwa ry’isi. 35 Kuko nashonje mukampa ico ndya. Naranyotewe murampa ico nywa. Nari kavantara muranyakira neza; 36 nari gusa muranyambika. Nararwaye muranyitwararika. Nari mw’ibohero murangarukirako.’ 37 Maze abagororotsi bazomwishura bati: ‘Mukama, ni ryari twakubonye ushonje tukakugaburira, canke unyotewe tukaguha ico unywa? 38 Ni ryari twakubonye uri kavantara tukakwakira neza, canke uri gusa tukakwambika? 39 Ni ryari twakubonye urwaye canke uri mw’ibohero tukakugarukirako?’ 40 Umwami na we azobishura ati: ‘Ni ukuri ndabibabwiye: Ku rugero mwabigiriye umwe muri aba batoyi bo hanyuma y’abandi muri bene wacu, ni jewe mwabigiriye.’

41 “Maze azoheza abwire abari ibubamfu bwiwe ati: ‘Mva iruhande, mwebwe abavumwe, muje mu muriro utazima wateguriwe Shetani* n’abamarayika biwe. 42 Kuko nashonje ntimwampa ico ndya, ndanyoterwa ntimwampa ico nywa. 43 Nari kavantara, ntimwanyakira neza; nari gusa, ntimwanyambika; nararwaye, ndaja mw’ibohero, ntimwanyitwararika.’ 44 Maze na bo nyene bazokwishura bati: ‘Mukama, ni ryari twakubonye ushonje, canke unyotewe, canke uri kavantara, canke uri gusa, canke urwaye canke uri mw’ibohero, ntihagire ico tugufasha?’ 45 Maze azobishura ati: ‘Ni ukuri ndabibabwiye: Ku rugero mutabigiriye umwe muri aba batoyi bo hanyuma y’abandi, nta vyo mwangiriye.’ 46 Abo bazoja mu kurandurwa kw’ibihe bidahera, mugabo abagororotsi baje mu buzima budahera.”

^ Mat. 25:41 Dufatiye ku kigiriki ni “Diyabolosi,” bisobanura ngo “Umubesheranyi.” Raba kandi Inyongera ya 21.
