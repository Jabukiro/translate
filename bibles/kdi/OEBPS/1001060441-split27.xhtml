Matayo  27 : 1 - 66

27 Bukeye, abakuru b’abaherezi n’abagabo b’inararibonye bose b’ico gisata bagira inama Yezu ngo bamwice. 2 Bamaze kumuboha baramujana, bamuhereza Pilato buramatari.

3 Maze Yuda, umwe yamugura, abonye yuko amaze gucirwa rubi, aradogombwa mu mutima aca asubiza abakuru b’abaherezi n’abagabo b’inararibonye vya biceri mirongo itatu vy’ifeza, 4 avuga ati: “Nacumuye mu kugura amaraso y’umugororotsi.” Na bo bati: “Bidutwaye iki? Birakuraba!” 5 Nuko ata vya biceri vy’ifeza mu rusengero, ava ng’aho aragenda, arimanika. 6 Mugabo ba bakuru b’abaherezi batora nya biceri vy’ifeza, bavuga bati: “Ntivyemewe kubitiburira mw’ishikaniro ryeranda, kuko ari ikiguzi c’amaraso.” 7 Bamaze kuja inama, babigura itongo ry’umubumvyi ryo guhambamwo ba kavantara. 8 Ni co gituma iryo tongo ryiswe “Itongo ry’Amaraso” kugeza n’uno musi. 9 Maze ivyari vyaravuzwe biciye kuri Yeremiya umuhanuzi biraranguka, ivyavuga biti: “Batora nya biceri mirongo itatu vy’ifeza, ikiguzi c’umuntu yaciriwe ikiguzi, uwo bamwe muri bene Isirayeli baciriye ikiguzi, 10 babitanga ku bw’itongo ry’umubumvyi, nk’uko Yehova yari yarantegetse.”

11 Yezu ahagarara imbere ya buramatari; buramatari amubaza iki kibazo, ati: “Wewe uri umwami w’Abayuda?” Yezu yishura ati: “Wewe ubwawe urabivuze.” 12 Mugabo, igihe ariko aragirizwa n’abakuru b’abaherezi be n’abagabo b’inararibonye, ntihagira ico yishura. 13 Maze Pilato amubwira ati: “Ntiwumva ivyo bariko barakwagiriza uko bingana?” 14 Yamara ntihagira ico amwishura, habe n’ijambo rimwe, bituma buramatari atangara cane.

15 Kandi igihe cose habaye umusi mukuru, buramatari yari afise umugenzo wo kurekurira isinzi imbohe, iyo baba bashaka. 16 Ico gihe bari bafise imbohe rurangiranwa yitwa Baraba. 17 Igihe rero bari bakoraniye hamwe, Pilato ababwira ati: “Mushaka ndabarekurire uyuhe, Baraba canke Yezu umwe bita Kristu?” 18 Kuko yari azi ko bari bamutanze kubera agashari. 19 Vyongeye, igihe yari yicaye ku ntebe y’imanza, umugore wiwe amutumako ati: “Ntiwiteranye n’uyo mugororotsi, kuko nacumukuye cane mu ndoto uno musi kubera we.” 20 Mugabo abakuru b’abaherezi be n’abagabo b’inararibonye bosha ya masinzi ngo asabe Baraba, Yezu na we yicwe. 21 Buramatari abishura ati: “Mushaka ndabarekurire uyuhe muri abo babiri?” Bavuga bati: “Baraba.” 22 Pilato ababwira ati: “None Yezu umwe bita Kristu na we ndamugire nte?” Bose bavuga bati: “Namanikwe!” 23 Avuga ati: “Mbega yemwe, none ikibi yakoze ni igiki?” Na ho nyene barushiriza gusemerera bati: “Namanikwe!”

24 Pilato abonye ko ata ciza bivuyemwo, ahubwo ko hariko havyuka intureka, yakira amazi, akarabira imbere ya nya sinzi, avuga ati: “Jewe ndacuna ku maraso y’uyu muntu. Birabaraba.” 25 Avuze ivyo, abo bantu bose bishura bati: “Amaraso yiwe naje kuri twebwe no ku bana bacu.” 26 Maze abarekurira Baraba, mugabo akubitisha Yezu hanyuma aramutanga ngo amanikwe.

27 Maze abasoda ba buramatari bajana Yezu ku kirimba ca buramatari, bakoraniriza umugwi wose w’abasirikare kuri we. 28 Bamwambura impuzu, bamwambika igikwemu c’agahama, 29 baraheza bajisha urugori rw’amahwa, barumushira ku mutwe, bamupfumbatisha n’irenga mu kuboko kw’ukuryo. Baza barapfukama imbere yiwe bamushinyaguriza bavuga bati: “Ndabugize, Mwami w’Abayuda!” 30 Nuko bamucirako, bafata rya renga batangura kurimuhonda mu mutwe. 31 Amaherezo, bamaze kumushinyaguriza, bamwambura ca gikwemu, bamushirako impuzu ziwe zo hejuru, bamutwara kumumanika.

32 Bariko barasohoka, babona imvukira y’i Sireni yitwa Simoni. Uwo mugabo bamutegeka kwikorera igiti co kubabarizwako ciwe. 33 Bageze ahantu hitwa Gologota, ni ukuvuga ngo: Ahantu h’Agahanga, 34 bamuha umuvinyu uvanze n’indurwe ngo anywe; mugabo amaze kuwuhonja, aranka kunywa. 35 Bamaze kumumanika, bagabura impuzu ziwe zo hejuru mu gupfinda ubupfindo, 36 hanyuma baricara, baramurinda. 37 Vyongeye, bamanika hejuru y’umutwe wiwe ikirego aregwa, canditse ngo: “Uyu ni Yezu Umwami w’Abayuda.”

38 Maze abambuzi babiri bamanikanwa na we, umwe iburyo bwiwe, uwundi ibubamfu bwiwe. 39 Nuko abahita batangura kumutuka, bazunza umutwe, 40 bavuga ngo: “Ewe wa muntu ugira ngo wosenyura urusengero ukarwubaka mu misi itatu, niwikize! Nimba uri umwana w’Imana, manuka uve kuri ico giti co kubabarizwako!” 41 N’abakuru b’abaherezi nyene hamwe n’abanditsi be n’abagabo b’inararibonye batangura kumushinyaguriza, bavuga ngo: 42 “Abandi yarabakijije; we ntashobora kwikiza! Ni Umwami wa Isirayeli; namanuke ubu ave kuri ico giti co kubabarizwako duheze tumwemere. 43 Yizigiye Imana; nimutabare ubu niyaba imunezwe, kuko yavuze ngo: ‘Ndi Umwana w’Imana.’” 44 Na ba bambuzi bari bamanikanywe na we batangura kumutuka.

45 Kuva kw’isaha ya gatandatu umwiza utwikira igihugu cose, gushika kw’isaha y’icenda. 46 Nko kw’isaha y’icenda, Yezu arasemerera n’ijwi rirenga ati: “Eli, Eli, lama sabakitani?”, ni ukuvuga ngo: “Mana yanje, Mana yanje, kubera iki umpevye?” 47 Bamwe mu bari bahagaze ng’aho ngo bavyumve, batangura kuvuga bati: “Ariko arahamagara Eliya.” 48 Buno nyene umwe muri bo aca arirukanga atora ikinyuko, akijobeka mu muvinyu ukarishe, agishira kw’irenga, aca aramuha ngo anywe. 49 Mugabo abandi bavuga bati: “Mureke! Reka turabe ko Eliya aza kumukiza.” [[Uwundi muntu atora icumu, amucumita mu rubavu, havayo amaraso n’amazi.]] 50 Yezu yongera gusemerera n’ijwi rirenga, aca aracikana.

51 Irido ry’aheranda rica ritantamukamwo kubiri, kuva hejuru gushika hasi, isi iranyiganyiga, ibitandara na vyo birasatagurika. 52 Imva na zo ziruguruka n’ibiziga vyinshi vy’aberanda bari barasinziriye birazikuruka 53 (inyuma y’aho azuriwe, abantu bavuye hagati mu mva binjira mu gisagara ceranda*), birabonwa n’abantu benshi. 54 Mugabo intwazangabo hamwe n’abari kumwe na yo barinze Yezu, babonye iyo nyamugigima be n’ibintu biriko biraba, batekerwa n’ubwoba, bavuga bati: “Ni ivy’ukuri, uyu yari Umwana w’Imana.”

55 Vyongeye, hari abagore benshi bariko babirabira nka hariya, bakaba bari baherekeje Yezu kuva i Galilaya kugira bamukorere; 56 muri bo harimwo Mariya Magadalena, na Mariya nyina wa Yakobo na Yoze, na nyina wa bene Zebedayo.

57 Kubera bwari bugorovye, haza umugabo w’umutunzi w’i Arimateya yitwa Yozefu, na we nyene akaba yari yaracitse umwigishwa wa Yezu. 58 Uwo mugabo aja kwa Pilato, asaba ikiziga ca Yezu. Maze Pilato ategeka ko gitangwa. 59 Yozefu atora nya kiziga, akizingira mu mpuzu y’ilino nziza, 60 agishira mu mva yiwe ikiri nshasha, iyo yari yimvye mu gitandara. Amaze gutembagariza ikibuye kinini ku muryango w’iyo mva, aragenda. 61 Mugabo Mariya Magadalena n’umwe Mariya wundi bagumaho, bicaye imbere y’iyo mva.

62 Bukeye, ukaba wari umusi ukurikira Umwiteguro, abakuru b’abaherezi n’Abafarizayo bakoranira imbere ya Pilato, 63 bavuga bati: “Mutungwa, twibutse yuko wa muhumvyi yavuze akiri muzima ngo: ‘Inyuma y’imisi itatu nzozurwa.’ 64 Tegeka rero barinde imva gushika ku musi ugira gatatu, kugira abigishwa biwe ntibahave baza ngo bamwibe, bakabwira abantu ngo: ‘Yarazuwe mu bapfuye!’, kandi ubwo buhumvyi bwa nyuma buzoba bubi kuruta ubwa mbere.” 65 Pilato ababwira ati: “Abarinzi murafise. Genda muyirinde uko muvyumva.” 66 Nuko baragenda, barinda nya mva mu gushira ikimenyetso kuri rya buye, no mu kuhashira abarinzi.

^ Mat. 27:53 Bashobora kuba bari baje kuvuga ivyo babonye.
