Matayo  28 : 1 - 20

28 Inyuma y’isabato, mu bucagucagu ku musi wa mbere w’iyinga, Mariya Magadalena n’umwe Mariya wundi baza kuraba imva.

2 Kandi raba, hari habaye nyamugigima ikomeye; kuko umumarayika wa Yehova yari yamanutse avuye mw’ijuru, aregera, atembagaza ca kibuye, araheza acicarako. 3 Yasa n’umuravyo, ivyambarwa vyiwe na vyo vyera derere nk’urubura. 4 Egome, kubera ukumutinya, abarinzi barajugumiye ku buryo bacika nk’abapfuye.

5 Mugabo nya mumarayika yishura nya bagore ati: “Ntimutinye, kuko nzi yuko muriko murondera Yezu umwe yamanikwa. 6 Nta wuri ng’aha, kuko yazuwe, nk’uko yabivuze. Ingo murabe aho yari aryamye. 7 Nimugende ningoga rero mubwire abigishwa biwe ko yazuwe mu bapfuye, kandi ehe arabitangira imbere kuja i Galilaya; ni ho muzomubonera. Ehe ndabibabwiye.”

8 Nuko bava kuri iyo mva ningoga na ningoga, bafise ubwoba n’akanyamuneza kenshi, birukanga kuvyiganira abigishwa biwe. 9 Maze Yezu ahura na bo, avuga ati: “Amahoro!” Baregera, bamufata ku birenge, bikubita imbere yiwe. 10 Maze Yezu ababwira ati: “Ntimutinye! Nimugende muvyiganire bene wacu, kugira baje i Galilaya; ni ho bazombonera.”

11 Bakiri mu nzira, bamwe mu barinzi baja mu gisagara, biganira abakuru b’abaherezi ivyari vyabaye vyose. 12 Bano bamaze gukoranira hamwe n’abagabo b’inararibonye no kuja inama, baha ba basoda igitigiri gihagije c’ibiceri vy’ifeza 13 bavuga bati: “Muravuge muti: ‘Abigishwa biwe baje mw’ijoro baramwiba dusinziriye.’ 14 Kandi nivyashika mu gutwi kwa buramatari, tuzomwumvisha anyurwe, namwe tubakize amaganya.” 15 Nuko bakira vya biceri vy’ifeza, bagira nk’uko babariwe; kandi iryo jambo ryarakwiragijwe mu Bayuda bose kugeza n’uno musi.

16 Ariko ba bigishwa cumi n’umwe baja i Galilaya ku musozi aho Yezu yari yababwiye, 17 bamubonye bikubita imbere yiwe, mugabo bamwe barakekeranya. 18 Yezu aregera, ababwira ati: “Ububasha bwose narabuhawe mw’ijuru no kw’isi. 19 Nimugende rero muhindure abantu bo mu mahanga yose abigishwa, mubabatiza mw’izina rya Data n’iry’Umwana n’iry’impwemu nyeranda, 20 mubigisha kwubahiriza ibintu vyose nabategetse. Kandi ehe ndi kumwe namwe imisi yose gushika ku nsozero y’ivy’isi.”
