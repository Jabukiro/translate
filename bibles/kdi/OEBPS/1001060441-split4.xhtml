Matayo  4 : 1 - 25

4 Maze Yezu arongowe n’impwemu aduga mu gahinga kugira ageragezwe na Shetani*. 2 Amaze imisi mirongo ine n’amajoro mirongo ine yisonzesha, arahava arasonza. 3 Vyongeye, Umugerageji araza amubwira ati: “Nimba uri umwana w’Imana, bwira aya mabuye acike imikate.” 4 Mugabo amwishura ati: “Vyanditswe ngo: ‘Umuntu ntakabeshweho n’umukate gusa, mugabo azobeshwaho n’ijambo ryose rizanana mu kanwa ka Yehova.’”

5 Maze Shetani amujana mu gisagara ceranda, amushira hejuru ku gakuta k’ikirindiro k’urusengero, 6 amubwira ati: “Nimba uri umwana w’Imana, ikororere hasi; kuko vyanditswe ngo: ‘Azoha itegeko abamarayika biwe ku bikwerekeye, na bo bazokurerura mu maboko yabo, ngo ntiwigere ukubita ikirenge kw’ibuye.’” 7 Yezu amubwira ati: “Vyanditswe kandi ngo: ‘Ntukageze Yehova Imana yawe.’”

8 Shetani yongera kumujana ku musozi muremure bidasanzwe, amwereka ubwami bwose bw’isi be n’ubwiza bwabwo, 9 hanyuma amubwira ati: “Ivyo vyose ndabiguha niwapfa kwikubita hasi ukansenga.” 10 Maze Yezu amubwira ati: “Ja hiyo, Shetani! Kuko vyanditswe ngo: ‘Yehova Imana yawe ni we utegerezwa gusenga, kandi ni we wenyene utegerezwa gukorera igikorwa ceranda.’” 11 Nuko Shetani aramureka, maze abamarayika baraza, batangura kumukorera.

12 Hanyuma yumvise ko Yohani yafashwe, avayo yinjira i Galilaya. 13 Amaze kuva i Nazareti, araza aba i Kaperinawumu iruhande y’ikiyaga mu ntara za Zebuloni na Nafutali, 14 ngo haranguke ivyavuzwe biciye kuri Yesaya umuhanuzi ngo: 15 “Ewe gihugu ca Zebuloni nawe gihugu ca Nafutali muherereye ku nzira y’ikiyaga, ku ruhande rwo hakurya ya Yorodani, Galilaya y’amahanga: 16 abantu bicaye mu mwiza barabonye umuco mwinshi, nayo abicaye mu karere k’igitutu c’urupfu, umuco warabarasiye.” 17 Guhera ico gihe Yezu atangura kwamamaza avuga ati: “Nimwigaye, kuko ubwami bwo mw’ijuru bwegereje.”

18 Ariko aragendagenda ku nkengera y’ikiyaga ca Galilaya abona abavukana babiri, Simoni yitwa Petero be na Andereya mwene wabo, bariko barekurira mu kiyaga urusenga rwo kuroba, kuko bari abarovyi. 19 Hanyuma ababwira ati: “Nimunkurikire, nanje nzobagira abarovyi b’abantu.” 20 Baca baheba za nsenga, baramukurikira. 21 Maze yigiye imbere, abona bandi babiri bavukana, ari bo Yakobo mwene Zebedayo na Yohani mwene wabo, bari kumwe na se Zebedayo mu bwato, bariko basana insenga zabo, hanyuma arabahamagara. 22 Baca basiga ubwato na se, baramukurikira.

23 Maze agendagenda i Galilaya hose, yigisha mu masinagogi yabo yongera yamamaza inkuru nziza y’ubwami, akiza n’indwara z’ubwoko bwose be n’ubumuga bw’ubwoko bwose mu bantu. 24 Maze inkuru yiwe ikwira i Siriya hose; bamuzanira abamerewe nabi bose, barembejwe n’indwara be n’imibabaro bitari bimwe, abiziziwe n’amadayimoni* be n’abarwaye intandara hamwe n’abamugaye, na we arabakiza. 25 Bica bituma amasinzi menshi amukurikira avuye i Galilaya n’i Dekapoli n’i Yeruzalemu n’i Yudaya no ku ruhande rwo hakurya ya Yorodani.

^ Mat. 4:1 Dufatiye ku kigiriki ni “Diyabolosi,” bisobanura ngo “Umubesheranyi.” Raba kandi Inyongera ya 21.

^ Mat. 4:24 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
