Matayo  9 : 1 - 38

9 Nuko yurira ubwato, arajabuka aja mu gisagara c’iwabo. 2 Hanyuma bamuzanira umuntu amugaye aryamye ku gitanda. Yezu abonye ukwizera kwabo, abwira ico kimuga ati: “Rema, kibondo; ibicumuro vyawe birababariwe.” 3 Bamwebamwe mu banditsi baca bibwira bati: “Uyu muntu ariko ararogota.” 4 Yezu na we amenya ivyiyumviro vyabo, avuga ati: “Kubera iki mwiyumvira ibintu bibi mu mitima yanyu? 5 Nk’akarorero, icoroshe ni ikihe? Ni ukuvuga ngo: Ibicumuro vyawe birababariwe, canke kuvuga ngo: Haguruka utambuke? 6 Ariko kugira mumenye yuko Umwana w’umuntu afise ububasha kw’isi bwo kubabarira ibicumuro...,” maze abwira nya kimuga ati: “Haguruka, tora igitanda cawe witahire.” 7 Na we aca arahaguruka, aritahira. 8 Amasinzi ngo abibone, atekerwa n’ubwoba, aninahaza Imana, yo yahaye abantu ububasha mwene ubwo.

9 Hanyuma Yezu ariko ararengana avuye aho hantu, abona umuntu yitwa Matayo yicaye ku biro vy’ikori, amubwira ati: “Nkurikira.” Aca arahaguruka aramukurikira. 10 Mu nyuma, igihe Yezu yari ku meza mu nzu, abatozakori n’abacumuzi benshi baraza, bicarana na we be n’abigishwa biwe. 11 Mugabo Abafarizayo ngo babibone, babwira abigishwa biwe bati: “Kubera iki umwigisha wanyu asangira n’abatozakori be n’abacumuzi?” 12 Arabumva, aca avuga ati: “Abafise amagara meza ntibakenera umuganga, ahubwo abarwaye ni bo bamukenera. 13 Nimugende rero mwige ico ibi bisobanura ngo: ‘Ico nshaka ni imbabazi, si ibimazi.’ Kuko ntaje guhamagara abagororotsi, ahubwo naje guhamagara abacumuzi.”

14 Maze abigishwa ba Yohani baza kuri we, babaza bati: “Kubera iki twe n’Abafarizayo twisonzesha mugabo abigishwa bawe ntibisonzeshe?” 15 Yezu aca ababwira ati: “None abagenzi b’umukwe hari impamvu bafise yo kugandara kandi umukwe akiri kumwe na bo? Mugabo hazoza imisi aho umukwe azokurwa muri bo, ni ho rero bazoheza bakisonzesha. 16 Nta wushonera ikiremo c’impuzu nshasha* ku mpuzu yo hejuru ishaje; kuko inkomezi zaco zose zokwega nya mpuzu yo hejuru, ugutabuka kugaca kwunyuka. 17 Eka mbere nta bashira umuvinyu mushasha mu mikuza y’inshato ishaje; kuko babigize iyo mikuza y’inshato iturika, nya muvinyu ugaseseka, ya mikuza na yo igashwanyagurika. Mugabo umuvinyu mushasha bawushira mu mikuza y’inshato mishasha, maze vyompi bikarama.”

18 Akiriko arababwira ivyo, umutware umwe yari yegereye atangura kwikubita imbere yiwe, avuga ati: “Ubu umukobwa wanje ategerezwa kuba amaze gupfa; mugabo ingo umurambikeko ikiganza, araheza asubire kuba muzima.”

19 Nuko Yezu arahaguruka, aramukurikira; abigishwa biwe na bo nyene baramukurikira. 20 Maze umugore amaze imyaka cumi n’ibiri ava amaraso, azanana inyuma yiwe, akora ku buyonga bw’impuzu yiwe yo hejuru; 21 kuko yaguma yibwira ati: “Ninapfa gukora ku mpuzu yiwe yo hejuru, ndakira.” 22 Yezu arahindukira, aramubona, avuga ati: “Rema, mukobwa wanje; ukwizera kwawe kuragukijije.” Kuva kuri iyo saha nya mugore arakira.

23 Hanyuma Yezu yinjiye mu nzu ya wa mutware akabona abariko bavuza imyironge n’isinzi riri mu ruyogoyogo, 24 avuga ati: “Nimuhave, kuko ako gakobwa katapfuye, ahubwo karasinziriye.” Avuze ivyo, batangura kumutwenga bamushinyagurira. 25 Nya sinzi rikimara gusohorwa hanze, arinjira, afata ukuboko nya gakobwa, gaca karahaguruka. 26 Emwe, inkuru y’ivyo ica ikwira muri ako karere kose.

27 Yezu ariko ararengana avuye aho hantu, abagabo babiri b’impumyi baramukurikira, basemerera bati: “Tugirire imbabazi, Mwana wa Dawidi.” 28 Amaze kwinjira mu nzu, nya bagabo b’impumyi baza kuri we, hanyuma Yezu ababaza ati: “Murizeye ko ivyo nshobora kubikora?” Baramwishura bati: “Egome, Mukama.” 29 Maze akora ku maso yabo, avuga ati: “Nibibabere nk’uko mwizeye.” 30 Amaso yabo aca arabona. Vyongeye Yezu arabihanikiriza ati: “Murabe neza ntihagire uwubimenya.” 31 Mugabo bamaze gusohoka hanze, baca bakwiragiza ivyiwe muri ako karere kose.

32 Buno bariko baragenda, bamuzanira umugabo w’ikiragi yiziziwe n’idayimoni; 33 hanyuma iyo dayimoni imaze kwirukanwa, nya mugabo w’ikiragi aravuga. Emwe, amasinzi arumirwa, avuga ati: “Ikintu nk’iki nticari bwakaboneke muri Isirayeli.” 34 Ariko Abafarizayo bavuga bati: “Umutware w’amadayimoni ni we amushoboza kwirukana amadayimoni.”

35 Hanyuma Yezu agendagenda mu bisagara vyose no mu bigwati vyose, yigisha mu masinagogi yabo, yamamaza inkuru nziza y’ubwami yongera akiza indwara z’ubwoko bwose n’ubumuga bw’ubwoko bwose. 36 Abonye amasinzi, bamutera ikigongwe, kuko bari baratabaguwe, basabagiye nk’intama zitagira umwungere. 37 Maze abwira abigishwa biwe ati: “Egome, ivyimburwa ni vyinshi mugabo abakozi ni bake. 38 Nimwinginge rero Umukuru w’iyimbura arungike abakozi mw’iyimbura ryiwe.”

^ Mat. 9:16 Ni ukuvuga, “impuzu itaritunatuna.”
