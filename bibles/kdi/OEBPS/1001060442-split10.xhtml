Mariko  10 : 1 - 52

10 Arahaguruka ava ng’aho, aza ku mipaka ya Yudaya no hakurya ya Yorodani, buno amasinzi yongera gukoranira kuri we, hanyuma nk’uko yari amenyereye kubigenza, yongera kubigisha. 2 Abafarizayo baregera, hanyuma kugira bamugeze, batangura kumubaza nimba vyemewe ko umugabo yirukana umugore. 3 Abishura ati: “Musa yabategetse iki?” 4 Bavuga bati: “Musa yarekuye ko umuntu yandika icete co gusezerera umugore agaheza akamwirukana.” 5 Mugabo Yezu ababwira ati: “Yabandikiye iryo bwirizwa kubera ugukomantara kw’imitima yanyu. 6 Ariko kuva mu ntango y’irema, ‘Yabagize umugabo n’umugore. 7 Ni co gituma umugabo azosiga se na nyina, 8 kandi abo babiri bazoba umubiri umwe’; ku buryo bataba bakiri babiri, ahubwo baba ari umubiri umwe. 9 Ku bw’ivyo rero, ico Imana yaboheye ku ngǒgo* imwe umuntu ntakagitanye.” 10 Bongeye kuba bari mu nzu, abigishwa batangura kumubaza ibibazo kuri ico kintu. 11 Na we ababwira ati: “Uwo wese yirukana umugore wiwe akarongora uwundi, aba arenze ibigo ku vyerekeye nya mugore, 12 kandi umugore ataye umugabo wiwe, aramutse arongowe n’uwundi, aba arenze ibigo.”

13 Buno abantu batangura kumuzanira abana batoyi ngo abakoreko; mugabo abigishwa barabakankamira. 14 Yezu abibonye biramushavuza, ababwira ati: “Nimureke abana batoyi baze kuri jewe; ntimugerageze kubabuza, kuko ubwami bw’Imana ari ubw’abameze nk’abo. 15 Ni ukuri ndabibabwiye: Uwo wese atakira ubwami bw’Imana nk’umwana mutoyi ntazogenda abwinjiyemwo.” 16 Araheza afata nya bana mu maboko yiwe atangura kubahezagira, abarambitseko ibiganza.

17 Hanyuma igihe yariko arasohoka ngo afate inzira, umuntu umwe aza arirukanga akubita ibipfukamiro imbere yiwe, amubaza ati: “Mwigisha mwiza, ni igiki ntegerezwa gukora ngo nze ntorane ubuzima budahera?” 18 Yezu amubwira ati: “Unyitira iki mwiza? Nta n’umwe ari mwiza, atari umwe, na we ni Imana. 19 Urazi amabwirizwa: ‘Ntiwice, Nturenge ibigo, Ntiwibe, Ntushinge intahe y’ikinyoma, Ntugunge, Tera iteka so na nyoko.’” 20 Nya muntu amubwira ati: “Mwigisha, ivyo vyose narabigumije kuva mu buto bwanje.” 21 Yezu aramwitegereza yumva aramukunze, aca amubwira ati: “Ubuze ikintu kimwe: Genda, ugurishe ivyo ufise vyose, uhe aboro, ni ho uzogira itunga mw’ijuru, maze uze unkurikire.” 22 Mugabo arijirwa yumvise iryo jambo, aca agenda intuntu ari yose, kuko yari atunze ibintu vyinshi.

23 Yezu amaze kweraguza, abwira abigishwa biwe ati: “Ese ukuntu kizoba ikigoye ku bari n’amahera kwinjira mu bwami bw’Imana!” 24 Mugabo abigishwa batangazwa cane n’amajambo yiwe. Nuko Yezu aca ababwira kandi ati: “Bibondo, ese ukuntu ari ikigoye kwinjira mu bwami bw’Imana! 25 Biroroshe ku ngamiya guca mu zuru ry’urushinge kuruta kwinjira mu bwami bw’Imana ku mutunzi.” 26 Noneho barushiriza kujorerwa, bamubwira bati: “None koko ni nde ashobora gukizwa?” 27 Yezu abaraba mu maso, avuga ati: “Ku bantu ntibishoboka, mugabo ku Mana si ukwo, kuko vyose bishoboka ku Mana.” 28 Petero atangura kumubwira ati: “Ehe twebwe twarasize vyose turagukurikira.” 29 Yezu avuga ati: “Ni ukuri ndabibabwiye: Nta n’umwe yasize inzu canke bene wabo canke bashikiwe canke nyina canke se canke abana canke amatongo ku bwanje no ku bw’inkuru nziza 30 atazoronka incuro ijana ubu muri iki kiringo, amazu na bene wabo na bashikiwe na ba nyina n’abana n’amatongo, hariko n’uruhamo, mu gihe* kizoza na ho ubuzima budahera. 31 Ariko benshi ba mbere bazoba aba nyuma, aba nyuma na bo babe aba mbere.”

32 Bari mu nzira baduga i Yeruzalemu, Yezu akaba yariko agendera imbere yabo, barumirwa; mugabo abariko barakurikira batangura gutinya. Yongera kwikebukanya bamwe cumi na babiri, atangura kubabwira ibi bintu vyabwirizwa kumushikira, ati: 33 “Raba tuduze i Yeruzalemu, kandi Umwana w’umuntu azogaburwa mu maboko y’abakuru b’abaherezi n’abanditsi, kandi bazomucira urwo gupfa, bamugabure mu maboko y’abanyamahanga, 34 bamushinyagurize, bamucireko, bamushishagure hanyuma bamwice, mugabo haciye imisi itatu azozuka.”

35 Nuko Yakobo na Yohani, ba bahungu babiri ba Zebedayo, baramwegera bamubwira bati: “Mwigisha, dushaka ko utugirira ico tugusaba cose.” 36 Ababwira ati: “Mushaka ko ndabagirira iki?” 37 Bamubwira bati: “Duhe kuzokwicara, umwe iburyo bwawe uwundi ibubamfu bwawe, mu buninahazwa bwawe.” 38 Mugabo Yezu ababwira ati: “Ntimuzi ico muriko murasaba. Mbega murashobora kunywa igikombe ndiko ndanywa, canke kubatizwa ibatisimu ndiko ndabatizwa?” 39 Bamubwira bati: “Turabishobora.” Yezu aca ababwira ati: “Igikombe ndiko ndanywa muzokinywa, n’ibatisimu ndiko ndabatizwa muzoyibatizwa. 40 Ariko ukwo kwicara iburyo bwanje canke ibubamfu bwanje si ivyanje ngo ndabitange, ahubwo ni ivy’ababiteguriwe.”

41 Nuko, aho bamwe cumi bandi bavyumviye, batangura gushavurira Yakobo na Yohani. 42 Mugabo Yezu amaze kubahamagara ngo baze kuri we, ababwira ati: “Murazi yuko ababoneka ko baganza amahanga bayakandamiza, be n’uko abakomakomeye bayo bayereka ko bayafiseko ububasha. 43 Ukwo si ko bimeze muri mwebwe; mugabo uwo wese ashaka gucika mukuru muri mwebwe ategerezwa kuba umusuku wanyu, 44 kandi uwo wese ashaka kuba uwa mbere muri mwebwe ategerezwa kuba umushumba wa bose. 45 Kuko mbere n’Umwana w’umuntu yaje, atari kugira akorerwe, ariko kugira akorere abandi, no kugira atange ubuzima bwiwe bwa ncungu* ya benshi.”

46 Hanyuma baza i Yeriko. Mugabo igihe we n’abigishwa biwe be n’isinzi ritari rito bariko barava i Yeriko, Barutimayo (mwene Timayo), umusezi w’impumyi, yari yicaye iruhande y’inzira. 47 Aho yumviye ko ari Yezu Umunyanazareti, atangura gusemerera avuga ati: “Yezu Mwana wa Dawidi, ngirira imbabazi!” 48 Benshi baca batangura kumubwira bamukarira ngo acereze; mugabo aguma arushiriza gusemerera ati: “Mwana wa Dawidi, ngirira imbabazi!” 49 Nuko Yezu arahagarara, avuga ati: “Nimumuhamagare.” Na bo bahamagara nya mpumyi, bayibwira bati: “Nureme, uhaguruke, araguhamagaye.” 50 Ita impuzu yayo yo hejuru, irabaduka, ija kwa Yezu. 51 Yezu amwishura ati: “Ushaka ndakugirire iki?” Nya mpumyi imubwira iti: “Rabuni*, nimpumuke.” 52 Yezu na we amubwira ati: “Genda, ukwizera kwawe kuragukijije.” Buno nyene arahumuka, aca atangura kumukurikira mu nzira.
