Mariko  11 : 1 - 33

11 Igihe bariko begereza i Yeruzalemu, i Betifage n’i Betaniya ku musozi w’Imyelayo, arungika babiri mu bigishwa biwe, 2 ababwira ati: “Nimugende muri kiriya kigwati mubona, hanyuma mukicinjiramwo murahasanga umwana w’indogobwa uziritse, ata muntu n’umwe aricarako; muwuziture, muwuzane. 3 Kandi nihagira uwubabwira ati: ‘Kubera iki mugira ivyo?’, muvuge muti: ‘Umukama arawukeneye, kandi aca awugarukana ng’aha.’” 4 Nuko baragenda basanga wa mwana w’indogobwa uziritse imbere y’umuryango, hanze ku kabarabara, baraheza barawuzitura. 5 Mugabo bamwe mu bari bahagaze ng’aho, batangura kubabwira bati: “Ni ibiki ko muzitura uwo mwana w’indogobwa?” 6 Bababwira nk’uko Yezu yari yavuze; baca barabareka baragenda.

7 Nuko bazanira Yezu nya mwana w’indogobwa, hanyuma bashira impuzu zabo zo hejuru kuri wo, na we awicarako. 8 Vyongeye, benshi basasa mu nzira impuzu zabo zo hejuru, mugabo abandi baca amashami mu matongo. 9 Kandi abagendera imbere n’abakurikira baguma basemerera bati: “Kiza, turatakamvye! Arahezagiwe uwuje mw’izina rya Yehova! 10 Burahezagiwe ubwami bwa data Dawidi buriko buraza! Kiza, turatakamvye, wewe uri iyo hejuru mu majuru!” 11 Nuko yinjira i Yeruzalemu, mu rusengero; hanyuma areraguza araba ibintu vyose, maze kubera ko amasaha yari amaze kugera kure, arasohoka aja i Betaniya ari kumwe na bamwe cumi na babiri.

12 Bukeye, igihe bari bamaze kuva i Betaniya, arasonza. 13 Ari nka hariya, abona igiti c’umusukoni cari gifise amababi, araheza aja kuraba ko kumbure yogisangako ikintu. Mugabo agishikiriye, ntiyagira ico ahasanga atari amababi, kuko kitari ikiringo c’insukoni. 14 Aca rero akibwira ati: “Ntihabe hakigira uwongera kurya insukoni zo kuri wewe, ibihe vyose.” Abigishwa biwe na bo bariko barumviriza.

15 Hanyuma baza i Yeruzalemu. Hano yinjira mu rusengero, atangura guhindira hanze abari mu vyo kugurishiriza no kugurira mu rusengero, yongera ahenagura imeza z’abakaba amahera be n’intebe z’abariko bagurisha inuma; 16 kandi ntiyareka ngo hagire n’umwe aca mu rusengero atwaye igikoresho co mu nzu, 17 mugabo akaguma yigisha avuga ati: “Ntivyanditswe none ngo: ‘Inzu yanje izokwitwa inzu yo gusengeramwo ku mahanga yose’? Mugabo mwebwe mwayigize isenga ry’abambuzi.” 18 Maze abakuru b’abaherezi n’abanditsi baravyumva, baca batangura kurondera ingene bomwica; kuko bamutinya kubera ko isinzi ryose ryaguma rijorezwa n’inyigisho yiwe.

19 Kandi iyo buhwibiriye, barasohoka bakava mu gisagara. 20 Mugabo igihe bariko bararengana mu gatondo ka kare, babona ca giti c’umusukoni camaze kwuma guhera mu mizi. 21 Nuko Petero aravyibuka, amubwira ati: “Rabi, raba! Ca giti c’umusukoni wavuma cumye.” 22 Yezu abishura ati: “Mwizere Imana. 23 Ni ukuri, ndababwiye yuko uwo wese azobwira uyu musozi ati: ‘Shinguka utererwe mu kiyaga,’ kandi akaba adakekeranya mu mutima wiwe, mugabo akizera yuko ivyo avuze biba, bizomugendera kuno. 24 Ni co gituma ndababwiye nti: Ibintu vyose musaba mu gusenga, mwizere ko ari nk’aho mumaze kubihabwa, kandi muzobironka. 25 Kandi igihe muhagaze musenga, mubabarire umuntu wese ikintu cose mupfa na we; kugira ngo So ari mw’ijuru na we abababarire amakosa yanyu.” 26* ——

27 Bongera kuza i Yeruzalemu. Igihe yariko aragendagenda mu rusengero, abakuru b’abaherezi n’abanditsi be n’abagabo b’inararibonye baza kuri we, 28 batangura kumubwira bati: “Ni ububasha ki butuma ukora ivyo bintu? canke ni nde yaguhaye ubwo bubasha bwo kubikora?” 29 Yezu ababwira ati: “Ndababaza ikibazo kimwe. Munyishure, nanje ndaheza ndababwire ububasha butuma nkora ivyo bintu ubwo ari bwo. 30 Ibatisimu ya Yohani yava mw’ijuru canke ku bantu? Nimunyishure.” 31 Nuko batangura kwiyumvira hagati yabo bati: “Nitwavuga ngo ‘Mw’ijuru,’ aca avuga ati: ‘Kubera iki none mutamwemeye?’ 32 Mugabo none, duhirahire tuvuge ngo ‘Ku bantu’?”... Batinya isinzi, kuko abo bose bemera yuko Yohani yari umuhanuzi vy’ukuri. 33 Nuko bishura Yezu bati: “Nta vyo tuzi.” Yezu na we ababwira ati: “Nanje sindababwira ububasha butuma nkora ivyo bintu ubwo ari bwo.”

^ Mrk. 11:26 Raba Mr 7:16, akajambo k’epfo.
