Mariko  13 : 1 - 37

13 Igihe yariko arasohoka ava mu rusengero, umwe mu bigishwa biwe amubwira ati: “Mwigisha, raba aya mabuye ingene ameze n’izi nyubakwa ingene zimeze!” 2 Ariko Yezu amubwira ati: “Urabona izo nyubakwa zihambaye? Nta buye rizogenda risigaye aha rigeretse ku rindi ridasenyuriwe hasi.”

3 Yicaye ku Musozi w’Imyelayo aho abona urusengero, Petero na Yakobo na Yohani na Andereya batangura kumubaza mu mwiherero bati: 4 “Tubwire: Ivyo bizoba ryari, kandi ni igiki kizoba ikimenyetso igihe ivyo bintu vyose bizoba bigira bishike ku nsozero?” 5 Yezu rero atangura kubabwira ati: “Mwiyubare ntihagire uwubazimiza. 6 Benshi bazoza mw’izina ryanje, bavuga ngo: ‘Ndi we,’ kandi bazozimiza benshi. 7 Vyongeye, nimwumva intambara n’amakuru y’intambara, ntimuze mutekerwe n’ubwoba; ivyo bitegerezwa kuba, mugabo iherezo ntirizoba riragera.

8 “Kuko ihanga rizohagurukira irindi hanga, n’ubwami bugahagurukira ubundi bwami; hazoba vya nyamugigima, biva hamwe bija ahandi; hazoba amapfa. Ivyo bizoba ari intango y’ububabare nk’ubw’ibise.

9 “Nayo mwebwe, mwiyubare; bazobashengeza mu masentare, kandi muzokubitirwa mu masinagogi mwongere muhagarikwe imbere ya ba buramatari n’abami babampora, kugira bibe intahe kuri bo. 10 Vyongeye, mu mahanga yose inkuru nziza itegerezwa kubanza kwamamazwa. 11 Mugabo igihe bazoba babashoreye ngo babashengeze, ntimuze mwiganyire imbere y’igihe ku vyo muzovuga; mugabo ico muzohabwa cose muri iyo saha, kizobe ari co muvuga, kuko atazoba ari mwebwe muvuga, mugabo azoba ari impwemu nyeranda. 12 Ikigeretseko, umuntu azotanga mwene wabo ngo yicwe, na se azotanga umwana, n’abana bazokurira hasi abavyeyi, babicishe; 13 kandi muzokwankwa n’abantu bose babahora izina ryanje. Mugabo uwihanganye gushika kw’iherezo ni we azokizwa.

14 “Ariko nimwabona ca kintu gisesemye giteza ubugesera gihagaze aho kitari gikwiye guhagarara (umusomyi nakoreshe ugutahura), abari i Yudaya bazoce batangura guhungira mu misozi. 15 Uwuzoba ari hejuru ku nzu ntaze amanuke, canke ngo yinjire kugira ico atoye mu nzu yiwe; 16 kandi uwuzoba ari mw’itongo ntaze agaruke ku bintu vyasigaye inyuma ngo atore impuzu yiwe yo hejuru. 17 Haragowe abagore bazoba bibungenze n’abazoba bonsa muri iyo misi! 18 Mugume musenga ngo ntibizobe mu rushana; 19 kuko iyo misi izoba ari iy’amakuba atigeze abaho kuva mu ntango y’ibiremwa Imana yaremye gushika ico gihe, kandi atazokwongera kubaho. 20 Nkako, iyo Yehova atagerura iyo misi, nta gifise umubiri cokize. Mugabo ku bw’abatowe yatoye, yarageruye iyo misi.

21 “Maze kandi nihagira uwubabwira ati: ‘Ehe Kristu ari ng’aha,’ ‘Ehe ari hariya,’ ntimuze muvyemere. 22 Kuko ba Kristu b’ikinyoma n’abahanuzi b’ikinyoma bazohaguruka, kandi bazokora ibimenyetso n’ibitangaza kugira ngo, nivyashoboka, bahuvye n’abatowe. 23 Mwebwe rero, mube maso; ndababwiye vyose imbere y’igihe.

24 “Mugabo muri iyo misi, inyuma y’ayo makuba, izuba rizocura umwiza, n’ukwezi ntikuzotanga umuco wakwo, 25 n’inyenyeri zizotibagurika ziva kw’ijuru, kandi ibinyabubasha vyo mw’ijuru bizonyiganyizwa. 26 Maze bazobona Umwana w’umuntu aza mu bicu ari n’ububasha be n’ubuninahazwa bwinshi. 27 Maze azotuma abamarayika, akoranirize hamwe abatowe biwe bo muri ya miyaga ine, kuva ku mpera y’isi gushika ku mpera y’ijuru.

28 “Nimufatire rero ku giti c’umusukoni, muhigire iki kigereranyo: Iyo ishami rikiri rito ryaco rikimara gutoha, rikazana amababi, muca mumenya ko ici* riri hafi. 29 Na mwebwe nyene, mukabona ivyo bintu biriko biraba, muze mumenye ko ari hafi, ku miryango. 30 Ni ukuri, ndababwiye yuko iri yaruka ritazogenda rivuyeho ivyo vyose bitabaye. 31 Ijuru n’isi bizovaho, mugabo amajambo yanje ntazovaho.

32 “Nayo ivy’uwo musi canke isaha, nta n’umwe abizi, baba abamarayika mw’ijuru canke Umwana, atari Data. 33 Mugume mureretse, mugume murikanuye, kuko mutazi aho igihe cashinzwe kizogerera. 34 Bimeze nk’umuntu afashe urugendo aja mu mahanga agasiga inzu yiwe, agaha ububasha abashumba biwe, umwe wese akamuha igikorwa ciwe, hanyuma agategeka umurinzi w’umuryango kuguma ari maso. 35 Nuko rero mugume muri maso, kuko mutazi igihe nyen’urugo azozirako, namba azoza buhwibiriye canke mu gicugu canke mu nkoko canke mu gatondo ka kare; 36 kugira ngo niyashika giturumbuka, ntasange musinziriye. 37 Mugabo ico ndababwiye, ndakibwiye bose: Mugume muri maso.”

^ Mrk. 13:28 Muri Palestina, ici ni co gihe ibiterwa bitotahara.
