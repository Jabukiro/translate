Mariko  7 : 1 - 37

7 Buno Abafarizayo na bamwe mu banditsi bari baje bava i Yeruzalemu bakoranira iruhande yiwe. 2 Hanyuma babonye bamwe mu bigishwa biwe bafungura n’iminwe ihumanye, ni ukuvuga iminwe idakaravye— 3 kuko Abafarizayo n’Abayuda bose batarya batabanje gukaraba iminwe kugeza mu nkokora, bakagumya umugenzo w’aba kera, 4 kandi iyo bavuye mu kaguriro, ntibarya batihumanuye mu kwimijagira amazi; hari n’iyindi migenzo myinshi bahawe ngo bayigumye, nko kwinika mu mazi ibikombe n’amakarabo n’inkono z’umuringa,— 5 abo Bafarizayo n’abanditsi baca bamubaza bati: “Kubera iki abigishwa bawe batigenza nk’uko umugenzo w’aba kera uri, mugabo bagafungura n’iminwe ihumanye?” 6 Ababwira ati: “Yesaya ntiyihenze igihe yavuga mu buhanuzi ibiberekeye, mwa biyorobetsi, nk’uko vyanditswe ngo: ‘Aba bantu bantera iteka n’iminwa yabo, mugabo imitima yabo yigijwe kure yanje cane. 7 Bansengera ubusa, kuko inyigisho bigisha ari amabwirizwa y’abantu.’ 8 Mureka ibwirizwa ry’Imana, mukagumya umugenzo w’abantu.”

9 Hanyuma ababwira ati: “Mubigiranye ubuhanga, murigiza ku ruhande ibwirizwa ry’Imana kugira ngo mugumye umugenzo wanyu. 10 Nk’akarorero, Musa yavuze ati: ‘Tera iteka so na nyoko,’ yongera ati: ‘Uwututse se canke nyina ntaze abure gupfa.’ 11 Mugabo mwebwe muvuga ngo: ‘Iyo umuntu abwiye se canke nyina ati: “Ico nari mfise cose nogufashisha ni korubani (ni ukuvuga ingabirano yeguriwe Imana),”’— 12 ntimuba mukimuretse ngo agire ikintu na kimwe akorera se canke nyina, 13 gutyo rero mugatituza ijambo ry’Imana imigenzo yanyu mwagiye murahererekana. Kandi murakora n’ibindi vyinshi mwene ivyo.” 14 Nuko yongera guhamagara isinzi ngo rize kuri we, ababwira ati: “Nimunyumvirize, mwebwe mwese, kandi mutegere insobanuro. 15 Nta kintu na kimwe kiva hanze y’umuntu cinjira muri we gishobora kumuhumanya; mugabo ibisohoka bivuye mu muntu ni vyo bihumanya umuntu.” 16* ——

17 Amaze kwinjira mu nzu kure ya rya sinzi, abigishwa biwe batangura kumubaza ibijanye n’ico kigereranyo. 18 Nuko ababwira ati: “Mbega namwe cokimwe na bo ntimutegera? Ntimuzi yuko ata kintu na kimwe kiva hanze kikinjira mu muntu gishobora kumuhumanya, 19 kuko kitinjira mu mutima wiwe, mugabo cinjira mu mara yiwe, hanyuma kigasohoka kikaja mu musarani?” Gutyo yari yerekanye ko ibifungurwa vyose bihumanuye. 20 Bitayeko, avuga ati: “Ibisohoka bivuye mu muntu ni vyo bihumanya umuntu; 21 kuko imbere mu mutima w’abantu ari ho ivyiyumviro bibi bisohoka biva: ubusambanyi, ukwiba, ubwicanyi, 22 ukurenga ibigo, ukugira umwina, ibikorwa vy’ububisha, ubuhendanyi, ubushirasoni*, ijisho ry’inzigo, ukurogota, ubwibone, ubusazi. 23 Ivyo bintu bibi vyose bisohoka biva imbere kandi birahumanya umuntu.”

24 Arahaguruka ava ng’aho aja mu turere twa Tiro na Sidoni. Hanyuma yinjira mu nzu, akaba atashaka ko hagira umuntu n’umwe abimenya. Yamara ntivyashobotse ko batamubona; 25 mugabo buno nyene umugore yari afise agakobwa karimwo impwemu ihumanye yumva ivyiwe, hanyuma araza akubita inkoro hasi ku birenge vyiwe. 26 Nya mugore yari Umugirikikazi, yamuka i Sirofenisiya; kandi yaguma amusaba kwirukana iyo dayimoni mu mukobwa wiwe. 27 Mugabo amubwira ati: “Banza ureke abana bahage, kuko atari vyiza gufata umukate w’abana ukawuterera utubwa.” 28 Ariko abishura ati: “Egome mutungwa, yamara nya tubwa aho twibereye munsi y’imeza, turya ku tuvunguka tw’abana.” 29 Aca amubwira ati: “Ko uvuze ivyo, genda; ya dayimoni yavuye mu mukobwa wawe.” 30 Nuko aragenda i muhira, asanga wa mwana mutoyi aryamye ku gitanda, ya dayimoni na yo yamuvuyemwo.

31 Yongera kugaruka ava mu turere tw’i Tiro aciye i Sidoni, aja ku kiyaga c’i Galilaya aciye hagati mu turere tw’i Dekapoli. 32 Hano bamuzanira umugabo w’igipfamatwi kandi atavuga ngo atobore, hanyuma baramwinginga ngo amurambikeko ikiganza. 33 Nuko amujana hirya ya rya sinzi ahiherereye hanyuma ashira intoke ziwe mu matwi ya nya mugabo, buno amaze gucira amate, amukora ku rurimi. 34 Hanyuma arangamiza mw’ijuru ariruhutsa cane, amubwira ati: “Efata,” ni ukuvuga ngo: “Zibuka.” 35 Ubushobozi bwiwe bwo kwumva buca buragaruka, icamugoba ururimi kirahava, aca atangura kuvuga nk’uko bisanzwe. 36 Nuko arabihanikiriza ngo ntihagire umuntu n’umwe babibwira; mugabo uko yarushiriza kubihanikiriza, ni ko barushiriza kubitangaza. 37 Emwe, barajorerwa bimwe vy’akataraboneka, bavuga bati: “Akoze ibintu vyose neza. Aratuma n’ibipfamatwi vyumva n’ibiragi bikavuga.”

^ Mrk. 7:16 Uyu murongo uraboneka mu mpinduro zimwezimwe za Bibiliya, mugabo ntuboneka mu vyandiko vy’iminwe bikurubikuru vy’ikigiriki vya kera binyuranye.

^ Mrk. 7:22 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
