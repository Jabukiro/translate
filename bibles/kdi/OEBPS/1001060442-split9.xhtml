Mariko  9 : 1 - 50

9 Ikigeretseko, abandanya kubabwira ati: “Ni ukuri ndabibabwiye: Hariho bamwe mu bahagaze ng’aha batazohonja urupfu na gato batabanje kubona ubwami bw’Imana bumaze kuza mu bubasha.” 2 Nuko haciye imisi itandatu, Yezu ahitana Petero na Yakobo na Yohani, abadugana ku musozi muremure ari bonyene gusa. Maze ahinduka ukundi imbere yabo, 3 impuzu ziwe zo hejuru na zo zicika izakaka, zirererana cane ku buryo ata mumesuzi n’umwe kw’isi yoshoboye kuzeza gutyo. 4 Vyongeye, Eliya hamwe na Musa barababonekera, bariko barayaga na Yezu. 5 Nuko Petero aca abwira Yezu ati: “Rabi, ni vyiza kuba turi ng’aha; reka rero dushinge amahema atatu, rimwe ryawe, irindi rya Musa, irindi rya Eliya.” 6 Nkako, ntiyari azi ico ari buvuge, kuko bari batekewe n’ubwoba. 7 Nuko hirema igicu, kibatwikiriza igitutu caco, maze ijwi riva muri nya gicu, riti: “Uyu ni Umwana wanje, umukundwa; nimumwumvirize.” 8 Ariko buno nyene bareraguza, ntihagira uwundi bongera kubona ari kumwe na bo, atari Yezu wenyene.

9 Bariko baramanuka bava kuri nya musozi, abategeka adakikiriza kutagira n’umwe biganira ivyo babonye, kugeza Umwana w’umuntu amaze kuzuka mu bapfuye. 10 Na bo iryo jambo barishira ku mutima, mugabo baguma babazanya ico ukwo kuzuka mu bapfuye gusobanura. 11 Batangura kumubaza bati: “Ni kubera iki abanditsi bavuga yuko Eliya ategerezwa kubanza kuza?” 12 Ababwira ati: “Eliya abanza kuza koko agasubiza ibintu vyose mu buryo; mugabo none bishoboka bite ngo habe handitswe ku Mwana w’umuntu yuko ategerezwa gushikirwa n’imibabaro myinshi akongera agafatwa nk’uwutagira ico amaze? 13 Mugabo ndabibabwiye: Eliya mu vy’ukuri yaraje, bamugirira ivyo bashaka vyose, nk’uko vyanditswe kuri we.”

14 Buno baje bagana abandi bigishwa, babona isinzi rinini ribakikije, n’abanditsi bariko baharira na bo. 15 Mugabo nya sinzi ryose ngo rimukubite ijisho rirajumararirwa, hanyuma ryirukanga iyo ari, ritangura kumuramutsa. 16 Na we ababaza ati: “Muriko muharira iki na bo?” 17 Umwe muri iryo sinzi amwishura ati: “Mwigisha, nakuzaniye umwana wanje kubera ko arimwo impwemu y’ikiragi; 18 kandi aho imufatiye hose iramukubita hasi, akabimba ifuro, akazyegenya amenyo, hanyuma akaregera. None nabwiye abigishwa bawe ngo bayirukane, mugabo ntibabishoboye.” 19 Aca ababwira ati: “Ewe wa yaruka ritagira ukwizera, nzogumana namwe kugeza ryari? Nzobihanganira kugeza ryari? Munzanire.” 20 Baramumuzanira. Mugabo ya mpwemu imubonye ica irahotagura nya mwana, hanyuma amaze kugwa hasi aguma akumbagara, abimba ifuro. 21 Abaza se wa nya mwana ati: “Ivyo bimaze igihe kingana iki bimushikira?” Na we ati: “Kuva mu bwana; 22 kandi incuro n’izindi iramuta mu muriro no mu mazi irondera kumwica. Mugabo nimba ushobora kugira ico ukoze, tugirire ikigongwe udufashe.” 23 Yezu amubwira ati: “Ugize ngo: ‘Nimba ushobora’! Erega vyose birashoboka ku wizeye.” 24 Buno nyene se wa nya mwana arasemerera ati: “Ndizeye! Mfasha aho mbuze ukwizera!”

25 Maze Yezu abonye yuko isinzi ririko rishwabaduka riza kuri bo, akankamira nya mpwemu ihumanye, mu kuyibwira ati: “Wa mpwemu y’ikiragi kandi y’igipfamatwi we, ndabigutegetse, muvemwo kandi ntugasubire kumwinjiramwo.” 26 Hanyuma imaze gusemerera no kwihotagura kenshi irasohoka; aca acika nk’uwapfuye, ku buryo abenshi muri bo bavuga bati: “Yapfuye!” 27 Mugabo Yezu amufata ukuboko aramuhagurutsa, na we arahaguruka. 28 Amaze kwinjira mu nzu abigishwa biwe batangura kumubaza biherereye bati: “Ni kubera iki twebwe tutashoboye kuyirukana?” 29 Na we ababwira ati: “Burya bwoko nta kindi gishobora kubusohora atari isengesho.”

30 Bava ng’aho baragenda, baca i Galilaya, mugabo ntiyashaka ko hagira n’umwe abimenya. 31 Kuko yariko yigisha abigishwa biwe ababwira ati: “Umwana w’umuntu abwirizwa gutangwa mu maboko y’abantu, kandi bazomwica, mugabo naho azoba yishwe, azozuka inyuma y’imisi itatu.” 32 Ariko ntibategera iryo jambo, kandi baratinya kumubaza ibibazo.

33 Hanyuma baza i Kaperinawumu. Ari mu nzu, ababaza ati: “Mwariko muharira ku biki mu nzira?” 34 Baguma bacereje, kuko mu nzira bari bahaririye hagati yabo ku wuruta abandi uwo ari we. 35 Araheza aricara, ahamagara bamwe cumi na babiri, ababwira ati: “Nimba umuntu ashaka kuba uw’imbere, ategerezwa kuba uw’inyuma ya bose n’umusuku wa bose.” 36 Maze afata umwana mutoyi, amuhagarika hagati yabo, amukikiza amaboko, araheza ababwira ati: 37 “Uwo wese yakiriye umwe mu bana batoyi mwene aba mw’izina ryanje, aba anyakiriye; kandi uwo wese anyakiriye, ntaba yakiriye jewe gusa, mugabo aba yakiriye n’uwantumye.”

38 Yohani amubwira ati: “Mwigisha, twabonye umuntu yariko yirukana amadayimoni akoresheje izina ryawe, natwe tugerageza kumubuza, kubera atariko ajana natwe.” 39 Mugabo Yezu avuga ati: “Ntimugerageze kumubuza, kuko ata n’umwe azokora igikorwa c’ububasha mw’izina ryanje ngo ace ashobora kuntuka ningoga; 40 kuko uwutaturwanya adushigikiye. 41 Kuko uwo wese abaha igikombe c’amazi yo kunywa kubera ko muri aba Kristu, ni ukuri ndabibabwiye, ntazogenda abuze impembo yiwe. 42 Mugabo uwo wese atsitaza umwe muri aba batoyi bemera, yopfuma ashirwa mw’izosi urusyo nka rumwe ruzungurutswa n’indogobwa, agatererwa vy’ukuri mu kiyaga.

43 “Kandi ikiganza cawe kiramutse kigutsitaje, gice; wopfuma winjira mu buzima ucitse igihimba c’umubiri hako ujana ibiganza bibiri muri Gehena*, mu muriro udashobora kuzima. 44* —— 45 Kandi nimba ikirenge cawe kigutsitaza, gice; wopfuma winjira mu buzima ucumbagira, hako utererwa muri Gehena ufise ibirenge bibiri. 46* —— 47 Kandi nimba ijisho ryawe rigutsitaza, rite kure; wopfuma winjira mu bwami bw’Imana ufise ijisho rimwe, hako utererwa muri Gehena ufise amaso abiri, 48 aho urunyo rwavyo rudapfa n’umuriro ntuzime.

49 “Kuko umuntu wese ategerezwa kurungwa umunyu w’umuriro. 50 Umunyu ni mwiza; mugabo umunyu uramutse utakaje ubukomezi bwawo, muzowurunga iki? Mugire umunyu muri mwebwe kandi mugumane amahoro hagati yanyu.”

^ Mrk. 9:43 Ikibanza caturirirwamwo umwavu inyuma ya Yeruzalemu.

^ Mrk. 9:44 Raba Mr 7:16, akajambo k’epfo.

^ Mrk. 9:46 Raba Mr 7:16, akajambo k’epfo.
