Luka  20 : 1 - 47

20 Kuri umwe muri nya misi, igihe yariko arigisha abantu mu rusengero yongera amenyesha inkuru nziza, abakuru b’abaherezi n’abanditsi hamwe n’abagabo b’inararibonye baregera, 2 bafata ijambo, bamubwira bati: “Tubwire ububasha butuma ukora ivyo bintu ubwo ari bwo, canke uwabuguhaye.” 3 Abishura ati: “Nanje ndababaza ikibazo, namwe muheze mumbwire: 4 Ibatisimu ya Yohani yava mw’ijuru canke ku bantu?” 5 Maze hagati yabo bagira ivyiyumviro bashitseko bati: “Nitwavuga ngo ‘Mw’ijuru,’ aca avuga ati: ‘Kubera iki mutamwemeye?’ 6 Mugabo nitwavuga ngo ‘Ku bantu,’ abantu bose uko bangana baradutera amabuye, kuko banyuzwe yuko Yohani yari umuhanuzi.” 7 Nuko bishura yuko batazi isôko ryayo. 8 Yezu na we ababwira ati: “Nanje sindababwira ububasha butuma nkora ivyo bintu ubwo ari bwo.”

9 Maze atangura guha abantu iki kigereranyo: “Umuntu umwe yateye umurima w’imizabibu, hanyuma awukotesha ku barimyi, araheza afata urugendo aja mu mahanga amarayo igihe kitari gito. 10 Mugabo igihe kigeze atuma umushumba kuri nya barimyi, ngo bamuhe ku vyamwa vya nya murima w’imizabibu. Ariko nya barimyi bamurungika imbokoboko bamaze kumuhuragura. 11 Mugabo arongera abatumako uwundi mushumba. Uwo na we nyene baramuhuragura bongera baramutetereza, bamurungika imbokoboko. 12 Yamara yongera kurungika uwugira gatatu; n’uyo nyene baramukomeretsa, bamuta hanze. 13 Kuri ivyo, nyen’umurima w’imizabibu avuga ati: ‘Nkore iki? Ndarungika umwana wanje w’umukundwa. Kumbure uyo wewe bazomwubaha.’ 14 Nya barimyi ngo bamukubite ijisho, batangura kwiyumvira hagati yabo bati: ‘Uyu ni we samuragwa; ingo tumwice intoranwa ibe rwacu.’ 15 Baca bamuterera inyuma ya nya murima w’imizabibu, baramwica. None nyen’umurima azobagira ate? 16 Azoza yice abo barimyi, nya murima na wo awuhe abandi.”

Bavyumvise bavuga bati: “Ntibikabe!” 17 Mugabo arabitegereza, avuga ati: “None ibi bintu vyanditswe bisobanura iki, ibivuga ngo: ‘Ibuye abubatsi bataye, iryo ni ryo ribaye ibuye nyamukuru rigumya imfuruka’? 18 Umuntu wese agwa kuri iryo buye azovunagurika. Nayo uwo rizogwako wese, rizomusya.”

19 Abanditsi n’abakuru b’abaherezi baca barondera kumushorerako amaboko kuri iyo saha nyene, mugabo batinya abantu; kuko bari bumvise yuko atanze ico kigereranyo ari bo yiyumvira. 20 Bamaze kumwihweza bitonze, batuma abantu bemerewe ingero mu mpisho ngo bigire abagororotsi, kugira ngo bamufatire mu majambo, babone kumushikiriza intwaro na buramatari*. 21 Hanyuma bamubaza bati: “Mwigisha, turazi ko uvuga kandi ukigisha neza be n’uko utagira nkunzi, mugabo ukigisha inzira y’Imana uhuje n’ukuri: 22 Vyoba vyemewe ko turiha ikori Sezari canke ntivyemewe?” 23 Mugabo amenya urwenge rwabo, araheza ababwira ati: 24 “Nyereka idenariyo. Iriko ishusho n’ivyandiko vya nde?” Bavuga bati: “Vya Sezari.” 25 Ababwira ati: “Uko biri kwose rero, nimwishure Sezari ivya Sezari, mugabo ivy’Imana muvyishure Imana.” 26 Erega, ntibashobora kumufatira kuri iryo jambo imbere y’abantu, ahubwo bumizwa n’inyishu yiwe, ntibavuga ikintu na kimwe.

27 Ariko bamwe mu Basadukayo*, bamwe bavuga ko ata zuka ririho, baraza, bamubaza 28 bati: “Mwigisha, Musa yatwandikiye ati: ‘Mwene wabo n’umuntu niyapfa afise umugore, ariko uwo mugabo akaba yagumye ata mwana afise, mwene wabo aze yabire nya mugore, ahagurukirize uruvyaro mwene wabo kuri nya mugore.’ 29 Urumva rero hari abavukana indwi; uwa mbere yabira umugore hanyuma apfa ata mwana afise. 30 Uwa kabiri rero, 31 n’uwa gatatu baramwabira. Mbere no kuri abo indwi biba gutyo: barapfuye ata bana basize. 32 Amaherezo, nya mugore na we arapfa. 33 None mw’izuka, azoba umugore wa nde muri abo? Kuko abo uko ari indwi baramwabiriye.”

34 Yezu ababwira ati: “Abana b’iki gihe bararongora kandi bagashingirwa, 35 mugabo ababonywe ko babereye gushika muri ca gihe kigiye kuza no kuzuka mu bapfuye, ntibarongora canke ngo bashingirwe. 36 Nkako, ntibashobora no kwongera gupfa, kuko bameze nk’abamarayika, bakaba n’abana b’Imana mu kuba abana b’izuka. 37 Mugabo ivy’uko abapfuye bazurwa, na Musa yarabihishuye, muri ya nkuru y’igisaka c’amahwa, igihe yita Yehova ‘Imana ya Aburahamu n’Imana ya Izahaki n’Imana ya Yakobo.’ 38 Ni Imana, atari iy’abapfuye, ahubwo y’abazima, kuko bose ari bazima kuri yo.” 39 Bamwe mu banditsi baca bavuga bati: “Mwigisha, uvuze neza.” 40 Kuko batabaye bacubahuka kumubaza n’ikibazo kimwe rudende.

41 Na we ababwira ati: “Bishoboka bite ngo bavuge yuko Kristu ari mwene Dawidi? 42 Kuko Dawidi ubwiwe avuga mu gitabu c’Amazaburi, ngo: ‘Yehova yabwiye Umukama wanje ati: “Icara iburyo bwanje 43 gushika ngize abansi bawe nk’intebe y’ibirenge vyawe.” ’ 44 Dawidi rero amwita ‘Umukama’; none aba umwana wiwe gute?”

45 Maze, igihe abantu bose bariko barumviriza, abwira abigishwa ati: 46 “Mwiyubare abanditsi bipfuza kugendagenda bari mu makanzu, bagakunda n’indamutso mu tuguriro n’ivyicaro vy’imbere mu masinagogi, n’ibibanza biboneka kuruta ibindi ku mfungurwa zo ku migoroba, 47 bakarotsa kandi inzu z’abapfakazi hanyuma bagatura amasengesho maremare bwa citwazo. Abo bazocirwa urubanza ruremereye kuruta.”

^ Luka 20:20 Ijambo ku rindi ni “ubukuru bwa buramatari.”

^ Luka 20:27 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
