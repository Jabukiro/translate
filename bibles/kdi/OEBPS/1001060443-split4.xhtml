Luka  4 : 1 - 44

4 Buno Yezu, yuzuye impwemu nyeranda, aragaruka avuye kuri Yorodani, hanyuma ajanwa n’impwemu hirya no hino mu gahinga 2 mu kiringo c’imisi mirongo ine ageragezwa na Shetani*. Ikigeretseko, nta co yarya muri iyo misi; nuko irangiye, arasonza. 3 Shetani aca amubwira ati: “Nimba uri umwana w’Imana, bwira iri buye ricike umukate.” 4 Mugabo Yezu aramwishura ati: “Vyanditswe ngo: ‘Umuntu ntakabeshweho n’umukate gusa.’ ”

5 Nuko amudugana ahakirurutse, amwereka mu kanya gatoyi ubwami bwose bw’isi; 6 hanyuma Shetani amubwira ati: “Ndaguha ubwo bubasha bwose n’ubuninahazwa bwabwo, kubera ko nabuhawe, kandi uwo nshaka wese ndabumuha. 7 Wewe rero, niwapfa kwikubita imbere yanje ukansenga, vyose biraba rwawe.” 8 Yezu amwishura ati: “Vyanditswe ngo: ‘Yehova Imana yawe ni we utegerezwa gusenga, kandi ni we wenyene utegerezwa gukorera igikorwa ceranda.’ ”

9 Buno amujana i Yeruzalemu amushira hejuru ku gakuta k’ikirindiro k’urusengero, amubwira ati: “Nimba uri umwana w’Imana, ikororere hasi uvuye ng’aha; 10 kuko vyanditswe ngo: ‘Azoha itegeko abamarayika biwe ku bikwerekeye, kugira ngo bakuzigame,’ 11 kandi ngo: ‘Bazokurerura mu maboko yabo, ngo ntiwigere ukubita ikirenge kw’ibuye.’ ” 12 Yezu amwishura ati: “Bivugwa ngo: ‘Ntukageze Yehova Imana yawe.’ ” 13 Nuko Shetani arangije ivyo amugerageresha vyose, aramureka kugeza aronse akandi karyo keza.

14 Hanyuma Yezu aragaruka i Galilaya, ari mu bubasha bw’impwemu. Maze amakuru meza avugwa kuri we akwiragira mu gihugu cose kihakikuje. 15 Vyongeye, atangura kwigisha mu masinagogi yabo, aterwa iteka na bose.

16 Hanyuma aza i Nazareti, aho yari yararerewe; kandi nk’uko yari amenyereye kubigira ku musi w’isabato, yinjira mw’isinagogi, hanyuma arahaguruka ngo asome. 17 Nuko ahabwa umuzingo w’umuhanuzi Yesaya, araheza azingurura nya muzingo, atora ahari handitswe ngo: 18 “Impwemu ya Yehova iri kuri jewe, kuko yandobanuje amavuta* kugira ngo menyeshe inkuru nziza aboro, yantumye kwamamaza ukubohorwa ku mbohe n’uguhumuka ku mpumyi, kurekura abajanjaguwe bakidegemvya, 19 kwamamaza umwaka wemerwa wa Yehova.” 20 Aca azinga nya muzingo, awusubiza umusuku hanyuma aricara; kandi amaso y’abari mw’isinagogi bose aguma ashinze kuri we. 21 Maze atangura kubabwira ati: “Uno musi ico canditswe muhejeje kwumva kirarangutse.”

22 Bose batangura kumushingira intahe nziza no gutangazwa n’amajambo y’igikundiro ava mu kanwa kiwe, bakavuga bati: “None uyu si umuhungu wa Yozefu?” 23 Aca ababwira ati: “Ntimuzobura kunyerekezako iki kigereranyo ngo: ‘Muganga, niwikize; ibintu twumvise ko vyabereye i Kaperinawumu nubikore no ng’aha mu karere iwanyu.’ ” 24 Mugabo avuga ati: “Ni ukuri ndababwiye yuko ata muhanuzi yemerwa mu karere iwabo. 25 Nk’akarorero, ndababwije ukuri: Hari abapfakazi benshi muri Isirayeli mu misi ya Eliya, igihe ijuru ryugarwa imyaka itatu n’amezi atandatu, ku buryo ikigoyi gikomeye gitera mu gihugu cose, 26 yamara Eliya nta n’umwe yarungitsweko muri abo bagore; ahubwo yarungitswe gusa i Zarefati mu ntara y’i Sidoni, ku mupfakazi. 27 Vyongeye, hari abanyamibembe benshi muri Isirayeli mu gihe ca Elisha umuhanuzi, yamara nta n’umwe muri bo yahumanuwe, atari Namani wa Musiriya.” 28 Buno abariko barumva ivyo bintu mw’isinagogi bose baca buzura ishavu; 29 baca barahaguruka bamunyarukana inyuma y’igisagara, bamujana hejuru ku musozi wari wubatsweko igisagara cabo, kugira ngo bamumandagarize epfo atanguze umutwe. 30 Mugabo abaca hagati arigira.

31 Nuko amanuka i Kaperinawumu, igisagara c’i Galilaya. Hanyuma yariko arabigisha kw’isabato; 32 bajorezwa n’uburyo yigisha, kuko amajambo yiwe yari n’ububasha. 33 Buno mw’isinagogi hari umuntu arimwo impwemu, idayimoni* ihumanye, hanyuma arasemerera n’ijwi rirenga ati: 34 “Aaa! Duhuriye ku ki nawe, ga Yezu Umunyanazareti? Uje kuduhonya? Ndazi neza uwo uri we, uri wa Mweranda w’Imana.” 35 Mugabo Yezu arayikankamira ati: “Cereza, kandi muvemwo.” Nuko, iyo dayimoni imaze gutura hasi nya mugabo hagati yabo, imuvamwo ata co imugize. 36 Ivyo bibaye, bose barajongoroka, batangura kuganira hagati yabo, bavuga bati: “Iyo mvugo ni bwoko ki, ko akoresha ububasha n’ubushobozi agategeka impwemu zihumanye, zigasohoka?” 37 Amakuru amwerekeye aguma rero akwira mu mihingo yose y’igihugu kihakikuje.

38 Amaze guhaguruka akava mw’isinagogi, yinjira mu nzu kwa Simoni. Buno inabukwe wa Simoni yari arembejwe n’inyonko ikaze, hanyuma bamusaba ngo amufashe. 39 Nuko ahagarara hejuru yiwe, akankamira ya nyonko, ica iramuvako. Ako kanya nyene aca arahaguruka, atangura kubakorera.

40 Mugabo izuba ririko rirarenga, abantu bose bari bafise abarwaye indwara zitari zimwe babazana kuri we. Arambika ibiganza kuri umwe wese muri bo, arabakiza. 41 N’amadayimoni yarava mu bantu benshi, agasemerera avuga ati: “Uri Umwana w’Imana.” Mugabo yarayakankamira ntayemerere kuvuga, kuko ano yari amuzi ko ari Kristu.

42 Ariko bukeye, arasohoka aja mu gahinga. Mugabo amasinzi atangura kumuhiga hirya no hino arateba ashika aho ari, hanyuma agerageza kumugumya ngo ntaje kure yabo. 43 Mugabo ababwira ati: “Ntegerezwa kumenyesha n’ibindi bisagara inkuru nziza y’ubwami bw’Imana, kubera ko ico ari co natumwe.” 44 Nuko abandanya kwamamaza mu masinagogi y’i Yudaya.
