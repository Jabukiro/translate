Luka  5 : 1 - 39

5 Igihe isinzi ryariko rimuhundarako ryongera ryumviriza ijambo ry’Imana, yari ahagaze iruhande y’ikiyaga ca Genesareti. 2 Hanyuma abona ubwato bubiri buhagaritswe ku nkengera y’ikiyaga, mugabo abarovyi bari babuvuyemwo, bariko boza insenga zabo. 3 Yuriye bumwe muri ubwo bwato, bukaba bwari ubwa Simoni, amusaba kubwigizayo gatoya uvuye i musozi. Maze aricara, hanyuma ari mu bwato atangura kwigisha ya masinzi. 4 Ahagaritse kuvuga, abwira Simoni ati: “Igira aho amazi agera kure, hanyuma muterere insenga zanyu murobe.” 5 Mugabo Simoni yishura ati: “Mwigisha, twacumukuye ijoro ryose ntitwafata namba, mugabo ko ubitegetse ndamanura insenga.” 6 Nuko ngo babigire, begeranya ifi indiri n’indiri. Nkako insenga zabo zitangura gutabuka. 7 Nuko bagirira ikimenyetso bagenzi babo mu bundi bwato ngo baze babafashe; na bo baraza, buzuza bwa bwato bwompi, ku buryo butangura kuzama. 8 Simoni Petero ngo abibone, aca yikubita imbere y’amavi ya Yezu, avuga ati: “Va aho ndi, Mukama, kuko ndi umunyabicumuro.” 9 Kuko aho bafatiye izo fi, we hamwe n’abari kumwe na we bose bajongorotse, 10 na Yakobo na Yohani, ba bahungu ba Zebedayo, bari bafatanije na Simoni. Mugabo Yezu abwira Simoni ati: “Yaco utinya. Guhera ubu uzohora uroba abantu ari bazima.” 11 Nuko bagarukana bwa bwato i musozi, baheba vyose baramukurikira.

12 Ikindi gihe ari muri kimwe mu bisagara, haza umugabo yuzuye imibembe! Akubise ijisho Yezu yubarara hasi, aramwinginga ati: “Mukama, ubishatse gusa, urashobora kumpumanura.” 13 Nuko, aramvura ukuboko amukorako, avuga ati: “Ndabishaka. Humanuka.” Buno nyene ya mibembe ica iramuvako. 14 Maze ategeka nya mugabo kutagira umuntu n’umwe abibwira, amubarira ati: “Mugabo genda wiyereke umuherezi, ushikane ishikanwa rijanye n’uguhumanurwa kwawe, nk’uko Musa yabitegetse, kugira bibabere intahe.” 15 Mugabo amakuru amwerekeye arushiriza gukwiragira, kandi amasinzi menshi yaratororokanira hamwe kugira ngo bumvirize no kugira ngo bakizwe indwara zabo. 16 Ariko we aguma ukwa wenyene mu bugaragwa yongera asenga.

17 Kuri umwe muri iyo misi yariko arigisha, kandi ng’aho hari hicaye Abafarizayo* n’abigisha b’itegeko bari baje bavuye mu bigwati vyose vy’i Galilaya n’i Yudaya n’i Yeruzalemu; kandi ububasha bwa Yehova bwari ng’aho kugira ngo akize. 18 Maze haza abantu bateruye ku gitanda umuntu yari amugaye, bakaba bariko barondera ingene bomwinjiza bakamushira imbere yiwe. 19 Nuko, babuze ingene bamwinjiza kubera isinzi, burira hejuru ku gisenge, bamucisha mu mategura, bamwururutsanya na ka gatanda hagati mu bari imbere ya Yezu. 20 Abonye ukwizera kwabo, avuga ati: “Wa muntu we, urababariwe ibicumuro vyawe.” 21 Kuri ivyo, abanditsi n’Abafarizayo batangura kwiyumvira, bavuga bati: “Ni nde uyo avuga ibirogotano? Ni nde ashobora kubabarira ibicumuro atari Imana yonyene?” 22 Mugabo Yezu amenya ivyiyumviro vyabo, abishura ati: “Muriko mwiyumvira ibiki mu mitima yanyu? 23 Icoroshe ni ikihe? Ni ukuvuga ngo: ‘Urababariwe ibicumuro vyawe,’ canke kuvuga ngo: ‘Haguruka utambuke’? 24 Mugabo kugira mumenye yuko Umwana w’umuntu afise ububasha kw’isi bwo kubabarira ibicumuro...” abwira nya muntu amugaye ati: “Ndabikubwiye: Haguruka, utore agatanda kawe witahire.” 25 Ako kanya nyene aca arahaguruka imbere yabo, atora kimwe yahora aryamako, aritahira, agenda aninahaza Imana. 26 Maze bose uko bangana bagwa mu gahundwe, batangura kuninahaza Imana, batekerwa n’ubwoba, bavuga bati: “Uno musi turabonye ibintu bitabaho!”

27 Inyuma y’ivyo arasohoka, abona umutozakori yitwa Lewi yicaye ku biro vy’ikori, amubwira ati: “Nkurikira.” 28 Na we asiga vyose arahaguruka aramukurikira. 29 Vyongeye, Lewi amutunganiriza inzimano ihambaye mu nzu iwe; kandi hari isinzi rinini ry’abatozakori n’abandi bari kumwe na bo ku meza. 30 Abafarizayo n’abanditsi babo baca batangura kwidodombera abigishwa biwe, bavuga bati: “Kubera iki musangira ibiribwa n’ibinyobwa n’abatozakori be n’abacumuzi?” 31 Yezu abishura ati: “Abafise amagara meza ntibakenera umuganga, ahubwo abarwaye ni bo bamukenera. 32 Sinaje guhamagara abagororotsi, ahubwo naje guhamagara abacumuzi ngo bigaye.”

33 Bamubwira bati: “Abigishwa ba Yohani barisonzesha kenshi bakongera bagatura intakambo, kandi n’ab’Abafarizayo ni ko bagira, mugabo abawe baririra bakinywera.” 34 Yezu ababwira ati: “Mbega mwoshobora gusaba abagenzi b’umukwe kwisonzesha kandi umukwe akiri kumwe na bo? 35 Yamara hazoza imisi aho umukwe azokurwa muri bo koko; bazoheza rero bisonzeshe muri iyo misi.”

36 Vyongeye, abandanya kubaha ikigereranyo ati: “Nta wutabura ikiremo ku mpuzu yo hejuru nshasha ngo agishonere ku mpuzu yo hejuru ishaje; mugabo abigize, ca kiremo gishasha kiraheza kigakwega, kandi ico kiremo cakuwe ku mpuzu yo hejuru nshasha ntikijana n’impuzu yo hejuru ishaje. 37 Vyongeye, nta wushira umuvinyu mushasha mu mikuza y’inshato ishaje; mugabo abigize, nya muvinyu mushasha uzoheza uturitse ya mikuza y’inshato, kandi uzoseseka, nya mikuza y’inshato na yo ishwanyagurike. 38 Mugabo umuvinyu mushasha utegerezwa gushirwa mu mikuza y’inshato mishasha. 39 Nta wanyoye umuvinyu ushaje yoshaka mushasha; kuko avuga ati: ‘Uwushaje ni wo mwiza.’ ”

^ Luka 5:17 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
