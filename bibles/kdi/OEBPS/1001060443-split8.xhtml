Luka  8 : 1 - 56

8 Haciye igihe gitoyi, agendagenda ava mu gisagara aja mu kindi, ava mu kigwati aja mu kindi, yamamaza yongera amenyesha inkuru nziza y’ubwami bw’Imana. Kandi bamwe cumi na babiri bari kumwe na we, 2 n’abagore bamwe bari barakijijwe impwemu mbi be n’indwara: Mariya bita Magadalena, uwo amadayimoni indwi yari yaravuyemwo, 3 na Yohana muka Kuza uwari ajejwe ivyo kwa Herodi, na Suzana be n’abandi bagore benshi babakorera bakoresheje ibintu batunze.

4 Igihe isinzi rinini ryari ryakoraniye hamwe n’abamukurikira bavuye mu bisagara, avuga akoresheje ikigereranyo ati: 5 “Umubivyi yarasohotse kubiba imbuto ziwe. Ariko arabiba, zimwe muri zo zigwa iruhande y’inzira maze barazihonyanga, inyoni zo mu kirere na zo zirazirya. 6 Izindi zigwa ku gitandara, hanyuma zimaze kumera, ziruma kubera zitagira akabombera. 7 Izindi zigwa mu mahwa, hanyuma nya mahwa vyakuranye arazinyoha. 8 Izindi zigwa mw’ivu ryiza, hanyuma zimaze kumera zirama incuro ijana.” Ariko aravuga ivyo bintu, arasemerera ati: “Uwuri n’amatwi yo kwumviriza, niyumvirize.”

9 Mugabo abigishwa biwe batangura kumubaza ico ico kigereranyo coba gisobanura. 10 Avuga ati: “Mwebwe mwarahawe gutegera amabanga meranda y’ubwami bw’Imana, mugabo ku bandi ni mu bigereranyo, kugira ngo naho baraba, barabire ubusa, kandi naho bumva, ntibatahure insobanuro. 11 Ehe rero insobanuro y’ico kigereranyo: Imbuto ni ijambo ry’Imana. 12 Iz’iruhande y’inzira ni abumvise, maze Shetani* akaza, agakura ijambo mu mitima yabo kugira ngo ntibemere ngo bakizwe. 13 Izo ku gitandara ni bamwe iyo bumvise iryo jambo baryakirana akanyamuneza, mugabo abo nta muzi bagira; bemera mu gihe gitoyi, mugabo mu gihe c’ukugeragezwa bakagwa. 14 Nayo izaguye mu mahwa, abo ni abumvise, mugabo, kubera ugutwarwa n’amaganya n’ubutunzi n’ibinezereza vy’ubu buzima, bakanyohwa buhere ntihagire ico bashikana ku gutungana. 15 Nayo izo mw’ivu ryiza, abo ni bamwe iyo bamaze kwumva iryo jambo n’umutima mwiza kandi umeze neza baheza bakarigumya, bakama ivyamwa bihanganye.

16 “Nta n’umwe iyo amaze kudomeka itara aryubikako ikintu canke ngo arishire munsi y’igitanda, ahubwo arishira ku gitereko caryo, kugira abinjira babone umuco. 17 Kuko ata kintu na kimwe canyegejwe kitazokwibonekeza, canke ikintu na kimwe cahishijwe bimwe vyitondewe kitazokwigera kimenyekana, ngo nticigere kija ahabona. 18 Nuko rero, mwitwararike ukuntu mwumviriza; kuko uwo wese afise azokwongerwa, mugabo uwo wese adafise, n’ico yiyumvira ko afise azocakwa.”

19 Buno nyina wiwe na bene wabo baza bamugana, mugabo ntibashobora kumushikira kubera isinzi. 20 Ariko bamumenyesha bati: “Nyoko wawe na bene wanyu bahagaze hanze bashaka kukubona.” 21 Abishura ati: “Mawe na bene wacu ni aba bumva ijambo ry’Imana bakarishira mu ngiro.”

22 Kuri umwe muri iyo misi, we hamwe n’abigishwa biwe burira ubwato, hanyuma ababwira ati: “Tujabuke tuje ku yindi nkengera y’ikiyaga.” Nuko barasoza. 23 Mugabo bariko baragenda arasinzira. Buno ikirura c’umuyaga gikaze cisuka ku kiyaga, ubwato barimwo butangura kwuzura amazi, baja mu kaga. 24 Amaherezo baja aho ari baramukangura, bavuga bati: “Mwigisha, Mwigisha, tugira duhone!” Arikangura, akankamira nya muyaga na nya gusuriranya kw’amazi, bica birahwama, haratekana. 25 Maze ababwira ati: “Ukwizera kwanyu kuri hehe?” Mugabo batekerwa n’ubwoba, baratangara, babwirana bati: “None uyu ni nde, ko ategeka n’imiyaga be n’amazi, bikamugamburukira?”

26 Hanyuma bashika ku nkengera y’ikiyaga mu gihugu c’Abageraseni giherereye ku ruhande rurabana na Galilaya. 27 Mugabo yururutse i musozi, umugabo umwe avuye mu gisagara yarimwo amadayimoni aza kumusanganira. Kandi yari amaze igihe kitari gito ata mpuzu yambara, ntabe i muhira, ariko akaba hagati mu mva. 28 Abonye Yezu, avuza induru, yikubita imbere yiwe, hanyuma n’ijwi rirenga avuga ati: “Mpuriye ku ki nawe, ga Yezu Mwana wa ya Mana Musumbavyose? Ndakwinginze, ntumbabaze.” 29 (Kuko yari yategetse nya mpwemu ihumanye kuva muri uwo muntu. Kuko yari imaze igihe kirekire imugumije, kandi kenshi akaboheshwa iminyororo ku maguru no ku maboko bamurinze, mugabo akavutagura nya mvuto hanyuma akajanwa na nya dayimoni mu gahinga.) 30 Yezu amubaza ati: “Witwa nde?” Na we avuga ati: “Legiyoni,” kubera ko amadayimoni menshi yari yaramwinjiyemwo. 31 Kandi yaguma amwinginga ngo ntayategeke kuja mu nyenga. 32 Ico gihe ubusho bw’ingurube nyinshi bwariko burarisha ku musozi; nuko amwinginga ngo ayemerere kwinjira muri zo. Na we arayemerera. 33 Maze nya madayimoni ava muri wa muntu yinjira muri za ngurube, nya busho na bwo buca bushunguruka imanga bwitera mu kiyaga, burasoma nturi. 34 Mugabo aho abungere baboneye ibibaye, barahunga, bavyigana mu gisagara no mu gihugu hagati.

35 Maze abantu baja kuraba ivyari vyabaye, hanyuma baza kuri Yezu basanga wa mugabo amadayimoni yavamwo ari ng’aho, yambaye, aroranye mu muzirikanyi, yicaye imbere y’ibirenge vya Yezu; baca batekerwa n’ubwoba. 36 Abari babibonye babiganira ingene nya mugabo yari yiziziwe n’amadayimoni yakijijwe. 37 Nuko abantu indiri bose bavuye mu gihugu kihakikuje c’Abageraseni bamusaba kubaviraho, kubera ko bari batekewe n’ubwoba bwinshi. Maze yurira ubwato aragenda. 38 Ariko nya mugabo amadayimoni yari yavuyemwo aguma amwinginga ngo agumane na we; mugabo arungika nya mugabo, ati: 39 “Subira i muhira, ugume wigana ibintu vyose Imana yakugiriye.” Nuko aragenda, atangaza mu gisagara hose ibintu vyose Yezu yamugiriye.

40 Yezu agarutse, isinzi rimwakirana ubugwaneza kuko bose bari bamwiteze. 41 Mugabo, haza umugabo yitwa Yayiro, kandi uwo mugabo yari umukuru ahagarikiye isinagogi. Yikubita imbere y’ibirenge vya Yezu, atangura kumwinginga ngo yinjire mu nzu iwe, 42 kubera ko yari afise umukobwa w’ikinege w’imyaka nka cumi n’ibiri, akaba yari hafi gupfa.

Igihe ariko aragenda, amasinzi aramugunda impande zose. 43 Maze umugore yari amaze imyaka cumi n’ibiri ava amaraso, akaba ata muntu n’umwe yari yarashoboye kumukiza, 44 aregera aturutse inyuma, akora ku buyonga bw’impuzu yiwe yo hejuru, maze ako kanya nyene kwa kuvirirana kwiwe guca kurahagarara. 45 Nuko Yezu avuga ati: “Ni nde yankozeko?” Igihe bose bariko barabihakana, Petero avuga ati: “Mwigisha, amasinzi ariko araguhundarako yongera akubanda.” 46 Yamara Yezu avuga ati: “Hariho umuntu yankozeko, kuko numvise ko hari ububasha bwamvuyemwo.” 47 Nya mugore abonye yuko atabuze kumenyekana, aca araza ajugumira, yikubita hasi imbere yiwe, ahishura imbere y’abantu bose icatumye amukorako n’ingene yaciye akira ako kanya nyene. 48 Mugabo amubwira ati: “Mukobwa wanje, ukwizera kwawe kuragukijije; niwigire amahoro.”

49 Akiriko aravuga, haza umuntu umwe aserukira umukuru ahagarikiye isinagogi, avuga ati: “Umukobwa wawe yapfuye; ntiwongere kugora umwigisha.” 50 Yezu avyumvise, amwishura ati: “Ntutinye, nugire ukwizera gusa, arakira.” 51 Ashitse kuri nya nzu, ntiyareka ngo hagire n’umwe binjirana atari Petero na Yohani na Yakobo hamwe na se na nyina ba nya mukobwa. 52 Mugabo abantu bose bariko bararira bongera bikubitagura ku gikiriza kubera intuntu abateye. Nuko avuga ati: “Nimureke kurira, kuko atapfuye, ahubwo arasinziriye.” 53 Baca batangura kumutwenga bamushinyagurira, kuko bari bazi yuko yapfuye. 54 Mugabo amufata ukuboko hanyuma arahamagara, ati: “Mukobwa wanje, haguruka!” 55 Hanyuma impwemu yiwe iragaruka, aca arahaguruka ako kanya nyene, aca ategeka ko bamuha ico arya. 56 Emwe, abavyeyi biwe bagwa mu gahundwe; mugabo abategeka kutagira n’umwe babwira ivyari vyabaye.

^ Luka 8:12 Dufatiye ku kigiriki ni “Diyabolosi,” bisobanura ngo “Umubesheranyi.” Raba kandi Inyongera ya 21.
