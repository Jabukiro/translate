Luka  9 : 1 - 62

9 Maze akoranya bamwe cumi na babiri, hanyuma abaha ubushobozi n’ububasha ku madayimoni, no kugira ngo bakize indwara. 2 Nuko abatuma kwamamaza ubwami bw’Imana no gukiza, 3 maze ababwira ati: “Ntimugire ikintu na kimwe mwitwaza ku bw’urugendo, yaba inkoni canke umupfuko w’ibifungurwa, canke umukate canke amafeza; canke ngo mugire impuzu zibiri zo munsi. 4 Mugabo aho hose mwinjiye mu nzu, muhagume hanyuma muhave. 5 Kandi aho hose abantu batabakiriye, muvuye muri ico gisagara mukunkumure umukungugu ku birenge vyanyu kugira ngo bibe intahe ibagiriza.” 6 Maze bafata inzira baca muri ako karere bava mu kigwati baja mu kindi, bamenyesha inkuru nziza bongera bakiza indwara ahantu hose.

7 Buno Herodi umutware w’intara yumva ibintu vyose biriko biraba, arazazanirwa cane kubera bamwe bariko bavuga yuko Yohani yari yazuwe mu bapfuye, 8 mugabo abandi bakavuga ko Eliya yari yabonetse, mugabo abandi na bo bakavuga ko umwe mu bahanuzi ba kera yari yazutse. 9 Herodi avuga ati: “Yohani naramuciye umutwe. None uwo numvako ibintu mwene ivyo ni nde?” Nuko arondera kumubona.

10 Hanyuma intumwa zigarutse zimwiganira ibintu vyose zakoze. Aca arazijana, arazihereranya mu gisagara citwa Betsayida. 11 Mugabo amasinzi abimenye, aramukurikira. Hanyuma ayakirana ubugwaneza aca atangura kuyabwira ivyerekeye ubwami bw’Imana, yongera akiza abari bakeneye gukizwa. 12 Maze umusi utangura kwira. Bamwe cumi na babiri buno baraza, bamubwira bati: “Rungika iryo sinzi kugira ngo rigende mu bigwati no mu gihugu hagati mu micungararo ryironse uburaro rironke n’ibifungurwa, kuko ng’aha turi mu gahinga.” 13 Mugabo ababwira ati: “Be ari mwebwe mubaha ico barya.” Bavuga bati: “Nta kindi dufise atari imikate itanu n’ifi zibiri, kiretse kumbure twebwe ubwacu tugiye tukagurira ivyo kurya aba bantu bose.” 14 Kukaba nkako, bari abagabo nk’ibihumbi bitanu. Mugabo abwira abigishwa biwe ati: “Nimubicarike nko mu gihe co gufungura, mu migwi y’abantu nka mirongo itanitanu.” 15 Babigira kuno, barabicarika bose. 16 Maze atora ya mikate itanu na za fi zibiri, arangamiza mw’ijuru, arabihezagira hanyuma arabimanyagura, aca atangura kubiha abigishwa ngo babishire imbere ya nya sinzi. 17 Nuko bose bararya barahaga, hanyuma ibisagutse baravyegeranya, haboneka ibisimbo cumi na bibiri vy’utumanyu.

18 Mu nyuma, igihe yariko arasenga ari wenyene, abigishwa bakoranira kuri we, hanyuma arababaza, ati: “Amasinzi avuga ko ndi nde?” 19 Bishura bati: “Bamwe ngo uri Yohani Umubatizi; mugabo abandi ngo uri Eliya, abandi na bo bakavuga yuko umwe mu bahanuzi ba kera yazutse.” 20 Maze ababwira ati: “Mweho none muvuga ko ndi nde?” Petero yishura ati: “Uri Kristu w’Imana.” 21 Maze abategeka abihanikiriza kutagira n’umwe babibwira, 22 mugabo avuga ati: “Umwana w’umuntu ategerezwa gushikirwa n’imibabaro myinshi no kwiyamirizwa n’abagabo b’inararibonye n’abakuru b’abaherezi be n’abanditsi, hanyuma akicwa, maze ku musi ugira gatatu akazurwa.”

23 Maze abandanya kubwira bose ati: “Nimba umuntu ashaka kunkurikira, niyiyanke atore igiti co kubabarizwako ciwe imisi yose, hanyuma agume ankurikira. 24 Kuko uwushaka gukiza ubuzima bwiwe azobubura; mugabo uwuheba ubuzima bwiwe kubera jewe azobukiza. 25 Mu vy’ukuri, umuntu vyomumarira iki yungutse isi yose, mugabo agahomba ubuzima bwiwe canke akamererwa nabi? 26 Kuko uwo wese aterwa isoni nanje be n’amajambo yanje, Umwana w’umuntu azoterwa isoni n’uyo muntu niyashika mu buninahazwa bwiwe n’ubwa Se n’ubw’abamarayika beranda. 27 Mugabo ndababwiye ukuri: Hariho bamwe mu bahagaze ng’aha batazohonja urupfu na gato batabanje kubona ubwami bw’Imana.”

28 Nkako, imisi nk’umunani inyuma y’ayo majambo, ahitana Petero na Yohani na Yakobo hanyuma aduga ku musozi gusenga. 29 Igihe yariko arasenga, mu maso hiwe hahinduka ukundi, ivyambarwa vyiwe na vyo birererana bica ibibatsi. 30 Vyongeye, ehe abagabo babiri bariko barayaga na we, bakaba bari Musa na Eliya. 31 Abo, baboneka bari n’ubuninahazwa hanyuma batangura kuganira ivyerekeye ukugenda kwiwe yabwirizwa gushitsa i Yeruzalemu. 32 Buno Petero n’abari kumwe na we bari baremerewe n’itiro; mugabo aho bamariye kwikangura koko, babona ubuninahazwa bwiwe na ba bagabo babiri bahagararanye na we. 33 Nuko igihe bano bariko baratandukana na we, Petero abwira Yezu ati: “Mwigisha, ni vyiza kuba turi ng’aha; reka rero dushinge amahema atatu, rimwe ryawe, irindi rya Musa, irindi rya Eliya,” mugabo adatahura ivyo yariko aravuga. 34 Mugabo ariko aravuga ivyo bintu, igicu kirirema hanyuma gitangura kubatwikiriza igitutu. Binjiye muri ico gicu, bagira ubwoba. 35 Hanyuma ijwi riva muri nya gicu, rivuga riti: “Uyu ni Umwana wanje, uwatowe. Nimumwumvirize.” 36 Hanyuma iryo jwi ryumvikanye, Yezu aboneka ari wenyene. Mugabo baguma banumye kandi ntibagira umuntu n’umwe biganira muri iyo misi ikintu na kimwe mu vyo babonye.

37 Ku musi ukurikira, bamanutse bavuye kuri nya musozi, isinzi rinini riza kumusanganira. 38 Maze umugabo umwe arasemerera ari muri nya sinzi, ati: “Mwigisha, ndakwinginze undabire umuhungu wanje, kuko ndamufise ari ikinege, 39 kandi ehe, impwemu iramufata, buno nyene agakoma akaruru, hanyuma ikamuhotagura akabimba ifuro, kandi imuvamwo bigoranye imaze kumutera inguma. 40 Nanje ninginze abigishwa bawe ngo bayirukane, mugabo ntibabishoboye.” 41 Yezu yishura ati: “Ewe wa yaruka ritagira ukwizera kandi rigoramanze, nzogumana namwe kugeza ryari? Nzobihanganira kugeza ryari? Zana umuhungu wawe ng’aha.” 42 Mugabo n’igihe nyene yariko aregera, nya dayimoni imukubita hasi iramuhotagura bimwe bikaze. Ariko Yezu akankamira nya mpwemu ihumanye hanyuma akiza wa muhungu, maze amushikiriza se wiwe. 43 Erega, bose bajorezwa n’ububasha bw’agakomeye bw’Imana.

Buno igihe bose bariko baratangarira ibintu vyose yariko arakora, abwira abigishwa biwe ati: 44 “Mushire aya majambo mu matwi yanyu, kuko Umwana w’umuntu abwirizwa gutangwa mu maboko y’abantu.” 45 Mugabo baguma badategera iryo jambo. Nkako, bararihishijwe ngo ntibaritahure, na bo baratinya kumubaza ivy’iryo jambo.

46 Maze ivyiyumviro bibinjiramwo ku bijanye n’uwobaye mukuru muri bo. 47 Yezu amenya ivyiyumviro biri mu mitima yabo, afata umwana mutoyi, amushira iruhande yiwe, 48 hanyuma ababwira ati: “Uwo wese yakiriye uyu mwana mutoyi mw’izina ryanje, na jewe aba anyakiriye, kandi uwo wese anyakiriye aba yakiriye n’uwantumye. Kuko uwigenza nk’umuto muri mwebwe mwese ari we mukuru.”

49 Yohani aca asubizayo ati: “Mwigisha, twabonye umuntu yariko yirukana amadayimoni akoresheje izina ryawe, natwe tugerageza kumubuza, kubera atari kumwe natwe.” 50 Mugabo Yezu amubwira ati: “Ntimugerageze kumubuza, kuko uwutabarwanya abashigikiye.”

51 Imisi iri mu gukwira ngo aduganwe, yerekeza amaso yiwe i Yeruzalemu yiyemeje kujayo. 52 Nuko atuma intumwa ngo zimwitangire imbere. Na zo ziragenda zinjira mu gisagara c’Abasamariya, kumugirira imyiteguro; 53 mugabo ntibamwakira, kubera ko amaso yiwe yari yerekeje i Yeruzalemu ngo ajeyo. 54 Abigishwa Yakobo na Yohani ngo babibone, bavuga bati: “Mukama, ushaka tubwire umuriro umanuke uvuye mw’ijuru ubarigate?” 55 Mugabo arahindukira arabahambarira. 56 Nuko baja mu kindi kigwati.

57 Buno bari mu nzira bagenda, umuntu umwe amubwira ati: “Nzogukurikira iyo uzoja hose.” 58 Yezu na we amubwira ati: “Imbwebwe zirafise imyobo n’inyoni zo mu kirere zikagira ubwikingo, mugabo Umwana w’umuntu we ntagira aho arambika umutwe.” 59 Maze abwira uwundi ati: “Nkurikira.” Nya muntu avuga ati: “Ndeka mbanze ngende mpambe dawe.” 60 Mugabo amubwira ati: “Reka abapfu bahambe abapfu babo, mugabo wewe genda umenyekanishe hose ubwami bw’Imana.” 61 Hanyuma uwundi na we avuga ati: “Ndagukurikira, Mukama; mugabo banza undeke nsezere abo mu rugo rwanje.” 62 Yezu amubwira ati: “Nta muntu ashize ukuboko kw’isuka irimishwa n’ibitungwa araba ibiri inyuma, abereye ubwami bw’Imana.”
