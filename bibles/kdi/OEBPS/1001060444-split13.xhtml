Yohani  13 : 1 - 38

13 Yezu rero, kubera ko yari azi imbere y’umusi mukuru wa pasika yuko isaha yiwe yari igeze y’uko ava muri iyi si akaja kwa Se, kubera yari yarakunze abiwe bari mw’isi, yarabakunze gushika kw’iherezo. 2 Bakibandanya kwihereza imfungurwa zo ku mugoroba, Shetani* akaba yari amaze gushira mu mutima wa Yuda Isikariyoti, mwene Simoni, ivyo kumugura, 3 we, kubera yari azi ko Se yari yashize ibintu vyose mu maboko yiwe be n’uko yaje avuye ku Mana akaba kandi yariko aja ku Mana, 4 arahaguruka bari kuri nya mfungurwa, arambika hambavu impuzu ziwe zo hejuru. Hanyuma atora igitambara, arakenyera. 5 Ashira amazi mw’ibesani, atangura kwoza ibirenge vy’abigishwa no kuvyumutsa akoresheje nya gitambara yari akenyeje. 6 Nuko ashikira Simoni Petero. Uno amubwira ati: “Mukama, unyoze ibirenge?” 7 Yezu amwishura ati: “Ico ndiko ndakora ntugitegera ubu, mugabo uzotegera inyuma y’ibi bintu.” 8 Petero amubwira ati: “Oya ntuzogenda unyogeje ibirenge.” Yezu amwishura ati: “Ntakwogeje, nta co uzoba usangiye nanje.” 9 Simoni Petero amubwira ati: “Mukama, ntibibe ibirenge vyanje gusa ahubwo n’amaboko be n’umutwe.” 10 Yezu amubwira ati: “Uwiyuhagiye nta kindi aba akeneye kwozwa atari ibirenge, mugabo aba atyoroye wese. Kandi mwebwe muratyoroye, ariko si mwese.” 11 Yari azi umuntu yagira amugure. Ni co catumye avuga ati: “Si mwese mutyoroye.”

12 Aho amariye kuboza ibirenge no kwambara impuzu yiwe yo hejuru akongera kwicara ku meza, ababwira ati: “Murazi ico ndabagiriye? 13 Mwebwe munyita ‘Mwigisha’ na ‘Mukama,’ kandi muvuga vyo, kuko ari ko ndi. 14 Nimba rero jewe, naho ndi Umukama n’Umwigisha, ndabogeje ibirenge, na mwebwe mubwirizwa kwozanya ibirenge. 15 Kuko ndabahaye icitegererezo, kugira ngo nk’uko ndabagiriye, abe ari ko namwe mugiriranira. 16 Ni ukuri kwose ndabibabwiye: Umushumba ntaruta shebuja, eka n’uwatumwe ntaruta uwamurungitse. 17 Nimba muzi ivyo bintu, muzoba muhiriwe mubikoze. 18 Sindiko mvuga mwebwe mwese; ndazi abo natoye. Mugabo ni ukugira ngo Icanditswe kiranguke, ngo: ‘Uwahora arya ku mukate wanje ambanguriye igitsintsiri.’ 19 Guhera kano kanya ndabibabwiye bitaraba, kugira ngo nivyashika koko mwemere yuko ndi we. 20 Ni ukuri kwose ndabibabwiye: Uwakiriye uwo ndungitse wese na jewe aba anyakiriye. Uwunyakiriye na we, aba yakiriye n’uwandungitse.”

21 Yezu amaze kuvuga ivyo, abura amahoro mu mutima, ashinga intahe, ati: “Ni ukuri kwose ndabibabwiye: Umwe muri mwebwe arangura.” 22 Abigishwa batangura kurabana, bazazaniwe bibaza uwo yariko abivugako uwo ari we. 23 Imbere y’igikiriza ca Yezu hari hicaye umwe mu bigishwa biwe, kandi Yezu yaramukunda. 24 Simoni Petero rero agirira uyo ikimenyetso n’umutwe, amubwira ati: “Tubwire uwo ariko abivugako.” 25 Nuko uno yegamira igikiriza ca Yezu, amubwira ati: “Mukama, ni nde?” 26 Yezu rero yishura ati: “Ni uwo nza guha ikimanyu ndumvye.” Nuko rero, amaze kudumba nya kimanyu, aragifata agiha Yuda, mwene Simoni Isikariyoti. 27 Yuda amaze kwakira ico kimanyu, Shetani aca amwinjiramwo. Yezu rero amubwira ati: “Ico uriko urakora gikore ningoga.” 28 Ariko nta n’umwe mu bari ku meza yamenye igituma amubwiye ivyo. 29 Nkako, kubera ko Yuda ari we yari afise isandugu y’amahera, bamwe bariko biyumvira ko Yezu yariko amubwira ati: “Gura ivyo dukeneye ku bw’umusi mukuru,” canke yuko akwiye kugira ico ahaye aboro. 30 Amaze kwakira ca kimanyu rero, buno nyene arasohoka. Kandi hari mw’ijoro.

31 Amaze gusohoka rero, Yezu avuga ati: “Ubu rero Umwana w’umuntu araninahajwe, kandi Imana iraninahajwe ku bijanye na we. 32 Kandi Imana ubwayo izomuninahaza, kandi izomuninahaza bidatevye. 33 Bana bato, ndacari kumwe namwe kandi gatoyi. Muzondondera; kandi nk’uko nabwiye Abayuda nti: ‘Iyo nja ntimushobora kuhaza,’ na mwebwe ndabibabwiye ubu. 34 Ndabahaye ibwirizwa rishasha: mukundane. Nk’uko nabakunze, abe ari ko namwe mukundana. 35 Iki ni co bose bazomenyerako yuko muri abigishwa banje: nimwagiriranira urukundo hagati yanyu.”

36 Simoni Petero amubwira ati: “Mukama, uriko uja hehe?” Yezu yishura ati: “Iyo ndiko ndaja ntushobora kunkurikira ubu, mugabo uzonkurikira mu nyuma.” 37 Petero amubwira ati: “Mukama, kubera iki ntashobora kugukurikira ubu? Nzokwigura.” 38 Yezu yishura ati: “Uzonyigura? Ni ukuri kwose ndabikubwiye: Isake ntigenda ibitse utaranyihakana gatatu.”

^ Yoh. 13:2 Dufatiye ku kigiriki ni “Diyabolosi,” bisobanura ngo “Umubesheranyi.” Raba kandi Inyongera ya 21.
