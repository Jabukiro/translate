Yohani  17 : 1 - 26

17 Yezu avuga ivyo bintu, hanyuma araramurira amaso mw’ijuru, avuga ati: “Data, isaha irageze; ninahaza umwana wawe, kugira ngo umwana wawe akuninahaze, 2 nk’uko wamuhaye ububasha ku kiri n’umubiri cose, kugira ngo ku vyerekeye igitigiri cose c’abo wamuhaye, abahe ubuzima budahera. 3 Kugira ngo baronke ubuzima budahera, ni uko baguma bironsa ubumenyi bwerekeye wewe, Imana yonyene y’ukuri, n’ubwerekeye uwo watumye, Yezu Kristu. 4 Narakuninahaje kw’isi, nkaba ndangije igikorwa wampaye ngo nkore. 5 Ubu rero ga Data, nuninaharize hamwe nawe, uninaharishije bwa buninahazwa nari mfise ndi kumwe nawe imbere y’uko isi ibaho.

6 “Narahishuriye izina ryawe abantu wampaye ubakuye mw’isi. Bari abawe, urabampa, kandi barubahirije ijambo ryawe. 7 Ubu baramenye yuko ibintu vyose wampaye biva kuri wewe; 8 kubera ko amajambo wampaye nayabahaye, kandi barayakiriye baramenya vy’ukuri yuko naje nguserukira, kandi baremeye yuko wantumye. 9 Ndabasabira; sinsabira isi, ahubwo nsabira abo wampaye; kubera ko ari abawe, 10 kandi ivyanje vyose bikaba ari ivyawe n’ivyawe bikaba ari ivyanje, kandi naraninahajwe muri bo.

11 “Vyongeye, sinkiri mw’isi, mugabo bo bari mw’isi, nanje ndiko ndaza kuri wewe. Data mweranda, ubarinde ku bw’izina ryawe wampaye, kugira ngo babe umwe* nk’uko turi. 12 Nkiri kumwe na bo narabarinda ku bw’izina ryawe wampaye; kandi narabazigamye, kandi nta n’umwe muri bo yarandutse atari wa mwana wo kuranduka, kugira ngo icanditswe kiranguke. 13 Mugabo ubu ndiko ndaza iwawe, kandi ndiko mvuga ibi bintu mw’isi kugira ngo bagire akanyamuneza kanje muri bo bimwe bishitse. 14 Narabahaye ijambo ryawe, mugabo isi yarabanse, kubera ko atari ab’isi, nk’uko nanje ntari uw’isi.

15 “Singusaba ngo ubakure mw’isi, ahubwo ngo ubarinde kubera wa mubisha. 16 Si ab’isi, nk’uko nanje ntari uw’isi. 17 Ubeze biciye ku kuri; ijambo ryawe ni ukuri. 18 Nk’uko wantumye mw’isi, na jewe narabatumye mw’isi. 19 Kandi ndiko ndiyeza ku bwabo, ngo na bo nyene bezwe biciye ku kuri.

20 “Sinsabira aba bonyene, ahubwo nsabira n’abanyizera biciye kw’ijambo ryabo; 21 kugira ngo bose babe umwe, nk’uko wewe, Data, wunze ubumwe nanje, nanje nkunga ubumwe nawe, ngo na bo nyene bunge ubumwe natwe, kugira ngo isi yemere yuko wantumye. 22 Vyongeye, narabahaye ubuninahazwa wampaye, kugira ngo babe umwe nk’uko turi umwe. 23 Jewe nkunga ubumwe na bo, nawe ukunga ubumwe nanje, kugira ngo batunganywe babe umwe, ngo isi imenye yuko ari wewe wantumye be n’uko wabakunze nk’uko wankunze. 24 Data, ku co wampaye, nshaka yuko aho ndi, na bo haba ari ho baba kumwe nanje, kugira ngo babone ubuninahazwa bwanje wampaye, kubera ko wankunze imbere y’ishingwa ry’isi. 25 Data ugororotse, isi koko ntiyakumenye; mugabo jewe narakumenye, kandi aba baramenye yuko wantumye. 26 Kandi narabamenyesheje izina ryawe, kandi nzorimenyekanisha, kugira ngo urukundo wankunze rube muri bo nanje nunge ubumwe na bo.”

^ Yoh. 17:11 Raba Yoh. 10:30, akajambo k’epfo.
