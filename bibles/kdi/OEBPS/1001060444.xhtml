Yohani  1 : 1 - 51

Nk’uko vyanditswe na Yohani

1 Mu ntango Jambo yariho, kandi Jambo yari kumwe n’Imana, kandi Jambo yari imana. 2 Uyo mu ntango yari kumwe n’Imana. 3 Ibintu vyose vyabayeho biciye kuri we, kandi bidaciye kuri we nta n’ikintu na kimwe cabayeho.

Icabayeho 4 biciye kuri we bwari ubuzima, kandi ubuzima bwari umuco w’abantu. 5 Kandi umuco urakayanganira mu mwiza, mugabo umwiza ntuwutsinda.

6 Hahagurutse umuntu yari yatumwe ngo aserukire Imana: izina ryiwe ryari Yohani. 7 Uyo muntu yaje ku bw’intahe, kugira ngo ashingire intahe umuco, ngo abantu b’uburyo bwose bemere biciye kuri we. 8 Ntiyari uwo muco, mugabo yari uwo gushingira intahe uwo muco.

9 Umuco w’ukuri uha umuco abantu b’uburyo bwose wagira uze mw’isi. 10 Yari mw’isi, kandi isi yabayeho biciye kuri we, mugabo isi ntiyamumenye. 11 Yaje iwe, mugabo abiwe ntibamwakiriye. 12 Ariko abamwakiriye bose yabahaye ububasha bwo gucika abana b’Imana, kubera ko bari bizeye izina ryiwe; 13 kandi ntibavyawe n’amaraso canke ukugomba kw’umubiri canke ukugomba kw’umuntu, ahubwo bavyawe n’Imana.

14 Nuko Jambo acika umubiri, aba hagati muri twebwe, kandi twaritegereje ubuninahazwa bwiwe, ubuninahazwa nk’ubw’umwana w’ikinege ava kuri se; kandi yari yuzuye ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye be n’ukuri. 15 (Yohani yaramushingiye intahe, egome, mu vy’ukuri yarasemereye—uyo ni we yabivuze—ati: “Uwuriko araza inyuma yanje yarandenganye, kuko yabayeho imbere yanje.”) 16 Kuko twese twaronse bivuye ku kunengesera kwiwe, egome, ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye ku buntu ata wari abukwiriye. 17 Kubera ko Itegeko ryatanzwe biciye kuri Musa, ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye be n’ukuri vyaje biciye kuri Yezu Kristu. 18 Nta muntu n’umwe yigeze kubona Imana; wa mwana w’ikinege* ari mu gikiriza ca Se ni we yayisiguye.

19 Iyi rero ni yo ntahe ya Yohani igihe Abayuda batuma kuri we abaherezi n’Abalewi bavuye i Yeruzalemu kumubaza bati: “Uri nde?” 20 Kandi yaremeye icese ntiyahakana, mugabo yemera icese ati: “Jewe sindi Kristu.” 21 Na bo bamubaza bati: “Ni gute none? Uri Eliya?” Na we ati: “Sindi we.” “Uri wa Muhanuzi none?” Na we arishura ati: “Oya!” 22 Nuko rero bamubwira bati: “Uri nde? kugira ngo duhe inyishu abaturungitse. Wewe wivugako iki?” 23 Avuga ati: “Ndi ijwi ry’umuntu ariko arasemerera mu gahinga, ati: ‘Nimugorore inzira ya Yehova,’ nk’uko Yesaya umuhanuzi yavuze.” 24 Ico gihe abari batumwe bari abo mu Bafarizayo*. 25 Nuko bamubaza ibibazo, bamubwira bati: “Ni kuki none ubatiza nimba ubwawe utari Kristu canke Eliya canke wa Muhanuzi?” 26 Yohani arabishura ati: “Jewe mbatiza mu mazi. Hagati muri mwebwe harahagaze umuntu mutazi, 27 umwe ariko araza inyuma yanje, mugabo umugozi w’inkweto* yiwe ntibinkwiriye kuwupfundurura.” 28 Ivyo vyabereye i Betaniya hakurya ya Yorodani, aho Yohani yariko arabatiriza.

29 Bukeye abona Yezu aza amugana, avuga ati: “Raba, Umwagazi w’intama w’Imana ukuraho igicumuro c’isi! 30 Uyo ni umwe navuga ku bimwerekeye nti: Inyuma yanje hariko haraza umugabo yandenganye, kubera ko yabayeho imbere yanje. 31 Na jewe sinari ndamuzi, mugabo icatumye nza mbatiza mu mazi kwari ukugira ngo ahishurirwe Isirayeli.” 32 Yohani kandi yarashinze intahe, ati: “Nabonye impwemu imanuka nk’inuma, ivuye mw’ijuru, hanyuma iguma kuri we. 33 Na jewe sinari ndamuzi, mugabo Umwe nyene yandungika kubatiza mu mazi ni we yambwiye ati: ‘Uwo wese ubona impwemu imanuka kuri we ikamugumako, uwo ni we abatiza mu mpwemu nyeranda.’ 34 Nanje narabibonye, kandi narashinze intahe yuko uwo ari we Mwana w’Imana.”

35 Bukeye na ho nyene, Yohani yari ahagararanye na babiri mu bigishwa biwe, 36 hanyuma yitegereje Yezu ariko aragendagenda, avuga ati: “Raba, Umwagazi w’intama w’Imana!” 37 Nya bigishwa babiri baramwumva avuga, baca bakurikira Yezu. 38 Maze Yezu arahindukira, hanyuma ababonye bariko baramukurikira, ababwira ati: “Murondera iki?” Bamubwira bati: “Rabi (bikaba bisobanura ngo Mwigisha), uba hehe?” 39 Ababwira ati: “Ingo, murahabona.” Nuko baragenda, barabona aho yaba, baraheza bagumana na we uyo musi; hari nko kw’isaha y’icumi. 40 Andereya mwene wabo na Simoni Petero yari umwe muri abo babiri bumva ivyo Yohani avuze bagaca bakurikira Yezu. 41 Uwo yabanje kurondera mwene wabo bwite Simoni, amubwira ati: “Twabonye Mesiya” (bikaba bisobanura ngo Kristu). 42 Amujana kuri Yezu. Yezu amwitegereje avuga ati: “Uri Simoni mwene Yohani; uzokwitwa Kefa” (bikaba bisobanura ngo Petero).

43 Bukeye, yipfuza kuja i Galilaya. Nuko Yezu abona Filipo, amubwira ati: “Nkurikira.” 44 Filipo na we yava i Betsayida, mu gisagara ca Andereya na Petero. 45 Filipo arondera Natanayeli, amubwira ati: “Twabonye umwe Musa yandika ivyiwe mw’Itegeko, n’Abahanuzi bakavyandika, ari we Yezu, wa mwana wa Yozefu, w’i Nazareti.” 46 Mugabo Natanayeli amubwira ati: “Hari ikintu ciza cova i Nazareti?” Filipo amubwira ati: “Ingo urabe.” 47 Yezu abona Natanayeli aza amugana, avuga ku bimwerekeye ati: “Raba, Umwisirayeli vy’ukuri, atarimwo ubuhendanyi.” 48 Natanayeli amubwira ati: “Jewe wamenye gute?” Yezu amwishura ati: “Imbere y’uko Filipo aguhamagara, igihe wari munsi y’igiti c’umusukoni, nakubonye.” 49 Natanayeli aramwishura ati: “Rabi, uri Umwana w’Imana, uri Umwami wa Isirayeli.” 50 Yezu amwishura ati: “Wemeye kubera ndakubwiye yuko nakubonye munsi y’igiti c’umusukoni? Uzobona ibiruta ivyo.” 51 Yongera kumubwira ati: “Ni ukuri kwose ndabibabwiye, muzobona ijuru ryuguruwe, n’abamarayika b’Imana baduga bongera bamanuka ku Mwana w’umuntu.”

^ Yoh. 1:18 Dufatiye ku vyandiko vy’iminwe vy’ikigiriki vya kera cane ni “ya mana y’ikinege.” Mu vyandiko vyo mu nyuma havuga ngo “wa Mwana w’ikinege.”

^ Yoh. 1:24 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ Yoh. 1:27 Canke, “isandali.”
