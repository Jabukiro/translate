Ivyakozwe  15 : 1 - 41

15 Hanyuma abantu bamwe baramanuka bavuye i Yudaya, batangura kwigisha abavukanyi ngo: “Mutagenyerewe nk’uko umugenzo wa Musa uri, ntimushobora gukizwa.” 2 Mugabo aho hamariye kuba ugucanamwo be n’uguharira kutoroshe hagati yabo na Paulo na Barunaba, bashinga yuko Paulo na Barunaba n’abandi bo muri bo baduga ku ntumwa n’abagabo b’inararibonye i Yeruzalemu ku vyerekeye ivyo bihari.

3 Nuko, ishengero rimaze kubaherekeza gatoyi, abo bagabo babandanya urugendo baca i Fenisiya n’i Samariya, bigana ido n’ido uguhinduka kw’abanyamahanga, batera akanyamuneza kenshi abavukanyi bose. 4 Bashitse i Yeruzalemu bakirwa neza n’ishengero n’intumwa n’abagabo b’inararibonye, baraheza babiganira ibintu vyose Imana yari yakoze biciye kuri bo. 5 Yamara, bamwe mu bo mu muce w’idini w’Abafarizayo bari bemeye barahaguruka mu bibanza vyabo, bavuga bati: “Ni nkenerwa kubagenyera no kubategeka kwubahiriza itegeko rya Musa.”

6 Maze intumwa n’abagabo b’inararibonye bakoranira hamwe ngo barabe ivy’ico kibazo. 7 Hamaze kuba ibihari vyinshi, Petero arahaguruka ababwira ati: “Bagabo, bene wacu, murazi neza yuko kuva mu misi yo mu ntango Imana yahisemwo muri mwebwe yuko biciye ku kanwa kanje abanyamahanga bumva ijambo ry’inkuru nziza bakemera; 8 kandi Imana, imwe izi umutima, yarashinze intahe mu kubaha impwemu nyeranda, nk’uko natwe yayiduhaye. 9 Kandi nta tandukaniro na ritoyi yagize hagati yacu na bo, mugabo yatyoroje imitima yabo ukwizera. 10 Ubu none, kubera iki muriko murageza Imana mu kuremeka abigishwa kw’izosi ingǒgo* baba ba sogokuruza canke twebwe ata washoboye kwikorera? 11 Ahubwo riho, twizigiye gukizwa biciye ku buntu ata wari abukwiriye bw’Umukama Yezu kimwe n’abo bantu nyene.”

12 Kuri ivyo nya bantu indiri bose baracereza, batangura kwumviriza ingene Barunaba na Paulo bigana ibimenyetso n’ibitangaza vyinshi Imana yakoze mu mahanga biciye kuri bo. 13 Baretse kuvuga, Yakobo arishura ati: “Bagabo, bene wacu, nimunyumve. 14 Simeyoni yiganye ata co asize inyuma ingene Imana yitayeho amahanga ari bwo bwa mbere ngo iyakuremwo igisata ku bw’izina ryayo. 15 Kandi amajambo y’Abahanuzi arahuye n’ivyo, nk’uko vyanditswe ngo: 16 ‘Inyuma y’ivyo bintu nzogaruka nongere kwubaka urusago rwa Dawidi rwatemvye; kandi nzokwongera kwubaka ibihomvagurike vyarwo nongere kuruhagarika, 17 kugira ngo abasigaye bo mu bantu baronderane ubwira Yehova, bari kumwe n’abo mu mahanga yose, igisata citirirwa izina ryanje, ni ko Yehova avuga, ari na we akora ivyo bintu 18 bizwi kuva kera.’ 19 Ni co gituma ingingo yanje ari iyo kutagora abo mu mahanga bariko bahindukirira Imana, 20 ahubwo kubandikira kwirinda ivyahumanijwe n’ibigirwamana, n’ubusambanyi, n’ibinizwe, n’amaraso. 21 Kuko kuva mu bihe vya kera Musa afise mu gisagara no mu kindi abamwamamaza, kubera ko ivyo yanditse bisomwa mu masinagogi n’ijwi ryumvikana buri sabato.”

22 Maze intumwa n’abagabo b’inararibonye hamwe n’ishengero ryose bashima kurungika i Antiyokiya abagabo batowe muri bo, bari kumwe na Paulo na Barunaba, ni bo Yuda ari we yitwa Barusaba be na Silasi, abagabo b’imboneza mu bavukanyi; 23 hanyuma bandika n’ukuboko kwabo bati:

“Intumwa n’abagabo b’inararibonye, abavukanyi, kuri abo bavukanyi b’i Antiyokiya n’i Siriya n’i Silisiya bo mu mahanga: Turabaramukije! 24 Kubera ko twumvise yuko hari abo muri twebwe babadurumbanishije amajambo, bagerageza gutembagaza imitima yanyu kandi ata mabwirizwa twabahaye, 25 twumvikanye duhurije hamwe maze dushima gutora abagabo tubarungikira bari kumwe n’abakundwa bacu Barunaba na Paulo, 26 abantu bahevye ubuzima bwabo ku bw’izina ry’Umukama wacu Yezu Kristu. 27 Turungitse rero Yuda na Silasi, ngo na bo nyene babiganire ku munwa ivyo bintu nyene. 28 Kuko impwemu nyeranda na twebwe ubwacu dushimye kutabongerako uwundi muzigo, kiretse ibi bintu vya nkenerwa: 29 kuguma mwirinze ivyashikaniwe ibigirwamana, n’amaraso, n’ibinizwe, n’ubusambanyi. Nimwaguma mwitandukanije neza n’ivyo bintu, muzotunganirwa. Mugire amagara meza!”

30 Nuko, aho abo bantu babarekeye bakagenda, bamanuka i Antiyokiya, bakoraniriza hamwe nya bantu indiri babaha rya kete. 31 Bamaze kurisoma, banezerezwa n’iyo ndemesho. 32 Hanyuma Yuda na Silasi, kubera yuko na bo nyene ubwabo bari abahanuzi, baremesha bene wacu n’insiguro nyinshi bongera barabakomeza. 33 Nuko, bamazeyo igihe gitoyi, abavukanyi barabareka baragenda amahoro ku bari babatumye. 34* —— 35 Ariko Paulo na Barunaba babandanya kumara igihe i Antiyokiya bigisha bongera bamenyesha inkuru nziza y’ijambo rya Yehova, bari kumwe n’abandi benshi nyene.

36 Haciye imisi mikeyi, Paulo abwira Barunaba ati: “Imbere ya vyose reka dusubireyo tugendere abavukanyi muri kimwekimwe mu bisagara twamamajemwo ijambo rya Yehova, turabe ingene bameze.” 37 Nayo Barunaba, yari yiyemeje kujana na Yohani na we, uwitwa Mariko. 38 Mugabo Paulo ntiyabona ko bibereye yuko bajana n’uyo, kubera ko yari yabataye kuva i Pamfiliya ntajane na bo ku gikorwa. 39 Haca haba ugutomboka kw’ishavu gukaze, ku buryo batandukana; hanyuma Barunaba ajana Mariko, bafata ubwato baja i Shipure. 40 Paulo atora Silasi, maze aragenda abavukanyi bamaze kumuragiza ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye bwa Yehova. 41 Mugabo aca i Siriya n’i Silisiya, akomeza amashengero.

^ Ivyak. 15:10 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ Ivyak. 15:34 Raba Ivk 8:37, akajambo k’epfo.
