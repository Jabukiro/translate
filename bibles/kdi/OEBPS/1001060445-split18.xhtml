Ivyakozwe  18 : 1 - 28

18 Inyuma y’ivyo, ava i Atene aza i Korinto. 2 Abona Umuyuda umwe yitwa Akwila, imvukira y’i Ponto yari aherutse kuva mu Butaliyano, na Pirisila umugore wiwe, kuko Kolode yari yategetse Abayuda bose kuva i Roma. Nuko aja iwabo, 3 kandi kubera yuko bari basangiye umwuga, aguma i muhira iwabo, barakora, kuko umwuga wabo wari uwo kujisha amahema. 4 Ariko yama ashikiriza insiguro mw’isinagogi buri sabato, agatuma Abayuda n’Abagiriki banyurwa.

5 Aho Silasi na Timoteyo bamanukiye bavuye i Makedoniya, Paulo atangura gufatīrwa cane na rya jambo, ashinga intahe ku Bayuda kugira ngo yerekane neza ko Yezu ari we Kristu. 6 Mugabo kubera yuko baguma babirwanya kandi bagatukana, akunkumura impuzu ziwe, ababwira ati: “Amaraso yanyu abe ku mitwe yanyu. Jewe ndacuna. Kuva ubu ngiye ku banyamahanga.” 7 Nuko arimuka ava ng’aho, aja mu nzu y’uwitwa Titiyo Yusito, umuntu yasenga Imana, inzu yiwe ikaba yari ibangikanye n’isinagogi. 8 Mugabo Kirisipo umukuru ahagarikiye isinagogi acika uwemera Umukama, n’abo mu rugo rwiwe bose. Benshi mu Bakorinto bavyumvise batangura kwemera, barabatizwa. 9 Vyongeye, mw’ijoro Umukama abwira Paulo mw’iyerekwa, ati: “Ntutinye, ahubwo guma uvuga, ntucereze, 10 kubera ko ndi kumwe nawe, kandi nta n’umwe azogutera ngo akugirire nabi; kuko mfise abantu benshi muri iki gisagara.” 11 Nuko aguma ng’aho umwaka umwe n’amezi atandatu, yigisha muri bo ijambo ry’Imana.

12 Igihe Galiyo yari buramatari wa Akaya, Abayuda bakurira hasi Paulo bahuje inama, bamujana imbere y’intebe y’imanza, 13 bavuga bati: “Uyu muntu aca kubiri n’amategeko, agatwara abantu ku yindi nyigisho mu vy’ugusenga Imana.” 14 Mugabo Paulo agira ateranure akanwa, Galiyo abwira Abayuda ati: “Iyaba koko ari inabi kanaka canke ikibi kibishe, emwe Bayuda, nari kuba mfise impamvu yo kubihanganira. 15 Mugabo nimba ari ibihari vyo ku majambo, ku mazina no kw’itegeko muri mwebwe, nimuvyirabire. Sinshaka kuba umucamanza w’ivyo bintu.” 16 Kuri ivyo aca arabirukana imbere y’intebe y’imanza. 17 Nuko bose bacakira Sositene umukuru ahagarikiye isinagogi, batangura kumukubitira imbere y’intebe y’imanza. Mugabo Galiyo ntiyitaho ivyo bintu na gatoyi.

18 Ariko Paulo ahamaze iyindi misi itari mike, asezera bene wacu, hanyuma afata ubwato aja i Siriya ari kumwe na Pirisila na Akwila; yari yigabanishije umushatsi i Kenkireya, kuko yari n’indagano. 19 Nuko bashika i Efeso, hanyuma abasigayo; mugabo we ubwiwe yinjira mw’isinagogi, azirikana hamwe n’Abayuda. 20 Naho baguma bamusaba kumarayo ikindi gihe, ntiyavyemeye 21 ahubwo yarabasezeye, ababwira ati: “Nzogaruka iwanyu, nimba Yehova abigomba.” Afata ikiyaga ava i Efeso 22 aramanuka gushika i Sezariya. Hanyuma araduga, aramutsa ishengero, araheza amanuka i Antiyokiya.

23 Amazeyo igihe gitoyi, avayo aragenda, ava hamwe aja ahandi mu gihugu c’i Galatiya n’i Firijiya, akomeza abigishwa bose.

24 Ico gihe Umuyuda umwe yitwa Apolo, imvukira y’i Alegizandiriya, umugabo azi kuvuga, ashika i Efeso; kandi yari umuhanga mu Vyanditswe. 25 Uwo mugabo yari yarigishijwe ku munwa mu nzira ya Yehova, kandi kubera yuko yarurumba impwemu, yaravuga akongera akigisha ivyerekeye Yezu mu buryo bugororotse, mugabo akaba yari azi gusa ibatisimu ya Yohani. 26 Nuko uwo mugabo atangura kuvugana ubutinyutsi mw’isinagogi. Aho Pirisila na Akwila bamwumviye, baramujana baramusigurira neza inzira y’Imana mu buryo bugororotse kuruta. 27 Bitayeko, kubera ko yashaka kujabuka akaja i Akaya, abavukanyi bandikira abigishwa, babahimiriza kumwakirana ubuntu. Nuko ashitseyo, afasha cane abari bemeye kubera ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye bw’Imana; 28 kuko yahinyuza rwose Abayuda ashimitse kandi ku mugaragaro, yongera yerekana akoresheje Ivyanditswe yuko Yezu ari we Kristu.
