Ivyakozwe  6 : 1 - 15

6 Muri iyo misi, igihe abigishwa bariko barongerekana, havyuka imyidodombo mu Bayuda bavuga ikigiriki, bidodombera Abayuda bavuga igiheburayo, kubera ko abapfakazi babo bariko birengagizwa mw’igaburwa ryo ku musi ku musi. 2 Nuko bamwe cumi na babiri bahamagara nya bigishwa indiri, bavuga bati: “Ntibihimbaye ko tureka ijambo ry’Imana ngo tuje mu vyo kugabura ibifungurwa ku meza. 3 Nuko rero, bene wacu, nimwironderere abagabo indwi bashingirwa intahe nziza bo muri mwebwe, buzuye impwemu n’ubukerebutsi, kugira ngo tubashinge iki gikorwa ca nkenerwa; 4 mugabo twebwe tuzokwihebera ugusenga n’ubusuku bwerekeye ijambo ry’Imana.” 5 Iryo jambo rihimbara nya bantu indiri bose, baraheza batora Sitefano umugabo yuzuye ukwizera n’impwemu nyeranda, na Filipo na Purokoro na Nikanori na Timoni na Parumena na Nikola uwinjiye idini ry’Abayuda w’i Antiyokiya; 6 babashira imbere y’intumwa, na zo zimaze gusenga, zibarambikako ibiganza.

7 Ijambo ry’Imana ribandanya rero gukura, igitigiri c’abigishwa na co kiguma kigwira cane i Yeruzalemu; kandi isinzi rinini ry’abaherezi ritangura kugamburuka ukwizera.

8 Nuko Sitefano, yuzuye igikundiro n’ububasha, yariko akora ibitangaza n’ibimenyetso bikomeye mu bantu. 9 Mugabo hahaguruka abantu bamwe bo mw’isinagogi yitwa Isinagogi y’Imbohorwe, n’abo mu Banyasireni no mu Banyalegizandiriya no mu b’i Silisiya no muri Aziya, ngo baharire na Sitefano; 10 yamara ntibashobora guhangana n’ubukerebutsi be n’impwemu yavugana. 11 Maze bosha abagabo mu mpisho kuvuga bati: “Twamwumvise avuga amajambo y’amarogotano kuri Musa no ku Mana.” 12 Baca bagumura abantu be n’abagabo b’inararibonye n’abanditsi, maze baramwubira, baramucakira, bamushira Inama Nkuru. 13 Nuko bazana ivyabona vy’ikinyoma, bivuga biti: “Uyu mugabo ntareka gutuka aha hantu heranda no gutuka Itegeko. 14 Nk’akarorero, twamwumvise avuga yuko uwo Yezu Umunyanazareti azosenyurira hasi aha hantu akongera agahindura imigenzo twaherejwe na Musa.”

15 Maze abicaye bose mu Nama Nkuru bakimuhanze amaso, babona ko mu maso hiwe hameze nko mu maso h’umumarayika.
