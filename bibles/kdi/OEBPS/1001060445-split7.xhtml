Ivyakozwe  7 : 1 - 60

7 Mugabo umuherezi mukuru avuga ati: “Ivyo ni ko biri?” 2 Na we ati: “Bagabo, bene wacu na ba data, nimwumve. Imana y’ubuninahazwa yarabonekeye sogokuruza wacu Aburahamu igihe yari i Mezopotamiya, imbere y’uko agerera i Harani, 3 imubwira iti: ‘Va mu gihugu cawe no mu ncuti zawe uze mu gihugu nzokwereka.’ 4 Maze ava mu gihugu c’Abakaludaya, agerera i Harani. Se wiwe amaze gupfa, Imana imukura ng’aho, ituma yimuka ngo agerere muri iki gihugu mubamwo ubu. 5 Yamara nta tongo ry’intoranwa yamuhaye muri co, oya, habe n’ahangana n’ubwaguke bw’ikirenge; mugabo yasezeranye kukimuha ngo kimubere inyegu, kandi hanyuma yiwe gihabwe uruvyaro rwiwe, kandi ata mwana yari bwagire. 6 Vyongeye, kuri ivyo Imana yavuze yuko uruvyaro rwiwe rwobaye kavamahanga mu gihugu kinyamahanga kandi abantu baho bakabagira abaja bakongera bakabateza amarushwa imyaka amajana ane. 7 ‘Kandi iryo hanga bazoshumbira nzoricira urubanza,’ ni ko Imana yavuze, ‘kandi mu nyuma bazovayo bankorere igikorwa ceranda aha hantu.’

8 “Yamuhaye kandi isezerano ry’ukugenyerwa; nuko avyara Izahaki hanyuma aramugenyera ku musi ugira umunani, Izahaki avyara Yakobo, Yakobo na we avyara ba bakuru b’imiryango cumi na babiri. 9 Nya bakuru b’imiryango bagirira ishari Yozefu, bamugurisha mu Misiri. Mugabo Imana yari kumwe na we, 10 hanyuma imurokora mu makuba yiwe yose yongera imuha igikundiro n’ubukerebutsi mu maso ya Farawo umwami wa Misiri. Na we yamushinze kuganza Misiri n’inzu yiwe yose. 11 Mugabo ikigoyi gitera mu Misiri hose n’i Kanani, emwe, amakuba akomeye; kandi ba sogokuruza bacu ntibaronka ibiribwa. 12 Mugabo Yakobo yumva ko mu Misiri hari ivyo kurya, aca atumayo ba sogokuruza bacu ubwa mbere. 13 Ubwa kabiri Yozefu yimenyesha bene wabo; ubwoko bwa Yozefu buca buramenyekana kuri Farawo. 14 Nuko Yozefu atumako Yakobo se wiwe n’incuti ziwe zose ngo bave aho hantu, bakaba bari abantu mirongo irindwi na batanu. 15 Yakobo amanuka mu Misiri. Hanyuma arasandaba; na ba sogokuruza bacu biba ukwo, 16 baraheza bimurirwa i Shekemu hanyuma baryamikwa mu mva Aburahamu yari yaguze amafeza na bene Hamori i Shekemu.

17 “Igihe kiriko kiregereza c’uko umuhango Imana yari yahaye Aburahamu icese uranguka, nya bantu barongerekana, baragwira mu Misiri, 18 gushika aho hahagurukiye uwundi mwami aganza Misiri, atari azi ivya Yozefu. 19 Uyo yakoresheje urwenge rwa politike mu kurwanya ubwoko bwacu, ahatira ku karenganyo ba se ngo bashire ahabona inzoya zabo, ngo ntizizigamirwe ubuzima. 20 Muri ico gihe nyene Musa aravuka, kandi yari mwiza vy’agahebuza imbere y’Imana. Yonkerezwa amezi atatu mu nzu kwa se. 21 Mugabo igihe yashirwa ahabona, umukobwa wa Farawo yaramutoye aramurera nk’umuhungu wiwe bwite. 22 Musa rero yigishijwe mu bukerebutsi bwose bw’Abanyamisiri. Nkako, yari umunyabubasha mu majambo yiwe no mu bikorwa vyiwe.

23 “Igihe rero yariko arakwiza umwaka wiwe ugira mirongo ine, haza iciyumviro mu mutima wiwe co kugendera bene wabo, bene Isirayeli. 24 Akubise ijisho umuntu umwe yariko ararenganywa, aramuburanira yongera ahōra uwariko aragirirwa nabi, mu kwica nya Munyamisiri. 25 Yibwira yuko bene wabo botegereye yuko Imana yariko ibaronsa ubukiriro biciye ku kuboko kwiwe, mugabo ntibabitegera. 26 Maze bukeye abaserukako bariko bararwana, aca agerageza kubasubiza hamwe ngo babe mu mahoro, avuga ati: ‘Bagabo, muri abavukana. Kubera iki mugiriranira ivy’akarenganyo?’ 27 Mugabo uwariko ararenganya mugenziwe, amushiburira nka hariya, avuga ati: ‘Ni nde yakugize umutware n’umucamanza kuri twebwe? 28 Mbega ushaka kunyica kumwe wica wa Munyamisiri ejo?’ 29 Kuri iryo jambo Musa arahunga hanyuma acika kavamahanga mu gihugu ca Midiyani, aho yavyariye abahungu babiri.

30 “Imyaka mirongo ine irangiye, abonekerwa n’umumarayika mu gahinga k’umusozi Sinayi mu rubeya rw’umuriro rw’igisaka c’amahwa. 31 Musa ngo abibone, biramutangaza. Mugabo akiriko aregera ngo atohoze, humvikana ijwi rya Yehova, rivuga riti: 32 ‘Ndi Imana ya ba sogokuruza bawe, Imana ya Aburahamu n’iya Izahaki n’iya Yakobo.’ Musa ahinda agashitsi, ntiyubahuka kubandanya gutohoza. 33 Yehova amubwira ati: ‘Kura inkweto* ku birenge vyawe, kuko ahantu uhagaze ari ubutaka bweranda. 34 Nabonye vy’ukuri akarenganyo abantu banje bari mu Misiri bagirirwa, kandi numvise ukuniha kwabo, nkaba manutse kubarokora. None rero nuze, ngutume mu Misiri.’ 35 Uyo Musa, uwo bihakanye bavuga ngo: ‘Ni nde yakugize umutware n’umucamanza?’, uyo ni we Imana yatumye bwa mutware n’umurokozi biciye ku kuboko kw’umumarayika yamubonekeye muri ca gisaka c’amahwa. 36 Uyo muntu yarabarongoye amaze gukora ibitangaza n’ibimenyetso mu Misiri no mu kiyaga Gitukura be no mu gahinga, mu myaka mirongo ine.

37 “Uyo Musa ni we yabwiye bene Isirayeli ati: ‘Imana izobahagurukiriza muri bene wanyu umuhanuzi ameze nkanje.’ 38 Uyo ni we yahavuye aba hagati mw’ishengero mu gahinga ari kumwe na wa mumarayika yavugana na we ku musozi Sinayi, aba kandi kumwe na ba sogokuruza, yongera aronka amajambo meranda mazima yo kubaha. 39 Uyo ba sogokuruza bacu baranse kumugamburukira, mugabo bamushiburira ku ruhande, maze mu mitima yabo bisubirira mu Misiri, 40 babwira Aroni bati: ‘Duhingurire imana zo kutuja imbere. Kuko uyo Musa, umwe yadukura mu gihugu ca Misiri, tutazi ivyamushikiye.’ 41 Nuko bakora inyana muri iyo misi hanyuma bazanira ikimazi nya kigirwamana, batangura kuryohererwa ibikorwa vy’amaboko yabo. 42 Nuko Imana irahindukira irabatanga ngo bakorere igikorwa ceranda ingabo zo mw’ijuru, nk’uko vyanditswe mu gitabu c’abahanuzi, ngo: ‘Mbega ni jewe mwashikaniye imbagwa n’ibimazi mu myaka mirongo ine mu gahinga, ga yewe nzu ya Isirayeli? 43 Ahubwo ihema rya Moloki be n’inyenyeri y’imana Refani ni vyo mwatwaye, bikaba ari ibishusho mwakoze ngo mubisenge. Ku bw’ivyo rero nzobambukana hirya ya Babiloni.’

44 “Ba sogokuruza bacu bari bafise ihema ry’intahe mu gahinga, nk’uko yatanze amategeko igihe yariko avugana na Musa ngo arikore yisunze icitegererezo yari yabonye. 45 Kandi ba sogokuruza bacu barisigaranye, na bo nyene bararizanye bari kumwe na Yosuwa baryinjiza mu gihugu cagabwa n’amahanga, ayo Imana yirukanye imbere ya ba sogokuruza bacu. Aho ni ho ryagumye gushika mu misi ya Dawidi. 46 Uyo yaragize ubutoni imbere y’Imana hanyuma asaba agateka ko kuronderera Imana ya Yakobo aho kuba. 47 Ariko Salomo ni we yayubakiye inzu. 48 Yamara rero, Musumbavyose ntaba mu nzu zakozwe n’amaboko; nk’uko umuhanuzi avuga, 49 ngo: ‘Ijuru ni intebe yanje y’ubwami, isi na yo ikaba intebe y’ibirenge vyanje. Ni inzu bwoko ki muzonyubakira?, ni ko Yehova avuga. Canke ikibanza canje co kuruhukiramwo ni ikihe? 50 Mbega ukuboko kwanje si kwo kwakoze ibi bintu vyose?’

51 “Yemwe bantu b’intabarirwa kandi batagenyerewe mu mitima no mu matwi: mwama murwanya impwemu nyeranda; nk’uko ba sogokuruza banyu bakora, ni ko namwe mukora. 52 Ni nde mu bahanuzi ba sokuruza banyu batahamye? Egome, barishe abamenyesheje hakiri kare ivyerekeye ukuza kwa wa Mugororotsi, uwo mwaguze mukongera mukica, 53 mwebwe abaronse Itegeko nk’uko ryashikirijwe n’abamarayika mugabo mukaba mutarigumije.”

54 Erega, ngo bavyumve, bumva birabasogose mu mitima baca batangura kumuhekenyera amenyo. 55 Mugabo we, yuzuye impwemu nyeranda, ahanga amaso mw’ijuru, abona ubuninahazwa bw’Imana na Yezu ahagaze iburyo bw’Imana, 56 avuga ati: “Ehe mbonye ijuru ryuguruye be n’Umwana w’umuntu ahagaze iburyo bw’Imana.” 57 Baca barasemerera n’ijwi ryabo ryose bongera bashira amaboko yabo ku matwi, maze bamugurukirako icarimwe. 58 Nuko bamaze kumuta inyuma y’igisagara, batangura kumutera amabuye. Ivyabona na vyo bishira hasi impuzu zavyo zo hejuru imbere y’ibirenge vy’umusore yitwa Sauli. 59 Babandanya gutera amabuye Sitefano ariko atakamba ati: “Mukama Yezu, akira impwemu yanje.” 60 Maze arapfukama, asemerera n’ijwi rikomeye ati: “Yehova, ntubaharureko iki gicumuro.” Ahejeje kuvuga ivyo aca arasinzira mu rupfu.

^ Ivyak. 7:33 Canke, “amasandali.”
