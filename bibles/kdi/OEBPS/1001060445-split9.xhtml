Ivyakozwe  9 : 1 - 43

9 Mugabo Sauli, agipfuhagira iterabwoba n’ubwicanyi ku bigishwa b’Umukama, aja ku muherezi mukuru, 2 amusaba amakete ajana ku masinagogi y’i Damasiko, kugira ngo abo abonye bose bo muri Ya Nzira abazane i Yeruzalemu baboshe, abagabo n’abagore.

3 Buno ariko aragenda, yegereza i Damasiko, umuco uvuye mw’ijuru urabiriza giturumbuka hirya no hino yiwe, 4 agwa hasi maze yumva ijwi rimubwira riti: “Sauli, Sauli, umpamira iki?” 5 Avuga ati: “Uri nde ga Mukama?” Na we ati: “Ndi Yezu, uwo uriko urahama. 6 Yamara rero, haguruka winjire mu gisagara, ico utegerezwa gukora na co uzokibwirwa.” 7 Ico gihe abagabo bari bafatanije na we urugendo bari bahagaze badakurako umunwa, bakumva koko umusamīro w’ijwi, mugabo ntihagire umuntu n’umwe babona. 8 Mugabo Sauli ava hasi, kandi naho amaso yiwe yakanura nta kintu na kimwe yabona. Nuko bamufata ukuboko bamujana i Damasiko. 9 Amara imisi itatu ata kintu na kimwe abona, atarya canke ngo anywe.

10 I Damasiko hari umwigishwa umwe yitwa Ananiya, maze Umukama amubwira mw’iyerekwa ati: “Ananiya!” Na we ati: “Ndi hano, Mukama.” 11 Umukama amubwira ati: “Haguruka, ugende kw’ibarabara ryitwa Irigororotse, hanyuma ku nzu ya Yuda urondere umuntu yitwa Sauli w’i Taruso. Kuko, ehe ariko arasenga, 12 kandi mw’iyerekwa, yabonye umugabo yitwa Ananiya yinjira akamurambikako ibiganza kugira ahumuke.” 13 Mugabo Ananiya yishura ati: “Mukama, narumvise kuri benshi ivy’uwo mugabo, ibibi vyose yakoreye aberanda bawe i Yeruzalemu. 14 Kandi ino arafise ububasha yahawe n’abakuru b’abaherezi bwo gushira mu mvuto abo bose bambaza izina ryawe.” 15 Mugabo Umukama amubwira ati: “Genda, kubera ko uwo muntu ari icombo nitoreye ngo ashingire intahe izina ryanje ku mahanga no ku bami no kuri bene Isirayeli. 16 Kuko nzomwereka neza ibintu vyose bitegerezwa kumushikira ku bw’izina ryanje.”

17 Nuko Ananiya aragenda, yinjira muri nya nzu, amurambikako ibiganza, hanyuma avuga ati: “Sauli, mwene wacu, Umukama, umwe Yezu yakubonekera kuri ya nzira wariko urazanana, yantumye, kugira ngo uhumuke wongere wuzuzwe impwemu nyeranda.” 18 Buno nyene ivyasa n’ibigaragamba birakoroka bivuye ku maso yiwe, aca arahumuka; nuko arahaguruka, arabatizwa, 19 hanyuma arafungura araheza aronka inguvu.

Amarana imisi n’abigishwa i Damasiko, 20 buno nyene aca atangura kwamamaza Yezu mu masinagogi, yuko Uyo ari Umwana w’Imana. 21 Mugabo abamwumva bose barajongoroka bakavuga bati: “Mbega uyo si umwe yayogeza ab’i Yeruzalemu bambaza iryo zina, akaba kandi yari yaje ino ari co nyene kimuzanye, kugira abajane baboshe ku bakuru b’abaherezi?” 22 Mugabo Sauli yaguma arushiriza kuronka ububasha, vyongeye akamaramaza Abayuda baba i Damasiko mu kwemeza mu buryo bwumvikana yuko uyo ari Kristu.

23 Maze imisi itari mike iri mu guhera, Abayuda baja inama yo kumwica. 24 Ariko Sauli amenya umugambi mubi bamugiriye. Mugabo n’amarembo baguma bayareretse bavyitondeye ku murango no mw’ijoro kugira bamwice. 25 Nuko abigishwa biwe baramufata bamwururutsa mw’ijoro bamucishije mu gitoboro c’uruhome, bamwururukiriza mu giseke.

26 Ashitse i Yeruzalemu agerageza kwifatanya n’abigishwa; mugabo bose baramutinya, kubera ko batemera ko ari umwigishwa. 27 Nuko Barunaba aramufasha, amujana ku ntumwa, na we azidondera ingene ku nzira yari yabonye Umukama be n’uko uno yari yavuganye na we, be n’ingene i Damasiko yari yavuganye ubutinyutsi mw’izina rya Yezu. 28 Agumana na bo, yinjira yongera asohoka i Yeruzalemu, avugana ubutinyutsi mw’izina ry’Umukama; 29 kandi yaraganira yongera aharira n’Abayuda bavuga ikigiriki. Mugabo bano barondera kumwica. 30 Aho bene wacu babimenyeye, bamumanukana i Sezariya hanyuma bamurungika i Taruso.

31 Maze koko ishengero, i Yudaya n’i Galilaya n’i Samariya hose, rija mu kiringo c’amahoro, rirubakwa; kandi kubera ko ryagendera mu gutinya Yehova no mu ruhoza rw’impwemu nyeranda, ryaguma rigwira.

32 Igihe Petero yariko aca mu mihingo yose, amanuka no ku beranda baba i Luda. 33 Ahasanga umuntu umwe yitwa Eneya, yari amaze imyaka umunani arambaraye ku nderuzo yiwe, kubera yari amugaye. 34 Petero amubwira ati: “Eneya, Yezu Kristu aragukijije. Haguruka usase igitanda cawe.” Na we buno nyene aca arahaguruka. 35 Kandi ababa i Luda no mu kiyaya ca Sharoni bose baramubona, hanyuma bano bahindukirira Umukama.

36 Mugabo i Yopa hari umwigishwa yitwa Tabita, rikaba risobanura ngo: Doruka. Yari agwije ibikorwa vyiza, agafasha cane aboro. 37 Mugabo muri iyo misi ararwara, hanyuma arapfa. Nuko baramwuhagira, baraheza bamushira mu cumba co hejuru. 38 Maze kubera ko i Luda hari hafi y’i Yopa, aho abigishwa bumviye ko Petero ari muri ico gisagara bamutumako abagabo babiri ngo bamwinginge bati: “Turakwinginze ntugonanwe gushika iwacu.” 39 Petero aca arahaguruka ajana na bo. Hanyuma ashitse, bamudugana muri ca cumba co hejuru; maze abapfakazi bose baza kuri we barira bongera berekana impuzu z’imbere n’impuzu zo hejuru Doruka yahora akora akiri kumwe na bo. 40 Mugabo Petero asohora abantu bose, hanyuma arapfukama, arasenga, maze arahindukira araba ca kiziga, avuga ati: “Tabita, haguruka!” Na we ateranura amaso, maze abonye Petero, aricara. 41 Amuha ukuboko, aramuhagurutsa, hanyuma ahamagara aberanda n’abapfakazi, amubashikiriza ari muzima. 42 Ivyo biramenyekana i Yopa hose, kandi benshi bacika abemera Umukama. 43 Aguma imisi itari mike i Yopa ku witwa Simoni, umuhinguzi w’inshato.
