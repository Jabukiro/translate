Ivyakozwe  1 : 1 - 26

Ivyakozwe n’intumwa

1 Inkuru ya mbere, ewe Tewofile, nayitunganije ku bintu vyose Yezu yatanguye gukora no kwigisha, 2 gushika ku musi yaduzwa, inyuma y’aho amariye guha amabwirizwa intumwa yatoye, akayabaha biciye ku mpwemu nyeranda. 3 Abo na bo nyene, biciye ku bimenyamenya vyinshi bifadika, yarabiyeretse ari muzima inyuma y’aho amariye kubabazwa, abonwa na bo mu misi mirongo ine yongera avuga ivyerekeye ubwami bw’Imana. 4 Igihe yari kumwe na bo, abaha aya mategeko, ati: “Ntimuve i Yeruzalemu, ahubwo mugume murindiriye ico Data yasezeranye, ico mwanyumvanye; 5 kubera ko mu vy’ukuri Yohani yabatirisha amazi, mugabo mwebwe mukazobatizwa mu mpwemu nyeranda mu misi mikeyi iri imbere.”

6 Igihe rero bari bakoraniye hamwe, batangura kumubaza bati: “Mukama, iki gihe ni ho ugira usubize ubwami Isirayeli?” 7 Ababwira ati: “Si ivyanyu kumenya ivy’ibihe canke ibiringo Data yashize mu bubasha bwiwe bwite; 8 mugabo muzoronka ububasha impwemu nyeranda niyabazako, kandi muzombera ivyabona i Yeruzalemu n’i Yudaya hose n’i Samariya, no gushika mu karere ka kure rwose k’isi.” 9 Amaze kuvuga ivyo, bakiriko bararaba, araduzwa hanyuma igicu kiramupfuka, ntibaba bakimubona. 10 Bagihanze amaso mw’ijuru akiriko aragenda, baja babona abagabo babiri bambaye impuzu zera bahagaze iruhande yabo, 11 bavuga bati: “Bagabo b’i Galilaya, kubera iki muhagaze muraba mw’ijuru? Uyo Yezu akuwe muri mwebwe akakirwa hejuru mw’ijuru, azoza ukwo nyene, nk’uko mumubonye aja mw’ijuru.”

12 Maze basubira i Yeruzalemu bavuye ku musozi witwa umusozi w’Imyelayo, uri hafi y’i Yeruzalemu, hakaba hari nk’urugendo rwo ku musi w’isabato. 13 Nuko binjiye, baduga mu cumba co hejuru, aho bari bacumbitse, ari Petero hamwe na Yohani na Yakobo na Andereya, Filipo na Toma, Barutelemi na Matayo, Yakobo mwene Alufayo na Simoni umunyamwete, na Yuda mwene Yakobo. 14 Abo bose bariko bakovya mu gusenga bahuje inama, bari kumwe n’abagore bamwe, na Mariya nyina wa Yezu na bene wabo.

15 Nuko muri iyo misi Petero arahaguruka hagati ya bene wabo, (nya sinzi ryose hamwe ryari rigizwe n’abantu nk’ijana na mirongo ibiri) avuga ati: 16 “Bagabo bene wacu, vyari bikenewe ko icanditswe kiranguka, ico impwemu nyeranda yavuze imbere y’igihe biciye mu kanwa ka Dawidi ku vyerekeye Yuda umwe yabera indongozi abafashe Yezu, 17 kubera ko yari yaharuwe muri twebwe kandi akaronka uruhara muri ubu busuku. 18 (Uwo nyene rero, yaguze itongo impembo y’ibitagororotse, hanyuma agwa atanguje umutwe, aturikira hagati na hagati, amara yiwe yose arandara. 19 Vyaramenyekanye no ku baba i Yeruzalemu bose, ku buryo iryo tongo ryiswe mu rurimi rwabo ngo: Akeludama, ni ukuvuga Itongo ry’Amaraso.) 20 Kuko vyanditswe mu gitabu c’Amazaburi, ngo: ‘Uburaro bwiwe nibucike umusaka, kandi ntihagire uwububamwo,’ kandi ngo: ‘Ibanga ryiwe ry’ubucungezi niritwarwe n’uwundi.’ 21 Birakenewe rero yuko mu bagabo bamye bakoranira hamwe natwe mu gihe cose Umukama Yezu yashishikara ibikorwa vyiwe muri twebwe, 22 kuva igihe yabatizwa na Yohani gushika ku musi yakirwa mw’ijuru avuye muri twebwe, umwe muri abo bagabo acika icabona hamwe natwe c’izuka ryiwe.”

23 Nuko bashikiriza babiri, Yozefu uwitwa Barusaba yatazirwa Yusito, na Matiyasi. 24 Maze barasenga bati: “Ewe Yehova, wewe uzi imitima ya bose, erekana muri aba bantu babiri uwo watoye, 25 ngo afate ikibanza c’ubu busuku n’ibanga ry’ukuba intumwa, ivyo Yuda yataye ngo yigire ahantu hiwe bwite.” 26 Nuko babagirako ubupfindo, nya bupfindo bugwa kuri Matiyasi; araheza aharuranwa na za ntumwa cumi n’imwe.
