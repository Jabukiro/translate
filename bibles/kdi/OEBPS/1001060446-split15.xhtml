Abaroma  15 : 1 - 33

15 Mugabo, twebwe abakomeye, tubwirizwa kwikorera ubugoyagoye bw’abadakomeye, ntitwishimishe ubwacu. 2 Umwe wese muri twebwe nashimishe mugenziwe mu vyiza kugira ngo yubakwe. 3 Kuko na Kristu atishimishije ubwiwe; mugabo nk’uko vyanditswe: “Ibitutsi vy’abagutuka vyaguye kuri jewe.” 4 Kuko ivyanditswe kera vyose vyanditswe kugira bitwigishe, kugira ngo biciye ku kwihangana kwacu no ku ruhoza ruva mu Vyanditswe tugire icizigiro. 5 Ya Mana rero itanga ukwihangana n’uruhoza ibahe kugira hagati yanyu wa mutima nyene Kristu Yezu yari afise, 6 kugira ngo muhuje inama muninahaze n’akanwa kamwe Imana ikaba na Se w’Umukama wacu Yezu Kristu.

7 Muhane ikaze rero, nk’uko na Kristu yaduhaye ikaze, ku bw’ubuninahazwa bw’Imana. 8 Kuko mvuga yuko Kristu mu vy’ukuri yacitse umusuku w’abagenyerewe ku bw’ukuri kw’Imana, kugira ngo yemeze imihango Imana yahaye ba sekuruza babo, 9 no kugira ngo amahanga aninahaze Imana kubera imbabazi zayo. Nk’uko vyanditswe ngo: “Ni co gituma wewe nzokwemanga icese mu mahanga, kandi nzohibongoreza izina ryawe.” 10 Irongera ikavuga iti: “Nezerwe, mwa mahanga, kumwe n’abantu biwe.” 11 Vyongeye kandi iti: “Shemeze Yehova, mwa mahanga mwese, kandi ibisata vy’abantu vyose nibimushemeze.” 12 Yesaya arongera akavuga kandi ati: “Hazoba umuzi wa Yese, kandi hazoba uwuhaguruka ngo aganze amahanga; kuri we ni ho amahanga azoshingira icizigiro cayo.” 13 Ya Mana itanga icizigiro ibuzuze akanyamuneza kose n’amahoro biciye ku kwemera kwanyu, kugira ngo mugwire mu cizigiro hamwe n’ububasha bw’impwemu nyeranda.

14 Na jewe ubwanje rero naranyuzwe ku biberekeye, bavukanyi banje, yuko na mwebwe ubwanyu mwuzuye ukumera neza, kuko mwujujwe ubumenyi bwose, be n’uko mushobora no gukeburana. 15 Ariko ndabandikiye ndushiriza kutarya umunwa ku bintu bimwebimwe, nk’aho nongeye kubibutsa, kubera ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye nahawe buvuye ku Mana 16 kugira ngo mbe umukozi akorera abantu wa Kristu Yezu ku mahanga, akora igikorwa ceranda c’inkuru nziza y’Imana, kugira ngo ishikanwa, ni ukuvuga, ayo mahanga, riboneke ko ryemerwa, ryejejwe biciye ku mpwemu nyeranda.

17 Ndafise rero imvo yo kwigina muri Kristu Yezu ku bintu vyerekeye Imana. 18 Kuko ntohirahira ngo mvuge ikintu na kimwe nimba atari ico mu vyo Kristu yakoreye muri jewe kugira ngo amahanga agamburuke biciye kw’ijambo ryanje no ku gikorwa canje, 19 hamwe n’ububasha bw’ibimenyetso n’ibitangaza, hamwe n’ububasha bw’impwemu nyeranda; ku buryo kuva i Yeruzalemu no kuzunguruka gushika muri Iliriya namamaje bimwe binogangije inkuru nziza yerekeye Kristu. 20 Muri ubwo buryo koko, nihaye intumbero yo kutamenyesha inkuru nziza aho Kristu yari amaze kuvugwa, kugira ngo sinsange ndiko nubakira ku mushinge w’uwundi muntu; 21 mugabo, nk’uko vyanditswe: “Abatamenyeshejwe ikintu na kimwe ku bimwerekeye bazobona, kandi abatumvise bazotegera.”

22 Ni na co catumye ngira ikintangira kenshi kimbuza gushika iwanyu. 23 Mugabo ubu ko ata cibare ngifise kitarakorwa muri izi ntara, kandi nkaba maze imyaka mikeyi ncambirwa gushika iwanyu 24 igihe nzoba ngiye muri Esupanye, nizigiye imbere ya vyose yuko igihe nzoba ndi ku nzira ngiyeyo, nzoca kubaraba hanyuma mukamperekezayo gatoyi, mbanje kwumva yuko inkumbu ndabafitiye ziheze ku rugero kanaka. 25 Mugabo ubu ngira nje i Yeruzalemu gukorera aberanda. 26 Kuko ab’i Makedoniya n’i Akaya bahimbawe no gusangira ivyabo n’abandi mu gutanga imfashanyo y’aboro bo mu beranda i Yeruzalemu. 27 Ego ni ko, barahimbawe no kubigira, yamara bari babafitiye umwenda; kuko nimba amahanga yararonse ku bintu vy’impwemu vyabo, na yo nyene abwirizwa kubakorera ku mugaragaro akoresheje ibintu bifasha umubiri w’inyama. 28 Ku bw’ivyo rero ninarangiza ivyo nkongera nkabashikiriza ico camwa ata nkomanzi, nzoja muri Esupanye nciye iwanyu. 29 Vyongeye, ndazi yuko ninaza iwanyu nzoza mfise ingero yuzuye y’umuhezagiro uva kuri Kristu.

30 Ndabahimiriza rero, bene wacu, biciye ku Mukama wacu Yezu Kristu no ku rukundo rw’impwemu, ngo mwihate hamwe nanje mu masengesho aturwa Imana ku bwanje, 31 ngo ndokorwe mu gukizwa abatemera i Yudaya no kugira ngo ubusuku bwanje ndangurira Yeruzalemu bwemerwe n’aberanda, 32 kugira ngo ninashika iwanyu ndi n’akanyamuneza kava ku kugomba kw’Imana, numve ko nduhuriwe kumwe namwe. 33 Ya Mana itanga amahoro ibane namwe mwese. Amen.
