Abaroma  9 : 1 - 33

9 Ndiko mvuga ukuri muri Kristu; sindiko ndabesha, kuko ijwi ryo mu mutima ryanje rishinga intahe hamwe nanje mu mpwemu nyeranda, 2 yuko mfise intuntu nyinshi n’umubabaro udatezura mu mutima wanje. 3 Kuko nopfuma jewe ubwanje mba igicibwa kuri Kristu nkaba nk’ikivume ku bavukanyi banje, incuti zanje hisunzwe umubiri, 4 abo, uko bari ukwo, bakaba ari Abisirayeli, bene ukugirwa abana na bene ubuninahazwa n’amasezerano na bene guhabwa Itegeko n’igikorwa ceranda be n’imihango; 5 ni bo bafise ba sogokuruza, ari na bo Kristu yakomotsemwo ku mubiri: Imana, iyiri hejuru ya vyose, ihezagirwe ibihe vyose. Amen.

6 Ariko si nk’aho ijambo ry’Imana ryananiwe. Kuko abakomoka kuri Isirayeli bose atari bo “Isirayeli” vy’ukuri. 7 Eka mbere bose si abana ngo ni uko ari uruvyaro* rwa Aburahamu, ahubwo vyanditswe ngo: “Ikizokwitwa ‘uruvyaro rwawe’ kizoboneka biciye kuri Izahaki.” 8 Ni ukuvuga ko abana b’umubiri atari bo bana b’Imana vy’ukuri, ahubwo abana b’umuhango ni bo baharurwa nk’uruvyaro. 9 Kuko ijambo ry’umuhango ryavuga uku: “Nk’aya mango nzoza, kandi Sara azoronka umuhungu.” 10 Yamara si ico gihe conyene, mugabo n’igihe Rebeka yasama inda y’amahasa kuri wa mugabo umwe, ari we Izahaki sogokuruza wacu: 11 kuko igihe batari bwavuke canke ngo bakore ikintu na kimwe ciza canke c’agahomerabunwa, kugira ngo intumbero y’Imana ku bijanye n’itorwa ryabo igume itavana n’ibikorwa, ahubwo igume ivana n’Umwe ahamagara, 12 Rebeka yabwiwe ati: “Bukuru azoba umushumba wa Butoyi.” 13 Nk’uko vyanditswe ngo: “Nakunze Yakobo, mugabo Esawu naramwanse.”

14 Tuvuge iki none? Mbega Imana irarenganya? Ntibikabe! 15 Kuko ibwira Musa iti: “Nzogirira imbabazi uwo nshatse kugirira imbabazi, kandi nzogirira impuhwe uwo nshatse kugirira impuhwe.” 16 Nuko rero, ntibiva ku wushaka canke ku wiruka, ahubwo biva ku Mana, ari yo igira imbabazi. 17 Kuko Icanditswe kibwira Farawo giti: “Iki nyene ni co catumye nkureka: ni ukugira ngo nerekanire kuri wewe ububasha bwanje, no kugira ngo izina ryanje ryamamare mw’isi yose.” 18 Nuko rero, uwo ishaka iramugirira imbabazi, mugabo uwo ishaka iramureka agacika intabarirwa.

19 Wombwira rero uti: “Ni kubera iki ibandanya gutora amahinyu? Kuko ni nde ahangara ukugomba kwayo gutomoye?” 20 Ewe muntu, uri nde none ngo wishure nabi Imana? Mbega icabumbwe cobwira uwakibumvye ngo: “Kubera iki wambumvye gutya?” 21 Ngw’iki? Mbega umubumvyi ntafise ububasha kw’ibumba bwo gukora mu kibumbe kimwe c’ibumba icombo gikoreshwa ibiteye iteka, n’ikindi gikoreshwa ibiteye isoni? 22 Nimba rero Imana, naho igomba kwerekana uburake bwayo no kumenyekanisha ububasha bwayo yarihanganiye n’ukwiyumanganya kwinshi ivyombo vy’uburake vyagizwe ibibereye gutikizwa 23 kugira ngo imenyekanishe ubutunzi bw’ubuninahazwa bwayo ku vyombo vy’imbabazi, ivyo yateguye imbere y’igihe ku bw’ubuninahazwa, 24 ni ukuvuga twebwe, abo yahamagaye atari mu Bayuda gusa ariko no mu mahanga, bitwaye iki? 25 Ni nk’uko ibivuga kandi muri Hoseya iti: “Abatari abantu banje nzobita ‘abantu banje,’ n’umugore atakundwa ndamwite ‘umukundwa’; 26 kandi ahantu babwiriwe ngo: ‘Ntimuri abantu banje,’ ni ho bazokwitirwa ‘abana b’Imana nzima.’”

27 Vyongeye, Yesaya arasemerera ku vyerekeye Isirayeli ati: “Naho igitigiri ca bene Isirayeli coba nk’umusenyi w’ikiyaga, amasigarira ni yo azokizwa. 28 Kuko Yehova azogira ico abaza kw’isi, abisozere kandi abitebutse.” 29 Kandi, nk’uko Yesaya yari yarabivuze kera ati: “Iyo Yehova nyen’ingabo* atadusigira uruvyaro, tuba twaracitse nka Sodomu, kandi tuba twaragizwe nka Gomora.”

30 Tuvuge iki none? Yuko abanyamahanga, naho badakurikirana ubugororotsi, bahambije ubugororotsi, bwa bugororotsi buva ku kwizera; 31 mugabo yuko Isirayeli, naho ikurikirana itegeko ry’ubugororotsi, itashikiriye iryo tegeko. 32 Kubera iki? Kubera itarikurikiranye biciye mu kwizera, mugabo nk’aho vyaciye mu bikorwa. Batsitaye kuri rya “buye ritsitaza”; 33 nk’uko vyanditswe ngo: “Ehe ndiko nshira i Siyoni ibuye ritsitaza n’igitandara kigwisha, mugabo uwushingira ukwizera kwiwe kuri ryo ntazoteterwa.”

^ Rom. 9:7 Raba Rm 4:13, akajambo k’epfo.

^ Rom. 9:29 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
