Abaroma  1 : 1 - 32

Ikete ryandikiwe Abaroma

1 Jewe Paulo umushumba wa Yezu Kristu kandi yahamagariwe kuba intumwa, yashizwe ku ruhande ku bw’inkuru nziza y’Imana, 2 iyo yasezeranye kera biciye ku bahanuzi bayo mu Vyanditswe vyeranda, 3 ku vyerekeye Umwana wayo, uwavuye mu ruvyaro rwa Dawidi hisunzwe umubiri, 4 mugabo ku bubasha akaba yiswe Umwana w’Imana hisunzwe impwemu y’ubweranda biciye ku kuzuka mu bapfuye—egome, Yezu Kristu Umukama wacu, 5 biciye kuri we tukaba twararonse ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye be n’ibanga ry’ukuba intumwa kugira ngo mu mahanga yose habe ukugamburuka kuvuye ku kwizera ku bijanye n’izina ryiwe, 6 muri ayo mahanga na mwebwe mukaba muri abahamagariwe kwegukira Yezu Kristu— 7 ndabandikiye mwebwe abakundwa b’Imana bose bari i Roma, bahamagariwe kuba aberanda:

Mugire ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye n’amahoro biva ku Mana Data wa twese no ku Mukama Yezu Kristu.

8 Imbere ya vyose, ndakengurukira Imana yanje biciye kuri Yezu Kristu ku biberekeye mwese, kubera ko ukwizera kwanyu kuvugwa mw’isi yose. 9 Kuko Imana, iyo nkorera igikorwa ceranda n’impwemu yanje ku bijanye n’inkuru nziza yerekeye Umwana wayo, ari yo cabona canje c’ukuntu nama ndabashira mu masengesho yanje ubudahengeshanya, 10 nsaba yuko nimba bishoboka ubu noza natunganirwa mu kugomba kw’Imana kugira ngo nze iwanyu. 11 Kuko ncambirwa kubabona, kugira ndabahe ingabirano y’ivy’impwemu kugira ngo mugirwe abashikamye; 12 canke riho, kugira habe uguhanahana indemesho muri mwebwe, umwe wese akaremeshwa biciye ku kwizera kw’uwundi, ukwanyu n’ukwanje.

13 Mugabo ga bene wacu, sinshaka ko muyoberwa yuko kenshi niyemeje kuza iwanyu, mugabo nkagira ikintangira gushika ubu, kugira ngo ndonke ivyamwa no muri mwebwe, cokimwe no mu yandi mahanga. 14 Ku Bagiriki no ku batari Abagiriki, ku nkerebutsi no ku mburabwenge, mfise umwenda: 15 nuko rero ku ruhande rwanje ndashashaye kubamenyesha inkuru nziza namwe abari iyo i Roma. 16 Kuko ntaterwa isoni n’inkuru nziza; nkako, ni yo bubasha bw’Imana buzanira ubukiriro umuntu wese afise ukwizera, ubwa mbere Umuyuda hanyuma n’Umugiriki; 17 kuko muri yo ari ho ubugororotsi bw’Imana buriko burahishurwa bivanye n’ukwizera kandi bikagana ku kwizera, nk’uko vyanditswe ngo: “Mugabo umugororotsi—azobeshwaho n’ukwizera.”

18 Kuko uburake bw’Imana buriko burahishurwa buvuye mw’ijuru ngo bururumbire ukutibanga Imana kwose n’ukutagororoka kwose kw’abantu bakandamiza ukuri mu nzira zitagororotse, 19 kubera ko ivyomenyekana ku vyerekeye Imana vyibonekeza muri bo, kuko Imana yabigize ivyibonekeza kuri bo. 20 Kuko kamere zayo zitaboneka zibonwa neza uhereye kw’iremwa ry’isi, kubera ko zitahurirwa ku bintu vyaremwe, ni zo bubasha bwayo bw’ibihe bidahera n’Ubumana bwayo, ku buryo ata co bireguza; 21 kuko naho bari bazi Imana batayininahaje bwa Mana canke ngo bayikengurukire, ahubwo bacitse ibijuju mu vyiyumviro vyabo, kandi imitima yabo y’imburabwenge yacitse iyitwikiriwe n’umwiza. 22 Naho nyene bemeza ko ari inkerebutsi, bacitse ibipfu, 23 ubuninahazwa bwa ya Mana itabora babuhindura ikintu kimeze nk’ishusho y’umuntu abora n’iy’inyoni n’ibiremwa vy’amaguru ane n’ibikwega inda hasi.

24 Imana rero, nk’uko ivyipfuzo vy’imitima yabo biri, yarabahebeye ubuhumane, kugira ngo imibiri yabo iteterezwe muri bo; 25 abo mbere ukuri kw’Imana bagukavye ikinyoma, bagasenga bakongera bagakorera igikorwa ceranda ikiremwa aho kubigirira Uwaremye, ari we ahezagirwa ibihe vyose. Amen. 26 Ni co gituma Imana yabahebeye inambu z’umubiri z’agaterasoni, kuko abagore babo bahinduye uko imibiri yabo isanzwe ikoreshwa igasigara ikoreshwa ibidakorwa; 27 kandi n’abagabo nyene baretse uko umubiri w’umugore usanzwe ukoreshwa, barurumba bimwe bikaze mu nambu ikomeye bagiriranira, abagabo ku bagabo, bagakora ibiteye isoni kandi bakaronka muri bo impembo ishitse, iyari ikwiranye n’ubuzimire bwabo.

28 Kandi nk’uko batashimye kumenya Imana mu buryo butagira amakosa, Imana yarabahebeye imero y’umuzirikanyi itemewe, ngo bakore ibitabereye, 29 n’ingene bari buzuye ukutagororoka kwose, ububisha, umwina, ububi, buzuye agashari, ubwicanyi, ugushihana, ubuhendanyi, agatima ko gukora ikibi, bakaba abavuga ubwongoshwe, 30 abambika ibara abandi, abanka Imana, abatisonera, abibona, abishima, abarementanya ibibi, abagambararira abavyeyi, 31 batagira ugutegera, bata amasezerano, batiyumvamwo ababo, abatagira imbabazi. 32 Naho abo bazi neza rwose ingingo igororotse yashinzwe n’Imana, yuko abaza barakora mwene ivyo bakwiriye gupfa, ntibaguma babikora gusa, ahubwo baranashigikira abaza barabikora.
