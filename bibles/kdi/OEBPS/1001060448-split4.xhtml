2 Abakorinto  4 : 1 - 18

4 Ni co gituma tudaheba, kuko dufise ubu busuku nk’uko imbabazi twagiriwe ziri; 2 mugabo twaranse ibintu vyo mu kinyegero biteye isoni, tutagendana urwenge canke ngo tujoborore ijambo ry’Imana, ahubwo riho mu gushira ahabona ukuri, turishimagiza kw’ijwi ryo mu mutima ry’umuntu wese imbere y’Imana. 3 Nimba rero inkuru nziza tumenyesha itwikiriwe vy’ukuri, itwikiriwe mu bahona, 4 muri bo imana y’iki gihe* ikaba yarahumye imizirikanyi y’abatemera, ngo umuco wa ya nkuru nziza ininahaye yerekeye Kristu we shusho y’Imana, ntushobore kumenēra. 5 Kuko tutiyamamaza ubwacu, ahubwo twamamaza Kristu Yezu ko ari Umukama, natwe ko turi abashumba banyu ku bwa Yezu. 6 Kuko Imana ari yo yavuze iti: “Umuco nukayangane uvuye mu mwiza,” kandi yakayanganiye imitima yacu ngo iyiboneshereze n’ubumenyi buninahaye bw’Imana imbere ya Kristu.

7 Ariko dufise iryo tunga mu vyombo vy’ivu, ngo ububasha burengeye ubusanzwe bube ubw’Imana, ntibube ubuva kuri twebwe. 8 Turatunatunwa mu buryo bwose, mugabo ntitubura isaganirizo; turazazanirwa, mugabo ntitubura rwose icanzo; 9 turahamwa, mugabo ntidusigwa mu kangaratete; turakubitwa hasi, mugabo ntidutikizwa. 10 Hose mu mubiri wacu twama twihanganira amagorwa yica yatejwe Yezu, ngo ubuzima bwa Yezu bubonekere kandi mu mubiri wacu. 11 Kuko twebwe abariho twama dushirwa imbere y’urupfu ku bwa Yezu, ngo ubuzima bwa Yezu bubonekere kandi mu mubiri wacu upfa. 12 Nuko rero, urupfu rukorera muri twebwe, ariko ubuzima bugakorera muri mwebwe.

13 Ko rero dufise impwemu y’ukwizera nk’imwe handitswe ivyayo ngo: “Narizeye, ni co catumye mvuga,” natwe turizera ari co gituma tuvuga, 14 tuzi yuko umwe yazura Yezu azotuzura na twebwe hamwe na Yezu kandi akazodushikiriza hamwe namwe. 15 Kuko ibintu vyose bihari ku bwanyu, kugira ngo ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye bwagizwe bwinshi bugwire bivuye ku gukenguruka kw’abandi benshi ku bw’ubuninahazwa bw’Imana.

16 Ni co gituma tudaheba; ahubwo naho umuntu turi inyuma ariko arashira, mu vy’ukuri umuntu turi imbere aguma agirwa mushasha ku musi ku musi. 17 Kuko naho amakuba ari ay’akanya gato kandi ahwahutse, atuzanira ubuninahazwa bw’uburemere burushiriza kuba ubuhebuje kandi bw’ibihe bidahera; 18 mu gihe tugumiza amaso yacu, atari ku biboneka, ahubwo ku bitaboneka. Kuko ibiboneka ari ivy’igihe gito, mugabo ibitaboneka bikaba ivy’ibihe bidahera.

^ 2 Kor. 4:4 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
