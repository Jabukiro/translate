Abanyefeso  5 : 1 - 33

5 Nimwigane Imana rero nk’abana ikunda, 2 kandi mubandanye kugendera mu rukundo, nk’uko na Kristu yabakunze akabigura, akaba ishikanwa n’ikimazi bihawe Imana ngo bibe akamoto keza.

3 Ubusambanyi n’ubuhumane bw’ubwoko bwose canke umunoho, no kuvugwa ntibikavugwe muri mwebwe, nk’uko bibereye abantu beranda; 4 ntihakabe n’ingeso ziteye isoni canke ibiyago vy’ubupfu canke ibijajuro biteye isoni, ivyo bikaba ari ibintu bitabereye, ahubwo habe ugukenguruka. 5 Kuko ibi mubizi mukaba kandi muvyemera icese, yuko ata musambanyi canke umuntu ahumanye canke umunyamunoho—bisobanura uwusenga ibigirwamana—azogira ico atorana mu bwami bwa Kristu n’Imana.

6 Ntihagire umuntu abahendesha amajambo agaragara, kuko ivyo bintu bihejeje kuvugwa ari vyo bituma uburake bw’Imana buza ku bana b’ubugambarazi. 7 Nuko rero ntimukifatanye na bo; 8 kuko kera mwari umwiza, mugabo ubu muri umuco mu bijanye n’Umukama. Mubandanye kugenda nk’abana b’umuco, 9 kuko ivyamwa vy’umuco bigizwe n’ubwoko bwose bw’ukumera neza n’ubugororotsi n’ukuri. 10 Mugume mwiyemeza neza ivyemerwa ku Mukama ivyo ari vyo; 11 kandi mureke gufatanya na bo mu bikorwa vyabo birumvye vy’umwiza, ahubwo nyabuna mubikangire, 12 kuko ivyo bakorera mu mpisho biteye isoni ku buryo ata n’uwokwubahuka kubicisha mu kanwa. 13 Kandi ibintu vyose bikangirwa birashirwa ahabona n’umuco, kuko ikintu cose gishirwa ahabona ari umuco. 14 Ni co gituma ivuga iti: “Ikangure wewe wisinzirira, uzuke uve mu bapfuye, Kristu azoheza akurasire umuco.”

15 Nuko rero mugume muraba neza ukuntu mugenda, ntimugende nk’abatagira ubukerebutsi ariko mugende nk’abantu b’inkerebutsi, 16 mwicungurira umwanya ubereye, kubera ko imisi ari mibi. 17 Mureke rero kuba imburabwenge, ahubwo mubandanye gutahura ukugomba kwa Yehova ukwo ari kwo. 18 Vyongeye, ntimuborerwe umuvinyu ari wo urimwo ubuhumbu, ahubwo mugume mwuzuzwa impwemu, 19 mubwirana amazaburi n’amashemezo aturwa Imana be n’indirimbo z’ivy’impwemu, muririmba mwongera mujanisha n’umuziki mu mitima yanyu mubigirira Yehova, 20 kandi mw’izina ry’Umukama wacu Yezu Kristu mukama mukenguruka ku bw’ibintu vyose, mubikengurukira Imana yacu ikaba na Data.

21 Umwe wese muri mwebwe nayobokere mugenziwe mutinya Kristu. 22 Abagore bayobokere abagabo babo nk’uko bayobokera Umukama, 23 kubera ko umugabo ari umutwe w’umugore wiwe nk’uko na Kristu ari umutwe w’ishengero, akaba umukiza w’uyo mubiri. 24 Nkako, nk’uko ishengero riyobokera Kristu, abe ari ko n’abagore bayobokera abagabo babo muri vyose. 25 Bagabo, mubandanye gukunda abagore banyu nk’uko na Kristu yakunze ishengero akaryigura, 26 kugira ngo aryeze, aryuhagize amazi biciye kuri rya jambo, 27 kugira ngo azane ishengero kuri we nyene mu bwiza bwakaka bwaryo, ritagira agatosi canke umunkanyari canke ico ari co cose muri mwene ivyo, ahubwo ribe ryeranda kandi ritagira ikirabagu.

28 Ukwo ni ko abagabo babwirizwa gukunda abagore babo nk’imibiri yabo bwite. Uwukunda umugore wiwe aba yikunda, 29 kuko ata muntu yigeze kwanka umubiri wiwe bwite; ahubwo arawugaburira akawubungabungana urukundo, nk’uko na Kristu agirira ishengero, 30 kubera ko turi ibihimba vy’umubiri wiwe. 31 “Ni co gituma umugabo azosiga se na nyina akifatanya* n’umugore wiwe, kandi abo babiri bazoba umubiri umwe.” 32 Iryo banga ryeranda rirakomeye. Aha ndiko mvuga ku bijanye na Kristu be n’ishengero. 33 Yamara rero kandi, umwe wese muri mwebwe ku ruhande rwiwe akunde umugore wiwe nk’uko yikunda; umugore na we ategerezwa kugirira umugabo wiwe icubahiro kigera kure.

^ Ef. 5:31 Raba Mt 19:5, akajambo k’epfo.
