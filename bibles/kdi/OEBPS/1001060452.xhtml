Abakolosayi  1 : 1 - 29

Ikete ryandikiwe Abakolosayi

1 Jewe Paulo intumwa ya Kristu Yezu biciye ku kugomba kw’Imana, na Timoteyo mwene wacu, 2 turabandikiye mwebwe aberanda mukaba n’abavukanyi b’abizigirwa bunze ubumwe na Kristu i Kolosayi:

Mugire ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye n’amahoro biva ku Mana ari yo Data.

3 Twama dukengurukira Imana Se w’Umukama wacu Yezu Kristu iyo tubasengeye, 4 kuva igihe twumva ivy’ukwizera kwanyu mu bijanye na Kristu Yezu be n’urukundo mufitiye aberanda bose 5 kubera icizigiro mubikiwe mw’ijuru. Ico cizigiro mwacumvise kera igihe havugwa ukuri kw’iyo nkuru nziza 6 yaje muri mwebwe, nk’uko nyene iriko irama ivyamwa kandi ikagwira mw’isi yose nk’uko iriko irabigira no muri mwebwe, kuva umusi mwumva kandi mukamenya neza ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye bw’Imana mu kuri. 7 Ivyo ni vyo mwamenyeye kuri Epafura umushumba mugenzi wacu mukundwa, akaba ari umusuku w’umwizigirwa wa Kristu mu kibanza cacu, 8 ari na we yaduhishuriye urukundo rwanyu mu buryo bw’impwemu.

9 Ni na co gituma twebwe, kuva umusi twavyumva, tudahengeshanya kubasengera no gusaba yuko mwokwuzuzwa ubumenyi butagira amakosa bw’ukugomba kwayo mu bukerebutsi bwose n’ugutegera kwo mu vy’impwemu, 10 kugira ngo mugende nk’uko bibereye Yehova ngo mumushimishe bimwe bishitse, uko mubandanya kwama ivyamwa mu gikorwa ciza cose no kugwira mu bumenyi butagira amakosa bw’Imana, 11 mukagirwa abanyabubasha n’ububasha bwose ku rugero rw’ubushobozi bwayo buninahaye, ngo mubone kwihangana bimwe bishitse mwongera mwiyumanganya munezerewe, 12 mukengurukira Data yabagize ababereye kuronka ku ntoranwa y’aberanda mu muco.

13 Yaraturokoye adukiza ububasha bw’umwiza atwimurira mu bwami bw’Umwana w’urukundo rwiwe, 14 muri uyo tukaba turonka ukubohorwa biciye ku ncungu*, ukubabarirwa ibicumuro vyacu. 15 Ni we shusho y’Imana itaboneka, imfura mu biremwa vyose; 16 kuko biciye kuri we ari ho ibindi bintu vyose vyaremwe mw’ijuru no kw’isi, ibiboneka n’ibitaboneka, zaba intebe z’ubwami canke ubutware canke intwaro canke ububasha. Ibindi bintu vyose vyaremwe biciye kuri we kandi biremerwa we. 17 Vyongeye, ni uw’imbere y’ibindi bintu vyose, kandi biciye kuri we ni ho ibindi bintu vyose vyabeshejweho, 18 akaba na we mutwe w’umubiri, ari ryo shengero. Ni we ntango, imfura mu kuva mu bapfuye, kugira ngo abe uwa mbere muri vyose; 19 kuko Imana yabonye ari vyiza ko ukunengesera kwose kuba muri we, 20 kandi biciye kuri we ikongera kwisubizako ibindi bintu vyose mu kugarukana amahoro biciye ku maraso yasheshe ku giti co kubabarizwako, vyaba ivyo kw’isi canke ivyo mw’ijuru.

21 Egome, mwebwe abari barigijwe kure kera mukaba n’abansi kubera yuko imizirikanyi yanyu yari ku bikorwa bibi, 22 ubu yarabasubije hamwe ikoresheje umubiri w’inyama w’uyo, biciye ku rupfu rwiwe, kugira ngo mushikirizwe muri beranda kandi mutagirako ikirabagu, ata n’ico muregwa imbere yayo, 23 mukire gusa kuguma mu kwizera, mushinzwe ku mushinge kandi mushikamye mudakurwa ku cizigiro c’iyo nkuru nziza mwumvise kandi yamamajwe mu vyaremwe vyose biri munsi y’ijuru. Iyo nkuru nziza ni yo jewe Paulo nabereye umusuku.

24 Ubu ndanezerwa mu mibabaro ndimwo ku bwanyu, nanje nkaba ndiko ndakwiza ivyasigaye ku makuba ya Kristu mu mubiri wanje ku bw’umubiri wiwe, ari wo shengero. 25 Nabaye umusuku w’iryo shengero mu buryo buhuye n’ububwiriza buva ku Mana, ubwo nahawe ku neza yanyu kugira ngo ijambo ry’Imana ryamamazwe bimwe bishitse, 26 rya banga ryeranda ryari ryaranyegejwe kuva mu bihe* vyarenganye no mu mayaruka yarenganye. Ariko ubu ryarahishuriwe aberanda bayo, 27 abo Imana yahimbawe no kumenyesha ubutunzi bw’ubuninahazwa bw’iryo banga ryeranda mu mahanga. Iryo banga ni Kristu yunze ubumwe namwe, icizigiro c’ubuninahazwa bwiwe. 28 Ni we tumenyekanisha, tugakebura umuntu wese kandi tukigisha umuntu wese mu bukerebutsi bwose, kugira ngo umuntu wese tumushikirize ari uwukwiye, yunze ubumwe na Kristu. 29 Egome, ico ni co ndiko nkorera cane, nihata mu buryo buhuye n’ukuntu ikora, ari na kwo kuriko gukorana ububasha muri jewe.

^ Kol. 1:14 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ Kol. 1:26 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
