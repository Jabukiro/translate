1 Timoteyo  2 : 1 - 15

2 Imbere ya vyose, ndahimiriza bose rero ngo intakambo, amasengesho, ugusabira abandi, ugukenguruka, bigirwe ku vyerekeye abantu b’uburyo bwose, 2 ku vyerekeye abami n’abo bose bari mu kibanza co hejuru; kugira ngo tubandanye kugira ubuzima butekanye kandi buri mu mahoro, turi n’ukwihebera Imana gukwiye kandi tudafyina. 3 Ivyo ni vyiza kandi biremerwa imbere y’Umukiza wacu, Imana, 4 umwe agomba ko abantu b’uburyo bwose bakizwa bagashika ku bumenyi butagira amakosa bwerekeye ukuri. 5 Kuko hari Imana imwe, n’umuhuza umwe hagati y’Imana n’abantu, akaba ari umuntu, ni we Kristu Yezu, 6 uwitanze ngo abe incungu* y’indinganire ku bwa bose... ivyo ni vyo bikwiye gushingirwa intahe ku bihe vyavyo nyene. 7 Ku bw’iyo ntahe ni ho nagenywe kuba umwamamaji n’intumwa—ndiko mvuga ukuri, sindiko ndabesha—umwigisha w’amahanga mu bijanye n’ukwizera be n’ukuri.

8 Ndashaka rero yuko ahantu hose abagabo bashishikara gusenga, bataritse amaboko adahemuka, ata burake n’impari. 9 Vyongeye, ndashaka ko abagore bishariza mu nyambaro itunganije neza, mu rupfasoni no mu kurorana kw’umuzirikanyi, atari mu gushazisha imideri yo gutsiba imishatsi, n’inzahabu canke imaragarita canke impuzu zizimvye cane, 10 ahubwo bibe mu buryo buberanye n’abagore bavuga ko batinya Imana, ni ukuvuga mu bikorwa vyiza.

11 Umugore niyige acereje mu kuyoboka gukwiye. 12 Sindekurira umugore kwigisha, canke kugira ubukuru ku mugabo, ahubwo nacereze. 13 Kuko Adamu ari we yabanje kuremwa, hanyuma Eva. 14 Vyongeye, Adamu ntiyahenzwe, mugabo umugore yarahenzwe rwose, ararenga itegeko. 15 Ariko azozigamwa biciye ku kwibaruka, bapfe gusa kuguma mu kwizera no mu rukundo no mu kwezwa hamwe n’ukurorana kw’umuzirikanyi.

^ 1 Tim. 2:6 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
