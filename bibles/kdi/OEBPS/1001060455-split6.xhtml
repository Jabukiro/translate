1 Timoteyo  6 : 1 - 21

6 Abari munsi y’ingǒgo y’ubushumba bose nibagume babona ko ba shebuja babereye iteka ryuzuye, kugira ngo izina ry’Imana be n’inyigisho yayo ntivyigere bivugwa nabi. 2 Vyongeye, abafise ba shebuja bemera ntibabagaye ngo ni uko ari bene wacu. Ahubwo riho, nibarushirize kuba abashumba, kuko abungukira ku gikorwa ciza cabo ari abemera n’abakundwa.

Ugume wigisha ivyo bintu wongera ushikiriza aya majambo yo guhimiriza. 3 Nimba hari umuntu yigisha izindi nyigisho kandi ntiyemere ya majambo ngirakamaro*, amwe y’Umukama wacu Yezu Kristu, canke ntiyemere inyigisho ihuye n’ukwihebera Imana, 4 aba yivyimvya, adategera ikintu na kimwe; ahubwo aba arwaye mu mutwe arwajwe n’ibibazo be n’impari ku majambo. Ivyo bivamwo agashari, ugushihana, ugutukana, imyikeko mibi, 5 impari zikaze ku bintu bitagira mvura zigirwa n’abantu bononekaye mu muzirikanyi banyazwe ukuri, bakiyumvira ko ukwihebera Imana ari uburyo bwo kuronka inyungu. 6 Nkako, ukwo kwihebera Imana, kuri kumwe n’ukwikwira, ni uburyo bwo kuronka inyungu ikomeye. 7 Kuko ata co twazanye mw’isi, kandi nta na kimwe dushobora kuyikuramwo. 8 Iyo rero dufise ivyo kurya n’ivyo kwambara, ivyo biratubumba.

9 Ariko abiyemeje kuba abatunzi bagwa mu kigeragezo no mu mutego no mu vyipfuzo vyinshi vy’ubupfu kandi bibagirira nabi, bidwibiza abantu mu gatikizo no mu mahonero. 10 Kuko urukundo rw’amahera ari umuzi w’ibibi vy’ubwoko bwose, kandi mu kurondera gushikira urwo rukundo bamwe barahubijwe bava ku kwizera, bitobekeranisha hose imibabaro myinshi.

11 Ariko weho ga muntu w’Imana, hunga ivyo bintu. Mugabo ukurikirane ubugororotsi, ukwihebera Imana, ukwizera, urukundo, ukwihangana, ubwitonzi. 12 Rwana indwano nziza y’ukwizera, ucakire ubuzima budahera wahamagariwe kandi ukabushingira intahe nziza icese imbere y’ivyabona vyinshi.

13 Imbere y’Imana, yo izigamira ubuzima ibintu vyose, n’imbere ya Kristu Yezu, we bwa cabona yashinze intahe nziza icese imbere ya Ponsiyo Pilato, ngutegetse 14 kwubahiriza ibwirizwa mu buryo butagira agatosi kandi butagira ico bunegurwa gushika ku kwigaragaza kw’Umukama wacu Yezu Kristu. 15 Ukwo kwigaragaza, wa muhirwa akaba na we Munyabubasha wenyene azokwerekana mu bihe vyakwo, we Mwami w’abaganza akaba n’Umukama w’abakama batwara, 16 umwe wenyene afise ukudapfa, aba mu muco utegerwa, wa wundi ata n’umwe mu bantu yamubonye canke ashobora kumubona. Iteka n’ubushobozi bibe ivyiwe ibihe bidahera. Amen.

17 Tegeka abatunzi bo muri iki gihe* kutihambaza, no kudashingira icizigiro cabo ku butunzi butari ubwo kwizigirwa, mugabo bagishingire ku Mana, yo ituronsa ku bwinshi ibintu vyose kugira ngo tubinezererwe; 18 ubategeke gukora ivyiza, kuba abatunzi mu bikorwa vyiza, gutanga batitangira, bagabirije gusangira n’abandi, 19 bibikire nk’ubutunzi ahatagira inkomanzi umushinge mwiza ku bwa kazoza, kugira bacakire ubuzima nyakuri.

20 Ewe Timoteyo, rinda ico wabikijwe, uhunge amajambo agaragara asiga iceyi ibintu vyeranda, uhunge n’amajambo avuguruzanya y’ivyitwa ngo ni “ubumenyi.” 21 Kubera uguhaya mwene ubwo bumenyi bamwe barakebeje bata ukwizera.

Ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye bubane namwe.

^ 1 Tim. 6:3 Canke, “ntungamagara.”

^ 1 Tim. 6:17 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
