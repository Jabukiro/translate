2 Timoteyo  2 : 1 - 26

2 Wewe rero, mwananje, guma wironsa ububasha mu buntu ata wari abukwiriye bujanye na Kristu Yezu, 2 kandi ibintu wanyumvanye bishigikiwe n’ivyabona vyinshi, ivyo bintu ubizeze abantu b’abizigirwa, abazoheza bakaba abakwije neza ibisabwa kugira bigishe abandi. 3 Bwa musoda mwiza wa Kristu Yezu, wemere umugabane wawe mu kwihanganira imibabaro. 4 Nta muntu akora nk’umusoda aja mu vy’urucuruza vyo mu buzima, kugira ngo ashimwe n’uwamwanditse ngo abe umusoda. 5 Vyongeye, mbere n’uwuhiganwa mu nkino ntiyambikwa urugori kiretse yahiganywe akurikije amategeko. 6 Umurimyi akora cane ni we ategerezwa kuba uwa mbere mu kuronka ku vyeze. 7 Ugume uzirikana ku vyo mvuga; Umukama azoguha vy’ukuri ugutahura muri vyose.

8 Wibuke ko Yezu Kristu yazuwe mu bapfuye kandi ko yari uwo mu ruvyaro* rwa Dawidi, nk’uko inkuru nziza namamaza iri; 9 ku bwayo ndiko nihanganira imibabaro kugeza no ku mvuto nk’inkozi y’ikibi. Yamara rero, ijambo ry’Imana ntiriboshwe. 10 Kubera ico, mbandanya kwihanganira ibintu vyose ku bw’abatowe, ngo na bo baronke ubukiriro buboneka mu bumwe na Kristu Yezu hamwe n’ubuninahazwa budahera. 11 Iri jambo ni iryo kwizigirwa: Mu vy’ukuri nimba twarapfanye na we, tuzobanaho kandi na we; 12 nitwabandanya kwihangana, tuzoganzanya kandi na we; nitwamwihakana, na we azotwihakana; 13 nimba turi abatizigirwa, we aguma ari umwizigirwa, kuko adashobora kwihakana we nyene.

14 Guma ubibutsa ibi bintu, ubategeke imbere y’Imana nka yo cabona, kutarwana bapfa amajambo, kikaba ari ikintu ata na kimwe kimaze, kuko conona abumviriza. 15 Ukore uko ushoboye kwose ngo wishikirize Imana nk’uwemewe, umukozi atagira ikimutetereza, akoresha neza ijambo ry’ukuri. 16 Mugabo uzibukire amajambo ata co arimwo asiga iceyi ibintu vyeranda; kuko bazogenda barushiriza kutibanga Imana, 17 kandi ijambo ryabo rizorandagata nk’igisebe kiboze. Muri abo harimwo Himenayo na Fileto. 18 Abo bantu nyene barataye ukuri, bavuga ko izuka rimaze kuba; kandi bariko baratembagaza ukwizera kwa bamwe. 19 Ariko umushinge ukomeye w’Imana uguma uhagaze, uriko iki kidodo, ngo: “Yehova arazi abiwe,” kandi ngo: “Umuntu wese avuga izina rya Yehova avavanure n’ibitagororotse.”

20 Mu nzu nini rero ntihabamwo gusa ivyombo vy’inzahabu n’ifeza, mugabo harabamwo n’ivy’ibiti n’ivy’ivu, bimwe bigakoreshwa ibiteye iteka mugabo ibindi bigakoreshwa ibitagira iteka. 21 Umuntu rero niyitandukanya n’ivyo vya kabiri, azoba icombo gikoreshwa ibiteye iteka, cejejwe, c’ingirakamaro kuri nyeneco, giteguriwe igikorwa ciza cose. 22 Hunga rero ivyipfuzo bijana n’ubuto, mugabo ukurikirane ubugororotsi, ukwizera, urukundo, amahoro, uri kumwe n’abambaza Umukama n’umutima udahumanye.

23 Bitayeko, wamirire kure ibibazo vy’ubupfu kandi vy’ukutamenya, uzi yuko bivamwo indwano. 24 Mugabo umushumba w’Umukama ntabwirizwa kurwana, ahubwo abwirizwa kuba umuntu yengenga kuri bose, ashoboye kwigisha, akaguma yiyumanganije mu mibabaro, 25 akigisha mu bwitonzi abafise agatima katabereye; kuko kumbure Imana yobaha ukwigaya gushikana ku bumenyi butagira amakosa bwerekeye ukuri, 26 bakagarura ubwenge bakava mu mutego wa Shetani*, kubera ko bafashwe na we ari bazima ku bw’ukugomba kw’uyo.

^ 2 Tim. 2:8 Ijambo ku rindi ni “urubuto.”

^ 2 Tim. 2:26 Dufatiye ku kigiriki ni “Diyabolosi,” bisobanura ngo “Umubesheranyi.” Raba kandi Inyongera ya 21.
