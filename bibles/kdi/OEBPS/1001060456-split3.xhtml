2 Timoteyo  3 : 1 - 17

3 Mugabo umenye ibi: yuko mu misi ya nyuma hazobaho ibihe bitoroshe kandi bigoye kwihanganira. 2 Kuko abantu bazoba ari abikunda, inkunzi z’amahera, abishima, abibona, abarogosi, abagambararira abavyeyi, intashima, ibihemu, 3 batiyumvamwo ababo, badashaka kwumvikana na gato, abambika ibara abandi, batagira ukwigumya, inkazi, badakunda iciza, 4 abaguranyi, intumva, abivyimvya, abakunda ibinezereza aho gukunda Imana, 5 bafise ishusho y’ukwihebera Imana mugabo bakihakana ububasha bwakwo; abo rero ubace kure. 6 Kuko muri abo ari ho hava ba bantu bacengetera ku rwenge mu ngo bagatwara nk’imbohe zabo abagore bagoyagoya baremerewe n’ibicumuro, batwarwa n’ivyipfuzo bitari bimwe, 7 bama biga yamara ntibigere bashika ku bumenyi butagira amakosa bwerekeye ukuri.

8 Nka kumwe rero Yane na Yambure barwanya Musa, ni ko n’abo nyene babandanya kurwanya ukuri, abantu bononekaye rwose mu muzirikanyi, batemewe ku bijanye n’ukwizera. 9 Yamara rero, ntibazorenga aho, kuko ubusazi bwabo buzokwibonekeza cane kuri bose, nk’uko n’ubusazi bw’abo bantu babiri bwibonekeje. 10 Mugabo wewe warakurikiraniye hafi inyigisho yanje, ingendo yanje y’ubuzima, intumbero yanje, ukwizera kwanje, ukwiyumanganya kwanje, urukundo rwanje, ukwihangana kwanje, 11 uguhamwa kwanje, imibabaro yanje, mwene vya bintu vyanshikira i Antiyokiya, muri Ikoniyo, i Lusitira, mwene rwa ruhamo nihanganira; yamara vyose Umukama yarandokoye abinkuramwo. 12 Nkako, abo bose bipfuza kubaho bihebera Imana bifatanije na Kristu Yezu na bo nyene bazohamwa. 13 Mugabo abantu b’ababisha be n’abahumvyi bazogenda barushiriza kuba babi, bazimiza bongera bazimizwa.

14 Ariko weho, ugume mu vyo wize ukumvishwa ukavyemera, uzi ababikwigishije abo ari bo 15 be n’uko guhera mu buhinja wamenye ivyanditswe vyeranda, ibishobora kukugira inkerebutsi ku bw’ubukiriro biciye ku kwizera kujanye na Kristu Yezu. 16 Icanditswe cose cahumetswe n’Imana kandi ni ngirakamaro ku kwigisha, ku gukangīra, ku kugorora ibintu, ku gutoza indero mu bugororotsi, 17 ngo umuntu w’Imana abe uwubishoboye neza, afise ibikenewe vyose ku bw’igikorwa ciza cose.
