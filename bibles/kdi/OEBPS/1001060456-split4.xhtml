2 Timoteyo  4 : 1 - 22

4 Ndagutegetse nshimitse imbere y’Imana na Kristu Yezu, umwe abwirizwa kuzocira imanza abazima n’abapfuye, kandi biciye ku kwigaragaza kwiwe n’ubwami bwiwe, 2 wamamaze rya jambo, uvyihatire mu gihe ciza, mu gihe kigoye, ukangire, ukankamire, uhimirize, mu kwiyumanganya kwose n’ubuhanga bwo kwigisha. 3 Kuko hazoshika igihe aho batazokwihanganira ya nyigisho ngirakamaro, mugabo nk’uko ivyipfuzo vyabo bwite biri, bazokwirundaniriza abigisha kugira ngo amatwi yabo yagazwe; 4 kandi bazokwica amatwi ngo ntibumve ukuri, bakeberezwe ku nkuru z’ikinyoma. 5 Ariko weho, uzigame ubwenge bwawe muri vyose, wihanganire imibabaro, ukore igikorwa c’umwamamaji w’injili, urangure ubusuku bwawe bimwe bishitse.

6 Kuko ubu ndiko ndasukwa nk’ishikanwa ry’ikinyobwa, kandi igihe c’ukubohorwa kwanje kirashitse. 7 Nararwanye ya ndwano nziza, narirutse mw’isiganwa kugeza kw’iherezo, naragumije ukwizera. 8 Kuva ubu, mbikiwe urugori rw’ubugororotsi, urwo Umukama, wa mucamanza agororotse, azompa bwa mpembo kuri wa musi, yamara si jewe jenyene, ahubwo ruzohabwa n’abo bose bakunze ukwigaragaza kwiwe.

9 Kora uko ushoboye kwose uze aho ndi vuba. 10 Kuko Dema yantaye kubera yuko yakunze ivy’iki gihe* akaja i Tesalonika; Keresansi yagiye i Galatiya, Tito i Dalumatiya. 11 Luka wenyene ni we ari kumwe nanje. Hitana Mariko muzanane, kuko ambera kirumara ku bw’igikorwa c’ubusuku. 12 Mugabo Tikiko namutumye i Efeso. 13 Niwaza, uzane ca gikwemu nasigira Karupo i Tirowa, na ya mizingo y’ibitabu, canecane iyo mu nsato.

14 Alegizandere wa mucuzi w’umuringa yarankoreye ibibi vyinshi—Yehova azobimwishura nk’uko ibikorwa vyiwe biri— 15 kandi nawe umwiyubare, kuko yarwanije amajambo yacu bimwe bikabije.

16 Mu kwiburanira kwanje kwa mbere nta n’umwe yagiye ku ruhande rwanje, mugabo bose barantaye—ivyo ntibabiharurweko— 17 mugabo Umukama yahagaze hafi yanje anshiramwo ububasha, kugira ngo biciye kuri jewe igikorwa c’ukwamamaza kirangurwe bimwe bishitse kandi amahanga yose acumve; kandi nararokowe mu kanwa k’intambwe. 18 Umukama azondokora mu gikorwa cose c’ububisha yongere ankize ku bw’ubwami bwiwe bwo mw’ijuru. Ubuninahazwa bube ubwiwe ibihe bitazoshira. Amen.

19 Undamukirize Pirisika na Akwila be n’urugo rwa Onesiforo.

20 Erasito yagumye i Korinto, mugabo nasize Tirofimo i Mileto arwaye. 21 Ukore uko ushoboye kwose ushike imbere y’urushana.

Ewubulo arakuramutsa, na Pudensi na Lino na Kolodiya na bene wacu bose.

22 Umukama abane n’agatima werekana. Ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye bwiwe bubane namwe.

^ 2 Tim. 4:10 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
