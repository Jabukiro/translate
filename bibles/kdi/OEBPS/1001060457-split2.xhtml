Tito  2 : 1 - 15

2 Ariko weho, ugume uvuga ibintu bibereye inyigisho ngirakamaro. 2 Abatama nibabe abatarenza urugero mu ngeso, badafyina, baroranye mu muzirikanyi, bafise amagara meza mu kwizera, mu rukundo, mu kwihangana. 3 N’abatamakazi nyene bagire inyifato y’abantu batinya Imana, ntibabe abambika ibara abandi canke abaja b’umuvinyu mwinshi, babe abigisha b’ivyiza; 4 ngo bakebanure abagore bakiri bato bagarure ubwenge kugira bakunde abagabo babo, bakunde abana babo, 5 babe abaroranye mu muzirikanyi, abatanduye, abitwararika ibikorwa vyo mu rugo, abameze neza, abayobokera abagabo babo bwite, kugira ngo ijambo ry’Imana ntiritukwe.

6 Guma uhimiriza n’abasore nyene babe abaroranye mu muzirikanyi, 7 muri vyose werekane ko uri akarorero k’ibikorwa vyiza; werekane ko utagira amahinyu mu nyigisho yawe, ko udafyina, 8 ko ufise imvugo imeze neza idashobora kugira ico igawa; kugira ngo umuntu ari ku ruhande ruturwanya amaramare, atagira ikintu na kimwe c’agahomerabunwa atuvugako. 9 Abashumba nibayobokere ba shebuja babo muri vyose, kandi babashimishe rwose, batavuga ngo na bo bavuge, 10 batiba, ahubwo berekane ukudahemuka kwiza bimwe bishitse, kugira ngo basharize inyigisho y’Umukiza wacu, Imana, muri vyose.

11 Kuko bwa buntu ata wari abukwiriye bw’Imana buzanira ubukiriro abantu b’uburyo bwose bwerekanywe, 12 bukatwigisha kwikunkumura ukutibanga Imana n’ivyipfuzo vy’isi no kubaho dufise ukurorana kw’umuzirikanyi n’ubugororotsi n’ukwihebera Imana muri iki gihe*, 13 mu gihe turindiriye ca cizigiro c’ubuhirwe n’ukwigaragaza kuninahaye kwa ya Mana ikomeye n’ukw’Umukiza wacu Kristu Yezu, 14 uwitanze ku bwacu kugira aturokore mu bugarariji bw’uburyo bwose yongere yihumanurire igisata yiharije, kigirira umwete ibikorwa vyiza.

15 Guma uvuga ivyo bintu, uhimiriza, ukangira n’ububasha bwose bwo gutegeka. Ntihagire umuntu n’umwe yigera agusuzugura.

^ Tito 2:12 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
