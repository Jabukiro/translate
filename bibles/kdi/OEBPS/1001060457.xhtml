Tito  1 : 1 - 16

Ikete ryandikiwe Tito

1 Jewe Paulo, umushumba w’Imana akaba n’intumwa ya Yezu Kristu hisunzwe ukwizera kw’abatowe b’Imana be n’ubumenyi butagira amakosa bwerekeye ukuri guhuye n’ukwihebera Imana, 2 hishimikijwe icizigiro c’ubuzima budahera, ubwo Imana idashobora kubesha yasezeranye imbere y’ibihe vya kera cane, 3 yamara mu bihe vyayo bwite ikaba yarahishuye ijambo ryayo muri ca gikorwa nazezwa c’ukwamamaza, kw’itegeko ry’Umukiza wacu, Imana; 4 ndakwandikiye wewe Tito, umwana wanje nyakuri hisunzwe ukwizera dusangiye:

Habe ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye n’amahoro biva ku Mana ari yo Data no kuri Kristu Yezu Umukiza wacu.

5 Iki ni co catumye ngusiga i Kirete: ni ukugira ngo ukosore ibintu vyari n’agahaze wongere ugene abagabo b’inararibonye mu gisagara no mu kindi, nk’uko nabigutegetse; 6 nimba hari umuntu ata co aregwa, umugabo w’umugore umwe, afise abana bemera batagirizwa ubuhumbu canke ngo babe ibigaba. 7 Kuko umucungezi ategerezwa kuba atagira ico aregwa bwa mubwiriza w’Imana, atari intagondwa, adakaza ishavu, atari umunyamatati w’imborerwa, atari uwukubitana, atanohokera inyungu mbi, 8 ahubwo abe ari umwakiranyi, uwukunda iciza, aroranye mu muzirikanyi, agororotse, intahemuka, yigumya, 9 agumya ijambo ryo kwizigirwa ku bijanye n’ubuhanga bwiwe bwo kwigisha, kugira ashobore guhimiriza biciye ku nyigisho ngirakamaro* no gukangira abayivuguruza.

10 Kuko hariho abantu benshi b’ibigaba, imvuzi z’ibitagira akamaro, kandi bahenda imizirikanyi y’abandi, canecane bamwe bumira ku kugenyerwa. 11 Ni nkenerwa kubazivya iminwa, kuko abo bantu nyene baguma batembagaza ingo uko zakabaye, mu kwigisha ivyo batabwirizwa kwigisha, kugira baronke inyungu mbi. 12 Umwe muri bo, umuhanuzi wabo bwite, yavuze ati: “Abanyakirete bama ari ababeshi, ni ibikoko biryana, abakunzi b’inda badashaka gukora.”

13 Iyo ntahe ni iy’ukuri. Ico nyene nigitume uguma ubakangira ubahambarira, kugira ngo bagire amagara meza mu kwizera, 14 batumviriza ibitito vy’Abayuda n’amabwirizwa y’abantu batera ibitugu ukuri. 15 Vyose ni ibidahumanye ku bantu badahumanye. Mugabo ku bantu bahumanye kandi batagira ukwizera nta kintu na kimwe kidahumanye, mugabo imizirikanyi yabo n’ijwi ryabo ryo mu mutima birahumanye. 16 Batura icese yuko bazi Imana, mugabo bakayihakanisha ibikorwa vyabo, kubera ko bateye ishishi bakaba n’abagambarazi, bakaba kandi batemewe ku bw’igikorwa ciza na kimwe.

^ Tito 1:9 Canke, “ntungamagara.”
