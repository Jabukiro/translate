Filemoni 1 - 25

Ikete ryandikiwe Filemoni

1 Jewe Paulo imbohe ku bwa Kristu Yezu, na Timoteyo mwene wacu, turakwandikiye wewe Filemoni umukundwa wacu akaba n’umukozi mugenzi wacu, 2 na Afiya mushiki wacu, na Arikipo umusoda mugenzi wacu, n’ishengero riri mu nzu yawe:

3 Mugire ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye n’amahoro biva ku Mana Data wa twese no ku Mukama Yezu Kristu.

4 Nama nkengurukira Imana yanje iyo ndagushize mu masengesho yanje, 5 kuko nguma numva ivy’urukundo rwawe n’ukwizera ufise ku Mukama Yezu no ku beranda bose; 6 kugira ngo ushiruke ubute mu gusangira n’abandi ukwizera kwawe kuko uzi yuko ikintu ciza cose muri twebwe kijanye na Kristu. 7 Kuko nagize akanyamuneza kenshi nkumva mpojejwe cane bitewe n’urukundo rwawe, kubera ko imitima* y’aberanda yaruhuriwe biciye kuri wewe, mwene wacu.

8 Ku bw’iyo mvo nyene, naho nari mfise umwidegemvyo mwinshi wo kuvuga mu bijanye na Kristu n’ububasha bwo kugutegeka gukora ikibereye, 9 nshimye kuguhimiriza riho nishimikije urukundo, kubera ko ndi uko ndi, Paulo umutama, egome, ubu nkaba kandi ndi imbohe ku bwa Kristu Yezu; 10 ndiko nguhimiriza ku vyerekeye umwana wanje, uwo nabereye nka se ndi mu mvuto, ari we Onezime, 11 umwe ata co yari akumariye kera mugabo ubu akaba akimariye wewe na jewe. 12 Uwo nyene ni we nkugarukaniye, egome, uwo, we bura bwanje.

13 Nari gushima kumwigumiriza kugira ngo agume ankorera mu kibanza cawe mu mvuto ndimwo ku bw’inkuru nziza. 14 Mugabo sinshaka gukora ikintu na kimwe utavyemeye, kugira ngo igikorwa cawe ciza ntikibe igikozwe ku gahato, ahubwo kibe ikivuye ku gushaka kwawe. 15 Kumbure mu vy’ukuri iki ni co catumye atoroka isaha imwe, kugira ngo wongere umuronke ku bw’ibihe vyose, 16 atakiri umushumba ahubwo aruta umushumba, ari umuvukanyi mukundwa, canecane kuri jewe, yamara bikarushiriza kuri wewe mu bucuti bw’umubiri no mu Mukama. 17 Nimba rero umbona nk’uwufatanije na wewe, numwakirane ubuntu nk’uko wonyakira. 18 Vyongeye, nimba hari ikibi yagukoreye canke hakaba hari ico agufitiye, ivyo ubimparureko. 19 Jewe Paulo nanditse n’ukuboko kwanje bwite: nzovyishura . . . siniriwe ndakubwira yuko n’ubuzima bwawe ubukesha jewe. 20 Egome, mwene wacu, ese nogira ico nungukiye kuri wewe mu bijanye n’Umukama: nuruhurire umutima wanje mu bijanye na Kristu.

21 Ndakwandikiye nizigiye ko ugamburuka, nzi yuko uzokora n’ibiruta ivyo mvuze. 22 Mugabo uretse ivyo, untegurire kandi uburaro, kuko nizigiye yuko biciye ku masengesho yanyu nzobarekurirwa.

23 Epafura imbohe mugenzanje mu bumwe na Kristu Yezu arakuramutsa, 24 na Mariko, Arisitariko, Dema, Luka, abakozi bagenzanje.

25 Ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye bw’Umukama Yezu Kristu bubane n’agatima mwerekana.

^ Flm. 7 Canke, “ikibabarwe.” Mu kigiriki ni “amara,” bikerekeza ku kanyengetera ko mu mutima gashika kure.
