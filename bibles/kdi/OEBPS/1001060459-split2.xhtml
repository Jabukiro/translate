Abaheburayo  2 : 1 - 18

2 Ni co gituma ari nkenerwa ko twitwararika ivyo twumvise kuruta uko bisanzwe, kugira ntitwigere tugenda akayombekere. 2 Kuko, nimba ijambo ryavuzwe biciye ku bamarayika ryabonetse ko ritanyiganyiga, kandi ukugabitanya kwose be n’ukugambarara kwose bikaba vyararonse agahembo gahuye n’ubutungane, 3 tuzorusimba dute nimba twafashe minenegwe ubukiriro buhambaye gutyo mu buryo bw’uko bwatanguye kuvugwa biciye ku Mukama wacu kandi bukemezwa ku bwacu n’abamwumvise, 4 mu gihe Imana yagiyemwo igashinga intahe biciye ku bimenyetso n’ibitangaza be n’ibikorwa vy’ububasha bitari bimwe, no ku kugabanganywa kw’impwemu nyeranda nk’uko ukugomba kwayo kuri?

5 Kuko abamarayika atari bo yahaye isi izoza ngo bayiganze, ari yo turiko turavuga. 6 Mugabo hari icabona catanze ikimenyamenya ahantu hamwe, giti: “Umuntu ni iki ku buryo umwibuka, canke umwana w’umuntu ku buryo umwitaho? 7 Wamugize uwuri munsi gatoyi y’abamarayika; wamwambitse urugori rw’ubuninahazwa n’iteka, wongera umushinga kugaba ibikorwa vy’amaboko yawe. 8 Vyose wabishize munsi y’ibirenge vyiwe ngo abiganze.” Kuko, kubera ko yamuhaye kuganza ibintu vyose, Imana nta na kimwe yasize ngo ntakiganze. Ariko ubu ibintu vyose ntiturabona biganzwa na we; 9 mugabo tubona Yezu, uwagizwe uwuri munsi gatoyi y’abamarayika, yambitswe urugori rw’ubuninahazwa n’iteka ku bwo kuba yarashikiwe n’urupfu, kugira ngo biciye ku buntu ata wari abukwiriye bw’Imana, ahonjereze urupfu umuntu wese.

10 Kuko vyari bibereye kuri wa wundi ibintu vyose biriho ku bwiwe kandi ibintu vyose bikaba biriho biciye kuri we, mu kuzana abana benshi ku buninahazwa, yuko atunganya ya Mboneza y’ubukiriro bwabo biciye ku mibabaro. 11 Kuko uwuriko areza n’abariko barezwa bose bava kuri umwe, kandi ico ni co gituma adaterwa isoni no kubita “bene wabo,” 12 igihe avuga ngo: “Nzomenyesha izina ryawe bene wacu; hagati mw’ishengero nzogushemeza n’ururirimbo.” 13 Kandi ngo: “Nzomwizigira.” Kandi ngo: “Raba! Jewe n’abana batoyi, abo Yehova yampaye.”

14 Rero, kubera ko nya “bana batoyi” ari abasangiye amaraso n’umubiri, na we nyene yaronse kuri ivyo bintu nyene, kugira ngo biciye ku rupfu rwiwe ahindure ubusa umwe afise uburyo bwo guteza urupfu, ari we Shetani*; 15 no kugira ngo aronse umwidegemvyo abo bose bari mu buja ubuzima bwabo bwose kubera ugutinya urupfu. 16 Kuko vy’ukuri atariko afasha abamarayika na gato, ahubwo ariko afasha uruvyaro* rwa Aburahamu. 17 Ku bw’ico rero yabwirijwe gucika nka “bene wabo” mu mice yose, ngo acike umuherezi mukuru w’umunyembabazi n’umwizigirwa mu vyerekeye Imana, kugira ngo atange ikimazi c’impongano ku bw’ibicumuro vy’abantu. 18 Kuko, kubera ko we ubwiwe yababajwe igihe yariko aragezwa, arashoboye gutabara abariko baragezwa.

^ Heb. 2:14 Dufatiye ku kigiriki ni “Diyabolosi,” bisobanura ngo “Umubesheranyi.” Raba kandi Inyongera ya 21.

^ Heb. 2:16 Ijambo ku rindi ni “urubuto.”
