Abaheburayo  5 : 1 - 14

5 Kuko umuherezi mukuru wese akuwe mu bantu agenwa ku bw’abantu ku bintu vyerekeye Imana, kugira ashikane amashikanwa n’ibimazi ku bw’ibicumuro. 2 Arashoboye kugirira ibiri ku rugero abantu ata co bazi be n’abahuba kuko na we nyene akikujwe n’ubugoyagoye bwiwe bwite, 3 kandi kubera bwo, abwirizwa gushikana amashikanwa ku bw’ibicumuro, ku bwiwe no ku bw’abantu.

4 Vyongeye, umuntu ntaronka iryo teka ari we aryihaye, ahubwo gusa arironka arihamagariwe n’Imana, nk’uko vyabaye no kuri Aroni. 5 Na Kristu rero ntiyininahaje mu gucika umuherezi mukuru, ahubwo yaninahajwe n’umwe yavuga ku bijanye na we ngo: “Uri umwana wanje; jewe uno musi ncitse so.” 6 Nk’uko avuga kandi ahandi hantu ngo: “Uri umuherezi ibihe vyose mu buryo bwa Melekisedeki.”

7 Mu misi y’umubiri wiwe Kristu yashikaniye ugutakamba n’ugusaba Umwe yari ashoboye kumukiza urupfu, hariko n’amaborogo akomeye be n’amosozi, kandi yarumviwe ku bw’ugutinya Imana kwiwe. 8 Naho yari Umwana wiwe, yarize kugamburuka biciye ku mibabaro yamushikiye; 9 kandi aho amariye kugirwa uwutagira akanenge, yacitse uwujejwe ubukiriro budahera ku bamugamburukira bose, 10 kuko yiswe n’Imana bimwe bitomoye umuherezi mukuru mu buryo bwa Melekisedeki.

11 Ku bimwerekeye turafise vyinshi vyo kuvuga kandi bigoye isigura, kuko mwacitse abakomantaye mu kuntu mwumva. 12 Kukaba nkako, naho mwabwirizwa kuba muri abigisha turavye igihe kihaciye, mwongeye gukenera uwubigisha guhera ku ntango ibintu vy’intango vy’amajambo meranda y’Imana; kandi mwacitse abakeneye amata, si ibifungurwa bigumye. 13 Kuko umuntu wese afungura amata aba atazi ijambo ry’ubugororotsi, kuko aba ari uruyoya. 14 Mugabo ibifungurwa bigumye ni ivy’abantu bahumuye, abo ububasha bwabo bwo gutegera bumenyerezwa gutandukanya iciza n’ikibi, biciye ku gukoreshwa.
