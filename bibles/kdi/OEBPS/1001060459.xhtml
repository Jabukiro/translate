Abaheburayo  1 : 1 - 14

Ikete ryandikiwe Abaheburayo

1 Imana, iyavuganye kera mu bihe vyinshi no mu buryo bwinshi na ba sogokuruza bacu biciye ku bahanuzi, 2 kw’iherezo ry’iyi misi yavuganye natwe biciye ku Mwana wayo, uwo yagenye kuba samuragwa w’ibintu vyose, kandi biciye kuri we ikaba ari ho yashizeho ibihe*. 3 Ni we gukayangana kw’ubuninahazwa bwayo n’ishusho nyayo y’akamere nyene kayo, kandi ashigikiza ibintu vyose ijambo ry’ububasha bwiwe; kandi amaze kudutyorora ibicumuro vyacu, yicara iburyo bwa Nyenubukuru mu bibanza vyo hejuru. 4 Yabaye rero uwuruta abamarayika, kuko yatoranye izina ry’agahebuza kuruta iryabo.

5 Nk’akarorero, ni nde mu bamarayika yigeze kubwira iti: “Uri umwana wanje; jewe uno musi ncitse so”? Kandi iti: “Jewe ubwanje nzomubera se, na we azombera umwana”? 6 Mugabo niyongera kuzana Imfura yayo mw’isi, ivuga iti: “Kandi abamarayika b’Imana bose nibikubite imbere yiwe.”

7 Vyongeye, ku bijanye n’abamarayika ivuga ngo: “Kandi abamarayika bayo ibagira impwemu, abakozi bayo bakorera abantu ikabagira urubeya rw’umuriro.” 8 Mugabo ku bijanye n’Umwana wayo, ngo: “Imana ni yo ntebe yawe y’ubwami ibihe bitazoshira, kandi agakoni k’ubutware k’ubwami bwawe ni agakoni k’ukugororoka. 9 Wakunze ubugororotsi, wanka ubugarariji. Ni co gituma Imana, Imana yawe, yakurobanuje amavuta* y’umwigino kuruta bagenzawe.” 10 Kandi ngo: “Mu ntango, ewe Mukama, washinze imishinge y’isi, kandi amajuru ni ibikorwa vy’amaboko yawe. 11 Vyo bizoyogera, mugabo wewe wamaho ibihe vyose; vyose bizosaza nka kurya kw’impuzu yo hejuru, 12 kandi uzobizinga nk’igikwemu, nk’impuzu yo hejuru; kandi bizohindurwa, ariko wewe wama uri wa wundi, kandi imyaka yawe ntizokwigera ihera.”

13 Mugabo ni nde mu bamarayika yigeze kuvugako ngo: “Icara iburyo bwanje, gushika ngize abansi bawe nk’intebe y’ibirenge vyawe”? 14 Mbega bose si impwemu ku bw’igikorwa gikorerwa abantu, zatumwe gukorera abagiye kuzotorana ubukiriro?

^ Heb. 1:2 Canke, “ibiriho.” Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ Heb. 1:9 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
