2 Petero  1 : 1 - 21

Ikete rya Kabiri rya Petero

1 Jewe Simoni Petero, umushumba akaba n’intumwa ya Yezu Kristu, ndabandikiye mwebwe abaronse ukwizera, kakaba ari agateka kangana n’akacu, biciye ku bugororotsi bw’Imana yacu n’ubw’Umukiza Yezu Kristu:

2 Ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye n’amahoro vyongerezwe kuri mwebwe biciye ku bumenyi butagira amakosa bwerekeye Imana n’ubwerekeye Yezu Umukama wacu, 3 kuko ububasha bw’ubumana bwayo bwaduhaye ku buntu ibintu vyose vyerekeye ubuzima n’ukwihebera Imana, biciye mu bumenyi butagira amakosa bwerekeye umwe yaduhamagara biciye mu buninahazwa n’ingeso nziza. 4 Biciye kuri ivyo bintu yaraduhaye ku buntu ya mihango y’agaciro kandi y’agahambaye, kugira ngo biciye kuri yo mube abasangiye akamere k’Imana, mukaba mwarakize ubwononekare buri mw’isi buva ku nambu ikomeye.

5 Egome, ku bw’iyo mvo nyene, namwe nimugire ico mushubijeyo mu kwihata mwivuye inyuma: muronse ukwizera kwanyu ingeso nziza, ingeso nziza zanyu muzironse ubumenyi, 6 ubumenyi bwanyu muburonse ukwigumya, ukwigumya kwanyu mukuronse ukwihangana, ukwihangana kwanyu mukuronse ukwihebera Imana, 7 ukwihebera Imana kwanyu mukuronse ukwiyumvamwo abavukanyi, ukwiyumvamwo abavukanyi mukuronse urukundo. 8 Kuko nimba ivyo bintu biriho muri mwebwe kandi bigasesekara, bizobabuza kuba abantu badakora canke barumvye ku bijanye n’ubumenyi butagira amakosa bwerekeye Umukama wacu Yezu Kristu.

9 Kuko iyo umuntu adafise ivyo, aba ari impumyi, ahumiriza ngo amaso yiwe ntabone umuco, kandi aba yibagiye uguhumanurwa kw’ibicumuro vyiwe vya kera. 10 Ku bw’ico ga bene wacu, murushirize gukora uko mushoboye kwose ngo mutume ihamagarwa ryanyu be n’itorwa ryanyu biba ibidakekeranywa kuri mwebwe; kuko nimwaguma mukora ivyo bintu mutazogenda muguye. 11 Nkako, aho ni ho muzoronswa rwose ukwinjira mu bwami budahera bw’Umukama wacu n’Umukiza Yezu Kristu.

12 Ku bw’ico, nzokwama niteguriye kubibutsa ivyo bintu, naho mubizi mukaba kandi mushinze imizi rwose mu kuri kuri muri mwebwe. 13 Mugabo mbona ko bibereye, mu gihe nkiri muri iyi taberenakulo, kubakangura mu kubibutsa, 14 nzi yuko vuba igihe co kwambura itaberenakulo yanje kiri hafi, nk’uko n’Umukama wacu Yezu Kristu yabinyeretse. 15 Rero, nzokora kandi uko nshoboye kwose igihe cose kugira ngo, ninamara kugenda, muze mushobore kuvyibukanya.

16 Oya, ntitwabamenyesheje ububasha n’ukuhaba vy’Umukama wacu Yezu Kristu twisunze inkuru z’ikinyoma zaremaremwe ku buhinga, ahubwo twabigize twisunze ukuba twariboneye ugukayangana kwiwe. 17 Kuko yahawe n’Imana ari yo Data iteka n’ubuninahazwa, hamwe amajambo nk’aya yayashikirizwa n’ubuninahazwa bw’agatangaza ngo: “Uyu ni umwana wanje, umukundwa wanje, uwo jewe nashimye.” 18 Egome, ayo majambo twarayumvise azanwa ava mw’ijuru igihe twari kumwe na we kuri wa musozi mweranda.

19 Nuko rero, turafise ijambo ry’ubuhanuzi ryagizwe ntakekeranywa kuruta; kandi muriko mugira neza kuryitwararika nk’itara rikayanganira ahantu h’umwiza, gushika umusi utamanzuye n’inyenyeri y’umutwenzi* igaseruka, mu mitima yanyu. 20 Kuko ubwa mbere ibi mubizi: yuko ata buhanuzi bw’Icanditswe buva ku vyiyumviro vy’umuntu ku giti ciwe. 21 Kuko ubuhanuzi ata ho bwigeze buzanwa n’ukugomba kw’umuntu, ahubwo abantu bavuze ivyava ku Mana nk’uko bari barongowe n’impwemu nyeranda.

^ 2 Pet. 1:19 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
