1 Yohani  1 : 1 - 10

Ikete rya Mbere rya Yohani

1 Kimwe cariho kuva mu ntango, ico twumvise, ico twabonye n’amaso yacu, ico twitegereje neza n’amaboko yacu akagikorakora, ku vyerekeye rya jambo ry’ubuzima 2 (egome, ubuzima bwarerekanywe, kandi twarabonye tukaba kandi turiko turashinga intahe twongera tubamenyesha ubuzima budahera bwari kumwe na Data tukaba kandi twarabweretswe), 3 kimwe twabona kandi tukumva ni na co turiko turabamenyesha, kugira ngo namwe musangire natwe. Ikigeretseko, ukwo gusangira kwacu kugirwa hamwe na Data be n’Umwana wiwe Yezu Kristu. 4 Ni co gituma tubandikiye ibi bintu kugira ngo akanyamuneza kacu kuzure.

5 Kandi ubu ni bwo butumwa twamwumvanye ari na bwo turiko turabamenyesha: yuko Imana ari umuco, kandi ntihaba umwiza namba mu bumwe na yo. 6 Iyo tuvuze ngo: “Dusangiye na yo,” yamara tukabandanya kugendera mu mwiza, tuba turiko turabesha kandi ntituba turiko tugendera mu kuri. 7 Ariko nimba tugendera mu muco nk’uko na yo ubwayo iri mu muco, turafise koko ugusangira hagati yacu, kandi amaraso ya Yezu Umwana wayo aratwoza igicumuro cose.

8 Iyo tuvuze ngo: “Nta gicumuro dufise,” tuba turiko turizimiza kandi ukuri ntikuba kuri muri twebwe. 9 Iyo twemeye icese ibicumuro vyacu, Imana ni inyizigirwa ikaba n’iyigororotse kugira ngo itubabarire ibicumuro vyacu yongere itwoze ukutagororoka kwose. 10 Iyo tuvuze ngo: “Ntitwacumuye,” tuba tuyigize umubeshi, kandi ijambo ryayo ntiriba riri muri twebwe.
