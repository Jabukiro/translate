Ivyahishuwe  10 : 1 - 11

10 Maze mbona uwundi mumarayika w’inkomezi amanuka ava mw’ijuru, yambaye igicu, kandi umunywamazi wari ku mutwe wiwe, mu maso hiwe hakaba hari hameze nk’izuba, amaguru yiwe na yo yari nk’inkingi z’umuriro, 2 kandi yari afise mu kuboko kwiwe akazingo gatoya kazinguruye. Ashinga ukuguru kwiwe kw’ukuryo ku kiyaga, mugabo ukw’ukubamfu agushinga kw’isi, 3 asemerera n’ijwi rirenga nk’igihe intambwe iroshe. Buno asemereye, ya mituragaro indwi irekura imyasano yayo bwite.

4 Igihe iyo mituragaro indwi yavuga, nagira nandike; mugabo nca numva ijwi rivuye mw’ijuru rivuga riti: “Ibintu iyo mituragaro indwi ivuze nubibike nk’ibidomweko ikidodo, kandi ntuvyandike.” 5 Maze wa mumarayika nabona ahagaze ku kiyaga no kw’isi aduza ukuboko kwiwe kw’ukuryo akwerekeza mw’ijuru, 6 arahira Uwubaho ibihe bitazoshira yaremye ijuru n’ibiri muri ryo n’isi n’ibiri muri yo n’ikiyaga n’ibiri muri co, ati: “Nta guteba kukihabaye; 7 mugabo mu misi y’ukwumvikana kw’ijwi ry’umumarayika agira indwi, igihe azoba agira avuze urumbete rwiwe, ibanga ryeranda ry’Imana nk’uko inkuru nziza iri, iyo yamenyesheje abashumba bayo bwite ari bo bahanuzi, rizoba koko rirangijwe.”

8 Hanyuma rya jwi numvise rivuye mw’ijuru ryongera kuvugana nanje riti: “Genda, utore urya muzingo uzinguruye uri mu kuboko kw’urya mumarayika ahagaze ku kiyaga no kw’isi.” 9 Nca nja kuri wa mumarayika ndamubwira kumpa ka kazingo gatoya. Araheza ambwira ati: “Kakire ukarye, kandi kaza gutuma mu nda yawe harura, mugabo mu kanwa kawe kaza gusosa nk’ubuki.” 10 Ndaheza nakira nya kazingo gatoya mu kuboko kwa wa mumarayika ndakarya, mu kanwa kanje gasosa nk’ubuki; mugabo aho mariye kukarya, mu nda hanje hararura. 11 Baca bambwira bati: “Utegerezwa kwongera kuvuga ubuhanuzi ku bijanye n’ibisata n’amahanga n’indimi n’abami benshi.”
