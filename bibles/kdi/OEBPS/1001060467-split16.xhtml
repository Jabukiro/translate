Ivyahishuwe  16 : 1 - 21

16 Maze numva ijwi rirenga rivuye aheranda ribwira ba bamarayika indwi riti: “Nimugende musuke mw’isi ayo mabakure indwi y’ishavu ry’Imana.”

2 Uwa mbere aragenda, asuka ibakure yiwe mw’isi. Maze igisebe kibabaza kandi gikaze kiza ku bantu bari bafise ikimenyetso ca ca gikoko kandi bariko basenga ishusho yaco.

3 Uwa kabiri na we asuka ibakure yiwe mu kiyaga. Gica gihinduka amaraso nk’ay’umuntu yapfuye, maze igifise ubuzima cose kirapfa, egome, ibiri mu kiyaga.

4 Uwa gatatu na we asuka ibakure yiwe mu nzuzi no mu masôko y’amazi. Bica bihinduka amaraso. 5 Ndaheza numva umumarayika w’amazi avuga ati: “Wewe, Uwuriho kandi yariho, Intahemuka, uragororotse, kuko wapfunditse izo ngingo, 6 kuko basheshe amaraso y’aberanda n’ay’abahanuzi, nawe ubahaye amaraso ngo banywe. Barabikwiriye.” 7 Maze numva igicaniro kivuga giti: “Egome, Yehova Mana, Mushoboravyose, ingingo zawe z’ubutungane* ni iz’ukuri kandi ziragororotse.”

8 Uwa kane na we asuka ibakure yiwe ku zuba; izuba rica rihabwa kubabuza abantu umuriro. 9 Maze abantu bababuzwa ubushuhe bukomeye, mugabo barogota kw’izina ry’Imana, iyifise ububasha kuri ivyo vyago, kandi ntibigaya ngo bayihe ubuninahazwa.

10 Uwa gatanu na we asuka ibakure yiwe ku ntebe y’ubwami ya ca gikoko. Ubwami bwaco buca bucurishwa umwiza, batangura kwihekenya indimi kubera umubabaro wabo, 11 mugabo barogota ku Mana yo mw’ijuru kubera imibabaro n’ibisebe vyabo, kandi ntibigaya ibikorwa vyabo.

12 Uwa gatandatu na we asuka ibakure yiwe kuri rwa ruzi runini Efurate, amazi yarwo aca arakama, kugira ngo inzira itegurwe ku bw’abami bava i burasirazuba.

13 Maze mbona amajambo y’amahumekano atatu ahumanye yasa n’ibikere ava mu kanwa ka ca gisato no mu kanwa ka ca gikoko no mu kanwa ka wa muhanuzi w’ikinyoma. 14 Ayo nkako ni amajambo ahumetswe n’amadayimoni kandi agakora ibimenyetso, kandi aja ku bami b’isi yose, ngo abakoranirize hamwe ku ntambara yo kuri wa musi uhambaye w’Imana Mushoboravyose.

15 “Ehe ndaje nk’igisuma. Hahiriwe uwuguma arikanuye akagumya impuzu ziwe zo hejuru, kugira ngo ntagende ari gusa maze abantu bakaraba isoni z’ubwambure bwiwe.”

16 Abakoraniriza hamwe ahantu hitwa Haru–Magedoni* mu giheburayo.

17 Uw’indwi na we asuka ibakure yiwe ku kirere. Ijwi rirenga rica risohoka rivuye aheranda kuri ya ntebe y’ubwami, rivuga riti: “Birakozwe!” 18 Maze haba imiravyo n’amajwi n’imituragaro, haba na nyamugigima ikomeye itari bwigere iba kuva abantu babaye kw’isi, iba nyamugigima igera kure cane, emwe, ikomeye cane. 19 Ca gisagara gikomeye gica gisadukamwo gutatu, n’ibisagara vy’amahanga biratemba; hanyuma Babiloni Akomeye aribukwa imbere y’Imana, kugira ngo ahabwe igikombe c’umuvinyu w’ishavu ry’uburake bwayo. 20 Vyongeye, izinga ryose rirahunga, n’imisozi ntiyaboneka. 21 Maze urubura rukomeye rw’iminana umwumwe upima nk’italanto* rumanuka ruvuye mw’ijuru rukoragurikira ku bantu, nya bantu na bo barogota ku Mana kubera ico cago c’urubura, kuko icago carwo cari gikomeye bidasanzwe.

^ Ivyah. 16:7 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ Ivyah. 16:16 Raba Inyongera ya 21.

^ Ivyah. 16:21 Italanto imwe ni nk’ibilo 20,4.
