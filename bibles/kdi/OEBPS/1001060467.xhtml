Ivyahishuwe  1 : 1 - 20

Ivyahishuriwe Yohani

1 Ivyahishuwe na Yezu Kristu, ivyo Imana yamuhaye ngo yereke abashumba bayo ibintu bitegerezwa kuba vuba. Na we yatumye umumarayika wiwe hanyuma abishikiriza biciye kuri we mu bimenyetso, bishikirizwa umushumba wayo* Yohani, 2 uwashingiye intahe ijambo Imana yatanze n’intahe Yezu Kristu yashinze, mbere n’ibintu vyose yabonye. 3 Hahiriwe uwusoma n’ijwi ryumvikana be n’abumva amajambo y’ubu buhanuzi, kandi bakubahiriza ibintu vyanditswe muri bwo; kuko igihe cashinzwe kiri hafi.

4 Jewe Yohani, ndabandikiye mwebwe mashengero indwi ari mu ntara ya Aziya:

Mugire ubuntu ata wari abukwiriye n’amahoro biva kuri “Umwe ariho kandi yariho kandi ariko araza,” no kuri za mpwemu indwi ziri imbere y’intebe y’ubwami yiwe, 5 no kuri Yezu Kristu, “ca Cabona Cizigirwa,” “Imfura mu kuva mu bapfuye,” n’“Umutware w’abami b’isi.”

Umwe adukunda kandi yatubohoye mu bicumuro vyacu biciye ku maraso yiwe bwite— 6 kandi yatugize ubwami, abaherezi ku Mana yiwe ikaba na Data—egome, ubuninahazwa n’ubushobozi bibe ivyiwe ibihe vyose. Amen.

7 Ehe ariko arazanana n’ibicu, kandi ijisho ryose rizomubona, n’abamucumise bazomubona; kandi imiryango yose yo kw’isi izokwikubitagura ku gikiriza yishwe n’intuntu kubera we. Egome, Amen.

8 “Ndi Alufa na Omega*,” ni ko Yehova Imana avuga, “Umwe ariho kandi yariho kandi ariko araza, Mushoboravyose.”

9 Jewe Yohani, mwene wanyu nkaba n’uwusangiye namwe amakuba n’ubwami n’ukwihangana mu kwifatanya na Yezu, nari mw’izinga ryitwa Patimo mporwa ukuvuga ivyerekeye Imana n’ugushingira intahe Yezu. 10 Biciye ku guhumekerwa nasanze ndi mu musi w’Umukama, maze numva inyuma yanje ijwi rikomeye nk’iry’urumbete, 11 rivuga riti: “Ico ubona ucandike mu muzingo uheze ukirungikire ya mashengero indwi: i Efeso n’i Simuruna n’i Perugamo n’i Tiyatira n’i Sarudi n’i Filadelufiya n’i Lawodiseya.”

12 Maze mpindukira kuraba nya jwi ryariko rivugana nanje, mpindukiye mbona ibitereko vy’amatara indwi vy’inzahabu, 13 kandi hagati ya nya bitereko vy’amatara mbona uwumeze nk’umwana w’umuntu, yambaye impuzu ishika ku birenge, akenyeje umukanda w’inzahabu mu gikiriza. 14 Vyongeye, umutwe wiwe n’umushatsi wiwe vyarera nk’ubwoya bw’intama bwera, nk’urubura, amaso yiwe na yo akaba yari ameze nk’urubeya rw’umuriro; 15 kandi amaguru yiwe yari ameze nk’umujumpu uramuye igihe wakaka mw’itanure; kandi ijwi ryiwe ryari rimeze nk’ugusuma kw’amazi menshi. 16 Kandi yari afise mu kuboko kwiwe kw’ukuryo inyenyeri indwi, mu kanwa kiwe hasokorokamwo inkota ndende y’ubugi bubiri, ikarishe, mu maso hiwe na ho hari hameze nk’izuba iyo rikayanganye mu bubasha bwaryo. 17 Aho ndamuboneye, ngwa nk’uwapfuye imbere y’ibirenge vyiwe.

Maze anshirako ukuboko kwiwe kw’ukuryo, avuga ati: “Ntutinye. Ndi uwa Mbere n’uwa Nyuma, 18 n’umwe ari muzima; kandi narapfuye, mugabo, ehe ndi muzima ibihe bitazoshira, kandi mfise impfunguruzo z’urupfu n’iz’ukuzimu. 19 Andika rero ivyo wabonye, n’ibiriho be n’ibizoba inyuma y’ibi. 20 Ku vyerekeye ibanga ryeranda ry’inyenyeri indwi wabonye mu kuboko kwanje kw’ukuryo, n’irya vya bitereko vy’amatara indwi vy’inzahabu: Zirya nyenyeri indwi zigereranya abamarayika ba ya mashengero indwi, birya bitereko vy’amatara indwi na vyo bikagereranya amashengero indwi.

^ Ivyah. 1:1 Canke, “wa Yezu.”

^ Ivyah. 1:8 Raba Inyongera ya 21.
