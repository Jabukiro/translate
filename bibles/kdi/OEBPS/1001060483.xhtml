12 Ikirangamisi c’amezi yo muri Bibiliya

Amezi y’Abayuda yahera ku mboneko y’ukwezi gushika ku yindi (Yes 66:23). Ijambo rimwe ry’igiheburayo choʹdhesh, “ukwezi” (It 7:11), rivuye kw’itsitso risobanura “imboneko,” irindi na ryo ryahinduwe ngo ukwezi, ari ryo yeʹrach, risobanura “ukwezi kwo ku mboneko.”
